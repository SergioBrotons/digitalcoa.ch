# 01 — ICP: Phase 02 Geneva Finance (C118)

## ICP: Geneva Finance Advisory

### Primary ICP
**Target:** CFOs and Treasurers at Geneva-headquartered financial institutions (private banks, commodity traders, asset managers)

**Profile:**
- CHF 500M-50B balance sheet; treasury function 2-20 people
- Exposure: SNB policy, interest rate risk, FX (CHF/EUR/USD), DLT/tokenization
- Decision-maker: CFO, Treasurer, Head of Treasury
- Buying signal: SNB rate decisions, DLT Act implementation deadline, sustainable finance reporting mandates
- Geography: Geneva canton; some Vaud references

### Secondary ICP
**Target:** Mid-size Geneva family offices (UHNW, CHF 100M-1B AUM)

**Profile:**
- Internal investment team 1-5 people; no dedicated treasury function
- Exposure: portfolio allocation, DLT assets, sustainable finance, tax residency
- Decision-maker: Family office CIO, principal
- Buying signal: BVK reform impact, lump-sum tax changes, DLT portfolio exposure

### Tertiary ICP
**Target:** Swiss asset managers (Geneva-based, SFAMA members)

**Profile:**
- SFAMA-regulated; fund administration via Swiss platforms ( SIX, VP Bank)
- Exposure: sustainable finance taxonomy, DLT fund issuance, FINMA enforcement
- Decision-maker: CEO, Head of Compliance
- Buying signal: DLT Act fund tokenization, ESG reporting, FINMA circular updates
