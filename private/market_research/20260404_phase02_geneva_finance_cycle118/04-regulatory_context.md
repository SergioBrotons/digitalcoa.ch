# 04 — Regulatory Context: Phase 02 Geneva Finance (C118)

## Active Regulatory Themes (Geneva Finance, April 2026)

### Theme 1: Sustainable Finance Taxonomy
- **Status:** Federal Council action plan adopted; SFAMA issuing guidance
- **Timeline:** Ongoing implementation; first reporting cycles 2026
- **Key documents:** EU taxonomy alignment framework, SFAMA sustainable finance guidelines, FINMA climate risk guidance
- **Advisory angle:** Gap analysis, taxonomy classification, disclosure compliance

### Theme 2: DLT Act / Tokenization
- **Status:** DLT Act in force; amended 2024-2025; FINMA DLT guidance updated
- **Timeline:** First tokenized fund products in market; regulatory sandbox closed, mainstream emerging
- **Key documents:** DLT Act, FINMA DLT/TAK guidance, SIX DD regulatory framework
- **Advisory angle:** DLT structuring, tokenized asset legal framework, investor onboarding for DLT securities

### Theme 3: SNB Interest Rate Policy
- **Status:** SNB maintaining policy rate corridor; monitoring FX and inflation
- **Key documents:** SNB monetary policy statements, SNB financial stability reports
- **Advisory angle:** Treasury risk management, FX hedging, net interest margin optimization

### Theme 4: FINMA AI Guidance
- **Status:** FINMA published AI guidance for Swiss financial institutions
- **Key documents:** FINMA circular on AI, AI model risk management guidance
- **Advisory angle:** AI governance framework, explainability requirements, FINMA compliance

### Theme 5: Real Estate Credit Quality
- **Status:** Rising rates affecting mortgage affordability; covenant monitoring intensifying
- **Key documents:** SNB mortgage market reports, FINMA credit risk guidance
- **Advisory angle:** Covenant restructuring, refinancing advisory, workout support

### Theme 6: FINMA Enforcement
- **Status:** Increased AML, greenwashing, operational resilience enforcement 2025-2026
- **Key documents:** FINMA enforcement actions, annual reports
- **Advisory angle:** Compliance framework review, enforcement defense, remediation planning
