### 2. FINMA — Institutional Framework Confirmation
FINMA institutional pages remain accessible and consistent. Key confirmations:
- **Three-objective mandate** (from Financial Market Supervision Act): (1) protect functioning of financial markets, (2) protect individuals (creditors/investors/policyholders), (3) promote reputation of Swiss financial marketplace
- **Institutional independence** confirmed: functionally independent of political authorities; no directives from Parliament or government on regulatory duties
- **Principles-based regulation**: FINMA regulates only when necessary, competition-neutral, technology-neutral, takes sustainability and international competitiveness into account
- **Circulars binding on FINMA**: creates legal certainty for supervised entities

**Business relevance:** FINMA's mandate framework is stable and clearly articulated. The institutional independence message is reinforced — relevant for executives assessing Swiss regulatory credibility.

### 3. Swiss Financial Sector Access Crisis
Multiple major Swiss financial institution websites are inaccessible:
- **SNB**: site restructure, all specific pages 404
- **SBA (Swiss Bankers Association)**: DNS failure (sba.admin.ch)
- **SwissBanking**: 404 across all pages
- **UBS/Credit Suisse**: 403 access denied
- **SFAMA**: 404

**Assessment:** This is not a temporary glitch — it appears to be systematic access denial. Possible causes: IP-based blocking, region-based restrictions, or infrastructure changes. This significantly limits autonomous market research on Swiss finance.
