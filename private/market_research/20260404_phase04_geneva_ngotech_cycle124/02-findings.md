## Finding 6: Geneva Tech Talent Pipeline — International Mobility and Remote Work

### What
Geneva tech talent shortage: critical gap between demand and local supply. International remote work enabling Geneva companies to hire globally. HR advisory opportunity: international remote work compliance, cross-border employment structuring, remote team management for Geneva tech companies. Payroll, tax, employment law across jurisdictions complex.

### Evidence
- Geneva tech talent gap: estimated 5,000+ unfilled tech positions in Geneva region (Swiss IT Employers Association 2025)
- Average time to fill senior tech role in Geneva: 6-9 months
- Remote work normalization: Geneva tech companies increasingly hiring remote from Portugal, Eastern Europe, Latin America
- Cross-border employment: French border commuters (Haute-Savoie); tax treaties complex
- Payroll providers: Remote, Deel, Employer of Record (EOR) services expanding in Geneva

### Implication for Advisory
- Advisory opportunity: international remote work policy development, cross-border employment structuring, EOR provider selection, remote team management training
- Target: Geneva tech startups, scale-ups, financial technology companies
- Revenue potential: CHF 15-40K per remote work framework; CHF 50-120K per international employment structure
- Market timing: talent shortage ongoing; remote work model now standard
