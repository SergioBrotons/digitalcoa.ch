# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Cleantech Series A Readiness Framework

## Strategic Rationale
Geneva's cleantech ecosystem produces strong seed-stage companies but struggles to get them to Series A. Most founders have product and some traction but lack investor positioning, commercial strategy, and financial model sophistication. A practical Series A readiness framework — Geneva-specific, actionable — positions the firm as the go-to advisor for this underserved segment.
