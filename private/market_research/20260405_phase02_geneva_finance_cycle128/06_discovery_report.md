## Key Discoveries

### 1. SNB FX Intervention Asymmetry — Advisory Gap
Geneva market participants lack systematic frameworks for interpreting the SNB's discretionary FX intervention posture. The SNB signals readiness but commits to no specific level — creating asymmetric risk for both CHF-strength and CHF-weakness scenarios. **Advisory opportunity:** signal monitoring framework, reaction function analysis, treasury positioning strategy.

### 2. FINMA AML Enforcement Wave — Time-Sensitive
FINMA's 2025 enforcement activity in private banking, combined with the March 2026 FATF mutual evaluation, has created a remediation imperative for Geneva private banks. High-risk client segments (Russian/CIS, complex structures, UBO gaps) require immediate enhanced due diligence. **Advisory opportunity:** remediation program design, independent compliance function review, FATF gap analysis.

### 3. BVG Infrastructure Debt — New Regulatory Window
The January 2026 BVG reform opened infrastructure debt as a permitted allocation for Swiss pension funds. Geneva institutional investors are now evaluating first-time allocations — creating demand for compliance, manager selection, and co-investment advisory. **Advisory opportunity:** BVG investment policy updates, infrastructure debt manager due diligence, co-investment structuring.

### 4. SERV Export Finance — Tariff Disruption
US tariff escalation Q1 2026 has strained SERV-covered Swiss exports in precision machinery, pharma intermediates, and specialty chemicals. SERV is seeing capacity strain. **Advisory opportunity:** tariff impact on SERV transactions, payment term restructuring, credit insurance portfolio optimization, SERV alternatives.

### 5. OECD Pillar Two — Wealth Structuring Under New Tax Regime
The first full year of OECD Pillar Two minimum tax (15%) is reshaping Geneva UHNW wealth structuring. Trust structures, family holding companies, and philanthropic vehicles require proactive analysis under the new regime. **Advisory opportunity:** Pillar Two impact analysis, Geneva family office restructuring, foundation governance review.

### 6. SARON Compound vs LIBOR — Technical Remediation
SARON is a compounded overnight rate; legacy contracts referencing SARON without proper compounding mechanics misprice interest. FINMA/SIX have flagged remediation timelines. **Advisory opportunity:** contract remediation audit, compounded SARON calculation framework, fallback clause standardization.
