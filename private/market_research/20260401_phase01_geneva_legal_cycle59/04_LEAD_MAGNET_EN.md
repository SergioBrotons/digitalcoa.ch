## The Envelope vs. The Letter

Not all AI automation carries the same risk. Before automating anything, ask:

**Is privileged or substantive client content involved?**

**Envelope (safe to automate now):**
- Routine routing and scheduling
- Time tracking and billing preparation
- Document formatting and filing
- Conflict check support
- Invoice chasing and payment tracking

**Letter (requires governance framework first):**
- AI interpreting case facts or drafting legal positions
- Contract analysis for substantive terms
- Section 301 compliance screening
- Tariff classification assistance
- Client advice generation

Small Geneva firms recover 8–14 hours per week on envelope tasks alone — without creating new exposure.
