### 3. SBA Stablecoin Position — "We Do Not Want a Luxembourg Scenario for Digital Assets"
- **Spokesperson:** Martin Hess (Swiss Bankers Association / SBVg)
- **Key quote:** "Switzerland must ensure it does not fall behind"
- **Context:** US GENIUS Act (federal stablecoin legislation) creates urgency
- **Historical:** DLT Act 2021 — early international milestone
- **Current problem:** FinIA amendment draft forces Swiss banks to create subsidiaries for stablecoin issuance
- **SBA position:** Banks should issue stablecoins under existing banking licence (like EU)
- **Competitive risk:** Mandatory outsourcing = "Luxembourg scenario" = bureaucratic burden without safety benefit
- **Concrete use case:** AllUnity (regulated German e-money institution) announced Swiss-franc stablecoin
- **Risk concerns:** Value stability, financial stability, disintermediation risk, stress tests needed
- **Source:** swissbanking.ch/en/media-politics/news/we-do-not-want-a-luxembourg-scenario-for-digital-assets

### 4. Nature Transition Financing — SBA / BCG / Quantis Study
- **Commissioned by:** Swiss Bankers Association
- **Conducted by:** Boston Consulting Group + Quantis (BCG sustainability arm)
- **Total annual financing need:** CHF 5.3 billion by 2050
- **Current spending:** CHF 3.2 billion/year
- **Gap:** CHF 2.1 billion/year incremental
- **Public/private split:** 85% publicly financed currently; private capital mobilization needed
- **Largest financing areas (>75% of total):**
  1. Water infrastructure (wastewater, river revitalisation, nature-based solutions)
  2. Hydropower rehabilitation
  3. Regenerative agriculture (capital investments + income support)
- **Context:** Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework (GBF) — Switzerland signed 2022
- **Challenge:** No "net zero" equivalent for nature; no national financing path previously existed
- **Source:** swissbanking.ch/en/media-politics/news/financing-options-for-switzerlands-nature-transition
