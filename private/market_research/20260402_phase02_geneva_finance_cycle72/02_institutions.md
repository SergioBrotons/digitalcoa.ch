# 02 — Geneva Finance: Key Institutions & Decision-Making Bodies

## Banks

### Global Systemically Important Banks (G-SIBs) — Swiss
- **UBS Group AG:** Global headquarters Geneva; primary Swiss G-SIB post-CS integration
- **Credit Suisse AG:** Fully integrated into UBS as of 2023; ongoing legal arbitrations

- **Mirabaud Group:** Geneva-based; private banking + asset management
- **Rothschild & Co:** Geneva presence; heritage private banking
