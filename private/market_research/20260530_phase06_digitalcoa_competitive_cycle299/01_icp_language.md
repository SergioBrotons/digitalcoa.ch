# ICP Language: DigitalCoa.ch — C299 Phase 06

## ICP Definition
Senior professional in Geneva — managing director, partner, or practice leader — personally accountable for AI governance decisions. Wants someone who understands the Geneva accountability ecosystem and can name the problem in their language before proposing a solution.

## DigitalCoa.ch ICP Language Patterns

### Before First Contact
- ICP arrives having already lived through consequence events — carrier follow-ups, client audit requests, Swissdamed deadline pressure
- ICP has tried to self-serve: read the forms, searched online, talked to peers, downloaded templates
- ICP is not seeking education — seeking specific calibration against their actual situation
- ICP uses consequence language, not awareness language — "I already signed this and now X is asking"
- Swissdamed deadline creates acute urgency in healthcare — weeks, not months

### On the Briefing Call
- "What exactly did I commit to when I signed?" (consequence activation — the question is now retrospective)
- "The carrier is asking about something I never documented — what do I tell them?" (post-signature crisis)
- "Can I still reconstruct what happened in 2023-2024?" (documentation window close awareness)
- "How do I prioritize when everything is urgent?" (cascade overwhelm)
- "Is my processor chain actually a problem?" (processor chain as named exposure)

### On the Engagement
- "I need something I can actually use this week" (urgent, not comprehensive — deadline-driven)
- "Can you look at what I already signed?" (post-signing review, not pre-signing intercept for existing clients)
- "What's the minimum documentation that actually protects me?" (proportionate methodology demand)
- "The Swissdamed deadline — can we deal with that first?" (healthcare-specific urgency hierarchy)

### ICP Self-Description Signals
- "I signed on my phone between meetings" (behavioral normalization acknowledgment)
- "I knew I should have reviewed it — I didn't have time" (post-hoc rationalization of normalized non-review)
- "The institutional client is now using my 2024 response as competitive intelligence" (downstream consequence recognition)
- "Everyone I know is in the same situation" (social proof of universality, not comfort)
- "July 1 is coming fast" (Swissdamed deadline activation — healthcare)
- "I didn't know the processor chain was my responsibility" (accountability gap discovery)

## Language to Avoid
- "AI governance framework" (too enterprise, wrong scale — ICP has consequence problem not design problem)
- "compliance solution" (wrong frame — ICP has accountability and urgency problem)
- "best practice" (no Geneva standard exists — undefined adequacy is the problem)
- "AI strategy" (ICP has consequence management problem not strategic planning problem)
- "tool selection" (wrong layer — ICP needs immediate consequence resolution not tool evaluation)
- "training" (wrong frame — ICP needs process and documentation, not education)

## Language That Lands
- "What you committed to when you signed" (retrospective consequence activation)
- "Before your carrier follows up" (specific timing — post-signing intervention point)
- "The processor chain is being probed directly now" (specific, named, now — not abstract risk)
- "July 1 — weeks away" (Swissdamed deadline activation)
- "What you're actually responsible for" (accountability precision)
- "The documentation window closed before you knew it existed" (irreversibility signal)
- "They built them independently — now they're being reviewed together" (four-track coherence problem)
- "The same engagement produces confidential intelligence for you and methodology evidence through accumulation" (compound asset)
- "I track carrier form evolution every cycle" (competitive intelligence signal)
- "You've already talked to [reference] about this" (circuit verification signal)
