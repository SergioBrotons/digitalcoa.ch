Vendor term discovery is showing undisclosed processing flows. Swissdamed July 1 deadline is creating documentation stampede with no classification methodology.

**Velocity indicator:** The temporal arc is complete: signing → silence → consequence arrival. The cascade is occurring across all four verticals simultaneously in the same structural form.

**Timing:** Immediate — consequences are already arriving. Not future risk, present-tense problem.

**Implication for DigitalCoa.ch:** Pre-signing review remains highest-value entry point for new ICPs. But consequence management retainer is the retention vehicle for ICPs already in the cascade. The conversion frame shifts from "help me understand what this means" to "help me manage what I've already committed to."

**Signal strength:** High — confirmed cross-vertical in Phase 05 C299 findings.

### Signal 02 — Behavioral Normalization of Non-Review: "Signed on My Phone Between Meetings"

**What is happening:** The normalized behavior in C299 is not ignorance — it's behavioral adaptation under documentation volume that is experienced as unmanageable. The ICP signs carrier forms on their phone between meetings, between patients, on trains, between client calls. The signature does not represent governance evaluation. This has become structural, not exceptional.

**Mechanism:** Documentation volume from nFADP, Swissdamed, carrier attestation, institutional questionnaires, EU AI Act obligations, and FINMA guidance has exceeded the capacity of any single professional to review documents carefully. The adaptive response — treating signature as administrative friction resolution — has become the default.

**Timing:** Ongoing — structural adaptation, not a phase that will pass.

**Implication for DigitalCoa.ch:** The service need is not "help me understand what I signed." The service need is "build a process so that my signature represents actual governance evaluation rather than administrative friction resolution." The pre-signing review service replaces the ritual with a process.

**Signal strength:** High — confirmed as behavioral normalization in Phase 05 C299, distinct from awareness gap in earlier cycles.

### Signal 05 — Four Regulatory Frameworks Creating Compound Documentation Burden Without Cross-Mandate Coherence

**What is happening:** Finance ICPs specifically are managing four independent documentation systems simultaneously: FINMA Circular 08/2024, institutional client questionnaire responses, nFADP ROPA/DPIA/Art.26, carrier attestation evidence — each with different evidentiary standards, update cycles, disclosure scopes, and responsible persons. The documentation built for one track may be internally inconsistent with the documentation built for another.

**The specific risk:** nFADP documentation that satisfies FDPIC may not protect the managing director in carrier attestation context. Institutional questionnaire response may describe AI governance architecture in terms that conflict with what was attested to FINMA. Carrier attestation may contain representations that cannot be defended under nFADP Art.63 criminal standard.

**Timing:** Ongoing — all four tracks are live and being reviewed together for the first time.

**Implication for DigitalCoa.ch:** Cross-mandate coherence methodology — a framework that satisfies multiple documentation requirements simultaneously while maintaining internal consistency — is the missing service category. Most acute in finance where all four tracks operate simultaneously for the same managing director.

**Signal strength:** High — confirmed in Phase 05 C299 as named discrete service gap.
