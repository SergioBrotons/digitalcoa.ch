## Core Value Proposition
AI governance is no longer optional — it is a client trust and competitive differentiator. Swiss law firms face a 36-point gap between individual AI use (70%) and formal firm-level AI adoption (34%). This gap creates both regulatory timing risk and a first-mover opportunity for firms that formalize governance now.
