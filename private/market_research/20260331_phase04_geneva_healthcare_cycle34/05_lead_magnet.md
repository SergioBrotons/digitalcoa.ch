**Frame:** Two separate registries, not interconnected

- Separate registration requirements for medical device actors

- EUDAMED registration for practices with EU nexus
- Swissdamed registration for all Swiss medical device actors

**Practical implication:**
- Register once with Swissdamed (Swiss market)

- Swissdamed does NOT automatically feed EUDAMED
