## Section 2: What's Already Live — GPAI Transparency Obligations

The EU AI Act's General-Purpose AI (GPAI) provisions have been operative since August 2, 2025. You've had approximately 8 months to comply.

## Section 4: The Dual Compliance Challenge

If your consulting firm has EU clients or EU market exposure, you're subject to TWO regimes simultaneously:

**Swiss domestic**: nFADP Art. 5(c) — Active since 2023; AI-based automated decisions about individuals require DPIA + human review

**EU extraterritorial**: EU AI Act GPAI — Active since August 2, 2025; transparency obligations already live for EU-nexus firms

No combined guidance exists for small consulting firms navigating both regimes.
