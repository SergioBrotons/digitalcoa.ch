# Phase 03: Geneva Consulting — Market Signals
# Cycle 16 — 2026-03-30

## Market Signals

### Signal 1: GPAI Transparency Obligations ACTIVE NOW (~8 months)
EU AI Act GPAI has been live since August 2, 2025. As of March 30, 2026, ~8 months of active obligation. Firms with EU nexus already owe transparency documentation. High-risk obligations activate August 2, 2026 (~4.5 months away).

### Signal 2: Federal Council First Draft May 2026 (~2 months)
Multi-stakeholder "AI Action Plan for Switzerland" (Digital Switzerland, Federal Councillor Rösti) will present First Draft in May 2026. This is the next external pressure spike for Geneva consulting firms. Demand for AI governance guidance will increase.

### Signal 3: 40% Geneva SME AI Skills Gap
PwC 2024 data: 40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI skills. Small consulting firms are both victims and potential providers of this gap.

### Signal 4: Federal Council Lean Approach Confirmed
Federal Council announced February 12, 2025: targeted sector-specific adjustments, NOT a comprehensive horizontal AI law. No cross-sector Swiss AI law before end-2026.

### Signal 5: GPAI Creates Dual Compliance Challenge
Small consulting firms with EU clients face nFADP (Swiss domestic) + EU AI Act GPAI (EU extraterritorial) dual compliance. No combined guidance exists for small firms.

### Signal 6: IT/Tech Sub-Segment Faces Strongest GPAI Pressure
IT and tech consulting firms with EU market presence face the most immediate GPAI pressure. This sub-segment is the strongest initial outreach target.
