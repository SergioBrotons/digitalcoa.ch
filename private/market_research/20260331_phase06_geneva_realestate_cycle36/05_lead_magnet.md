## Lead Magnet: "The Small Real Estate Agency AI Governance Question"

### Positioning
For Geneva-area small real estate agencies and property managers (1–10 agents) who use AI tools for property valuations, listing descriptions, and tenant screening — but have never formalised AI governance. **Retroactive compliance** is the unique framing: "govern the AI you already have."

### Core Value Proposition
**75% of Geneva RE firms use AI** — the highest adoption rate in the portfolio. Yet GAIGI (the industry body) has no public AI governance guidance (gaigi.ch 404). FADP Art. 5(c) has been operative since September 1, 2023 — already 2.5 years. Most RE firms have been using AI without any governance framework: all retroactive exposure is already in violation.

### Primary Entry Point
**Shadow AI Audit (Decision 0)**: Map every AI tool used in property transactions, listings, tenant screening, and valuations before assessing FADP disclosure requirements and DPIA obligations.

### 7-Section Framework

1. **RE AI Tool Inventory** — Map every AI tool used (listings, valuations, tenant screening, market analysis)
2. **Shadow AI Exposure Assessment** — Surface retroactive FADP violations across all RE AI use
3. **Retroactive Compliance Audit** — "Govern the AI you already have" — FADP Art. 5(c) disclosure gap
4. **Property Valuation AI DPIA** — High-risk automated decision-making = mandatory DPIA
5. **Tenant Screening AI Assessment** — Creditworthiness = EU AI Act Annex III high-risk
6. **Listing Description AI Disclosure** — Purpose, functionality, data sources for AI-generated content
7. ** remediation Roadmap** — 30/60/90-day retroactive compliance sequence

### Urgency Framing
- **75% AI adoption HIGHEST portfolio** — widest governance gap
- **FADP Art. 5(c) already 2.5 years operative** — retroactive exposure already in violation
- **GAIGI institutional 404** — no industry guidance, no public AI governance standard
- **Tenant screening AI** = likely EU AI Act high-risk (Annex III)
- **Swissdamed July 1 ~13 WEEKS** — healthcare spillover
- **Andermatt May 26 ~8 WEEKS** — FDPIC conference = enforcement signal

### Unique Framing
**"Govern the AI you already have"** — unlike other verticals preparing for future deadlines, RE firms need to govern AI they've been using for months/years without any framework. This retroactive compliance angle is unique to RE in the portfolio.
