# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Consulting

## Strategic Positioning Angle
Geneva consulting firms face a dual pressure: AI is simultaneously a competitive threat (AI-native boutiques disrupting traditional delivery) and an internal operational challenge (consultants unsure how to use AI tools without quality loss). The advisor who helps Geneva consulting firms navigate AI adoption — both as a service offering and as an internal capability — occupies a defensible, two-sided positioning opportunity.

## Lead Magnet Concepts

### Option A: "AI Strategy Roadmap for Geneva Consulting Firms"
A structured advisory document helping Geneva management consulting and professional services firms: (1) develop an AI-enabled service offering, (2) build internal AI-assisted research and delivery capabilities, and (3) avoid AI adoption pitfalls that damage consulting quality and client trust.

**Format:** Executive strategy briefing (12-16 pages) + workshop proposal
**Distribution:** Managing Partners, Practice Leads, Business Development at Geneva consulting firms
**Value:** Two-sided positioning — positions advisor as AI strategy expert for consulting firms AND for consulting firms' clients

### Option B: "UN/IO Procurement Advisory — Getting Geneva Boutiques on the Approved Vendor List"
A practical guide for Geneva boutique consulting firms navigating UN/IO procurement rules: vendor registration, procurement framework compliance, and competitive positioning for Geneva-based IO contracts.

**Format:** Practical guide (10-12 pages) + Q&A session
**Distribution:** Managing Partners, Business Development at Geneva boutiques targeting UN/IO contracts
**Value:** Addresses a specific, high-value Geneva niche market; positions advisor as UN/IO procurement specialist

### Option C: "Geneva Energy Transition Consulting — Market Opportunity Assessment"
A market sizing and competitive positioning document for Geneva consulting firms considering energy transition advisory as a new practice area: market size, key buyers, regulatory drivers, competitive landscape.

**Format:** Market assessment report (15-20 pages) + strategy session
**Distribution:** Managing Partners, Practice Leads considering new practice areas
**Value:** Growth-oriented positioning; targets Geneva's natural advantage in commodity trading sustainability advisory

## Preferred Format (Initial)
Option A as primary — AI strategy for consulting firms is the most defensible two-sided positioning play: (1) AI advisory for Geneva consulting firms as clients; (2) AI advisory using the consulting firm's own methodology as proof of concept.

## Target CTA
"Schedule a 60-minute AI strategy session for your consulting firm" — targeted at Managing Partners and Practice Leads at Geneva consulting firms.
