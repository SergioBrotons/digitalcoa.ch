## Competitive Positioning Gaps

### Underserved / Differentiated Opportunities
1. **AI strategy for Geneva consulting firms** — No Geneva boutique positioned as AI strategy advisor specifically for consulting/professional services firms
2. **UN/IO procurement advisory for Geneva boutiques** — Complex procurement rules block small boutiques from IO contracts; advisory on navigating UN/IO procurement could unlock new market
3. **Energy transition + Geneva commodity trading consulting** — Unique Geneva vertical: sustainability strategy for commodity trading houses
4. **Healthcare regulatory strategy** — Geneva healthcare cluster (Swissmedic, HUG, biotech) underserved for regulatory strategy consulting
5. **Trade association management consulting** — Geneva's concentration of international trade associations (ICHA, FIA, WEF, WHO) as a niche client segment

## Competitive Threats
- Big Four bundling AI strategy with technology implementation
- AI-native consulting models (Harvey, EvenUp, etc.) disrupting traditional consulting delivery
- In-house consulting teams absorbing strategy work
- Freelance consultant platforms reducing mid-market consulting demand
