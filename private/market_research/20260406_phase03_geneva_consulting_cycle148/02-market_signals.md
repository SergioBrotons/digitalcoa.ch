# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva Consulting

## Active Signals (Q1-Q2 2026)

### Market Signals
- **Geneva consulting market growth:** Post-pandemic normalization; financial services consulting demand steady; public sector consulting driven by cantonal/federal digitization mandates
- **AI consulting surge:** 80% of Swiss organizations using AI tools in 2025 (up from 37% in 2024); creating demand for AI strategy, governance, and implementation consulting
- **International organization (IO) consulting:** UN/IO procurement (UNOG, WHO, WTO, ITU, UNCTAD) generates significant Geneva consulting demand; new procurement rules creating boutique advisory opportunities
- **Energy transition consulting:** Geneva-based energy trading firms, commodity houses, and cantonal energy companies driving demand for sustainability strategy, ESG integration, and net-zero transition advisory
- **Healthcare consulting:** Geneva's healthcare cluster (HUG, biotech startups) creating consulting demand for regulatory strategy, market access, and digital health
- **Trade association consulting:** Geneva hosts major international trade associations (ICHA, FIA, WEF, WHO); trade association management consulting is a niche Geneva vertical

### Technology Signals
- **AI-powered consulting tools:** Geneva consultants exploring ChatGPT Enterprise, Copilot, specialized consulting AI tools (Komo, Harvey, EvenUp)
- **Knowledge management:** Firm-level knowledge management systems gaining traction; AI-assisted research and synthesis becoming competitive differentiator
- **Proposal automation:** AI tools for RFP response generation, competitive intelligence, presentation design
- **Remote/hybrid delivery:** Post-pandemic client engagement model; Geneva consultants serving EU and international clients remotely

### Talent Signals
- **Consultant mobility:** Geneva consulting talent competing with Geneva financial institutions, NGOs, and international organizations
- **AI skills gap:** Senior consultants lacking AI tool proficiency; new hybrid roles emerging (consultant + AI specialist)
- **Geneva-specific hiring:** French-speaking Romandie consultants preferred for local market; English fluency required for international engagements
