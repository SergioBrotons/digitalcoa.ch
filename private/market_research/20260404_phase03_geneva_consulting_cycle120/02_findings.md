# 02 — NET-NEW Findings
**Phase:** Phase 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C120 | **Date:** 2026-04-04

## Research Question
What are the highest-value, differentiated consulting advisory opportunities in Geneva for C120, distinct from C119 and C118 angles?

---

## Finding 1: Post-CSRD Reporting Integration — Operationalizing Sustainability Data

### Signal
Many Swiss companies completed initial CSRD compliance setup in 2025-2026 but now face the challenge of integrating sustainability data into operational workflows. The "first report" phase is giving way to "operational integration" phase.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Companies need help integrating sustainability KPIs into business operations, not just reporting
- **Distinct angle:** Not CSRD compliance setup (C118) — this is post-adoption operationalization consulting
- **Revenue model:** Operating model design, data integration, process redesign
- **Geneva specificity:** Large Swiss corporates with EU market exposure are most advanced in CSRD but need integration support

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Beyond CSRD Compliance: Operationalizing Sustainability Data"
- Target ICP: CFOs, sustainability officers, operations executives at Swiss corporates

---

## Finding 2: Board Governance Advisory — ESG Oversight Requirements

### Signal
Institutional investors and proxy advisors are increasingly demanding evidence of board-level ESG oversight. Swiss companies are responding by formalizing board ESG committees or expanding audit committee mandates.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Board effectiveness reviews, ESG committee charter design, board training programs
- **Distinct angle:** Not ESG integration (C118) — this is governance design at board level, not operational implementation
- **Revenue model:** Governance framework design, board evaluation, ESG committee setup
- **Geneva specificity:** Geneva's corporate density (large-caps, multinationals) makes this particularly relevant

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Board ESG Oversight: What Swiss Boards Must Demonstrate"
- Target ICP: Board members, corporate secretaries, institutional investors, governance committees

## Finding 3: Change Management for AI Adoption — Organizational Readiness

### Signal
Swiss companies are deploying AI tools (LLMs, automation, analytics) but encountering organizational resistance and adoption failures. Change management consulting for AI is a growing demand.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Organizational readiness assessments, change management programs, AI adoption roadmaps
- **Distinct angle:** Not digital transformation (C118) — this is AI-specific change management with distinct organizational challenges
- **Revenue model:** Readiness assessments, change management programs, training design
- **Geneva specificity:** Geneva's professional services sector (banks, insurers, commodity traders) are actively deploying AI

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Why AI Deployments Fail: Organizational Readiness for Swiss Companies"
- Target ICP: CIOs, CHROs, transformation officers, executive committees

## Finding 4: Strategic Partnership Structuring — Non-EU Market Entry via Switzerland

### Signal
Non-EU companies (UK, US, Asia-Pacific) increasingly use Switzerland as a European gateway. Geneva-based advisory firms can structure strategic partnerships, joint ventures, and market entry strategies.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Market entry strategy, partnership structuring, regulatory interface for non-EU companies establishing Swiss presence
- **Distinct angle:** Not trade facilitation (C118) — this is strategic partnership and JV structuring, not customs/trade operations
- **Revenue model:** Strategy engagements, partnership structuring, ongoing advisory retainers
- **Geneva specificity:** Geneva's international ecosystem and neutral positioning make it the preferred Swiss entry point

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Switzerland as European Gateway: Strategic Market Entry for Non-EU Companies"
- Target ICP: Non-EU corporate development teams, family offices, venture capital

## Finding 5: Crisis Communications — Regulatory Investigation Support

### Signal
Swiss companies facing regulatory investigations (FINMA, COMCO, FCA equivalent) need structured crisis communications support — media strategy, stakeholder communication, and investigation response coordination.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Crisis communication planning, investigation response support, media strategy during enforcement actions
- **Distinct angle:** Not regulatory change communication (C119 proactive) — this is reactive crisis support during live investigations
- **Revenue model:** Crisis retainers, investigation support mandates, communications coaching
- **Geneva specificity:** Geneva's concentration of regulated entities (banks, commodity traders, pharmaceutical companies) face higher investigation risk

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Regulatory Investigation Response: Crisis Communications for Swiss Companies"
- Target ICP: GCs, CCOs, communications officers at regulated entities

## Finding 6: Market Entry Advisory — Non-EU Companies Establishing Swiss Presence

### Signal
Non-EU companies are establishing Swiss subsidiaries and operational presences to access European markets post-Brexit and amid EU regulatory complexity. Geneva is a preferred location for headquarters and commercial operations.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Subsidiary setup, employment structuring, regulatory licensing, banking relationships for non-EU companies
- **Distinct angle:** Not covered in prior cycles (distinct from strategic partnerships, trade facilitation, market entry from C118/C119)
- **Revenue model:** Project-based market entry mandates, ongoing advisory retainers
- **Geneva specificity:** Geneva's international schools, diplomatic community, and business infrastructure make it preferred for HQ placement

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Swiss Market Entry: What Non-EU Companies Must Know About Geneva"
- Target ICP: Corporate development teams, family offices, international expansion officers
