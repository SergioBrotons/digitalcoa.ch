## Target Profile (Cycle 2 baseline; Cycle 3 updates pending)

### Firm Characteristics
- **Headquarters:** Geneva (Rue du Rhône, La Ville, Champod, Acacias districts)
- **Size:** 2–20 employees; typically 2–5 advisors; some larger at 10–20
- **AUM range:** CHF 50M–2B (small boutique); some mid-tier at CHF 2–15B
- **Structure:** SARL, Sàrl, or small SA; often family-owned or founder-led
- **Clients served:** UHNW and HNW individuals, families, Swiss resident executives, some international
- **Regulatory:** FINMA-licensed or affiliated via SRO (VQF, FINIA); subject to Swiss AML/KYC obligations

### Primary Decision-Maker: Managing Director / Founder
- 45–65 years old
- Legal or economics background; may hold CFA, CIIA, or legal certification
- Sole final decision-maker or with 1–2 partners
- Digitally aware but not necessarily tech-native
- Time-poor; values efficiency and credibility
- Primary concern: client retention, regulatory compliance, AUM growth, succession planning

### Secondary: Operations / Compliance Lead
- 35–55 years old
- Handles day-to-day compliance, reporting, client onboarding
- More likely to engage with tools and process documentation
- Pain points: KYC refresh cycles, regulatory reporting, data handling
- Influences recommendations upward to Managing Director
