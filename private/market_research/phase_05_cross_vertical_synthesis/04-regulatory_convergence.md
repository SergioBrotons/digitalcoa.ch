## Regulatory Landscape Overview — All Verticals

### Universal Regulation: nFADP

**Status:** In force since 2023; enforcement accelerating

The Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP / LRPD) applies to all four verticals without exception. It is the single most universal regulatory hook for cross-vertical positioning.

**nFADP Articles by Relevance to DigitalCoa.ch Advisory:**

| Article | Content | Vertical Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 5(c) | Sensitive personal data categories | Healthcare (health data = highest sensitivity), Legal (client data), Finance (financial data), Consulting (client data) |
| Art. 8 | Lawfulness of processing | Universal |
| Art. 12 | Information obligations | Universal |
| Art. 14 | Right to access | Universal |
| Art. 26 | Data security obligations | Universal |
| Art. 29 | Data protection impact assessments | Universal for AI systems processing sensitive data |

**Key Insight:** nFADP is the universal entry point. Every vertical principal understands they have data protection obligations. nFADP compliance is the hook that opens the advisory conversation.

### Universal Standard: ISO 42001 (AI Management Systems)

**Status:** Published 2023; adoption growing; becoming procurement-relevant

ISO 42001 is the first international standard for AI management systems. It provides a certifiable framework for how organizations govern AI — not just what AI systems they use.

**Why ISO 42001 Matters Cross-Vertically:**
- It provides a framework that transcends industry-specific regulation
- It creates a defensible standard for AI governance that clients can point to
- It positions DigitalCoa.ch as an ISO 42001 implementation advisor — not just a nFADP compliance consultant
- It is increasingly being required in procurement (consulting firms, healthcare organizations)

## The Convergence Insight

### Shared Regulatory Logic
All four verticals face the same three-layer regulatory logic:

1. **Data Layer:** nFADP obligations for any personal/sensitive data processed
2. **AI System Layer:** Questions about what AI tools are being used, how, and whether they comply with data obligations
3. **Governance Layer:** How the organization structures decision-making, accountability, and oversight for AI systems

This three-layer logic is identical across all four verticals. DigitalCoa.ch's advisory framework can be built once and adapted per vertical — this is a significant operational efficiency.

### The nFADP-ISO 42001 Stack
The most powerful cross-vertical positioning is the combination of:
- **nFADP compliance** (universal, mandatory, understood by all principals)
- **ISO 42001 governance** (advanced, differentiating, becoming procurement-relevant)

This stack positions DigitalCoa.ch as the advisor that helps SMBs move from nFADP compliance (table stakes) to ISO 42001 AI governance excellence (competitive differentiation).
