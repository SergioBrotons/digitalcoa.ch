### 4. GPAI Self-Assessment Gap — Consulting Firm's Own Governance Before Client Advisory (NET-NEW C61 Framing Update)
**Source:** Synthesis from C58–C60 Phase 03 + April 1, 2026 Federal Council data

EU AI Act GPAI obligations have been in force since August 2, 2025. Geneva consulting firms using external AI models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) for client work — proposal drafting, market research, financial modeling, legal research — face their own GPAI obligations. Most have not addressed them.

**Implication (NET-NEW C61 — sharper framing):** The GPAI self-assessment gap is no longer just an internal ethics question. It is a regulatory compliance question. Any Geneva consulting firm using external AI for client deliverables is potentially within scope of EU AI Act GPAI obligations if the output is used in the EU market. The consulting firm that has documented its own GPAI compliance has: (1) a defensible position; (2) a living case study for client advisory; (3) a competitive differentiator vs. firms that have not addressed it.
