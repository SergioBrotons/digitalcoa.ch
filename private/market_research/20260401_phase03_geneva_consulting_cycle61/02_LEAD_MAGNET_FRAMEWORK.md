# Lead Magnet Framework — Phase 03 Geneva Consulting/SMB — Cycle 61

## Lead Magnet Topic
**"The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework" — Q2 2026 Edition**

## Core Question Being Asked
Is your consulting firm ready to advise clients on AI governance — or are you using AI on client work without documenting your own compliance?

## Lead Magnet Format
Decision framework / self-assessment guide — PDF-ready

## Angle
Geneva small consulting firms (5–50 employees) face a specific paradox: they are expected to advise clients on AI transformation and governance, but most have not addressed their own EU AI Act GPAI exposure. EU AI Act GPAI obligations have been in force since August 2, 2025 — and most consulting firms don't know they may already be in scope. This framework helps the consulting firm assess its own exposure before advising clients.

## Target Persona
- Geneva small consulting firm (strategy, management, finance, HR consulting)
- 5–50 employees
- Using AI tools for client deliverables (proposals, research, analysis, reports)
- Advising clients on AI transformation or digital strategy
- Primary fear: advising clients credibly while not having addressed own governance
- Secondary fear: missing the August 2, 2026 GPAI deadline

## Value Proposition
A two-track self-assessment: (1) does the EU AI Act apply to your firm? (2) what is your client advisory readiness?

1. Swiss-EU bilateral package not imminent — multi-framework burden continues (admin.ch April 1, 2026)

6. What to do this quarter (three ranked actions)
