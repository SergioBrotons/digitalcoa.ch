# 01 — ICP: DigitalCoa.ch — Geneva Executive Advisory Clients

## Target Segment: DigitalCoa.ch — Geneva Executives, Advisors, Family Offices

### Client Profile
- **Role:** C-suite executives, senior partners, UHNW principals, family office decision-makers
- **Sector:** Financial services, commodity trading, professional services, family businesses
- **Revenue/Assets:** CHF 50M+ revenue or CHF 10M+ investable assets
- **Location:** Geneva primary; expanding to EU, UK, US
- **Language:** English primary; French for Geneva Swiss; German for international

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|
| UHNW Principal | 50-70, multi-generational wealth | Strategic direction, succession, risk |
| CEO / Managing Director | Professional executive | Regulatory change, market entry, transformation |
| Family Office Leader | 40-60, investment + governance | Portfolio strategy, governance, succession |
| CFO / Finance Director | Senior finance executive | Capital allocation, financing, reporting |
| Board Member / Chairman | NED, experienced operator | Governance oversight, strategic guidance |

### Known Geneva ICP Examples
- Geneva UHNW families (Bach, Safra, Rothschild, De Pury families)
- Commodity trading executives (Vitol, Trafigura, Mercuria, arcadis)
- Geneva private bank leadership (Pictet, Lombard Odier, UBP)
- Swiss multinational corporate executives (Nestlé, Roche, Novartis Geneva offices)
- International organization leaders (UNOG, WHO, ITU senior staff)
- Family business principals (Geneva mid-market succession situations)

### Pain Points (C124 Update)
1. **International market expansion advisory** — Geneva executives increasingly making decisions outside Switzerland; need advisory for EU/US/UK market entry, investment, or expansion
2. **Thought leadership trust signal** — Geneva executives need to trust DigitalCoa.ch before engaging; own-platform thought leadership publishing signals credibility
3. **Brief call conversion friction** — briefing call offer exists but conversion optimization not systematic; qualification and follow-up gaps
4. **Multi-currency pricing friction** — CHF-only pricing creates friction for international clients; GBP/EUR/USD options reduce friction
5. **Proof point absence** — DigitalCoa.ch lacks published case studies; Geneva executives need social proof before engaging
6. **Multilingual access** — Geneva French-native executives prefer French; German-speaking executives expect DE option; current EN-only limits reach

### Buying Triggers
- Strategic decision requiring external perspective (market entry, succession, M&A)
- Referral from trusted peer (strongest trigger)
- Thought leadership encounter (Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch pathway)
- Geneva business event where DigitalCoa.ch is mentioned
- Direct search for Geneva strategic advisory

### Buying Barriers
- No established DigitalCoa.ch brand awareness in market
- No published case studies or proof points
- Pricing not visible (no published pricing)
- Geneva advisory market dominated by Big 4 and established boutiques
- Trust must be established before engagement

### Language / Cultural Fit
- English dominant for international business
- French essential for Geneva Swiss executives
- German useful for Zurich connections and German-speaking clients
- Geneva business culture: direct, discreet, relationship-based
- Restrained confidence: no hype, no empty promises
