## Key Regulatory Developments (C124)

### 1. FINMA Investment Advisory Scope
- Switzerland: investment advisory separate from strategic advisory
- Strategic advisory: not FINMA-regulated
- Investment advisory with specific recommendations: requires licensing
- DigitalCoa.ch scope: strategic advisory vs. investment advice — distinction matters
- GDPR/FADP: client data handling regardless of advisory type

### 2. Swiss Contract Law for Advisory Engagements
- SwissCode of Obligations (OR): advisory contract framework
- Engagement letter requirements: scope, fee, confidentiality, liability
- International clients: jurisdiction and governing law clauses
- Switzerland-EU: international arbitration vs. Swiss courts

### 3. Multi-Jurisdictional Client Engagements
- Geneva-based advisor + EU client: which jurisdiction applies
- Contractual choice of law: typically Swiss law acceptable internationally
- FATCA/CRS: financial account reporting for US/-EU clients
- Switzerland-US tax treaty: relevant for US persons with Swiss assets

## Regulatory Risk Summary
- **Low regulatory risk:** Strategic advisory (not investment advice) — no FINMA license required
- **Low regulatory risk:** GDPR/FADP compliance — standard data handling
- **Medium regulatory risk:** If engagement involves investment recommendations — FINMA licensing required
- **External risk:** International client jurisdictions — contract structure matters
