## Content Consumption Profile
- **Reading behavior:** Long-form, contemplative — not scroll-binge consumption
- **Device:** Desktop likely dominant (professional context)
- **Return visits:** Expected; content should reward repeat reading
- **Engagement model:** Newsletter subscription, direct contact intent

## Audience Pain Points (Hypotheses)
1. Information overload — too much content, not enough interpretation
2. Generic AI-generated content — hunger for authentic, expert-voice content
3. Disconnected data points — need synthesis and context
4. Trust scarcity — hard to find credible, independent analysis
5. Time poverty — high-level professionals have limited reading time

