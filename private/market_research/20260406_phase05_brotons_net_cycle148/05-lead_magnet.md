# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Brotons.net Audience Growth

## Brotons.net's Lead Capture Model
Current model: Newsletter subscription → direct contact intent
Goal: Grow qualified readership → increase return visits → increase newsletter/contact intent

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "The European Strategy Weekly Brief"
**Format:** Weekly email (5-minute read)
**Target:** Existing Brotons.net audience + new subscribers
**Value:** Condensed strategic intelligence — interpretation of the week's key developments
**Lead capture:** Already subscribed (this IS the lead magnet)
**Revenue path:** None directly — audience-building only
**Note:** This is Brotons.net's existing implicit model; formalize it

### Option B: "The Independent European Strategy Report" (Annual)
**Format:** 30-50 page annual report
**Target:** Senior executives, advisors, policy professionals
**Value:** Comprehensive review of European strategic landscape
**Lead capture:** Email + professional profile
**Revenue path:** Warm — leads to advisory conversations
**Note:** High effort but positions Brotons.net as the authority

### Option C: "Brotons.net Reader Community" (Newsletter Extension)
**Format:** Exclusive weekly deep-dive for subscribers
**Target:** Most engaged Brotons.net readers
**Value:** Deeper analysis, early access, direct Q&A access
**Lead capture:** Opt-in for existing subscribers
**Revenue path:** Deepens trust → warm advisory leads
**Note:** Low cost, high engagement, natural progression

### Option D: "The European Strategy Reading List" (Curated)
**Format:** Monthly curated reading list (5-8 items with annotations)
**Target:** Knowledge workers, analysts, executives
**Value:** Quality filter on information overload
**Lead capture:** Email + professional role
**Revenue path:** Indirect — builds authority and loyalty
**Note:** Low effort, consistent touchpoint

## Lead Magnet Recommendation

**Primary:** Option A/C hybrid — Formalize weekly brief + add exclusive subscriber tier
**Secondary:** Option D (Reading List) — monthly touchpoint, easy to produce
**Long-term:** Option B (Annual Report) — high authority, major project

## Subscription Conversion Path
```
Content Discovery → Subscribe to Newsletter → Weekly Brief → 
Reader Community (opt-in) → Direct Contact Inquiry → Advisory Conversation
```
