## Content Performance Signals

### Content Types That Work in Thought Leadership Space
1. **Interpretive synthesis** — taking complex events and providing expert interpretation
2. **Data-driven analysis** — original research or unique data interpretation
3. **Contrarian takes** — well-argued positions that challenge conventional wisdom
4. **Long-form deep dives** — 2,000-5,000 word pieces that reward reading
5. **Series/formats** — consistent branded formats (e.g., weekly brief)

### Content Types That Underperform
- News aggregation without interpretation
- SEO-padding thin content
- Generic "top 10" listicles
- AI-generated-feeling content
- Content without clear point of view
