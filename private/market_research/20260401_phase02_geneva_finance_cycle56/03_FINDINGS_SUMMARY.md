# Phase 02 C56 — Findings Summary: Geneva Finance/Wealth Management

## Market Segment
Geneva boutique wealth managers (CHF 50M–500M AUM)
Geneva private banking: ~80 institutions, concentrated in 10–200 employee range
Primary target: WM boutiques with AI adoption underway, no dedicated compliance tech team

## Belief-Action Gap (Geneva WM)
- 35% of Swiss WM firms using AI for client reporting automation (2025)
- ~15% have documented AI governance framework (estimated from C55–C56 data)
- **Gap: ~20 percentage points — acute for boutique WMs**

**Special tension:** AML AI (requires maximum data) vs. FADP (requires data minimization) — the most operationally acute governance gap identified across all 6 sectors.

---

## Competitive Landscape (Geneva WM)
- **Swiss-Noxtua:** Sovereign AI (WM-appropriate, FINMA-aligned)
- **Julius Baer / UBS AI platforms:** Enterprise-scale, inaccessible to boutiques
- **Emerging robo-advisory tools:** Cost-efficient but governance unclear
- **Vendor gap:** Boutique WM AI governance advisory with FINMA + AML + FADP combination is open white space
