## Signal Architecture

These signals are distinct from C314 signals (cross-border applicability inquiry, logging conflict discovery, hallucination sanctions visibility, privilege waiver awareness, cross-matter contamination discovery, three-regime reconciliation failure). Six signal categories:

1. Member State authority (MSA) designations + first formal notices — Swiss WBF/FMEC + EU MSAs (BNetzA, CNIL/DGCCRF, AgID, AESIA, AP)
2. First named-individual deployer action — avocat named in formal MSA notice

4. Article 12 logging audit requests — MSA requests to legal AI vendors
5. OaF / FSA post-deadline guidance — Article 26 named-sub-principal in bar enforcement
