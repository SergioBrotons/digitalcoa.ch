    *   Opportunity: A Geneva-based advisory service at the intersection of WIPO standards and national IP laws could cater to emerging AI innovators.

5.  **Humanitarian tech procurement reform:**

    *   Opportunity: This opens new pathways for Swiss startups to supply technology solutions to UN/IO systems, requiring support in navigating these new procurement channels.

6.  **Geneva AI safety governance:**
    *   Observation: An emerging AI safety research community is forming in Geneva, with ties to EPFL and WIPO AI policy discussions.
    *   Opportunity: Advisory services can bridge the gap between AI technical standards and the Geneva diplomatic process, focusing on AI safety governance.
