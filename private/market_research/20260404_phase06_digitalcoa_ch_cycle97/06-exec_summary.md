# 06 — Executive Summary: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch

## Research Question
How should DigitalCoa.ch leverage C97 Phase 01-05 findings to strengthen its service positioning and conversion?

## Key Findings (C97 — NET-NEW)

### Finding 1: 2026 regulatory cascade creates a specific service positioning opportunity for DigitalCoa.ch
The 6 major FINMA/legislative changes in 2026 create a clear, named service positioning: **"FINMA 2026 Regulatory Governance."** No Geneva advisory firm is offering this specifically.

**Implication:** DigitalCoa.ch should create a dedicated FINMA 2026 Governance landing page or service module, positioning Governance Architecture as the implementation vehicle for regulatory compliance.

### Finding 2: Brotons.net FINMA 2026 Radar series is the conversion engine
Brotons.net's "FINMA 2026 Radar" content series will drive qualified traffic to DigitalCoa.ch. Each article should have explicit service CTAs linking to DigitalCoa.ch Governance Architecture page.

**Implication:** Content-to-service bridge must be built intentionally. Every article needs a CTA. Without this, traffic remains unconverted.

### Finding 3: French-language service page is a critical gap
DigitalCoa.ch appears to be primarily English. Geneva's French-speaking executive community will not engage deeply with English-language service pages.

**Implication:** At minimum, a French-language service overview or landing page must be created. This requires owner approval and likely translation.

### Finding 4: Decision Diagnostic maps directly to "regulatory readiness assessment" need
The Decision Diagnostic (2-5 days) is the natural entry point for executives who need quick clarity on whether and how the 2026 regulatory changes affect them.

**Implication:** Reframe Decision Diagnostic as "2026 Regulatory Readiness Diagnostic" — creates urgency and relevance. Requires owner approval for content updates.

### Finding 5: Four ICP segments from C97 research map to DigitalCoa.ch services
- General Counsel → Governance Architecture (regulatory compliance)
- CIO/Head of Technology → Governance Architecture (AI/cyber/resilience)
- Managing Partner/CEO → Decision Diagnostic → Governance
- COO/Operations (startups/NGOs) → Decision Diagnostic → modules

**Implication:** Service page content should speak directly to each of these 4 segments with specific regulatory change language.

### Finding 6: Competitive positioning is defensible but requires content velocity
DigitalCoa.ch's position ("executive-grade, rapid, framework-driven, bilingual, independent") is unique. But first-mover content advantage on FINMA 2026 is available for only 4-8 weeks.

**Implication:** Brotons.net must publish the FINMA 2026 Radar series immediately; DigitalCoa.ch service pages must be ready to receive the traffic.
