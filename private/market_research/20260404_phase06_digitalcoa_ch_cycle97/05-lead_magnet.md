### Service Page Gaps Identified (C97)

- **What:** A specific "Governance Architecture" module focused on FINMA 2026 regulatory cascade
- **Why:** Executives searching for "FINMA 2026" or "AMLA compliance" need to find DigitalCoa.ch
- **Action:** Create FINMA 2026 landing page or service module; link from Brotons.net FINMA 2026 Radar articles

#### Gap 2: French-Language Service Entry Point
- **What:** DigitalCoa.ch appears to be primarily English
- **Why:** Geneva's French-speaking executives need French-language entry point
- **Action:** Create French-language service overview page or translate existing service descriptions

#### Gap 3: Regulatory Readiness Assessment (Decision Diagnostic framed for 2026)
- **What:** A specific Decision Diagnostic framed around 2026 regulatory changes
- **Why:** Creates urgency and relevance for prospects unsure if they need full Governance Architecture
- **Action:** Update Decision Diagnostic page to reference FINMA 2026 changes and self-assessment angle
