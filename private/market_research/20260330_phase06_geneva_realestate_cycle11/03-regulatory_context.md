- Institutional/government focus
- SMB gap intact — not serving small RE agencies

### nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection — operative Sep 2023)
- Art. 5(c): active — AI processing of client personal data (property transactions, tenant information, buyer/seller profiles) requires lawful basis
- Art. 22: DPIA requirement for high-risk processing

### EU AI Act GPAI (Operative since Aug 2, 2025)
- ~8.5 months already live
- Transparency, copyright, incident documentation obligations active
- EU market nexus: applies to RE agencies with EU clients, EU property marketing

### EU AI Act High-Risk (August 2, 2026 — ~16 months)
- If placing AI on EU market, operating EU-intended output, or acting as EU importer/distributor
