## Core Promise

## Problem Statement (3 layers)

### Layer 1: The Productivity Opportunity Is Real — And Time-Sensitive
Deloitte: 69% of senior executives expect AI to transform their industry. 51% believe there will be a competitive shakeout. Small consulting firms that don't capture GenAI efficiency now will face clients who expect the same productivity gains — and competitors who deliver them.

### Layer 2: GPAI August 2 Is Your Internal Deadline AND Your Client Advisory Product
GPAI (EU AI Act General-Purpose AI) implementation on August 2, 2026 creates a dual obligation:
- Your firm needs to be GPAI-ready (internal)
- Your clients will come to you for GPAI guidance (external advisory)

The two-track readiness product positions the consulting firm as both GPAI-compliant and GPAI-advisory-ready.

### Layer 3: Agentic AI Is the Next Wave — You Need to Be Ahead of Your Clients
While most SMBs are still evaluating GenAI chatbots, agentic AI systems are moving from decision-support to autonomous action. Consulting firms that understand agentic AI governance now will be the trusted advisors when clients face that next wave.
