## NET-NEW C59 Findings

### 1. Deloitte: 69% Foresee AI Transformation, 51% See Industry Shakeout
- **What:** 69% of senior executives expect AI transformation of operations. 51% of digitally leading firms believe there will be a shakeout, with traditional players severely challenged. 52% expect increased commoditisation by 2028.
- **Source:** Deloitte/ThoughtLab study
- **Why it matters for consulting:** The competitive pressure driving SMB AI adoption is validated by major research. The consulting firm that frames AI governance as competitive protection — not just compliance — has a stronger value proposition.

### 2. PWC: GenAI Productivity Narrative Validated
- **What:** Early GenAI pilots showing significant advisor time savings: automated documentation + AI research assistants. Productivity narrative (labor shortage + efficiency) is confirmed at enterprise level and applicable to consulting/SMB.
- **Source:** PWC Switzerland
- **Why it matters for consulting:** Lead with productivity, close with governance. The SMB client who understands the efficiency opportunity will listen. The governance framing then protects them.

### 3. Agentic AI: From Decision-Support to Orchestrated Action
- **What:** Agentic AI systems executing complex tasks autonomously within defined parameters. Next evolution beyond GenAI chatbots and assistants.
- **Source:** PWC Switzerland
- **Why it matters for consulting:** Consulting firms advising on AI strategy must be ahead of the agentic AI curve. SMB clients asking about AI are already behind the leading edge.
