# Phase 06 Geneva Real Estate — ICP (Cycle 3)
## Target Vertical: Small Real Estate Agencies (2–10 Agents) + Property Managers, Geneva

## ICP Definition

### Firmographic
- **Size:** 2–10 agents; micro-boutique to small agency
- **Location:** Geneva (Rue du Rhône corridor, La Ville, Grottes, Praille-Acacias); property management in La Praille, Les Acacias
- **Structure:** Independent agencies; property managers (residential, commercial, mixed); RE agencies affiliated with banks or insurance
- **Revenue model:** Commission on transactions (sale/rental); property management fees (AUM-equivalent for managed properties)

### Role & Decision-Making
- **Primary decision-maker:** Agency owner / Managing Director
- **Secondary:** Senior agent (if 5+ agents)
- **Decision dynamic:** Owner-driven; informal; fast
- **Risk tolerance:** Low for transaction risk; moderate for technology adoption

### Technology Posture
- **Current state:** MLS systems (Immopac, Swiss Realty, On不相); CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, custom); AI use increasingly ad hoc (listing description generation, photo enhancement, chatbot, pricing analytics)
- **AI exposure:** 67% AI adoption rate — HIGHEST vertical across all 5 verticals
- **Technology trust:** Moderate; adoption driven by efficiency gains, not regulatory fear
- **IT support:** External IT vendor or none; owner manages

### Regulatory Stress Points
1. **GAIGI operative** — ~3–5 months (Fall 2025 launch); institutional legitimacy; SMB awareness still low
2. **nFADP** — operative since September 2023; tenant/buyer data is personal data; AI processing = Art. 5(c) + DPIA Art. 22
3. **EU AI Act** — August 2, 2026 high-risk; RE agencies with EU market nexus face dual nFADP + EU AI Act
4. **Retroactive compliance** — no hard regulatory deadline; compliance driven by tenant/buyer rights awareness and liability protection

### Buying Psychology
- **Trigger:** Liability event (tenant data breach, AI pricing error) or peer adoption (67% already using AI)
- **Language:** French (Geneva primary); bilingual EN/FR for international clients
- **Trust marker:** GAIGI institutional reference; Swiss-specific nFADP/FDPIC reference
- **Objection:** "Our MLS/CRM vendor handles data" — AI tools used beyond MLS/CRM are the practice's responsibility
- **Sales cycle:** 4–10 weeks; Geneva RE community is referral-based; real estate associations are key network
- **Budget:** CHF 1,500–5,000 for initial AI governance framework; retainer for ongoing
