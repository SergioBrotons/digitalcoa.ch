### SECTION 4: AMLA + nFADP — The Most Urgent Intersection (CYCLE 6 SHARPENED)
**Revised AMLA ~2-3 months away: Enhanced KYC/AML obligations hit soonest**

Key questions — if AI is used in any of these:
- Client onboarding or KYC verification → AMLA + nFADP dual obligations (DPIA required)
- Ongoing transaction monitoring → AMLA + nFADP dual obligations
- Beneficial ownership verification → AMLA + nFADP dual obligations
- Client risk scoring or stratification → AMLA + nFADP dual obligations

**Cycle 6 added urgency:** AMLA is no longer "upcoming." It is ~8-12 weeks away. Any WM using AI in KYC/AML workflows should have a DPIA and documented AMLAI controls in place now — before implementation.

---

### SECTION 5: CARF/CRS 2.0 + LETA — The Data Governance Layer
**CARF/CRS 2.0 — ALREADY OPERATIVE (3 months)**

AI systems processing CARF-reportable financial account data:
- Must have documented data accuracy controls (AI hallucination risk = CARF accuracy risk)
- Must have audit trail for regulatory inspection
- Must ensure data completeness and integrity

**LETA H2 2026 (~3-6 months away) — Cycle 6 sharpening:**

Geneva WMs with international client bases: LETA governs cross-border information requests.
AI systems processing client data potentially subject to LETA procedures need:
- Legal hold and data retention protocols
- Cross-border data transfer documentation
- Client consent management under nFADP Art. 8–13

---

### SECTION 6: EU AI Act — The August 2, 2026 Deadline
**For Geneva WMs with EU market nexus**

If your WM uses AI systems with EU-based data centers, EU-intended outputs, or EU client-facing AI:
- High-risk obligations operative August 2, 2026 (~15 months)
- Transparency: AI-generated output must be disclosed
- Human oversight: meaningful human control over AI-driven decisions
- Non-discrimination: AI-driven portfolio allocation must not discriminate

**Geneva-specific exposure (Cycle 6):** Most Geneva boutique WMs serve UHNWI clients with EU connections. The probability of EU AI Act nexus is structurally high. Assume EU nexus unless explicitly assessed otherwise.
