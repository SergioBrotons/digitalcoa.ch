## Regulatory Context — Market Signal Overlay

The following regulatory developments create urgency signals in the market:

1. **FDPIC May 8 2025:** Explicit confirmation that nFADP applies directly to AI-supported data processing. This is the market signal that nFADP enforcement is AI-specific, not just general data protection.

2. **EU AI Act GPAI Rules (August 2025):** Geneva consulting firms with EU clients will face these requirements. Cross-border complexity creates advisory demand.

3. **CoE AI Convention (March 2025):** Switzerland signed. Legal amendments to enable ratification expected by end of 2026. Framework for 2027+.

4. **nFADP enforcement reality:** PFPDT has enforcement capacity. Fines up to CHF 250,000 for individuals. "As soon as possible" breach notification requirement.
