## Swiss Consulting Market — Context (Preliminary)

### Market Size & Scope
- Switzerland consulting market: estimated CHF 15–20B annually
- Geneva: significant cluster of SMEs and multinational HQs = consulting demand
- Small consulting firms (2–15 people) represent majority of consulting landscape
- Competition: large strategy consultancies (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) at top; small boutiques at bottom

### Key Trends (2025–2026) (Hypotheses — Require Validation)

**1. Digital Transformation Consulting Demand**
- SMEs seeking digital transformation guidance = opportunity for consulting firms
- AI adoption: small consultancies exploring AI tools for research, slide decks, reports
- Remote/hybrid work: consulting delivery models changing

**2. Regulatory Context for Swiss Consulting**
- Non-regulated sector (unlike finance or legal): fewer compliance barriers
- However: data protection (nFADP) applies to client data processing
- Some consulting niches (financial consulting, HR consulting) may have regulatory dimensions

**3. Geneva-Specific Consulting Ecosystem**
- Strong SME culture in Geneva
- Multinational HQs create demand for strategy and management consulting
- CCIG (Chambre du commerce) networking
- Startup ecosystem: Geneva has active startups (Fongit, Genilem) creating consulting demand

**4. AI Impact on Consulting**
- AI tools (research, writing, analysis) increasingly available to small consultants
- Efficiency gains from AI adoption; competitive pressure to adopt
- Client expectations shifting toward faster turnaround
