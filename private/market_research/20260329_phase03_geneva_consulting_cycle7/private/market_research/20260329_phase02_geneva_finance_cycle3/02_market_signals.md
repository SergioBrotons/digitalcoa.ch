## Geneva WM Market Context (Cycle 2 baseline)

### Structural Characteristics
- Rue du Rhône, La Ville, Champod, Acacias districts — traditional Geneva finance
- Many micro-boutiques (2–5 people, SARL/Sàrl) registered via FINMA/SRO
- FINMA license registry is the authoritative count source
- Key differentiator: personal relationships + discretion; digital tool adoption is culturally sensitive

### Regulatory Pressure Points
1. **FINMA Guidance 08/2024** — AI vendor due diligence, governance frameworks
2. **nFADP Art. 5(c)** — Legitimate interest basis for AI processing of client financial data
3. **nFADP Art. 26** — Automated decision-making disclosure obligations
4. **SRO compliance** — VQF, FINIA; AML/KYC ongoing
5. **AEOI/FATCA** — Annual reporting cycles

### Buying Triggers
- FINMA inspection or supervisory communication about AI
- Client data breach or near-miss
- New hire (compliance officer, operations lead) who surfaces AI governance gaps
- Industry event or peer discussion about AI tool adoption
- Succession planning triggering data/infrastructure review
