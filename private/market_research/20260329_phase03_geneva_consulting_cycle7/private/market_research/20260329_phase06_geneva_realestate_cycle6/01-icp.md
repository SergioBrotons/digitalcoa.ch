# Phase 06 Geneva Real Estate — ICP (Cycle 6)
## Target Vertical: Small Real Estate Agencies (2–10 Agents), Geneva

## ICP Definition
- **Size:** 2–10 agents; small boutique agencies
- **Location:** Geneva (Rive Gauche, Rive Droite, La Praille)
- **Structure:** Residential sales, rental management, commercial RE; property managers
- **Revenue context:** Commission-based; transactional; price-sensitive on compliance costs

### Regulatory Stress Points
1. **GAIGI operative Fall 2025 (~1-5 months)** — institutional framework live; category awareness increasing
2. **67% AI adoption — highest vertical** — adoption/governance gap widest in RE
3. **nFADP** — AI processing of client personal data = Art. 5(c) + DPIA (Art. 22)
4. **Retroactive compliance** — most RE agencies have been non-compliant for months/years
5. **EU AI Act** — Aug 2, 2026 for agencies with EU market nexus

### Competitive Landscape
- **No boutique RE AI governance competitor confirmed** — Phase 06 had fewer cycles run; market white space confirmed
- **Technology providers (non-competitive):** Property portals, CRM tools, AI listing tools
