## Key Findings

### 1. Addressable Market
~600 Geneva consulting firms in the 2–15 employee range (Geneva Canton registry, Cycle 1 baseline). Sub-segments: strategy/management (~35%), IT consulting (~25%), HR/organisational (~20%), ESG/sustainability (~15%), specialist (~5%).

### 2. The nFADP AI Problem Is Immediate
Every small Geneva consulting firm using ChatGPT, Claude, or similar AI tools is likely operating without:
- A signed DPA with the AI vendor
- A documented legal basis for processing client data via AI
- A 72-hour breach notification procedure

This is a live, concrete exposure. PFPDT enforcement is active. **This is the entry point.**

### 3. No Boutique Competitor in This Space
Across Phase 01–05 research cycles, no Geneva or Swiss boutique advisory firm has explicit AI governance positioning for small professional practices. First-mover window is open across all five verticals.

### 4. Bilingual FR+EN Is Mandatory
Geneva's consulting elite operates in French and English. FR-first for local reach; EN for international org clients and LinkedIn.
