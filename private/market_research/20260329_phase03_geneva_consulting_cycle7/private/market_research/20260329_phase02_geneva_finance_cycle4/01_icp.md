## ICP: Geneva Boutique Wealth Manager / Independent Asset Manager

### Vertical Definition
Small to mid-sized wealth management firms and independent asset managers based in Geneva, typically serving high-net-worth individuals (HNWI) and families. Focus on investment advisory, portfolio management, and estate planning services. Uses AI tools for portfolio analysis, client communication, risk modeling, or administrative automation.

### Firm Profile

| Attribute | Typical Profile |
|---|---|
| **Employees** | 2–20 |
| **AUM** | CHF 100M–2B+ |
| **Client type** | HNWI, UHNWI, family offices |
| **Geneva presence** | Rue du Rhône, Cours de Rive, La Place, La Praille |
| **Technology stack** | Mix of legacy (Bloomberg, MS Office) + modern AI SaaS |
| **AI adoption** | 91% using external AI SaaS tools (FINMA 08/2024 signal) |
| **Regulatory framework** | FINMA Circ. 2018/3 (outsourcing), FINMA 08/2024 (AI), nFADP |
| **Key decision-maker** | Managing Partner, CEO, CIO |
| **Pain points** | Client data sensitivity, nFADP obligations, AI vendor due diligence, regulatory uncertainty |

### Addressable Firm Types

1. **Boutique WM firms** — CHF 100M–500M AUM, 2–15 employees, independent
2. **Family offices** — single-family or multi-family, often 2–10 employees
3. **Independent asset managers (IAMs)** — affiliated with Swiss Bankers Association or independent
4. **Wealth planning advisors** — estate planning, tax advisory, 2–10 employees

### ICP Pain Points (from Cycle 3)

1. **nFADP Art. 5(c)** — automated decision-making with legal effect on clients
2. **FINMA 08/2024** — AI-specific supervisory expectations becoming operative
3. **Vendor due diligence** — ensuring AI SaaS vendors meet Swiss regulatory standards
4. **Client data confidentiality** — AI tools processing sensitive financial/wealth data
5. **DPIA obligation** — data protection impact assessment for AI-based profiling
6. **GDPR dual compliance** — clients with EU assets or residence
7. **Health data** — wealth management clients with health-related insurance products
