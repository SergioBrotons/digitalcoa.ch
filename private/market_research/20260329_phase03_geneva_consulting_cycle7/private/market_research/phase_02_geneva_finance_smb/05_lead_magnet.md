## Format Rationale

**Format:** Downloadable PDF checklist + accompanying brief (1-page executive summary)
**Length:** 10 assessment points; 2–3 pages total
**Investment level:** Low (impulse download)
**Conversion goal:** Briefing call request, consultation inquiry, newsletter signup

### Why This Format Works
- Boutique wealth managers are skeptical of long reports; a checklist is actionable and low-commitment
- Self-assessment creates awareness of gaps without requiring immediate external help
- Scoring creates natural conversation entry point: "I scored 5/10 — what should I focus on?"
- Swiss professional culture values structure and thoroughness; checklist format aligns

### Assessment Points (10 items)

**Section A: AI Governance (4 points)**

1. **AI Decision-Making Transparency**
   Do you know which AI tools are making or influencing decisions about your clients?
   - Score: 0 (No visibility) to 3 (Documented AI inventory)

2. **Vendor Accountability**
   Have you documented the AI tools used by third-party vendors in your client service workflow?
   - Score: 0 (No) to 3 (Yes, with contracts and due diligence files)

3. **AI Risk Assessment**
   Have you conducted a formal AI risk assessment for your firm?
   - Score: 0 (Never) to 3 (Yes, reviewed annually)

4. **Human Oversight Mechanism**
   Can you demonstrate that a human reviews AI-generated outputs before client delivery?
   - Score: 0 (No process) to 3 (Formal review protocol documented)

**Section B: Data Compliance (4 points)**

5. **Client Data Inventory**
   Do you maintain a current inventory of where client data lives (systems, vendors, jurisdictions)?
   - Score: 0 (No) to 3 (Live inventory, reviewed quarterly)

6. **Cross-Border Data Transfer Compliance**
   Are your AI vendors and cloud providers assessed against nFADP cross-border transfer rules?
   - Score: 0 (No assessment) to 3 (Formal assessment documented)

7. **KYC/AML AI Tool Compliance**
   Are your AI-powered KYC or AML tools documented and approved by your SRO?
   - Score: 0 (Don't know) to 3 (Yes, SRO-approved and documented)

8. **Data Breach Response Plan**
   Do you have a tested response plan for an AI-related or vendor-related data breach?
   - Score: 0 (No plan) to 3 (Tested plan, staff trained)

**Section C: Strategic Readiness (2 points)**

9. **AI Adoption Roadmap**
   Does your firm have a documented (even informal) plan for AI adoption over the next 24 months?
   - Score: 0 (No plan) to 3 (Yes, board-reviewed)

10. **Staff AI Literacy**
    Can your compliance and operations team articulate the compliance risks of the AI tools you currently use?
    - Score: 0 (No) to 3 (Yes, trained annually)

### Scoring Guide
- **0–12: Foundation Building** — Your firm needs to establish AI governance basics before adoption
- **13–20: Developing** — You have some foundations; focus on documentation and compliance alignment
- **21–26: Advanced** — Strong position; focus on strategic roadmap and continuous improvement
- **27–30: Leader** — You're ahead of most boutiques; consider sharing learnings or advising peers
