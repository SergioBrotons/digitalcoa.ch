### Section 5: The FINMA AI Guidance Baseline (December 2024)

FINMA's December 2024 guidance establishes four pillars for AI governance in supervised entities:

1. **Governance:** Board-level accountability for AI systems; documented AI inventory
2. **Risk Management:** nFADP DPIA for all AI processing personal data; ongoing monitoring
3. **Transparency:** Client disclosure of AI-assisted decisions; explainability where feasible
4. **Non-Discrimination:** Testing for algorithmic bias; fairness audit procedures

**Reality check:** If your firm has not addressed these four pillars in any structured way, you are behind FINMA's baseline expectation.
