# 04 — Regulatory Context: Phase 01 Geneva Legal (C118)

## Active Regulatory Themes (Geneva Legal Advisory, April 2026)

### Theme 1: DORA Implementation (Swiss transposition)
- **Status:** Federal Council bill submitted; Parliament reviewing
- **Timeline:** Implementation 2026-2027; references EU DORA technical standards
- **Impact:** All Swiss financial institutions with EU counterparties or operations
- **Key documents:** Federal Council DORA bill, FINMA draft circular, EIOPA guidelines
- **Advisory angle:** Gap analysis, implementation roadmap, board-level briefing

### Theme 2: FADP Cross-Border Transfer Updates
- **Status:** FDPIC guidance published 2025-2026; enforcement increasing
- **New risk:** AI model training data transfers to US/UK/India
- **Key documents:** FADP (revised), FDPIC guidance on adequacy and SCCs
- **Advisory angle:** Transfer impact assessments, AI training data FADP compliance

### Theme 3: FATF Mutual Evaluation Follow-Through
- **Status:** Evaluation published March 2026; FINMA/FEDPOL implementing recommendations
- **Key focus areas:** Beneficial ownership registries, AML for real estate, crypto asset exposure
- **Key documents:** FATF mutual evaluation report Switzerland, FINMA AML guidance
- **Advisory angle:** UBO compliance remediation, AML framework upgrade

### Theme 4: ComCo Digital Platform Enforcement
- **Status:** Active investigation stream; Federal Administrative Court rulings
- **Key documents:** Cartel Act (Art. 7, 12), digital platform guidance
- **Advisory angle:** Platform compliance audits, data portability mandates, interoperability

### Theme 5: Geneva Arbitration Developments
- **Status:** CAS processing sports disputes; ICC arbitration up; WIPO IP arbitration steady
- **Advisory angle:** Geneva-seated arbitration evidence management, award enforcement, treaty claims

### Theme 6: Swiss Sanctions / SECO Compliance
- **Status:** Ongoing; Russia/Crimea/Snake Island sanctions; North Korea, Iran
- **New:** CARB (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) compliance — sanctions adjacent
- **Advisory angle:** Compliance audits, training, transaction review
