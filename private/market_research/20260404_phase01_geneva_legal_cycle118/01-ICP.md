# 01 — Ideal Client Profile: Phase 01 Geneva Legal (C118)

## ICP: Geneva Legal Operations + Regulatory Intelligence Advisory

### Primary ICP
**Target:** In-house legal departments at Geneva-headquartered financial institutions (private banks, commodity traders, asset managers, insurance companies)

**Profile:**
- 50-500 employees; legal team 3-30 lawyers
- Regulatory exposure: FINMA, SECO, sanctions, CRS/FATCA, DORA
- Key pain: regulatory change velocity outpacing internal capacity
- Decision-maker: General Counsel / Head of Legal Operations
- Geography: Geneva canton; some Lausanne, Zurich references
- Language: French primary; English fluent
- Buying signal: regulatory deadline (DORA Jan 2025, DORA implementation Swiss law 2026, CARB compliance)

### Secondary ICP
**Target:** Geneva law firms (mid-size, 20-100 lawyers) seeking specialized regulatory support

**Profile:**
- Practice areas: financial services regulatory, sanctions/compliance, data protection, digital
- Pain: capacity overflow on technical regulatory mandates (DORA, FADP-AI, CARB)
- Decision-maker: Managing Partner / Practice Group Head
- Buying signal: client demand for specialized expertise they don't have in-house

### Tertiary ICP
**Target:** Commodity trading firms with Geneva presence

**Profile:**
- Russian/Eastern European trade exposure; sanctions compliance load
- In-house legal + compliance teams stretched
- Geneva-based; English + French + Russian language mix
- Buying signal: SECO audit, sanctions review, new trade corridor advisory

## Non-ICP (Avoid)
- Small Geneva law firms (< 10 lawyers) — too small, too transactional
- Retail-facing consumer businesses — wrong regulatory surface
- Law firms seeking external litigation capacity — not the model
