# 05 — Lead Magnet Concepts: Phase 01 Geneva Legal (C118)

## Lead Magnet Strategy

### Primary Lead Magnet: "The Geneva Regulatory Intelligence Briefing"
**Format:** Monthly 2-page briefing; bilingual EN/FR
**Distribution:** LinkedIn newsletter + direct email to GC contacts
**Content:** One regulatory theme per issue; Geneva-specific analysis; practical implications; recommended actions
**CTA:** "Subscribe — Monthly Geneva Regulatory Intelligence" → Calendly discovery call

### Secondary Lead Magnet: "DORA Readiness Assessment for Swiss Financial Institutions"
**Format:** 10-point self-assessment checklist + 30-min recorded briefing
**Target:** C-suite, legal operations, compliance officers
**CTA:** Download in exchange for email → automated nurture sequence

### Tertiary Lead Magnet: "Geneva Arbitration Handbook" (long-form)
**Format:** 20-page guide; Geneva-seated international arbitration essentials
**Target:** In-house counsel, private equity GCs
**CTA:** PDF download → LinkedIn DM follow-up

### Quarterly Content Piece: "FATF Evaluation — What It Means for Geneva"
**Format:** Webinar recording + slide deck
**Timing:** Q2 2026 (post-FATF report publication)
**CTA:** Registration → live Q&A with founder
