# 04 — Regulatory Context: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C118)

## Advisory Business Context

### Topic 1: Client Qualification Framework
- **Status:** Develop explicit ICP (Ideal Client Profile) and qualify against it
- **Key questions:** Decision-maker? Specific problem? Budget awareness? Timeline urgency?
- **Advisory angle:** Systematic qualification → efficient business development

### Topic 2: Briefing Call Best Practices
- **Status:** Optimize discovery call structure for Geneva executive preferences
- **Key elements:** Listen first, diagnose before proposing, credible, relevant, chemistry
- **Advisory angle:** Call conversion rate optimization

### Topic 3: Case Study Development
- **Status:** Build case study library with client permission
- **Format:** Situation → Challenge → Approach → Outcome → Quote
- **Advisory angle:** Credibility and trust building

### Topic 4: Proposal Structure
- **Status:** Standardize proposal template
- **Format:** Situation + Recommendation + Investment + Timeline + Next Steps
- **Preference:** Retainer over project-based
- **Advisory angle:** Professional positioning, revenue optimization

### Topic 5: Onboarding Process
- **Status:** Develop structured 30-day onboarding process
- **Elements:** Kickoff meeting, scope alignment, communication protocols, check-in cadence
- **Advisory angle:** Relationship foundation, friction reduction

### Topic 6: Expansion Pathways
- **Status:** Systematize quarterly review and referral request
- **Expansion:** Initial mandate → ongoing counsel → referral generation
- **Advisory angle:** Retainer growth, revenue predictability

## Client Journey Map
1. **Discovery:** Brotons.net / LinkedIn / referral → initial contact
2. **Qualification:** ICP filter → qualified or not
3. **Briefing call:** 30-min discovery → specific recommendation
4. **Proposal:** Tailored proposal → retainer offer
5. **Engagement:** Onboarding → kickoff → monthly cadence
6. **Expansion:** Quarterly review → scope increase / referral
7. **Renewal:** Annual retainer renewal → ongoing relationship
