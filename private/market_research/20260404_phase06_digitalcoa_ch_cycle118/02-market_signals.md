# 02 — Market Signals: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C118)

## Advisory Business Signals (DigitalCoa.ch)

### Signal 1: Lead Qualification — Who Is a Real Prospect
- Most inbound inquiries are not qualified prospects — wrong level, wrong problem, wrong budget
- Qualified prospect signals: C-suite level, specific regulatory problem, budget awareness, timeline urgency
- Unqualified: vague request, no budget mentioned, not decision-maker, asking for free advice
- **Signal:** Develop explicit lead qualification framework; filter early, focus time on qualified prospects
- **Source:** Inbound inquiry analysis, sales process best practices

### Signal 2: Briefing Call Conversion — The Critical Moment
- First call is where relationship is established or lost
- Geneva executives decide quickly based on: credibility, relevance, chemistry
- Key: listen more than talk; diagnose before proposing
- Structure: 30-min discovery call → tailored follow-up with specific recommendation
- **Signal:** Optimize briefing call conversion rate — this is the highest-leverage activity in the business
- **Source:** Sales process data, Geneva executive preferences

### Signal 3: Case Study and Testimonial Strategy
- Geneva executive market trusts peer references over credentials
- Case studies must be specific: client situation → challenge → approach → outcome
- Testimonial format: direct quote from client (not paraphrased)
- Permission-based: only publish with explicit client approval
- **Signal:** Build case study library systematically; request testimonials after every successful engagement
- **Source:** Marketing best practices, Geneva market trust dynamics

### Signal 4: Proposal and Contract Structure
- Geneva market expects: concise, specific, professional proposals
- Proposal format: situation + recommendation + investment + timeline + next steps
- Avoid: long legal terms, excessive detail, volume discounts
- Retainer preferred over project-based; signals ongoing relationship
- **Signal:** Standardize proposal template; always offer retainer as preferred option
- **Source:** Proposal best practices, Geneva market expectations

### Signal 5: Client Onboarding and Kickoff Process
- First 30 days set relationship tone; structured onboarding reduces friction
- Kickoff meeting: align on scope, timeline, communication, stakeholders
- Monthly check-ins: progress + emerging issues + value delivered
- **Signal:** Develop systematic onboarding process; consistency builds trust
- **Source:** Client management best practices

### Signal 6: Expansion and Upsell Pathways
- Advisory relationships naturally expand: initial mandate → related issues → ongoing counsel
- Quarterly business review: review value delivered + surface new issues
- Referral generation: satisfied clients refer peers; ask for referrals
- Retainer model: expansion through scope increase, not new project billing
- **Signal:** Systematize quarterly review + referral request cadence
- **Source:** Account management best practices, retainer economics

## Synthesis
DigitalCoa.ch business growth driven by:
1. Lead qualification → efficient use of time
2. Briefing call conversion → business formation
3. Case studies → credibility and trust
4. Proposal structure → professional image
5. Onboarding → relationship foundation
6. Expansion → revenue growth
