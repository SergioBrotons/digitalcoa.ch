# 01 — ICP: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C118)

## ICP: DigitalCoa.ch Advisory Clients

### Primary ICP — Executive Decision-Makers (Geneva Financial Sector)
**Profile:**
- C-suite, Managing Director, Partner, Head of Function
- Financial institution (private bank, asset manager, commodity trader, insurance)
- Located Geneva; some Zurich references
- Problem: regulatory change, strategic decision, organizational challenge
- Budget authority: CHF 10,000-200,000 for advisory engagement
- Language: French primary; English fluent

### Secondary ICP — Family Offices and UHNW Structures
**Profile:**
- Single family office or UHNW multi-family office
- Decision-maker: Principal, Family CIO, external advisor
- Problem: governance, succession, regulatory exposure, investment strategy
- Budget: variable; often relationship-driven
- Geography: Geneva, Vaud

### Tertiary ICP — Professional Services Firm Partners
**Profile:**
- Law firm partners, accounting firm partners with regulatory/client issues
- Problem: client matter requiring specialist support beyond firm capability
- Budget: firm pays or client pays through matter
- Language: English + French

## Non-ICP (Avoid)
- SMEs with limited budget (< CHF 5,000) — wrong size engagement
- Requests for volume work (legal research, document review) — not the model
- Prospects without budget authority or decision-making capacity
- Anyone seeking the lowest price — not the client
