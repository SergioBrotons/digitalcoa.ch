# 05 — Lead Magnet: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C118)

## Lead Magnet Strategy (DigitalCoa.ch)

### Primary Lead Magnet: "Executive Discovery Call" (Direct)
**Format:** 45-min 1:1 video call with senior executive
**Distribution:** Brotons.net → LinkedIn → direct referral
**Content:** Geneva regulatory intelligence relevant to executive's specific situation
**CTA:** "Book a Briefing Call" → Calendly → qualification → proposal
**Conversion:** Highest-value lead magnet; direct path to advisory engagement

### Secondary Lead Magnet: "DigitalCoa.ch Insights" (Newsletter)
**Format:** Quarterly 2-page briefing on Geneva executive landscape
**Distribution:** Substack + direct email to executive list
**Content:** Regulatory trends, strategic implications, practical recommendations
**CTA:** Subscribe → nurture → discovery call

### Tertiary Lead Magnet: "The Geneva Regulatory Readiness Assessment"
**Format:** Self-assessment tool + 20-min debrief call
**Target:** C-suite, GCs, Compliance Officers at Geneva financial institutions
**CTA:** Complete assessment → receive report → optional debrief call → qualification

## Conversion Pathway
1. Brotons.net article / LinkedIn → newsletter subscription
2. Newsletter → thought leadership credibility → briefing call request
3. Briefing call → diagnostic → tailored proposal
4. Proposal → retainer engagement
5. Engagement → value delivered → quarterly review → expansion + referral

## Testimonial / Case Study Strategy
- Request testimonials at engagement conclusion (not after every interaction)
- Format: specific, qualitative, client voice
- Use: proposals, website, LinkedIn, Brotons.net
- Always ask permission; offer client review before publishing
