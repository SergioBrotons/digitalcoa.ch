# 03 — Competitive Landscape: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C118)

## Competitive Set: Geneva Executive Advisory

### Tier 1 — Management Consultancies
**McKinsey, BCG, Bain**

- Strengths: brand, resources, global reach
- Weakness: Geneva is secondary market; expensive; not specialist
- **Threat:** When clients need scale, they call McKinsey

### Tier 2 — Geneva Boutiques
**Boutique strategy and regulatory consultancies**

- Strengths: Geneva-specific, relationship-based
- Weakness: inconsistent quality, limited international perspective
- **Opportunity:** Differentiate on depth, voice, and thought leadership

### Tier 3 — Individual Practitioners
**Solo advisors, retired executives**

- Strengths: flexibility, senior attention
- Weakness: no team, no continuity, limited range
- **Opportunity:** Position as senior practitioner with network access

## DigitalCoa.ch Differentiation

### Key Differentiators
1. **Geneva depth + international perspective** — not just Geneva, not just global — both
2. **Regulatory intelligence as foundation** — advisory grounded in current regulatory reality
3. **Executive voice** — calm, direct, no consultant-speak
4. **Brotons.net as credibility engine** — public thought leadership precedes client relationship
5. **Bilingual EN+FR** — rare in Geneva executive advisory

### Underserved Positioning
1. **Regulatory strategy** — not just compliance, not just strategy — regulatory strategy
2. **Executive advisory for financial sector** — highly specific, highly targeted
3. **Independent** — no conflicts, no product to sell
4. **Accessible** — direct access to senior advisor (not junior team)

## Positioning Recommendation
**"DigitalCoa.ch: The Geneva executive advisory for regulatory strategy and organizational decision-making"**

- Senior, independent, Geneva-based
- Grounded in regulatory intelligence
- Bilingual EN+FR
- Accessible directly; no account manager layer
