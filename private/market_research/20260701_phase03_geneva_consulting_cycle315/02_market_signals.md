## Signal Architecture

C315 Phase 03 captures the post-deadline sub-consultant Article 26 enforcement signals specific to the Geneva boutique consulting market. C314 P03 signals were pre-deadline readiness signals. C315 P01 signals were post-deadline legal enforcement signals. C315 P02 signals were post-deadline FINMA-Article 26 finance enforcement signals.
