## Market Map (C315 P03)

The Geneva boutique consulting AI accountability market in July 2026 is structurally similar to the C314 P03 market (pre-deadline readiness) and the C315 P01-P02 markets (post-deadline enforcement in legal and finance verticals) but with a critical operational shift specific to the consulting vertical: the post-deadline sub-consultant Article 26 enforcement signals — sub-consultant AI authorization roster audit, sub-consultant carrier attestation cycle, sub-consultant handover-window discontinuity, Article 50 deliverable transparency enforcement, sub-consultant vendor sub-processor audit, three-regime sub-consultant enforcement coordination — are now active signals, not hypothetical scenarios.

The C314 P03 market offered pre-deadline readiness frameworks. The C315 P01 market offered post-deadline legal enforcement navigation. The C315 P03 market needs post-deadline sub-consultant Article 26 enforcement navigation — applied to the named sub-consultant individual as the integrating mechanism across MSA Article 99 + nFADP Art.63 + carrier attestation.
