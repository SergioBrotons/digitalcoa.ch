## Opening Problem Statement

**The gap is not adoption. It is governance.**

69% of legal professionals in Switzerland are already using AI tools. 42% are using legal-specific AI. 70% of law firms are increasing AI adoption in 2026. Yet only 34% of firms have formal AI governance policies.

The gap between individual AI use and firm-level accountability is your primary risk — and your primary business opportunity.

**Four pressures are converging:**
1. **The FADP is already operative.** FDPIC has significantly increased enforcement resources (2024/2025 Annual Report). No new law required. Your obligations exist now.
2. **The FADP has hard prohibitions.** Mass facial recognition and social scoring are explicitly prohibited.
3. **Swiss legal AI tools (FABIAN, CASUS) now exist.** There is no data residency excuse.
4. **The first formal regulatory pressure arrives May 2026** (digitalswitzerland First Draft). Formal legislation follows end-2026.
