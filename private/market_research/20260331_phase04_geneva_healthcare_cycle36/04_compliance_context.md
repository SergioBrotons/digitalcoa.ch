## Compliance Complexity (Healthcare-Specific)

### Swissdamed Registration Challenge
Swissdamed AND EUDAMED are NOT interconnected — medical device dealers must:
1. Register with Swissdamed (Swissmedic system)
2. Register separately with EUDAMED (EU system)
3. Maintain both registrations independently

This dual registration burden is a significant administrative challenge for small practices.

### Clinical AI Risk Profile
**57% AI errors/hallucinations as top threat** — unique to healthcare vertical:
- AI diagnostic imaging: false negatives = delayed treatment
- AI symptom triage: incorrect urgency classification = patient safety risk
- Patient communication AI: privacy breaches = FADP + professional secrecy violations

### FADP Clinical Context
Patient data = special category under FADP. AI processing of patient data requires:
- Explicit consent or legal basis
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization
- DPIA for high-risk processing
- Privacy by Design
