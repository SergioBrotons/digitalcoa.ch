## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

**Big 4 Legal Arms (DLA Piper, Baker McKenzie, Clyde & Co, Hogan Lovells)**
- Dominate Geneva FS regulatory advisory; low independent conflict-free advisory
- ICP perception: conflicted for clients with bank relationships; institutional memory of Big 4 audit conflicts
- Differentiation opportunity: independent non-conflicted positioning

**Geneva Law Firms (Lenz & Staehelin, Vischer, Walder Wyss, CMS, Python)**
- Deep Swiss law expertise; relationship-driven; premium pricing
- ICP perception: trusted but expensive; limited independent candid counsel
- Differentiation opportunity: independent perspective on regulatory change impact

**Boutique Geneva Advisory (e.g., the firm's positioning)**
- Independent non-conflicted advisory; smaller scale, niche regulatory depth
- Opportunity: trusted-peer introduction via Brotons.net content; conflict-free candid counsel
- Risk: discoverability vs. relationship-based competition

**Geneva Bar Association / CCIG peer networks**
- Unofficial referral; trust-based
- Opportunity: Brotons.net as institutional trust network entry point

| Competitor Type | Weakness | Our Positioning |
