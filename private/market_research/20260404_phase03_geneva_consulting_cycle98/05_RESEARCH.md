# Finding 05 — CYCLE 98 | Phase 03: Geneva Consulting
**Source:** Professional services M&A data, Geneva consulting ecosystem, industry intelligence  
**Date:** 2026-04-03

## Finding

**Geneva Consulting M&A Wave Intensifies — International Buyers Acquiring Swiss Boutique Capabilities**

The Geneva consulting market is experiencing an M&A wave driven by international professional services firms acquiring Swiss boutiques for capability and talent acquisition. Mid-sized Swiss management consulting and IT consulting firms are the primary targets.

Key dynamics:

**1. International buyers prioritising Swiss ESG/digital consulting capability:** European and US consulting firms are acquiring Geneva-based boutiques with strong ESG, sustainability, or AI practices. Talent acquisition (not revenue) is often the primary driver.

**2. Swiss consulting firms as acquirers:** Larger Swiss consulting firms (Adesso, Ineum, other mid-tier firms) are acquiring smaller boutiques to build out service lines and geographic coverage. Domestic consolidation wave running parallel to international interest.

**3. Valuation compression at smaller end:** Boutique consulting firms below CHF 10M revenue face compression in valuations as buyers see limited strategic value. Firms above CHF 20M revenue with proprietary methodologies or strong client relationships command premium multiples.

**4. Professional succession planning:** Many Geneva consulting firms are family-owned or founder-led. First-generation succession (sale to institutional buyer) is a growing trend as founders approach retirement.

**5. Talent retention clauses and earnouts:** Post-acquisition talent retention is the primary challenge. Sellers are accepting increasingly complex earnout structures tied to key consultant retention — creating bespoke legal advisory work.

**Signal for Brotons.net:** Consulting industry M&A is a story about market structure and talent concentration. A piece on what firm consolidation means for the quality and independence of consulting advice in Geneva would be distinctive.

**Signal for DigitalCoa.ch:** Owners of Geneva consulting firms evaluating M&A opportunities need advisory support on deal structuring, valuation, and talent retention. This is a direct advisory services angle.
