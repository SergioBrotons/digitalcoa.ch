# Finding 01 — CYCLE 98 | Phase 03: Geneva Consulting
**Source:** Swiss consulting industry analysis, IMD Lausanne, Geneva-Keep, Geneva Economic Development Office  
**Date:** 2026-04-03

## Finding

**AI Adoption Driving Internal Consulting Budget Cuts — External Strategy Consultants Win on Specificity**

Geneva-based corporations are significantly reducing internal consulting staff while increasing selective external spend on highly specific AI strategy engagements. The bifurcation is pronounced: generalist internal strategy teams are being cut, while external mandates for AI integration strategy, data architecture, and AI governance are expanding.

Key dynamics:

**1. "AI strategy" replacing "digital transformation":** The previous wave of digital transformation consulting (2018-2023) has peaked. Geneva clients now want specific AI integration roadmaps, AI governance frameworks, and AI risk assessments — not generic digital strategy.

**2. Consulting firm M&A for AI capability:** Major strategy firms (Boston, McKinsey, Accenture, Capco) are acquiring specialized AI firms to bolt on capability. Geneva consulting firms with AI specialisation are acquisition targets or are being hired as independent specialists.

**3. Geneva-based AI boutique consultancies growing:** A cluster of Geneva-based AI consulting boutiques (focused on financial services AI, compliance AI, and pharma AI) is gaining traction. These firms differentiate on vertical specialization, not generalist consulting.

**4. "Fractional AI officer" model emerging:** Some independent AI consultants in Geneva are offering fractional Chief AI Officer services to mid-sized companies — a new category that sits between consulting mandate and interim executive.

**5. AI consulting rate compression for general work:** Basic AI strategy work (chatbot implementation, document automation) is being commoditised. Premium rates are reserved for complex AI governance, AI regulatory compliance, and AI-driven business model redesign.

**Signal for Brotons.net:** The consulting industry's AI-driven transformation is a meta-story that Geneva professionals find fascinating. A sharp analysis of how the consulting model itself is changing would attract broad readership.

**Signal for DigitalCoa.ch:** Executive clients who need to commission AI strategy work need to understand how to select and structure engagements with AI consultancies. Advisory content that helps them avoid generic AI consulting and demand specificity = direct value.
