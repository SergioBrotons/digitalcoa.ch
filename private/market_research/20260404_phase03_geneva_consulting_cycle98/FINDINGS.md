## NET-NEW Findings (6)

### Finding 01: AI Strategy Consulting — Generalist Decline, Specialisation Premium
- Internal consulting budgets cut; external spend on specific AI governance and integration rising
- AI boutique consultancies in Geneva are acquisition targets and independent growth stories
- "Fractional AI Officer" model emerging as new consulting category
- Basic AI strategy being commoditised; premium for complex AI risk and governance

### Finding 02: HR Consulting Shifts from Development to Employee Relations
- Swiss OR revisions on remote work/collective redundancy create new compliance mandates
- DEI consulting driven by Gender Equality Act revisions + EU obligations
- Labour market tightness sustains executive search and compensation benchmarking
- HR tech implementation (Workday, SAP) generating stable consulting revenue

### Finding 03: Sustainability Consulting — Strategy Saturated, Assurance Scarce
- CSRD assurance shortage creates premium opportunity for verified sustainability reporting
- Scope 3 emissions accounting = multi-year consulting revenue
- Swiss Green Bond second-party opinions = specific niche for Geneva verification firms
- GRI/CSRD dual-alignment = specialised consulting niche

### Finding 04: Executive Search — Board/C-Suite Retained Search Holds; Middle Market Stressed
- Internal talent marketplaces reducing external mandates below C-suite
- Functional specialisation (ESG exec search, CIO/CFO separation) commanding premiums
- EU regulatory expertise premium in Geneva pharma, finance, sustainability
- Interim executives for transformation mandates = higher-margin model

### Finding 05: Geneva Consulting M&A Wave — International Buyers Targeting Boutique Capability
- European/US firms acquiring Geneva ESG and AI boutiques for talent
- Domestic consolidation running parallel; firms above CHF 20M commanding premium multiples
- Founder/family succession sales increasing; earnout/talent retention structures complex

### Finding 06: IT Consulting Talent Crisis — AI/Cybersecurity Premium Widening Boutique Gap
- AI/data science talent commanding 30-50% salary premiums; boutiques can't compete
- Cybersecurity advisory demand sustained by attacks on Swiss financial targets
- In-house IT consulting build reducing external mandate pool for boutiques
- Onshore delivery premium re-establishing for high-complexity AI/cybersecurity work
