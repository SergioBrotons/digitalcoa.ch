1. **An AI tool inventory with configuration records** — not just what tools you use, but what version, what settings, what data flows through each one, and what you intended each tool to do when you activated it.

This is not a policy document. It's a personal document, and it needs to exist before you need it.

3. **A reassessment trigger architecture** — because AI tools change, vendors update models, staff leave, and client circumstances evolve. Your governance architecture needs to know when to reassess, and it needs to do so before conditions have drifted past the point where reassessment catches the problem.

3. **Une architecture de déclenchement de réévaluation** — parce que les outils IA changent, que les fournisseurs mettent à jour leurs modèles, que le personnel part, et que les circonstances clients évoluent. Votre architecture de gouvernance doit savoir quand réévaluer, et elle doit le faire avant que les conditions n'aient dérivé au-delà du point où la réévaluation capture le problème.
