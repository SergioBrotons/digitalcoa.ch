# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## DigitalCoa.ch

**Primary Purpose:** Convert Geneva executive audience from Brotons.net readers to advisory clients

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Geneva Executive Briefing Call (Primary CTA)**
- Format: 30-minute discovery call
- Content: Executive's specific challenge, brief advisory perspective, fit assessment
- CTA: "Schedule a briefing call" — prominent on DigitalCoa.ch
- Conversion logic: High-quality, low-commitment

**Option B: Regulatory Impact Assessment (On-Demand)**
- Format: Scoped assessment + findings + roadmap
- Content: Executive's specific regulatory challenge, impact analysis, recommended actions
- CTA: Briefing call to discuss assessment
- Conversion logic: Demonstrates value before engagement

**Option C: Quarterly Geneva Regulatory Briefing (Subscription)**
- Format: Quarterly briefing + priority advisory access
- Content: Top regulatory developments, strategic implications, ahead-of-curve perspective
- CTA: "Join quarterly briefing" + advisory subscription
- Conversion logic: Relationship building + value demonstration

**Option D: Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch Referral Pathway (Automated)**
- Format: Content-based introduction to advisory services
- Content: Relevant article → advisory context → briefing call CTA
- CTA: "Explore how this applies to your situation"
- Conversion logic: Warm context, not cold pitch

**Recommended Primary:** Option A (Briefing Call) — highest conversion rate for qualified ICP
**Recommended Secondary:** Option D (Brotons.net pathway) — warm context, trust established

**Conversion Mechanics:**
1. Brotons.net reader → Geneva regulatory article
2. Article → DigitalCoa.ch service context + CTA
3. Reader books briefing call
4. Briefing call → qualification + proposal
5. Proposal → engagement

**Key CTA Placement:**
- DigitalCoa.ch homepage: Prominent "Schedule a Briefing Call"
- Service pages: Specific CTAs per service
- Brotons.net articles: Contextual DigitalCoa.ch CTA
- LinkedIn: Profile → DigitalCoa.ch link
- Email newsletter: DigitalCoa.ch service note
