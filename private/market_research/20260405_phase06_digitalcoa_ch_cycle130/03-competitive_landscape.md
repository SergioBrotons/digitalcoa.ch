# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## DigitalCoa.ch

**Direct Competitors:**

*Big 4 / Major Consultancies:*
- PwC Geneva: Regulatory, risk, Basel IV (conflict constraints)
- Deloitte Geneva: Financial services advisory (conflict constraints)
- KPMG Geneva: Risk + compliance (conflict constraints)
- EY Geneva: Transaction + regulatory (conflict constraints)

*Boutique Specialist Advisors:*
- Regulatory specialists: Various independent firms
- Geneva-focused: Arendi, Ferrande, other boutiques
- International: McKinsey, BCG, Bain (strategy)

**Competitive Advantages of DigitalCoa.ch:**
- Independent, conflict-free: No audit conflicts; can advise where Big 4 cannot
- Geneva institutional depth: Local knowledge + network
- Boutique quality: Senior attention, not pyramid staffing
- Brotons.net credibility: Content-based trust building
- Remote delivery: Geographic flexibility without quality loss

**Differentiation Challenge:**
- "Geneva boutique" → credibility establishment takes time
- Independent positioning: Requires demonstrated expertise
- Price positioning: Value justification vs. Big 4 brand
- Scale limitations: Solo/small team capacity constraint

**Positioning Strategy:**
- "Independent Geneva regulatory intelligence + advisory"
- "Where institutional depth meets boutique attention"
- "Conflict-free — where Big 4 cannot go"
- "Brotons.net insight, DigitalCoa.ch action"

**Client Selection:**
- Geneva financial institutions with regulatory complexity
- Family offices with succession + governance needs
- Companies with Switzerland-EU strategic decisions
- Executives facing personal liability exposure
- Organizations needing independent perspective
