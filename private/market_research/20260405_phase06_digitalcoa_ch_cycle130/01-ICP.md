# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## DigitalCoa.ch — ICP

**Primary ICP: Geneva Executive Decision-Makers**

**Segment A: Geneva Financial Institution Executives**
- CEO/CFO/CRO of Geneva private banks, asset managers, commodity traders
- Board members of Geneva financial institutions
- Head of Compliance / MLRO
- Managing partners of Geneva law firms with regulatory practices
- Pain: Regulatory complexity, Basel IV, FINMA enforcement, bilateral uncertainty
- Trigger: FINMA examination, enforcement notice, board decision, regulatory deadline

**Segment B: Family Business Principals (UHNW/HNW)**
- Family principals of Geneva-based family offices
- Wealth transfer/succession decision-makers
- Next-generation family members being prepared for governance roles
- Pain: Succession planning, governance, wealth preservation, family conflict
- Trigger: Succession event, family crisis, regulatory change, investment decision

**Segment C: International Organization / Diplomatic Ecosystem**
- IO senior management with commercial interests
- Diplomats transitioning to private sector
- IO foundation directors
- Pain: Cross-border regulatory navigation, Switzerland-EU, reputational risk
- Trigger: IO departure, commercial venture launch, regulatory issue

**Decision Psychology:**
- Relationship-driven: Trust through introduction, not cold outreach
- Evidence-based: Proof of credibility before engagement
- Time-constrained: Need concise, relevant, actionable
- Risk-aware: Regulatory, reputational, financial risk prioritization
- Geneva-centric: Local knowledge + international perspective

**Engagement Journey:**
1. Discover: Brotons.net content, LinkedIn, referral
2. Qualify: Content credibility + peer trust
3. Engage: Briefing call, discovery conversation
4. Convert: Proposal + engagement
5. Retain: Quarterly review + referral generation

**Geography:** Geneva primary, Vaud, Ticino, Zurich; International Geneva
**Language:** EN primary, FR useful, DE appreciated
**Budget:** CHF 50K-500K+ per engagement
