### SECTION 5: The 4-Step Retroactive Compliance Path (Unique RE Entry Point)

Unlike other verticals (where the question is "prepare for upcoming deadlines"), RE's unique position is **retroactive compliance** — you're already non-compliant. Here's the path:

**Step 1 — Inventory (now):** Document every AI tool used, what data it accessed, for how long. You cannot undo past non-compliance, but you must know the scope.

**Step 2 — Assess (within 30 days):** Which AI tools processed personal data without DPIA? Which clients were affected? Is any EU-resident data involved (EU AI Act nexus)?

**Step 3 — Remediate (within 60 days):** Complete DPIA-lite for each AI tool. Implement data subject rights processes. Align with GAIGI standards.

**Step 4 — Govern (ongoing):** Establish ongoing AI governance framework. Document for future FDPIC inquiries. GAIGI alignment demonstrates good faith.
