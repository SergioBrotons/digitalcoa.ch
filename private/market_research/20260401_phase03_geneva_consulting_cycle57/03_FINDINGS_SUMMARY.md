## NET-NEW C57 Findings

1. **AI is #1 Swiss fintech category**: 35% of 529 Swiss fintech companies active in AI (IFZ 2026, April 1, 2026) — first time AI surpasses DLT and process digitalization. Geneva consulting firms advising financial sector clients face surging demand for AI advisory.

2. **Swiss fintech consolidation**: Swiss fintech is in a phase of consolidation and specialization. New entrants offset by exits. Governance advisory differentiates in a consolidating market.

3. **Structural labor shortage**: KOF study (March 2026) confirms 400,000 worker shortage over 10 years. AI adoption is becoming mandatory for SMB clients, not optional. Consulting firms must advise on AI governance alongside AI adoption.

4. **G7 Évian + US Section 301**: Indirect exposure for Geneva consulting firms with SMB clients in trade-exposed sectors. Consulting firms should map client exposure proactively.

5. **GPAI systemic risk advisory gap**: Most small Geneva consulting firms not GPAI-ready with 4 months to August 2, 2026 deadline. First-mover advantage available.
