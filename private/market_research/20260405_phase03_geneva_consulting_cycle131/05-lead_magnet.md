# 05 — Lead Magnet
## Geneva Consulting — C131

## Recommended Lead Magnet: "Geneva SME Regulatory Compliance Calendar H2 2026"

**Format:** Annual calendar + brief advisory guide (6-8 pages EN + FR executive summary)
**Target:** CEO, CFO, Managing Director of Geneva SMEs (10-500 employees); Geneva family businesses preparing for Art. 716a compliance
**Primary CTA:** Download the Calendar; book a compliance readiness review

**Content Framework:**

1. **Geneva SME compliance deadline calendar H2 2026 — Q1 2027:**
   - Art. 716a board diversity: Board nomination cycle deadline (typically Q4 AG planning for Jan 2026 compliance)
   - EU CH bilateral: Negotiation milestones and contingency planning windows
   - OR Art. 964j-k: Supply chain due diligence documentation requests — respond and document
   - CARB/CBAM: Swiss CARB implementation timeline and CBAM Phase 2 September 2026
   - R&D tax incentive: Application windows for Geneva OCE vouchers and federal R&D deductions
   - DORA: EU-facing Swiss institutions ICT third-party risk documentation

2. **Geneva SME "Compliance Without Overwhelm" framework:**
   - Priority matrix: urgency × impact for each compliance item
   - Simplified documentation templates for supply chain due diligence (below-500-employee SMEs)
   - Board diversity compliance: what independent advisory can and cannot do

3. **Cantonal and federal incentive capture checklist:**
   - R&D tax incentive eligibility self-assessment
   - Geneva OCE innovation voucher checklist
   - Application timing and documentation requirements

4. **Dual-track EU-CH strategic planning brief:**
   - Scenario planning framework for Geneva SMEs with EU exposure
   - Key decision points and timing for contingency vs. agreement implementation

**Distribution:**
- Brotons.net primary publication (EN + FR)
- LinkedIn newsletter excerpt (calendar top 3 deadlines)
- Geneva Chamber of Commerce (CCIG) member communications (if/when partnership approved)
- DigitalCoa.ch secondary distribution

**Differentiation:**
This calendar serves Geneva SMEs who currently have no single reference document covering their full regulatory compliance obligations. It positions independent advisory as the "Geneva SME compliance partner" without competing with Big 4 on audit or general consulting on scale.
