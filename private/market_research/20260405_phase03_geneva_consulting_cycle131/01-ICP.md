# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva Consulting — C131

**Firm Types:**
- Geneva SMEs with EU market exposure (trading, manufacturing, services)
- Swiss listed companies with Geneva HQ or significant Swiss operations
- Geneva SMEs in supply chains of larger companies (responding to due diligence demands)
- Geneva family businesses preparing for board diversity compliance Jan 2026
- International organizations with Geneva-based staff transitioning to private sector
- Geneva SMBs seeking cantonal/SECO innovation incentives
- Swiss interim management buyers (CFOs, CEOs of Geneva firms)

**Decision-Maker:**
- CEO / Managing Director (SMEs)
- CFO / Finance Director (compliance programs)
- Board Nomination Committee Chair (diversity quota compliance)
- HR Director (interim management, training)
- Family Business Owner / Patriarch (succession and governance)
- IO HR Director / departing IO professional

**Pain Points C131:**
- EU-CH institutional agreement uncertainty blocking mid-term investment decisions
- Art. 716a board diversity quota Jan 2026 compliance (listed companies)
- OR Art. 964j-k supply chain due diligence documentation requests from larger buyers
- Specialized interim compliance managers: where to find CARB/CRS/DORA/ESG experts
- R&D tax incentive and innovation voucher application complexity
- JMDI/UN pension optimization and tax residency planning for departing IO staff

**Urgency Triggers:**
- Jan 1 2026 Art. 716a board diversity compliance deadline
- EU-CH bilateral III negotiation milestones (Parliamentary sessions)
- Annual board nomination cycle (Q4 typically)
- CARB implementation timeline (EU CBAM Phase 2 Sept 2026)
- SECO/Swiss innovation voucher application windows
- IO professional departure timelines (typically Q4 or end of contract)

**Geography:** Geneva; Vaud; Geneva–Lausanne axis
**Language:** FR primary for Geneva SMEs; EN primary for IO professionals and listed companies
**Budget Cycle:** Q4 board governance budget; ad hoc SME strategic projects
