# 04 — Regulatory Context
## Geneva Consulting — C131

## EU-CH Bilateral Agreement

**Legal basis:** Bilateral Agreements I and II between Switzerland and the EU; ongoing institutional agreement negotiation
**Key issue:** Switzerland-EU institutional agreement covering financial services, public procurement, aviation; Federal Council continues negotiation while preparing contingency measures
**Status:** Negotiation ongoing; no agreement signed as of April 2026; mid-term planning uncertainty persisting for Geneva SMEs
**Impact:** Geneva SMEs with EU market access deferring investment; dual-track compliance strategy advisory in demand

## Art. 716a Board Diversity Quota

**Legal basis:** Swiss Code of Obligations Art. 716a as amended by Federal Assembly; applies to listed companies
**Key requirement:** 40% women on board of directors; effective January 1, 2026
**Applicability:** Swiss listed companies (SIX Swiss Exchange); companies with registered office or principal place of business in Switzerland
**Enforcement:** Shareholder disclosure requirements; no direct FINMA sanction but reputational/AGM pressure
**Geneva dimension:** ~30 significant Geneva-HQ or Geneva-major-presence listed companies in scope
**Status:** Compliance deadline active; board nomination cycles for 2026 ongoing H2 2025 through Q1 2026

## OR Art. 964j-k Supply Chain Due Diligence

**Legal basis:** Swiss Code of Obligations Art. 964j-k; aligned with German LkSIS and EU CSRD supply chain requirements
**Key requirement:** Due diligence on own operations and supply chain; prohibition on using suppliers using forced labor or child labor; reporting obligation
**Threshold:** Mandatory for companies with 500+ employees
**Cascade effect:** Companies below 500 employees facing due diligence documentation demands from larger buyers; Geneva trading houses and banks requesting supplier documentation
**Status:** In force; enforcement primarily through private law (contractual liability) rather than public enforcement

## CARB — Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism

**Legal basis:** Swiss CARB legislation (Federal Council dispatch passed Parliament); aligned with EU CBAM
**Key requirement:** Importers of certain carbon-intensive goods must purchase CARB certificates corresponding to carbon price paid in country of origin
**Timeline:** EU CBAM Phase 2 from September 2026; Swiss CARB implementation following similar timeline
**Geneva dimension:** Geneva commodity traders and metal traders with carbon-intensive imports affected
**Status:** Implementation approaching; advisory demand increasing for Geneva trading houses

## R&D Tax Incentive Programs

**Legal basis:** Swiss Direct Federal Taxes Art. 35b-c (R&D additional deduction); Cantonal tax incentives (Geneva reduction of cantonal tax rate for R&D activities)
**Key programs:**
- Federal R&D additional deduction (additional 50% of qualifying R&D costs beyond normal deduction)
- OCE Geneva innovation vouchers (up to CHF 17,500 per qualifying SME project)
- SECO/inno-swiss innovation coaching programs
**Status:** Active programs; application windows rolling; advisory demand for optimization
