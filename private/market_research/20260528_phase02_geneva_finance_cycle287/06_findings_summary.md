**Finding 05: SRO Guidance Vacuum Creates Operative Uncertainty Resolved Differently by Risk Tolerance, Not by Information**

Interpretation A: "No SRO guidance means no specific obligations" → operating without building documentation

The Geneva accountability ecosystem (carriers + institutional counterparties + informal circuit intelligence) is forming standards faster than formal regulatory process articulates them. MDs who wait for SRO guidance to arrive are waiting for standards the formal process hasn't named yet.
