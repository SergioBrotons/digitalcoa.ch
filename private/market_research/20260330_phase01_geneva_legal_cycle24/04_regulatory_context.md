# Phase 01 — Geneva Legal: Regulatory Context
## Cycle 24 | 2026-03-30

## Active Regulatory Landscape

### Swiss Domestic — ACTIVE NOW

#### nFADP (Federal Act on Data Protection)
- **Art. 5(c)**: Profiling and automated individual decision-making — RIGHT TO HUMAN REVIEW active
- **Art. 21**: Right to object to automated decisions
- **Proportionality principle**: Enforcement balanced against professional secrecy for lawyers
- **FDPIC enforcement trajectory**: 1,053 complaints; 6 enforcement actions 2025–2026; legal sector ABSENT

#### FDPIC (Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner)
- **Enforcement portfolio building**: AI investigations across transport, banking, retail, social media
- **Wearables guidance March 2026**: enforcement pattern expansion to proximity/professional services
- **Penalty architecture**: No direct fines for lawyers under nFADP (professional secrecy constraint); criminal prosecution route only
- **Annual Report Jun 30, 2025**: 1,053 complaints; enforcement trajectory confirmed

#### SBA AI Guidelines (Swiss Bar Association)
- **Published**: June 2024
- **Status**: Non-binding baseline
- **Principles**: Confidentiality, competence, non-discrimination, human oversight
- **Assessment**: National baseline for Swiss lawyers; no small-firm-specific guidance

#### Federal Administrative Court (Bundesverwaltungsgericht)
- **June 2025 ruling**: AI cannot be named as inventor; human contributor can be named
- **Principle**: Human accountability for AI-assisted work product

### Extraterritorial — ALREADY ACTIVE

#### EU AI Act (firms with EU market nexus)
- **GPAI transparency obligations**: ACTIVE since August 2, 2025 (~8.5 months)
  - Systematic reflection: prohibited unless human oversight ensured
  - Emotional manipulation: prohibited
  - Social scoring: prohibited
  - Deep fake disclosure: required
  - AI system registration: encouraged for GPAI model providers
- **High-risk obligations**: August 2, 2026 (~16 months)
  - Listed in Annex III (credit, employment, essential services, etc.)
  - Conformity assessment required before market placement
  - Geneva law firms serving EU clients or placing AI on EU market: may be subject

#### Council of Europe AI Convention
- **Adopted**: May 2025 (ETS No. 225)
- **Status**: Not yet in force; Switzerland signatory; ratification process pending
- **Implementation**: Via FDJP consultation draft end-2026
