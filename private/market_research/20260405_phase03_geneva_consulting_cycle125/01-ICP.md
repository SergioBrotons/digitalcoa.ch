# 01 — ICP: Geneva Consulting Market

## Target Segment: Swiss SME & Mid-Market Companies (Geneva-Anchored)

### Company Profile
- **Size:** CHF 20M–500M annual revenue
- **Employees:** 50-500
- **Locations:** Geneva primary; Vaud; Swiss romande; some with international operations
- **Sectors:** Trading, manufacturing, services, technology, professional services
- **Status:** Swiss SME sector under pressure from regulatory change, FX volatility, and digital transformation challenges

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|------|---------|-------------|
| CEO / Managing Director | Owner or professional manager | Regulatory compliance, cost structure, strategic direction |
| CFO | Finance leader | Reporting requirements (CSRD), cost restructuring, interim finance gaps |
| COO | Operations leader | Digital transformation, operational efficiency, supply chain |
| Board / Shareholders | Ownership governance | Risk oversight, ESG, succession planning |

### Known Geneva/Swiss SME Examples
- Medium-sized commodity trading companies
- Swiss manufacturing firms (precision, pharma supply, food)
- Professional services firms
- Technology companies (SaaS, fintech, digital health)
- Family-owned businesses in Geneva and Vaud

### Pain Points (C125 Update)
1. **CSRD double materiality assessment** — Large enterprise CSRD compliance cascading to SME supply chain; Swiss SME needing help understanding obligations and preparing assessments
2. **Geopolitical risk frameworks** — Swiss neutrality under pressure; multinationals and commodity traders needing structured geopolitical risk advisory
3. **CFO talent gap** — Difficulty recruiting experienced CFOs; interim CFO demand for CFO transitions, project work, and crisis situations
4. **FDI screening compliance** — Non-Swiss acquirers and Swiss targets needing FDI screening advisory; SECO process navigation
5. **CHF cost restructuring** — CHF strength affecting competitiveness; companies needing operational efficiency and cost structure advisory
6. **Digital transformation recovery** — Failed or stalled transformation programs needing rescue advisory and recovery planning

## 01 — ICP: Swiss Multinational & Large Enterprise

## Target Segment: Swiss Large Enterprises & Multinationals (Geneva-Anchored)

### Company Profile
- **Size:** CHF 500M+ annual revenue; 500+ employees
- **Locations:** Geneva headquarters or major Swiss office; international operations
- **Sectors:** Trading, banking, insurance, pharma, commodities, multinational services
- **Status:** Under pressure from CSRD, geopolitical risk, digital transformation, and regulatory compliance

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|------|---------|-------------|
| CEO | Strategic leader | Geopolitical risk, regulatory change, digital transformation |
| CFO | Finance leader | CSRD reporting, cost structure, interim CFO needs |
| Chief Risk Officer / General Counsel | Risk/compliance | Geopolitical risk, FDI screening, regulatory compliance |
| Board / Audit Committee | Governance | ESG oversight, risk oversight, CSRD assurance |

### Pain Points (C125 Update)
1. **CSRD double materiality assessment** — Financial materiality + environmental/social impact assessment required; large companies need specialized advisory support
2. **Swiss geopolitical risk advisory** — US/EU/China tensions affecting Swiss multinational operations; need for structured geopolitical risk frameworks
3. **Interim CFO / finance function outsourcing** — Board-level CFO transitions, CFO departures, or crisis situations requiring experienced interim CFO support
4. **FDI screening for Swiss targets** — Foreign investment in Swiss companies with sensitive operations triggering SECO review; advisory needed for acquirers and targets
5. **CHF competitiveness and cost restructuring** — Ongoing CHF strength pressure on Swiss multinational cost base; efficiency and restructuring advisory
6. **Digital transformation governance** — Failed transformation programs; board-level oversight and rescue advisory needed
