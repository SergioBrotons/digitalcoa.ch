# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Consulting

## Key Regulatory Developments — Geneva Consulting (C125)

### Regulatory Item 01: CSRD Double Materiality Assessment
**Authority:** EU CSRD (effective); Swiss alignment in progress
**Status:** Large EU-listed Swiss companies in first CSRD reporting wave; Swiss alignment legislation advancing
**Key Requirements:**
- Double materiality assessment: financial materiality + impact materiality
- Materiality assessment drives disclosure scope
- EU taxonomy alignment for Swiss companies with EU operations
- SME supply chain cascade: large companies requiring supplier ESG data
**Timeline:** First Swiss CSRD reports due 2026-2027 (large companies)
**Advisory Implications:** Materiality assessment methodology, stakeholder engagement, data collection frameworks, reporting template development
**Source:** EU CSRD, Swiss Federal Council
