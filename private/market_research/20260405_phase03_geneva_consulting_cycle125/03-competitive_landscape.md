# 03 — Competitive Landscape: Geneva Consulting

## Geneva Consulting — Competitive Landscape (C125)

### Key Players

| Firm | Type | Strengths | Weaknesses | Geneva Relevance |
|------|------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| Big 4 (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY) | Global professional services | Scale, brand, CSRD/ESG practices | Expensive, generic | High — Geneva large enterprise |
| Top-tier strategy (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) | Strategy consulting | Strategy, transformation | Very expensive, limited SME | Medium — Geneva MNC |
| Swiss boutique consulting firms | Niche specialists | Sector knowledge, Swiss focus | Limited scale | High — Geneva SME/Mid-market |
| Interim CFO providers | Specialized | CFO search, finance function outsourcing | Limited broader consulting | High — SME/Mid-market CFO gap |
| Independent consultants | Solo/small | Flexibility, specialized knowledge | No scale, no brand | Medium |
| Geneva local consulting firms | Regional | Geneva relationships, French language | Limited international reach | High — Geneva domestic |

### Competitive Gaps

**Gap 01: CSRD Double Materiality Assessment**
Big 4 have CSRD practices but expensive. Swiss boutique firms with strong ESG expertise are underrepresented. Independent sustainability consultants lack capacity. Opportunity: specialized double materiality assessment practice for Geneva large enterprises and SME cascade market.

**Gap 02: Geopolitical Risk Advisory**
Swiss-focused geopolitical risk advisory is fragmented. International risk firms (Control Risks, Marsh, Eurasia) have Swiss presence but Geneva commodity trading and multinational advisory is underserved. Opportunity: Geneva-centric geopolitical risk framework advisory for trading houses and multinationals.

**Gap 03: Interim CFO Finance Function Outsourcing**
Market growing but fragmented. No clear Geneva-focused market leader for interim CFO and finance function outsourcing. Recruitment firms and independent CFOs doing this ad hoc. Opportunity: integrated interim CFO + finance function support for Geneva SME/mid-market.

**Gap 04: FDI Screening Advisory**
SECO FDI screening advisory is nascent. Transaction advisors (investment banks, M&A boutiques) have some expertise but SECO-specific advisory is underdeveloped. Opportunity: specialized FDI screening advisory for Geneva M&A transactions.

**Gap 05: Digital Transformation Recovery**
Different skill set from transformation implementation. Most firms focus on launching transformations, not rescuing failed ones. Independent turnaround advisors and specialized firms exist but Geneva market underserved. Opportunity: transformation rescue practice for Geneva companies.
