### 1. EDÖB AI Guidance — No Update Since September 2025 ✓

**Source:** edoeb.admin.ch  
**Status:** The September 24, 2025 AI and Data Protection guidance ("KI und Datenschutz") confirmed as the most current EDÖB position. No new AI-specific guidance published as of April 3, 2026.

**EDÖB confirmed position (from Sep 2025, still current):**
- Swiss Data Protection Act (DSG) directly applicable to AI-supported data processing
- No separate AI law required
- Transparency obligation: chatbot disclosure, right to object to training data
- DSFA (Data Protection Impact Assessment) required for high-risk AI
- Social scoring and mass surveillance prohibited
- X/Grok case closed March 20, 2025 (no further action)

**Timeline consistency:** Nov 2023 → May 2025 → Sep 2025 → No further EDÖB AI publications as of Apr 3, 2026

**Analysis (EN):**  
EDÖB has taken a wait-and-see approach after September 2025. No new enforcement actions, guidance updates, or publications since. The regulatory baseline remains: DSG applies directly to AI, no separate AI law, DSFA required for high-risk. Organizations that haven't acted since September 2025 are now significantly behind EDÖB's stated requirements. The window for proactive compliance positioning is now.

**Analysis (FR):**  
L'EDÖB maintient son approche attentiste après septembre 2025. La ligne de base réglementaire reste : le LPD s'applique directement à l'IA, aucune loi IA séparée requise, AEPD nécessaire pour l'IA à haut risque.
