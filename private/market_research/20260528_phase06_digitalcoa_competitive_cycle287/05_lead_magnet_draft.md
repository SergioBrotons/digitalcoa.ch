## Format and Delivery Notes

**Briefing structure (90 minutes):**
1. Diagnostic: What accountability moments are active in your practice right now (15 min)
2. Mapping: What the documentation window requires specifically for your situation (30 min)
3. Prioritization: What to address first and why (15 min)
4. Working session: Specific questions you brought to the briefing (30 min)
5. Summary: What you've learned, what to do next, what to defer (10 min)

**Pre-briefing preparation:**
- ICP completes a 10-question diagnostic questionnaire (sent with booking confirmation)
- Sergio reviews the questionnaire 24 hours before the briefing
- The briefing is not a discovery call — it is a structured diagnostic working session

**Post-briefing:**
- Summary note sent within 24 hours: what was addressed, what was decided, what the next action is
- Optional: 60-minute follow-up working session (CHF 1,200) for ICPs who want to begin evidence architecture building immediately

**Lead magnet as first qualification:**
The briefing format qualifies the ICP before the engagement begins. The professional who books a 90-minute briefing is demonstrating a specific accountability position: they have a specific moment, they need a specific answer, and they are willing to invest in a structured conversation to get it. This is the right ICP. The professional who wants training, a framework presentation, or a vendor relationship is not.
