# 04 — Regulatory Context
## Geneva Consulting

1. **CSRD — Phase 2 Implementation**

   - ESRS taxonomy alignment: Swiss EU-listed entities
   - Limited assurance requirement: 2026-2027
   - Geneva SME supply chain compliance: downstream pressure

2. **DORA — EU ICT Risk Management**
   - ICT third-party risk management: critical providers

   - Geneva financial institutions: most exposed
   - ICT incident reporting: operational resilience

3. **Switzerland-EU Institutional Agreement**
   - Q2 2026 critical negotiation phase

   - Financial services passporting questions

4. **Basel IV / FAIR**
   - Capital planning advisory window
   - SME credit risk frameworks
   - Geneva banks: RWA floor preparation

5. **Swiss Board Governance**
   - 40% board diversity quota: implementation pressure

6. **Geopolitical Risk**
   - US tariff escalation: SERV export finance demand
   - Russia/Ukraine: sanctions compliance demand
   - Switzerland-EU: bilateral III uncertainty
   - China/US strategic competition: Geneva trade impact
