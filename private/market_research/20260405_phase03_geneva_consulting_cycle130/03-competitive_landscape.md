# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## Geneva Consulting

**Direct Competitors:**
- Big 4 strategy + risk (McKinsey, BCG, Bain on strategy): PwC, Deloitte, KPMG, EY risk practice
- Regional boutiques: Arendi (Geneva), Ferrande (Geneva), Zweiweg (Zurich)
- Interim management firms: executive interim partners, Swiss Interim
- Regulatory consultancies: various specialist firms
- Law firm consulting arms: Lenz & Staehelin advisory, Walder Wyss consulting

**Differentiation Gap:**
- Switzerland-EU institutional bridge advisory for SMEs: under-served
- Geopolitical risk advisory with Geneva commodity trading focus: limited
- CSRD implementation without Big 4 audit conflict: independent preferred
- Digital transformation failure rescue: specialized niche
- Family business governance + succession: under-served in Geneva
- DORA ICT TPRM for Swiss entities with EU exposure: nascent

**Positioning Opportunity:**
- Geneva-centric regulatory change management
- Switzerland-EU SME strategic advisory
- Commodity trading operational risk advisory
- Independent CSRD/DORA implementation (no audit conflict)
- Family business governance specialist

**Market Structure:**
- Geneva consulting market: estimated CHF 2-3B annually
- SME segment (sub-250 employees): under-served by premium advisory
- Family businesses: 90%+ of Swiss companies; succession wave 2025-2035
- Commodity trading: 500+ companies Geneva; operational risk advisory demand
