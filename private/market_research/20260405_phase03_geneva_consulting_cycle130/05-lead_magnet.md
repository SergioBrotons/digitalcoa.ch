# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## Geneva Consulting

**Target:** Managing Directors, CFOs, and Board Members of Geneva SMEs and family businesses

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Geneva SME Regulatory Change Radar (Quarterly)**
- Format: 4-page executive briefing
- Content: Top 5 regulatory changes affecting Geneva SMEs, action framework, timeline
- CTA: Regulatory readiness assessment
- Distribution: CCIG channels + direct email

**Option B: Family Business Governance Briefing (On-Demand)**
- Format: 3-page decision brief
- Content: Succession readiness, board governance, family constitution frameworks
- CTA: Family business governance consultation
- Timing: Q4 planning + ad hoc

**Option C: Switzerland-EU Bilateral III — SME Impact Analysis (On-Demand)**
- Format: Situation analysis + scenario planning
- Content: Bilateral III scenarios, SME implications, contingency planning
- CTA: Scenario planning workshop
- Timing: Q2 2026 critical

**Option D: Geopolitical Risk Geneva Briefing (Monthly/Quarterly)**
- Format: 2-page briefing
- Content: Tariff/sanctions developments, Geneva trade impact, risk mitigation
- CTA: Risk mitigation consultation

**Recommended Primary:** Option A (Regulatory Radar for SMEs) — broadest ICP reach
**Recommended Secondary:** Option C (Bilateral III Impact) — timing-critical, high relevance

**Bilingual:** EN primary, FR available
