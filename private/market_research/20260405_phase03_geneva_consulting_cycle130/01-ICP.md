# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva Consulting — ICP

**Firm Types:**
- Geneva-based management consultancies (5-50 consultants)
- Boutique regulatory consulting firms
- Professional services firms (accounting + advisory)
- Interim management providers
- Swiss SME family businesses (Geneva/Vaud)
- Commodity trading house operational functions
- International organization procurement/operations

**Decision-Maker:**
- Managing Director / CEO
- CFO / Finance Director
- Board of Directors
- Procurement / Operations Director
- Family Business Principal

**Pain Points:**
- Regulatory change management overload (CSRD, DORA, Data Act, Swiss AI Act)
- Geopolitical risk advisory: tariff escalation, sanctions, Switzerland-EU
- Digital transformation program failure recovery
- CSRD double materiality assessment implementation
- Switzerland-EU institutional agreement SME strategic planning
- M&A due diligence: regulatory + operational
- Interim specialized talent gaps (CRO, CFO, compliance)
- Board ESG governance compliance (40% quota + FINMA expectations)

**Urgency Triggers:**
- Regulatory deadline approaching
- Board ESG governance review
- M&A transaction initiation
- Digital transformation failure
- Geopolitical disruption event
- Family business succession planning

**Geography:** Geneva, Vaud, Western Switzerland
**Language:** EN primary, FR essential, DE useful
**Budget Cycle:** Q4 + ad hoc project-based
