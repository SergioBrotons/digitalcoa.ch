## Regulatory Context

### GAIGI — Operative ~7–10 Months (Fall 2025 + March 2026)
**Status**: Operative — category awareness established, SMB gap intact
**Geneva relevance**: First-mover positioning opportunity for small RE firms

### nFADP Art. 5(c) — Active Since 2023
**Status**: ACTIVE NOW
**Obligation**: AI making automated decisions about individuals (tenants, buyers, sellers) requires DPIA + human review

### Swissdamed — July 1, 2026 (~12 weeks)
**Status**: Spillover effect
**RE relevance**: Real estate firms using AI diagnostic tools (property inspection AI, valuation AI) may face Swissdamed obligations

### EU AI Act GPAI — Active Since August 2, 2025 (~8 months)
**Status**: ACTIVE NOW
**RE relevance**: RE firms with EU property clients, EU market operations, or EU-intended outputs face immediate GPAI obligations

### EU AI Act High-Risk — August 2, 2026 (~4.5 months)
**Status**: Approaching
**RE relevance**: High-risk AI systems used in property valuation, tenant screening may face high-risk obligations
