## Section 3: What You're Already Legally Obligated to Do

### nFADP Art. 5(c) — Active Since 2023
If your AI makes decisions about individuals — tenants, buyers, sellers — based solely on automated processing, you need a documented DPIA and human review mechanism.

### EU AI Act GPAI — Active Since August 2, 2025 (~8 months)
If your agency has EU clients, EU property transactions, or outputs intended for EU markets: your GPAI transparency obligations are already live.

The Geneva AI Group Institute framework is operative. Category awareness is established. SMB gap is intact — you're in a first-mover window.
