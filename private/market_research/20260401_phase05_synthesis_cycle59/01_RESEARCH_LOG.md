### 2. FINMA Survey Confirms Systemic AI Adoption + Incomplete Governance
**Source:** Phase 02 C59

FINMA formal survey (Nov 2024-Jan 2025, ~400 institutions):

- Only 50% have explicit AI strategy

- Top AI risks: data quality, data protection, explainability

Cross-sectoral implication: The governance gap is systemic, not sector-specific. Every sector has firms operating AI without documented frameworks.

EU AI Act high-risk obligations fully enforced August 2026 for asset managers. Cross-border regulatory convergence confirmed: Federal AI Ordinance + FINMA guidance + EU AI Act. Consulting firms advising EU clients need EU-compliant AI governance frameworks.

Swissdamed July 1, 2026: ~90 days. Swissmedic leadership enforcement posture less predictable. Three-layer data protection framework: FADP + Swissdamed + Federal AI Ordinance. Data minimization vs. AI utility tension most acute in healthcare.
