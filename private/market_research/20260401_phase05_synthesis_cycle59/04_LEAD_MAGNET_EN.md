## The Three-Layer Convergence

Every Geneva firm faces three concurrent frameworks — regardless of sector:

**Layer 1 — FADP (Swiss Data Protection Law)**
Patient data, client data, counterparty data — all protected. AI tools processing this data must comply.

**Layer 2 — Federal AI Ordinance (January 1, 2025 — 15+ months operative)**
Human oversight of AI-assisted decisions. Documentation requirements. Emerging enforcement posture.

**Layer 3 — Sector-Specific Obligations**
- Healthcare: Swissdamed (July 1, 2026 — 90 days)
- Finance: FINMA guidance + EU AI Act (August 2, 2026 — ~4 months)
- Legal: SBA guidelines + Section 301 (April 15, 2026 — 14 days)
- Consulting: GPAI readiness (August 2, 2026 — ~4 months)

Most firms are exposed across all three layers without a documented framework.
