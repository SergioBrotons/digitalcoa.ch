## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors (Content Platforms)

**LinkedIn Newsletter / Geneva Professionals**
- Institutional thought leadership distribution
- ICP perception: algorithm-driven; professional but generic
- Brotons.net differentiation: curated institutional intelligence vs. algorithmic feed

**Substack / Geneva Writers**
- Individual professional newsletters
- ICP perception: personal perspective; variable quality; limited institutional depth
- Brotons.net differentiation: institutional intelligence platform with primary source credibility

**Swiss Romandie Publications (Le Temps, Tribune de Genève, RSR)**
- General media; limited Geneva institutional depth
- ICP perception: broad audience; insufficient regulatory precision
- Brotons.net differentiation: Geneva-specific regulatory intelligence depth

**IHEID/CCIG/SFAMA Publications**
- Institutional but limited distribution; peer-focused
- Brotons.net differentiation: wider distribution + Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch pathway

## Competitive Differentiation Angles

| Competitor Type | Weakness | Brotons.net Positioning |

| Swiss Romandie media | Broad audience; insufficient depth | Geneva regulatory precision; EN+FR bilingual |
