### Key Competitive Insight: Large Firms Help Build the Case
Large firms publishing AI guidance, FDPIC analysis, and regulatory trackers serve to:
1. Build awareness of AI governance obligations (market education)
2. Position for enterprise AI governance mandates when they arrive
3. Do NOT serve or advise small law firms (2–5 attorneys) on AI governance

**The gap remains**: No boutique Geneva firm or independent advisor is addressing the small law firm AI governance segment.

### Swiss Law Firm AI Adoption Trend
Increasing adoption of generative AI by law firms globally (100,000 potential hours by 2036 per firm benchmark). This expands the addressable market for AI governance advisory — but no one is serving the small firm segment in Geneva.

### OFL Registry Data
~150–250 small law firms in Geneva with AI exposure (estimated 20–30% of OFL-registered firms). None are being served by boutique advisors on AI governance.
