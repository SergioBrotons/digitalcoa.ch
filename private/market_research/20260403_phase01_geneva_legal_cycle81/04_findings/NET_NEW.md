3. **Collective force of proposal:** Promote specialized courts (Tribunal de commerce upcoming); magistrate election reform; attorney privilege protection against surveillance/interception
- **Source:** Same odage.ch editorial
- **Significance:** Strategic angles — digital sovereignty, attorney privilege, specialized courts

- **Significance:** Institutional evolution relevant to Geneva commercial litigation
