# 01-ICP.md — Brotons.net

## Brotons.net — ICP Definition

### Primary ICP: Geneva Executive Decision-Makers
- **Profiles:** GCL/GC at Geneva FS institutions; CFO/Treasurer at Geneva commodity traders, banks, insurers; Managing Partner at Geneva law firms; Executive Directors at Geneva IOs/NGOs
- **Decision role:** Strategic decision-maker, advisor, or gatekeeper for external advisory procurement
- **Information consumption:** Scan mode for discovery (LinkedIn, newsletter); deep-read for decision-support (direct website visit); peer recommendation for trust transfer
- **Language:** EN-primary for international/regulatory; FR-primary for Swiss domestic/relationship
- **Trigger:** Regulatory uncertainty; strategic decision requiring external perspective; peer recommendation
- **Trust marker:** Author credibility, institutional affiliation, primary source citation, no commercial clutter

### Secondary ICP: Geneva Institutional Professionals
- Senior professionals at Geneva institutions (IHEID faculty, SFAMA, Geneva Bar Association, CCIG, ICRC/WFP procurement)
- Professionals who influence or make advisory procurement decisions
- Value: Professional development + institutional intelligence

### Tertiary ICP: Swiss Romandie Business Community
- French-speaking Swiss business professionals outside Geneva (Lausanne, Neuchâtel, Fribourg)
- Value: Swiss Romandie-specific regulatory and business intelligence
- Language: FR-primary; EN as secondary

### What Brotons.net Does NOT Want
- Generic AI-generated content without primary source evidence
- Commercial advertising or promotional framing
- US/UK-centric analysis without Swiss regulatory nuance
- Content that feels like marketing rather than intelligence

### Strategic Implication
Brotons.net is a thought leadership and intelligence platform — not a marketing channel. Its value is a function of editorial credibility, primary source quality, and institutional trust. Growth comes from institutional citation, peer recommendation, and SEO discovery — not advertising or paid promotion.
