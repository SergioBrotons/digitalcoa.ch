# 05-Lead Magnet — Brotons.net | C136

## Brotons.net — Lead Magnet Concepts | C136

### Lead Magnet Option A: "Geneva Institutional Intelligence Briefing — Quarterly Subscription"
**Format:** Quarterly 8-page briefing — EN + FR bilingual
**Content:** Primary source analysis of Geneva multilateral developments: WTO outcomes, UN agency decisions, Swiss regulatory changes, Geneva institutional calendar highlights — all with executive action implications
**CTA:** "Subscribe to the Geneva Institutional Intelligence Quarterly — primary source analysis, no noise"
**Why it works:** Converts content consumption into structured subscription relationship; primary source quality differentiates from free Geneva institutional newsletters; bilingual EN+FR covers full Swiss Romandie audience; quarterly cadence drives recurring touchpoints

### Lead Magnet Option B: "Swiss Romandie Executive Regulatory Guide — Annual 2026 Edition"
**Format:** Annual publication (48 pages) — FR-primary with EN executive summary
**Content:** Comprehensive guide to regulatory landscape for Swiss Romandie executives: AI liability, CSRD, BVG reform, sanctions, FADP, trade compliance, carbon markets — all Geneva-relevant, FR-authored, with practitioner checklists
**CTA:** "Request the 2026 Swiss Romandie Regulatory Guide — free for Geneva executives"
**Why it works:** High perceived value; establishes Brotons.net as the definitive Swiss Romandie regulatory intelligence source; drives institutional distribution (CCIG, AER, IHEID alumni); evergreen value (annual edition)

### Lead Magnet Option C: "Brotons.net Geneva Conference Intelligence — Pre-Event Briefing"
**Format:** Pre-event 2-page briefing — EN primary
**Content:** 2-3 week pre-event intelligence: what Geneva executives should know before the WTO Public Forum / WIPO Assemblies / WHO EB session — with primary source documents and institutional context
**CTA:** "Register for pre-event Geneva intelligence briefing — [Event Name]"
**Why it works:** Unique format competitors do not offer; directly serves Geneva executive institutional calendar need; converts event-driven audience to ongoing reader; builds event partnership opportunity

### Recommended Priority: Lead Magnet Option A (Quarterly Institutional Intelligence Briefing)
**Rationale:** Closest to Brotons.net core editorial identity; converts content engagement into structured subscription; establishes Brotons.net as the primary Geneva institutional intelligence provider; drives recurring touchpoints without editorial production overload; bilingual naturally fits quarterly format.

### Content Production Requirements
- Format: 8-page quarterly briefing, EN+FR bilingual
- Sources: admin.ch, FINMA, SNB, WTO, UNOG, WIPO, ITU, WHO, SECO primary documents
- Production: 4x/year (Q1-Q4 2026)
- First edition: Q2 2026 (available mid-May 2026)
- Distribution: Direct email subscription + Brotons.net gated download

### Distribution Strategy
1. Existing newsletter subscribers — upsell to quarterly subscription tier
2. Brotons.net website — gated download with email capture
3. IHEID alumni network — institutional distribution partnership
4. CCIG member briefings — inclusion (pending partnership)
5. LinkedIn newsletter — awareness + subscription CTA
6. Geneva institutional events — physical/digital event distribution

### Conversion Path
Quarterly Briefing download → Day 10 follow-up (briefing review + feedback) → Day 30 subscription confirmation → Day 60 introduction to broader Brotons.net advisory network → Advisory engagement pathway

### Institutional Citation Strategy Integration
The Quarterly Institutional Intelligence Briefing is specifically designed for institutional citation: IHEID faculty can reference it in publications; SFAMA members can cite it in conference proceedings; CCIG can distribute via member channels. The citation network activation strategy is embedded in the product design.
