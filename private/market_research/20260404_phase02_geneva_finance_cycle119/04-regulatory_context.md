# 04 — Regulatory Context
**Phase:** Phase 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C119 | **Date:** 2026-04-04

## Regulatory Context: Geneva Finance — C119

### Swiss Banking / AML
- **FINMA BankO Art. 37** — enhanced AML measures for correspondent banking
- **Russian asset freezing** — SECO sanctions implementation, OFSI-equivalent enforcement
- **FINMA Circular 2011/5** — AML compliance framework, risk-based approach
- **Key implication:** Enhanced due diligence demand, Swiss banks needing AML framework upgrades

### Basel IV / FAIR
- **FAIR (domestic implementation of Basel IV)** — 72.5% RWA floor confirmed
- Credit capacity implications for commodity traders and corporate borrowers
- RWA optimization demand — structured finance, security packages
- **Key implication:** Basel IV advisory, credit structure optimization

### BVG Reform (Pension Funds)
- **BVG reform (Occupational Pensions Act revision)** — in implementation phase
- New pension formula (BVG 21) — lower conversion rate, funding implications
- Investment rule changes — increased illiquid asset allocation permitted
- **Key implication:** Investment policy advisory, manager selection, governance

### Cross-Border Fund Distribution
- **FINMA Circular on Fund Distribution** — cross-border marketing compliance
- **Swiss Fund Distribution Agreement** — bilateral arrangements with EU/UK
- **MiFID interface** — Swiss distribution rights, passport complexity
- **Key implication:** Fund documentation, cross-border marketing compliance, Swiss distribution agreements

### Commodity Trade Finance
- **Basel IV impact** — RWA for commodity trade finance increasing
- **Swiss Structured Trade Finance** — receivables, inventory, prepayment structures
- **USTR (Uniform Customs and Practice)** — documentary credit standardization
- **Key implication:** Structured finance advisory, Basel IV optimization, documentation

### Swiss Fintech
- **DLT Act (Distributed Ledger Technology)** — tokenization, DLT securities
- **Payment Institution License (PI)** — PSD2/EMD2 interface
- **FINMA Sandbox** — innovation-enabling framework
- **Key implication:** Fintech licensing advisory, sandbox strategy
