## Active Regulatory Framework

### 1. EU AI Act — GPAI Obligations Operative Since August 2, 2025
- **Status:** OPERATIVE — GPAI obligations active since August 2, 2025
- **Content:** Transparency, acceptable use policies, copyright compliance for GPAI model providers and deployers
- **Geneva consulting implication:** Consulting firms using EU-market GPAI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, etc.) are GPAI deployers — obligations apply NOW
- **High-risk obligations:** Operative August 2, 2026 (~15 months)
- **Key GPAI obligations for consulting firms:**
  - Transparency: disclose AI-generated content to clients
  - Copyright: ensure AI outputs don't infringe third-party IP
  - Technical documentation: maintain records of AI system usage
  - Incident reporting: report serious AI incidents to authorities

### 2. EU AI Act — High-Risk Obligations (August 2, 2026)
- **Status:** ~15 months from now
- **Content:** High-risk AI systems face stricter requirements: conformity assessment, risk management, data governance, transparency, human oversight, accuracy
- **Geneva consulting implication:** IT/tech consultants using AI in high-risk sectors (finance, healthcare, legal — all present in Geneva) face high-risk obligations
- **Dual compliance:** EU AI Act high-risk + nFADP = two independent compliance frameworks
