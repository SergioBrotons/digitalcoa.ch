## Key Market Signals — Geneva Asset Management / Family Offices

- **Signal:** Capital optimization advisory — helping IAMs understand counterparty bank resilience

### 2. SARON Transition Completeness + Rate Environment
- SARON (Swiss Average Rate Overnight) fully established as reference rate for CHF mortgages and corporate loans
- SARON compound averages replaced legacy LIBOR references by 2022; transition complete
- SNB policy rate held at 0.25% (March 2026 assessment); franc remains strong
- Interest rate hedging demand: 10-year fixed mortgages at 2.5–3.5% creating refinancing discussions
- **Signal:** Rate advisory and hedging strategy for Geneva real estate portfolios (aligned with C100 finding 04)

### 3. ESG / SFDR Disclosure — FINMA Greenwashing Enforcement Active
- EU SFDR (Sustainable Finance Disclosure Regulation) applies to Swiss UCITS/AIFMs marketing to EU investors

- Geneva private banks (Pictet AM, Lombard Odier, Swisscanto) under EU regulator scrutiny for sustainability claims
- Anti-boycott risk: ESG strategies that exclude certain sectors may face anti-boycott regulations
- **Signal:** ESG label defensibility reviews, taxonomy gap analysis (Swiss vs. EU), SFDR Article 8/9 fund compliance

### 4. Switzerland — Russia / Sanctions Enforcement Escalation
- SECO (State Secretariat for Economic Affairs) maintains Switzerland's independent Russia sanctions regime

- Geneva private banks: continued pressure to wind down Russia-related mandates
- Sanctions compliance consulting demand: UBO identification, transaction monitoring for Russia exposure
- **Signal:** AML/sanctions compliance reviews for Geneva wealth managers with historical Russia exposure

### 5. OECD Pillar 2 / Global Minimum Tax — Structuring Implications
- Switzerland enacted Pillar 2 (Global Minimum Tax) via MTAA (Minimum Tax Act); effective 2024
- 15% minimum effective tax rate for large multinational groups (>$750M revenue threshold)
- UHNWI family holding structures (Swiss AG, foundation, trusts) may face substance scrutiny
- Geneva family offices advising clients with qualifying revenues: Pillar 2 implications for investment structures
- Canton of Geneva offering competitive tax rulings — but Pillar 2 limits aggressive planning
- **Signal:** Family holding structure review, substance design, cantonal tax ruling renewal

### 6. DLT Act / Tokenization — Early Infrastructure Emerging
- Switzerland DLT Act (in force since 2021): legal framework for uncertificated digital securities
- SIX Digital Exchange (SDX) operational: institutional-grade tokenized securities infrastructure
- FINMA licensing pathway for DLT trading facilities established

- Stablecoin guidelines under FinIA: Federal Council aiming to finalize 2026
- **Signal:** DLT/tokenization strategy for asset managers, stablecoin custody compliance advisory

### 7. 13th AHV Pension — January 2026 Implementation
- Swiss Parliament approved 13th AHV pension; annual payment in December starting 2026
- Affects all AHV beneficiaries — including wealthy UHNWI with AHV contributions
- BVG (occupational pension) reform rejected by referendum September 2024 — no imminent 2nd pillar change
- Minimum BVG interest rate maintained at 1.25% (2025); key figures largely unchanged for 2026
- Pillar 3a: retroactive top-up payments allowed from January 2025 for contribution gaps
- **Signal:** Wealth planning integration with AHV 13th pension; Pillar 3a optimization
