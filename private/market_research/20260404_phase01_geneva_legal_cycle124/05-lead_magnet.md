# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Commodity Trading Disputes Advisory

## Strategic Rationale
Geneva is the world's #1 commodity trading hub. Law firms with commodity trading practices are experiencing a surge in CHF/USD volatility-driven disputes. Most have no systematic advisory framework for this specific market moment. A targeted lead magnet — practical, Geneva-specific, immediately useful — positions the firm as the go-to resource without requiring client interviews or primary research.

## Lead Magnet Concept

### English Version
**Title:** Geneva Commodity Trading Dispute Tracker — Q1/Q2 2026
**Format:** PDF briefing + live spreadsheet (updated monthly)
**Length:** 8-12 pages + appendix
**Distribution:** Direct email to Geneva trading house GCs, CFOs, General Counsel networks; Geneva Bar Association member mailing; IBA Geneva 2026 pre-conference distribution
