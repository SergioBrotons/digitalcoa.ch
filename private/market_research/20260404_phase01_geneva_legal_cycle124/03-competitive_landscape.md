## Competitive Dynamics (C124 Update)

### New Entrants / Shifts
1. **Deloitte Legal Geneva** — CBAM/SVUT customs advisory team forming; leveraging global sustainability practice
2. **Big 4 Trade Finance Practices** — PwC and KPMG growing commodity trading contract advisory in Geneva
3. **Legal Tech (Relativity/Everlaw)** — expanding Geneva presence for e-discovery in commodity disputes; AI-assisted review becoming standard
4. **CBAM Specialist Advisors** — new boutique consultancies emerging (e.g., Carbon Legal Advisory, Geneva-based) focusing on carbon certificate valuation disputes
5. **Digital Notarization Vendors** — SwissNotar, Prillye, Certum positioning for digital deed market before framework finalization

### Positioning Gaps
- **CHF/USD volatility dispute advisory** — no Geneva firm has published a systematic advisory framework on commodity contract re-opener clause analysis in the context of CHF strength
- **CBAM/SVUT customs penalty defense** — very few Geneva lawyers have hands-on experience; market gap
- **Digital notarization advisory** — most firms waiting for final rules; early-mover advantage available
- **BGFA Art. 28 foreign lawyer admission** — narrow window for advisory before consultation closes
- **EU-CH cross-border data transfer for litigation** — treated as IT issue, not legal advisory
