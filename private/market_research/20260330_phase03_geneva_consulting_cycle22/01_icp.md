# ICP — Phase 03: Geneva Consulting / Small Firms
## Cycle 22 | 2026-03-30

## Vertical Definition
Small consulting firms (2–20 employees) and independent consultants operating in Geneva, specializing in management consulting, IT consulting, strategy consulting, and professional services. Firms that advise clients on business decisions while increasingly using AI tools internally and for client deliverables.

## Ideal Client Profile
- **Who:** Partners, principals, and senior consultants at small consulting firms in Geneva
- **Pain:** Facing dual compliance pressure (nFADP + EU AI Act) without clear guidance; GPAI transparency obligations already active; May 2026 Federal Council First Draft ~2 months away; 40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI skills
- **Behavior:** Already using AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, etc.) for client work without formal AI governance policy
- **Trigger:** GPAI transparency obligations active since August 2, 2025 (~8.5 months); Federal Council May 2026 First Draft ~2 months
- **Geography:** Geneva canton, Geneva Lake Leman region
- **Decision-maker:** Partners, principals, practice heads
