# Regulatory Context — Phase 03: Geneva Consulting
## Cycle 22 | 2026-03-30

## Dual Compliance Framework (Confirmed)

### Layer 1: Swiss Domestic — nFADP
- **nFADP** (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection) — fully operative
- Art. 5(c): Data processing must be lawful, proportionate; AI-based processing requires legal basis
- Art. 5(f): Automated decision-making requires transparency and right to human review
- Enforcement: FDPIC (Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner)

### Layer 2: EU Extraterritorial — EU AI Act
- **GPAI (General Purpose AI) — ACTIVE SINCE AUGUST 2, 2025 (~8.5 months)**
  - Transparency obligations already operative
  - Applies to firms with EU nexus (EU clients, EU-intended outputs, EU import/distribution)
- **High-Risk Obligations — August 2, 2026 (~16 months)**
  - High-risk AI system obligations activate August 2, 2026
  - ~9 months advisory window remaining

### Federal Council "Lean Approach" (Confirmed)
- NO cross-sector Swiss AI law before end-2026
- Sector-specific guidance fills the gap (FINMA, SBA, FDPIC)
- Federal Council First Draft AI Action Plan May 2026 (~2 months)
