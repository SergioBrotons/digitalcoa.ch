## Regulatory Context: Geneva Boutique Wealth Management AI Governance

### Layer 1: Swiss Domestic Obligations

**FinSA/FinSO Conduct Rules**
- Operative since January 1, 2025
- Client suitability, disclosure, documentation obligations
- AI-assisted advice must meet same standards as human advice

**FINMA Circ. 2026/1 (Nature Risks)**
- Operative since January 1, 2026
- Nature-related risks in financial services
- AI applications in ESG/sustainable investing may trigger additional obligations

**SRO Membership Obligations**
- AMLA scope expansion under review: lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs triggering SRO membership discussions
- AI governance becoming a de facto SRO review criterion

### Layer 2: Swiss International Obligations

**CARF/CRS 2.0**
- Status: LIVE OPERATIVE since January 1, 2026 (~3 months already)
- Automated exchange of financial account information
- AI applications in data collection, processing, and reporting within scope
- Retroactive compliance exposure: 3 months already operative

**AMLA/LETA**
- Status: H2 2026 (~3-6 months away)
- Implementing ordinance expected
- FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027 creating pressure
- 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register (LETA)
- SRO membership expansion implications

**FATF Mutual Evaluation**
- Status: 2026-2027
- Switzerland's evaluation elevates AMLA to international scrutiny
- Wealth managers are a primary evaluation target
- FINMA/SRO enforcement posture expected to tighten during evaluation period

### Layer 3: EU Extraterritorial

**EU AI Act**
- Status: High-risk obligations activate August 2, 2026 (~4.5 months)
- Applies if: EU client nexus, EU-directed output, AI systems processing EU data subjects
- High-risk categories: AI in employment (hiring/promotion), access to essential services (financial)
- Wealth managers with EU clients or EU-directed investment advice likely in scope

**GDPR + EU AI Act Overlap**
- Client financial data + AI = dual GDPR + EU AI Act obligations
- Data protection impact assessment required for high-risk AI

### Other Relevant Regulations

**Swissdamed (July 1 2026 — ~13 WEEKS)**
- Spillover: Healthcare clients of WM cascade Swissdamed questions
- Wealth managers with healthcare entrepreneur clients face immediate Swissdamed-related questions

**SIF Sustainable Finance (March 27 2026)**
- "Major opportunity" confirmed by SIF
- AI applications in sustainable investing = government-recognized priority
