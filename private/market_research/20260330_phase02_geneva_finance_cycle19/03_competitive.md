### Competitive White Space Summary
**The confirmed gap:**
- **Target:** Geneva boutique wealth managers (CHF 200M–2B AUM; 3-12 staff)
- **Need:** Practical AI governance for portfolio AI, client reporting AI, risk modeling AI
- **Price point:** CHF 5,000–25,000 for initial AI governance assessment/framework
- **Format:** AI governance workshop, compliance gap assessment, AI policy framework

**What exists:** AMLA compliance advisors, FinSA/FinSO advisors, SRO compliance firms, Big 4 enterprise compliance
**What does NOT exist:** Boutique WM-specific AI governance advisory in Geneva
