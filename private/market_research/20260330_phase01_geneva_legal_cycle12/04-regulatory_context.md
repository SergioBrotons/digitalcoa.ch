## Regulatory Context — Phase 01 Cycle 12

### Active Now (Geneva Legal):
- **nFADP Art. 5(c):** Active since Sept 1, 2023 — transparency obligations for AI-based data processing
- **SBA Guidelines (June 2024):** Non-binding AI use framework for Swiss lawyers — 3 acceptable methods under professional secrecy and data protection
- **FDPIC enforcement trajectory:** 4 AI investigations 2025-2026 (law firms NOT targeted = timing signal)

### Near-Term (2026):
- **Swissdamed Jul 1, 2026:** ~12 weeks — data processing obligations spill over to law firms handling health data
- **EU AI Act Aug 2, 2026:** ~4-5 months — transparency obligations for EU-nexus firms; #1 forcing function
- **AMLA/LETA H2 2026:** ~3-6 months — law firm clients (wealth management, corporate) face AML obligations

### Medium-Term (end-2026):
- **FDJP consultation draft:** End-2026 — CoE AI Convention implementation bill (transparency, data protection, non-discrimination, oversight)
- **Federal Council Digital Switzerland Strategy 2026:** December 2025 — AI priorities: digital sovereignty, digital host state, e-ID
- **DETEC/OFCOM non-binding governance plan:** End-2026 — voluntary codes, self-declaration mechanisms
- **Federal Council lean approach confirmed:** No cross-sector binding Swiss AI law before end-2026

### Key Cycle 12 Additions:
