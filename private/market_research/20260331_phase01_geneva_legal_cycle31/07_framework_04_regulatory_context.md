## Active Regulatory Landscape

### nFADP / FADP (Swiss Data Protection)
- **Status**: Operative since September 1, 2023
- **AI applicability**: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing without new legislation (FDPIC May 8, 2025 explicit confirmation)
- **Key obligations**: Purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, security, accountability
- **FDPIC enforcement**: 1,053 complaints 2024/2025; 6 enforcement actions 2025-2026; law firms NOT yet targeted
- **Expected disclosure**: Purpose + functionality + data sources for AI deployments (FDPIC guidance)

### FDPIC AI Guidance
- **Status**: Active and operative
- **Source**: edoeb.admin.ch
- **Expectations**: Purpose disclosure, functionality disclosure, data sources disclosure for AI
- **Enforcement pattern**: Transport → retail → social media → (next: law firms?)
- **Key risk**: Criminal liability CHF 250,000 for intentional nFADP failures

### Federal Council AI Action Plan
- **Timeline**:
  - Zero Draft: January 2026
  - Second consultation: January 20, 2026 CONFIRMED
  - First Draft: May 2026 CONFIRMED
  - Final: End-2026 (FDJP formal)
- **Approach**: Sector-specific, lean, principles-based
- **No cross-sector AI law** before end-2026 confirmed

### EU AI Act
- **High-risk obligations**: Active since August 2, 2025 (GPAI) / August 2, 2026 (full high-risk)
- **Extraterritorial reach**: Swiss firms with EU clients, EU-nexus activities, or placing AI on EU market
- **Geneva exposure**: Law firms with EU clients or EU-nexus matters subject to high-risk obligations
- **Key obligations**: Transparency, human oversight, accuracy, robustness, documentation

### GPAI (General Purpose AI)
- **Status**: Operative since August 2, 2025 (~8 months active)
- **Obligations**: Transparency for GPAI systems
- **Geneva exposure**: Law firms using ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini for legal work

### Professional Obligations
- **SBA AI Guidelines**: June 2024 non-binding baseline (confidentiality, competence, non-discrimination, human oversight)
- **ANBLES AI Working Group**: February 2026 confirmed (Swiss bar association signal)
- **Criminal liability**: CHF 250,000 for intentional FADP failures
