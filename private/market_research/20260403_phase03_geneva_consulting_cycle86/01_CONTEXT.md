- **FONGIT** — Geneva's primary tech startup accelerator (30+ years, 250+ startups, CHF 1B+ raised, 1,800+ jobs)

- **SICTIC** — Swiss ICT Investor Club (500+ investors, 2,500+ startups screened, 135 matchmaking events)
