### The 2026 Compliance Calendar

**The Four Deadlines:**

1. **Swissdamed — July 1, 2026 (~12 weeks):** NO grace period for high-risk SaMD (AI diagnostic imaging, AI symptom triage). Immediate hard deadline. Healthcare-first = community-wide event.

2. **AMLA/LETA — H2 2026 (~3-6 months):** FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027 adds external pressure. SRO membership required for advisors. 30-day discrepancy reporting obligation.

3. **FDJP Consultation Draft — End-2026 (~9 months):** Will crystallize AI governance standards. Market-wide awareness spike triggers. Competitive entry risk accelerates.

4. **EU AI Act High-Risk — August 2, 2026 (~15 months):** Already live: GPAI obligations since Aug 2, 2025 (~7.5 months). High-risk obligations incoming Aug 2, 2026.
