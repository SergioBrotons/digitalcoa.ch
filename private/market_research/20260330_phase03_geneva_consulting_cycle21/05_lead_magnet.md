## Lead Magnet Framework

### Title
**The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework:**
*Nine Decisions Before Your Consulting Firm Uses AI*

---

### Section 1 — GPAI Is Already Active (You Are Already Obligated)
- EU AI Act GPAI obligations operative since August 2, 2025 (~8.5 months)
- If your firm uses AI that affects EU users or outputs: obligations already apply

### Section 2 — The Federal Council First Draft (~2 Months)
- May 2026 = next external pressure event for Swiss consulting firms
- Federal Council lean approach: sector-specific measures, no cross-sector law

### Section 3 — The 40% AI Skills Gap (Your Opportunity)
- 40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI implementation skills
- Consulting firms who master AI governance first = advisory advantage

### Section 4 — Dual Compliance: EU AI Act + nFADP
- EU AI Act (transparency, GPAI) + Swiss nFADP (Art. 5(c) DPIA)
- Firms with EU nexus face both simultaneously

### Section 5 — What AI Is Your Firm Using? (Inventory)
- Client-facing AI tools
- Internal operations AI
- Data processing AI
- AI that affects EU users

### Section 6 — IT/Tech Sub-Segment: Strongest GPAI Pressure
- M&A IT sector +28% growth = highest AI adoption = highest GPAI exposure

### Section 7 — Labor Shortage = AI Adoption Driver
- 400,000 worker shortage = pressure to adopt AI = governance gap widens

### Section 8 — Anti-Pattern
- No AI inventory
- No GPAI transparency documentation
- No nFADP DPIA
- No dual compliance assessment

### Section 9 — Nine-Decision Framework

4. [ ] EU nexus determined
5. [ ] Federal Council First Draft implications assessed
