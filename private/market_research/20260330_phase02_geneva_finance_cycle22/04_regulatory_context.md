# Regulatory Context — Phase 02: Geneva Finance
## Cycle 22 | 2026-03-30

## 3-Layer Compliance Stack (Confirmed)

### Layer 1: Swiss Domestic Regulatory Layer
- **nFADP** (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection) — fully operative
  - Art. 5(c): Data processing must be lawful, proportionate; AI-based processing requires legal basis
  - Art. 5(f): Automated decision-making requires transparency and right to human review
  - Enforcement: FDPIC (Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner)
- **FINMA Supervisory Framework**
  - FINMA Circ. 08/2024 (FinSA/FinSO conduct rules, operative Jan 1, 2025) — current baseline
  - FINMA Circ. 2026/1 on nature risks (effective Jan 1, 2026)
  - No 2026-specific FINMA AI circular confirmed — regulatory vacuum
- **LETA** (Financial Institutions Act) — revision in progress
  - 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register
  - Implementation: H2 2026 (~3–6 months)

### Layer 2: Swiss International Compliance Layer
- **CARF/CRS 2.0** — already operative since January 1, 2026 (~3 months)
  - Common Reporting Standard Automatic Exchange of Information Version 2.0
  - Multi-jurisdictional reporting obligations
- **AMLA** (Anti-Money Laundering Act) — revision in progress
  - AMLA consultation concluded January 30, 2026 (machinery active)
  - Implementation: H2 2026 (~3–6 months)
  - Advisor scope expansion: lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs must join FINMA-recognized SRO
- **FATF Mutual Evaluation** — 2026–2027
  - Financial Action Task Force evaluation of Switzerland
  - External forcing function elevating AMLA compliance to international event
  - Creates reputational and operational risk for non-compliant WMs

### Layer 3: EU Extraterritorial Layer
- **EU AI Act** — high-risk obligations activating August 2, 2026 (~16 months)
  - Geneva WMs with EU market nexus subject if:
    - Placing AI systems on EU market
    - Operating AI systems with EU-intended outputs
    - Acting as EU importer/distributor of AI systems
  - GPAI (General Purpose AI) obligations already active since August 2, 2025 (~8 months)
- **EU AI Act Article 53 — GPAI System Providers**
  - Transparency obligations, technical documentation, copyright compliance
  - Already operative for GPAI providers/users
