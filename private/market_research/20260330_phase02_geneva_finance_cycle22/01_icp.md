# ICP — Phase 02: Geneva Finance / Boutique Wealth Management
## Cycle 22 | 2026-03-30

## Vertical Definition
Boutique wealth management firms (AUM CHF 50M–500M), family offices, independent asset managers (IAMs), and multi-family offices operating in Geneva with 2–20 employees. Firms that are too large for solo practice but too small for Big 4 infrastructure — the "forgotten middle" of Swiss wealth management.

## Ideal Client Profile
- **Who:** C-level wealth managers, partners, CIOs at boutique WM firms and family offices in Geneva
- **Pain:** Operating under triple-layer compliance pressure (Swiss domestic + international + EU extraterritorial) with no in-house compliance infrastructure; FATF evaluation creates external pressure; AMLA/LETA deadlines approaching
- **Behavior:** Already using AI tools (InvestGlass, Additiv, Altoo, etc.) for portfolio management and client reporting but without formal AI governance framework
- **Trigger:** FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027; AMLA/LETA mid-2026 deadlines; CARF already 3 months live (Jan 1 2026)
- **Geography:** Geneva canton, canton of Vaud (Lausanne), Geneva Lake Leman region
- **Decision-maker:** Partners, CIO, Compliance Officer (if exists)
