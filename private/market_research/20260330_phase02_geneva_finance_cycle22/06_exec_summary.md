## Key Findings

### 1. Triple-Layer Compliance Already Active
Three compliance layers are converging on Geneva boutique WMs simultaneously:
- **Layer 1 (Swiss Domestic):** nFADP Art. 5(c) + FINMA Circ. 2026/1 — NOW ACTIVE
- **Layer 2 (Swiss International):** CARF/CRS 2.0 live Jan 1 2026 (~3 months already); AMLA/LETA ~3–6 months
- **Layer 3 (EU Extraterritorial):** EU AI Act GPAI obligations active since Aug 2 2025 (~8 months)

### 2. FATF Evaluation Timeline Refined (End 2026 or 2027/2028)
Financial Action Task Force mutual evaluation of Switzerland refined to **end of 2026 or 2027/2028** (from "2026-2027"). Geneva WMs have slightly more runway than previously estimated — but AMLA/LETA H2 2026 is still the near-term forcing function.

### 3. AMLA/LETA Consultation Closed January 30, 2026
Implementation machinery active; ordinances expected H2 2026 (~3–6 months). 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register becomes mandatory. Advisor scope expansion (lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs must join FINMA-recognized SRO).

### 4. CARF Already 3 Months Live
CARF/CRS 2.0 has been operative since January 1, 2026. Many boutique WMs have not updated reporting infrastructure. Compliance gap already accumulating.

### 5. Structural Competitive Absence
No boutique WM AI governance competitor confirmed across 20+ consecutive cycles. Named indirect competitors (Abilene Advisors, AI Risk, MTF Solutions, TA Advisory, iAvva AI) are adjacent, not direct competitors. Technology vendors (InvestGlass, Additiv, Altoo) are tools, not advisors.

### 6. Regulatory Vacuum
FINMA has not published WM-specific AI guidance in 2026. FINMA Circ. 08/2024 remains the baseline. This vacuum creates both risk (no guidance) and opportunity (first mover advantage).

### 7. 76% AI Adoption in Geneva Financial Services SMEs (NEW)
PwC early 2024 survey: 76% of Geneva financial services SMEs already adopted AI systems — HIGHEST sector adoption rate. Governance gap is wider than previously estimated. 2026 is the "year of serious AI adoption" (agentic AI).

### 8. First-Mover Window Intact: ~12–18 Months
Window from March 30, 2026: CARF already live, AMLA/LETA ~3–6 months, FATF end-2026/2027-2028, EU AI Act Aug 2 ~16 months. No boutique competitor confirmed. Window is closable by potential entrants.
