# Phase 03 — Geneva Consulting / Small Consulting Firms
## Executive Summary

**Cycle 34 — 2026-03-31**

---

## Situation
Small Geneva consulting firms (2–15 employees) are caught in a structural trap: high individual AI use (63%, PwC Dec 2024) combined with near-zero firm-level governance, driven by a 40% AI skills gap and a 73% AI project failure rate. **⭐ NEW in Cycle 34:** 43% of firms have no formal AI policy and no plans to create one (Wolters Kluwer March 2026) — confirming structural inertia, not temporary caution.

The EU AI Act's GPAI provisions have been operative since August 2, 2025 (~8.5 months). Geneva's international hub position means most small consulting firms have EU-nexus clients, triggering GPAI transparency obligations **that are already active.**

---

## Problem
Three compounding problems:

1. **Shadow AI is prevalent** — consultants using AI tools for client-adjacent work (proposals, analysis, reports) without firm knowledge, consent, or disclosure framework
2. **GPAI already operative** — firms with EU-nexus clients have been in potential violation for ~7+ months (no grace period; Aug 2, 2025 was the operative date)
3. **Structural inertia is real** — 43% have no plans to ever create a policy; the 73% failure rate from skills gap has created learned helplessness

The 46-point governance gap (Phase 01 Cycle 34) confirms: individual AI use far outpaces any formal governance framework at the firm level.

---

## Opportunity
The 43% no-policy finding is simultaneously the challenge and the opportunity:
- Firms that act NOW become the compliant alternative clients can point to
- The Shadow AI audit (Decision 0) is low-cost, fast, and immediately valuable
- Andermatt May 26, 2026 (~8 weeks) creates a natural awareness peak to time outreach against
- **No boutique competitor exists** for small consulting firm AI governance in Geneva (9th+ consecutive confirmation)

---

## Recommended Lead Magnet
**"The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"**

A 5-decision structured framework:
- **Decision 0:** Shadow AI Audit (what are your consultants already running?)
- **Decision 1:** EU-Nexus check (is GPAI already your problem?)
- **Decision 2:** nFADP Art. 5(c) baseline (Swiss disclosure obligation)
- **Decision 3:** GPAI transparency (EU-nexus disclosure)
- **Decision 4:** Build firm-level AI governance policy

Primary CTA: Shadow AI Audit template (Decision 0 — lowest friction entry point)
