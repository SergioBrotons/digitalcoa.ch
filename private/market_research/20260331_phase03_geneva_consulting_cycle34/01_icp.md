# Phase 03 — Geneva Consulting / Small Consulting Firms
## ICP: Ideal Customer Profile

**Cycle 34 — 2026-03-31**

---

## ICP Definition

### Primary ICP
- **Firm type:** Small consulting firms, management advisors, IT consultants
- **Location:** Geneva metropolitan area
- **Size:** 2–15 employees (micro to small)
- **Sub-segments:**
  - Management consulting (strategy, operations, HR)
  - IT/Tech consulting (software, infrastructure, cybersecurity)
  - Financial consulting (CFO advisory, M&A support)
  - Marketing/communications consulting

### Decision-Maker Archetype
- **Primary:** Founding partner / Managing director / CEO (often same person in 2–15 firm)
- **Secondary:** Senior consultant with operational responsibility
- **Psychographic:** Under pressure on two fronts — client delivery AND firm governance. Has heard of AI, is being asked about it by clients, likely using it informally. Has NOT thought through firm-level AI policy.

### Core Problem
- Advisors using AI tools individually (client research, proposal drafting, internal analysis) **without any firm-level governance framework**
- **⭐ NEW Cycle 34:** 43% of firms have NO formal AI policy AND NO plans to create one (Wolters Kluwer March 2026)
- Shadow AI: consultants deploying personal AI tools for client-adjacent work — FADP exposure, professional liability exposure, client expectation mismatch
- **GPAI operative since Aug 2, 2025** — firms with EU-nexus clients have been in violation for ~7+ months

### Urgency Drivers (Cycle 34 — Updated)
| Driver | Timing | Urgency |
|--------|--------|---------|
| GPAI operative | **~8.5 months active** (Aug 2, 2025) | **CRITICAL — already in violation for EU-nexus firms** |
| 43% no policy + no plans | NEW Cycle 34 | **Structural inertia signal — window narrowing** |
| 73% abandoned AI projects | Skills gap | **High — failure precedent** |
| Andermatt AI Action Plan | ~8 weeks | **External pressure event** |
| Swissdamed | ~13 weeks | **Spillover awareness** |
| Federal Council First Draft | ~2 months | **Upcoming pressure event** |
| EU AI Act high-risk | ~19 weeks | **GPAI escalates Aug 2** |

### Dual Compliance Obligation
1. **nFADP** (Swiss) — Art. 5(c) transparency obligations already active; FADP applies to AI-supported data processing without new legislation
2. **EU AI Act / GPAI** (extraterritorial) — transparency obligations active since Aug 2, 2025 for firms with EU-nexus clients; high-risk provisions activate Aug 2, 2026

### What This ICP Needs
1. **Awareness** that GPAI is already operative (not future risk)
2. **Recognition** that Shadow AI = compliance exposure
3. **Framing** that AI governance is solvable for small firms (not enterprise complexity)
4. **First step** that is cheap, fast, and locally credible
