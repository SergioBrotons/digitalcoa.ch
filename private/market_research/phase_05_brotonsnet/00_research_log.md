## NET-NEW Findings C114 Phase 05
1. **LinkedIn Pay-to-Play Shift: Algorithm Rewarding Niche Credibility Over Reach** — LinkedIn shifting toward pay-to-play; algorithm rewarding relevance, credibility, consistency over broad reach; 89% B2B marketers using LinkedIn; 62% generating quality leads; Brotons.net: LinkedIn Newsletter format, personal profile amplification, paid boosting for flagship pieces increasingly necessary; quality and niche targeting primary competitive advantage
2. **LinkedIn Newsletter as Primary Distribution Format** — LinkedIn Newsletters benefit dual-touchpoint (notification + email); personal newsletters growing faster than company newsletters; ideal for Geneva senior practitioners already on platform; lower friction than email subscription; repurposing path: LinkedIn article → Brotons.net long-form → Substack excerpt → email digest
3. **"Hidden Buyers" in B2B Decision-Making: Legal/Compliance/Finance as Secondary ICP** — Hidden buyers from finance, ops, legal, compliance consuming thought leadership (63% spend 1+ hr/week); compelling TL makes them receptive to outreach; Brotons.net content reaching and influencing legal counsel, compliance officers, CFOs creates upstream pipeline influence; secondary ICP expands addressable audience beyond primary GC/MD tier
4. **Swiss Romandie French-Language Substack Gap** — Genuine gap in Swiss Romandie French-language business Substack; Reddit 2025 confirms difficulty finding niche French Substacks for Swiss business; Brotons.net French-language edition would be differentiated with low competition; Romandie = 2M French-speaking residents; no dedicated Geneva financial/legal intelligence Substack in French identified
5. **Content Oversaturation Raising Value of Practitioner Voice and Original Synthesis** — 54% C-suite/decision-makers spending 1+ hr/week on thought leadership but oversaturation diluting value; quality/relevance premium increasing; Brotons.net lens/platform positioning directly addresses this; original synthesis, practitioner frameworks, genuine interpretation (not aggregation) becomes more valuable as noise increases
6. **"Power of Two" LinkedIn Strategy: Personal Profile + Company Page Amplification** — Individual professional visible credible voice + institutional company page = critical for LinkedIn thought leadership; personal profile content typically faster growth; Brotons.net authorship model decision: personal practitioner voice vs. institutional brand; personal profiles likely outperform institutional brand for thought leadership on LinkedIn
