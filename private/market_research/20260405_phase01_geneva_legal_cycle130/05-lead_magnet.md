# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## Geneva Legal

**Target:** General Counsel + Managing Partners at Geneva financial institutions + law firms

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Regulatory Radar — Geneva Legal Risk Briefing (Quarterly)**
- Format: 4-page executive briefing (PDF + EN/FR)
- Content: Top 5 regulatory changes, enforcement precedents, advisory demand signals
- Distribution: Direct email to ICP list + Geneva Bar Association channels
- Differentiation: Independent, conflict-free perspective vs. Big 4/PwC product

**Option B: Geneva Board Liability Briefing (On-Demand)**
- Format: 2-page decision brief
- Content: Personal liability risk assessment under AktG Art. 716a + FINMA enforcement precedent
- CTA: 30-minute consultation offer
- Trigger: Board appointment, regulatory examination, enforcement notice

**Option C: Switzerland-EU Institutional Agreement Advisory Note (On-Demand)**
- Format: Situation analysis + scenario planning
- Content: Bilateral III implications for financial services + legal practice
- CTA: Strategy session offer
- Timing: Q2 2026 critical juncture

**Option D: AI Governance for Legal Functions — Geneva Readiness Assessment**
- Format: Self-assessment framework + consultation offer
- Content: AI tool governance, professional responsibility, FADP compliance
- CTA: Free readiness assessment + advisory roadmap

**Recommended Primary Lead Magnet:** Option A (Regulatory Radar) for pipeline building + thought leadership credibility
**Recommended Secondary:** Option D (AI Governance) for differentiation + early mover advantage

**Bilingual:** EN primary, FR translation available
