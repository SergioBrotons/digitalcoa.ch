# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva Legal — ICP

**Firm Types:**
- Mid-to-large Geneva law firms (20-200 attorneys)
- Boutique specialized advisory firms (regulatory, finance, ESG)
- In-house legal departments of Geneva-headquartered financial institutions
- Commodity trading houses with Geneva legal functions

**Decision-Maker:**
- Managing Partner / General Counsel
- Head of Compliance / Chief Compliance Officer
- Finance & Risk Committee members
- Board-level legal risk oversight (Swiss AktG Art. 716a)

**Pain Points:**
- Regulatory change volume and velocity (CSRD, DORA, Data Act, Swiss AI Act)
- AI governance and professional responsibility for lawyers using GenAI
- Cross-border sanctions compliance (SECO, OFAC, EU autonomous measures)
- Post-CS resolution: increased personal liability for board/legal
- Switzerland-EU institutional agreement uncertainty
- Litigation funding and third-party funding disclosure obligations

**Urgency Triggers:**
- FINMA examination findings
- ICC/FIA arbitration clause activation
- Regulatory enforcement notice
- Board-level legal risk review
- Succession or firm restructuring event

**Geography:** Geneva + Vaud (Lausanne) financial hub
**Language:** EN primary, FR secondary, DE useful
**Budget Cycle:** Q4 budget planning, mid-year regulatory reserve
