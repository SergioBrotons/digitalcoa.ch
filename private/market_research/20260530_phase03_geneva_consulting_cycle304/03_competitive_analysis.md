# Competitive Analysis: Geneva Consulting — C304 Phase 03

## What Exists, What's Missing

### Current Competitive Landscape

**What Exists:**
- Big 4 consulting firms (methodology frameworks, not sized for boutique)
- Legal tech vendors (AI governance for law firms, not consulting)
- Compliance consultants (generic GDPR/nFADP, not methodology-specific)
- CRM and proposal tools (no AI governance component)
- Swiss business associations (principles, not actionable)

**What's Missing (for 2–50 Employee Geneva Consulting Boutiques):**
- Methodology documentation for AI use
- Engagement letter AI clause templates
- Client AI governance questionnaire response service
- Sub-consultant AI delegation framework
- Carrier attestation methodology support
- nFADP Art.63 personal liability protection

---

## The White Space

1. **Methodology AI documentation template** — A proportionate template set for documenting AI use in consulting proposals and deliverables. Not ISO 27001 — specific to consulting methodology documentation.

2. **Engagement letter AI clause drafting** — Specific clauses for consulting engagement letters that allocate AI-related liability, define data processing obligations, and document client consent.

3. **Client AI questionnaire response methodology** — A structured approach to responding to client AI governance questionnaires that produces defensible documentation without revealing proprietary methodology.

4. **Sub-consultant AI delegation framework** — A delegation framework that enables managing directors to authorize sub-consultant AI use while maintaining accountability visibility.

5. **Carrier attestation methodology documentation** — Pre-signing review of carrier attestation forms with specific methodology documentation requirements for consulting firms.

6. **nFADP Art.63 personal liability structuring** — Personal liability protection framework specific to managing directors of consulting firms. What the corporate structure does and doesn't protect, and what additional measures are required.

---

## The Gap

The gap is not generic compliance. The gap is consulting-specific methodology documentation that enables the managing director to:
- Respond to client AI questionnaires with defensible documentation
- Include AI clauses in engagement letters that allocate liability appropriately
- Delegate to sub-consultants with documented accountability
- Support carrier attestation with methodology documentation
- Protect personal assets under nFADP Art.63

No Geneva-specific service addresses these for small consulting boutiques.
