## DigitalCoa.ch — Regulatory Tailwinds

### Key Regulatory Drivers Supporting Positioning
1. **FINMA AI Survey (2024-2025):** 50% Swiss FIs using AI; governance frameworks now expected by regulator
2. **FINMA Circular 2011/7 (outsourcing):** AI vendor relationships require governance; critical functions at risk
3. **Swiss nFADP (data protection):** AI tool usage on personal data requires lawful basis and governance
4. **mHRDD (mandatory human rights due diligence):** Board-level governance accountability for Swiss large companies
5. **OECD Pillar 2:** Board oversight of tax governance for multinationals with CHF 750M+ revenue
6. **DORA (EU Digital Operational Resilience Act):** ICT governance frameworks required; Swiss firms with EU exposure affected

### How These Regulations Drive DigitalCoa.ch Demand
- FINMA examination findings create urgency for governance remediation
- AI adoption mandates governance infrastructure before deployment
- Board-level accountability for AI decisions creates demand for decision governance frameworks
- Regulatory deadlines create conversion triggers: clients engage when facing specific compliance deadlines
