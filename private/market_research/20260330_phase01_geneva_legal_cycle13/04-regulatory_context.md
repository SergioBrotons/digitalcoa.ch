## Regulatory Context — Phase 01 Cycle 13

### Active Regulations (Geneva Legal — Carry-Forward):

**nFADP (FedParl Data Protection Act) — ACTIVE:**
- Art. 5(c): Transparency obligations for automated data processing — ACTIVE since September 2023
- FDPIC May 8, 2025 explicit confirmation: nFADP applies directly to AI-based data processing
- DPIA required for high-risk AI data processing

**EU AI Act — GPAI ACTIVE SINCE AUG 2, 2025:**
- GPAI transparency obligations active NOW for EU-nexus firms
- High-risk AI obligations activate August 2, 2026 (~4-5 months)
- Geneva firms with EU clients, AI on EU market, or EU-intended output: immediate obligation

**SBA AI Guidelines (June 2024) — SOFT GUIDANCE:**
- Non-binding; 3 acceptable methods for AI under professional secrecy
- Establishes minimum human oversight standard
- Does NOT constitute mandatory compliance framework

**CoE AI Convention — RATIFIED:**
- Federal Council ratified February 12, 2025; signed March 27, 2025
- FDJP tasked with drafting implementation bill
- Expected consultation end-2026
