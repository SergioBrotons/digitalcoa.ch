Swiss pharma companies face direct exposure; Novartis, Roche primary targets. Geneva-based pharma-adjacent financial services and healthcare investment implications.

3. **Hedge Funds and Iran War — Market Regime Divergence, Geneva Fund Performs (April 2, 2026)**

A Geneva-based fund specifically noted as outperforming. Geopolitical risk premium embedded in European markets. Geneva hedge fund industry exposed to regime change from Middle East conflict.

4. **Iran Hormuz Toll — Geneva Commodity Trading Exposure (April 3, 2026)**
   Le Temps Chronique: Iran implementing strait toll in Hormuz (similar to French/Italian highway model). Tehran asserting sovereignty and generating revenue. Geneva-based commodity traders and shipping firms have direct exposure to increased tanker insurance costs and Hormuz transit fees. Geneva's role as commodity trading hub (particularly oil) means this has structural implications for trading house P&L.
