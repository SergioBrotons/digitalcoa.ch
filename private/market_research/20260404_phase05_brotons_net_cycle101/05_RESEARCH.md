## Lead Magnet Concepts — Brotons.net Audience Growth

### Concept A: "Geneva Regulatory Radar — Weekly Bitext"
**Format:** Weekly 5-item digest (email + LinkedIn)
**Angle:** 5 Geneva regulatory/policy developments each week with 2-sentence interpretation
**Why valuable:** Predictable value; builds reading habit; positions Brotons.net as Geneva intelligence source
**CTA:** "Subscribe to the Geneva Regulatory Radar"
**Cadence:** Weekly, every Monday 7am Geneva time
**Monetization potential:** Sponsorship once list reaches 2,000+

### Concept B: "WTO Watch — Monthly Deep Dive"
**Format:** Monthly 10-page analysis (PDF + email)
**Angle:** WTO dispute settlement, trade policy developments, Geneva trade intelligence
**Why valuable:** Highly differentiated; no dedicated EN WTO analysis newsletter; builds authority
**CTA:** "Subscribe to WTO Watch"
**Cadence:** Monthly, aligned with WTO Dispute Settlement Body meetings
**Audience:** Trade lawyers, commodity traders, Geneva multinational compliance teams

**Angle:** ITU AI for Good, UN AI Advisory Body, WHO health AI governance developments
**Why valuable:** Early positioning in emerging topic; no dedicated publication covering Geneva AI governance
**CTA:** "Subscribe to Geneva AI Governance Briefing"
**Cadence:** Quarterly (aligns with ITU/UN AI governance calendar)
**Audience:** Geneva IO professionals, AI companies, multinational compliance teams

### Concept D: "Swiss Sanctions Compliance Update — Bi-Weekly"
**Format:** Bi-weekly 3-item brief (email only)
**Angle:** SECO/FINMA sanctions developments; Russia-specific; practical compliance implications
**Why valuable:** High-urgency topic; compliance professionals need timely updates
**CTA:** "Subscribe to Swiss Sanctions Watch"
**Cadence:** Bi-weekly
**Audience:** Compliance professionals, private bankers, commodity trading compliance
**Sensitivity note:** Must be factually precise; error risk is high

### Concept E: "Geneva Compliance Calendar — Annual PDF"
**Format:** Annual calendar (PDF, free download)
**Angle:** Key regulatory deadlines, publication dates, Geneva institutional events for compliance professionals
**Why valuable:** Practical utility; high shareability; list-building tool
**CTA:** "Download the Geneva Compliance Calendar — free"
**Production:** December for following year; evergreen value
