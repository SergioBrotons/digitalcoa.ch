## Target Segment: Brotons.net Readership

### Primary Audience
- **IHEID alumni** (Université de Genève / Institut de hautes études internationales et du développement): 10,000+ members globally; policy-academic crossover professionals
- **Geneva business professionals** (CCIG members): 3,000+; practical business intelligence seekers
- **Finance professionals** (SFAMA-adjacent): regulatory updates, compliance intelligence
- **International organization professionals**: WTO, WHO, UNOG, ILO staff; policy analysis consumers
- **Geneva corporate executives**: strategy, geopolitics, trade, regulatory intelligence

### Audience Pain Points
- Information overload from Geneva institutional outputs (WTO reports, FINMA circulars, UN documents)
- Limited time to synthesize dense regulatory/policy material
- Need for practical, actionable intelligence in English (non-French speakers) and French
- Seeking independent interpretation — not official positions or press releases
- Gap between academic policy frameworks and practical business application

### Content Consumption Patterns
- Most active: LinkedIn Tuesday–Thursday 7–9am and 5–7pm (Geneva time)
- Lower engagement: Mondays, Fridays, holiday periods
- Email newsletters: 25–35% open rate for Geneva B2B (vs. 20–25% industry average)
- Mobile reading increasing; long-form pieces read on desktop; summaries consumed on mobile

### What Brotons.net Should Be
- English-language intelligence layer between Geneva institutional activity and global audience
- Independent, interpretive, high signal-to-noise ratio
- The publication professionals recommend to colleagues
