## Editorial and Legal Framework — Brotons.net

### Content Considerations
- **No financial advice**: content must not constitute financial, legal, or investment advice
- **No specific company recommendations**: coverage must not constitute investment recommendations
- **Source attribution**: WTO, FINMA, UN documents must be accurately cited; no misrepresentation of sources
- **Correction policy**: factual errors corrected transparently; methodology disclosed

### Intellectual Property
- Geneva institutional documents (WTO, UN, WHO): generally public domain; cite appropriately
- FINMA circulars: public regulatory documents; fair use for commentary
- Third-party data: comply with data provider terms; note limitations

### Editorial Independence
- No sponsored content without clear disclosure
- Advertiser/sponsor relationships must not influence editorial decisions
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure for any content where author has related commercial interests

### AI-Generated Content
- AI-assisted drafting acceptable; human editorial review required
- AI-generated content without human review: do not publish
- AI translation: human review of AI-assisted FR translations required (Swiss regulatory term accuracy critical)

### Platform Considerations
- Substack canonical tags: configure correctly to avoid duplicate content SEO issues
- LinkedIn native: platform terms apply; Brotons.net links acceptable
- Newsletter sponsorship: must maintain editorial independence; no sponsor content influence
