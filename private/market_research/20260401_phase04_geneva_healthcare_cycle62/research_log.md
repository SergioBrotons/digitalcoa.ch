### 3. FADP Art. 21 Medical AI — Automated Decisions in Patient Care
**Source:** Phase 01 C62 FADP Art. 21 analysis
- FADP Art. 21 applies to AI systems processing patient data in medical practices
- Rights: human review upon request; transparency obligations
- Exemptions: direct contract conclusion where request granted; explicit consent
- Criminal penalties: up to CHF 250,000 for intentional violations
- **Implication:** Small medical practices using AI for diagnosis support, patient scheduling, billing = potentially in scope for Art. 21
