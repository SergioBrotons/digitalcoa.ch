## FADP Compliance Stack for Real Estate

### Art. 5(c) — Retroactive Compliance
The FADP legal basis for AI processing of tenant/buyer data must be documented retroactively. If you have been using AI on client data for 2.5 years without documentation, your first task is back-documentation.

### Art. 21 — Automated Decision-Making
Tenant screening, application scoring, or pricing recommendations that rely on automated AI processing may trigger Art. 21 human review rights. Tenants/buyers have the right to request human review of AI-assisted decisions.

### Art. 16 — DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment)
AI tools used for:
- Property valuation → FADP high-risk → DPIA required
- Tenant credit scoring → FADP high-risk → DPIA required
- Market analysis using client data → DPIA required

### Privacy by Design for Real Estate AI
- AI tools used for listings (descriptions, photos, virtual tours) → no client data, lower risk
- AI tools used for tenant screening → high-risk → documentation required
- AI tools used for pricing → high-risk → DPIA + human review required
