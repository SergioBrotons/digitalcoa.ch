### Signal 3: Swiss Verbandsklage Reform Definitively Rejected
**Indicator:** Both Council of States and National Council Legal Committee rejected comprehensive class action bills; no damages-based class action mechanism in Swiss law
**Source:** web_search (Swiss Holdings, Chambers Practice Guide)
**Signal strength:** MEDIUM-HIGH — legislative fact, not speculation
**Business implication:** International clients pursuing class action claims against Swiss defendants face different landscape than EU/US; Geneva firms advising both plaintiff-side (Art. 89 association actions) and defendant-side

**EN/FR:**
- EN: Switzerland definitively rejects class actions for damages — only injunctive relief under Art. 89 SCCP available; advisory opportunity for cross-border litigation strategy
- FR: La Suisse rejette définitivement les actions collectives en dommages — seul le recours en cessation prévu à l'art. 89 CPC est disponible; opportunité de conseil pour la stratégie de litige transfrontalier
