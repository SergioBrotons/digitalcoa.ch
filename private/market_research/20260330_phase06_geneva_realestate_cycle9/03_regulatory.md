## Regulatory Framework: Geneva Real Estate AI Governance

### GAIGI — Operative ~7–10 Months (Fall 2025)
Geneva AI Governance Institute. Institutional/government focus. Raises Geneva RE ecosystem awareness of AI governance.

### nFADP Art. 5(c) — ACTIVE NOW
AI-based data processing: transparency, purpose limitation, data minimisation. Applies to all RE firms processing personal data (tenant, buyer/seller data) via AI.

### EU AI Act GPAI — ACTIVE SINCE AUG 2, 2025
For RE firms with EU market nexus. Transparency obligations now active.

### Retroactive Compliance: Unique RE Entry Point
Unlike other verticals (anticipatory framing), RE can use retroactive compliance: "Govern the AI you already have." Lower-friction entry for firms already using AI.
