### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch Client Retention and Expansion Revenue — Referral Engine**

Client retention and referral strategy for DigitalCoa.ch:
- Quarterly review framework:
  - **Frequency**: quarterly (not monthly — quarterly signals strategic relationship, not operational hand-holding)
  - **Format**: 60-minute video call; agenda sent 48 hours in advance; structured around: (1) What regulatory developments have occurred this quarter that affect you? (2) What are your top 3 priorities for next quarter? (3) Are there any issues with our engagement?
  - **Deliverable**: brief written summary of discussion + proposed next steps within 5 business days
  - **Purpose**: not a sales call — it's strategic check-in; client feels cared for; uncovers expansion opportunities; surfaces at-risk signals early
- Expansion revenue opportunities:
  1. **Follow-on engagements**: at quarterly review, ask "are there other areas where we could add value?" — natural upsell moment
  2. **Adjacent services**: introduce Fractional CFO or training services when relevant; "we've been helping you with DORA — we also offer board training on DORA implications"
  3. **Scope expansion**: add team members (virtually) on existing engagement when demand increases; keep same relationship owner
- Referral strategy:
  1. **At engagement close**: ask for referral while trust is high — "do you know anyone else who might benefit from this type of advisory?" — most clients know someone
  2. **At quarterly review**: ask "who else in your network might benefit from a conversation?" — normalize the ask
  3. **After delivering great work**: referral ask is most natural when client is most satisfied; have referral request ready (don't over-use)
  4. **Reciprocity**: if you refer someone to a client, they're more likely to refer back
- Building referral culture:
  1. Make it easy: have a simple referral "introduction" email template ready; client just forwards or CCs
  2. Don't make it awkward: "I have someone I'd like you to meet" is better than "can you refer me to your CFO network"
  3. Value referrals: if someone refers a lead that becomes a client, acknowledge it — small gift or dinner
  4. Track referrals in CRM: know which clients are referral sources; focus relationship energy on those clients
- Geneva referral ecosystem:
  1. **Law firm partners**: Geneva law firms (Lenz Cailler, Borel & Barbey, Vischer, Walder Wyss) have clients needing advisory services; build relationships with managing partners
  2. **Brotons.net audience**: Brotons.net readers are potential referral sources — they know Geneva executives who would benefit from DigitalCoa.ch
  3. **Geneva business associations**: CCIG events, IHEID alumni events, SFAMA working groups — relationship-building venues for referral generation
  4. **Geneva private bankers**: Pictet, Lombard Odier, Bordier relationship managers have UHNWI clients needing advisory; build 2-3 key banker relationships
- Warning signs of at-risk clients: communication becomes infrequent, no response to meeting requests, budget reviews show cuts, internal champion changes role; address early through direct conversation

### FR
**Fidélisation des clients DigitalCoa.ch et revenus d'expansion — Moteur de recommandation**

Stratégie de fidélisation et de recommandation pour DigitalCoa.ch :
- Cadre de revue trimestrielle :
  - **Fréquence** : trimestrielle (pas mensuelle — signaux de relation stratégique)
  - **Format** : appel vidéo 60 minutes ; ordre du jour envoyé 48 heures à l'avance
  - **Objectif** : pas un appel de vente — c'est une vérification stratégique ; le client se sent soigné ; découvre opportunités d'expansion ; surfaces signaux de risque tôt
- Opportunités de revenus d'expansion :
  1. **Engagements de suivi** : lors de la revue trimestrielle, demander s'il y a d'autres domaines où ils pourraient ajouter de la valeur
  2. **Services adjacents** : introduire CFO fractionnaire ou services de formation quand pertinent
  3. **Extension de périmètre** : ajouter des membres d'équipe (virtuellement) sur engagement existant
- Stratégie de recommandation :
  1. **À la clôture de l'engagement** : demander une recommandation pendant que la confiance est élevée
  2. **À la revue trimestrielle** : demander qui d'autre dans leur réseau pourrait bénéficier d'une conversation
  3. **Après avoir livré un excellent travail** : demande de recommandation plus naturelle quand le client est très satisfait
- Écosystème de recommandation genevois :
  1. **Associés de cabinets d'avocats** : cabinets genevois ont des clients nécessitant services consultatifs ; construire relations avec associés>gérants
  2. **Audience Brotons.net** : lecteurs Brotons.net sont sources de recommandation potentielles
  3. **Associations commerciales genevoises** : événements CCIG, événements alumni IHEID, groupes de travail SFAMA
