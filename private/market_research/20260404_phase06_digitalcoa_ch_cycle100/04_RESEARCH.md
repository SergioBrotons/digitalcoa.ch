## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 4)

### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch Service Line Expansion — Adjacent Revenue Opportunities**

Service line expansion framework for DigitalCoa.ch:
- Adjacent service criteria: (1) leverages existing expertise (low ramp-up); (2) serves existing client base (easy to cross-sell); (3) has attractive margins; (4) doesn't require major new credentialing or infrastructure; (5) enhances core positioning, not dilutes it
- High-priority adjacent services for DigitalCoa.ch:
  1. **Fractional CFO / Finance Director** (highest priority): natural extension of regulatory advisory for CFO-level clients; CHF 8,000-20,000/month retainer; leverages Geneva CFO network; existing regulatory clients already have CFO relationships; day-rate CHF 3,000-4,000 equivalent
  2. **Regulatory project management**: managing actual compliance projects (not just advising) — DORA gap remediation project, mHRDD implementation project; provides deliverable certainty for clients; can command premium project fees
  3. **Board advisory services**: preparation for board meetings (board packs, regulatory updates for boards), board effectiveness reviews; Geneva board market underserved by independent advisors;CHF 5,000-15,000/board mandate
  4. **Training and workshops**: executive training on regulatory topics (DORA for boards, mHRDD for compliance teams); day-rate of CHF 5,000-8,000 for training engagements; repeatable and scalable
  5. **Regulatory technology selection**: helping clients select and implement compliance tech (RegTech vendor selection, implementation oversight); growing demand post-DORA; can charge success fee on implementation
- Lower priority (require more investment):
  6. **Crisis management advisory**: regulatory investigation response, sanctions enforcement support; high-stakes, high-margin; requires specific expertise and reputation
  7. **M&A due diligence (regulatory)**: regulatory due diligence for transactions; requires deal experience; better as referral relationship with M&A advisors than standalone service
- Services to avoid:
  - Audit/accounting: requires credentialing, different business model, competes with Big 4
  - Legal services: requires bar admission; can partner with Geneva law firms instead
  - IT implementation: requires technical staff; too far from advisory core
- Expansion sequencing: (1) Add Fractional CFO now (leverage existing relationships); (2) Add Training/Workshops next quarter (high margin, leverages existing knowledge); (3) Add Board Advisory when first board mandate secured; (4) Add Regulatory PM when first DORA/mHRDD project identified
- Revenue model impact: adding fractional CFO and training can increase annual revenue per senior advisor by 30-50% without proportional cost increase

**Extension de lignes de services DigitalCoa.ch — Opportunités de revenus adjacents**

Cadre d'extension de lignes de services pour DigitalCoa.ch :
