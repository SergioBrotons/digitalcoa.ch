### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch Pricing Strategy — Optimizing Revenue Model**

Advisory pricing models — Geneva market context:
- Day-rate model: most common in Geneva for boutique advisory; range CHF 2,800-4,500/day for boutique partner-level; CHF 4,500-8,000/day for Big 4 partner; clear and simple; incentivizes efficiency but can create client anxiety about hours
- Project-based (fixed fee): predictable for client; risk for advisory if scope creeps; suitable for well-defined deliverables (e.g., DORA gap assessment = fixed price); requires precise scoping; increasingly preferred by CFOs
- Monthly retainer: provides revenue predictability; suitable for ongoing advisory relationship (e.g., regulatory monitoring, board advisory); typically CHF 8,000-20,000/month for 5-10 days/month commitment; client must feel they are getting value or they cancel
- Value-based pricing: pricing based on value delivered (e.g., "this DORA gap assessment will save you CHF 200,000 in potential FINMA fines"); harder to implement but highest margin; requires strong value communication
- Hybrid models: retainer + success fee (e.g., fixed retainer + % of transaction value for M&A advisory); retainer + variable fee tied to outcomes; increasingly used in Geneva for complex advisory mandates
- What Geneva clients actually prefer:
  - Large MNEs/institutions: prefer project-based or retainer (predictable budgeting)
  - SMEs/family businesses: prefer day-rate (simpler, more transparent)
  - PE-backed companies: prefer project-based or hybrid
- Pricing for DigitalCoa.ch recommended approach:
  1. **Discovery phase**: free or low-cost initial consultation (2 hours); establishes relationship and uncovers needs
  2. **Diagnostic/proposal**: structured scope + fixed price proposal for defined deliverable
  3. **Engagement**: project-based preferred; day-rate for undefined scope or ongoing advisory
  4. **Retainer**: for key clients with regular advisory needs; offers predictability and relationship depth
- Rate positioning: CHF 3,000-3,500/day positions as premium boutique (above average Geneva boutique, below Big 4); CHF 4,000-4,500/day positions as elite boutique (equivalent to Big 4 partner quality)
- Pricing communication: never apologize for rates; frame as quality signal; emphasize senior-only model (no junior teams burning hours)

### FR
**Stratégie de tarification DigitalCoa.ch — Optimisation du modèle de revenus**

Modèles de tarification du conseil — contexte du marché genevois :
- Modèle tarif journalier : plus commun à Genève pour conseil boutique ; gamme CHF 2 800-4 500/jour pour niveau associé boutique ; clear and simple ; incentives efficacité mais peut créer anxiété client sur les heures
- Projet (forfait) : prévisible pour client ; risque pour advisor si périmètre extensions ; convient pour livrables bien définis
- Retainer mensuel : prévisibilité des revenus ; convient pour relation conseil ongoing ; typiquement CHF 8 000-20 000/mois
- Tarification basée sur la valeur : tarification basée sur la valeur livrée ; plus difficile à implémenter mais marge la plus élevée
- Approche recommandée pour DigitalCoa.ch :
  1. **Phase découverte** : consultation initiale gratuite ou low-cost (2 heures)
  2. **Diagnostic/proposition** : périmètre structuré + proposition à prix fixe pour livrable défini
  3. **Engagement** : basé sur projet preferred ; tarif journalier pour périmètre indéfini
  4. **Retainer** : pour clients clés avec besoins consultatifs réguliers
