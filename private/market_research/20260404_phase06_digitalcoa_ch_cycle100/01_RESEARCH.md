## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 1)

### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch Competitive Positioning — Geneva Boutique Advisory Landscape**

Competitive landscape for DigitalCoa.ch — Geneva boutique advisory market:
- Big 4 in Geneva: PwC Geneva (500+ professionals), Deloitte (300+), EY, KPMG — dominant in audit/tax/regulatory compliance; advisory practices exist but often subscale relative to audit; PwC and Deloitte have strongest advisory brands
- Geneva boutique consultancies: dozens of independent strategy and management consulting firms; strategy boutiques (Bovet Strategor, Rev比例, PLC Consulting), sector specialists (commodity trading, pharma, fintech), functional specialists (finance, HR, operations)
- The "best of" model in Geneva: boutique firms formed by ex-Big 4 partners offering similar quality at lower cost; typically 5-20 people; leverages senior partner experience without junior staffing pyramid; growing trend in Geneva
- DigitalCoa.ch positioning options:
  1. **Sector specialist**: deep expertise in Geneva regulatory/compliance landscape (financial regulation, trade law, sanctions) — differentiates from generalist boutiques; clients hire for specific expertise not generalist advice
  2. **Geographic specialist**: Geneva institutional market specialist (UN/IO, Geneva MNEs, commodity trading houses) — understanding of Geneva business culture and networks; hard to replicate for Zurich/Paris-based firms
  3. **Format differentiator**: remote-first, senior-only engagement model — faster, more personal, more affordable than Big 4; particularly attractive to SMEs and family businesses
  4. **Intelligence layer**: combining Brotons.net thought leadership with advisory services — intellectual credibility as client acquisition channel; competitors cannot replicate without Brotons.net
- Competitive differentiation framework: be specific (not "we advise Geneva companies" but "we advise Geneva financial institutions on DORA compliance"); own a category (not "strategy consulting" but "regulatory strategy for Geneva asset managers")
- What Geneva clients actually want from boutique advisory: senior attention (not handed off to juniors), faster response, genuine expertise, no conflicts of interest, English + French capability, reasonable fees
- Pricing against Big 4: DigitalCoa.ch can price at 50-70% of Big 4 rates for equivalent partner quality; Geneva clients will pay premium for boutique if differentiation is clear
- Client selection: boutique advisory should be selective — not every client fit; choose clients where DigitalCoa.ch has genuine expertise advantage; declining misaligned work builds reputation

### FR
**Positionnement concurrentiel DigitalCoa.ch — Paysage du conseil boutique genevois**

Paysage concurrentiel pour DigitalCoa.ch — marché du conseil boutique genevois :
- Big 4 à Genève : PwC Genève (500+ professionnels), Deloitte (300+), EY, KPMG — dominants en audit/fiscal/conformité réglementaire ; pratiques consultatives existent mais souvent sous-dimensionnées vs. audit

- Options de positionnement DigitalCoa.ch : (1) Spécialiste sectoriel — expertise profonde en paysage réglementaire/compliance genevois ; (2) Spécialiste géographique — marché institutionnel genevois (ONU/IO, MNEs genevoises, maisons de commerce) ; (3) Différenciateur format — modèle remote-first, senior-only ; (4) Couche intelligence — combinant pensée directive Brotons.net avec services consultatifs
