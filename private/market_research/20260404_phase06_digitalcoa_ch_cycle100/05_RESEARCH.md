## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 5)

### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch Lead Qualification and Pipeline Management**

Lead qualification and CRM for boutique advisory practice:
- Lead sources for DigitalCoa.ch:
  1. **Brotons.net email list engagement** (warm): email opens, content downloads, article shares; highest conversion potential; 3+ opens of specific regulatory content = qualified interest
  2. **Referrals** (warm): from existing clients, law firm partners, Geneva professional networks; highest conversion rate (30-50%); require personal follow-up within 24 hours
  3. **LinkedIn inbound** (warm): Geneva professionals reaching out via LinkedIn; moderate conversion; requires consistent LinkedIn presence
  4. **Web contact form** (cold): website inquiry; lower conversion; requires qualification call before proposing
  5. **Conference/events** (warm): Geneva business events, IHEID, CCIG; high-value for relationship building
- Lead scoring framework:
  - **Cold** (score 1-5): visited website once, no engagement with content
  - **Warm** (score 6-15): opened email newsletter, followed LinkedIn, downloaded lead magnet
  - **Qualified** (score 16-30): attended webinar, opened 3+ Brotons.net regulatory emails, referred by trusted source
  - **Sales-ready** (score 31+): requested consultation, responded to outreach, referred by existing client
- CRM setup: HubSpot CRM (free tier sufficient for boutique); pipeline stages: Lead → Qualified → Discovery Call Scheduled → Proposal Sent → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost
- Key CRM fields for Geneva advisory: Company, Decision-Maker (Name, Title, Email, Phone), Lead Source, Last Contact, Next Action, Estimated Deal Size, Decision Timeline
- Conversion process:
  1. New lead → auto-responder within 2 hours (personal email, not template)
  2. Within 48 hours → personalized outreach (LinkedIn connection + message or email)
  3. If no response → follow-up once after 7 days; then move to nurture sequence
  4. Discovery call → within 5 business days of qualification
  5. Proposal → within 3-5 business days of discovery call
- Pipeline metrics to track: lead-to-proposal conversion rate (benchmark: 20-30% for boutique advisory); proposal-to-close rate (benchmark: 50-60%); average deal size; sales cycle length (benchmark: 4-8 weeks for Geneva advisory)
- Geneva-specific considerations: Geneva clients move slowly; decision cycles can be 3-6 months; maintain contact through value-add content during long decision cycles; don't pressure for decision
- Nurture strategy: for leads that aren't ready: monthly value-add email (regulatory update, article) for 6 months before re-engaging with sales intent; Geneva clients may convert after 12-18 months
