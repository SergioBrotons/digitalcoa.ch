## Phase 06 Cycle 43

## Core Message (EN)
*"75% of Swiss real estate agencies use AI. The FADP has been in force for 2.5 years. GAIGI has no public guidance for you. The question isn't whether you use AI — it's whether your governance is ready."*

## Core Message (FR)
*"75% des agences immobilières suisses utilisent l'IA. Le FADP est en vigueur depuis 2,5 ans. La GAIGI n'a pas de lignes directrices publiques pour vous. La question n'est pas si vous utilisez l'IA — c'est si votre gouvernance est prête."*

## Primary Pain Points
1. **75% AI adoption widest gap** — almost everyone uses AI, almost no one has formal governance
2. **GAIGI institutional 404** — no guidance exists for you
3. **FADP 2.5+ years accumulated** — obligations always existed
4. **Retroactive compliance** — you already use AI, now govern it
5. **Tenant screening AI** — likely EU AI Act high-risk

## Retroactive Compliance Framing
*"You've already adopted AI. That's not the question anymore. The question is: can you document how you use it, and are you ready when a tenant asks?"*

## Objection Handling

### "The FADP has been around for years"
→ Exactly. 2.5 years of operative obligations already accumulated.
→ FDPIC enforcement in real estate is not confirmed absent — it's a timing question.

### "We don't have EU clients"
→ FADP applies regardless of client nationality.
→ EU AI Act Annex III applies if any EU nexus exists in tenant screening.

### "Our agency is too small"
→ 75% AI adoption = highest in portfolio despite small size.
→ Size doesn't create exposure. AI use does.

Agency. Pragmatic. Geneva real estate voice.
Calm authority. No hype. Practical urgency.
