## Section 4: Swissdamed Reporting — What Actually Triggers an Obligation

**Understanding when Swissdamed applies to your AI tools.**

**Serious Incident definitions under Swissdamed:**
- Death or unexpected deterioration in health condition
- Life-threatening injury
- Permanent disability or birth defect
- Event requiring intervention to prevent above outcomes

**AI/SaMD specific triggers:**
- AI diagnostic error leading to incorrect treatment decision
- AI imaging system failure affecting diagnosis
- AI triage system directing patient to inappropriate care level
- Data security breach affecting AI diagnostic outputs

## Section 5: nFADP — Patient Data AI Governance

**Swissdamed is urgent, but nFADP is already active.**

Every AI tool processing patient personal data triggers Swiss Federal Data Protection Act obligations:

**Key nFADP obligations for AI-using medical practices:**
- **Lawful basis:** Identify the legal basis for processing patient data through AI (consent, legitimate interest, or legal obligation)
- **DPIA:** Data Protection Impact Assessment required for high-risk AI processing
- **Data minimisation:** Only process data necessary for the AI tool's specific purpose
- **Patient rights:** Ensure patients can access, correct, and delete AI-processed data about them
- **Vendor DPAs:** Data processing agreements with all AI vendors
