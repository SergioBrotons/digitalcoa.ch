- Big Law AI opinions

- EU AI Act specialist consultancy opinions (descriptive, not operational)

**Q4 (C312 — Specific + Evidence)** — Occupied by DigitalCoa since C312. Evidence-led, specific to ICP's situation.

**Q4 (C313 — Post-Pause Universal)** — Occupied by DigitalCoa since C313. Evidence-led, post-pause calibrated, cross-vertical.

**No competitor occupies Q4 in any C312, C313, or C314 elevation.** DigitalCoa is the only operator in the Q4 quadrant across the C312 → C313 → C314 elevation sequence.
