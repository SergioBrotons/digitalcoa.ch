# Messaging Framework — Geneva Consulting AI Governance
## Phase 03 Cycle 44

## Core Message
**"73% of consulting AI projects fail — usually not because of the technology, but because of the governance gap. GPAI has been operative since August 2025. Do your EU-nexus client engagements comply?"**

## Key Themes

### Theme 1: GPAI Already Operative
**No Grace Period**
> "The EU AI Act's GPAI provisions became operative August 2, 2025. If your consulting firm serves EU clients or has EU-nexus operations, you have been in the compliance window for approximately 8 months."

### Theme 2: FADP Directly Applicable
**No New Law Required**
> "FADP applies to your AI tools today. FDPIC confirmed in May 2025: existing obligations already cover AI-supported data processing. No new legislation required for enforcement."

### Theme 3: 73% Failure Rate = Governance Opportunity
**Failure Is a Feature, Not a Bug**
> "73% of AI projects in consulting firms fail. The primary cause is not the technology — it's the governance gap: no skills framework, no documentation, no compliance protocol. That's where advisory value lives."

### Theme 4: 40% Skills Gap
**The Unknown Unknown**
> "40% of Geneva SMEs report an AI skills gap. Most consulting firms don't know what they don't know about AI governance. That's the most dangerous position."

### Theme 5: Two-Timeline Urgency
**May 2026 + End-2026**
> "digitalswitzerland publishes its AI Action Plan First Draft in May 2026. Federal Council formal legislation follows end-2026. The window for first-mover advantage is closing."

## Tone
- Calm, precise, expert
- No hype, no scare tactics
- Failure-prevention framing over compliance framing
