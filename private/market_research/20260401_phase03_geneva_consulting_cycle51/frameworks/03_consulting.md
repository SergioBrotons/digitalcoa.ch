## GPAI Obligations (~8 months operative)

EU GPAI (General Purpose AI) active since August 2, 2025:
- Transparency obligations for general-purpose AI models
- Documentation of AI training data sources
- Energy and resource consumption disclosure
- Compliance with EU copyright law for training data

**GPAI scope:** Consulting firms producing deliverables with AI assistance for EU public sector = direct scope.

## 9-Section Framework

1. AI Inventory — tools and purposes
2. Data Classification — client data processed by AI
3. Vendor Due Diligence — documented AI vendor practices
4. Output Review Protocol — human review before client delivery
5. FADP Compliance — Art. 5(c), Art. 21, DPIA, hard prohibitions
6. GPAI Obligations (if EU-nexus)
7. Employee Policy — clear AI use guidelines
8. Incident Response — AI error protocol
9. Training — governance capability alongside compliance
