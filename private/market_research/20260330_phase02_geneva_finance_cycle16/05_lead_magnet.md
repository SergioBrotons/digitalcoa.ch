## Section 2: What's Already Live and Enforceable

### CARF/CRS 2.0 — You've Had 3 Months
You've been under CARF obligations since January 1, 2026. As of today, approximately 3 months of live obligation.
If your AI processes financial account data or tax information for clients, your CARF compliance obligations are active now.

**What CARF means for your AI:**
- AI systems handling CRS-covered data need documented governance
- Automated data processing for financial reporting = CARF obligations
- Your AI vendors may not have CARF-compliant documentation

### AMLA/LETA H2 2026 — The Near-Term Pressure (~3–6 months)

The Anti-Money Laundering Act revision is machinery-active with a consultation that concluded January 30, 2026.

**AML obligations that will hit:**
- 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register
- SRO membership obligations if you handle real estate transactions, establish non-operational entities, or provide domicile services
- Enhanced due diligence for AI-assisted client onboarding

**The FATF Connection:**
FATF mutual evaluation 2026–2027 means Geneva WM firms face international pressure on top of domestic AML requirements.
This is international credibility at stake.

## Section 3: The 3-Layer Compliance Stack for Geneva Boutique WM

**Layer 1 — Swiss Domestic**
- nFADP Art. 5(c): AI-based automated decisions about individuals require DPIA + human review
- Active since 2023 — years to comply, not months

**Layer 2 — Swiss International**
- CARF/CRS 2.0: Live since Jan 1 2026 (~3 months already operative)
- AMLA: H2 2026 (~3–6 months away)
- LETA: H2 2026 (~3–6 months away)
- FATF evaluation: 2026–2027 (international pressure driver now active)

**Layer 3 — EU Extraterritorial**
- EU AI Act GPAI: Active since Aug 2 2025 (~8 months live)
- High-risk obligations: Aug 2 2026 (~4.5 months away)
- Applies if you have EU clients, EU-intended outputs, or operate as EU importer/distributor

**The Compliance Gap:**
No FINMA guidance specifically addresses AI governance for boutique WM firms.
FINMA Guidance 08/2024 remains the baseline — no 2026 specific circular.
You're operating in a guidance vacuum with 3 layers of active and approaching regulation.
