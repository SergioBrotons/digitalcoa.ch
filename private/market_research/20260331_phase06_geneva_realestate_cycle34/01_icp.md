# Phase 06 ICP — Geneva Real Estate / Small Agencies + Property Managers

**Cycle:** 34  
**Date:** 2026-03-31  
**Portfolio Position:** Widest governance gap — 75% AI adoption vs. minimal formal governance

---

## Ideal Customer Profile

### Firmographics
- **Vertical:** Small real estate agencies, property management firms (Geneva-focused)
- **Size:** 2–15 employees
- **Geography:** Geneva canton, French-speaking Switzerland
- **Structure:** Owner-managed or small partnership
- **Regulatory context:** cantonal + federal immatriculation, FADP, upcoming Swissdamed

### Decision-Making Unit
- Owner / Managing Director (sole decision-maker in firms <5 employees)
- Office manager (admin + compliance focal in firms 5–15)
- External accountant / fiduciaire (occasional advisor on compliance)

### Technology Baseline
- Desktop + mobile; cloud-adjacent (WhatsApp-heavy communication)
- Email + local drives; some Google Workspace / Microsoft 365
- Near-universal individual AI tool use (estimated 75%+ per Phase 01 Cycle 33)
- Minimal IT governance; often no dedicated IT person
- Shadow AI is the norm, not the exception

### Psychographic / Behavioral
- Compliance as cost, not value — "another regulatory burden"
- Skeptical of abstract frameworks; respond to concrete risk framing
- Price-sensitive; prefer bundled/flat-rate advisory
- Trust local networks, cantonal associations, peer recommendations
