# ICP Language — Phase 03 Geneva Consulting — Cycle 290
# Small Consulting Boutique, 2–50 Employees — Geneva

## How the ICP Talks About AI Problems

---

### On Client AI Governance Questionnaires

**EN:**
"We answered their AI questionnaire. Now every time we deliver something, they might be measuring it against what we wrote."
— The questionnaire response is an implied warranty. The boutique does not track whether its delivery practice matches its stated method.

**FR:**
"Nous avons répondu à leur questionnaire IA. Maintenant, chaque fois que nous livrons quelque chose, ils pourraient l'évaluer par rapport à ce que nous avons écrit."

---

### On Carrier Attestation

**EN:**
"I signed the carrier form. But I've never actually seen a policy that says AI is covered. Nobody can tell me in writing."
— Two separate questions being answered as one: what the attestation requires and what the policy covers. Neither carrier nor broker can confirm coverage explicitly.

**FR:**
"J'ai signé le formulaire de l'assureur. Mais je n'ai jamais vu une police qui dit que l'IA est couverte. Personne ne peut me le confirmer par écrit."

---

### On AI Deliverable Version State

**EN:**
"The recommendation has a date on it. But nobody can tell you what the model was thinking on that date versus what it's thinking now."
— The timestamp certifies presentation, not analytical validity. The model version state generating the analysis is unknown and potentially different from current model state.

**FR:**
"La recommandation a une date. Mais personne ne peut vous dire ce que le modèle pensait à cette date par rapport à ce qu'il pense maintenant."

---

### On Peer Silence

**EN:**
"I've asked three peers. Nobody really has an answer. Which means nobody is comfortable talking about it."
— Peer circuit absence of concrete AI governance responses indicates universally unresolved status, not absence of concern. Silence is an embarrassment signal.

**FR:**
"J'ai demandé à trois pairs. Personne n'a vraiment de réponse. Ce qui signifie que personne n'est à l'aise pour en parler."

---

### On Engagement Letter AI Disclosure

**EN:**
"If I write in the engagement letter that we use AI, I think that creates some kind of data processing obligation I'm not sure I've satisfied. But if I don't write it, the client might claim they didn't know."
— Disclosure creates nFADP processor obligations; non-disclosure risks client disclosure claims. No Geneva protocol maps this trade-off at boutique scale.

**FR:**
"Si j'écris dans le mandat que nous utilisons l'IA, je pense que cela crée une sorte d'obligation de traitement de données dont je ne suis pas sûr de l'avoir satisfaite. Mais si je ne l'écris pas, le client pourrait claim qu'il ne savait pas."

---

### On Geneva Accountability Formation

**EN:**
"By the time anything gets published, the decision has already been made somewhere. The question is whether you were in the room."
— Operative accountability expectations form through informal channels before formal doctrine arrives. Published guidance describes standards already in effect.

**FR:**
"Quand quelque chose est publié, la décision a déjà été prise quelque part. La question est de savoir si vous étiez dans la pièce."

---

### On Personal Accountability Weight

**EN:**
"I'm the one who signs things. I'm the one who's exposed. And I can't actually tell you what we're doing with AI half the time."
— MD accountability without corresponding operational visibility. The personal signature on carrier forms commits to what the MD cannot verify.

**FR:**
"C'est moi qui signe les documents. C'est moi qui suis exposé. Et je ne peux pas vraiment vous dire ce que nous faisons avec l'IA la moitié du temps."

---

### On Sub-Consultant AI Delegation

**EN:**
"We've subbed out part of the analysis. Their team uses AI. I have no idea what they're using or whether it creates exposure for us."
— Three-party chain: client → prime boutique → sub-consultant AI tool. Prime boutique bears processor obligations without vendor evaluation.

**FR:**
"Nous avons externalisé une partie de l'analyse. Leur équipe utilise l'IA. Je n'ai aucune idée de ce qu'ils utilisent ou si cela crée une exposition pour nous."

---

### Core Linguistic Frameworks Used by This ICP

1. **Implied warranty framing:** The questionnaire response documents what the boutique committed to. Every subsequent deliverable is measured against it.
2. **Two-separate-questions framing:** Attestation adequacy and insurance coverage are structurally different questions that carriers and brokers answer as one.
3. **Presentation vs. validity framing:** Timestamp vs. analytical framework state. What matters is not when the deliverable was presented but what model state produced it.
4. **Peer silence as signal:** The absence of peer responses on concrete AI governance methodology is interpreted as embarrassment, not absence of concern.
5. **Disclosure paradox framing:** Engagement letter AI language creates nFADP obligations while protecting from client disclosure; non-disclosure does the reverse.
6. **Velocity framing:** Operative standards form before publication. Being in the room vs. reading the document creates structurally different accountability positions.
