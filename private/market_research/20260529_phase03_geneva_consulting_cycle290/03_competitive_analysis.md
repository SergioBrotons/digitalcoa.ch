# Competitive Analysis — Phase 03 Geneva Consulting — Cycle 290
# Small Consulting Boutique, 2–50 Employees — Geneva

## What Exists in Market vs. What's Missing

---

### Category 1: Client AI Governance Questionnaire Response Preparation

**What Exists:**
- Generic AI governance questionnaire response templates available through large consulting firms (Deloitte, KPMG, PwC — prohibitively scaled for 2–50 employee boutiques)
- General GDPR/nFADP compliance frameworks not calibrated to consulting deliverables or Geneva-specific regulatory context
- Professional service firm compliance documentation following generic ISO/ISO 42001 frameworks not specific to consulting methodology or Geneva accountability formation

**What's Missing:**
- Pre-questionnaire response alignment methodology: mapping the boutique's actual delivery practice against questionnaire language before submission
- Client-counterpart audit trail architecture: maintaining a cross-referenced record between questionnaire response claims and actual deliverable production practice
- Geneva-specific questionnaire response calibration for institutional client AI governance standards with institutional contact intelligence
- **No Geneva market service prepares the boutique's own questionnaire response against what it can actually perform—not what it wants to say.**

---

### Category 2: Carrier AI Attestation Pre-Signing Review

**What Exists:**
- Carrier-furnished AI attestation forms describing what the signature commits the signatory to (written by carrier legal, from carrier perspective)
- General insurance broker advisory on professional liability coverage (not Geneva-calibrated, not AI-specific)
- Post-signature carrier clarification requests (after signature)

**What's Missing:**
- Pre-signing carrier form review from the signatory's personal liability perspective—not the carrier's adequacy evaluation but the MD's exposure analysis
- PI policy coverage mapping against carrier form commitments: what is covered, what gap remains uninsured, what discharge pathway exists
- Geneva-specific carrier form pre-signing review service proportionate to boutique scale and pricing (CHF 2,000–3,000 range)
- **No Geneva market service offers pre-signing carrier AI attestation review from signatory-side personal liability perspective.**

---

### Category 3: Sub-Consultant AI Delegation Authorization Architecture

**What Exists:**
- Standard engagement letter templates with general sub-consultant liability provisions
- nFADP Article 9 third-party processor template clauses (generic, vendor not consulting-context)
- General vendor AI terms review (technology law firms, not Geneva boutique calibrated)

**What's Missing:**
- Sub-consultant AI delegation chain mapping: client → prime boutique → sub-consultant → AI tool, with mapped processor obligations the prime boutique bears
- nFADP Article 9 engagement letter architecture specific to consulting three-party deletion chains
- Geneva consulting-specific AI delegation authorization protocol proportionate to 2–50 employee boutique governance capacity
- Sub-consultant AI vendor evaluation methodology for boutique professional liability exposure
- **No Geneva market service offers sub-consultant AI delegation authorization architecture for consulting boutiques operating in complex multi-party delivery chains.**

---

### Category 4: AI Deliverable Version State Documentation

**What Exists:**
- General AI model version tracking for IT/SaaS purposes (software engineering contexts)
- AI audit frameworks addressing model documentation for regulatory compliance (institutional, not boutique consulting scale)
- Document versioning for professional work product (Word track changes, legal document version control—not AI model version state)

**What's Missing:**
- Geneva consulting-specific deliverable version state documentation protocol: mandate book-level tracking architecture proportionate to boutique scale
- Timestamp integrity methodology distinguishing presentation date from analytical framework validity date
- AI model update propagation documentation for active boutique mandate book
- **No Geneva market service offers AI deliverable version state documentation architecture for consulting boutiques operating multi-mandate active client books.**

---

### Category 5: Engagement Letter AI Disclosure Decision Framework

**What Exists:**
- Generic engagement letter templates with optional AI use disclosure language
- nFADP data processing disclosure clauses (designed for data processing contexts, not consulting methodology disclosure)
- Legal privilege analysis for consulting work product (not AI-specific, not Geneva-calibrated)

**What's Missing:**
- Engagement letter AI disclosure decision mapping: nFADP processor obligations created by disclosure language vs. client disclosure risk from non-disclosure
- Geneva-specific proportionality framework for boutique engagement letter AI disclosure decisions
- Engagement letter AI disclosure architecture that maps privilege protection vs. nFADP compliance vs. client disclosure in a single proportionate framework
- **No Geneva market service offers engagement letter AI disclosure decision framework tailored to Geneva consulting boutique professional liability architecture.**

---

### Category 6: Geneva Accountability Formation Intelligence

**What Exists:**
- Published Federal Council AI doctrine and FINMA guidance (lagging operative standards, arrived after decisions already made)
- General Swiss AI regulatory intelligence services (national scope, not Geneva-specific)
- Industry association publications (SVA for finance, not covering management consulting)

**What's Missing:**
- Geneva accountability formation velocity intelligence: tracking informal circuit signals to identify which accountability expectations are crystallizing before formal publication
- Carrier iteration pattern analysis: mapping how carrier questionnaires evolve across generations to anticipate next attestation要求
- Geneva institutional contact intelligence: sustained informal circuit participation providing position formation signals not available through published channels
- **No Geneva market service offers Geneva accountability formation intelligence function proportionate to boutique scale designed specifically for managing the velocity differential between informal and formal accountability standard-setting.**

---

## Structural Market Gap Summary

| Service Category | Exists in Geneva Market | Gap Type |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-questionnaire response alignment | Generic/enterprise only | Boutique-proportionate service absent |
| Carrier form pre-signing review | Carrier-side only | Signatory-side personal liability review absent |
| Sub-consultant AI delegation architecture | Generic sub-consultant provisions | nFADP Art.9 chain mapping absent |
| AI deliverable version state documentation | IT/software contexts only | Consulting mandate book architecture absent |
| Engagement letter AI disclosure decision framework | Generic templates | Geneva-calibrated decision framework absent |
| Accountability formation velocity intelligence | National/general only | Geneva-specific formation intelligence absent |

**Geneva market currently has no named service category for any of the six discrete accountability architecture needs facing small Geneva consulting boutiques.** The market serves enterprise clients and generic compliance needs. The boutique-scale structural accountability problems have no proportionate service response.
