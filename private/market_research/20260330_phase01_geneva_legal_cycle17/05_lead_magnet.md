**Content:** nFADP Art. 5(c) — AI processing of personal data requires explicit legal basis. Client case files, opposing party information, employee data, business contracts — all in scope.

### Section 4: The EU AI Act — The Hidden Pressure
**Content:** Geneva firms with EU nexus face EU AI Act high-risk obligations activating Aug 2, 2026 (~4.5 months away).

**Three nexus triggers:**

- Operating AI systems with EU-intended output
- Acting as EU importer/distributor of AI systems

**Transparency obligations (GPAI):** Already operative since Aug 2, 2025 for firms with EU market activity.

**Timing signal:** Firms that build governance frameworks now gain first-mover advantage before FDPIC enforcement reaches professional services.
