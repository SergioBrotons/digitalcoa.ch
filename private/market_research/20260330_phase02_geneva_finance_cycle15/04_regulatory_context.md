1. **CARF/CRS 2.0** — LIVE since Jan 1 2026 (~3 months)

   → AI systems processing tax/financial data face immediate obligations

2. **FINMA Guidance 08/2024** — Current baseline

3. **nFADP Art. 5(c)** — ACTIVE since 2023
