## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary Segment
Boutique wealth management firms in Geneva: 2–15 employees, UHNWI/HNWI client base, regulated by FINMA or SRO member, using AI tools for portfolio management, client reporting, or risk analysis without formal AI governance framework.

### Core Problem
AMLA/LETA machinery active (consultation concluded Jan 30, 2026) + FATF mutual evaluation 2026–2027 driving dual pressure on Geneva WM firms. AI tools processing client data without documented governance = compounding compliance risk.

### Timing Signal
- CARF/CRS 2.0: live since Jan 1 2026 (~3 months)
