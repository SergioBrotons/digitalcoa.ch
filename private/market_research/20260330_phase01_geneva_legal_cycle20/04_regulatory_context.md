## Key Regulatory Findings (Cycle 20)
1. **Federal Council sector-specific approach CONFIRMED:** Switzerland will NOT enact a cross-sector AI law similar to EU AI Act before end-2026. CoE AI Convention integration is the primary legislative vehicle.
2. **SBA AI guidelines June 2024:** National baseline exists for Swiss lawyers; not operational small-firm implementation guide.
3. **ANBLES Feb 2026 AI Working Group Report:** Confirmed published; scope covers AI developments tracking; did not produce small-firm-specific operational guidance.
4. **FDPIC enforcement gap:** Sector-specific approach means enforcement expands sector-by-sector — legal not yet confirmed in FDPIC sequence.

---

## Two-Timeline Architecture (Confirmed)
**Timeline A — Formal Federal Council (legislative):**
- Consultation draft: End-2026 (FDJP)
- Legislation: 2027+

**Timeline B — Multi-Stakeholder / Industry (non-legislative):**
- Federal Council First Draft via digitalswitzerland: May 2026 (~2 months)
- Professional body guidance: SBA (June 2024), ANBLES (Feb 2026)

The gap between formal legislation and industry self-regulation creates the advisory opportunity.
