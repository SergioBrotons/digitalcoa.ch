# Phase 02 Cycle 34 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Boutique Wealth Management

## 3-Layer Compliance Architecture (WM-Specific)

### Layer 1: FADP / nFADP (Federal Act on Data Protection)

- **Operative since:** Sep 1, 2023
- **AI-specific obligations:**
  - Art. 5(c): Automated decision-making disclosure (client-facing AI decisions)
  - Privacy by Design: AI system procurement documentation
  - DPIA (Art. 22): Required for high-risk AI processing (client scoring, risk assessment, profiling)
- **FDPIC enforcement pattern:** Legal sector absent → **WM = next sector timing signal**
  - 1,053 FDPIC complaints (2024/2025 Annual Report)
  - 6 FDPIC enforcement actions 2025-2026 (transport, banking, retail, social media)
  - **Law firms NOT targeted → WM firms NOT targeted → timing signal**
- **FDPIC expects:** Purpose + functionality + data sources disclosure for AI deployments

### Layer 2: FINMA Supervision

- **FINMA Circ. 08/2024:** AI governance baseline — operative since August 2024 (~8 months)
- **FINMA Circ. 2026/1:** Additional supervisory communications
- **SRO membership:** Triggering activities (real estate transactions, non-operational entities, domicile services)
- **Advisor scope expansion:** Lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs must join FINMA-recognized SRO
- **FATF evaluation scope:** FINMA-supervised institutions under FATF microscope 2026-2027/2028

### Layer 3: AMLA / CARF / CRS

#### CARF (Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework)
- **Operative since:** Jan 1, 2026 (bilateral CARF arrangements live)
- **Full implementation:** Jan 1, 2027 at earliest (Federal Council Nov 26, 2025 decision)
- **Obligation:** Automatic exchange of crypto-asset information between jurisdictions
- **Boutique WM relevance:** Crypto-holding clients, digital asset exposure

#### CRS 2.0 (Common Reporting Standard Version 2.0)
- **Operative since:** Jan 1, 2026 (confirmed; corrected from earlier confusion)
- **Mandatory NOW:** For FINMA-supervised institutions with cross-border clients
- **No grace period** — live filing obligation
- **Corrected:** Previously conflated with CARF timeline in Phase 02 Cycle 33

#### AMLA / LETA
- **AMLA consultation:** Concluded Jan 30, 2026 (machinery active)
- **LETA Transparency Register:** 30-day discrepancy reporting obligation
- **Implementation:** H2 2026 (~3–6 months)
- **Advisor scope expansion:** Mandatory SRO membership for a wider range of advisors
