### AXIS 1: nFADP — Patient Data AI Processing (Active Since Sep 2023)

**Hook:** Patient data is sensitive personal data under Swiss law. Any AI tool processing patient records triggers nFADP obligations — and the FDPIC has named healthcare a priority enforcement sector.

- nFADP operative since September 1, 2023 — 2.5 years of potential non-compliance exposure
- FDPIC 2024/2025 Annual Report: healthcare = priority enforcement sector
- Only 42% of medical groups have formal AI governance policies (MGMA Jan 2026) — **58% have NOTHING**

**Reality check:** If your practice uses AI for anything involving patient data — scheduling, EMR documentation, diagnostic support — without a documented AI governance framework, you have an active compliance gap.
