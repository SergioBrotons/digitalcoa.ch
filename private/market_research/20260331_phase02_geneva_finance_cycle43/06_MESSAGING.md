## Objection Handling

### "We're below FINMA threshold"
→ CRS 2.0 applies to all Swiss financial institutions regardless of FINMA supervision status.
→ FATF mutual evaluation applies to Switzerland as a whole, not just large institutions.

### "Our custodian bank handles this"
→ CRS/CARF obligations sit with the reporting institution, not the custodian.
→ Your AI inventory and governance is your firm's responsibility.

### "We don't use AI yet"
→ 95% scaled AI across WM sector — if you're not using AI, you're losing competitive position.
→ 78% exploring agentic AI — the governance question applies regardless.
