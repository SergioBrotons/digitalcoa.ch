## The Regulatory Wall Is Arriving. Most Boutique WMs Are Not Ready.

FINMA surveyed 400 Swiss financial institutions in late 2024 and early 2025. The results: 50% are already using AI. 91% are using generative AI (chatbots, text generation, portfolio analytics). Only 50% have an explicit AI strategy.

That means half of Switzerland's financial institutions — many of them boutique WMs like yours — are deploying AI without a documented governance framework.

Meanwhile, August 2026 brings the EU AI Act's high-risk AI obligations into full enforcement for asset managers. Switzerland's Federal AI Ordinance has been operative for 15 months. And FINMA's Guidance 08/2024 makes clear: outsourcing AI to BigTech providers creates direct supervisory oversight obligations.

You're exposed across three frameworks simultaneously. Most boutique WMs don't know it.

---

## What's Creating the Exposure

### Three Concurrent Regulatory Frameworks
- **Federal AI Ordinance** (in force January 1, 2025) — human oversight of AI decisions required
- **FINMA Guidance 08/2024** — AI governance and risk management expectations for Swiss financial institutions
- **EU AI Act** — high-risk obligations enforced August 2026 (~4 months) for asset managers serving EU clients

### FINMA's Outsourcing Risk Flag
FINMA explicitly identified outsourcing to third-party AI providers as a key operational risk. If your AI tools are hosted on US BigTech infrastructure — and 91% of Swiss institutions are — you're subject to FINMA's outsourcing oversight requirements. Most firms haven't documented this.

### Agentic AI: When AI Acts, Not Just Advises
The next wave isn't AI that recommends. It's AI that autonomously executes: automated portfolio rebalancing, AI-driven tax-loss harvesting, algorithmic trade execution within defined parameters. When AI acts, your human oversight obligation becomes a direct operational requirement. Many WMs are already exposed without knowing it.

## The AI Governance Readiness Checklist

Score yourself across these seven areas:

| Area | Key Question | Red Flag |
|---|---|---|
| **AI Tool Inventory** | Do you know every AI tool touching client data, and where it's hosted? | US-hosted AI without documentation |
| **FINMA Outsourcing Risk** | Have you assessed BigTech AI provider risk against FINMA expectations? | No outsourcing policy for AI |
| **EU AI Act Exposure** | Do you serve EU clients with AI-assisted advisory? | No EU AI Act gap analysis |
| **Human Oversight Documentation** | Can you demonstrate human review of AI-assisted decisions? | No review logs or sign-off process |
| **FADP Art. 21/26** | Are AI-assisted decisions affecting clients subject to human review? | No documented review process |
| **Agentic AI Audit** | Is any AI autonomously acting vs. advising (rebalancing, trading, tax)? | Autonomous AI without oversight docs |
| **XAI Explainability** | Can you explain AI-driven portfolio decisions to clients and regulators? | Black-box AI without documentation |

Score 7+/10: You're ahead of most. The checklist helps you document and defend your position.
Score below 7/10: You have clear gaps. This is your trigger to act.
