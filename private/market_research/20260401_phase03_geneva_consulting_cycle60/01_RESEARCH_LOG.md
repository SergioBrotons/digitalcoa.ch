### 3. The Two-Track GPAI Readiness Product: Internal Governance + Client Advisory (NET-NEW C60 — Sharper Framing)
**Source:** Synthesis from C58+C59 Phase 03 + Phase 01 C60 findings

The two-track GPAI readiness offering is now sharper:
- **Track 1 (Internal):** The consulting firm itself must have AI governance in order before advising clients credibly. GPAI obligations already in force since August 2, 2025 mean this is not optional.
- **Track 2 (Client):** The consulting firm's client offering should be structured as: "GPAI readiness assessment" (Are you in scope?) + "EU AI Act exposure assessment" (Do you have EU market exposure?) + "Governance framework development" (What do you need to do?)

**Consulting implications (NET-NEW C60):** The firm that follows its own advice — building a governance framework for itself first — has a living case study to show clients. This is the consulting firm's competitive advantage over generalist IT implementation firms.
