# Geneva Signal Scout
## Intelligence Workflow

### Objective
Identify high-value leads in Geneva experiencing AI governance, data privacy, or decision-making transitions.
Target ICPs (Ideal Client Profiles) based on who struggles with these challenges.

---

## The Four ICPs

### ICP 1: Financial Institutions (Wealth Management / Family Offices)
**Who:** Private banks, multi-family offices, asset managers in Geneva
**Their pain:** Client data privacy, AI for investment decisions, regulatory compliance, board governance
**Firms to track:**
- Pictet & Cie, Lombard Odier, UBP, Edmond de Rothschild
- Mirabaud & Cie, Syz & Partners, Bordier & Cie
- Kaiserer Loth, Gonet & Cie, Banca Safra Sarasin
- Swiss Wealth Management firms

### ICP 2: Healthcare & Life Sciences
**Who:** Hospitals, clinics, medtech, biotech
**Their pain:** Patient data (LPD), AI diagnostics, HIPAA/FINMA overlap, clinical decision support
**Firms to track:**
- HUG (Hôpitaux Universitaires de Genève)
- Clinique Générale-Beaulieu, Clinique La Colline
- Medtech startups in GenevaBioTech cluster
- Pharma regional offices

### ICP 3: Industrial & Manufacturing
**Who:** Swiss industrial companies, engineering firms, supply chain
**Their pain:** IP protection, AI automation, predictive maintenance, quality control
**Firms to track:**
- watch/manufacturing clusters
- Industrialautomation players
- Engineering consultancies
- Swiss manufacturing conglomerates

### ICP 4: Legal & Professional Services
**Who:** Law firms, audit firms, consulting, notaries
**Their pain:** Client confidentiality, AI document review, due diligence, data governance
**Firms to track:**
- Major Geneva law firms
- Big 4 audit firms (Geneva offices)
- Trust and corporate service providers
- Notaries
