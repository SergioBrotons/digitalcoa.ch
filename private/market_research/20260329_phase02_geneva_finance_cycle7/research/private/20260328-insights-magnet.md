# Insights Magnet
## Authority Content Workflow

### Objective
Build authority in Geneva market through targeted, Swiss-specific insights
that establish you as THE expert on AI Governance for financial institutions.

---

## Content Pillars (Rotate weekly)

### Pillar 1: Swiss Data Privacy & AI
- LPD (Loi sur la Protection des Données) + LLM integration
- PFADP updates affecting AI usage
- Cross-border data flows from Switzerland
- Septante/Nonante localization

### Pillar 2: AI Governance for Family Offices
- How Swiss family offices should approach AI governance
- Board-level AI decision frameworks
- Risk appetite for generative AI
- Geneva specificities

### Pillar 3: Regulatory Horizon
- FINMA guidance on AI
- Swiss regulatory landscape changes
- What compliance teams need to know
- Timeline for adaptation

### Pillar 4: Practical Implementation
- Building internal AI policies
- Vendor selection frameworks
- Staff training considerations
- Audit trails and documentation

---

## Tone & Voice
- **First person singular**: "I help executives..." (NOT "we")
- **Calm, precise, restrained** — no hype
- **French-CH context**: septante/nonante for numbers
- **Short paragraphs**: 2-3 sentences max
- **Signal words**: however, therefore, notably

---

## Format (600 words max)

### Structure
```
# [Topic]: [Subtitle]

[2-3 sentence hook — what's happening]

## The Analysis
[Body — 2-3 paragraphs of insight]

## What This Means
[For whom and why it matters]

## The Opportunity
[What decision-makers can do]
```

---

## Publishing Rules
- ❌ NO em dashes (—) — AI tell
- ❌ NO "leverage", "empower", "cutting-edge"
- ❌ NO exclamation marks
- ✅ Colons in titles, not dashes
- ✅ Internal linking to service pages
- ✅ Bilingual: French + English versions

---

## Workflow
1. Pick pillar (rotate)
2. Research source material
3. Draft in English
4. Review/translate to French
5. Save as `YYYYMMDD-insight-[topic].md`
6. Push to `research/private/` for review
7. YOU approve → staging → production
