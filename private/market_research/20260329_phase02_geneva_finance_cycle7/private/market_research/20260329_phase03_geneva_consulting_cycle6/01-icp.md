# Phase 03 Geneva Consulting — ICP (Cycle 6)
## Target Vertical: Small Consulting Firms (2–20 Consultants), Geneva

## ICP Definition

### Firmographic
- **Size:** 2–20 consultants; small boutique or specialized practice
- **Location:** Geneva (Praille-Acacias, La Praille, or emerging periphérie)
- **Structure:** Management consulting, IT consulting, strategy, HR consulting, marketing consulting
- **Revenue context:** CHF 500K–5M; project-based or retainer; price-sensitive on technology

### Technology Posture
- **AI exposure:** High — research, analysis, drafting, presentation, client communication all AI-eligible tasks
- **AI use:** ChatGPT/Gemini/Copilot for client deliverables; often personal accounts; firm-unapproved
- **IT support:** Usually minimal; occasional external IT

### Regulatory Stress Points
1. **EU AI Act GPAI** — operative since Aug 2, 2025 (~7.5 months); active obligation
2. **nFADP** — AI processing of client business data = Art. 5(c) triggered; DPIA required
3. **IT/Tech sub-segment** — highest EU AI Act Annex III risk; finance/healthcare/education/critical infrastructure AI systems
4. **FDJP consultation** — end-2026 (~9 months); will crystallize standards
5. **Dual compliance** — nFADP + EU AI Act simultaneously

### Competitive Landscape
- **No boutique competitor confirmed:** 6th consecutive cycle
- **Technology providers (non-competitive):** ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Salesforce Einstein, ServiceNow AI
- **Market gap:** No dedicated small consulting AI governance advisory in Geneva
