## Lead Magnet Architecture

### The 3-Question Framework

**Question 1: What AI Are You Using? (Inventory)**
- Property valuation AI tools
- Tenant / credit screening systems
- Smart building / IoT systems
- Marketing automation / chatbots
- CRM with AI features
- Access control / facial recognition

→ This surfaces the agency's actual AI footprint, creating awareness of data processing activities

**Question 2: What Data Is Being Processed? (Classification)**
- Tenant personal data
- Tenant financial data
- Biometric data (facial recognition)
- Property data (non-personal)
- Smart building occupant data
- Buyer/seller contact data

→ This triggers Art. 5(c) sensitivity awareness and DPIA requirements

**Question 3: Do You Have nFADP Obligations? (Compliance)**
- Are data subjects informed about AI processing?
- Do you have data processing agreements with AI vendors?
- Have you conducted DPIAs where required?
- Can tenants/buyers exercise their data rights?
- Do you have breach notification procedures?
- Is your managing director aware of personal liability?

→ This creates urgency and positions DigitalCoa.ch as the solution
