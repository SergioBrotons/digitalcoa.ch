## Competitive Timing Signal

**Why no boutique competitor has emerged (7 cycles):**
1. **Market uncertainty:** FDJP consultation not yet published — advisors waiting for regulatory clarity before positioning
2. **nFADP complexity:** Small firm AI governance is technically challenging to scope and price
3. **No Geneva precedent:** No FDPIC enforcement case in legal sector — no forcing function to drive demand
4. **Economics:** Small firm engagement economics challenging for boutique advisory model

**What will trigger competitive entry:**
- FDJP consultation draft publication (end-2026) — will provide regulatory clarity
- First FDPIC law firm enforcement case — will create immediate demand
- Market activation post-August 2026 EU AI Act deadline — demand spike

**First-mover advantage window: ~12–18 months** (stable)
