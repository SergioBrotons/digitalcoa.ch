## Switzerland Consulting Market — Baseline

### Market Size (Cycle 1 Data — Retained)
- Swiss management consulting market: ~USD 3.58B (2025), growing at ~4.5% CAGR through 2031
- Source: Mordor Intelligence
- Geneva's role: #2 hub in Switzerland after Zurich; 10% rate premium vs. national average

### Geneva-Specific Premium Dynamics
- Geneva premium driven by: private banking, commodity trading, international organisations
- Multilingual expertise (FR/EN/DE minimum) is baseline, not differentiator
- Key sectors: financial services, pharmaceuticals, precision manufacturing, commodities, international orgs

## AI Adoption Signals — Cycle 2 Refinements

### General Swiss AI Adoption (Cycle 1 Data)
- ~50% of Swiss firms using AI in some form (various surveys, 2024)
- AI adoption in services: growing but uneven; large firms lead

### Small Consulting Firm AI Adoption — Key Pattern
- **Mass adoption of ChatGPT/Claude at individual level** — unmanaged, ungoverned
- **Firm-level AI strategy** — rare in firms <20 employees
- **Client-driven AI requirements** — growing in RFPs; methodology sections now ask about AI
- **nFADP has raised the stakes** — consultants using AI tools without DPA are unknowingly exposed
- **Competitive pressure building** — larger boutiques building AI-augmented delivery; small firms feel the gap widening

## Geneva Business Context

### Firm Count Estimates
- Geneva Canton: ~800+ registered consulting firms (various sizes)
- Estimated ~600 firms in 2–15 employee range (ICP addressable)
- ~75% are 1–5 person operations (sole proprietors + micro-boutiques)

### Key Clustering Areas
- Rue du Rhône corridor
- La Praille business district
- Grand-Saconnex / Lancy

### Client Base (Geneva)
- International orgs: UN, WHO, WTO, ICRC, ITU — large consulting mandates
- NGOs and associations — growing segment for sustainability/strategy consulting
- Multinationals: commodities traders, pharma, precision manufacturing
