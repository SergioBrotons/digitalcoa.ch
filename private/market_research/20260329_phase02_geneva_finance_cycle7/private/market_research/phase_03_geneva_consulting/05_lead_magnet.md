## Vertical: Consulting / SMB

## Research Status: FRAMEWORK

## Lead Magnet Concept (Preliminary)

### Proposed Title (EN)
**"The Small Consulting Firm AI Readiness Checklist"**
A practical, step-by-step guide to adopting AI tools safely and compliantly in a small Swiss consulting practice.

### Proposed Title (FR)
**"Checklist de Readiness IA pour Petits Cabinets de Conseil"**

---

## Value Proposition

**What it does:** Helps small Geneva consulting firms (2–15 people) assess their current AI tool adoption level, identify compliance gaps (nFADP), and plan safe AI integration.

**Target pain:** Consultants want to use AI tools for research, slides, reports, and client deliverables — but are uncertain about data protection obligations, confidentiality, and quality control.

**Format:** Digital checklist (PDF or Notion template) with accompanying short guide (5–10 pages).

---

## Content Outline (Preliminary)

### Section 1: Why AI Matters for Small Consulting Firms
- Efficiency gains (real examples)
- Competitive pressure: clients expect faster turnaround
- Geneva market context

### Section 2: AI Readiness Self-Assessment (Checklist)
- Current AI tool inventory
- Data handling practices
- Client confidentiality protocols
- nFADP compliance gap assessment
- Quality control for AI-assisted deliverables

### Section 3: Quick Wins — AI Tools for Small Consultancies
- Research (Perplexity, Claude)
- Slide creation (Canva, Gamma)
- Document drafting (Claude, ChatGPT)
- Project management (Notion + AI)

### Section 4: Safe AI Adoption Protocol
- Data classification (what can/cannot go into AI tools)
- Client communication about AI use
- Quality review process for AI-assisted work
- AI vendor due diligence checklist

### Section 5: Compliance Checklist (nFADP)
- Record of processing activities
- Data breach response plan
- Third-party AI tool assessment
- Consent management

### Section 6: Next Steps
- 30/60/90-day AI adoption roadmap
- When to engage outside help (DigitalCoa.ch)
