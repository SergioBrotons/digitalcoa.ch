## Target Profile (Preliminary)

### Firm Characteristics (Hypotheses — Require Validation)
- **Headquarters:** Geneva (likely La Ville, Champod, or Geneva city center)
- **Size:** 2–15 employees; typically 2–5 consultants
- **Structure:** SARL, Sàrl, or small SA; often founder-led or partnership
- **Revenue range:** CHF 500K–5M annually
- **Clients served:** SMEs, startups, occasionally large corporates, public entities
- **Specializations:** Management consulting, strategy, HR consulting, IT consulting, marketing consulting, financial consulting

### Decision-Maker Profile — Primary: Managing Director / Founder
- 40–60 years old
- Professional background (engineer, economist, lawyer, or MBA)
- Operates as final decision-maker
- Digitally aware but values efficiency over novelty
- Time-poor; values ROI and practical tools
- Primary concern: client acquisition, project delivery, talent retention, revenue growth

### Decision-Maker Profile — Secondary: Senior Consultant / Associate
- 30–45 years old
- More likely to engage with tools, methodologies, and tech adoption
- Influences or recommends tools to managing director
- Pain points: project management, client reporting, research and analysis tooling
