## Competitive Tool Landscape (Preliminary)

### Research & Analysis Tools
- **Market research platforms:** Statista, IBISWorld, Euromonitor (SMB access increasing)
- **AI research assistants:** Perplexity, Claude, ChatGPT (research and synthesis)
- **Data analysis:** Excel, Google Sheets, Airtable, Notion

### Presentation & Deliverable Tools
- **Slide creation:** PowerPoint, Google Slides, Canva
- **Document creation:** Google Docs, Notion, Microsoft 365
- **PDF/report generation:** Notion, Canva, Adobe

### Project & Client Management
- **CRM:** HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce (for business development)
- **Project management:** Asana, Monday.com, Trello, Notion
- **Client portals:** Custom or basic extranet solutions

### Communication & Collaboration
- **Video conferencing:** Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams
- **Async collaboration:** Slack, Microsoft Teams, Email
