# Phase 03 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Consulting Vertical

## Lead Magnet Concept

### Working Title
> **"The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"**
> *(French: "Guide de Décision IA pour Petits Cabinets de Conseil")*

### Core Value Proposition
A practical, visual decision framework that helps small Geneva consulting firm leaders (2–15 people) navigate AI adoption without:
- Spending months evaluating vendors
- Risking client data confidentiality
- Overcomplicating their practice
- Falling behind competitors who are adopting carelessly

### Format Options
| Format | Pros | Cons | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PDF Guide** (12–16 pages) | Credible, downloadable, bilingual | Requires distribution | ✅ Primary format |
| **Interactive Web Tool** | Engaging, differentiates | Development cost | Consider as Phase 04 add |
| **Email Course (3-part)** | Builds relationship | Slower nurture | Supplementary |
| **LinkedIn carousel** | High reach, low friction | Shallow | Supplement PDF |

### Recommended Primary Format: Bilingual PDF Guide (EN+FR)

## Content Outline

### Cover
- Title: "The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"
- Subtitle: "How to adopt AI tools without compromising client trust or burning your team"
- DigitalCoa.ch branding
- Language toggle note: "Also available in French"

### Section 1: The Honest Landscape (2 pages)
- What's actually happening: AI adoption in Swiss consulting
- Why small firms are behind (and why that's not all bad)
- The two failure modes: adopting too fast (risk) vs. ignoring AI (competitive)
- The Geneva-specific context: international clients, nFADP, multilingual requirements

**EN Hook:** "You've probably tried ChatGPT for a proposal. Now you're wondering: is this helping me or creating liability?"

**FR Hook:** "Vous avez probablement testé ChatGPT pour un devis. Maintenant, vous vous demandez : est-ce que cela m'aide ou crée un risque ?"

### Section 2: The AI Decision Framework (4 pages)
- Visual framework: 3-axis model
  - **Axis 1:** Client Data Sensitivity (Low / Medium / High)
  - **Axis 2:** AI Output Impact (Advisory / Client-Facing / Internal)
  - **Axis 3:** Integration Complexity (Standalone / Integrated / Embedded)
- Decision matrix: which AI tools fit which quadrant
- Tool categories mapped to quadrants

### Section 3: The Confidentiality Checklist (2 pages)
- Practical checklist for evaluating AI vendors
- Questions to ask before sending any client data to an AI tool
- The nFADP compliance mini-guide for AI tools
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) red flags

### Section 4: Implementation Roadmap (2 pages)
- Month 1–3: Quick wins without risk
- Month 4–6: Structured adoption
- Month 7–12: Competitive differentiation via AI-augmented delivery
- Resource recommendations (curated, not comprehensive)

### Section 5: The Geneva Advantage (1 page)
- Why Geneva consulting firms are uniquely positioned to lead AI adoption
- Multilingual AI, cross-border clients, international org mandates
- How small size is actually an advantage in AI agility

### Closing CTA
- "Ready to make your first AI decision with confidence? Let's talk."
- Link to: digitalcoa.ch/contact or calendar booking
- Secondary: Newsletter signup

## Bilingual Strategy Notes

### French-First Preference for Geneva
- Geneva is French-speaking; FR content resonates more
- But Geneva's consulting elite is often bilingual (FR + EN)
- **Recommendation:** FR as default, EN as secondary version
- Both versions should be high quality — don't machine-translate

### Content Tone
- Not academic — practical and direct
- Acknowledge the pressure without amplifying anxiety
- No hype, no scare tactics — calm, authoritative, like a trusted peer
- Use "we" (DigitalCoa.ch) sparingly; focus on "you and your firm"
