## Why Large Firms Won't Capture This Segment
- **Pricing:** Small practice (CHF 300K–1.2M revenue) cannot afford Big 4 rates
- **Relationship:** Physicians need trusted peer-adjacent advisor, not anonymous consultancy
- **Language:** Geneva + Vaud bilingual (FR + EN) — large firms often EN-only or FR-only
- **Scope:** Small practice needs practical clarity, not comprehensive compliance program
- **Accessibility:** 1-hour consultation at CHF 300–500 is accessible; 20-hour project at CHF 30,000 is not
- **Institutional AI gap:** Large firms focus on hospital/institutional AI; small practice segment ignored

---

## Why IT/Tech Vendors Won't Capture This Segment
- **Incentive mismatch:** Vendors want to sell tools; governance advisory is orthogonal to their revenue model
- **Expertise gap:** SwissGPT Health (AlpineAI) is a product; doesn't provide nFADP compliance framework
- **Swissmedic knowledge:** Most IT vendors unfamiliar with medical device classification requirements
- **CHUV competition:** Institutional AI market is where IT vendors focus; small practice governance is unsexy
