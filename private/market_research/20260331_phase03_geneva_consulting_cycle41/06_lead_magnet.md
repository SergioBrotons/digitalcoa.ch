# Phase 03 Geneva Consulting — Lead Magnet: "The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework" — Cycle 41

**Geneva Consulting Firms — AI Governance Decision Framework**

---

## C41 Lead Magnet Framing

### The Core Insight
**Consulting firms face a unique combination: high AI use + high project failure rate + GPAI exposure.**

73% of AI projects fail due to skills gaps.
Yet consulting firms are early AI adopters.
And GPAI has been operative since August 2, 2025 (~8 months) for firms with EU nexus.

40% of Geneva SME consulting firms have an AI skills gap.
This is not just a governance problem — it is a service quality problem.

---

## The 9-Section Framework

**Section 1: Shadow AI Audit (Decision 0)**
Identify all AI tools used by consultants personally and in client engagements.
Risk: GPAI transparency gap + FADP Art. 5(c) exposure.

**Section 2: GPAI Obligations Assessment (EU Nexus)**
Assess which client engagements trigger GPAI obligations.
M&A advisory, cross-border consulting, EU-funded projects = likely GPAI scope.
Document GPAI compliance requirements.

**Section 3: FADP Art. 21/5(c) Compliance**
AI-assisted consulting deliverables require human review capability.
AI-generated reports require client disclosure.
Document FADP compliance protocols.

**Section 4: AI Skills Gap Assessment**
40% of Geneva SME consulting firms have AI skills gap.
73% of AI projects fail due to skills gap.
Assess firm's AI project capability.

**Section 5: AI Project Failure Risk Mitigation**
Skills gap → project failure risk.
Governance framework can reduce failure risk.
AI governance = service quality framework.

**Section 6: Agentic AI Governance (78% Exploring)**
78% of Swiss firms exploring agentic AI.
Agentic AI = autonomous agents taking actions without human-in-the-loop.
Consulting firms may use agentic AI for research, analysis, reporting.
New risk category requiring governance framework.

**Section 7: Client Disclosure Protocols**
FADP Art. 5(c): must disclose AI in client communications.
GPAI Art. 50: must disclose AI interaction to users.
Update client engagement letters and deliverables.

**Section 8: Swiss-Sovereign AI Stack**
US-hosted AI tools create CLOUD Act exposure.
Client confidentiality requires Swiss/EU hosting.
FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL: Swiss-sovereign, FADP-aligned.

**Section 9: Formal AI Policy**
Create firm-level AI governance policy.
Define approved AI tools, use cases, data handling.
Assign AI governance responsibility.
