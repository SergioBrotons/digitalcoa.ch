**~8 months already operative**

**GPAI Provisions Active:**
- Transparency obligations for AI interactions
- Human oversight requirements for high-risk AI

- Conformity assessment for Annex I AI systems

**Swiss Consulting Firms with EU Nexus = GPAI Scope**
- M&A advisory with EU clients
- Cross-border consulting engagements
- EU-funded project consulting
- Subsidiary/branch operations in EU

**Key GPAI Timeline:**
- **Aug 2 2025**: GPAI provisions operative (already ~8 months)
- **Aug 2 2026**: Full EU AI Act applies (12 months after GPAI)
- **May 2026**: digitalswitzerland AI Action Plan First Draft (external pressure)
- **end-2026**: Federal Council formal legislation (Swiss compliance deadline)

---

### Secondary: FADP — Operative since September 1, 2023
**2.5+ years already live**

**FDPIC confirmed (May 2025)**: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing
- No new legislation required
- Existing FADP obligations already operative

**Art. 21 FADP: Human Review**
- Automated decisions producing legal effects must allow human review
- AI-assisted consulting deliverables = Art. 21 applicability

**Art. 5(c) FADP: Transparency**
- Must disclose AI involvement in client communications
- AI-generated reports, deliverables = disclosure required

**Art. 16 FADP: Data Integrity**
- AI systems processing personal data must maintain integrity
- Client data confidentiality extends to AI tools

**DPIA**: Required for high-risk AI (client analysis, strategic recommendations)
