# Lead Magnet Framework — Phase 04 Geneva Healthcare — Cycle 61

## Lead Magnet Topic
**"The Small Medical Practice AI Decision Framework" (Swissdamed Edition) — Q2 2026**

## Core Question Being Asked
Does your Geneva medical practice have Swissdamed remediation completed — and have you assessed which of your AI tools face EU AI Act conformity obligations?

## Lead Magnet Format
Decision framework / self-assessment guide — PDF-ready

## Angle
Geneva small medical practices (1–10 physicians) face a compressed timeline: Swissdamed enforcement is July 1, 2026 (~91 days away), EU AI Act Annex III healthcare AI conformity assessments are August 2, 2026 (~4 months), and most practices have not completed either. This framework helps the practice assess its dual compliance exposure and prioritize remediation actions.

## Target Persona
- Geneva small medical practice (general practice, specialist, dental, physiotherapy)
- 1–10 physicians / practitioners
- Using AI tools (scheduling, billing, clinical notes, diagnostic support, patient communication)
- No formal AI governance policy
- Primary fear: Swissdamed enforcement + Swissmedic audit
- Secondary fear: serving EU patients with AI tools that may face EU AI Act Annex III classification

## Value Proposition
Practical self-assessment that surfaces the dual compliance gap (Swissdamed + EU AI Act) and gives the practice a prioritized action plan starting with the most urgent deadlines.

## Key Hook Points (from C61 findings)
1. Swissdamed July 1, 2026 — ~91 days — enforcement posture confirmed (no delay from Federal Council April 1, 2026)
2. EU AI Act Annex III — AI diagnostic tools, clinical decision support, patient monitoring likely within scope for EU patients
3. Swissdamed remediation window has closed — this is now enforcement mode, not preparation mode
4. Federal Council sector-specific healthcare AI guidance not yet materialized — practice operates under general frameworks
5. Data sovereignty in healthcare AI: patient trust + FADP compliance + international patient data

## Structure
1. Does Swissdamed apply to your practice? (3 qualifying questions)
2. Swissdamed compliance gap assessment (are you ready for July 1, 2026?)
3. Your AI tool inventory (clinical and administrative AI — each needs FADP assessment)
4. EU AI Act Annex III exposure (does your AI diagnostic or clinical support tool face EU requirements?)
5. Data sovereignty and international patients (HNW/UHNW international patients — FADP + data sovereignty)
6. Prioritized action plan: what to do in the next 30 days, 60 days, and 90 days
