(Translation: SVITpedia is an intelligent chatbot based on state-of-the-art AI, specifically tailored to the needs of the real estate industry.)

- **Trained on:** SVIT's accumulated real estate professional knowledge base (from SVIT's own publications, educational content, and association knowledge)

### Critical governance observation:

**SVITpedia has NO visible FADP or AI governance framework.**

The quality assurance description focuses on:
- Expert review of content relevance and correctness
- Topic relevance, professional precision, and comprehensibility
- Algorithm optimization for answer relevance

There is NO mention of:
- FADP Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- FDPIC AI update obligations (September 24, 2025)
- Transparency obligations when AI generates responses
- Personal data processing in chatbot interactions
- GDPR/EU AI Act compliance for French-speaking users

**Implication:** SVIT — Switzerland's leading real estate industry association — deployed a production AI chatbot (March 2025) without visible AI governance compliance framework. This is the most concrete evidence of the Geneva real estate AI governance gap.
