### Federal Council vs. EU AI Act: The Core Strategic Tension
**C62 confirms** this is the central strategic tension for all Geneva SMB sectors:
- Federal Council: technology-neutral, principles-based, sector-specific approach
- EU AI Act: comprehensive, extraterritorial, Annex III high-risk classification
- Swiss-EU bilateral package: no AI harmonization
- Result: Swiss firms with EU exposure face dual framework burden

**For DigitalCoa.ch advisory positioning:** The Federal Council approach is the operative Swiss baseline; EU AI Act is the extraterritorial overlay for EU-facing activities.
