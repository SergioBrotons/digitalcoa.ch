## DigitalCoa.ch Competitive Landscape (C125)

### Advisory Competitors

| Firm | Type | Strengths | Weaknesses | DigitalCoa.ch Differentiation |
|------|------|-----------|------------|------------------------------|
| Big 4 (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY) | Global professional services | Brand, scale, resources | Expensive, generic, slow | Geneva-specific, independent, faster |
| Strategy firms (McKinsey, BCG, Bain) | Strategy consulting | Strategy brand, senior talent | Very expensive, corporate culture | Geneva context, independent judgment |
| Swiss boutique consultancies | Niche specialists | Swiss market knowledge | Limited scale | Independent, Geneva sector depth |
| Independent Geneva advisors | Solo/small | Flexibility, personal relationships | No scale, capacity limits | Systematic, scalable advisory model |
| Geneva sector associations | Industry bodies | Network access, credibility | Limited advisory capability | Advisory excellence, not association |

### Competitive Gaps

**Gap 01: Post-Briefing-Call Systematic Follow-Up**
Most advisors under-invest in post-call follow-up. DigitalCoa.ch can differentiate through systematic, respectful, high-value follow-up that respects Geneva executive decision-making pace.

**Gap 02: Geneva-Specific Proposal Format**
Big 4 proposals are polished but generic. Independent advisors often produce informal proposals. A Geneva-specific proposal format (concise, substantive, Geneva context) differentiates.

**Gap 03: Remote Delivery Quality Standards**
Many independent advisors deliver remotely but inconsistently. Professional remote delivery standards (video quality, secure communication, document standards) differentiate DigitalCoa.ch.

**Gap 04: Systematic Referral Program**
Most independent advisors rely on accidental referrals. Systematic referral program design captures organic referrals more efficiently; differentiated from transactional referral incentive programs.

**Gap 05: Content-to-Client Nurture Sequence**
Most advisors don't connect their content to their sales process. brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch pathway is a unique differentiator that no Geneva competitor has.
