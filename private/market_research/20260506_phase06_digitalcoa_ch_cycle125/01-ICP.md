# 01 — ICP: DigitalCoa.ch Service Clients

## Target Segment: Geneva Executive Decision-Makers

### Client Profile
- **Role:** CEO, Managing Director, CFO, Board Member, or Senior Executive
- **Company:** Geneva-based company CHF 20M-500M+ revenue; or international company with Geneva operations/interest
- **Sector:** Trading, finance, professional services, manufacturing, technology, family business
- **Decision-making:** Typically solo or small senior team; fast decision-making when trust is established
- **Language:** EN or FR; often bilingual; some DE
- **Status:** Geneva executives are relationship-driven buyers; trust precedes capability

### Decision-Making Psychology
| Stage | What the Executive Needs | DigitalCoa.ch Role |
|-------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Awareness | Geneva-relevant intelligence that confirms their thinking | brotons.net content |
| Consideration | Analysis that helps them understand their problem better | brotons.net + DigitalCoa.ch insights |
| Decision | Trusted advisor who understands Geneva context | Brief call → proposal |
| Engagement | Execution that delivers value without micromanagement | Advisory delivery |
| Expansion | Continued value; referral to peers | Satisfaction + referral program |

### Known Geneva Executive Types
- **Trading House Executive:** Fast decisions, direct communication, substance over style
- **Family Business CEO:** Succession, governance, long-term thinking
- **Corporate Executive (MNCI):** Regulatory, strategic, board communication
- **Finance Executive:** Risk, reporting, investor relations
- **Professional Services Partner:** Strategy, positioning, client development

### Pain Points (C125 Update)
1. **Post-briefing-call uncertainty** — what happens next? Follow-up that converts vs. follows up to close
2. **Proposal evaluation** — Geneva executives are skeptical of generic proposals; need Geneva-specific framing
3. **Remote advisory trust** — can remote delivery match in-person quality? Geneva executives need reassurance
4. **Time poverty** — Geneva executives are extremely busy; every touchpoint must be high-value and concise
5. **Trust verification** — Geneva executives verify through references, content, and direct conversation
6. **Referral hesitation** — Geneva executives don't refer unless they have high confidence in the advisor

## Target Segment: Geneva Companies Considering Advisory Engagements

### Company Profile
- **Size:** CHF 20M-500M+ revenue
- **Location:** Geneva or Vaud
- **Stage:** Growth, transition, crisis, or strategic inflection point
- **Trigger events:** Leadership change, regulatory change, market disruption, succession, expansion

### Buying Triggers
- New CEO or Managing Director appointment
- Regulatory change affecting their business
- Market disruption (tariffs, FX, competitive pressure)
- Board or shareholder pressure
- Succession planning urgency
- Strategic planning cycle

### Buying Barriers
- Established advisory relationships (Big 4, existing consultants)
- Trust verification challenges
- Pricing uncertainty
- Remote delivery skepticism
- Decision-making speed (Geneva executives deliberate but decisive)

### Language / Cultural Fit
- French primary for Geneva domestic; English for international business
- German useful for cross-border CH-EU matters
- Direct, substantive communication — no marketing fluff
- Geneva business culture: quality, credibility, trust
