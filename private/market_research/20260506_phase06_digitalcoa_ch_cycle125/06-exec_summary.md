## NET-NEW Findings: 6 Key Discoveries (C125 Phase 06)

### Finding 01: Post-Briefing-Call Conversion — Systematic Follow-Up
After briefing call, Geneva executives need clear next steps. Systematic follow-up that respects their decision-making pace (2-6 weeks) and provides high-value touchpoints converts better than aggressive follow-up. Key: what to send, when, how to nurture without pressure.

### Finding 02: Geneva Executive Proposal Format
Geneva executives expect proposals that are concise (2-4 pages), Geneva-specific (not generic), substance-first (capability before credentials), and clear on scope/pricing/timeline. Big 4 proposals are polished but lack Geneva context. A Geneva-specific proposal format differentiates.

### Finding 03: Briefing Call Conversion Optimization
Briefing call is the inflection point from content relationship to advisory relationship. Optimized structure: pre-call brief, structured 30-minute call, clear next-step definition at end, same-day follow-up. Conversion rate optimization at this stage is the highest-leverage activity for DigitalCoa.ch.

### Finding 04: Remote Delivery Quality Standards
Post-pandemic Geneva executives accept remote delivery but expect professional quality: video (not phone-only), screen-shared documents, secure communication, prompt follow-up. Remote delivery quality is a credibility signal. Sub-standard remote delivery signals sub-standard advisory.

### Finding 05: Geneva Referral Program Design
Geneva executives refer based on trust, not incentives. Systematic referral program: referral moment identification, trust-based mechanics (not transactional incentives), and warm peer introductions. Organic referrals from satisfied clients are the primary new client acquisition channel.

### Finding 06: Content-to-Client Nurture Sequence
Systematic approach to converting brotons.net readers to DigitalCoa.ch clients. Sequence: brotons.net piece → retargeting/follow-up email → aligned thought leadership → brief call invitation → briefing call → proposal. brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch pathway is a unique differentiator no Geneva competitor has.
