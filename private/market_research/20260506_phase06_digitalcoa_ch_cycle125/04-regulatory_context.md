# 04 — Regulatory Context: DigitalCoa.ch Service

## DigitalCoa.ch Service Context (C125)

### Context Item 01: Geneva Advisory Market Standards
**Status:** Geneva advisory market expects high standards: substance, credibility, confidentiality, professional delivery
**Key considerations:** Professional liability, contract standards, confidentiality agreements, engagement letter practices
**Implications for DigitalCoa.ch:** Professional engagement frameworks, contract standards, delivery quality assurance

---

### Context Item 02: Swiss Contract Law for Advisory Engagements
**Status:** Swiss contract law (OR obligations) governs advisory engagements; specific terms required for mandate contracts, liability limitations, IP ownership
**Key considerations:** Engagement letter vs. full contract, liability limitation clauses, work product ownership, termination provisions
**Implications for DigitalCoa.ch:** Standard engagement letter templates, liability framework, IP protection

---

### Context Item 03: Data Protection for Advisory Work (FADP)
**Status:** Swiss FADP applies to client data in advisory engagements; client information, project data, and communications require data protection compliance
**Key considerations:** Data processing agreements, client consent, data retention, secure communication
**Implications for DigitalCoa.ch:** Client data protection framework, secure communication standards, FADP compliance

---

### Context Item 04: Remote Advisory Delivery Standards
**Status:** No specific regulation for advisory delivery format; professional standards and client expectations define quality
**Key considerations:** Video conferencing standards, document sharing security, communication channel security
**Implications for DigitalCoa.ch:** Remote delivery quality standards, client expectation management, security protocols

---

### Context Item 05: Professional Liability and Insurance
**Status:** Professional liability insurance recommended for advisory services; Swiss market standards for coverage
**Key considerations:** Coverage limits, policy terms, claims history
**Implications for DigitalCoa.ch:** Professional liability coverage, client confidence, risk management
