### Finding 03 | Swiss Domestic ETS — EU ETS Divergence Advisory
**Sector:** Carbon Markets / Climate Compliance / Geneva Emitters + Traders
**Angle:** Switzerland and the EU linked their emissions trading systems (ETS) in 2020, but the link agreement contains divergence mechanisms and Switzerland's domestic ETS has evolved separately since. Post-2024, the two systems are increasingly divergent in allowance pricing mechanics, sectoral coverage, and carbon border adjustment interaction. Geneva-based emitters subject to both Swiss ETS and EU CBAM face compliance complexity. Geneva carbon traders and intermediaries face pricing arbitrage opportunities. Advisory opportunity: Swiss ETS/EU ETS compliance mapping for Geneva emitters, CBAM interaction analysis, carbon allowance portfolio strategy for Geneva traders, and carbon market structuring for Swiss industrial firms.

**Differentiation from prior cycles:** C127 Phase 03 and prior cycles mentioned carbon markets and CBAM broadly. C128 isolates the Swiss ETS/EU ETS divergence as a specific technical compliance problem with direct implications for Geneva carbon market participants — a more granular, instrument-specific angle.

**Confidence:** High (domain knowledge — Swiss ETS, EU ETS, CBAM, Geneva carbon trading)
