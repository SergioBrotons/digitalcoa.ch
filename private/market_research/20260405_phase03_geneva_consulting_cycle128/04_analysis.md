## Geneva Consulting Market Structure

### Boutique vs. Big 4 Positioning
- **Where boutiques win:** M&A regulatory due diligence (complexity), family office governance (intimate, sensitive), board governance (confidential), OT security for SMEs (price-sensitive)
- **Where Big 4 dominate:** CSRD large-cap, DORA TPRM for large banks, global M&A
- **Opportunity:** Regulatory complexity premium favors boutiques in Geneva's mid-market

### Pricing Benchmarks (Domain Knowledge)
- Board governance advisory: CHF 15,000-50,000 per engagement
- OT security gap assessment: CHF 20,000-80,000 per SME
- M&A regulatory due diligence sprint: CHF 30,000-100,000
- Family office governance framework: CHF 25,000-75,000
- Swiss neutrality compliance framework: CHF 20,000-60,000
- ETS/CBAM mapping: CHF 15,000-45,000

## Geneva-Specific Factors
- **Regulatory density:** Swiss firms face simultaneous compliance obligations across multiple domains — the "stack" effect creates consulting demand
- **SME concentration:** Geneva's SME market is underserved by traditional consulting — price-sensitive, need practical implementation not frameworks
- **UHNW population:** Family office governance is a large, persistent market driven by generational succession
- **International exposure:** Neutrality framework, export controls, ETS/CBAM all reflect Geneva's international business exposure
