## ICP: Small Consulting Firm (Geneva)

### Core Characteristics
- **Size:** 2–15 consultants; boutique strategy, IT, management, HR, or specialized consulting
- **Geography:** Geneva; Romandie expansion
- **Client base:** SMBs, startups, mid-market companies in Geneva and Romandie
- **AI exposure:** Uses AI tools for analysis, reporting, client deliverables, proposal generation
- **Primary pressure:** GPAI ~8.5 months live (Aug 2 2025); EU AI Act transparency obligations ACTIVE NOW; Federal Council First Draft May 2026 (~2 months)

### New Market Signal — M&A IT Sector +28% (Cycle 17 NEW)
- Swiss SME M&A: 208 deals in 2025 (+16% vs 2024)
- **IT services/software: +28% of domestic and inbound transactions** (from +14% in 2024) — AI/tech sector leading
- Healthcare/life sciences: +16%
- Inbound (foreign acquisition of Swiss SMEs): 65% increase — record level
- **Implication:** More consulting demand in IT/tech due diligence + post-merger AI integration → more AI governance exposure for consulting firms

### Labor Shortage Signal — 400,000 Workers (Cycle 17 NEW)
- Swiss SME labor shortage: 400,000 workers gap (KMU/SECO, KOF/Centre for Economic Research, Mar 25 2026)
- **Implication:** SMBs turning to AI to address labor shortage → increased AI adoption → increased AI governance need → consulting firms advising SMBs on AI adoption carry governance advisory opportunity

### Timing Signal
- **GPAI transparency obligations:** ACTIVE NOW (Aug 2 2025 — ~8.5 months live)
- **Federal Council First Draft May 2026:** ~2 months away — next external pressure event
- **Advisory window:** ~9 months to Aug 2 2026 high-risk activation

### Pain Points
- GPAI obligations already active; consulting firms using AI tools may already be non-compliant
- 40% Geneva SMEs lack AI skills (PwC 2024) — AI adoption ahead of governance
- No sector-specific guidance for consulting firms from Swiss authorities
- Federal Council "lean approach" means no cross-sector law before formal process end-2026

### Competitive Landscape
- **9th+ consecutive confirmation:** All identified competitors enterprise-focused (Swiss Cyber Institute, Govern AI, Swiss Infosec AG, AI Legal & Strategy AG, Modulos AG, GAIGI) — NOT small firm

### Addressable Market (Geneva)
- ~500–800 small consulting firms in Geneva (2–15 consultants)
- Romandie expansion: ~1,200–1,800 firms across Vaud, Geneva, Neuchâtel
