# Phase 06 Geneva Real Estate — Cycle 41: Regulatory Context

**Cycle 41 — March 31, 2026**

---

## Regulatory Architecture

### Primary: FADP — Operative since September 1, 2023
**2.5+ years already live**

**FDPIC confirmed (May 2025)**: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing

**Art. 5(c) FADP: Transparency**
- Must disclose AI involvement in client communications
- AI-assisted property valuations = disclosure required
- AI-generated listings, descriptions, market analyses = disclosure required

**Art. 21 FADP: Human Review**
- Automated decisions producing legal effects must allow human review
- AI-assisted tenant screening, property valuations = Art. 21 applicability

**Art. 16 FADP: Data Integrity**
- AI systems processing personal data must maintain integrity
- Tenant data, property data, client data = Art. 16 scope

**DPIA**: Required for high-risk AI (property valuation AI, tenant screening AI)

---

### Secondary: EU AI Act (Extraterritorial)
**GPAI operative since August 2, 2025**
