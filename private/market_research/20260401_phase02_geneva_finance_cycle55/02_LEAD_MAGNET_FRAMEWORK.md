# Phase 02 — Lead Magnet Framework — Cycle 55

## Lead Magnet Title
**"The Boutique Wealth Manager's AI Governance Readiness Checklist"**

## ICP (Ideal Client Profile)
Geneva/Swiss Romandie boutique wealth managers and asset managers (AUM CHF 50M–500M)
- Using or piloting AI tools for portfolio management, reporting, client communication
- FINMA Guidance 08/2024 obligations already operative (since Dec 18, 2024)
- No dedicated AI governance or risk framework
- ~75pt belief-action gap (high AI belief, low AI governance)
- Decision-maker: CEO / Managing Partner / CIO

## Core Problem Statement
Boutique wealth managers believe in AI but lack the governance infrastructure to deploy it safely — and FINMA enforcement is accelerating.

## Primary Entry Point (Decision 0)
AI Governance Readiness Assessment: "How ready are you to deploy AI in a way that satisfies FINMA 08/2024?"

## Key Tension
AI enthusiasm (50%+ already using) vs. AI governance gap (~75pt belief-action gap) vs. FINMA enforcement trajectory

## CARF/CRS 2.0 Urgency
CARF (Crypto-Asset Reporting Framework) and CRS (Common Reporting Standard) 2.0 both operative January 1, 2026. AI-assisted data processing for CRS/CARF reporting = new risk surface.

## Agentic AI Three-Tier Liability (Applicable to WM)
- AI assistants: low autonomy, reactive → portfolio reporting automation
- AI agents: moderate autonomy, goal-directed → rebalancing triggers, compliance monitoring
- Agentic AI: high autonomy, autonomous execution → autonomous portfolio execution = maximum liability

## Competitive Positioning
FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL: Swiss-sovereign AI governance for boutique wealth managers
- Swiss-Noxtua: Swiss-sovereign legal AI (client confidentiality)
- FABIAN/CASUS: Swiss-sovereign workflow AI
- SMB white space: no SMB-focused WM AI governance competitor

## CTA
AI Governance Readiness Assessment (30-point checklist against FINMA 08/2024)

## Differentiators
- FINMA-native (Guidance 08/2024 framework)
- Swiss-sovereign (data residency)
- Boutique WM specialization
- CARF/CRS 2.0 AI compliance mapping
- Agentic AI liability clarity
