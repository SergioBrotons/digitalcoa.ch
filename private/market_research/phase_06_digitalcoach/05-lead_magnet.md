# 05 — Lead Magnet: DigitalCoa.ch (Executive Lead Generation)

## Strategic Positioning Angle
DigitalCoa.ch is the executive-grade trusted advisor for Geneva's regulatory complexity. The lead magnet strategy should convert executive browsers into briefing call requesters — low-friction entry point (30-minute executive briefing) that allows trust to form before formal engagement.

## Lead Magnet Concepts

### Option A: "Executive Briefing — Regulatory Convergence" (Primary Lead Magnet)
A 30-minute one-on-one executive briefing on regulatory convergence (DORA-parity, ISA, FINMA AI, CRS 2.0, FinIA, nature-related risk) — synthesized for board-level understanding. No slides, no pitch — just executive-to-executive conversation.

**Format:** 30-minute virtual or in-person briefing (Geneva preferred)
**Distribution:** DigitalCoa.ch website CTA, Brotons.net cross-links, LinkedIn outreach
**Value:** Executive sees expertise directly; no sales process; trust forms in conversation
**CTA:** "Request an Executive Briefing" — name, company, brief context

### Option B: "Geneva Executive AI Governance Briefing" (Secondary Lead Magnet)
A 45-minute executive briefing specifically on AI governance: what boards and C-suites need to know about AI liability, compliance, and strategic positioning.

**Format:** 45-minute briefing + one-page AI Governance Board Brief
**Distribution:** LinkedIn C-suite targeting, Brotons.net AI Governance Series cross-link
**Value:** AI governance is timely; boards need this now; positions DigitalCoa.ch as the Geneva AI governance executive advisor

### Option C: "Family Enterprise Succession Advisory — Initial Consultation" (Niche Lead Magnet)
A 60-minute advisory session for Geneva family offices and family enterprises facing succession — structured needs assessment + initial advisory observations.

**Format:** 60-minute advisory session + written summary of key considerations
**Distribution:** Family office networks, Geneva private banking referrals
**Value:** Succession wave 2026-2030 creates sustained demand; high-touch initial engagement builds relationship

## Preferred Format (Initial)
Option A as primary — executive briefing is the lowest-friction, highest-trust entry point for executive advisory services. Option B as secondary for AI governance-specific leads.

## Target CTA
"Request an Executive Briefing" — on DigitalCoa.ch homepage and key service pages. Name + company + brief context only (no lengthy forms).
