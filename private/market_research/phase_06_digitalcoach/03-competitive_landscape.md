# 03 — Competitive Landscape: DigitalCoa.ch vs. Executive Advisory Competitors

## Primary Competitors

### Big Four Executive Advisory
- **PwC Switzerland** — Executive advisory, regulatory consulting, digital transformation; huge brand, high price, slow
- **KPMG Switzerland** — Executive advisory, risk, governance; strong brand, broad service offering
- **EY Switzerland** — Executive advisory, transaction advisory, regulatory; strong in capital markets
- **Deloitte Switzerland** — Executive advisory, consulting, risk; largest professional services brand globally

### Boutiques and Specialists
- **Brotons.net** — Thought leadership → warm pathway to DigitalCoa.ch (owned, not competitor)
- **Lenz & Staehelin** — Legal advisory, some executive advisory; primarily legal
- **Walder Wyss** — Financial regulatory, fintech; strong in regulatory
- **MME AG** — Fintech/blockchain regulatory; niche positioning
- **Kellerhals Carrard** — Financial services dispute resolution

### Executive Coaching/Consulting
- **Executive coaching firms** — Ronald Harry, other executive coaches; non-regulatory
- **Strategy boutiques** — McKinsey alumni boutiques; non-regulatory focus
- **Crisis communication firms** — SEC Newgate, other crisis advisors; separate from regulatory

## Competitive Positioning Gaps

### DigitalCoa.ch Differentiation Opportunities
1. **Geneva executive AI governance** — No Geneva boutique positioned as executive-grade AI governance advisor for C-suites and boards
2. **Regulatory convergence advisory for executives** — DORA + ISA + FINMA synthesis at executive level — Big Four do this at enterprise level, not for boutiques
3. **Family enterprise succession advisory** — Geneva family offices + succession wave + regulatory exposure = unique advisory vertical
4. **Geneva-specific intelligence for executives** — International executives with Geneva operations need Geneva-specific trusted advisor
5. **Crisis preparedness advisory** — Pre-crisis relationship building with executive counsel — not reactive crisis management

## Competitive Threats
- Big Four bundling executive advisory with audit/consulting — hard to displace once engaged
- International executive advisory brands expanding Swiss presence
- In-house legal teams absorbing work previously outsourced
- Price competition from freelance former Big Four advisors
