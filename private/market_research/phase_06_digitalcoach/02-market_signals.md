# 02 — Market Signals: DigitalCoa.ch (Executive Advisory Platform)

## Active Signals (Q1-Q2 2026)

### Executive Advisory Market Signals
- **Executive demand for regulatory certainty:** C-suites and boards increasingly seeking expert counsel on AI governance, DORA-parity, and regulatory convergence — not just legal advice
- **Family office advisory surge:** UHNW succession wave 2026-2030 driving demand for structured advisory on governance, succession, regulatory exposure
- **Board-level AI governance:** Boards increasingly asking "what is our AI governance framework?" — creating demand for executive-level AI advisory
- **Crisis preparedness advisory:** Post-CS/UBS, post-FINMA enforcement escalation, executives want pre-established relationships with crisis advisors
- **Executive briefing as entry point:** Busy executives prefer 30-60 minute briefings over formal proposals — briefing call as primary conversion mechanism

### Lead Generation Platform Signals
- **B2B executive lead quality matters more than volume:** One qualified executive lead (C-suite, GC, board member) is worth more than 100 generic marketing leads
- **Trust signals for executives:** Credentials, testimonials, thought leadership, brand clarity — executives need trust before engagement
- **Inbound vs. outbound for executive services:** Executive advisory services convert better from inbound (executives seeking counsel) than outbound (cold outreach)
- **DigitalCoa.ch ↔ Brotons.net pathway:** Brotons.net thought leadership → warm transfer to DigitalCoa.ch for commercial intent readers

### Executive Platform Competitive Signals
- **Big Four dominate executive advisory:** PwC, KPMG, EY, Deloitte capture most executive advisory spend
- **Boutique differentiation:** Executives use boutiques for specific expertise, relationship depth, and faster access — not for commodity advisory
- **LinkedIn for executive relationships:** Executives vet advisors on LinkedIn before first call — DigitalCoa.ch LinkedIn presence important
