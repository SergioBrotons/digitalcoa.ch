## Key Regulatory Framework for Advisory Services

### Professional Services Regulation (Switzerland)
| Area | Regulation | Impact on DigitalCoa.ch |
|---|---|---|
| Professional title | Geneva cantonal professional registration | Advisor credentials must be clearly stated |
| Mandate contracts | OR Art. 394ff | Advisory engagements governed by mandate contract rules |
| Liability | OR Art. 41, Art. 904 | Advisor liability for negligent advice; limitation clauses |
| Data protection | nFADP | Client data in advisory engagements; consent and security |
| AML (if applicable) | GwG revised 2024-2025 | If advisory involves financial transactions, AML obligations apply |

### Advisory Service-Specific Considerations
- **FinSA applicability:** If advisory involves financial instrument advice, FinSA licensing may apply — DigitalCoa.ch must clarify scope
- **FINMA relationship:** Advisory ≠ FINMA-supervised activity; distinction must be clear
- **Art. 41 OR (AI civil liability):** If advisory involves AI governance, AI liability framework creates both risk and service opportunity

### Geneva-Specific Considerations
- **Geneva Cantonal professional license:** For certain regulated advisory activities
- **Language requirements:** French for Geneva Romandie clients; English for international
- **Geneva arbitration clause:** Advisory contracts should specify Geneva-seated arbitration for disputes
