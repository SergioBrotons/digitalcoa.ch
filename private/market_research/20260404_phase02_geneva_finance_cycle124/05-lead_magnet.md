# 05 — Lead Magnet: Basel IV / FAIR Geneva Bank Readiness Assessment

## Strategic Rationale
Basel IV/FAIR implementation deadline is January 2027 — ~9 months away. Geneva mid-tier banks and securities dealers need to finalize RWA optimization and capital planning now. Most lack internal expertise to conduct comprehensive gap analysis. A targeted Basel IV readiness assessment framework — Geneva-specific, immediately actionable — positions the firm as the authoritative voice before the market gets crowded.
