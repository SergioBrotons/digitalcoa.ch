## Competitive Dynamics (C124 Update)

### New Entrants / Shifts
1. **Tokenization platforms (SDX/SYX)** — institutional-grade digital securities infrastructure now operational; private banks can tokenize bonds and fund units
2. **Multi-family offices (MFOs) growing** — single family offices consolidating into MFOs; new entrants like Alpian (digital private bank) competing for mid-UHNW
3. **Deloitte Private (Geneva)** — succession advisory growing; targeting UHNW families transitioning wealth
4. **Independent trust companies** — new Geneva cantonal trust licenses; specialist succession boutiques emerging
5. **SERV utilization increasing** — more exporters seeking government-backed export insurance; creating advisory demand for SERV applications

### Positioning Gaps
- **UHNW succession advisory** — private banks offer wealth planning, not specialist multi-generational succession architecture; gap
- **DLT tokenization advisory** — few Geneva advisors have practical tokenization implementation experience; gap
- **Basel IV optimization** — smaller Geneva banks lack internal expertise; gap
- **VVG digital distribution** — insurance brokers need IT and compliance advisory; underserved
