## Key Regulatory Developments (C124)

### 1. Basel IV/FAIR — January 2027 Implementation
- Swiss Federal Council confirmed January 2027 effective date
- FINMA FAIR requirements finalized October 2025
- 72.5% RWA floor binding for most Geneva mid-tier banks
- ~9 months remaining for capital planning and optimization
- SFBC (Swiss Fund and Asset Management Association) guidance published

### 2. FINMA DLT Act Revision — Q1 2026 Update
- Updated DLT implementation guidance published February 2026
- Tokenized securities custody standards clarified
- Transfer and redemption procedures still gaps
- SDX operating under DLT license; SIX Group infrastructure available

### 3. VVG Insurance Reform — Parliamentary Debate
- Spring Session 2026 parliamentary debate ongoing
- Key provisions: digital disclosure, embedded insurance, cancellation rights
- Expected implementation: 2027-2028
- Geneva insurance companies preparing compliance roadmaps

### 4. CHF/EUR Parity — SNB Monitoring
- SNB Financial Stability Report 2025 flagged real estate market risk
- SNB rate decisions: potential further cuts if deflation risk rises
- CHF/EUR near parity creating export competitiveness concerns
- Geneva real estate mortgage stress under SNB monitoring

### 5. U.S. Tariffs on Swiss Exports — SERV Response
- U.S. tariffs on Swiss watches (25%), specialty chemicals, machinery
- SERV new commitments up ~15% in recent quarters
- SERV capacity: ~CHF 10B exposure limit; sufficient headroom
- Swiss government position: WTO-compatible response under discussion

### 6. UHNW Succession — No Specific Regulation
- No specific regulatory framework for succession advisory
- Trust and foundation structures regulated by FINMA/cantonal authorities
- Anti-money laundering rules apply to new client onboarding
- CRS/FATCA compliance required for cross-border structures

- **High regulatory risk:** Basel IV capital planning — deadline-driven, no flexibility
- **Medium regulatory risk:** VVG digital distribution — implementation ahead but timeline uncertain
- **Medium regulatory risk:** DLT tokenization — regulatory clarity improving but gaps remain
