# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## Geneva Finance

**Direct Competitors:**
- Big 4 audit/finance advisory (PwC, Deloitte, KPMG, EY): Basel IV, risk, tokenization
- Swiss private banks internal teams: limit external advisory
- Specialized consultancies: Agility Finance, Alternative Advisory, Zanders
- Law firms: Lenz & Staehelin, Walder Wyss, Wenger & Vieli (regulatory finance)
- Asset management consultants: LACP,的头寸

**Differentiation Gap:**
- Independent Basel IV capital optimization (non-audit): under-served
- FINMA enforcement response: Big 4 conflict-of-interest; independent preferred
- Switzerland-EU institutional agreement financial services advisory: high demand
- DLT tokenization: independent technical + legal + regulatory counsel
- SRO architecture advisory for new market entrants: specialized niche

**Positioning Opportunity:**
- Independent, conflict-free Basel IV advisory
- FINMA enforcement response (without audit conflict)
- Switzerland-EU financial services bridge advisory
- Tokenization strategy + regulatory navigation

**Market Structure:**
- Geneva private banking: 18+ banks, consolidation ongoing
- Asset management: 150+ FINMA-authorized managers
- Commodity trading: 500+ companies, Geneva concentration
- Insurance: 15+ life insurers, Geneva/Zurich
- Pension funds: 2,000+ occupational pension funds CH
