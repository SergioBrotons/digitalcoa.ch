# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## Geneva Finance

**Target:** CFO, CRO, CEO of Geneva financial institutions + commodity trading houses

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Basel IV Geneva Readiness Index (Annual Assessment)**
- Format: Self-assessment framework + benchmark report
- Content: RWA floor readiness, capital optimization gaps, FINMA examination preparation
- CTA: Advisory roadmap consultation
- Distribution: FINMA-authorized institutions + commodity trading houses

**Option B: FINMA Enforcement Tracker — Geneva Financial Institutions (Quarterly)**
- Format: 4-page briefing + case analysis
- Content: FINMA enforcement trends, precedent implications, compliance gaps
- CTA: Compliance gap assessment offer
- Differentiation: Independent perspective vs. Big 4 audit product

**Option C: Switzerland-EU Financial Services Bridge — Impact Assessment (On-Demand)**
- Format: Situation analysis + scenario planning
- Content: Bilateral III implications for Geneva financial institutions
- CTA: Scenario planning workshop
- Timing: Q2 2026 critical juncture

**Option D: DLT/Tokenization Geneva Readiness Briefing (On-Demand)**
- Format: Regulatory briefing + decision framework
- Content: DLT Act options, FINMA authorization pathways, market opportunity
- CTA: Strategy session offer

**Recommended Primary:** Option A (Basel IV Readiness Index) — high urgency, measurable value
**Recommended Secondary:** Option B (FINMA Enforcement Tracker) — thought leadership + ICP engagement

**Bilingual:** EN primary, FR translation available
