# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva Finance — ICP

**Firm Types:**
- Geneva-headquartered private banks (UA, PKB, Bordier, Baumann)
- Commodity trading houses with Geneva treasury functions
- Swiss asset managers (FINMA-authorized, AUM CHF 1-50B)
- Insurance companies (Zurich/Geneva, life + P&C)
- Pension funds (BVG pillars + occupational pension funds)
- Family offices (single + multi-family, Geneva concentration)
- SROs and financial industry associations

**Decision-Maker:**
- CFO / Treasurer
- Chief Risk Officer / Head of Risk
- CEO / Managing Director
- Board Risk Committee
- Head of Compliance / MLRO

**Pain Points:**
- Basel IV/FAIR January 2027 RWA floor preparation
- FINMA enforcement escalation: personal liability, greenwashing, AML
- SNB FX intervention asymmetry affecting hedging programs
- DLT Act tokenization: authorization + operational gaps
- BVG reform: infrastructure debt allocation transition
- SARON transition compound vs. simple vs. SOFR comparison
- EU AI Act dual-framework exposure for cross-border entities
- Sanctions compliance: SECO + OFAC extraterritoriality

**Urgency Triggers:**
- FINMA examination cycle
- Rating agency capital adequacy review
- Board risk committee meeting
- Regulatory deadline (Jan 2027 Basel IV)
- Enforcement notice or administrative measure
- Cross-border M&A or restructuring

**Geography:** Geneva + Zurich axis; Vaud; Ticino
**Language:** EN primary, FR useful, DE secondary
**Budget Cycle:** Q4 + Q1 regulatory budget; ad hoc enforcement response
