## ⭐ NET-NEW C63 (vs. C62)

### 1. FDPIC AI & Data Protection — September 24, 2025 Update (FRESHER than prior cycles)
- Face manipulation programs (AI-generated content) must ALWAYS be clearly indicated
- Criminal law dimension newly articulated: AI face/image/voice manipulation may be "completely unlawful in a specific case"
- Authoritarian-state AI applications (real-time facial recognition, social scoring) explicitly prohibited under FADP
- DPIA required for high-risk AI-supported data processing under FADP

### 2. Swiss Bar Association AI Guidelines — June 2024 (CONFIRMED)
- Professional secrecy + data protection obligations for AI tool use in legal practice
- Human oversight required for strategic decisions
- AI acceptable for legal research and contract drafting with oversight
- Creates two-track standard: bar guidelines + FADP + EU AI Act for cross-border work

### 3. FINMA Guidance 08/2024 — AI Expectations for Swiss Financial Institutions
- Governance, risk management, outsourcing expectations for Swiss FIs using AI
- Relevant for law firms advising financial sector clients on AI governance

### 4. FDPIC Joint Statement — February 23, 2026 (NET-NEW)
- FDPIC joined 60 DPAs on AI-generated images and privacy
- Four principles: robust protective measures, transparency, erasure mechanisms, child protection

### 5. Section 301 Status — April 1, 2026
- Federal Council officially rejects allegations
- April 15 written comments deadline ~14 days away
- Investigation A (manufacturing/currency) + Investigation B (forced labour)
- Target tariff imposition ~July 24, 2026
