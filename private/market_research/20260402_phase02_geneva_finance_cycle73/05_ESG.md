# 05 — ESG / Sustainable Finance

## C73 NET-NEW Findings

### SBA Nature Transition — Deeper Data from SwissBanking.ch
- **Source:** swissbanking.ch — "Financing options for Switzerland's nature transition" (June 24, 2025 — but confirmed current)
- **Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework (GBF):** 196 countries signed December 2022; Switzerland included
- **BCG/Quantis Study commissioned by SBA:**
  - 10 key financing areas identified for strengthening nature resilience
  - Annual need: CHF 5.3 billion by 2050
  - Current spend: CHF 3.2 billion/year (primarily public)
  - Gap: CHF 2.1 billion/year incremental (~66% increase needed)
  - 85% publicly financed due to publicly owned assets + limited commercial viability
  - Top areas (>75% of demand): water infrastructure (wastewater, river revitalisation, hydropower rehabilitation) + regenerative agriculture
- **Role of banks:** Loans, investments, advisory services, project financing
- **Key finding:** "Greater mobilisation of private capital needed" given continuing public finance constraints
- **Implication:** Nature transition finance is the next ESG frontier after climate; water + regenerative agriculture as priority areas
