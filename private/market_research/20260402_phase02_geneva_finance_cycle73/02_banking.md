# 02 — Banking / SBA Position

## C73 NET-NEW Findings

### SBA/SBVg — Stablecoin FinIA Opposition — Detailed Position
- **Source:** swissbanking.ch — "We Do Not Want a Luxembourg Scenario for Digital Assets" (2026)
- **Martin Hess (SBVg):**
  - US GENIUS Act is "the real game changer" — first clear federal legislation for stablecoins
  - Switzerland's DLT Act 2021 was an early milestone but US has leapfrogged with GENIUS Act
  - Switzerland must act to remain competitive in global location competition
  - AllUnity (Germany, regulated e-money institution) announced Swiss-franc stablecoin launch — "a deliberate strategic positioning" knowing Swiss regulatory framework is in development
- **Natalie (SBVg lead on FinIA consultation response):**
  - Current Federal Council FinIA draft has "one major flaw": would force Swiss banks to create subsidiaries as payment instrument institutions to issue stablecoins
  - Banks are already comprehensively regulated under highest supervisory standards
  - If a bank can grant loans or accept deposits, it should be able to issue stablecoins directly under its banking license
  - EU: this is already possible under banking license
  - Mandatory outsourcing requirement would create competitive disadvantage without adding safety
- **SBA/SBVg demand:** Mandatory "stress tests" for stablecoin issuance covering:
  - Value stability genuine guarantees
  - Financial market stability protection
  - Disintermediation risk mitigation
