# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Legal

## Key Regulatory Developments — Geneva Legal (C126)

### Regulatory Item 01: ComCo Digital Markets Enforcement
**Authority:** Competition Commission (ComCo), Swiss Federal Council
**Status:** Increasing enforcement in digital markets; platform economy focus
**Key Issues:** Platform market power, algorithmic coordination, data access
**Geneva Impact:** Digital platforms and companies with significant market power
**Advisory Implications:** ComCo investigation response, compliance frameworks

---

### Regulatory Item 02: DORA Implementation — Switzerland
**Authority:** FINMA
**Status:** DORA-aligned ICT risk management guidance for Swiss financial institutions
**Key Requirements:** Operational resilience, ICT incident reporting, third-party ICT risk
**Geneva Impact:** Banks, asset managers, insurers in Geneva
**Advisory Implications:** DORA gap assessment, framework development, implementation support

---

### Regulatory Item 03: FADP AI Training Data Guidance
**Authority:** FDPIC
**Status:** Interpretive guidance on AI training on Swiss personal data
**Key Requirements:** Consent or legitimate interest; no research opt-out
**Geneva Impact:** All law firms and corporations using AI tools
**Advisory Implications:** FADP AI compliance frameworks, policy templates

---

### Regulatory Item 04: Geneva Arbitration Surge
**Authority:** ICC, SCAI
**Status:** Case volumes growing significantly; Geneva remains global #1 seat
**Key Drivers:** Commodity trading disputes, sanctions-related disputes
**Geneva Impact:** Geneva law firms with arbitration practices; commodity trading houses
**Advisory Implications:** Arbitration strategy, procedural management, arbitrator selection

---

### Regulatory Item 05: FATF Mutual Evaluation — Switzerland
**Authority:** FATF, SECO, FINMA
**Status:** Mutual evaluation March 2026; findings driving AML/CFT updates
**Key Requirements:** AML/CFT framework review, follow-up recommendations
**Geneva Impact:** Swiss financial institutions subject to FATF evaluation
**Advisory Implications:** FATF gap assessments, remediation frameworks

---

### Regulatory Item 06: CARB Compliance — Swiss Companies with US Exposure
**Authority:** CARB, Swiss FOEN
**Status:** Swiss CO2 regulations with CARB-equivalent provisions
**Key Requirements:** CARB compliance for companies with US market exposure
**Geneva Impact:** Geneva commodity trading houses with US operations
**Advisory Implications:** CARB compliance frameworks, documentation, audit preparation
