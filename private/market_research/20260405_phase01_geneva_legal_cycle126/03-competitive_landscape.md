### Competitive Gaps

**Gap 01: ComCo Digital Enforcement Advisory**
ComCo digital enforcement is growing but advisory expertise is concentrated in a few firms. Opportunity: digital markets competition compliance frameworks for Geneva platforms.

**Gap 02: DORA-Aligned ICT Risk Advisory**
DORA-aligned ICT risk management advisory for Swiss financial institutions. Most firms have cybersecurity expertise but lack integrated DORA compliance advisory.

**Gap 03: FADP AI Training Data Compliance**
FADP AI training data guidance is new. Most firms are reactive. Opportunity: standardized FADP AI compliance frameworks for law firms and corporate clients.

**Gap 04: FATF Evaluation Follow-Up**
FATF mutual evaluation findings driving AML/CFT compliance updates. Advisory opportunity: FATF gap assessments and remediation frameworks for Geneva financial institutions.

**Gap 05: CARB Compliance for Swiss Trading Companies**
CARB compliance for Swiss companies with US exposure is underserved. Advisory opportunity: CARB compliance frameworks for Geneva commodity trading houses.
