## Geneva NGO/Tech Competitive Landscape

### IO-Adjacent Advisory
- **Where Geneva has structural advantage:** IO standards interpretation, humanitarian sector access, multilateral climate finance, IP-adjacent advisory
- **Competition:** Limited in Geneva specifically; global competitors (Oliver Wyman, Accenture, Dalberg) dominate large IO advisory mandates
- **Boutique opportunity:** Niche, high-trust, relationship-dependent advisory at the intersection of IO frameworks and Swiss implementation

### Key Differentiation Factor
Geneva's physical proximity to IOs is a real advantage for advisory: being in the same time zone, cultural context, and city creates relationship access that remote competitors cannot replicate.

## Geneva-Specific Factors
- **IO concentration:** ITU, WIPO, ICRC, UNOG, WHO, UNEP, WTO — Geneva hosts more IOs than any other city
- **Humanitarian corridor:** ICRC, HCR, CICR — Geneva is the global humanitarian capital; digital transformation is a major theme
- **Health tech cluster:** Geneva has a concentrated health tech ecosystem (HUG, EPFL, biotech) with Swissmedic regulatory access
- **Climate finance:** proximity to UNEP, GCF, climate funds creates positioning advantage for advisory intermediaries
