## Active Obligations (Already Live)

### 1. Revised Federal Data Protection Act (nFADP / nDSG)
- **Effective:** September 1, 2023
- **Status:** OPERATIVE — no grace period
- **AI treatment:** FDPIC confirmed nFADP applies directly to AI-based data processing without new legislation
- **FDPIC AI guidance:** FDPIC expects purpose + functionality + data sources disclosure for AI deployments (edoeb.admin.ch, ongoing 2026)
- **Key obligations for law firms:**
  - Art. 5(c): Lawfulness, proportionality, data minimization
  - Art. 26-27: Data protection impact assessment (DPIA) for high-risk processing
  - Art. 7-8: Privacy by Design and Privacy by Default
  - Art. 52: Criminal liability up to CHF 250,000 for intentional failures
  - Art. 63-64: FDPIC enforcement powers (administrative, not direct fines)

### 2. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
- **Trigger:** High-risk data processing (Art. 26-27 nFADP)
- **Legal AI applications likely HIGH-RISK:**
  - Contract analysis AI (processing large volumes of client confidential data)
  - Due diligence AI (M&A, corporate transactions)
  - AI-assisted legal research (external data sources)
  - AI HR tools (employee performance, screening, monitoring)
- **Status:** OPERATIVE — no future implementation date
- **Penalty:** Criminal liability for intentional failures

### 3. Privacy by Design / Privacy by Default (Art. 7-8 nFADP)
- **Scope:** AI system procurement and deployment
- **Status:** OPERATIVE — part of nFADP since Sep 1, 2023
- **Implication:** AI procurement decisions trigger compliance obligations before deployment

### 4. EU AI Act — Extraterritorial (Swiss Firms with EU Nexus)
- **High-risk obligations activate:** August 2, 2026
- **GPAI transparency obligations:** OPERATIVE since August 2, 2025 (~8 months already)
- **Relevance:** Swiss firms placing AI on EU market, operating EU-intended AI output, or acting as EU importer/distributor
- **Legal firms with EU clients or EU office:** Subject to high-risk obligations August 2, 2026

### 5. GPAI Transparency Obligations
- **Status:** OPERATIVE since August 2, 2025 (8+ months already)
- **Applies to:** General-purpose AI systems used in firm operations
- **Requirements:** Technical documentation, transparency to recipients
