### Competitive Conclusion

**The competitive gap is structural, not temporary.** Every cycle confirms:
1. Large firms (Homburger, Lenz & Staehelin, Walder Wyss, VISCHER) serve enterprise only
2. Named boutique AI governance firms (FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL) do not have confirmed SMB-Geneva-legal focus
3. No new entrant has established in the SMB Geneva legal AI governance space in 10+ consecutive cycles
4. The gap is white space, not ignorance — law firms know AI governance matters but no one is offering it to them

**The window is first-mover, not permanent.** 53% of small firms already using gen AI (doubled from 27% in 2023). The governance gap will attract competitors as market awareness grows. The window is now — before May 2026 First Draft press coverage drives awareness.
