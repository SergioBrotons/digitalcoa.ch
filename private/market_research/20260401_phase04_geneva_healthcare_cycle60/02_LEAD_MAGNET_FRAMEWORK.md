## Content Sections

### Section 1: The Three-Layer Regulatory Stack for Medical Practices
1. Swissdamed: Pharmacovigilance and medical device adverse event reporting — July 1, 2026 (~91 days)
2. FADP Art. 21: Automated decision-making obligations for patient data — ongoing
3. Federal AI Ordinance: Human oversight requirements for AI-assisted clinical decisions — 15+ months operative
4. EU AI Act: GPAI obligations in force since August 2, 2025; high-risk AI assessments August 2, 2026

### Section 2: The Medical Practice AI Governance Audit (5 Questions)
1. Swissdamed: Have we documented our adverse event reporting process and designated responsible personnel?
2. FADP: Do we have documented patient consent protocols for AI-assisted clinical decisions?
3. Federal AI Ordinance: For each AI tool used in clinical or administrative contexts, is there a human oversight step?
4. EU AI Act: Do we serve EU patients or use AI tools sourced from EU providers?
5. Data sovereignty: Are our AI tools hosted on Swiss or EU infrastructure?

### Section 3: The Swissdamed Remediation Checklist (91 Days)
- [ ] Swissdamed registration completed
- [ ] Adverse event reporting process documented
- [ ] Designated Swissdamed responsible personnel named
- [ ] Staff training on Swissdamed reporting obligations
- [ ] Medical device AI tools inventoried and reporting protocols confirmed
- [ ] Integration with existing pharmacovigilance system reviewed

### Section 4: August 2, 2026 EU AI Act Preparedness
- [ ] AI diagnostic and clinical decision support tools inventoried
- [ ] EU AI Act applicability assessed (EU patients / EU AI providers)
- [ ] Human oversight protocols documented for all clinical AI use
- [ ] Patient disclosure language for AI-assisted decisions
- [ ] Data processing agreements with AI vendors reviewed for Swiss/EU hosting
