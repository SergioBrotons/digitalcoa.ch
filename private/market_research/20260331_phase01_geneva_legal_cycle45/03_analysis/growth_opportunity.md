# Phase 01 — Geneva Legal — Cycle 45
## Analysis: Growth Opportunity

- Geneva canton: ~800 law firms (various sizes)
- Target ICP: Solo practitioners to 50-lawyer firms = ~700 firms
- Addressable (AI-using, no formal governance): ~490 firms (70% of 700)
- Serviceable addressable market (within 12-18mo first-mover window): ~150-200 firms

## Competitive White Space
No SMB-focused, Geneva-based, French/English AI governance advisory firm has been confirmed in 45+ competitive scans.
- All named competitors are enterprise-only or international
- FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL is the closest structural competitor (Zurich-based)
- Gilliéron Avocat operates at solo practitioner level but different positioning (IP/IT)
- LEXR operates at enterprise advisory level
- LAW·RENCE is a platform, not an advisory firm

## Shadow AI as Entry Point
- 71%/67% individual AI use = Shadow AI prevalence
- Law firm employees using AI without firm knowledge or policies
- FDPIC exposure + professional liability exposure + confidentiality risk
- "Decision 0" audit = map all AI tools already in use before governance framework

## Entry Sequence
1. Shadow AI audit (Decision 0) — map existing AI tools
2. FADP compliance gap assessment
3. DPIA for high-risk AI use cases
4. Privacy by Design procurement framework
5. Ongoing monitoring and compliance

## Pricing Sensitivity
- Solo/small firms price-sensitive; value-based pricing tied to quantified ROI
- 260h/year efficiency × CHF 300-500/h = CHF 78,000-130,000 potential value
- Governance engagement at CHF 3,000-8,000 positions as accessible entry

## Sales Cycle
- Decision 0 = low-friction entry (discovery + mapping, no commitment)
- FADP gap assessment = first formal deliverable
- Target: 3-6 month decision cycle for small firms
