## Structural Competitive White Space

### The Gap
**No Geneva-based or Swiss firm offers SMB-accessible AI governance advisory for small/mid-market law firms (1-50 lawyers)**

### Why This Gap Exists
1. Large firms serve enterprise clients (more revenue per engagement)
2. SMB AI governance is novel (market awareness low until recently)
3. Specialized AI governance requires combined FADP + legal tech expertise
4. Swiss-market focus required (data residency, FADP, not EU AI Act primary)

- FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL is the closest structural competitor
- Positioned as Swiss-sovereign, SMB-accessible
- However: FABIAN is one firm — market is large enough for multiple players
