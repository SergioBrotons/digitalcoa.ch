## FADP Compliance Stack (5 Layers)

**Layer 1 — Art. 21 FADP: Human Review**
Right to human review for automated decisions producing legal effects.
AI-assisted contract analysis, legal opinions, due diligence = Art. 21 applicability.

**Layer 2 — Art. 5(c) FADP: Transparency**
Must disclose AI involvement in client communications.
Client-facing AI outputs require explicit disclosure.

**Layer 3 — Art. 16 FADP: Data Security**
AI systems processing personal data must maintain integrity.
Client confidentiality obligations extend to AI tools.

**Layer 4 — DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment)**
Required before deploying high-risk AI.
Contract analysis, due diligence, legal research = likely high-risk.

**Layer 5 — Privacy by Design**
Privacy implications must be assessed before AI procurement.
Swiss/EU hosting preferred (US CLOUD Act risk).
