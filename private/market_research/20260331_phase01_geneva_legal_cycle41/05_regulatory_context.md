**Cycle 41 — March 31, 2026**

## Regulatory Architecture

### Primary: Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)
**Status: Operative since September 1, 2023 — 2.5+ years already live**

#### Key Articles for AI Governance

**Art. 21 FADP: Right to Human Review**
- Automated decisions producing legal effects must allow human review
- AI-assisted contract analysis, due diligence, legal opinions = Art. 21 applicability
- **Action**: Audit AI tools for automated decision-making; ensure human review capability

**Art. 5(c) FADP: Transparency**
- Must disclose AI involvement in client communications
- Client-facing AI outputs require explicit disclosure
- **Action**: Update client communication templates for AI disclosure

**Art. 16 FADP: Data Integrity and Security**
- AI systems processing personal data must maintain integrity and security
- Client confidentiality obligations extend to AI tools
- **Action**: Assess AI vendor security; document data handling procedures

**Art. 5(a)/(b) FADP: Lawfulness**
- Data processing must be lawful, fair, and transparent
- AI training data = potential lawfulness issue
- **Action**: Assess AI tool training data provenance

#### FADP Compliance Stack
1. **Art. 21**: Human review for automated decisions
2. **Art. 5(c)**: AI transparency disclosure
3. **Art. 16**: Data security for AI systems
4. **DPIA**: Required for high-risk AI (contract analysis, due diligence)
5. **Privacy by Design**: Mandatory for AI procurement

#### FADP Enforcement
- **FDPIC confirmed**: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing (May 2025)
- No new legislation required — existing FADP obligations already operative
- FDPIC enforcement posture: guidance → enforcement when sector identified as priority
- **Legal sector ABSENT from FDPIC enforcement** = timing signal

## Secondary: EU AI Act (Extraterritorial)
**Operative since August 2, 2025 (GPAI) — ~8 months active**

#### Applicability to Swiss Law Firms
- Firms with EU nexus (EU clients, EU operations) = EU AI Act applicable
- Annex III high-risk AI: legal analysis AI used in EU cross-border matters
- Transparency + human oversight obligations for high-risk AI
- **Dual compliance burden**: FADP (Swiss) + EU AI Act (if EU nexus)

#### Key Obligations
- Transparency for AI interactions (Art. 50 EU AI Act)
- Human oversight for high-risk AI (Art. 14 EU AI Act)
- Technical documentation for high-risk AI (Art. 11 EU AI Act)
- Registration for certain high-risk AI systems (Art. 51 EU AI Act)
