# 01-ICP.md — Geneva Consulting SMB/Mid-Market

## Geneva Consulting — SMB / Mid-Market

### Refined ICP Definition
Managing directors, CFOs, and founders at:
- Geneva SMBs and mid-market companies (CHF 5M–300M revenue)
- Geneva professional services firms (law firms, consultancies, family offices)
- Cross-border businesses with EU/UK/CH exposure (commodity traders, trading houses)
- Family businesses with succession or governance complexity

### Business Decision-Maker Profile
- **Role:** Managing Director, CFO, Founder, CEO, Board Member
- **Concern:** Regulatory cost, talent gaps, geopolitical exposure, succession, digital transformation
- **Trigger events:** Regulatory deadline, competitive pressure, founder succession, geopolitical disruption, M&A
- **Buying behaviour:** Outcome-focused; ROI-driven; peer reference; senior attention
- **Budget authority:** CHF 20K–1M/year advisory; project-based with clear deliverables
- **Language:** EN-primary for international; FR for domestic operations

### What This ICP Does NOT Want
- Generic consulting firm slides
- Regulatory summaries without Geneva-specific action items
- Expensive long-term retainers without clear ROI
- Big 4 layered team approach

### Strategic Implication
Geneva SMB decision-makers are reachable via Brotons.net content + CCIG peer networks. Independent boutique positioning with senior attention + clear deliverables differentiates from Big 4.
