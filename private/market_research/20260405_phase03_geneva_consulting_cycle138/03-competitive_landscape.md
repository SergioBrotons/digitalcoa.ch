## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

**Big 4 Consulting (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY)**
- Dominate Geneva SMB/mid-market advisory; large teams; premium pricing
- ICP perception: expensive; layered teams; generic frameworks
- Differentiation opportunity: senior attention + independent perspective + Geneva-specific depth

**Specialist Boutiques (Management Consultancy, Interim CFO Firms)**
- Growing in Geneva; specialist depth; interim management focus
- ICP perception: variable quality; limited cross-functional breadth
- Differentiation opportunity: integrated regulatory + finance + strategy advisory

**Regional Consulting Firms**
- CCIG-adjacent; strong local networks; limited international perspective
- Differentiation opportunity: international regulatory intelligence + independent positioning
