## Section 1 — The Situation
The EU AI Act's GPAI transparency obligations have been active since August 2, 2025. That means if your consulting firm has EU clients, uses EU-based AI tools, or provides services that touch the EU market, you've been subject to AI governance obligations for 8+ months.

Most small consulting firms don't know this. Federal Council's lean approach means Switzerland isn't forcing the issue domestically — but EU extraterritoriality doesn't care about Swiss domestic policy.

---

## Section 2 — Does the EU AI Act Apply to Your Firm?
1. Do you have EU-based clients?
2. Do you use AI tools provided by EU companies?
3. Do you provide services intended for EU markets or EU users?
4. Are you an EU importer or distributor of AI systems?

If yes to any of the above: GPAI transparency obligations are already active for your firm.

---

## Section 3 — GPAI Transparency: What It Requires Now
- Disclose that your firm uses AI in service delivery
- Document the AI systems used and their purpose
- Maintain transparency with clients about AI involvement
- Technical documentation for AI systems used
