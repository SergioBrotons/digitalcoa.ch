## Market Signals — Phase 03 Cycle 14

### 1. Swiss SME AI Adoption — Rapid Acceleration (NEW)
- Swiss SME AI adoption: **22% → 34% within one year** (October 2025 data)
- **60%** of Swiss SMBs view AI as opportunity (up from 35% favorability)
- 156% projected AI investment surge by 2030
- AI for SMBs: back-office automation, customer contact, lead qualification
- GenAI central to SMB marketing and content creation
- IT solution discovery: GenAI tools + cloud marketplaces becoming primary channels

### 2. Governance Gap — Majority Still Underserved (NEW)
- **Majority of Swiss SMEs still lack clear internal AI guidelines**
- Data protection risk = primary concern when applying AI without guidelines
- 40% Geneva SMEs lack AI skills (PwC 2024 — carry-forward from prior cycles)
- Compliance gap: FADP may offer SME exemptions; GDPR has no size exemptions
- Competitive opportunity: Swiss domestic positioning = FADP exemptions vs EU clients = GDPR

### 3. Regulatory Timeline — Window Longer Than Estimated (NEW)
- **Full Swiss AI legislation not before 2028** (significant refinement)
- Federal Council First Draft AI Action Plan May 2026 (~2 months)
- EU AI Act GPAI enforcement Aug 2, 2026 (~4.5 months)
- MRA update risk from 2027: dual certification for AI products may be required
- CoE AI Convention FDJP consultation draft end-2026

### 4. EU AI Act Extraterritorial — Penalties Confirmed
- EU AI Act penalties: up to **€35M or 7% global annual turnover**
- Swiss firms must comply if: placing AI on EU market, EU users, EU-output users
- GDPR no size exemptions: even micro-SMEs with EU nexus must comply
- GPAI obligations: Aug 2, 2025 (operative) + Aug 2, 2026 (enforcement)

### 5. Geneva Cluster Events
- AI for Good Global Summit Jul 7-10, 2026 Palexpo Geneva
- Geneva as hub for AI governance discussions = market awareness signal

### Key Market Signal for Outreach
**Adoption accelerating faster than governance capability:**
- 34% Swiss SMEs using AI; majority still lack internal guidelines
- FADP vs GDPR gap: Swiss firms with EU nexus face GDPR (no SME exemptions); pure-Swiss firms have FADP exemptions
- Entry angle: "Your GenAI tool use creates FADP obligations — do you have your AI inventory and DPIA process?"
