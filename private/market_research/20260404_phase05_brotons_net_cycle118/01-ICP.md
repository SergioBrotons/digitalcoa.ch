# 01 — ICP: Phase 05 Brotons.net (C118)

## ICP: Brotons.net Audience

### Primary ICP — Geneva-Based Professionals (Primary Reader)
**Profile:**
- 35-55 years old; senior professional or executive
- Financial services, law, consulting, diplomacy, NGOs
- Geneva-based; some Lausanne, Zurich, international
- Reads in English; some French
- Motivated by: intellectual challenge, Geneva-relevant insights, strategic context
- Engagement pattern: newsletter + occasional deep reads; not casual browser
- Decision-maker: shapes thinking, influences decisions, makes recommendations

### Secondary ICP — International Readers with Geneva Interest
**Profile:**
- Broader Switzerland watchers, European policy professionals
- Alumni of Geneva institutions (IHEID, IUHEI, Graduate Institute)
- UN/IO professionals globally
- Reads in English; values Geneva perspective
- Motivated by: Geneva as lens on global issues

### Tertiary ICP — Prospective Advisory Clients (Indirect)
**Profile:**
- C-suite, senior executives considering advisory services
- Discover Brotons.net through thought leadership
- Convert through trusted relationship (not cold outreach)
- Motivated by: demonstrated expertise, strategic thinking quality

## Non-Reader (Avoid Optimizing For)
- Students (too early in career; low conversion value)
- General news consumers (wrong content fit)
- Social media scroll traffic (low engagement, no retention value)
