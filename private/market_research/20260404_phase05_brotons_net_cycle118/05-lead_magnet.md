# 05 — Lead Magnet: Phase 05 Brotons.net (C118)

## Lead Magnet Strategy

### Primary Lead Magnet: "Geneva Regulatory Intelligence Report" (Quarterly)
**Format:** 8-12 page quarterly report on Geneva financial/policy landscape
**Distribution:** Substack + LinkedIn; premium for paid subscribers
**Content:** Analysis + interpretation + implications — not news summary
**CTA:** Subscribe to Brotons.net → quarterly report → discovery call

### Secondary Lead Magnet: "The Geneva Briefing" (Weekly Newsletter)
**Format:** 500-word weekly; bilingual
**Distribution:** Substack newsletter; LinkedIn preview
**Content:** One key topic deeply analyzed; Geneva angle; practical implications
**CTA:** Free subscription → nurture → paid tier upgrade

### Tertiary Lead Magnet: "Executive Briefing Call" (Direct)
**Format:** 30-min 1:1 call; senior executives only
**Distribution:** Via LinkedIn outreach or referral
**Content:** Geneva intelligence relevant to executive's specific situation
**CTA:** Direct Calendly booking → advisory relationship

## Content-Lead Magnet Funnel
1. LinkedIn article / Substack essay → newsletter subscription
2. Newsletter → quarterly report (paid tier) → direct engagement
3. Speaking / event → audience → newsletter → paid tier → advisory
4. Referral from advisory clients → Brotons.net subscription → trust formation
