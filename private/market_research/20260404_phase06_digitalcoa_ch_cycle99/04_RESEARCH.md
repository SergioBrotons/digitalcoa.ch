## Findings (Cycle 99 — Slot 4)

### EN
**DigitalCoa.ch vs. Brotons.net — Clear Role Separation**

Confirmed positioning (aligned with C98 findings):

**Brotons.net = Hub / Thought Leadership Platform:**
- Purpose: intellectual authority, interpretive analysis, Geneva market intelligence
- Audience: Geneva professionals (finance, legal, consulting, IO/NGO) seeking understanding and perspective
- Content: research-grounded analysis, synthesis, commentary on Geneva developments
- Tone: calm, sharp, restrained, credible (SOUL.md tone)
- Outcome: credibility formation, audience relationship, trust building
- Does NOT: promotional framing, service marketing, direct sales

**DigitalCoa.ch = Advisory Services Platform:**
- Purpose: client acquisition, service delivery framework, conversion
- Audience: Geneva executives (CEOs, CFOs, GCs, Board Members) seeking to act on insights
- Content: service descriptions, engagement pathways, trust signals, client outcomes
- Tone: executive-grade, structured, calm, commercially credible
- Outcome: qualified lead conversion, retainer initiation, advisory relationship
- Does NOT: opinionated analysis, ongoing commentary, intellectual debate

**How they support each other:**
- Brotons.net thought leadership → establishes credibility → executive searches DigitalCoa.ch when ready to act
- DigitalCoa.ch client work → generates experience → informs Brotons.net content (anonymised)
- Both share: Geneva-specific expertise, research-grounded approach, quality standard

**Critical boundary:**
- Brotons.net must never feel like a sales funnel for DigitalCoa.ch (audience will disengage)
- DigitalCoa.ch must never try to be Brotons.net (loses advisory clarity)
- Crossover: topic-relevant service mentions in Brotons.net conclusions (per C98 finding) — appropriate when genuinely useful

**Brand separation principle:** Brotons.net readers should feel they're accessing independent analysis. DigitalCoa.ch visitors should feel they're approaching a credible advisory relationship. The two brands must remain distinct experiences.

### FR
**DigitalCoa.ch vs. Brotons.net — séparation claire des rôles**

Positionnement confirmé (aligné avec conclusions C98) :

**Brotons.net = Hub / Plateforme de pensée directive :**
- Purpose : autorité intellectuelle, analyse interprétative, renseignement sur le marché genevois
- Audience : professionnels genevois cherchant compréhension et perspective
- Contenu : analyse fondée sur la recherche, synthèse, commentaire sur les développements genevois
- Ton : calme, tranchant, retenu, crédible
- Outcome : formation de crédibilité, relation audience, construction de confiance
- Ne fait PAS : framing promotionnel, marketing de service, vente directe

**DigitalCoa.ch = Plateforme de services advisory :**
- Purpose : acquisition client, cadre de prestation de service, conversion
- Audience : exécutifs genevois cherchant à agir sur des aperçus
- Contenu : descriptions de service, parcours d'engagement, signaux de confiance, résultats client
- Ton : qualité exécutive, structuré, calme, crédible commercialement
- Outcome : conversion de lead qualifié, initiation de retainer, relation advisory
- Ne fait PAS : analyse d'opinion, commentaire ongoing, débat intellectuel

**Comment ils se soutiennent :**
- Pensée directive Brotons.net → établit crédibilité → execs cherchent DigitalCoa.ch quand prêts à agir
- Travail client DigitalCoa.ch → génère expérience → informe contenu Brotons.net (anonymisé)

**Limite critique :**
- Brotons.net ne doit jamais sembler être un entonnoir de ventes pour DigitalCoa.ch
- DigitalCoa.ch ne doit jamais essayer d'être Brotons.net
