### EN
**Advisory Client Retention and Expansion — Geneva Executive Relationships**

Geneva advisory client retention dynamics:

Ongoing value delivery:
- Geneva executives stay with advisors who consistently deliver perspective beyond their own knowledge
- Advisory value = telling clients what they don't know they need to know (anticipation, not just reaction)
- Regulatory updates delivered proactively: Geneva executives want to know about relevant regulatory changes before they become urgent
- Peer intelligence (anonymised): Geneva executives value hearing how other Geneva companies are handling similar challenges

Retention mechanisms:
- Quarterly regulatory review (30-min video call): keeps relationship active, surfaces new issues, demonstrates ongoing value
- Geneva executive loyalty: once trust established, Geneva executives rarely switch advisors; they value continuity especially for complex regulatory relationships
- Multi-year relationships common in Geneva for regulatory advisory; trust and consistency > competitive pricing

Expansion (upsell/cross-sell):
- Initial engagement → broader mandate: Geneva executives who trust an advisor on regulatory matters naturally expand to strategy, organizational, and other challenges
- Geneva board advisory: regulatory advisor often becomes board advisory relationship
- Geneva family office: single family office engagement expands to multiple family entities
- Geneva M&A advisory: regulatory due diligence → transaction advisory expansion

Referral generation:
- Geneva executives refer peers when: the advisor exceeded expectations AND they can introduce without personal risk
- Safe referral introduction: Geneva executives introduce advisors through trusted intermediaries (bankers, lawyers) rather than direct introduction
- Geneva professional community: small enough that advisor performance (positive or negative) travels fast
- Referrals are quality signals: Geneva executive referrals are high-quality leads (peer has already been vetted)

Relationship maintenance:
- Geneva executives do not want to be "managed" — prefer organic relationship updates
- 2-3 substantive touches per quarter > constant contact
- Thought leadership delivery (Brotons.net content) maintains brand presence without being intrusive
- Annual in-person meeting (even if advisory relationship is primarily remote)

Key retention risk: complacency. Geneva executives will stay with an advisor indefinitely unless they feel the advisor is no longer providing value. Regular evidence of continued relevance is essential.

### FR
**Rétention et expansion du client advisory — relations avec exécutifs genevois**

Dynamiques de rétention du client advisory genevois :

Livraison de valeur continue :
- Exécutifs genevois restent avec des advisors qui livrent systématiquement une perspective au-delà de leur propre connaissance
- Valeur advisory = dire aux clients ce qu'ils ne savent pas qu'ils ont besoin de savoir
- Mises à jour réglementaires proactives : veulent connaître les changements réglementaires pertinents avant qu'ils deviennent urgents
- Intelligence de pair (anonymisée) : valorisent entendre comment d'autres entreprises genevoises gèrent des défis similaires

Mécanismes de rétention :
- Revue réglementaire trimestrielle (appel vidéo 30 min)
- Fidélité des exécutifs genevois : rarement changent d'advisor ; valorisent la continuité pour relations réglementaires complexes
- Relations pluriannuelles communes pour conseil réglementaire

Expansion :
- Engagement initial → mandat plus large
- Conseil réglementaire → souvent devient conseil de board
- Family office single → expansion à entités familiales multiples
- Due diligence réglementaire M&A → expansion vers conseil transactionnel

Génération de referral :
- Exécutifs genevois réfèrent quand : advisor a dépassé les attentes ET ils peuvent introduire sans risque personnel
- Introduction sécurisée : via intermédiaires de confiance (banquiers, avocats) plutôt que directe
- Communauté professionnelle genevoise : assez petite pour que la performance advisor (positive ou négative) voyage vite

Risque de rétention clé : complaisance. Les exécutifs genevois restent avec un advisor indéfiniment sauf s'ils sentent que l'advisor ne fournit plus de valeur.
