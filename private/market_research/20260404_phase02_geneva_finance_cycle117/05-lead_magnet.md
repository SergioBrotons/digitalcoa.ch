# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Finance Decision-Makers

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "Geneva Asset Manager Operations Maturity Assessment"
**Format:** Interactive PDF / Web-based assessment (12 questions)
**Target:** COO, Operations Director at IAMs and MFOs
**Value:** Benchmark their operations against industry standards; reveals gaps in reporting, data management, compliance
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, AUM range, primary challenge
**Conversion path:** Assessment results → consultation offer → operational advisory engagement

### Option B: "The Geneva AM Regulatory Change Tracker"
**Format:** Quarterly email briefing (5-minute read)
**Target:** CIO, Compliance Officer, CEO
**Value:** Curated regulatory changes from FINMA, SFAMA with operational implications
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, role, regulatory focus areas
**Conversion path:** Quarterly briefing subscription → nurture sequence → advisory engagement

### Option C: "DORA Readiness Framework for Geneva Asset Managers"
**Format:** Structured guide (25 pages, EN + FR summary)
**Target:** COO, CTO, Risk Manager
**Value:** Practical DORA compliance framework adapted for Swiss AMs; gap assessment checklist
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, role, AUM, current DORA readiness
**Conversion path:** Chapter 1 → full guide → DORA readiness assessment offer

### Option D: "The Geneva Family Office Technology Survey"
**Format:** Annual research report with benchmarking data
**Target:** Single/multi-family office leaders, CIOs
**Value:** Unique benchmarking data on technology adoption, spending, priorities
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, AUM, role
**Conversion path:** Report → webinar invite → advisory relationship

## Lead Magnet Recommendation

**Primary:** Option C (DORA Readiness Framework) — timely (DORA adoption imminent), specific, actionable, positions DigitalCoa as regulatory-interpretive authority for Geneva finance

**Secondary:** Option B (Regulatory Change Tracker) — recurring touchpoint, builds trust, low production cost, high frequency

**Long-term:** Option D (Family Office Survey) — unique data asset, high authority, differentiates from all competitors

## Bilingual Considerations
- Primary deliverable in English (industry language)
- French executive summary for Geneva-specific resonance
- Landing page in both EN + FR
- Swiss-specific examples throughout (not EU-generic)
