## Compliance Stack (FADP + FINMA)
- FADP Art. 5(c): Lawfulness of data processing
- FADP Art. 21: Right to human review of automated decisions (portfolio management = automated decisions)
- FADP Art. 26: Transparency obligations (AI use disclosure; data used for model training)
- FINMA Guidance 08/2024: AI inventory + risk classification + responsibility assignment + staff training
- CARF/CRS 2.0: Tax transparency reporting (operative Jan 1 2026)
- FATF: External pressure / mutual evaluation 2026–2028

## Shadow AI Audit — Decision 0 (Primary Entry Point)
Six questions that surface where AI is already in use without governance:
1. Which GenAI tools are your RMs using today?
2. Are any AI outputs going to clients (reports, summaries, recommendations)?
3. Is client data being entered into AI tools?
4. Are AI tools being used for portfolio construction or risk assessment?
5. Who reviews AI-generated content before it reaches clients?
6. Do your IT/vendor contracts address AI third-party risk?
