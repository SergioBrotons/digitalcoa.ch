## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

**Big 4 IO Advisory (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY)**
- Dominate IO/NGO technology advisory; large institutional relationships
- ICP perception: expensive; layered teams; generic frameworks; potential conflict with audit relationships
- Differentiation opportunity: independent perspective; Geneva-specific IO/NGO depth; senior attention

**Specialist Humanitarian Tech Advisory Boutiques**
- Niche; emerging in Geneva; limited institutional credibility
- Differentiation opportunity: Brotons.net institutional trust network + Geneva IO credibility + independent non-conflicted positioning

**IO Internal Technology Teams**
- ICRC IT, UNHCR IT, WHO IT — internal capacity growing but gaps remain
- Opportunity: advisory + implementation support for internal teams
