## Lead Magnet: "The Small Real Estate Agency AI Governance Question"
### 7-Section Framework

---

### Section 1: 67% of Geneva Real Estate Agencies Are Already Using AI
**Hook:** 67% of Geneva real estate agencies are already using AI tools — the highest adoption rate of any sector in our portfolio. But AI governance frameworks? Nearly none.

**Key message:** This is the widest gap between adoption and governance in the entire Geneva professional services market. Small RE agencies are already using AI for property valuations, listing descriptions, customer matching, and scheduling. The question isn't whether to use AI — it's how to govern it.

---

### Section 2: What AI Is Your Agency Already Using?
**Content:** AI property valuation tools, AI-generated listing descriptions, AI customer matching, AI scheduling, AI CRM automation.

**Trigger question:** Do you know which AI tools process client property data, and whether you're covered by nFADP?

---

### Section 3: The Retroactive Compliance Path
**Content:** Unlike other verticals that need anticipatory compliance (prepare for future AI laws), RE agencies need retroactive compliance: govern the AI you already have.

**Unique positioning:** "You're already using AI. Let's make sure it's properly governed."

---

### Section 4: nFADP + Property Data
**Content:** Client property data = personal data under nFADP. AI processing of property search data, client financial information, tenant data requires lawful basis under Art. 5(c).

**DPIA requirement:** High-risk AI processing of personal data requires Data Protection Impact Assessment.

---

### Section 5: GAIGI — Institutional, Not SMB
**Content:** GAIGI (Geneva AI Governance Institute) is operative — but gaigi.ch returns 404 for small agency queries. GAIGI is institutionally focused, not SMB-accessible.

**Implication:** Small RE agencies have no institutional AI governance guidance available. This is DigitalCoa.ch's white space.

---

### Section 6: The 4-Step Retroactive Compliance Path
1. **Inventory:** Map all AI tools currently used in agency operations
2. **Classify:** Identify which tools process personal data (client property data, tenant data)
3. **Govern:** Implement nFADP Art. 5(c) lawful basis + DPIA if high-risk
4. **Document:** Create AI governance documentation for agency compliance

---

### Section 7: Why Act Now + CTA
**Content:** 67% AI adoption = highest gap = largest opportunity. GAIGI operative but not SMB-accessible. Swissdamed Jul 1 spillover (~12 weeks) for agencies handling healthcare property. nFADP obligations active NOW.

**CTA:** Introductory consultation offer — retroactive compliance framework
