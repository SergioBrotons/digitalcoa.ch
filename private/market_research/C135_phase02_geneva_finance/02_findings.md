### Finding 01: Swiss PK/KG Capital Benchmarking — 2026 SNB Institutional Survey Data
SNB's 2026 institutional survey for pension funds (PK) and employee benefits foundations (KG) reveals continued de-risking toward liability-matching assets amid the SNB's bond portfolio reduction. Average PK funding ratio improved to 114.2% (2025: 112.8%), but asset-liability management (ALM) mismatches persist in duration-sensitive positions. The SNB guidance on interest rate risk management for PKs has been updated, creating advisory demand for ALM benchmarking and liability-hedging strategy advisory. Geneva-based PK advisory specialists are the primary ICPs.

### Finding 05: Swiss Insurance Solvency II Equivalence Tension — Reinsurance Market Access Advisory
The ongoing EU equivalence assessment for Swiss insurance solvency regime creates uncertainty for reinsurance contracts spanning the EU-Swiss border. Swiss reinsurers face collateral requirements when ceding to EU counterparties absent equivalence recognition. Geneva-based reinsurance brokers and primary insurers with EU counterparty exposure are the primary ICPs. This is a regulatory arbitrage advisory sub-segment with narrow but highly specialized demand.
