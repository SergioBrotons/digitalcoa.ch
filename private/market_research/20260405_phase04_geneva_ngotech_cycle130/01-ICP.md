# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva NGO/Tech — ICP

**Firm Types:**
- International organizations (UNOG, ITU, WHO, WMO, WTO, ILO, UNHCR, ICRC)
- Humanitarian NGOs with Geneva presence
- Geneva-based cleantech and deeptech startups (Series A-B)
- UN specialized agencies with IT/procurement functions
- Multilateral development banks (World Bank, EBRD, EIB — Geneva offices)
- Geneva think tanks and diplomatic missions
- Swissmedic, Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (EPFL)
- Geneva innovation ecosystems (Fongit, TechBridge, HEPIA)

**Decision-Maker:**
- Chief Information Officer / IT Director
- Chief Operating Officer
- Procurement Director / Head of IT procurement
- Head of Digital Transformation
- Technology and Innovation Director
- Board / Investment Committee (for startups)

**Pain Points:**
- UN/IO AI governance standards: ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF → corporate implementation
- Procurement complexity: UN/IO vendor qualification, compliance requirements
- Cleantech ESG due diligence: investor + institutional requirements
- WIPO blockchain/AI IP: PROOF platform, patent portfolio strategy
- Swissmedic SaMD pathway: regulatory complexity, KVG telemedicine reimbursement
- Humanitarian tech contracting: ICRC/UNHCR procurement pathways
- Geneva startup scaling: Series A gap, international expansion

**Urgency Triggers:**
- UN procurement cycle (annual/biennial)
- Investor ESG due diligence requirements
- Regulatory submission deadlines (Swissmedic, FDA)
- WIPO patent filing deadlines
- Climate finance compliance (Art. 6, GCF)
- Technology partnership or licensing negotiation

**Geography:** Geneva + Lausanne (EPFL, SwissTech), Western Switzerland
**Language:** EN primary, FR secondary
**Budget Cycle:** UN biennial, annual for NGOs/startups
