# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## Geneva NGO/Tech

**Target:** CIOs and procurement directors of Geneva IOs/NGOs + founders/CEOs of Geneva cleantech/healthtech startups

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Geneva Cleantech Investment Readiness Briefing (Quarterly)**
- Format: Market update + self-assessment framework
- Content: Series A landscape, ESG due diligence requirements, investor readiness
- CTA: Investment readiness assessment
- Distribution: Fongit, TechBridge, Geneva startup networks

**Option B: UN/IO Procurement Pathway — Swiss Vendor Guide (On-Demand)**
- Format: Step-by-step guide (PDF + consultation)
- Content: ICRC/UNHCR qualification requirements, compliance checklist, pathway
- CTA: Procurement qualification consultation
- Distribution: Geneva Chamber of Commerce, Swiss Export Association

**Option C: WIPO AI IP Advisory Note — Geneva Tech Companies (On-Demand)**
- Format: Regulatory briefing + strategy framework
- Content: WIPO PROOF platform, patent strategy in AI age, blockchain IP protection
- CTA: IP strategy consultation

**Option D: Swissmedic SaMD Regulatory Roadmap (On-Demand)**
- Format: Regulatory pathway + reimbursement framework
- Content: Swissmedic submission, KVG Art. 29 reimbursement, market entry
- CTA: Regulatory roadmap consultation

**Recommended Primary:** Option A (Cleantech Investment Readiness) — broad appeal
**Recommended Secondary:** Option B (UN/IO Procurement Guide) — distinct niche

**Bilingual:** EN primary, FR useful
