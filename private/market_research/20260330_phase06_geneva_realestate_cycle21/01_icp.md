## ICP: Small Real Estate Agencies (Geneva)

### Demographics
- **Agency size:** 2-10 employees; 1-3 partners
- **Location:** Geneva
- **Types:** Residential sales, commercial, property management, tenant representation
- **Revenue:** Transaction-based; property management retainer

### Pain Layer
- **67% AI adoption:** HIGHEST of any vertical in portfolio = widest governance gap
- **GAIGI ~7-10 months operative:** Institutional confirmed 404 (not SMB-accessible)
- **Retroactive compliance:** Unique entry point — firms already using AI without governance
- **Swissdamed spillover:** ~13 weeks; healthcare deadline creates broader awareness
- **nFADP Art. 5(c) active:** DPIA for AI processing property/tenant data

### Decision-Maker Profile
- **Managing partner** — personally accountable
- **AI adoption:** Using AI for property valuation, listing descriptions, tenant screening, market analysis
- **No legal/compliance staff**
- **Low awareness of AI governance obligations**

### Buying Psychology
- **Lower friction entry than anticipatory framing** — firms already using AI; frame is "govern what you have"
- **Liability fear:** AI decisions affecting property transactions, tenant screening
- **Swissdamed spillover:** Healthcare clients asking about AI governance

### Window
- GAIGI: ~7-10 months operative
- Swissdamed: ~13 WEEKS spillover
- Competitive: 7th+ absence confirmed
- Retroactive compliance unique positioning
