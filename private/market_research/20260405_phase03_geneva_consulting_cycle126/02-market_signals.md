## Market Signal 3: CARB Tax Implementation — Geneva Commodity Trader Compliance Advisory

**Signal:**
The Swiss Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CARB / SVUT — SVUT: Sammlung von CO2-Kosten an der Landesgrenze) implementation creates new compliance obligations for Geneva-based commodity traders importing carbon-intensive goods (steel, aluminum, cement, fertilizers, electricity, hydrogen).

Key CARB provisions:
- Importers of carbon-intensive goods must surrender CARB certificates matching the embedded carbon content
- Certificate price aligned with Swiss CO2 levy on heating fuels (currently ~CHF 120/tonne CO2)
- Third-country exporters must provide carbon content documentation
- Exemptions for countries with equivalent carbon pricing (EU ETS equivalent)

Geneva commodity traders with significant imports of covered goods face compliance cost increases and need advisory on cost pass-through strategies, documentation systems, and strategic positioning.

**Quantitative Context:**
- Swiss CARB-covered import volume: ~CHF 5-8B annually (estimated)
- Geneva commodity trader share: significant portion of Swiss commodity imports (steel, aluminum, fertilizers)
- CARB cost per tonne CO2: ~CHF 120 (aligned with Swiss domestic CO2 levy)
- Estimated CARB cost impact per Geneva commodity trading firm: CHF 500K-5M annually (depending on import volume and carbon intensity)

**Implication for DigitalCoa.ch:**
Advisory opportunity: Help Geneva commodity traders understand CARB scope, structure compliant documentation processes, develop CARB cost pass-through strategies in commodity contracts, and assess competitive implications of CARB for their specific commodity streams.

**EN:** "CARB implementation creates immediate compliance costs and competitive dynamics for Geneva commodity traders — advisory on documentation, cost pass-through, and strategic positioning represents a new and urgent advisory category."
**FR:** "La mise en œuvre du SVUT crée des coûts de conformité immédiats et des dynamiques concurrentielles pour les maisons de trading genevoises — le conseil en documentation, en передача затрат et en positionnement stratégique représente une nouvelle catégorie de conseil urgente."
