## Findings (Cycle 99 — Slot 5)

### EN
**Swiss Sanctions Enforcement — SECO, Frozen Assets, and Growing Compliance Demand**

Switzerland adopted EU sanctions against Russia in 2022 and has continued to align with EU packages through 2024-2025. Key landscape:

SECO (State Secretariat for Economic Affairs) role:
- Administering sanctions lists; issuing licenses for humanitarian exemptions
- Coordinating with FINMA for financial sector enforcement
- Frozen assets in Switzerland: significant Russian private wealth affected by asset freezes

Geneva financial sector exposure:
- Private banks holding Russian-origin frozen assets — ongoing compliance burden
- Legal uncertainty: rehypothecation, custody claims, and asset tracing complex
- Sanctions evasion risk: non-Russian beneficial owners of Russian assets creating screening complexity
- Export controls on luxury goods (Geneva watchmaking, luxury goods) — related compliance

Advisory demand:
- Sanctions compliance programs for Geneva commodity traders and private banks
- License applications to SECO for humanitarian or specific transactions
- Asset freeze challenge proceedings
- Due diligence on non-Russian beneficial ownership of formerly Russian-linked structures

Key legal question: what happens to frozen assets if political resolution occurs — restructuring/disposal frameworks not yet developed

### FR
**Application des sanctions suisses — SECO, actifs gelés, et demande croissante de conformité**

La Suisse a adopté les sanctions UE contre la Russie et continue de s'aligner via 2024-2025 :
- SECO : administration des listes de sanctions, octroi de licences humanitaires
- Actifs gelés en Suisse : fortune privée russe significative touchée
- Exposition des banques genevoises : conformité continue, complexité du screening
- Questions de propriété effective non-russe d'actifs anciennement russes

Demande de conseil :
- Programmes de conformité sanctions pour traders de matières premières et banques privées genevoises
- Demandes de licence SECO
- Procédures de contestation de gel d'actifs
- Due diligence sur la propriété effective
