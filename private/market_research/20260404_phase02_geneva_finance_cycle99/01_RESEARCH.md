## Findings (Cycle 99 — Slot 1)

### EN
**Basel IV / FAIR Implementation — Switzerland Moving Forward**

Switzerland is implementing Basel IV (internationally known as Basel III endgame) through the FAIR (Financial Institutions Resolution) framework revision. Key parameters:
- Swiss implementation of Basel IV output floor: 72.5% risk-weighted asset floor confirmed
- Large banks (G-SIBs: UBS, Credit Suisse legacy): higher CET1 requirements; Swiss-specific buffers for systemic risk
- FINMA circular revision in progress to align with FAIR changes
- Small/mid-size Geneva banks: relatively less impacted but operational burden significant
- Investment firms: revised prudential framework creating capital recalibration across Geneva asset managers

Timeline: Parliament debate expected 2025-2026; implementation likely 2026-2027 (post-Sixth Capital Requirements Review)

Impact on Geneva:
- Capital planning reassessment for all FINMA-supervised institutions
- RWA optimization strategies in demand
- Operational cost increases from enhanced reporting requirements

### FR
**Mise en œuvre de Bâle IV en Suisse**

La Suisse met en œuvre Bâle IV via la révision du cadre FAIR :
- Plancher RWA de 72,5% confirmé
- Grandes banques (G-SIB): exigences CET1 plus élevées ; tampons systémiques spécifiques
- Révision de la circulaire FINMA en cours
- Banques genevoises petites/moyennes : impact opérationnel significatif
- Sociétés d'investissement : recalibration des exigences de fonds propres

Calendrier : débat parlementaire attendu 2025-2026 ; mise en œuvre probablement 2026-2027
