## Finding 1: Geneva Deeptech Spin-offs — IO/Institutional Research Commercialization

### Signal
Geneva's international institutions (CERN, ISO, ITU, WMO) generate significant research with commercial potential. Technology transfer from these institutions to startup ecosystem is accelerating, with Geneva-based incubators actively supporting spin-offs.

### Market Opportunity
- **Advisory demand:** Researchers and institutions need commercialization strategy, IP structuring, and startup formation support
- **Distinct angle:** Not general startup ecosystem growth levers (C119) — this is deeptech/commercialization from institutional research
- **Revenue model:** Technology transfer advisory, startup formation, IP strategy
- **Geneva specificity:** CERN, ITU, ISO, WMO are uniquely concentrated in Geneva

### Relevance for DigitalCoa.ch / Brotons.net
- Potential insight: "Geneva's Deeptech Advantage: Technology Transfer from International Institutions"
- Target ICP: Researchers, technology transfer offices, startup founders, venture capital
