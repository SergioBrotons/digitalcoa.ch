The specific mechanism by which AI accumulated in your practice before you made any governance decision. How to discover what is actually operating under your name. Why the first question is not "which AI should I use" but "what AI is already operating under my name that I didn't authorize."

Why building documentation now is evaluated against forming standards, not arrived standards. Why the burden-of-proof inversion that happens when the window closes is not recoverable through retroactive reconstruction. What the window property means for your specific situation right now.

**6. The four service categories that are structurally absent from the Geneva market.**
The specific services that don't exist — pre-signing carrier attestation review, proportionate AI tool inventory and authorization architecture, client AI governance questionnaire response support, documentation window evidence architecture. Why the absence is structural, not just undersupplied.
