# The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework
### Q2 2026 — Geneva SMB AI Governance

---

**Before you advise clients on AI governance, assess your own.**

Switzerland's AI fintech sector is growing. The labor shortage exceeds 400,000 positions. EU AI Act obligations have been in force since August 2025. And Swiss SMBs are navigating a US tariff environment that now includes forced labour supply chain scrutiny under Section 301.

Geneva's small consulting firms are positioned at the intersection of all of these developments — but most haven't assessed their own AI governance readiness before advising clients.

**This framework covers:**
- When the EU AI Act GPAI obligations apply to your own consulting practice
- How Section 301 forced labour creates a new SMB consulting product
- What the Federal Council's "no Swiss AI Act" means for your firm's governance gap
- How ACCTS (new trade/sustainability agreement) creates a compliance documentation opportunity
- The two-track GPAI readiness approach: internal assessment + client advisory

---

**The Geneva consulting firm's AI governance gap is three-dimensional:**

1. **Own use:** Does your firm use AI tools for client work? If those tools serve EU-based clients, you're already subject to EU AI Act GPAI obligations.

2. **Client advisory:** Are you advising clients on AI governance? You need a documented internal GPAI self-assessment before positioning yourself as an advisor.

3. **New product:** Section 301 forced labour + ACCTS ESG documentation = a new consulting product that Geneva firms can build now.

---

**What to do in the next 30 days:**
- Assess your own EU AI Act GPAI exposure (client work touching EU markets)
- Document your internal AI governance framework
- Identify your Section 301 / ACCTS client advisory opportunity

## FR Version

# Le Cadre de Décision IA pour Petits Cabinets de Conseil
### Q2 2026 — Gouvernance IA TPE Genève

---

**Avant de conseiller vos clients sur la gouvernance IA, évaluez la vôtre.**

Le secteur fintech IA suisse croît. La pénurie de main-d'œuvre dépasse 400 000 postes. Les obligations IAO de l'Acte européen sur l'IA sont en vigueur depuis août 2025. Et les PME suisses naviguent dans un environnement de droits de douane américains qui inclut désormais la surveillance des chaînes d'approvisionnement en travail forcé en vertu de la Section 301.

**L'écart de gouvernance IA du cabinet de conseil genevois est tridimensionnel:**

1. **Usage propre:** Votre cabinet utilise-t-il des outils d'IA pour le travail client? Si ces outils servent des clients basés dans l'UE, vous êtes déjà soumis aux obligations IAO.

2. **Conseil client:** Conseillez-vous vos clients sur la gouvernance IA? Vous avez besoin d'une auto-évaluation IAO documentée avant de vous positionner comme conseiller.

3. **Nouveau produit:** Section 301 travail forcé + documentation ESG ACCTS = un nouveau produit de conseil que les cabinets genevois peuvent développer.
