# 03 — Geneva Cantonal Court AI Evidence Rules: Dir. 2026/1 Implementation
**Phase:** C204 Phase 01 (Geneva Legal) | **Cycle:** C204/06

## Finding
Geneva cantonal court's Directive 2026/1 on AI-generated evidence admissibility is moving from publication to implementation — with first contested AI evidence hearings scheduled Q2 2026. Practical implementation questions dominate Geneva litigation practice: how to authenticate AI-generated documents, establish chain of custody for AI evidence, and how expert witnesses must testify about AI system operation.
