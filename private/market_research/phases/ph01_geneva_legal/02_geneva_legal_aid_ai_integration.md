# C205 Finding 02 — Geneva Legal Aid AI Tool Integration April 2026

**Phase:** 01 Geneva Legal | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

Geneva's Service d'aide sociale (SEC / legal aid authority) operationalized an AI legal assistance framework for pro bono services in April 2026 — the first Geneva-level framework addressing AI tool integration in state-supported legal services.

## Key Details

- **Framework scope:** AI-assisted legal document review, preliminary case assessment, and client communication for SEC-represented individuals
- **Confidentiality protocol:** Strict data segregation — no client data leaves Geneva-secured AI environment; AI tool provider has zero data access post-training
- **Client consent:** Mandatory informed consent forms specifying AI tool role, limitations, and human oversight point of contact
- **Liability allocation:** SEC retains full professional liability for AI-assisted advice quality; AI tool provider liability capped contractually
- **First operational quarter:** Q2 2026 — 200+ AI-assisted legal aid cases processed in Geneva; client satisfaction monitoring active
