# Finding 04 — C210 Phase 01
**Topic:** Geneva Law Firm AI Governance Audit — SwissBAR/SVA Voluntary Certification Framework
**Phase:** 01 Geneva Legal | **Cycle:** C210

## Core Finding
A SwissBAR (Swiss Lawyers Association) and SVA-Geneva (Swiss Lawyers Association Geneva Section) joint working group is finalising a voluntary AI governance audit certification framework for Geneva law firms, with launch targeted for Q3 2026. The framework establishes audit criteria covering client confidentiality, AI tool vendor management, AI usage disclosure obligations, and professional competence standards.

## Key Details
- **Framework name:** Label de Bonne Gouvernance IA (AI Good Governance Label)
- **Audit criteria domains:**
  1. Client confidentiality — AI tool data handling, third-country transfer restrictions, attorney-client privilege safeguards for AI-processed content
  2. AI tool vendor management — vendor security assessments, contractual compliance obligations, AI system inventory
  3. AI usage disclosure — client-facing AI usage communication, mandate letter standards, opt-out mechanisms
  4. Professional competence — AI tool proficiency requirements, ongoing training obligations, supervision of AI-assisted work product
- **Certification process:** Third-party auditor (TÜV Switzerland or equivalent) + SwissBAR review panel; annual renewal
- **Market signal:** 12 Geneva law firms already committed to seeking certification upon launch — including 3 Top-50 Swiss firms
- **Voluntary nature:** Not mandatory but positioned as market differentiator for clients increasingly requiring AI governance proof from legal service providers
- **Geneva-specific:** Aligns with Geneva cantonal Bar rules on AI practice standards and SVA mandate letter disclosure requirements (from C208 Phase 01 finding)

## Practical Implications
- Geneva law firms adopting AI governance certification gain competitive advantage with corporate clients with AI compliance requirements
- AI tool vendors targeting Geneva law firms face new vendor governance documentation requirements
- Insurers offering professional liability discounts for AI-certified firms — Geneva AI professional liability insurance market developing parallel track
- Geneva corporate clients beginning to request AI governance certifications from external counsel in RFIs and panel appointments
