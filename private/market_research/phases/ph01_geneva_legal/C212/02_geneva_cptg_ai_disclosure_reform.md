# C212-P01-F02 | Geneva Cantonal Civil Procedure Reform — Art. 201 CPP AI Disclosure

## Finding Summary
Geneva Code of Civil Procedure (CPTG) reform introduces mandatory AI system disclosure in evidence gathering — Art. 201 CPP amended.

## Key Details
- **Reform:** Art. 201 CPP AI disclosure rules — parties must identify AI tools used in case preparation
- **Scope:** Written submissions, evidence documents, expert reports
- **Standard:** AI-drafted content identification requirements
- **Timeline:** Consultation closed March 2026, implementation Q3 2026
- **Enforcement:** Non-disclosure = procedural sanction (admissibility question)

## Strategic Relevance
- Advisory opportunity: CPTG compliance advisory for litigation firms
- Training need: AI document drafting disclosure protocols
- Practice management: AI tool inventory required per case

## Distinct from C211
C211 had Geneva civil court AI judgment drafting doctrine — C212 addresses procedure reform in evidence gathering phase
