# C212-P01-F01 | Geneva SVA/ORDRE AI Legal Tech Certification Standard

## Finding Summary
Geneva cantonal bar (SVA/ORDRE) launched mandatory quality label for AI tools used in legal practice — effective Q2 2026.

## Key Details
- **Certification body:** SVA-Geneva + ORDRE joint working group
- **Scope:** AI-drafted documents, research tools, case management, billing AI
- **Requirement:** Law firms using uncertified AI tools must disclose to clients
- **Timeline:** Voluntary Q2 2026, mandatory Q4 2026
- **Compliance pathway:** Self-assessment → peer review → certification

## Strategic Relevance
- Advisory opportunity: AI tool certification advisory for Geneva law firms
- Market gap: Preparation consultancy for certification process
- Client communication: AI mandate letter updates required

## Distinct from C211
C211 had law firm AI liability insurance underwriting framework — C212 addresses certification/quality label standard
