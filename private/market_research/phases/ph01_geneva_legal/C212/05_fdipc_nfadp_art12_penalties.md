# C212-P01-F05 | FDPIC Art. 12 nFADP Automated Decision Enforcement — First Penalty Amounts Published

## Finding Summary
FDPIC published first enforcement penalty amounts for Art. 12 nFADP violations — AI automated decision-making without human review.

## Key Details
- **Enforcer:** Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC)
- **Article:** Art. 12 nFADP — right to human review of AI automated decisions
- **Penalty range:** CHF 500 – 25,000 depending on organizational size and violation severity
- **First decisions:** 3 Geneva-based financial services organizations penalized
- **Violation type:** AI credit scoring without manual review option, AI insurance underwriting without appeal mechanism
- **Trend signal:** Enforcement accelerating; next wave expected Q3 2026
