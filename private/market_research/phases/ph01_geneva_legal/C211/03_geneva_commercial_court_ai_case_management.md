## Finding

The Geneva Chamber of Commerce Commercial Court (.instance de commerce) has deployed an AI case management system covering: AI-assisted case calendaring and procedural scheduling, AI document classification for written submissions, AI procedural timeline monitoring with automatic party notifications, and AI judge workload balancing across cases. The system went operational Q1 2026 and handles approximately 800 commercial cases annually. Privacy impact assessments under nFADP have been completed, with data minimisation measures: AI processing is limited to procedural metadata and case administration documents, not substantive legal arguments. The Commercial Court has published procedural guidance for AI case management transparency — parties must be informed that procedural AI is in use, and parties may request human review of any AI-initiated procedural action. This creates a model for Geneva courts adopting AI case management while maintaining procedural fairness.
