## Finding

The court's ruling (TGest 2026/AI/001) establishes that AI drafting assistance is permissible under Swiss procedure provided: (1) the judge exercises independent intellectual engagement with the case before reviewing AI-generated drafts, (2) the AI draft is not presented to parties without judge endorsement, (3) the judge can identify and deviate from any AI-generated reasoning errors. The ruling creates a framework for Geneva courts using AI judicial assistance while maintaining judicial independence — the intellectual deliberation must precede AI draft review, preventing AI from anchoring judicial reasoning.
