## Finding

Swiss professional liability insurers have developed an underwriting framework for law firms deploying AI legal tools, operational since Q1 2026. The framework assesses AI liability risk across: (1) AI system autonomy level — higher autonomy increases premium loading, (2) AI output review procedures — documented human review protocols reduce premiums, (3) AI vendor contract structures — limitation of liability clauses and indemnification provisions affect coverage adequacy, (4) client disclosure practices — whether clients are informed AI tools are used in their matters affects coverage terms. Geneva law firms face AI professional liability exposure gaps: most Swiss law firm professional liability policies were written before AI tools existed and may not explicitly cover AI-assisted legal work. The new underwriting framework creates standard endorsement language for AI professional liability, with Geneva SVA/ORDRE coordinating member firm adoption.
