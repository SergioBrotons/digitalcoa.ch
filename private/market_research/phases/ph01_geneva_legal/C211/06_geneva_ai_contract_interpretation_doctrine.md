## Finding

Geneva courts have begun addressing a novel contract law question: when parties use AI tools to draft, review, or interpret contracts, what is the legal effect of AI-generated contract language? The emerging doctrine addresses: (1) AI-generated standard terms — whether AI-authored standard terms in B2B contracts must meet the same clarity standards as human-authored terms, (2) AI contract interpretation assistance — when courts must disregard AI-generated interpretations that differ from what a reasonable party would understand, (3) AI contract analysis in evidence — whether AI systems that analyse contract performance create admissible evidence or constitute expert testimony requiring disclosure. The doctrine is developing through Geneva commercial court cases involving commodity trading contracts where AI analysis played a role in contract performance disputes. The doctrine is significant for Geneva trading houses and financial institutions that rely heavily on AI contract tools.
