## 04 — Trust Signal Audit

### What Signals Work for This ICP

**What works:**
- Pattern recognition evidence: specific description of situations navigated, Swiss institutional contexts worked in, board questions answered
- Process exit clarity: understanding of what the engagement looks like, when it ends, what the executive takes away
- Discretion practice: demonstrated over stated — how the advisor talks about other engagements without revealing specifics
- Structural independence: no vendor affiliations, no product sales, no conflicts
- Behavioral independence: the advisory conversation itself demonstrates independence through what the advisor will not do

**What does not move the needle:**
- Certification badges
- Client logos or testimonials
- Years of experience claims without pattern specificity
- Frameworks presented as credentials
