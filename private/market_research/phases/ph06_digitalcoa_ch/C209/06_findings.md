# Finding 06 | C209 | DigitalCoa.ch

## Topic
DigitalCoa.ch Brotons.net conversion pathway optimisation — strengthening referral to advisory pipeline

## EN
DigitalCoa.ch is systematically optimising the conversion pathway from Brotons.net content consumption to DigitalCoa.ch advisory enquiry — extending beyond the Brotons.net referral architecture noted in C208 finding 4, which established the referral mechanism without optimising conversion quality. The optimisation work covers four dimensions: first, intent-signal design — DigitalCoa.ch advisory CTAs are being placed at the conclusion of Brotons.net content that signals genuine regulatory decision-making need (enforcement case analysis, compliance timeline pieces, framework comparison posts) rather than placed uniformly across all content; second, warm referral mechanism — when a Brotons.net reader clicks a DigitalCoa.ch CTA, the referral includes context about which specific Brotons.net content was consumed, enabling DigitalCoa.ch to personalise its first response; third, trust-transfer design — the referral pathway explicitly acknowledges the Brotons.net intellectual lineage, making clear that DigitalCoa.ch advisory is the 'applied' extension of Brotons.net's analytical work, reducing the trust-building friction an executive would otherwise face with a cold advisory engagement; fourth, feedback loop — DigitalCoa.ch is tracking which Brotons.net content topics most frequently generate advisory enquiries, and sharing this data with Brotons.net editorial to inform future content prioritisation — creating a data-sharing relationship that benefits both platforms. The optimisation is generating measurable improvements in advisory enquiry quality, with executives arriving at DigitalCoa.ch with more specific AI governance challenges than was the case under the undifferentiated referral approach.

## FR
DigitalCoa.ch optimise systématiquement le sentier de conversion de la consommation de contenu Brotons.net vers la demande de conseil DigitalCoa.ch — s'étendant au-delà de l'architecture de référence Brotons.net notée au résultat C208 n°4, qui avait établi le mécanisme de référence sans optimiser la qualité de conversion. L'optimisation couvre quatre dimensions: premièrement, conception des signaux d'intention; deuxièmement, mécanisme de référence chaleureux; troisièmement, conception du transfert de confiance; quatrièmement, boucle de rétroaction. L'optimisation génère des améliorations mesurables dans la qualité de la demande de conseil, les exécutifs arrivant chez DigitalCoa.ch avec des défis de gouvernance IA plus spécifiques que dans l'approche de référence indifférenciée.
