# Finding 02 | C209 | DigitalCoa.ch

## Topic
DigitalCoa.ch executive lead nurturing sequence — from content consumption to advisory enquiry

## EN
DigitalCoa.ch is designing an executive lead nurturing sequence that systematically converts high-intent executive content consumers into advisory enquiry conversations — extending beyond the executive briefing CTA noted in C208 skipped items. The sequence is triggered when an executive engages with DigitalCoa.ch content at a threshold level indicating genuine advisory interest (not casual browsing): opening more than three DigitalCoa.ch newsletters, attending a Brotons.net event, or directly visiting DigitalCoa.ch advisory service pages from a Brotons.net content link. The nurturing sequence operates in three stages: Stage 1 (Days 1-7) — personalised executive brief delivered via email, summarising a specific AI governance development directly relevant to the executive's known industry context, framed as a 'headsup' rather than a sales pitch, with a soft invite to schedule a 30-minute call; Stage 2 (Days 14-21) — conditional on Stage 1 engagement (email opened within 48 hours), a deeper analysis piece with a specific regulatory timeline relevant to the executive's sector, concluding with a non-committal invitation to a DigitalCoa.ch executive roundtable; Stage 3 (Days 30-45) — for executives who engaged with both Stage 1 and Stage 2, a direct but informal check-in asking whether the executive is facing an AI governance challenge that DigitalCoa.ch could help with. The sequence is explicitly designed to respect executive time and trust — no more than three contacts, always providing genuine value, and stopping immediately if the executive indicates disinterest.

## FR
DigitalCoa.ch dessine une séquence de nurturance de leads exécutifs qui convertit systématiquement les consommateurs de contenu exécutif à haute intention en conversations de demande de conseil — étendant au-delà du CTA de briefing exécutif noté dans les éléments sautés C208. La séquence est déclenchée lorsqu'un exécutif s'engage avec le contenu DigitalCoa.ch à un niveau seuil indiquant un intérêt véritable pour le conseil: ouvrir plus de trois newsletters DigitalCoa.ch, assister à un événement Brotons.net, ou visiter directement les pages de services de conseil DigitalCoa.ch depuis un lien de contenu Brotons.net. La séquence de nurturance opère en trois étapes: Stage 1 — briefing exécutif personnalisé, Stage 2 — piece d'analyse plus profonde, Stage 3 — vérification directe. La séquence est explicitement conçue pour respecter le temps et la confiance des exécutifs — pas plus de trois contacts, fournissant toujours une valeur véritable.
