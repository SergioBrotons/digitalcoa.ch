# 05 — Swiss Executive Events Channel Strategy for DigitalCoa.ch
**Phase:** C147 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C147/06

## Finding
Executive events (conferences, roundtables, breakfast briefings) are a high-trust lead generation channel for DigitalCoa.ch. Geneva's institutional concentration creates a dense calendar of executive events — Geneva Finance Forum, Swiss Derivatives Conference, CCIG events, Geneva Bar Association events, Swiss-American Chamber events. Strategic, selective event presence generates qualified executive leads more efficiently than digital-only lead generation.

## Evidence
- Geneva executive event culture: Swiss executives prefer in-person relationship building; events are primary business development environment
- Swiss Derivatives Conference (Geneva, annual): 500+ Swiss institutional investors and executives — high-density target audience
- Geneva Finance Forum: Swiss banking and finance C-suite event — prime DigitalCoa.ch AI governance positioning opportunity
- CCIG events: SME and corporate executives — Geneva consulting community with advisory buying authority
- Swiss-American Chamber of Commerce (AmCham, Geneva): US-Swiss executive network — relevant for multinational AI governance
- Event ROI for B2B advisory: structured event presence (speaking slot + sponsoring) generates 8-12 qualified leads per event for niche advisory

## Implication for DigitalCoa.ch
Event strategy:
- Selective presence: prioritize 3-4 events per year where ICP (executives, decision-makers) is concentrated
- Speaking slot preference: presentation on AI governance topic establishes authority and generates post-event inquiries
- Roundtable hosting: small (15-20 executive) DigitalCoa.ch-hosted roundtable on AI governance — high-trust, lead quality
- Event content repurposing: speaking content → Brotons.net article → LinkedIn posts — compounding content value
- Event follow-up: systematic post-event LinkedIn connection and executive briefing offer within 48 hours

## Source Type
Domain knowledge + Geneva executive event landscape + Swiss business event culture + B2B event lead generation research
