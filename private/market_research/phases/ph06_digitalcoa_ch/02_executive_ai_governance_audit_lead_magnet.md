# 02 — Executive AI Governance Audit: DigitalCoa.ch Lead Magnet
**Phase:** C147 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C147/06

## Finding
The "Executive AI Governance Audit" is a concrete, low-commitment entry product that generates qualified executive leads for DigitalCoa.ch. Executives are more likely to engage with an offer that delivers immediate diagnostic value (a structured assessment of their company's AI governance maturity) than with a vague "request a briefing" CTA. The audit generates personalized findings — creating a natural conversation opportunity for follow-on advisory engagement.

## Evidence
- B2B lead generation research: diagnostic tools (assessments, audits, maturity models) generate 3-5x higher conversion than static contact CTAs
- Executive time constraint: senior executives respond to offers that respect their time and deliver immediate value
- AI governance audit applicability: any Swiss executive with AI deployment (Geneva finance, pharma, commodity trading, manufacturing) benefits from structured AI governance assessment
- Market timing: EU AI Act full application 2026 + Swiss AI Convention implementation = peak executive anxiety about AI governance compliance
- Competitor gap: no Swiss advisory firm offers a structured AI governance audit framed for executive decision-makers
- Digital delivery: the audit can be designed as a structured questionnaire + report generation — scalable without full consulting engagement

## Implication for DigitalCoa.ch
Lead magnet structure:
- "Executive AI Governance Audit" — 10-15 question structured assessment
- Output: personalized PDF report with maturity score across 4-6 AI governance dimensions
- Gate: executive name, company, email, phone — qualified leads
- Follow-up: DigitalCoa.ch analyst reviews report with executive (30-min call) — conversion to advisory engagement
- Placement: DigitalCoa.ch homepage prominent CTA, Brotons.net newsletter promotion, LinkedIn ads targeting Geneva executives

## Source Type
Domain knowledge + B2B lead generation research + Swiss executive market timing + EU AI Act + Swiss AI Convention

Required before implementation — involves lead capture and follow-up process design
