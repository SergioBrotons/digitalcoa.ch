# C206 Finding 06E — DigitalCoa.ch AI Regulatory Intelligence Subscription — Institutional Access Tier

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription with institutional access tier — providing FDPIC/FINMA/SBA informal guidance access, regulatory timeline intelligence, and Geneva institutional subscriber community for subscribers — functioning as both capacity multiplier and warm advisory pipeline mechanism.

## Key Details

- **Institutional access tier design:** AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription with tiered access — basic regulatory scan, institutional access tier with informal guidance access, premium advisory pipeline tier
- **Informal guidance access:** Subscribers gain access to DigitalCoa.ch informal guidance relationships (FDPIC, FINMA, SBA, SECO) — regulatory interpretation intelligence not available from public sources
- **Regulatory timeline intelligence:** Monthly regulatory horizon scan with Geneva-specific AI governance timeline intelligence — when FDPIC will act, FINMA guidance publication timing, SBA enforcement priorities
- **Geneva institutional subscriber community:** Institutional subscribers (UNOG compliance officers, Geneva cantonal AI managers, Geneva corporate AI governance leads) create peer intelligence network — subscriber community as value proposition
- **Capacity multiplier:** Subscription scales DigitalCoa.ch reach without senior advisory time — institutional subscribers receiving ongoing intelligence without full advisory engagement
- **Advisory pipeline mechanism:** Subscription subscribers as warm advisory leads — regulatory intelligence engagement identifies advisory need before it becomes acute; subscription as first step in advisory relationship

## Strategic Angle for DigitalCoa.ch

- Subscription tier development: structuring AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription with institutional access tier
- Informal guidance relationship development: strengthening FDPIC/FINMA/SBA informal guidance relationships for subscriber value
- Subscriber community development: building Geneva institutional subscriber community around AI Regulatory Intelligence
