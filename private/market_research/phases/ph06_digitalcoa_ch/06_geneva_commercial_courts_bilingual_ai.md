# 06 — DigitalCoa.ch Service Portfolio Adjacent Expansion: Strategic Fit Assessment
**Phase:** C204 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C204/06

## Finding
DigitalCoa.ch's adjacent service expansion should be assessed against three criteria: (1) Geneva institutional intelligence leverage, (2) executive relationship adjacency, and (3) brand coherence. Services that leverage existing institutional intelligence and executive relationships with brand coherence are viable. Services that require different institutional contexts or dilute brand focus should be avoided. Based on these criteria, three adjacent services pass the test: AI governance board training, AI governance crisis advisory, and AI governance M&A due diligence (executive-level, not operational).

## Evidence
- Adjacent service criteria: Geneva institutional intelligence leverage (high for regulatory-adjacent services), executive relationship adjacency (high for board/executive level), brand coherence (AI governance authority)
- AI governance board training: leverages Geneva institutional intelligence, uses existing executive relationships, brand coherent — high strategic fit
- AI governance crisis advisory: Geneva institutional intelligence (regulatory enforcement, incident response), executive relationships — high strategic fit
- AI governance M&A executive due diligence: leverages Geneva AI governance intelligence, serves executive decision-makers, brand coherent — medium-high strategic fit
- AI implementation consulting: requires operational delivery capability, different institutional context — not strategic fit
- Geneva luxury brand AI governance advisory: brand coherence but different institutional context (luxury brands vs. financial/institutional) — medium fit, requires separate capability building
- International AI governance advisory: leverages Geneva institutional intelligence as Swiss authority signal but requires different institutional context — selective entry only

## Advisory Implication
DigitalCoa.ch should expand selectively: AI governance board training and AI governance crisis advisory are natural adjacencies. AI governance M&A executive due diligence is viable. All three leverage existing intelligence and relationships while maintaining brand coherence. International expansion should remain Geneva-anchored.

## Confidence
High — strategic assessment framework, market evidence
