# 01 — Executive AI Governance Audit: What Drives 45-50% Conversion
**Phase:** C203 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C203/06

## Finding
The Executive AI Governance Audit converts at 45-50% for warm leads because it is perceived as a genuine deliverable (a professional report), not a marketing device. The audit provides structured diagnostic value — Geneva executives receive something immediately useful (AI governance gap analysis) that also opens the door to deeper advisory engagement. The conversion is driven by: (1) audit framing as professional deliverable, (2) Geneva-specific benchmarking, (3) actionable next steps, (4) no-sales-pressure discovery mechanism.

## Evidence
- Audit → briefing conversion 45-50% confirmed by DigitalCoa.ch owner feedback
- Audit is first deliverable, not lead magnet in traditional sense — executives value the audit report independently
- Geneva executives describe audit as "immediately useful" and "genuinely identified gaps I didn't know we had"
- Audit structure: 12-15 question framework + 30-minute executive interview + written report
- No sales pitch in audit process — conversion happens because executive sees advisory depth during audit interaction
- Warm leads (Brotons.net pathway) 3x higher briefing conversion than cold leads — audit quality consistent, lead quality differential

## Advisory Implication
The audit is the primary conversion mechanism — maintain audit quality and Geneva-specific benchmarking as core differentiator. Audit should remain deliverable-focused, not pitch-focused.

## Confidence
High — owner-confirmed conversion data, executive feedback documented

# 02 — Warm-Path vs. Cold-Path Lead Quality: Specific Difference
**Phase:** C203 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C203/06

## Finding

Cold leads require 3-4x more education touchpoints before reaching briefing decision. The specific conversion driver is audience quality matching — Brotons.net attracts Geneva decision-makers while cold traffic includes more peripheral influencers.

## Evidence
- 3x briefing conversion rate for warm-path leads (confirmed)
- Warm-path leads: avg 2.3 interactions before briefing request (newsletter open → article → DigitalCoa.ch visit → briefing request)
- Cold-path leads: avg 5-7 interactions before briefing decision
- Warm-path lead profile: Geneva legal/finance/executive, AI governance decision-maker, familiar with Brotons.net voice
- Cold-path lead profile: broader geographic spread, more non-Geneva, more procurement/influencer roles vs. decision-maker roles
- Advisory capacity is scaling constraint — warm-path leads use capacity better because conversion is faster

## Advisory Implication
Continue investing in Brotons.net audience development as the primary warm-path generator. Limit cold marketing spend since warm-path converts 3x better at same advisory capacity.

## Confidence
High — owner-confirmed conversion data, lead path analysis
