# C207-03 — DigitalCoa.ch Advisory Capacity Constraint Resolution
**Phase:** C207 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
DigitalCoa.ch advisory capacity constraint is structural — demand exceeds capacity. Resolution pathways: partner network development (associate advisors, specialized sub-advisors for sub-segments), AI regulatory intelligence subscription as capacity multiplier (subscription scales without consuming advisory hours), executive community events as group-format advisory (12 executives per event vs. 1:1 advisory). Capacity constraint resolution determines whether DigitalCoa.ch can capture international org and family office mandate pipeline.

## Evidence
- Structural advisory capacity constraint at DigitalCoa.ch
- Partner network development: associate advisors, specialized sub-advisors
- AI regulatory intelligence subscription as capacity multiplier
- Executive community events as group-format advisory (12 executives per event)
- Capacity constraint determines international org and family office mandate capture
- Subscription scales without consuming advisory hours

## Advisory Implication
DigitalCoa.ch capacity constraint resolution as strategic imperative. Partner network development for sub-sector expertise (life sciences, international orgs, family office). AI regulatory intelligence subscription as recurring revenue model that scales. Executive community events as efficient group advisory format. Capacity constraint resolution strategy determines whether DigitalCoa.ch can capture the mandate pipeline created by Brotons.net content and UNAIWG compliance deadline demand.

## Confidence
High — demand exceeding capacity observation, resolution pathway logic

## FR
### Résolution de la Contrainte de Capacité de Conseil de DigitalCoa.ch
La contrainte de capacité de conseil structurelle de DigitalCoa.ch — la demande dépasse la capacité. Voies de résolution: développement du réseau de partenaires (conseillers associés, sous-conseillers spécialisés), abonnement à l'intelligence réglementaire IA comme multiplicateur de capacité (l'abonnement s'échelonne sans consommer d'heures de conseil), événements communautaires exécutifs comme format de conseil de groupe (12 directeurs par événement). La résolution de la contrainte de capacité détermine si DigitalCoa.ch peut capturer le pipeline de mandats des organisations internationales et des family offices.
