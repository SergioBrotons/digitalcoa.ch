# C205 Finding 05 — DigitalCoa.ch Capacity Constraint Resolution Models

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

DigitalCoa.ch has three capacity constraint resolution models — premium price escalation, AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription, and senior advisor associate model — with models 2+3 most consistent with brand positioning and premium advisory positioning.

## Key Details

- **Model 1 — Premium price escalation:** Raise advisory prices to reduce demand volume — addresses capacity constraint but risks brand positioning as exclusive vs. premium, may reduce mandate conversion rates, signals scarcity rather than demand strength
- **Model 2 — AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription:** Associate-delivered subscription product scales reach without senior advisory hours — senior advisor provides oversight and quality control, associates deliver routine intelligence, subscription generates warm mandate pipeline; maintains premium positioning while multiplying reach
- **Model 3 — Senior advisor associate model:** Train and develop associate-level advisors who can deliver portions of advisory mandates under senior oversight — retains quality control while enabling mandate volume scaling; requires associate development investment, risks quality dilution if not managed carefully
- **Model 2+3 combined:** Subscription (model 2) generates and warms pipeline; senior advisor manages relationship and high-value advisory deliverables; associates handle research and supporting deliverables under senior oversight — maximizes both quality and reach
- **Brand positioning compatibility:** Models 2+3 preserve DigitalCoa.ch premium positioning — subscription is intelligence-tier service, associates are under senior guidance, neither dilutes core advisory brand
- **Implementation requirements:** Model 2 requires subscription product design and delivery infrastructure; Model 3 requires associate hiring and development program; both require 3-6 months to operationalize

## Strategic Angle

- Capacity constraint is primary growth limiter for DigitalCoa.ch — resolving it unlocks revenue growth without brand dilution
- Model 2+3 combined approach is most aligned with institutional intelligence moat and premium positioning
- Decision on which model(s) to implement is strategic — requires owner approval
