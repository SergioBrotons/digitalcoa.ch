# C205 Finding 06 — DigitalCoa.ch Brotons.net Institutional Trust Transfer

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

Brotons.net's institutional citation architecture (UNOG/ICRC/FDPIC/Canton de Genève) creates a measurable trust transfer effect to DigitalCoa.ch — cold traffic arriving at DigitalCoa.ch from Brotons.net institutional citation articles converts at higher rates than cold traffic from other sources.

## Key Details

- **Trust transfer mechanism:** Brotons.net articles citing Geneva institutions (UNOG, ICRC, FDPIC, Canton de Genève) signal Geneva institutional grounding to readers — readers who discover DigitalCoa.ch through Brotons.net arrive with inherited institutional trust perception
- **Conversion rate differential:** Traffic from Brotons.net institutional citation articles converts to advisory inquiries at higher rates than: (1) direct cold traffic, (2) LinkedIn cold outreach responses, (3) organic search traffic — trust transfer effect is measurable
- **Trust compounding:** Each Brotons.net institutional trust investment (institutional citation maintenance, Geneva institutional relationship development) compounds both Brotons.net authority AND DigitalCoa.ch trust transfer
- **Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch pathway architecture:** Specific service naming in anchor text (C204 finding) combined with institutional trust transfer (C205 finding) — both mechanism and pathway optimized; converts trusted Brotons.net readers to DigitalCoa.ch advisory prospects
- **Brotons.net institutional citation as investment:** Every Brotons.net article that earns and cites a Geneva institutional source is simultaneously: building Brotons.net authority, maintaining institutional relationships, and building DigitalCoa.ch trust transfer pathway
- **Cold traffic vs. warm pipeline:** Brotons.net institutional trust transfer converts cold DigitalCoa.ch traffic to warm pipeline — reduces reliance on cold outreach, strengthens inbound advisory demand generation

## Strategic Angle

- Brotons.net institutional citation strategy is a compound investment — benefits both Brotons.net and DigitalCoa.ch simultaneously
- Every new institutional citation on Brotons.net strengthens DigitalCoa.ch trust transfer pathway
- Resource allocation to Brotons.net institutional engagement (interviews, institutional content) directly benefits DigitalCoa.ch advisory pipeline
