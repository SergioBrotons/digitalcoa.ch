# C206 Finding 06A — DigitalCoa.ch Swiss-EU AI Governance Bridge Advisory

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

DigitalCoa.ch can position as the advisory firm for the Swiss-EU AI governance interface — Swiss companies with EU market access and EU companies with Swiss operations need navigation of dual AI governance frameworks, creating unique bilateral advisory demand that Geneva's non-EU position makes possible.

## Key Details

- **Swiss-EU AI governance interface advisory:** Advisory for Swiss companies navigating EU AI Act requirements while remaining Swiss AI Act compliant; advisory for EU companies with Swiss subsidiaries managing AI governance across Swiss-EU interface
- **Switzerland non-EU position advantage:** Switzerland not subject to EU AI Act directly but Swiss companies with EU market access face EU AI Act requirements; creates unique advisory demand for Swiss-EU AI governance navigation
- **Multinational executive clientele:** Geneva-based multinational executives (pharma, commodities, luxury goods, financial services) managing Swiss-EU AI governance across corporate groups — discrete advisory mandate type
- **Bilateral framework expertise:** DigitalCoa.ch institutional intelligence on both Swiss AI governance developments and EU AI Act evolution enables bilateral advisory positioning
- **Council of Europe Convention addition:** Switzerland's Council of Europe AI Convention ratification adding third framework layer — Swiss-EU-Convention tripartite advisory emerging
- **Advisory mandate design:** Swiss-EU AI governance bridge advisory as standalone service or integrated with broader AI governance advisory mandates

## Strategic Angle for DigitalCoa.ch

- Swiss-EU AI governance bridge advisory service: developing service line for multinational clients navigating Swiss-EU AI governance
- EU AI Act compliance for Swiss companies: advisory on EU AI Act classification and compliance for Swiss companies with EU market access
- Swiss AI governance for EU subsidiaries: advisory on Swiss AI Act requirements for EU parent company subsidiaries in Switzerland
