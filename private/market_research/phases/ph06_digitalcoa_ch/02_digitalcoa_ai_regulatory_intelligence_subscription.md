# C205 Finding 02 — DigitalCoa.ch AI Regulatory Intelligence Subscription Product Design

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The AI Regulatory Intelligence subscription product should be designed as a trust-building lead nurturing mechanism — monthly regulatory horizon scan, executive briefing summaries, regulatory timeline intelligence, and informal institutional guidance access — converting Geneva executives before their advisory need becomes acute.

## Key Details

- **Product offering design:** Monthly AI regulatory intelligence package: (1) 3-5 Geneva/Swiss AI regulatory developments with interpretation, (2) regulatory timeline intelligence — what FDPIC/FINMA/SBA likely to act on and when, (3) executive briefing summary format for internal circulation, (4) quarterly live Q&A session
- **Trust-building function:** Subscription converts Geneva executives to DigitalCoa.ch relationship before competitive advisory need — executives who receive monthly intelligence develop trust in DigitalCoa.ch institutional intelligence before they need formal advisory
- **Institutional informal guidance access:** Subscribers gain access to informal regulatory intelligence not publicly available — FDPIC informal signals, FINMA examination priorities, SECO guidance timing; requires DigitalCoa.ch institutional network to deliver
- **Pricing:** CHF 800-1,500/month per executive subscriber; annual subscription discount; Geneva corporate subscribers targeting 20-30 executive subscribers in first year
- **Demand generation:** Subscription generates warm advisory demand — subscribers who face acute AI governance need convert to advisory mandates at higher rates than cold outreach targets
- **Capacity multiplier:** AI Regulatory Intelligence scales DigitalCoa.ch reach without consuming senior advisory hours — associate-delivered intelligence product with senior oversight; enables capacity constraint management

## Strategic Angle

- Subscription as capacity multiplier and demand generation simultaneously — addresses both scaling and demand creation
- Institutional informal guidance access is the product differentiator — cannot be replicated by generalist AI regulatory trackers
- Subscription converts Geneva executives to warm pipeline over 6-12 months
