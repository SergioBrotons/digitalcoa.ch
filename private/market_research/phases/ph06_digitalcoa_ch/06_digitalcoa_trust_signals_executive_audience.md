# 06 — DigitalCoa.ch Trust Signals for Executive Audience
**Phase:** C147 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C147/06

## Finding
DigitalCoa.ch must explicitly signal trustworthiness to senior executives who have been burned by generic consulting marketing. The trust signals that matter to Geneva executives are: demonstrated Swiss institutional credibility, track record specificity, methodology transparency, and professional affiliation. Generic trust badges (e.g., "award-winning") are discounted; institutional trust signals are premium.

## Evidence
- Executive B2B website trust research: Swiss executives highest-trust signals are (1) institutional affiliation, (2) specific case study, (3) professional publication citation, (4) methodology disclosure
- Geneva executive skepticism: Geneva's concentration of sophisticated professional services buyers means executives are immune to generic consulting marketing — they can detect hollow claims
- Competitor analysis: Swiss advisory websites typically fail at trust signals — they over-rely on testimonials and under-rely on institutional proof
- DigitalCoa.ch positioning (executive, AI governance, Geneva institutional depth) — trust signals should amplify this specific positioning
- LinkedIn credibility: Geneva executives check LinkedIn profiles before engaging — DigitalCoa.ch principals' LinkedIn presence is a trust signal
- Professional association membership: Geneva Bar Association, Swiss Business Federation (economiesuisse), Swiss Financial Analysts — institutional affiliations signal credibility

## Implication for DigitalCoa.ch
Trust signal implementation:
- "Featured in / Cited by" section: Geneva institutional sources where DigitalCoa.ch analysis appears (WTO, UN Geneva, Geneva Bar Association publications)
- Specific methodology page: how advisory engagements are structured, what executives can expect, how results are measured
- Geneva institutional proof: speaking at Geneva institutions, publications in Geneva professional journals
- Principal LinkedIn profile: active, institutional content-focused profile as trust signal
- Professional affiliation display: Swiss institutional memberships prominently displayed
- Case study framing: outcome-specific (not testimonial-generic) — "Executive briefing → board AI governance framework adopted"

## Source Type
Domain knowledge + B2B executive trust research + Swiss executive skepticism analysis + DigitalCoa.ch positioning
