**Geneva legal boutiques:** Legal-regulatory framing that addresses legal questions. Missing the executive accountability perspective that precedes legal questions and the informal circuit intelligence that determines what adequate means.

**Tech/cyber security firms:** Technology security framing that addresses tool security, not decision accountability. Missing the governance and professional liability dimensions that technology security framing cannot address.

**This practice structural advantage:** Geneva-French informal circuit participation producing Geneva-calibrated methodology that cannot be replicated by external analysis. The structural position is the competitive moat, not the content volume or methodology claims.

## 06 — Findings Summary

**Finding 1: Adequacy definition vacuum as primary demand generation mechanism for Geneva-specific advisory**
The undefined adequacy standard is generating demand from executives who cannot confirm compliance with a requirement nobody has defined. The vacuum is the primary demand generation mechanism for Geneva-specific advisory that general frameworks cannot address.

**Finding 2: Federal AI Coordination Office decision crystallization as structural event creating anticipatory positioning value**
The decision crystallization creates anticipatory positioning value that disappears after the decision arrives. The window for providing this positioning analysis is the months before the decision, not after.

**Finding 3: Pre-engagement disqualification logic performing trust formation function independent of content evaluation**
The documented refusal decisions function as trust formation before any content is evaluated. The refusal demonstrates accurate self-assessment. The ICP evaluates fit before evaluating analysis.

**Finding 4: Naming structurally unnamed problems as primary value creation mechanism distinct from analysis delivery**
The primary value is not analysis delivery. It is problem naming. Problems that are structurally unnamed cannot be addressed systematically. The naming function creates value before the analysis function.

**Finding 5: Geneva circuit participation as non-replicable trust formation mechanism**
The structural position inside the Geneva informal circuit is the trust formation mechanism that cannot be replicated by content production, expertise claims, or methodology documentation.

**Finding 6: Return engagement occasion density as pricing architecture design variable determining sustainable volume**
The density of return occasions across the calendar determines which executives can engage and when. The pricing architecture should be designed for sustainable volume at the density the market actually produces, not theoretical maximum.
