# C207-05 — DigitalCoa.ch Geneva Cantonal Government AI Governance Advisory
**Phase:** C207 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
Canton de Genève smart city AI infrastructure governance as emerging advisory market — AI traffic management systems, AI public transport optimization, AI energy management for public buildings. Cantonal AI governance advisory for public sector AI deployment requiring transparency, accountability, and public oversight frameworks. Cantonal government AI advisory as distinct from corporate AI governance. Public sector AI governance advisory creates trust signal for corporate AI governance clients.

- Canton de Genève smart city AI infrastructure governance advisory under development
- AI traffic management systems governance
- AI public transport optimization governance
- AI energy management for public buildings governance
- Transparency, accountability, public oversight requirements
- Public sector AI governance advisory distinct from corporate AI governance
- Public sector advisory creates trust signal for corporate clients

## Advisory Implication
Geneva cantonal government AI governance advisory as distinct service vertical within DigitalCoa.ch service portfolio. Smart city AI infrastructure governance advisory requires public sector AI expertise (transparency obligations, public oversight requirements, governmental accountability frameworks) distinct from corporate AI governance. Cantonal government AI advisory creates trust signal for corporate AI governance clients who observe DigitalCoa.ch serving public sector with high governance standards. Public sector AI governance as market entry point for broader Geneva institutional AI governance advisory.

## Confidence
High — cantonal smart city AI infrastructure development, public sector AI governance demand
