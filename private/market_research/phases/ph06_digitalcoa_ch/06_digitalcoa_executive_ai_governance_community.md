# C206 Finding 06C — DigitalCoa.ch Executive AI Governance Community — Geneva Private Circle Format

**Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

A Geneva Executive AI Governance Community as a private circle engagement format — quarterly private dinner for 8-12 Geneva executives with AI governance mandate — creates trust-building environment that converts to advisory mandates while establishing DigitalCoa.ch as the center of Geneva AI governance executive intelligence.

## Key Details

- **Private circle format:** Quarterly private dinner for Geneva executives responsible for AI governance — corporate AI governance officers, compliance executives, board members with AI oversight mandate
- **Format design:** 8-12 executives per dinner; Chatham House rules environment; institutional intelligence sharing; DigitalCoa.ch Geneva AI governance updates; guest institutional speaker opportunity
- **Executive intelligence value:** Executives with AI governance mandate benefit from peer intelligence sharing environment — challenges, solutions, regulatory intelligence shared among peers
- **Trust-building mechanism:** Private community environment creates trust through peer interaction before commercial conversation — advisory mandates emerge organically from community participation
- **DigitalCoa.ch positioning:** Community positions DigitalCoa.ch as the center of Geneva AI governance executive network — network effect as competitive moat
- **Advisory pipeline mechanism:** Community participants as warm advisory leads — Geneva executive AI governance mandate identified through community participation

## Strategic Angle for DigitalCoa.ch

- Community curation: selecting Geneva executives for community participation — AI governance mandate as qualification criterion
- Community facilitation: structuring community format and institutional intelligence sharing environment
