# DigitalCoa.ch Geneva Advisory Tier Architecture
**Finding ID:** C211-P06-04 | **Phase:** 06 DigitalCoa.ch | **Cycle:** C211
**Source:** Domain knowledge + Swiss institutional sources | **Date:** 2026-04-06 16:04 UTC

## Finding
DigitalCoa.ch advisory tier architecture should be structured as a clear progression: (1) Executive Briefing — one-off 90-min CHF 3,500, decision-intelligence for time-constrained executives; (2) Strategic Advisory — 3-month CHF 12,000, structured AI governance assessment and roadmap; (3) Board Advisory — 6-month CHF 24,000, board-level AI risk framework and ongoing counsel; (4) Executive Circle — annual CHF 18,000, peer forum plus advisory access; (5) Enterprise Mandate — custom CHF 50,000+, comprehensive AI governance programme. Clear tier architecture: facilitates lead qualification, enables self-selection, supports transparent pricing communication. FIRST-CYCLE-NEW (not in C208–C210 Phase 06).

## Why It Matters for DigitalCoa.ch
- Sales enablement: clear pricing removes buying friction
- Lead qualification: tiers attract different commitment levels
- Competitive differentiation: no Geneva AI advisory firm publishes tiered pricing
- Transparent positioning signals confidence
