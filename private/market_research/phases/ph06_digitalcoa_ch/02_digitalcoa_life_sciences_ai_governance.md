# C207-02 — DigitalCoa.ch Geneva Life Sciences AI Governance Advisory
**Phase:** C207 Phase 06 (DigitalCoa.ch) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding

Geneva pharma AI governance advisory extends to clinical AI, AI R&D governance, AI regulatory compliance for AI-enabled medical devices. Life sciences AI governance as premium-tier advisory service with longer sales cycle but high engagement value.

## Advisory Implication
Geneva life sciences AI governance advisory as premium service offering for DigitalCoa.ch. Board-level AI governance advisory for pharma and medtech requires specialized regulatory knowledge (Swissmedic, EU MDR, FDA AI/ML-based software as medical device) combined with AI governance expertise. Life sciences AI governance advisory has longer sales cycle but higher engagement value per mandate. DigitalCoa.ch can develop specialized life sciences AI governance service line with dedicated sector positioning.

High — Geneva life sciences concentration, Swissmedic regulatory activity
