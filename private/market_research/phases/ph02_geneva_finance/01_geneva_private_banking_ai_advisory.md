# C205 Finding 01 — Geneva Private Banking AI Advisory Compliance

**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

FINMA issued updated wealth management AI advisory guidance in Q1 2026, requiring Geneva private banks to rebuild algorithmic suitability documentation and disclosure frameworks for AI-generated investment recommendations.

## Key Details

- **Guidance scope:** All Swiss FIs offering AI-assisted investment advisory — applies specifically to Geneva private banks with AI wealth management platforms
- **Suitability documentation:** AI-generated recommendations must document: data inputs, model logic category, human review sign-off, client risk profile matching
- **AI recommendation disclosure:** Clients must receive written disclosure when investment recommendation was AI-generated; opt-in consent for ongoing AI advisory services
- **Robot-advisor oversight:** Geneva banks using external AI advisory platforms (robo-advisor vendors) must conduct vendor AI model due diligence and document oversight mechanisms
- **Compliance deadline:** Q2 2026 — FINMA examination priorities include AI wealth management suitability documentation
- **Geneva impact:** Major Geneva private banks (domiciled in canton) with AI advisory platforms must complete compliance gap assessment before Q3 2026 examination cycle
