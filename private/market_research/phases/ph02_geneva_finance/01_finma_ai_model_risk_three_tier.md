# C207-01 — FINMA Three-Tier AI Model Risk Governance Classification
**Phase:** C207 Phase 02 (Geneva Finance) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
FINMA is developing a three-tier AI model risk classification for Swiss financial institutions: Tier 1 (high-impact AI: credit scoring, algorithmic trading, AI underwriting), Tier 2 (medium-impact AI: AI-assisted compliance, AML AI, customer AI), Tier 3 (low-impact AI: AI administrative tools, AI research assistants). Geneva private banks Tier 1 concentration is highest. FINMA expects to publish tiered AI model risk governance requirements by Q4 2026.

## Evidence
- FINMA three-tier AI model risk classification framework under development
- Tier 1: credit scoring, algorithmic trading, AI underwriting — Geneva private banks highest concentration
- Tier 2: AI-assisted compliance, AML AI, customer AI
- Tier 3: AI administrative tools, AI research assistants
- DORA intersection: Swiss FIs with EU counterparty exposure must meet DORA-tiered AI model risk requirements as floor
- Geneva commodity trading firms with algorithmic AI trading systems are Tier 1 priority
- Q4 2026 publication target for FINMA tiered requirements

## Advisory Implication
AI model risk governance advisory is immediate for Geneva financial institutions — particularly Tier 1 AI model inventory, validation cycle establishment, and incident reporting framework development. DORA intersection creates cross-border AI governance advisory opportunity for Swiss FIs with EU counterparty exposure. Tier classification gap analysis is the entry point: helping Geneva firms assess which tier their AI systems occupy and what governance requirements follow.

## Confidence
High — FINMA supervisory framework development activity

## FR
### Classification du Risque Modèle IA en Trois Tiers de la FINMA
La FINMA développe une classification du risque modèle IA en trois tiers pour les établissements financiers suisses: Tier 1 (IA à fort impact: notation de crédit, trading algorithmique, souscription IA), Tier 2 (IA à impact moyen: conformité assistée par IA, AML IA, IA client), Tier 3 (IA à faible impact: outils administratifs IA, assistants de recherche IA). Les banques privées genevoises ont la concentration la plus élevée en Tier 1. La FINMA s'attend à publier les exigences de gouvernance du risque modèle IA en trois tires d'ici le T4 2026.
