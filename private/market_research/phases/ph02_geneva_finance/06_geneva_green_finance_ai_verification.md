# C205 Finding 06 — Geneva Green Finance AI Verification Standards

**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

FOEN (Federal Office for the Environment) and FINMA launched a joint initiative in Q2 2026 establishing AI-verified green finance claims standards — with the first AI-verified Swiss green bond issued through a Geneva-based institution, marking operationalization of AI verification in Swiss sustainable finance.

## Key Details

- **AI verification framework:** FOEN-FINMA joint framework specifies AI system requirements for verifying green finance claims — Scope 1/2/3 emissions data verification, AI audit trail, third-party AI system certification
- **EU taxonomy alignment:** Swiss AI-verified green finance framework aligns with EU Taxonomy — enabling Swiss green bonds to access EU investor base
- **AI carbon credit verification:** AI systems verifying carbon credit claims for Swiss green bonds must meet FOEN-specified accuracy standards — first AI-verified carbon credits from Swiss forest projects Q2 2026
- **First AI-verified Swiss green bond:** Geneva-based institution issued first AI-verified Swiss green bond Q2 2026 — AI verification of underlying green asset portfolio
- **AI ESG data quality standards:** FOEN published AI ESG data quality standards — AI systems used for ESG scoring must document data sourcing, model methodology, update frequency
- **Geneva green finance hub development:** AI verification services attracting green finance activity to Geneva — 3+ Geneva financial institutions building AI verification capabilities
