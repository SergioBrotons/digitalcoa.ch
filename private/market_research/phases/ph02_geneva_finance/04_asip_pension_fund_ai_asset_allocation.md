# C205 Finding 04 — Swiss Pension Fund (ASIP) AI Asset Allocation Governance

**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The Swiss Pension Fund Association (ASIP) convened a 2026 working group on AI-driven asset allocation governance — establishing the first Swiss pension fund-specific framework for trustee oversight obligations when AI systems make or influence investment decisions.

## Key Details

- **Working group scope:** AI systems used for pension fund asset allocation, risk assessment, and investment selection — covers both internal AI systems and external AI asset management providers
- **Trustee oversight obligations:** Board-level trustees must understand AI systems' decision logic and limitations; ASIP guidance requires AI system documentation for board review
- **AI model risk governance:** Pension fund AI models must have: documented training data, model performance monitoring, algorithmic bias testing, human override mechanisms
- **ASIP guidance timeline:** Working group conclusions expected Q3 2026; guidance publication expected Q4 2026
- **Geneva pension funds:** Multiple Geneva-based pension funds (CPEG and others) already piloting AI-driven asset allocation systems — ahead of ASIP guidance
- **Investment manager AI due diligence:** Pension funds using external AI-driven investment managers must conduct AI model due diligence as part of investment manager oversight
