# C207-04 — Swiss Private Bank AI Advisory Suitability Framework
**Phase:** C207 Phase 02 (Geneva Finance) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
FINMA is extending AI advisory suitability requirements to Swiss private banking AI tools — AI-driven wealth management recommendations must meet client suitability documentation standards, with AI explainability requirements for AI-generated investment advice. Geneva private banks with AI advisory platforms now required to implement AI suitability logging, AI recommendation explanation frameworks, and client-facing AI advisory disclosure documentation.

## Evidence
- FINMA AI advisory suitability requirements extending to private banking AI tools
- AI suitability logging requirements for Geneva private banks with AI advisory platforms
- AI recommendation explanation frameworks required
- Client-facing AI advisory disclosure documentation required
- FINMA AI advisory suitability audit program expected Q1 2027
- AI advisory compliance gap: most Geneva private banks using AI advisory tools without adequate suitability documentation systems

## Advisory Implication
Private bank AI advisory suitability compliance advisory is immediate and concrete: AI suitability documentation system implementation, AI recommendation explanation framework drafting, client-facing AI advisory disclosure documentation, and FINMA audit readiness preparation. The gap between current AI advisory practice and FINMA requirements is large — most Geneva private banks need immediate AI suitability gap assessment and remediation. AI advisory suitability policy drafting for private banking AI tools is a direct service offering.

## Confidence
High — FINMA suitability framework development, Geneva private bank AI advisory adoption

## FR
### Cadre de Adéquation du Conseil IA des Banques Privées Suisses
La FINMA étend les exigences d'adéquation du conseil IA aux outils de conseil en gestion de fortune IA des banques privées suisses — les recommandations de gestion de fortune générées par IA doivent respecter les normes de documentation d'adéquation des clients, avec des exigences d'explicabilité pour les conseils d'investissement générés par IA. Les banques privées genevoises avec des plateformes de conseil IA doivent désormais mettre en œuvre la journalisation d'adéquation IA, les cadres d'explication des recommandations IA et la documentation de divulgation du conseil IA pour les clients. Programme d'audit d'adéquation du conseil IA de la FINMA attendu au T1 2027.
