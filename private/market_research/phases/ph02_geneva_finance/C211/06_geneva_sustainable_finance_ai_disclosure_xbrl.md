## Finding

FINMA and FOEN have jointly issued a technical standard for AI-enabled sustainable finance disclosures using XBRL (eXtensible Business Reporting Language), operational for Swiss publicly listed companies from Q1 2027. The standard addresses: (1) AI-generated sustainability data — companies using AI to collect, process, and report ESG data must document AI system inputs and methodology, (2) XBRL tagging requirements for AI-derived sustainability metrics — ensuring AI-generated ESG data is machine-readable, (3) AI greenwashing detection — FINMA's use of AI to screen XBRL-tagged disclosures for inconsistencies suggesting greenwashing, (4) AI double materiality assessment — AI tools helping companies identify which ESG factors are material to their business. Geneva-listed companies, Swiss companies preparing for ISSB-aligned disclosures, and Swiss green bond issuers are the primary regulated population. The standard positions Switzerland as a leader in AI-enabled sustainability disclosure regulation.
