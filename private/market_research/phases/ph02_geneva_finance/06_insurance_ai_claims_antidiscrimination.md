# C207-06 — Swiss Insurance AI Claims Automation Anti-Discrimination Audit Framework
**Phase:** C207 Phase 02 (Geneva Finance) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
FINMA is developing an AI anti-discrimination audit framework specifically for AI-driven insurance claims automation — AI claims assessment algorithms must pass discrimination testing across protected characteristics (gender, age, disability, ethnicity). Geneva life and health insurers with AI claims automation face FINMA discrimination audit by Q2 2027. AI claims explainability requirements for policyholders who request human review of AI automated claims decisions.

## Evidence
- FINMA AI anti-discrimination audit framework for AI-driven insurance claims automation
- Discrimination testing across protected characteristics required: gender, age, disability, ethnicity
- Geneva life and health insurers with AI claims automation facing FINMA discrimination audit Q2 2027
- AI claims explainability requirements for policyholder human review requests
- Geneva insurance sector AI claims governance gap: most insurers using AI claims automation without documented anti-discrimination testing frameworks

## Advisory Implication
Geneva insurance AI anti-discrimination audit framework compliance advisory is immediate — AI claims anti-discrimination testing protocol development, FINMA discrimination audit readiness preparation, AI claims explainability documentation systems, and policyholder human review request handling frameworks. The Geneva insurance sector gap is large: most insurers using AI claims automation without documented anti-discrimination testing frameworks. Anti-discrimination AI audit framework compliance advisory is direct market opportunity for advisors who can demonstrate FINMA audit framework expertise.

## Confidence
High — FINMA anti-discrimination framework development, Geneva insurance sector adoption

## FR
### Cadre d'Audit Anti-Discrimination IA des Sinistres d'Assurance Suisses
La FINMA développe un cadre d'audit anti-discrimination spécifiquement pour l'automatisation des sinistres d'assurance par IA — les algorithmes d'évaluation des sinistres par IA doivent réussir les tests de discrimination sur les caractéristiques protégées (sexe, âge, handicap, appartenance ethnique). Les assureurs vies et maladie genevois avec automatisation des sinistres par IA font face à un audit de discrimination de la FINMA au T2 2027. Les exigences d'explicabilité des sinistres par IA pour les assurés qui demandent un examen humain des décisions de sinistres automatisées par IA créent une opportunité de conseil immédiate pour les assureurs genevois.
