# C206 Finding 06 — Swiss Insurance AI Underwriting Regulatory Framework FINMA 2026

**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

FINMA is developing Switzerland's first dedicated guidance on AI underwriting in insurance — addressing Swiss Solvency Test AI model risk requirements, AI pricing fairness and discrimination prohibitions, and policyholder explainability rights for AI-underwritten decisions, with Geneva's insurance sector (life, health, commercial) preparing governance frameworks ahead of Q4 2026 guidance.

## Key Details

- **AI underwriting scope:** FINMA guidance will cover AI systems used in insurance underwriting — risk assessment, premium pricing, coverage decision, policy condition customization
- **Swiss Solvency Test (SST) AI overlay:** AI underwriting models subject to SST capital requirements — AI model risk capital charges, model validation documentation for SST purposes
- **AI pricing fairness:** FINMA prohibiting AI underwriting systems that produce discriminatory pricing outcomes — demographic proxy detection, fairness metric documentation requirements
- **Policyholder explainability:** Insured parties have right to explanation of AI-underwritten coverage decisions — AI decision explanation requirements, human review request rights
- **Geneva insurance concentration:** Geneva home to major insurers (AXA Switzerland, Generali Switzerland, Swiss Life Switzerland) and reinsurance (Swiss Re, Munich Re Geneva office) — Geneva as primary implementation hub
- **Timeline:** FINMA guidance expected Q4 2026; industry consultation ongoing; Geneva insurers beginning gap analysis and AI governance framework development
