**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

A Geneva-based DLT trading platform filed for Switzerland's first DLT trading facility license under FINMA's DLT-Ordinance in Q1 2026 — featuring an AI matching engine and smart contract custody framework, marking a significant step in tokenized securities infrastructure.

## Key Details

- **License type:** DLT trading facility (DLTTF) under Art. 178 FinfraG — distinct from traditional exchange license; allows tokenized securities trading and settlement
- **AI matching engine:** AI-driven price discovery and order matching for tokenized securities — operational continuity requirements, AI system failure protocols

- **FINMA review status:** Active review Q1-Q2 2026; expected decision Q3 2026
- **Geneva financial center significance:** First Geneva-based DLTTF application; establishes canton as DLT securities trading hub; competitive with Zurich crypto valley
- **DLT vs traditional exchange:** Lower capital requirements (CHF 500K min vs CHF 5M+); lighter ongoing obligations; tailored for SME tokenization

# C206 Finding 02B — FINMA AI Model Risk Governance Requirements 2026 (DORA Intersection)

**Phase:** 02 Geneva Finance | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

FINMA is developing standalone AI model risk governance requirements for Swiss financial institutions, with specific provisions addressing the DORA intersection for Swiss FIs with EU counterparty exposure — creating a two-track AI model risk framework for Geneva's financial sector.

## Key Details

- **AI model inventory:** FINMA requiring Swiss FIs to maintain comprehensive AI model inventories — model purpose, inputs, outputs, validation records, ownership
- **Model validation cycles:** Annual AI model re-validation requirements; continuous monitoring for model drift; AI incident reporting within 72 hours
- **DORA intersection:** Swiss FIs with EU counterparty exposure must demonstrate DORA-equivalent AI model governance; FINMA developing "DORA parity" assessment methodology
- **Affected institutions:** Geneva private banks (30+ institutions), Geneva commodity trading firms with financial market participation, DLT trading facilities, Geneva fintechs
- **AI model risk capital:** Basel IV IRB AI model documentation requirements cross-referenced; FINMA AI model risk overlay on capital calculations
- **Timeline:** FINMA guidance expected Q3 2026; transitional period 12 months; Geneva FIs beginning gap analysis now
