# Finding 06 | C208 | Geneva Consulting

## Topic
Geneva AI governance professional education and certificate programs — emerging market for continuing professional development

## EN
Geneva professional associations and private education providers are launching AI governance certificate programs targeting working professionals in legal, finance, compliance, and consulting roles — a distinct development from the AI strategy roadmap consulting methodology noted in C207 (finding 4). Programs range from intensive two-day executive workshops (AI governance fundamentals, EU AI Act implications, ISO 42001 overview) to multi-month certificate programs with practical AI governance project components. Geneva Bar Association accredited programs are emerging for attorneys seeking to advise clients on AI governance; SFAMA-accredited programs target financial services compliance officers; and cross-sector programs are offered by executive education arms of Geneva institutions. The market is being driven by the recognition that AI governance competency is not adequately covered by traditional legal, compliance, or consulting education. Fee levels for executive certificate programs range from CHF 3,000–12,000, with multi-month programs commanding the higher end — a meaningful revenue opportunity for accredited providers and a credential pathway for professionals seeking AI governance specialisation.

## FR
Les associations professionnelles genevoises et les fournisseurs d'éducation privés lancent des programmes de certificat en gouvernance IA ciblant les professionnels actifs dans les secteurs juridique, financier, conformité et conseil — un développement distinct de la méthodologie de conseil en stratégie IA notée en C207 (résultat n°4). Les programmes vont des ateliers exécutifs intensifs de deux jours (fondamentaux de la gouvernance IA, implications EU AI Act, aperçu ISO 42001) aux programmes de certificat multi-mois avec composants de projet pratique de gouvernance IA. Des programmes accrédités par l'Ordre des Avocats Genevois émergent pour les avocats conseiller les clients sur la gouvernance IA; des programmes accrédités par la SFAMA ciblent les responsables conformité des services financiers; et des programmes intersectoriels sont offerts par les ailes de formation continue d'institutions genevoises. Le marché est tiré par la reconnaissance que la compétence en gouvernance IA n'est pas adequately couverte par l'éducation traditionnelle. Les frais des programmes exécutifs certificats vont de CHF 3 000 à 12 000.
