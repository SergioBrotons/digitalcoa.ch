# Finding 01 | C208 | Geneva Consulting

## Topic
ISO 42001 AI management system certification uptake in Geneva — consulting implementation market

## EN
ISO/IEC 42001 (AI Management System standard) certification is gaining traction among Geneva-based multinationals and professional services firms as a competitive differentiator in AI governance — distinct from the AI readiness assessment frameworks in C207 (finding 1), which were about evaluating preparedness without certification as the endpoint. Geneva consulting firms are positioning ISO 42001 certification as a structured pathway to demonstrate AI governance maturity to regulators, counterparties, and institutional clients. The certification process involves a gap analysis against the 42001 clauses (including AI policy, risk identification, AI system impact assessment, and supply chain AI oversight), implementation of a management system, and third-party audit. The consulting opportunity is significant: firms that guide clients through full 42001 certification can offer multi-month engagements covering gap analysis, implementation support, documentation, and audit preparation. Early uptake is concentrated in financial services and life sciences — sectors with highest regulatory AI scrutiny — with professional services firms themselves also beginning certification processes.

## FR
La certification ISO/IEC 42001 (norme de système de gestion de l'IA) gagne du terrain auprès des multinationales et cabinets de services professionnels basés à Genève comme facteur de différenciation concurrentiel en gouvernance IA — distincte des cadres d'évaluation de préparation IA en C207 (résultat n°1), qui évaluaient la préparation sans faire de la certification le point final. Les cabinets de conseil genevois positionnent la certification ISO 42001 comme une voie structurée pour démontrer la maturité de gouvernance IA aux régulateurs, contreparties et clients institutionnels. Le processus de certification implique une analyse des écarts contre les clauses 42001, la mise en œuvre d'un système de management, et un audit tiers. L'opportunité de conseil est significative: les cabinets guidant les clients à travers la certification 42001 peuvent proposer des missions multi-mois. L'adoption précoce est concentrée dans les services financiers et les sciences de la vie.
