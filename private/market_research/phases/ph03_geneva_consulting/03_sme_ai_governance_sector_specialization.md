# C207-03 — Swiss SME AI Governance Advisory — Sub-Sector Specialization
**Phase:** C207 Phase 03 (Geneva Consulting) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
Geneva consulting firms are developing AI governance advisory service lines for specific SME sub-sectors — medical practices (FOPH AI health tool compliance), legal practices (SVA AI practice rules compliance), architectural firms (AI design tool IP and liability), accounting firms (AI audit tool professional standards). SME AI governance advisory is moving from generic frameworks toward sector-specific AI governance compliance programs.

## Evidence
- Sector-specific AI governance compliance programs emerging for Geneva SMEs
- Medical practices: FOPH AI health tool compliance advisory
- Legal practices: SVA AI practice rules compliance programs
- Architectural firms: AI design tool IP and liability advisory
- Accounting firms: AI audit tool professional standards compliance
- Geneva consulting firms with sector-specific AI governance expertise gaining competitive advantage
- Sub-sector AI governance specialization as differentiation strategy

## Advisory Implication
SME sector-specific AI governance advisory: developing sub-sector AI governance compliance programs for Geneva's key SME verticals. Sector-specific AI governance service development requires specialized regulatory knowledge (FOPH, SVA, professional body requirements) combined with AI governance expertise. Geneva consulting firms with sector-specific deep expertise will outcompete generalist firms on both credibility and retention. Sub-sector AI governance specialization as consulting firm market positioning strategy.

## Confidence
High — Geneva consulting firm sub-sector specialization activity

## FR
### Conseil en Gouvernance IA pour les PME Suisses — Spécialisation par Sous-Secteur
Les cabinets de conseil genevois développent des services de conseil en gouvernance IA pour des sous-secteurs spécifiques de PME — cabinets médicaux (conformité des outils de santé IA du FOPH), études d'avocats (conformité aux règles de pratique IA du SVA), bureaux d'architecture (propriété intellectuelle et responsabilité des outils de conception IA), cabinets comptables (normes professionnelles des outils d'audit IA). Le conseil en gouvernance IA pour les PME passe de cadres génériques à des programmes de conformité en gouvernance IA spécifiques à chaque secteur. Les cabinets de conseil genevois spécialisés dans un secteur précis gagnent un avantage concurrentiel sur les cabinets généralistes.
