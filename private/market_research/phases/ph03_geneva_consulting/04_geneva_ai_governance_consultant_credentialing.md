# C206 Finding — ISO/IEC 42001 Certification Audit Services Geneva AI Governance Market Entry

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

ISO/IEC 42001 AI management system certification is creating a distinct consulting sub-service in Geneva: pre-certification gap analysis and implementation advisory — with Swiss designated certification bodies (SQS, SwissTS) now operational and Geneva consulting firms building ISO 42001 certification practice service lines.

## Key Details

- **ISO 42001 certification market maturity:** SQS and SwissTS now offering ISO/IEC 42001 certification audits in Switzerland; certification bodies accepting applications from Geneva organizations
- **Pre-certification gap analysis:** Consulting firms offering structured gap analysis between current AI governance practices and ISO 42001 requirements — CHF 20K-80K for comprehensive gap analysis
- **Implementation roadmap advisory:** ISO 42001 implementation requires documented AI policies, risk assessment methodology, AI system inventory, internal audit program — consulting firms structuring implementation roadmaps
- **CAB market for conformity assessments:** Third-party conformity assessment bodies (CABs) required for high-risk AI system conformity assessments under Swiss AI Act; ISO 42001 certification as precondition for CAB designation
- **Geneva market dynamics:** Small boutique firms investing in ISO 42001 certification as competitive moat; larger firms using ISO 42001 credentialing as staff development framework
- **Consolidation pressure:** ISO 42001 certification costs creating barriers for small boutiques; market consolidation around certified firms expected
