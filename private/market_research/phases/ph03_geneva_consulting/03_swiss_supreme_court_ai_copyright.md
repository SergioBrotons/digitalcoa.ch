# 03 — Geneva SME AI Readiness: DIMAIN Phase 2 Learnings and Diagnostic Frameworks
**Phase:** C204 Phase 03 (Geneva Consulting) | **Cycle:** C204/06

## Finding
DIMAIN Phase 2 is generating concrete learnings about Geneva SME AI readiness that go beyond the 87-firm participation numbers. The program has revealed specific AI governance maturity patterns: Geneva SMEs cluster into three readiness tiers (AI-aware/not-started, AI-active/unstructured, AI-structured/governing), with most falling into the middle tier. This diagnostic framework is proving more useful than generic AI readiness assessments and is becoming the de facto assessment tool for Geneva SME AI governance.

## Evidence
- DIMAIN Phase 2 findings published Q1 2026: three-tier AI governance maturity model for SMEs
- Geneva SME AI readiness diagnostic: 40% AI-active but unstructured, 30% AI-aware not started, 20% AI-structured
- DIMAIN Phase 2 specific AI governance needs: AI vendor due diligence, AI data handling policies, AI incident response
- CCIG SME survey Q1 2026: 70% of Geneva SMEs using at least one AI tool without formal governance policy
- Geneva SME AI governance training demand: DIMAIN running workshops, external consultants being engaged
- AI governance maturity assessment becoming prerequisite for Geneva SME government contract eligibility

## Advisory Implication
SME AI readiness diagnostic as client acquisition tool — free assessment that surfaces AI governance needs and creates consulting engagement opportunity. DIMAIN methodology provides replicable framework for SME AI governance advisory.

## Confidence
High — DIMAIN Phase 2 findings, CCIG survey, government contract linkage
