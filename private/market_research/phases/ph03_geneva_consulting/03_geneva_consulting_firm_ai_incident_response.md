# C205 Finding 03 — Geneva Consulting Firm AI Risk Incident Response

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

First Geneva consulting firm AI client incident response cases emerged in Q1 2026 — AI system failures causing client financial loss with consulting firm liability exposure, establishing AI incident response methodology as a discrete consulting service line.

## Key Details

- **AI incident response cases:** Q1 2026 — first Geneva consulting firm client incidents involving AI advisory system failures; financial sector client losses; insurance claim triggers
- **Consulting firm liability exposure:** Geneva consulting firms face professional liability claims when AI systems recommended by the firm fail — AI recommendation responsibility clarification needed
- **AI incident response methodology:** New discrete consulting service emerging — AI incident root cause analysis, client notification protocols, regulatory reporting obligations, AI system remediation
- **Professional liability insurance:** Swiss professional liability insurers now requiring AI incident response protocols as policy condition — Geneva consulting firms must have documented AI incident response plans
- **Geneva consulting firm response:** Major Geneva consulting firms developing proprietary AI incident response methodologies; competitive differentiator emerging
- **Regulatory reporting:** FINMA and FDPIC both require AI incident notification under Swiss AI Act and nFADP provisions — AI incident response must address regulatory reporting timelines

- AI incident response methodology development: helping firms build documented, insurable AI incident response capabilities
