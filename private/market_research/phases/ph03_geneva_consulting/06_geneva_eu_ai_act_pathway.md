# C206 Finding 06A — Geneva AI Governance Advisory — EU AI Act Non-Member State Pathway

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

Switzerland's non-EU status creates a unique AI governance advisory opportunity in Geneva: Swiss companies with EU market access need EU AI Act compliance frameworks without being directly subject to the regulation — positioning Geneva as a trusted jurisdiction for AI governance frameworks bridging Swiss and EU requirements.

## Key Details

- **EU AI Act extraterritorial reach:** EU AI Act applies to AI systems placed in the EU market or used by EU entities — Swiss companies serving EU customers directly subject to EU AI Act requirements
- **Swiss non-member state advantage:** Switzerland not bound by EU AI Act but Swiss standards increasingly referenced as "equivalent" — creates advisory market for bridging Swiss AI governance and EU AI Act compliance
- **EU AI Act compliance tiers:** Prohibited AI systems (Article 5), high-risk AI systems (Annex III), general-purpose AI systems — Swiss exporters to EU market need classification analysis
- **Geneva trusted jurisdiction positioning:** Geneva's international reputation and proximity to EU create market positioning as "trusted third party" jurisdiction for AI governance frameworks satisfying both Swiss and EU requirements
- **Advisory service design:** AI governance gap analysis (Swiss vs EU AI Act), EU AI Act compliance roadmap for Swiss companies, EU AI Act technical documentation preparation
- **Market sizing:** Hundreds of Swiss companies in financial services, pharma, luxury goods, commodities with EU market access need EU AI Act compliance advisory

## Strategic Angle for DigitalCoa.ch

- EU AI Act classification advisory: helping Swiss companies determine EU AI Act risk category for their AI systems
- EU AI Act compliance roadmap: structuring Swiss company EU AI Act compliance programs
- EU AI Act technical documentation: advisory on required documentation for EU AI Act conformity assessment
- Swiss-EU AI governance bridge framework: positioning Geneva AI governance advisory as bridging Swiss and EU AI requirements
