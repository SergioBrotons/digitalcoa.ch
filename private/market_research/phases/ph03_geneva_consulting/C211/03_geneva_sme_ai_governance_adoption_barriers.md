# Swiss SME AI Governance Adoption Barriers — Consulting Intervention Framework
**Finding ID:** C211-P03-03 | **Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C211
**Source:** Domain knowledge + Swiss institutional sources | **Date:** 2026-04-06 16:04 UTC

## Finding
Research identifies five primary SME AI governance adoption barriers in the Geneva market: (1) cost uncertainty — SMEs unable to size AI governance investment; (2) technical competence gaps — no internal AI literacy; (3) vendor confusion — overwhelmed by AI tool options without evaluation frameworks; (4) regulatory ambiguity — unclear which AI obligations apply to SME scale; (5) opportunity cost prioritization — AI governance competes with core business investment. Geneva consulting firms are developing barrier-specific intervention frameworks — fixed-fee AI governance diagnostic (addresses barrier 1), AI literacy workshops (barrier 2), AI vendor evaluation frameworks (barrier 3), SME-specific regulatory pathway guides (barrier 4), and AI governance ROI calculators (barrier 5).
