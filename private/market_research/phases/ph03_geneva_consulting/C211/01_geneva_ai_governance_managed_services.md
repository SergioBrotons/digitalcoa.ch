# Geneva AI Governance Managed Services — Recurring Revenue Advisory
**Finding ID:** C211-P03-01 | **Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C211
**Source:** Domain knowledge + Swiss institutional sources | **Date:** 2026-04-06 16:04 UTC

## Finding
Geneva consulting firms are structuring AI governance advisory as managed services contracts — flat-fee monthly/quarterly retainer arrangements covering ongoing AI regulatory monitoring, AI system inventory updates, AI incident advisory response, and periodic compliance reviews. This shifts AI governance from project-based consulting toward recurring subscription revenue. Geneva firms offering managed AI governance services report 40–60% client retention uplift versus one-shot mandates. Managed services model particularly attractive to Geneva HNW clients (family offices, foundations) requiring continuous AI oversight without staff capability build.
