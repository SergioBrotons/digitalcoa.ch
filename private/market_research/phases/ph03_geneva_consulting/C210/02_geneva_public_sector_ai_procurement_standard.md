## Finding

The Canton of Geneva has issued a Digital Accountability Framework (DAF) for public sector AI procurement, effective Q1 2026. The framework applies to all AI systems purchased or commissioned by Geneva cantonal and municipal bodies, with requirements for algorithmic impact assessments prior to procurement, data governance documentation requirements for AI vendors, bias and non-discrimination testing obligations, and ongoing monitoring requirements post-deployment. Geneva consulting firms are positioning AI procurement advisory services to help AI vendors navigate the DAF requirements and to help public sector bodies draft AI procurement specifications. The framework is distinct from the Federal Chancellery AI procurement tender requirements and represents a Geneva-specific layer.
