# C205 Finding 01 — Swiss AI Act Conformity Assessment

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The Swiss AI Act's conformity assessment requirements for high-risk AI systems are creating a new consulting market in Geneva — with third-party conformity assessment bodies (CABs) emerging and consulting firms positioning as pre-assessment gap analysis providers before formal CAB audits.

## Key Details

- **Conformity assessment scope:** Swiss AI Act designates high-risk AI systems (healthcare, finance, critical infrastructure, education, law enforcement, public services) requiring mandatory third-party conformity assessment before deployment
- **CAB market emergence:** 3+ Swiss organizations pursuing CAB designation for AI conformity assessment — audit market developing for Geneva-based AI deployers
- **Pre-assessment gap analysis:** Consulting firms offering pre-CAB audit gap analysis — identifying Swiss AI Act compliance gaps before formal conformity assessment; CHF 20K-80K engagement range
- **High-risk AI sectors in Geneva:** Healthcare AI (Geneva university hospitals), financial AI (Geneva banks), AI in public services (Geneva cantonal administration AI systems)
- **Swiss AI Act timeline:** Draft bill progressing through Parliament; conformity assessment provisions expected in force 2027; Geneva organizations beginning preparation now
- **International recognition:** Swiss conformity assessment framework designed for EU AI Act mutual recognition — enables Swiss AI systems to access EU market via conformity assessment equivalence
