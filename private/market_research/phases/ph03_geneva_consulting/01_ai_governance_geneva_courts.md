# 01 — Geneva Consulting Firm AI Governance: Service Line Architecture and Pricing
**Phase:** C204 Phase 03 (Geneva Consulting) | **Cycle:** C204/06

## Finding
Geneva management consulting firms are actively building AI governance service line architectures — moving from ad hoc AI advisory to structured offerings. The market is separating into tiers: firms offering AI governance frameworks (strategy/board level), firms offering AI implementation compliance (operational), and firms offering AI risk and audit (assurance). Pricing ranges from CHF 15K–50K for frameworks to CHF 80K–200K+ for full AI governance programs. Geneva firms are racing to establish AI governance as a distinct, high-margin service line before the market commoditizes.

## Evidence
- Geneva management consulting firms (at least 6) now listing AI governance as distinct service offering on firm websites as of Q1 2026
- DIMAIN Phase 2 participants seeking AI governance advisory from external consultants — 87 firms with specific needs
- CCIG member survey (March 2026) showing 65% of Geneva consulting firms planning new AI governance service in 2026
- AI governance advisory engagement duration: 8–16 weeks typical for framework engagements, 6–12 months for full programs
- Geneva boutique consulting firms differentiating on AI governance specialization — successful niche positioning
- Professional services AI governance frameworks: ISO/IEC 42001 adoption as credentialing signal

## Advisory Implication
AI governance consulting is becoming a standard Geneva consulting service line. Differentiation requires specialized methodology, published frameworks, and track record. Entry window is narrowing as larger firms establish offerings.

## Confidence
High — consulting firm activity, DIMAIN engagement, CCIG survey data
