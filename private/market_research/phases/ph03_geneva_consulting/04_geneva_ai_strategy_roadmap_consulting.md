# C207-04 — Geneva Management Consulting AI Strategy and Roadmap Development
**Phase:** C207 Phase 03 (Geneva Consulting) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
Swiss and Geneva management consulting firms are developing AI strategy and roadmap development methodologies for executive clients — AI strategic opportunity assessment, AI adoption roadmap design, AI governance framework selection (ISO 42001, EU AI Act, national AI frameworks), AI investment prioritization frameworks. Geneva as center for AI strategy advisory for multinational corporate HQs headquartered in Switzerland. Management consulting AI strategy methodology as premium-tier advisory service distinct from operational AI implementation.

## Evidence
- AI strategic opportunity assessment methodologies under development
- AI adoption roadmap design frameworks
- AI governance framework selection: ISO 42001, EU AI Act, national AI frameworks
- AI investment prioritization frameworks
- Geneva as center for AI strategy advisory for multinational corporate HQs in Switzerland
- AI strategy advisory as premium-tier service distinct from operational implementation
- AI strategy advisory generating follow-on AI governance implementation mandates

## Advisory Implication
AI strategy and roadmap development as premium-tier consulting service for Geneva management consulting firms targeting C-suite and board-level clients. AI strategy methodology documentation, investment prioritization frameworks, and governance framework selection tools are key service components. Multinational corporate HQs in Geneva are priority targets for AI strategy advisory — these organizations have the budget and the governance complexity that justifies premium AI strategy fees. AI strategy advisory as client acquisition entry point for broader AI governance implementation mandates.

## Confidence
High — Geneva management consulting firm methodology development, multinational HQ concentration

## FR
### Conseil en Stratégie IA et Développement de Feuille de Route à Genève
Les cabinets de conseil en gestion genevois élaborent des méthodologies de conseil en stratégie IA et de développement de feuille de route pour les clients executives — évaluation des opportunités stratégiques IA, conception de feuille de route d'adoption IA, sélection du cadre de gouvernance IA (ISO 42001, loi européenne sur l'IA, cadres nationaux), cadres de priorisation des investissements IA. Genève comme centre de conseil en stratégie IA pour les sièges de multinationales basés en Suisse. Le conseil en stratégie IA comme service de niveau premium distinct de la mise en œuvre opérationnelle de l'IA.
