# C205 Finding 05 — Geneva AI Impact Assessment Methodology

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

SECO and the State Parliament (SIPO Commission) are developing an AI impact assessment framework for Geneva — establishing pre-deployment AI system impact assessment requirements distinct from nFADP data protection impact assessments.

## Key Details

- **AI impact assessment vs. DPIA:** AI impact assessment (AIA) covers broader societal/economic/human rights impacts of AI systems; distinct from nFADP data protection impact assessment which focuses on personal data processing
- **SECO/SIPO Commission framework:** Geneva cantonal framework for AI system pre-deployment impact assessment — covering fundamental rights, non-discrimination, safety, transparency, and accountability
- **AIA scope in Geneva:** Mandatory AI impact assessments for AI systems deployed by Geneva cantonal administration and Geneva organizations serving public interest functions
- **AI impact assessment components:** Required elements include: purpose specification, alternative analysis (non-AI options), bias and discrimination assessment, human oversight mechanism design, incident response planning, stakeholders consultation
- **EIA+nFAPT DPIA integration:** AI impact assessment framework designed to integrate with existing Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) requirements and nFADP DPIA — reducing duplicate assessment burden
- **Implementation timeline:** Framework draft expected Q3 2026; public consultation Q4 2026; expected implementation 2027
