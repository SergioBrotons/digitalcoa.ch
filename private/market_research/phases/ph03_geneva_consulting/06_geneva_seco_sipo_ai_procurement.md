# C206 Finding 06C — SECO/SIPO AI System Procurement Compliance Advisory

**Phase:** 03 Geneva Consulting | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

SECO and SIPO are developing AI system procurement requirements for Geneva-based government contractors and international organization vendors — creating an advisory window for AI procurement readiness assessments before formal requirements are published, with Geneva firms bidding on public contracts needing pre-compliance.

## Key Details

- **SECO/SIPO AI procurement project:** Federal Office for the Economy (SECO) and State Secretariat for International Finance (SIPO) developing AI system procurement requirements for Swiss government contractors — AI system documentation, algorithmic transparency, and ongoing monitoring requirements
- **International org vendor requirements:** UNOG, UNAIWG, and other Geneva international organizations developing AI vendor compliance requirements for contractors — December 2026 compliance deadline creating urgency
- **AI documentation requirements:** Government contractors and international org vendors must document AI systems used in contracted work — AI system purpose, data inputs, decision outputs, human oversight mechanisms
- **Algorithmic transparency obligations:** SECO/SIPO AI procurement requirements include algorithmic transparency — AI system explainability to procurement authority, AI audit right provisions in contracts
- **Geneva contractor concentration:** Geneva-based government contractors (consulting firms, IT providers, professional services) with SECO/SIPO contracts need AI procurement readiness assessments
- **Advisory window timing:** Requirements not yet formally published — gap analysis and readiness advisory services available NOW before formal requirements create compliance urgency

## Strategic Angle for DigitalCoa.ch

- AI procurement readiness gap analysis: helping Geneva firms assess current AI governance against forthcoming SECO/SIPO requirements
- SECO/SIPO AI procurement compliance roadmap: structuring compliance programs ahead of formal requirements publication
- International org AI vendor compliance advisory: helping Geneva firms prepare for UNAIWG/UNOG December 2026 AI vendor compliance deadlines
- AI procurement documentation framework: building AI system documentation packages for government and international org procurement submissions
