# C207-01 — Geneva AI Readiness Assessment Frameworks for SMEs
**Phase:** C207 Phase 03 (Geneva Consulting) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
Geneva consulting firms are developing standardized AI readiness assessment methodologies targeting SME clients — diagnostic tools assessing AI governance maturity, AI tool adoption readiness, AI professional liability exposure, and AI regulatory compliance gaps. CCIG piloting AI readiness assessment program with member firms. AI readiness assessment as client acquisition funnel for consulting firms offering full AI governance implementation programs.

## Evidence
- Geneva consulting firms developing standardized AI readiness assessment methodologies for SMEs
- AI governance maturity diagnostic tools
- AI professional liability exposure assessment
- AI regulatory compliance gap analysis
- CCIG piloting AI readiness assessment program with member firms
- AI readiness assessment as client acquisition funnel for AI governance implementation
- Geneva as hub for French-speaking SME AI governance readiness assessment methodologies

## Advisory Implication
AI readiness assessment advisory for consulting firms: standardized diagnostic tool development, AI readiness framework methodology documentation, CCIG pilot participation, and AI readiness-to-implementation client funnel design. SME AI readiness assessment as scalable entry-level service leading to full AI governance implementation mandates. Geneva consulting firms can position AI readiness assessment as the first step in a multi-stage AI governance advisory client journey — standardized, efficient, and replicable across the SME market.

## Confidence
High — CCIG pilot activity, Geneva consulting firm methodology development

## FR
### Cadres d'Évaluation de la Préparation IA pour les PME Genevoises
Les cabinets de conseil genevois élaborent des méthodologies standardisées d'évaluation de la préparation IA pour les clients PME — outils de diagnostic évaluant la maturité de gouvernance IA, la préparation à l'adoption d'outils IA, l'exposition à la responsabilité professionnelle IA et les lacunes de conformité réglementaire IA. La CCIG lance un programme pilote d'évaluation de la préparation IA avec ses membres. L'évaluation de la préparation IA sert de entonnoir d'acquisition de clients pour les cabinets proposant des programmes complets de mise en œuvre de gouvernance IA.
