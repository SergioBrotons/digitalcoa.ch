# C207-01 — WHO AI Health Governance Framework 2026
**Phase:** C207 Phase 04 (Geneva NGO/Tech) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
WHO Geneva is finalizing its AI health governance framework (Q2 2026 publication expected) — AI-assisted clinical decision support systems, AI diagnostics, AI health data governance, and AI health system procurement standards. WHO framework will set global standards for AI health deployment in under-resourced health systems. Geneva-based health institutions (HUG, private clinics, healthtech startups) are early adopters and contributors to WHO AI health governance development.

## Evidence
- WHO AI health governance framework finalization Q2 2026
- AI-assisted clinical decision support systems coverage
- AI diagnostics standards
- AI health data governance requirements
- AI health system procurement standards
- WHO framework sets global standards for under-resourced health systems
- Geneva health institutions early adopters and contributors
- WHO AI health governance framework compliance as market access requirement

## Advisory Implication
Geneva healthtech firms serving WHO member state markets need WHO AI health governance framework compliance. AI health governance advisory for Geneva health institutions: WHO framework compliance implementation, AI health product market access strategy, and AI health governance documentation for regulatory submissions. WHO AI health governance framework as market access requirement creates immediate demand for compliance advisory. Geneva health AI governance advisory as distinct vertical within broader AI governance advisory market.

## Confidence
High — WHO framework development activity, Q2 2026 publication timeline

## FR
### Cadre de Gouvernance IA de la Santé de l'OMS 2026
L'OMS Genève finalise son cadre de gouvernance IA pour la santé (publication attendue au T2 2026) — systèmes d'aide à la décision clinique assistés par IA, diagnostics IA, gouvernance des données de santé IA et normes d'approvisionnement des systèmes de santé IA. Le cadre de l'OMS établira des normes mondiales pour le déploiement de l'IA dans les systèmes de santé mal dotés en ressources. Les établissements de santé genevois (HUG, cliniques privées, startups healthtech) sont des utilisateurs précoces et des contributeurs au développement de la gouvernance IA de la santé de l'OMS.
