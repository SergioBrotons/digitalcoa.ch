# C206 Finding 06F — Geneva International Organization AI Training Hub — ITU/UNOG Capacity Building

**Phase:** 04 Geneva NGO/Tech | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The International Telecommunication Union (ITU) in Geneva is developing AI governance training programs for developing country governments — positioning Geneva as the international AI governance capacity building hub, with UNAIWG AI training coordination and Swiss development cooperation AI governance programs creating a compounding multi-stakeholder ecosystem.

## Key Details

- **ITU AI governance training programs:** ITU developing AI governance training for developing country government regulators — AI policy development, AI regulation design, AI compliance assessment
- **Geneva as capacity building hub:** Geneva hosts ITU, UNOG, UNAIWG, and multiple UN agencies — Geneva as natural hub for international organization AI training coordination
- **UNAIWG AI training coordination:** UNAIWG coordinating AI training programs across 14 Geneva-based UN agencies — avoiding duplication, harmonizing AI governance messages, sharing AI training resources
- **Swiss development cooperation AI programs:** SDC funding AI governance training for Swiss development cooperation partner countries — AI governance as component of Swiss development programs
- **African and Asian government AI regulators:** ITU and UNAIWG AI governance training targeting African and Asian government AI regulators — building AI governance capacity in developing countries
- **Swiss AI governance framework as training content:** ISO 42001, Swiss AI Act principles, nFADP, and Swiss AI governance standards being used as training content for developing country AI regulators — Swiss AI governance as international export
