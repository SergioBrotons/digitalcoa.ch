# 04 — Geneva Climate Fintech Article 6 AI Tool Deployment: First Implementations
**Phase:** C204 Phase 04 (Geneva NGO/Tech) | **Cycle:** C204/06

## Finding
Geneva's 17 Article 6 climate fintech companies are moving from tool development to deployment — with first Article 6 AI compliance tools live in Q1 2026. Article 6 of the Paris Agreement carbon market framework requires sophisticated AI systems for carbon credit tracking, MRV (measurement, reporting, verification), and cross-border carbon credit transfer智能化. Geneva companies are at the forefront of building these systems, and early deployment experience is revealing both capability and governance gaps.
