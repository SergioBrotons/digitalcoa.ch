# Finding 05 | C209 | Geneva NGO/Tech

## Topic
Geneva cantonal cross-agency AI procurement framework — standardising vendor AI governance requirements

## EN
Geneva's cantonal government has developed a cross-agency AI procurement framework that standardises AI governance requirements across all cantonal departments and publicly funded institutions — moving beyond the smart city AI infrastructure focus of C208 finding 5 to address procurement governance comprehensively across all Geneva cantonal AI deployments. The framework requires that all AI vendors supplying systems to Geneva cantonal agencies must meet minimum AI governance standards as a condition of public procurement: mandatory AI system documentation (model purpose, training data description, known limitations, human oversight mechanisms), AI system audit rights (Geneva cantonal government reserves the right to audit AI vendor's model development and deployment processes), bias and fairness testing requirements (AI systems used in public-facing services must be tested for demographic fairness before deployment and annually thereafter), and incident reporting obligations (AI vendors must report AI system failures that could affect public services within 24 hours to the cantonal digital transformation office). The framework is being coordinated with the Federal Chancellery's AI procurement guidelines and positions Geneva as the leading Swiss canton for standardised AI procurement governance. Geneva-based AI vendors supplying to cantonal clients are investing in compliance with the framework as a competitive advantage in the public procurement AI market.

## FR
Le gouvernement cantonal de Genève a développé un cadre d'approvisionnement IA inter-agences qui normalise les exigences de gouvernance IA à travers tous les départements cantonaux et les institutions financées publiquement — allant au-delà de l'infrastructure de ville intelligente du résultat C208 n°5 pour aborder la gouvernance de l'approvisionnement de manière globale. Le cadre exige que tous les fournisseurs IA fournissant des systèmes aux agences cantonales genevoises répondent à des normes minimales de gouvernance IA comme condition d'approvisionnement public: documentation obligatoire des systèmes IA, droits d'audit des systèmes IA, exigences de test des biais et de l'équité, et obligations de rapport des incidents. Le cadre est coordonné avec les directives d'approvisionnement IA de la Chancellerie fédérale et positionne Genève comme le canton suisse de premier plan pour la gouvernance normalisée de l'approvisionnement IA. Les fournisseurs IA genevois fournissant à des clients cantonaux investissent dans la conformité au cadre comme avantage concurrentiel sur le marché de l'approvisionnement IA public.
