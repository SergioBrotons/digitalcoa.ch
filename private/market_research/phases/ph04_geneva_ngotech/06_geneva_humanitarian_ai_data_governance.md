# C206 Finding 06C — Geneva Humanitarian AI Data Governance — UNHCR AI Refugee Management Systems

**Phase:** 04 Geneva NGO/Tech | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

UNHCR Geneva AI systems for refugee management are creating a humanitarian AI data governance framework with broader implications — AI-assisted refugee registration, needs assessment, and protection risk detection establishing Geneva as the hub for humanitarian AI data governance standard-setting, with ICRC/UNHCR/FOEN triangular coordination emerging.

## Key Details

- **UNHCR AI refugee management systems:** UNHCR deploying AI systems in refugee operations — AI-assisted registration (biometric, document verification), AI needs assessment (vulnerability scoring), AI protection risk detection
- **Humanitarian AI data governance:** UNHCR developing AI data governance framework for humanitarian contexts — data minimization, purpose limitation, AI system oversight in refugee operations
- **Geneva humanitarian AI governance hub:** Geneva hosts UNHCR, ICRC, and UNOG — Geneva as the global center for humanitarian AI governance standard-setting
- **ICRC/UNHCR/FOEN triangular coordination:** Three Geneva-based organizations developing coordinated AI governance approaches — ICRC field AI ethics, UNHCR operational AI, FOEN AI environmental monitoring creating cross-sector AI governance coordination
- **AI data protection in humanitarian contexts:** nFADP and GDPR intersection in UNHCR refugee data — AI systems processing sensitive personal data across borders; data protection as humanitarian concern
- **Humanitarian AI governance training:** ICRC AI ethics operationalization handbook (Q1 2026) now being adopted by UNHCR and other Geneva-based humanitarian organizations — Geneva Humanitarian AI Governance Community forming
