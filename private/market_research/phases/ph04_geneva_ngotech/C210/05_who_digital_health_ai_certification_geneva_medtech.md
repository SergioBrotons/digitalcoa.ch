## Finding

The World Health Organisation has launched a Digital Health AI Certification programme for AI systems used in healthcare delivery, with a Geneva-based secretariat managing European and EFTA country applications. The certification covers AI systems for clinical decision support, patient triage, drug interaction checking, and public health surveillance. Geneva medtech companies and healthtech startups are among the first applicants. The certification aligns with, but is distinct from, Swissmedic SaMD approval — WHO certification addresses global health quality standards while Swissmedic addresses Swiss market authorisation. Companies pursuing both certifications use WHO certification as a de facto pre-certification step, strengthening Swissmedic application packages. Geneva healthtech AI companies are also using WHO certification as a market access tool for low- and middle-income country procurement.
