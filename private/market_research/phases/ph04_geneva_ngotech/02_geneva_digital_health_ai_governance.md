# C205 Finding 02 — Geneva Digital Health AI Governance

**Phase:** 04 Geneva NGO/Tech | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) published a patient safety AI framework in 2026, mandating AI system registration, incident reporting, and governance requirements for AI-assisted clinical decision support systems in Geneva hospitals.

## Key Details

- **FOPH framework scope:** AI-assisted clinical decision support systems (CDSS) in Swiss hospitals — covers AI diagnostic assistance, treatment recommendation AI, patient monitoring AI, AI triage systems
- **Mandatory AI system registration:** Swiss hospitals must register AI systems with FOPH — including technical specifications, training data summary, clinical validation evidence, human oversight protocols
- **AI patient safety incident reporting:** AI-related patient safety incidents must be reported to FOPH under existing medical device incident reporting — AI-specific incident categories under development
- **Geneva hospital AI governance procurement requirements:** HUG (Hôpitaux Universitaires de Genève) implementing FOPH framework — AI vendor due diligence requirements, AI system documentation requirements for procurement
- **Clinical AI validation standards:** FOPH framework specifies clinical validation evidence requirements for hospital AI — real-world performance monitoring, bias testing, demographic subgroup performance documentation
- **Timeline:** Framework published Q1 2026; registration requirements effective Q3 2026; Geneva hospital compliance timeline aligned with national implementation
