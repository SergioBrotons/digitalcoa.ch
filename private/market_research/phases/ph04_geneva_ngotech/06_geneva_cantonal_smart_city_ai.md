# C206 Finding 06E — Geneva Cantonal Smart City AI Infrastructure Governance

**Phase:** 04 Geneva NGO/Tech | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The Canton de Genève is developing a governance framework for municipal AI systems — addressing AI traffic management, public transport optimization, and public building energy management, creating cantonal AI system transparency requirements that Geneva-based firms working with public sector AI need to anticipate.

## Key Details

- **Cantonal smart city AI initiatives:** Geneva implementing AI systems in municipal infrastructure — AI traffic light optimization, AI public transport scheduling, AI public building energy management
- **Municipal AI procurement governance:** Canton de Genève developing AI procurement requirements for public sector AI systems — AI vendor documentation requirements, algorithmic transparency obligations
- **Public sector AI transparency:** Geneva cantonal government applying AI transparency requirements to public sector AI — public disclosure of AI systems used in citizen-facing services
- **AI system registration:** Canton requiring registration of AI systems used in public infrastructure — AI system inventory for municipal AI deployed in Geneva
- **Public transport AI governance:** AI systems managing Geneva public transport (tpg) — AI scheduling optimization, AI passenger flow management, AI maintenance prediction
- **Geneva enterprise AI governance implications:** Geneva companies bidding on cantonal smart city AI projects need to anticipate cantonal AI procurement requirements before they are formally published
