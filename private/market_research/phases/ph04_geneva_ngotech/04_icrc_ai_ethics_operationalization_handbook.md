# C205 Finding 04 — ICRC AI Ethics Operationalization Handbook Q1 2026

**Phase:** 04 Geneva NGO/Tech | **Cycle:** C205 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

The ICRC published an AI ethics operationalization handbook in Q1 2026 — making abstract AI ethics principles actionable for humanitarian field operations, establishing Geneva as a hub for humanitarian AI governance training.

## Key Details

- **Handbook purpose:** Translating ICRC's AI ethics principles into field-operational guidance — AI system procurement checklist, AI deployment decision framework, AI incident response, AI stakeholder engagement protocols
- **Field AI deployment decision framework:** ICRC framework helps field teams decide when AI deployment is appropriate — human rights impact assessment, proportionality analysis, fallback procedures
- **Geneva humanitarian AI governance training hub:** ICRC using Geneva as hub for humanitarian AI governance training — workshops for other Geneva-based humanitarian organizations; UNHCR, WWF, CICR adopting framework
- **UNHCR adoption:** UNHCR studying ICRC handbook for potential adoption across field operations — Geneva-based UNHCR AI governance aligning with ICRC framework
- **WHO health AI guidelines:** ICRC handbook influencing WHO AI ethics guidelines for health AI in humanitarian settings — Geneva-based WHO AI health governance reference document
- **Broader NGO adoption:** Geneva-based international NGOs (CICR, UNHCR, OMS/WHO, WWF, CICV) forming Geneva Humanitarian AI Governance Community — sharing AI ethics operationalization experience
