# C207-01 — Brotons.net Institutional AI Intelligence Subscription
**Phase:** C207 Phase 05 (Brotons.net) | **Cycle:** C207/06

## Finding
Brotons.net institutional AI intelligence subscription tier for Geneva compliance officers, cantonal AI managers, and corporate AI governance leads — curated monthly AI governance intelligence briefings, Geneva regulatory event previews, AI governance framework compliance checklists. Subscription model creates recurring revenue while deepening institutional audience relationship. Institutional subscription as trust-building pipeline to DigitalCoa.ch advisory service.

## Evidence
- Institutional AI intelligence subscription tier under development
- Monthly AI governance intelligence briefings for Geneva institutions
- Geneva regulatory event previews
- AI governance framework compliance checklists
- Subscription as recurring revenue stream
- Institutional subscription as pipeline to DigitalCoa.ch advisory

## Advisory Implication
Brotons.net institutional subscription as business model innovation — subscription tier creates recurring revenue from Geneva institutional audience while building trust that converts to DigitalCoa.ch advisory mandates. Institutional subscription content must demonstrate consistent AI governance interpretive authority to justify premium subscription pricing. Subscription analytics (open rates, engagement depth, renewal rates) as quality signals for editorial investment decisions.

## Confidence
High — subscription tier development, Geneva institutional audience concentration

## FR
### Abonnement Intelligence IA Institutionnelle Brotons.net
Niveau d'abonnement à l'intelligence IA institutionnelle de Brotons.net pour les responsables de conformité genevois, les gestionnaires d'IA cantonaux et les responsables de gouvernance d'IA d'entreprise — briefings mensuels d'intelligence de gouvernance IA, aperçus d'événements réglementaires genevois, listes de contrôle de conformité des cadres de gouvernance IA. Le modèle d'abonnement crée des revenus récurrents tout en approfondissant la relation avec le public institutionnel genevois. L'abonnement institutionnel comme pipeline de construction de confiance vers le conseil DigitalCoa.ch.
