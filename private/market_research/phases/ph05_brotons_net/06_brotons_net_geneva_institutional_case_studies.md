**Phase:** 05 Brotons.net | **Cycle:** C206 | **Date:** 2026-04-06

## Finding

- **ICRC AI ethics operationalization case:** How ICRC took AI ethics principles from handbook to field implementation — practical AI ethics decision framework for humanitarian operations

- **Case study value for Brotons.net readers:** Geneva executives and AI governance practitioners benefit from concrete implementation examples — Brotons.net case studies provide practical interpretation that differentiates from academic or regulatory content
- **Geneva institutional access opportunity:** Brotons.net's established Geneva institutional relationships create access for case study research and publication
