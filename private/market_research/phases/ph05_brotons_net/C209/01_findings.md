# Finding 01 | C209 | Brotons.net

## Topic
Brotons.net SEO content gap analysis — Swiss institutional topic coverage versus competitors

## EN
Brotons.net is conducting a structured SEO content gap analysis comparing its topic coverage against Swiss institutional and international AI governance content competitors — distinct from the SEO keyword research noted in C208 skipped items. The analysis maps three layers: first, Swiss institutional source coverage (Federal Council AI reports, FDPIC guidance, FINMA consultations, Geneva cantonal government AI documents) — identifying which of these primary sources have not yet been covered or analysed on Brotons.net; second, competitor coverage mapping — identifying which AI governance topics Swiss institutional sites, Geneva consulting firm blogs, and international AI governance platforms are ranking for that Brotons.net has not addressed; third, audience query mapping — using known reader interests (from newsletter engagement patterns, reader question submissions, and event attendance) to identify topics where audience demand exceeds current content supply. The gap analysis is producing a prioritised content opportunity list for the editorial team, ranked by audience relevance, SEO opportunity, and Brotons.net competitive advantage (topics requiring genuine expertise rather than commoditised information). Early findings suggest Brotons.net is undercovering Geneva cantonal AI implementation content, FINMA AI enforcement case analysis, and Swiss-EU AI regulatory convergence — all high-relevance topics where Brotons.net's institutional knowledge base gives it a natural advantage over generic AI governance publications.

## FR
Brotons.net mène une analyse d'écart de contenu SEO structurée comparant sa couverture thématique avec les concurrents en contenu de gouvernance IA institutionnelle suisse et internationale — distincte de la recherche de mots-clés SEO notée dans les éléments sautés C208. L'analyse cartographie trois couches: premièrement, couverture des sources institutionnelles suisses; deuxièmement, cartographie de la couverture des concurrents; troisièmement, cartographie des requêtes d'audience. L'analyse d'écart produit une liste priorisée d'opportunités de contenu pour l'équipe éditoriale, classée par relevance pour l'audience, opportunité SEO et avantage concurrentiel Brotons.net. Les premières conclusions suggèrent que Brotons.net sous-couvre le contenu d'implémentation IA cantonal genevois, l'analyse des cas d'application FINMA, et la convergence réglementaire IA suisse-UE — tous des sujets à haute relevance où la base de connaissances institutionnelles de Brotons.net lui donne un avantage naturel sur les publications génériques de gouvernance IA.
