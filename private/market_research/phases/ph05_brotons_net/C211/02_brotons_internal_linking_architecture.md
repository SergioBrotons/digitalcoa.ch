## Finding
Brotons.net internal linking architecture can be systematically optimised around AI governance content clusters — hub-and-spoke model where pillar pages (broad AI governance topics) link to supporting articles (specific cantonal AI rules, AI tool categories, sector applications). Internal linking improvement increases page authority distribution, reduces bounce rate, and extends reader session duration. Systematic internal linking audit and optimisation recommended: map existing articles into 5–7 content clusters, identify orphaned articles with no internal links, establish hub page link equity distribution.
