## Lead Magnet Sections (Cycle 54 Framework)

### Section 1: Swissdamed Registration Audit — Are Your AI Medical Devices Registered?
- Do you use AI-powered medical devices (SaMD) — diagnostic AI, imaging AI, monitoring AI?
- Are they registered in Swissdamed before July 1, 2026?
- Are any devices vigilance cases requiring immediate registration (no transition period)?
- Are your AI medical device vendors registered as economic operators?

### Section 2: Swissmedic AI Guidelines — Are Your AI Medical Devices Compliant?
- Have your AI medical devices been classified according to Swissmedic guidelines?
- Do manufacturers bear conformity responsibility?
- Is your SaMD (Software as a Medical Device) properly registered?
- Are AI medical devices providing documentation for clinical use?

### Section 3: FADP — Patient Data in AI Systems
- Are AI tools processing patient data FADP-compliant?
- Is patient consent for AI processing documented?
- Are AI systems processing patient data properly secured?
- Is your AI vendor's FADP compliance verified?

### Section 4: Human-in-the-Loop — Physician Accountability
- Does your practice have a documented human oversight policy for AI-assisted decisions?
- Who is accountable when an AI-assisted diagnosis or recommendation is wrong?
- Is physician training on AI-assisted tools documented?
- Is professional liability coverage adequate for AI-assisted practice?

### Section 5: AI Tool Inventory — Shadow AI in Medical Practice
- What AI tools are used for administrative tasks (scheduling, billing, records)?
- What AI tools are used for clinical support (imaging, diagnostics, monitoring)?
- Are AI tool vendors providing FADP-compliant data processing agreements?
- Are AI tools configured for local/Swiss data storage (FADP + Swiss sovereignty)?

### Section 6: Geneva Medical AI Ecosystem
- Can your practice leverage Geneva's AI healthcare hub (HUG, CHUV Meditron)?
- Open-source local deployment options (Meditron) = Swiss-hosted patient data
- Geneva Initiative for Global AI Governance = international credibility
