# Competitive Analysis: Geneva Legal — C305 Phase 01

## What Exists, What's Missing

### Current Competitive Landscape

**What Exists:**
- FSA (bar association) guidance — principles-based, not operational
- Carrier attestation forms — architecture without methodology
- Generic AI governance frameworks — designed for enterprises, not boutique firms
- Legal tech vendors — selling tools, not evidence systems
- Compliance consultants — generic GDPR, not Geneva professional context

**What's Missing (for 2–5 Attorney Geneva Firms):**
- Geneva-specific carrier attestation preparation
- Article 321 CC vendor contract review methodology
- Retention protocol for AI-assisted matters
- Client intake AI response framework
- Junior associate supervision framework
- "Adequate" definition for small Geneva firms

---

## The White Space

1. **Carrier Attestation Evidence Production System** — Not a policy template. A documentation system that produces the specific records the carrier form architecture requires. Authorization records, supervision logs, review decisions.

2. **Article 321 CC Vendor Contract Review** — Systematic review of AI vendor contracts against professional secrecy obligations. What data goes where, who can access it, what the vendor's obligations are, what the exposure is.

3. **AI Retention Protocol for Legal Matters** — Defined retention periods for AI use records in legal matters. What to keep, how long, in what format. Geneva-specific guidance that doesn't exist anywhere else.

4. **Client Intake AI Response Framework** — Clear, confident response to client questions about AI use. Not a vague clause — a specific, client-protective framework that builds trust.

5. **Junior Associate Supervision System** — Documentation framework that enables supervising AI use you don't understand. Not requiring fluency — requiring accountability visibility.

6. **"Adequate" Definition for Small Geneva Firms** — First to define becomes the authority. A specific, operational definition of adequate AI governance for 2–5 attorney Geneva firms. The standard the carrier form assumes exists.

---

## The Gap

The gap is not information. The gap is evidence production. The market offers frameworks. What no one offers is the documentation system that produces the specific records carrier form architecture requires. The first to build that system for Geneva boutique firms becomes the authority.
