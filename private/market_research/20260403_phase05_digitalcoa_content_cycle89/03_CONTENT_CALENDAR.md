## Content Quality Standards

### For Each Article:
- [ ] Opening: Executive-ready in 2 sentences maximum
- [ ] Key data: 3-5 data points maximum; cite sources inline
- [ ] Implication: What this means for the executive reader — in 1 paragraph
- [ ] CTA: Natural next step without aggressive sales tone
- [ ] Metadata: Complete before publication (OG, canonical, schema)
- [ ] Internal links: Connect to related DigitalCoa content

### Avoid:
- More than 800 words per article (executive time is scarce)
- Multiple jargon terms without definition
- Hedging without adding value
- Generic conclusions that could apply to any country
