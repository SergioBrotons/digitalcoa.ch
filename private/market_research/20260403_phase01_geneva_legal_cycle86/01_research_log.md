### 4. Geneva Commercial Court Proposal — ODAGE + GILA Campaign (Sep 2025)
- **Current Bâtonnière:** Sandrine Giroud (ODAGE) + President, Geneva International Legal Association (GILA)
- **Ask:** Create dedicated Geneva commercial court as single cantonal instance (skip Court of Justice appeal)
- **Benefit:** Faster decisions → direct TF appeal path; specialized judges from relevant commercial sectors
- **Legal basis:** Art. 6 CPC (since 2011, allows cantons to establish commercial courts); revised CPC Jan 2025 adds English proceedings option
- **History:** Commercial court existed 1815–1892 in Geneva; revived proposal from late 1980s
- **Status (as of Apr 2026):** "Constructive discussions" with judiciary ongoing; not yet operational
- **Contrast:** Zurich moving toward "Zurich International Commercial Court" (ZICC) expected 2026
- NET-NEW: Detailed campaign specifics (Sandrine Giroud quotes, efficiency arguments, judge profile proposal) not in C85

### 5. CPC Revision (Jan 1, 2025) — International Commercial Courts Framework
- **What:** Revised Swiss CPC allows cantons to establish international commercial courts
- **English proceedings:** Art. 129(2) allows proceedings in English or other Swiss official languages upon party request
- **Requirements:** Commercial activity of at least one party + CHF 30,000 value threshold (or non-patrimonial) + registration in Swiss or foreign commercial register
- **Federal Supreme Court:** Art. 42(1bis) extends English submissions to state court appeals (not decisions)
- **NET-NEW vs C85:** Zurich ZICC timeline (expected 2026) and English-language details not previously surfaced
