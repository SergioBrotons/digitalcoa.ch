### 3. DIFC Digital Economy Court — NEW Institutional Development ✓

**Source:** difccourts.ae / dec.difccourts.ae  
**Finding:** DIFC Courts has established a dedicated **Digital Economy Court (DEC)** with specialized jurisdiction covering:
- Big data
- Blockchain
- **AI**
- Cloud services
- Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)
- 3D printing
- Robotics

**Five-Year Growth Strategy announced with 2025 service statistics.**

**Analysis (EN):**  
DIFC Courts is actively positioning itself as the preferred forum for digital economy disputes, including AI. This competes directly with Swiss arbitration for technology dispute resolution market share. Geneva-based AI companies operating internationally may face a choice between DIFC DEC and Swiss arbitration for tech disputes. DIFC DEC's willingness to handle AI-specific disputes is a strategic move. Broaders trend: international courts competing with arbitration for technology disputes.

**Analysis (FR):**  
Les DIFC Courts se positionnent comme forum préféré pour les différends de l'économie numérique, y compris l'IA. Concurrence directe avec l'arbitrage suisse. Les entreprises genevoises IA opérant à l'international peuvent choisir entre le DEC du DIFC et l'arbitrage suisse. Tendance large : les tribunaux internationaux concurrencent l'arbitrage pour les différends technologiques.

**DigitalCoa angle:** HIGH — Digital Economy Courts creating new jurisdictional options for AI disputes; implications for Swiss arbitration positioning and Geneva AI governance advisory.
