# Phase 03 — Geneva Consulting: Regulatory Framework
## Cycle 70 | 2026-04-02 | Geneva Consulting AI Governance

## Applicable Regulatory Framework

### 1. FADP — Consulting AI Impact
- DPIA required for consulting firms using AI systems processing personal data
- Client data, employee data, stakeholder data: all potentially in scope
- FDPIC: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing (September 24, 2025)
- Source: edoeb.admin.ch

### 2. EU AI Act — Consulting Firm GPAI Exposure
- GPAI obligations in force since August 2, 2025
- Consulting firms developing or deploying GPAI systems: in direct scope
- August 2, 2026: double deadline (HR AI conformity + legacy GPAI grandfathering expiry)
- Consulting firms advising clients: must have own GPAI conformity to credibly advise
- Source: Lenz & Staehelin analysis

### 3. Section 301 — Consulting Sector Exposure
- Consulting firms with U.S. clients: same Section 301 exposure as other Swiss firms
- Forced labour angle: supply chain ESG documentation for consulting firms with vendor networks
- U.S. multinational consulting engagements: tariff/regulatory complexity advisory opportunity
- Source: Dorsey law firm; admin.ch

### 4. SwissHoldings Framework (Major Swiss Consulting/Client Influence)
- Self-regulation + close collaboration model endorsed
- Digital bureaucracy paradox (Sulzer/Adecco): simplify processes before AI
- Legal Certainty event series: stable institutional frameworks → investment → demand for consulting
- Source: SwissHoldings.ch primary source (C69/C70)

### 5. Swiss Business Association Endorsement
- SwissHoldings endorses Federal Council sector-specific approach
- Swiss consulting sector: benefits from self-regulation preference
- AI governance: consulting firms positioned as guides to self-regulation compliance

## Regulatory Enforcement Posture
- FDPIC: guidance-only; no formal enforcement
- EU AI Act GPAI: self-assessment; conformity assessment bodies required
- Overall: consulting firms must self-assess; credible advisory requires own compliance
