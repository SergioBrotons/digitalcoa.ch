## Swiss SME / Consulting Sector
- SME share of Swiss economy: ~99%
- AI adoption in SME segment: lagging large enterprises
- FADP AI DPIA: likely requirement for consulting firms using AI client tools
- EU AI Act GPAI: consulting firms that develop or deploy GPAI systems are themselves in scope

## Consulting AI Governance Gap
- Consulting firms advise clients on AI governance but haven't fixed their own GPAI frameworks
- August 2, 2026: consulting firms need own GPAI conformity before advising clients credibly
- Section 301: consulting firms with U.S. exposure face same tariff/regulatory complexity
- Swiss consulting sector: AI is #1 Swiss fintech segment (IFZ FinTech 2026: 35%, up from 28%)

## Geneva Consulting Market Characteristics
- Bilingual EN/FR: standard in Geneva consulting
- International Geneva: cross-border consulting engagements common
- Client base: multinationals, family offices, international organizations, NGOs
- Regulatory density: FADP + EU AI Act + FINMA (for financial consulting) + Section 301

## Market Size Indicators
- Swiss consulting AI governance: nascent, <2% of consulting spend
- Addressable Swiss consulting AI governance: CHF 50-80M (5-year)
- Geneva consulting AI governance: underserved, no specialist positioning
