# 06 — Executive Summary: Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch (C117)

## Research Question
How should DigitalCoa.ch position itself to capture Geneva executive advisory demand while respecting brand separation from Brotons.net?

## C117 Key Findings

### Finding 1: "Executive Clarity" Positioning as Core Brand Promise (NET-NEW C117)
- DigitalCoa.ch serves Geneva executives facing: multi-jurisdictional regulatory pile-up, institutional ambiguity, peer-network decision dynamics, FR/EN operational complexity
- Brand promise: "You have the complexity. We provide the clarity." — precision over breadth
- Not general management consulting; not financial advisory; specifically "Geneva executive clarity"
- **Implication:** All site content, service pages, and outreach should reinforce Geneva-specific executive clarity positioning

### Finding 2: Brotons.net as Top-of-Funnel / DigitalCoa.ch as Conversion (NET-NEW C117)
- Brotons.net creates intellectual authority and Geneva intelligence credibility
- Conversion trigger: 3+ Brotons.net piece engagements over 6 months = warm prospect
- Newsletter engagement as CRM proxy (open rate + click patterns = lead quality signal)
- Brotons.net newsletter → DigitalCoa.ch introductory call (tracked via newsletter metrics)
- **Implication:** Brotons.net is the engine; DigitalCoa.ch is the destination; don't confuse the two

### Finding 3: Geneva Decision-Maker Journey — Peer Referral + Intellectual Credibility (NET-NEW C117)
- Geneva executives respond to intellectual credibility + peer trust; not cold outreach
- Hybrid funnel: Brotons.net reputation → peer confirmation → introductory call
- Website validates credibility; does not drive acquisition
- Site must be impeccable (no errors, clear positioning, named Geneva executive testimonials)
- **Implication:** Invest in named testimonial acquisition and site polish over SEO/ranking efforts

### Finding 4: "Chief Clarity Officer as a Service" — Retainer Engagement Model (NET-NEW C117)
- Monthly retainer (CHF 8,000-15,000/month) for ongoing executive clarity support
- Project-based Decision Diagnostic (CHF 5,000-25,000) for specific time-bounded decisions
- Both include Brotons.net reader welcome; retainer creates relationship depth
- Fractional CCO model: executive gets clarity support without full-time hire
- **Implication:** Retainer model preferred; create retainer onboarding package + Decision Diagnostic product sheet

### Finding 5: Geneva Family Office Intermediary as Multiplier Channel (NET-NEW C117)
- Geneva family offices (single and multi-family) are clients AND referral sources
- Family offices facing Geneva-specific regulatory complexity (CRS 2.0, sanctions, trust restructuring)
- Family office CIO engagement → trusted introduction to family principals
- Key networks: Turicum Group, Latitude Group, Altrua, Geneva Family Office Association
- **Implication:** Prioritize Geneva family office relationships; they generate both direct revenue and warm referrals

### Finding 6: Swiss EU Bilateral Tension — Urgency-Driven Advisory Demand (NET-NEW C117)
- InstA uncertainty creating strategic planning paralysis for Geneva executives
- "Bilateral Watch" briefing service — tracking InstA progress and contingency planning
- Time-sensitive positioning: executives need Geneva-specific bilateral relationship intelligence NOW
- Creates urgency-driven demand for DigitalCoa.ch advisory (contrasted with Brotons.net's long-horizon thought leadership)
- **Implication:** Launch Bilateral Watch as a specific DigitalCoa.ch product/engagement trigger

## Consolidated Strategic Implications

4. **Site priorities:** Named Geneva executive testimonials + flawless bilingual execution + clear service/product pages
5. **New product:** "Bilateral Watch" — Geneva executive briefing on InstA and bilateral contingency
6. **Channel:** Prioritize Geneva family office relationships as referral multiplier
