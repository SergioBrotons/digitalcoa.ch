### Section 2: Decision 1 — Do You Have EU AI Act GPAI Exposure?

GPAI applies if:
- EU-based clients
- AI outputs used in EU
- AI outputs affecting EU persons

If YES → Transparency obligations active NOW (disclose AI system + AI-generated content)

If NO → nFADP Art. 5(c) applies but not GPAI

---

### Section 3: Decision 2 — Are You Processing Personal Data via AI?

If AI processes personal data → nFADP Art. 5(c) obligations NOW:
- Transparency to clients
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimisation
- Inform clients when AI processes their data

---

### Section 4: Decision 3 — What Is Your AI Risk Classification?

**HIGH RISK (GPAI Annex III — August 2, 2026 obligations):**
- AI used in employment decisions
- AI used in credit/financial advisory
- AI used in education/vocational training
- AI used in healthcare advisory
- AI used in legal advisory

**STANDARD RISK:** Research AI, drafting AI, analysis AI, forecasting AI — GPAI transparency only (ACTIVE NOW)

---

### Section 5: The 3-Layer Compliance Stack

**Layer 1 — GPAI (EU AI Act, active Aug 2, 2025; high-risk Aug 2, 2026):**
- EU nexus → transparency + disclosure obligations NOW
- High-risk → conformity assessment August 2, 2026
- Fines: up to EUR 35M or 7% global turnover

**Layer 2 — nFADP (Swiss, active NOW):**
- Art. 5(c): AI transparency, purpose limitation, data minimisation
- Art. 23: right to know if interacting with AI
- FDPIC enforcement: 4 AI investigations 2025-2026 (none targeting consulting yet — timing signal)

**Layer 3 — Federal Council Framework (end-2026+):**

- Federal Council AI Action Plan First Draft May 2026
- Non-binding measures end-2026
