- **Core argument:** compliance cost analysis, scenario planning, and export strategy recalibration — consulting and advisory demand is immediate
- **CTA:** precision manufacturing compliance audit
- **Tone:** analytical, executive-focused

### Angle 3: "Geneva as RegTech Hub: The Compliance Tech Opportunity"

- **Tone:** Brotons voice — analytical, institutional, illuminating
