## Findings (Cycle 99 — Slot 5)

### EN
**Content Partnership Opportunities in Geneva**

Geneva's institutional density creates partnership opportunities for thought leadership distribution:

Potential partners:
- Geneva think tanks and research institutes: Graduate Institute (IHEID) research centers, CCIG (Chambre de commerce, d'industrie et des services de Genève), Geneva International Forum, ICTSD (International Centre for Trade and Sustainable Development)
- Professional associations: Geneva Bar Association, Swiss Fund and Asset Management Association (SFAMA), Swiss Business Federation (economiesuisse Geneva), Association Genevoise des Études Fiscales
- Geneva media: Le Temps (digital), AGEFI (financial), Genève.home (local), Le Courrier (political)
- IO/NGO knowledge platforms: UNOG Library, WTO Research Portal, WHO Digital Health Observatory, ICRC Humanitarian Knowledge Hub

Partnership types:
- Content cross-promotion: Brotons.net research featured in partner newsletters (mutual benefit)
- Co-authorship: Geneva institution experts contributing guest content to Brotons.net (authority boost + audience access)
- Event-linked content: Brotons.net coverage of Geneva conferences (Geneva Summit, WTO MC, etc.) with institutional promotion
- Data partnerships: Geneva institutional data (WTO trade stats, UN demographics) referenced in Brotons.net content → institutional citation/link back
- Newsletter exchanges: Geneva professional newsletters featuring Brotons.net content; Brotons.net featuring Geneva institutional announcements

Benefits:
- Audience development: access to established Geneva professional communities
- Authority: association with respected Geneva institutions
- Distribution: partner promotion amplifies reach beyond organic growth
- SEO: authoritative inbound links from Geneva institutions

Risks/considerations:
- Content independence: partnerships must not compromise editorial independence
- Time cost: relationship management requires resources
- Brand alignment: partner brands must align with Brotons.net positioning
