# Phase 02 ICP — Geneva Finance / Boutique Wealth Management
## Cycle 11 (2026-03-30)

## Addressable Profile
Boutique wealth management firms in Geneva, single-family offices, independent asset managers (IAMs) with 1-10 employees. Geneva-based. SRO membership (AOOS, OFS, AVS) or planning. Clients: UHNWI, HNW families, entrepreneurs. AI used internally for portfolio management, risk analysis, compliance monitoring, client reporting — but not as an external advisory service.

FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027 adds international scrutiny. No dedicated boutique WM AI governance competitor confirmed (9+ cycles).
