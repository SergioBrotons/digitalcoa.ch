## ICP Definition (DigitalCoa.ch)

Senior professional in Geneva — managing director, partner, or practice leader — personally accountable for AI governance decisions. Wants someone who understands the Geneva accountability ecosystem and can name the problem in their language before proposing a solution.

## ICP Language Across the Four Vertical Research Cycles (C296 Phases 01–04)

### Legal ICP (Phase 01 — Small Law Firm 2–5 Attorneys)

**How they talk about the problem:**
- "I signed something and I don't know what it means"
- "I got the carrier renewal and there are new AI questions"
- "My associate uses AI and I'm responsible for what comes out"
- "I have no idea what my AI tools are actually doing with client data"
- "The SAV guide told me what to do but not how to do it"
- "I don't have IT, I don't have compliance, I have myself"

**Language triggers that activate engagement:**
- Personal liability language ("your name is on the attestation, your personal exposure is on the line")
- Carrier renewal framing ("before your next renewal is when the attestation comes")
- Shadow AI recognition ("your associate's AI use is your AI exposure")
- Professional privilege protection language ("your AI tool may not be a privileged channel — here's why that matters")

**What they don't say but mean:** "I already made decisions about AI without knowing I was making governance decisions, and now I need someone to help me understand what I've already committed to."

### Finance ICP (Phase 02 — Boutique Wealth Management 50–200 Employees)

**How they talk about the problem:**
- "My client sent me a 40-page AI governance questionnaire and I don't know how to answer it"
- "I signed the carrier attestation and I'm still not sure what 'adequate AI governance' means for my firm"
- "I'm essentially the Chief AI Officer and I don't know what that means"
- "The SRO has never said anything about AI — but they will"
- "My portfolio construction AI may have made decisions I didn't authorize"
- "I have multiple carriers and each one asks different things"

**Language triggers that activate engagement:**
- Institutional client questionnaire framing ("the client who sent you the questionnaire is comparing your response against every other firm on their roster")
- Multiple carrier non-standardization recognition ("you're signed to different attestation instruments with different requirements and you don't know if they're consistent")
- Portfolio construction AI authorization language ("your AI is making continuous decisions and you cannot document the authorization chain")
- Cross-mandate coherence paradox ("you have four documentation systems and they're probably giving different pictures of the same reality")

**What they don't say but mean:** "The people who are actually evaluating my AI governance are running Comparative processes I don't know about, and my responses are being used in ways I never anticipated when I signed."

### Consulting ICP (Phase 03 — Boutique 2–50 Employees)

**How they talk about the problem:**
- "My client sent me their AI governance questionnaire and it blocked the engagement"
- "I'm accountable for sub-consultant work I can't evaluate"
- "I can't explain how I arrived at this recommendation — the machine generated it"
- "My engagement letter says I'm providing professional judgment on deliverables that are co-authored by AI"
- "My client is asking questions about AI methodology and I don't have answers"
- "I accepted AI tool terms without reading them and I think that created obligations I'm still accumulating"

**Language triggers that activate engagement:**
- Client DDQ blocking language ("the client questionnaire is preventing the engagement from moving forward — you need to respond before they move on")
- Sub-consultant chain language ("you're responsible for AI work two steps down the chain that you can't audit")
- Proposal indefensibility language ("you've made proposals with AI-assisted analysis and you can't explain the reasoning chain if the client asks")
- Personal liability converging language ("everything circles back to your name on the engagement letter and your personal exposure")

**What they don't say but mean:** "I built my methodology reputation on intellectual rigor, and AI created a hole in that reputation I can't yet see the edges of."

### Healthcare ICP (Phase 04 — Independent Practice 2–10 Physicians)

**How they talk about the problem:**
- "The carrier sent an AI attestation and I don't know what 'adequate AI governance' means for my practice"
- "My patient brought in AI-generated health analysis and I made a different decision — I don't know how to document that"
- "We've been using AI documentation tools for two years and I can't tell you what AI contributed to any specific record"
- "FMH says human oversight but doesn't say what that actually means for me on a Wednesday morning"
- "We switched AI vendors mid-patient-record and I don't know what that means for the existing records"
- "I have two other physicians in the practice and I don't know what AI tools they're each using"

**Language triggers that activate engagement:**
- Carrier attestation stampede language ("the carrier attestation is arriving in 2–4 weeks and you've been asked to document AI governance retroactively")
- Multi-physician diffusion language ("when you sign the attestation, you're certifying to AI surface you cannot enumerate because you don't control what the other physicians activated")
- Patient-override documentation void language ("the moment of AI input evaluation and physician override is the highest-accountability clinical decision moment and there is no documentation category for it")
- AI competency false adequacy language ("your training certificates do not map to any FMH-defined AI competency requirement — you have compliance-looking evidence that would not survive substantive challenge")

**What they don't say but mean:** "I'm trying to comply with a standard that was never defined for a practice of my size, and the documentation I'm building to show compliance is probably the evidence that proves I wasn't."

## Common Language Across All Four Verticals

**The unifying phrase:** "I'm accountable for something I don't fully understand."

**Phrase variants by vertical:**
- Legal: "I signed something I can't explain"
- Finance: "I'm the Chief AI Officer and I don't know what that means"
- Consulting: "I delivered something I can't defend"
- Healthcare: "I'm responsible for AI decisions I didn't make"

**Emotional register:** The ICP is not looking for education. They are looking for someone who has already understood their specific situation, can name it precisely, and can tell them what to do next. The engagement trigger is recognition: the moment the ICP reads or hears their specific structural situation described in their own terms.

**Urgency language that lands:**
- Retrospective framing ("what you've already committed to")
- Carrier concrete framing ("before your next renewal")
- Personal liability framing ("your personal exposure, not your firm's")
- Stampede recognition ("everyone's scrambling and creating evidence that works against them")

**Language that misses:**
- Abstract regulatory compliance framing
- Efficiency/productivity framing
- Technology feature descriptions
- General AI governance frameworks applied without Geneva calibration

## Language Implications for DigitalCoa.ch's Positioning

The ICP responds to:
1. **Naming precision in their vertical's specific geometry** — not generic AI governance language
2. **Retrospective activation** — "what you've already done" before "what you should do"
3. **Carrier/attestation concrete framing** — named renewal dates and specific form language
4. **Personal liability language** — "your name, your exposure, your attestation"
5. **Geneva-specific calibration** — terms and references that only someone participating in the Geneva ecosystem would use

The practice's competitive advantage is the vocabulary library built across four verticals — the ability to speak each ICP's language precisely, demonstrated through naming precision in positioning copy and lead magnets.
