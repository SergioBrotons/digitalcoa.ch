### 3. Geneva Construction Workers — Local Wage Exception Under Threat
**Source:** Le Temps, 01.04.2026
**NET-NEW vs C85:** Geneva construction disputes noted previously; this is new escalation.

- Geneva unions (Unia, SIT, Syna) called "déclaration de guerre" against employer association
- Cause: Employer association denounced Geneva-specific provisions giving local masons better conditions than rest of Switzerland
- Threat: "collapse of salaries without precedent" — CHF 400-500/month less
- History: Autumn 2025 — thousands of construction workers struck in multiple Swiss cities; two days in Geneva
- December 2025 compromise: renewed national construction convention; new workday/travel regulations; salary package; purchasing power mechanism
- New 2026: CHF 4/day site indemnity (rising to CHF 9 in 2028)
- Employer denounced Geneva exception, triggering union response
- Represents Geneva-specific labor market regulatory arbitrage

**Brotons angle:** Geneva labor market — regulatory arbitrage between Geneva and Swiss national norms
**DigitalCoa angle:** Geneva employer compliance — risk of labor cost increases from local exception defense
