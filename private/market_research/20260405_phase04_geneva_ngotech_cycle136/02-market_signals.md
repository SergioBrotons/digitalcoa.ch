### Signal 1: European Health Data Space (EHDS) — Swiss Alignment and Geneva Health Data Governance
The EU European Health Data Space (EHDS) regulation was adopted 2024 and enters implementation 2025-2026. Switzerland is not an EU member but has bilateral health data exchange agreements with the EU (Bilateral II negotiations ongoing). The EHDS creates: MyHealth@EU cross-border health data exchange infrastructure; health data secondary use framework for research/policy; European DHP registry interoperability. Geneva health institutions (HUG, private clinics, health tech companies) face EHDS alignment decisions — full participation, partial participation, or strategic wait-and-see. Federal Office of Public Health (FOPH) and Canton of Geneva are developing Swiss position on EHDS alignment. Advisory opportunity: EHDS alignment assessment for Geneva health institutions; Swiss-EU health data interoperability advisory; health data secondary use governance framework for Swiss research institutions.

**Source basis:** FOPH (bag.admin.ch); European Health Data Space regulation; Geneva health tech ecosystem; HUG/Hôpital cantonal; Swiss eHealth strategy

### Signal 2: Article 6 Carbon Markets — MRV Infrastructure Advisory for Geneva Commodity Traders
Article 6 of the Paris Agreement (corresponding adjustment/voluntary cooperation) reached operational framework status at COP29 (2024). Geneva commodity traders active in voluntary carbon markets (VCM) and compliance carbon markets (Ceres, Gold Standard, VCMI) face: corresponding adjustment requirements for cross-border carbon credits; MRV (Measurement, Reporting, Verification) infrastructure to satisfy EU Environmental Goods Regulation; Swiss ETS and EU ETS alignment questions for Swiss entities. Geneva commodity trading houses with carbon market operations need MRV advisory, corresponding adjustment compliance strategy, and EU alignment roadmap. Advisory opportunity: Article 6 MRV framework for Geneva commodity traders; corresponding adjustment compliance advisory; EU carbon border adjustment mechanism (CBAM) + Article 6 interaction advisory.

**Source basis:** UNFCCC Article 6 rules; EU ETS; Swiss ETS; Geneva commodity trading association; VCMI/IC-VCM; State Secretariat for Economic Affairs (SECO)

### Signal 4: Swiss Space Ordinance — Orbital Launch Liability and Commercial Space Governance
The revised Swiss Space Objects Ordinance (SR 748.941) implementing the Space Act (SR 748.94) entered into force progressively 2023-2026. Switzerland's space regulatory framework now covers: orbital launch authorisation, satellite operations licensing, launch liability insurance requirements, and debris mitigation obligations. Geneva hosts several commercial space companies and satellite operators (Planet Labs Geneva office historically; various satcom entities). The regulatory framework is administered by Swiss Federal Office of Civil Aviation (FOCA) with UNOOSA coordination. Advisory opportunity: Swiss space licensing compliance advisory for Geneva commercial space entities; launch liability insurance structuring; orbital debris mitigation compliance; international space law advisory (Outer Space Treaty, Liability Convention) for Geneva-based space businesses.

**Source basis:** FOCA space governance (bazl.admin.ch); Swiss Space Act (SR 748.94); UNOOSA; Geneva commercial space sector; ESA coordination
