# Phase 01 Geneva Legal — Regulatory Context — Cycle 40

## Active Regulatory Framework

### Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) — OPERATIVE SINCE SEP 1, 2023

**Art. 21 FADP** ⭐ PRIMARY: Right to human review
- Automated decisions producing legal effects must allow human review
- Directly relevant to: AI-assisted contract analysis, legal opinions, due diligence
- **Obligation: Implement human review capability for AI outputs used in legal decisions**

**Art. 5(c) FADP** ⭐: Transparency
- Controllers must disclose when AI is used in client interactions
- Must identify the logic involved in AI systems
- **Obligation: Disclose AI involvement in client-facing deliverables**

**Art. 16 FADP**: Data integrity and security
- AI systems processing personal data must maintain integrity
- Security measures appropriate to risk
- **Obligation: Data security for AI systems (especially client data)**

### DPIA — Data Protection Impact Assessment
- Required for high-risk AI processing under FADP
- Legal AI: contract analysis, due diligence, legal research = likely HIGH-RISK
- Must conduct DPIA BEFORE deploying high-risk AI
- **Obligation: Document DPIA for any AI tool used in legal work**

### Privacy by Design / Privacy by Default
- FADP requires privacy considerations built into AI systems from outset
- Applies to AI procurement decisions
- **Obligation: Assess privacy implications of AI tools before procurement**

## FDPIC Enforcement Pattern (Critical)

### Enforcement Absence — Legal Sector
- FDPIC 2024/2025: 1,053 total complaints; legal sector ABSENT
- FDPIC enforcement actions 2025-2026: transport x2, banking, retail x2, social media
- Zero enforcement actions against law firms
- **Enforcement gap = timing risk, not absence of obligation**

### Enforcement Architecture
- No direct administrative fines under nFADP (unlike GDPR)
- Criminal route only: intentional FADP violations
- Criminal liability: up to CHF 250,000
- FDPIC prefers guidance → voluntary compliance (until enforcement priority shifts)

### Enforcement Signal
- FDPIC Data Protection Day Jan 28, 2026 (guidance mode)
- FDPIC bodycam investigation Feb 26, 2026 (physical AI surveillance)
- FDPIC Feb 2026 joint statement on AI-generated images (60 DPAs)
- FDPIC pattern: guidance → then enforcement when sector identified as priority
- **Legal sector absent = next priority timing signal**
