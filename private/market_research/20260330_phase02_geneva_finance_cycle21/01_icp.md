## ICP: Boutique Wealth Managers (Geneva)

### Demographics
- **Firm type:** Boutique wealth management / family office (CHF 50M–500M AUM)
- **Size:** 2–15 employees; 1–3 partners
- **Location:** Geneva (primary), Vaud (secondary)
- **Client type:** UHNW/HNW families, entrepreneurs, retirees

### Pain Layer
- **CARF already operative:** Jan 1, 2026 (~3 months already live)
- **AMLA/LETA dual pressure:** ~3-6 months; consultation concluded Jan 30, 2026
- **FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027:** International compliance event elevating AMLA
- **3-layer compliance stack:** Swiss domestic + Swiss international + EU extraterritorial
- **Advisor scope expansion:** Lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs must join FINMA-recognized SRO
- **Swissdamed spillover:** ~13 weeks; healthcare deadline creating broader AI governance awareness

### Decision-Maker Profile
- **Managing partner / CIO** — accountable for compliance AND performance
- **Compliance overload** — CARF + AMLA + FATF simultaneously
- **AI adoption:** Using AI for portfolio analysis, client reporting, risk modeling without formal governance
- **No dedicated compliance officer** — partner-level responsibility

### Buying Psychology
- **Regulatory fear > efficiency desire** — FATF evaluation = existential threat
- **Wants compliance certainty** — checklist approach preferred
- **Time-poor** — wants "done for me" framework
- **Peer-influenced** — Geneva WM community closeness

### Window
- CARF: Already operative (Jan 1, 2026)
- AMLA/LETA: ~3-6 months (H1 2026)
- FATF: 2026-2027 evaluation
- Swissdamed: ~13 WEEKS
- Competitive: 10th+ absence confirmed
