## Lead Magnet Framework

### Title
**The Boutique Wealth Manager's AI Governance Readiness Checklist:**
*Nine Steps to Compliance Before CARF, AMLA, and FATF Arrive*

---

### Section 1 — CARF Is Already Live (You Are Already Obligated)

**Hook:**
The Common Reporting Standard (CRS 2.0 / CARF) became operative on January 1, 2026. You are not preparing for a future obligation — you are managing a present one.

---

### Section 2 — AMLA and LETA: ~3-6 Months (The Window Is Closing)

**Hook:**
Anti-Money Laundering Act revisions and the Large Transactions Act are in their final implementation phase. Geneva wealth managers have approximately 3-6 months before expanded obligations activate.

---

### Section 3 — FATF: The External Pressure You Cannot Control

**Hook:**
Switzerland's FATF mutual evaluation in 2026-2027 transforms AMLA compliance from a technical obligation into an international credibility event.

---

### Section 4 — The 3-Layer Compliance Stack

**Hook:**
Geneva boutique wealth managers face three simultaneous compliance layers: Swiss domestic, Swiss international, and EU extraterritorial.

---

### Section 5 — Your AI Tools and Client Data (Inventory)

**Hook:**
What AI tools does your firm use? What client data is in those systems?

---

### Section 6 — SRO Membership: Are You Covered?

**Hook:**
AMLA scope expansion now requires lawyers, notaries, trustees, and CSPs to join a FINMA-recognized SRO.

---

### Section 7 — FATF 30-Day Discrepancy Reporting

**Hook:**
LETA Transparency Register requires 30-day reporting of discrepancies. Do you know what triggers this obligation?

---

### Section 8 — Anti-Pattern: What Ungoverned AI Looks Like in WM

**Hook:**
Portfolio AI with no DPIA. Client reporting AI with no data processing agreement. Risk modeling AI with no oversight documentation.

---

### Section 9 — The Nine-Step Checklist

**Framework:**
1. [ ] CARF obligation assessment completed
2. [ ] AMLA/LETA gap analysis completed
3. [ ] AI tool inventory completed
4. [ ] nFADP Art. 5(c) DPIA for AI processing
5. [ ] SRO membership status confirmed
6. [ ] 30-day discrepancy reporting process documented
7. [ ] AI vendor Data Processing Agreements in place
8. [ ] FATF evaluation readiness assessment
9. [ ] Annual AI governance review scheduled
