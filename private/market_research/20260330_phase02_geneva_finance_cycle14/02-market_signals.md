### 2. CARF vs. CRS 2.0 Timing (NEW — Significant Refinement)
- **CARF legal framework**: live January 1, 2026
- **CARF cross-border exchange**: DELAYED until at least January 2027 (~9+ month delay)
- **CRS 2.0**: operative January 1, 2026 — no delay; digital assets, e-money, CBDCs, tokenized assets in scope
- Implication: Geneva WM must implement CARF/CRS 2.0 infrastructure now; actual exchange pressure deferred

### 3. AMLA/LETA H2 2026 — Concrete Obligations
- LETA Transparency Register: beneficial owners registered with Federal Office of Justice
- 30-day discrepancy reporting: KYC vs. Transparency Register
- Advisor scope expansion: real estate transactions, non-operational entity establishment, domicile services
- FINMA SRO obligations for newly-defined advisors
- Penalties up to CHF 500,000 for intentional violations

### 4. FINMA Guidance 08/2024 — Detailed Requirements
- AI inventory mandatory (all AI tools classified)
- Risk classification mandatory
- Outsourcing: additional tests, controls, contractual clauses for third-party AI
- Testing and monitoring: mandatory; independent reviews
- Employee expertise requirement
- EU AI Act Aug 2026 outlook: institutions should consider international standards

### 5. Geneva Wealth Management Context

- Bitcoin/cryptocurrency part of investment landscape (limited conviction)
- Swissdamed Jul 1 spillover: WM advising healthcare clients = client advisory obligation

- Every Geneva WM must have CARF/CRS 2.0 infrastructure operative January 1, 2026 (done)
