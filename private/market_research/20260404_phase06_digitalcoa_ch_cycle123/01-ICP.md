# 01 — ICP: DigitalCoa.ch Client Profiles

## Target Segment: Geneva Executives, C-Suite, and Senior Decision-Makers

### Client Profile
- **Type:** CEOs, CFOs, CROs, Managing Directors, Board Members, Executive Directors of Geneva institutions
- **Organization:** Banks, asset managers, multinationals, family offices, IO/NGOs, Geneva professional services firms
- **Budget authority:** CHF 50K-500K+ per engagement
- **Decision style:** Relationship-driven; trusted introduction preferred; direct outreach works with credible positioning

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Pain Points | Advisory Need |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| CEO | Strategic positioning, regulatory change, talent | Strategic advisory, trusted counsel |
| CFO | Financial strategy, reporting, Basel IV, FX | Financial risk advisory, regulatory |
| CRO | Credit risk, operational resilience, AML | Risk framework advisory |
| Managing Director | P&L, strategy, market positioning | Strategic advisory, execution support |
| Board Member | Governance, oversight, ESG | Board advisory, governance review |
| Executive Director (IO/NGO) | Strategy, funding, partnerships | Strategic advisory, positioning |

### Known Geneva ICP Types
- Geneva private banks (Pictet, Lombard Odier affiliates)
- Geneva commodity trading firms
- Geneva-based multinationals (consumer goods, industrials)
- Geneva IO/NGO executive directors
- Geneva family offices (UHNW)
- Geneva professional services firm partners

### Pain Points (C123 Update)
1. **Regulatory implementation burden** — Basel IV, DORA, ESG disclosure; need trusted advisory to navigate without Big 4 conflicts
2. **Strategic positioning under uncertainty** — Geopolitical risk, rate normalization, FX exposure; need independent strategic counsel
3. **Transformation program oversight** — Failed digital programs, M&A integration; need independent sounding board
4. **Board governance pressure** — ESG oversight, risk committee formation; need board advisory capability
5. **Talent and organizational challenges** — Specialized risk talent gap, governance restructuring; need practical advisory
6. **Referral network activation** — Satisfied clients not systematically referring; need structured referral program

### Buying Triggers
- Regulatory examination finding
- Transformation program distress
- Board governance change
- Peer referral from trusted Geneva professional
- Geneva institutional event (WEF, UN) creating strategic urgency
- SNB/FINMA announcement creating advisory need

### Buying Barriers
- Existing advisory relationships (Big 4 audit conflicts)
- Skepticism of "consultant" value
- Budget approval process
- Decision time (executive consensus required)
- Trust and credibility — must be earned

### Language / Cultural Fit
- French primary for Geneva executive; English for international
- Direct, no-nonsense; executive time is precious
- Credibility over marketing; track record essential
- Relationship-driven; introduction preferred
- Calm, confident, independent voice
