## Compliance Complexity (WM-Specific)

### The 3-Layer Compliance Stack
Wealth managers face a unique compliance complexity: three independent regulatory frameworks simultaneously:

1. **FINMA Layer**: FINMA Circ. 08/2024 governance expectations; fiduciary duty extends to AI use
2. **AMLA/FATF Layer**: CARF + CRS 2.0 live; 30-day discrepancy reporting; FATF evaluation pending
3. **FADP Layer**: Art. 5(c) active; purpose/functionality/data sources disclosure required

This 3-layer stack means WM AI governance is not just FADP — it's simultaneously FINMA + AML + FADP. This complexity is a barrier for small WM firms AND a differentiation opportunity for advisory.

### Shadow AI Exposure (WM Specific)
- Advisors using unapproved AI for portfolio analysis → FINMA governance breach
- AI tools processing client financial data without disclosure → FADP breach
- AI used in client communication without AI disclosure → FINMA + FADP breach
- AI used for due diligence without documentation → AMLA/CARF exposure
