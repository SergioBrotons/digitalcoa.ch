## Active Regulations

### CARF / CRS 2.0 (Operative January 1, 2026)
- ~3 months already live as of March 30, 2026
- Crypto Asset Reporting Framework + Common Reporting Standard 2.0
- Immediate automatic exchange obligations
- Geneva WM firms with crypto holdings or cross-border clients already obligated

### AMLA / LETA (H2 2026 — ~3-6 months)
- Consultation machinery active since January 30, 2026
- 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register
- SRO membership obligations for: lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs with triggering activities
- Advisor scope expansion: real estate transactions, establishing non-operational entities, domicile services
- **FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027** adds international scrutiny layer

### FATF Mutual Evaluation (2026-2027)
- Switzerland's AMLA overhaul directly driven by FATF evaluation
- FATF assessors will examine: SRO coverage, beneficial ownership verification, AMLA compliance documentation
- Geneva WM firms with advisor-scope operations face dual pressure: domestic AMLA/LETA + FATF evaluator scrutiny
- Elevates AMLA from domestic regulatory update to international compliance event

### Swissdamed (July 1, 2026 — ~12 weeks)
- Healthcare deadline creates Geneva regulatory cluster awareness
- Spillover effect: WM professionals more attuned to regulatory calendar proximity

### EU AI Act (GPAI operative Aug 2, 2025 — ~8 months)
