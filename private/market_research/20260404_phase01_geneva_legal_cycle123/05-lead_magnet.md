# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Legal Sector

## EN Version

### Primary Concept: "Geneva Legal Regulatory Radar — H2 2026"

**Format:** Monthly or bi-monthly briefing note (2-3 pages)
**Distribution:** LinkedIn (targeted to Geneva GCs, Managing Partners), Geneva Bar Association channels
**Content focus:**
- Top 3 regulatory developments affecting Geneva legal market
- Practical advisory implications for law firms and in-house teams
- Timeline/action items for each development
- One "what to watch" emerging issue

**H2 2026 edition topics (C123 cycle):**
1. Swiss litigation funding regulation — what firms need to know now
2. BGer AI authorship ruling — preparation checklist for IP practices
3. Sanctions-GDPR collision — interim protocol for financial intermediaries

**CTA:** "Need a framework for [topic]? DigitalCoa.ch offers advisory design workshops."

### Secondary Concept: "Geneva Arbitrage — Quarterly Geneva Arbitration & Disputes Review"

**Format:** Quarterly market intelligence report
