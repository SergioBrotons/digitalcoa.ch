## Key Regulations
- **FADP** (Sep 1 2023) — directly applicable to AI (FDPIC May 2025 confirmation)
  - Art. 5(c): data processing notifications
  - Art. 21: right to human review for automated decisions producing legal effects
  - Art. 5: transparency obligations for AI systems
  - DPIA required for high-risk AI (contract analysis, due diligence = likely high-risk)
  - Privacy by Design mandatory for AI procurement
- **EU AI Act** (Aug 2 2026) — extraterritorial for Swiss firms with EU nexus
- **CoE AI Convention** (signed March 2025) — Federal Council end-2026 incorporation
- **GPAI** (EU) — Aug 2 2025 — already operative for EU-nexus firms

## FADP Compliance Stack (5-Layer)
1. Art. 5(c) — data processing transparency
2. Art. 21 — right to human review (automated decisions)
3. Art. 5 — transparency for AI-assisted work product
4. DPIA — required for high-risk AI processing
5. Privacy by Design — AI procurement assessment
