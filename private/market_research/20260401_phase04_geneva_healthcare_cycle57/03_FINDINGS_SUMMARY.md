2. **Healthcare labor shortage dual-purpose**: Swiss structural labor shortage (400,000 workers) affects healthcare severely. AI in medical practices serves dual purpose: regulatory compliance AND workforce substitution. Swissdamed governance framework is also a workforce strategy framework.

4. **Swissdamed 3 months remaining**: Enforcement confidence increasing with new leadership. The window for "we're still preparing" is functionally closed.
