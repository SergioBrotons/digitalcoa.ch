## Phase 05 — Key Cross-Vertical Findings (C294)

### 1. Market Is Accountability Infrastructure
The addressable need is not more AI tooling. It is accountability infrastructure: defensible AI decision-making + attestation-ready documentation + personal liability protection. The market gap is structural and unnamed.

### 2. First-Mover Window Is Open
No Geneva boutique advisor has claimed the SMB AI accountability infrastructure position in any vertical. Window open; 12–18 month estimate before clearer competitive signals.

### 3. Documentation Window Is Irreversible — Present-Tense
Firms building during the open window are building defensible position. Firms deferring are accumulating window-period decisions without documentation. Retroactive documentation cannot reconstruct what decisions were made or why. This is the urgency framing.

### 4. nFADP Art.63 Personal Liability Is Universal Urgency Trigger
The CHF 250,000 criminal fine applies to individuals who sign engagement letters, vendor agreements, and carrier attestations. General liability insurance does not cover it. Personal assets exposed. Under-communicated across all four verticals.

### 5. Attestation Pre-Signing Review Is Universal Service Prototype
Legal (SAV), Finance (carrier), Consulting (insurance broker), Healthcare (carrier) — same service architecture: map what the attestation commits you to before you commit to it. No competitor is offering this in Geneva.

### 6. Lead Magnet Portfolio Is Coherent but Incomplete
Phase 01 (Legal) draft complete. Phases 02–04 need drafting. Portfolio architecture validated. Cross-sell triggers identified. Primary distribution channel is Geneva informal peer circuit (not cold outreach).
