# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva NGO/Tech

## Key Regulatory Developments — Geneva NGO/Tech (C125)

### Regulatory Item 01: UN/IO AI Governance Frameworks
**Authority:** ITU, UN AI Advisory Board, WSIS
**Status:** Geneva-based bodies actively developing AI governance standards, procurement guidelines, and policy frameworks
**Key Frameworks:**
- ITU: AI for GoodSummit, technical standards for AI systems
- UN AI Advisory Board: global AI governance recommendations
- WSIS: digital development frameworks with AI components
**Geneva Impact:** Member state delegations, IO institutions, Geneva tech firms with IO exposure
**Advisory Implications:** AI policy framework development, IO procurement advisory, member state AI governance consulting
