# 01 — ICP: Geneva NGO/Tech Market

## Target Segment: Geneva International Organizations & Humanitarian Actors

### Organization Profile
- **Type:** UN agencies, international organizations, diplomatic missions, humanitarian NGOs
- **Locations:** Geneva — host to 40+ international organizations including UNOG, WHO, ITU, WIPO, WTO, ICRC, UNHCR
- **Operations:** Global programs; significant procurement; technology adoption increasing
- **Status:** Geneva is the world's #1 hub for international organizations and humanitarian operations

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Director of Operations / CIO | Senior executive | Technology modernization, procurement efficiency |
| Procurement Director | Senior procurement professional | Vendor management, contract compliance, Geneva vendor preferences |
| Program Director | Humanitarian/development professional | Technology solutions for field operations, data management |
| Country/Regional Director | Field operations leader | Remote connectivity, security, digital program management |

### Key Geneva Organizations
- UNOG (UN Office at Geneva)
- WHO (World Health Organization)
- ITU (International Telecommunication Union)
- WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization)
- WTO (World Trade Organization)
- ICRC (International Committee of the Red Cross)
- UNHCR (UN High Commissioner for Refugees)
- UNCTAD, ILO, and 30+ other UN agencies

### Pain Points (C125 Update)
1. **ICRC digital supply chain modernization** — Major CHF 50-80M logistics technology investment; humanitarian tech contracting opportunities for Geneva firms
2. **UNHCR biometric expansion** — 15-country biometric identity management expansion; Geneva tech firms competing for contracts
3. **UN/IO AI governance** — Geneva-based ITU/WSIS/UN AI Advisory Board setting global AI governance standards; advisory demand from member states and IO institutions
4. **Humanitarian tech contracting** — ICRC and UNHCR technology procurement increasing; Geneva-based tech firms and consultants positioned for contracts
5. **Digital operations security** — Cybersecurity and data protection for sensitive humanitarian operations
6. **Remote connectivity for field operations** — Technology for humanitarian field operations in low-connectivity environments
