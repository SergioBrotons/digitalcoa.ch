### Finding 2: EDÖB DSG/AI — Active Framework (CONFIRMATION)
**Source:** C85 Phase 06 (confirmed unchanged)  
**Date:** September 2025 (guidance active)

**Three obligations:**
1. **Transparency** — Chatbot disclosure, training data right to object
2. **Human Review** — High-risk AI requires human oversight
3. **DSFA (Data Protection Impact Assessment)** — Required for high-risk AI

**Prohibited:**
- Social scoring
- AI surveillance

**Swiss nDSG directly applicable to AI:**
- No separate AI law needed
- Existing data protection framework covers AI governance
- **Implication:** Organizations already subject to AI governance obligations; FINMA guidance will add sector-specific layer

**FINGING:** EDÖB framework provides current compliance baseline; FINMA guidance will layer on financial sector specifics.
