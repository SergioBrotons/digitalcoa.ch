# Market Signals: Geneva Healthcare — C299 Phase 04

## Current Problems, Complaints, and Failures

### Primary Market Signals

**1. Carrier Attestation for Medical Practices**
Medical liability insurers are beginning to ask about AI tool usage at renewal. Small practices have no framework for answering these questions. The attestation asks for evidence of "adequate AI governance" — undefined for solo and small group practices.

**2. Patient-Generated AI Content**
Patients arriving with consumer AI-generated health analyses. The physician must evaluate and document AI input they did not request. No protocol exists for this documentation category.

**3. FMH Human Oversight undefined**
FMH states human oversight requirement without operational definition. Small practices cannot determine what documented oversight looks like.

**4. Swiss Hosting ≠ Compliance**
Swiss data residency confused with Swiss data protection compliance. Many practices using AI tools with US-based API processing regardless of Swiss storage label.

**5. Vendor Terms Acceptance**
AI tool terms of service accepted without review. "By continuing to use" is a legal acceptance. No practice has a vendor term monitoring process.

**6. No proportionate framework exists**
No Geneva-specific, small-practice-calibrated AI governance framework exists. Existing frameworks designed for hospitals or large group practices.

---

## Failure Modes

**Attestation failure:** Practice signs carrier attestation without documentation to support it. If a claim occurs, the attestation becomes evidence of inadequate governance.

**Documentation failure:** AI-assisted decision documented only as the final clinical decision. The AI's contribution is invisible in the record.

**Vendor failure:** Practice uses AI tool that retains training data or uses inputs for model improvement. Patient data potentially exposed without practice knowledge.

**Oversight failure:** Physician accepts AI recommendation without documented evaluation. If the recommendation was wrong, no evidence exists that human oversight occurred.
