# ICP Language: Geneva Healthcare — C299 Phase 04

## How Independent Practices Talk About AI Problems

### The Geneva Healthcare ICP

Independent physician practices, 2–10 professionals, Geneva-based. Senior physician personally accountable for every clinical and administrative decision. License is irreplaceable. AI tools entering clinical and administrative workflows without formal evaluation.

---

## ICP Language Patterns

### The Liability Frame
- "If something goes wrong and I used AI, am I still covered?"
- "The carrier asked about AI and I didn't know what to say"
- "I'm responsible for everything that happens in this practice — including things I didn't directly do"
- "My license is on the line. I can't afford to get this wrong."

### The FMH Oversight Problem
- "FMH says human oversight — but what does that actually mean in practice?"
- "I reviewed the AI output and made a decision. Is that oversight?"
- "If the AI is better than me at this, is it still oversight if I just agree with it?"
- "The requirement says human oversight. It doesn't say what that looks like."

### The Patient AI Content Problem
- "A patient came in with an AI analysis last week. I had to pretend I understood what it was."
- "Patients are bringing in AI-generated health information. How do I document that I evaluated it?"
- "If I disagree with the AI, what do I write in the record?"

### The Vendor Trust Problem
- "I signed up for this tool and started using it. I don't actually know what it does with the data."
- "Swiss hosting — that should be fine, right?"
- "The terms of service were 40 pages. I didn't read them."
- "By continuing to use the service, you agree to..."

### The Documentation Problem
- "I have no idea what I should be documenting."
- "Everything I read says I need AI governance. Nothing tells me what that looks like for a 3-person practice."
- "I don't have a compliance department. I don't even have an administrator most days."

### The Personal Liability Fear
- "If a patient sues me and I used AI — does my insurance cover that?"
- "Is the fine personal? Could they come after my house?"
- "I signed something for the carrier. I'm not sure what I committed to."

## French ICP Language

### Cadre de responsabilité
- "Si quelque chose tourne mal et que j'ai utilisé l'IA, suis-je encore couvert ?"
- "L'assureur a posé des questions sur l'IA et je ne savais pas quoi répondre"
- "Ma licence est en jeu. Je ne peux pas me permettre de me tromper."

### Le problème de surveillance FMH
- "La FMH dit surveillance humaine — mais qu'est-ce que cela signifie en pratique ?"
- "J'ai examiné le résultat de l'IA et pris une décision. Est-ce une surveillance ?"
- "La requirement dit surveillance humaine. Rien ne dit à quoi cela ressemble."

### Le contenu IA du patient
- "Un patient est venu avec une analyse IA la semaine dernière. J'ai dû faire semblant de comprendre."
- "Les patients apportent des informations de santé générées par l'IA. Comment documenter que je l'ai évaluée ?"

### Le problème de confiance des fournisseurs
- "J'ai signé pour cet outil et commencé à l'utiliser. Je ne sais pas vraiment ce qu'il fait des données."
- "L'hébergement suisse — c'est bien, n'est-ce pas ?"

### La peur de la responsabilité personnelle
- "Si un patient me poursuit et que j'ai utilisé l'IA — mon assurance couvre-t-elle cela ?"
- "L'amende est-elle personnelle ?"
