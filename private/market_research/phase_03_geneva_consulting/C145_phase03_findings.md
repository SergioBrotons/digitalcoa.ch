## Findings

1. **Geneva AI Consulting Market — 80% AI Adoption But No Boutique Positioning** — Swiss AI adoption 80% in 2025 (from 37% in 2024); no Geneva boutique positioned as AI strategy advisor for consulting firms. Dual opportunity: (1) AI advisory services for consulting firms' clients; (2) AI adoption strategy for consulting firms themselves. AI-native boutiques (Harvey, EvenUp) disrupting mid-market. Advisory: AI strategy roadmap for Geneva consulting firms.

2. **UN/IO Procurement Advisory — Getting Geneva Boutiques on the Approved Vendor List** — Geneva hosts UNOG, WHO, WTO, ITU, UNCTAD, WEF, 170+ diplomatic missions. UN/IO procurement rules block small boutiques from competitive bids. Advisory: vendor registration support, procurement framework compliance, IO consulting bid strategy. Uniquely Geneva niche Zurich/Basel cannot replicate.

3. **Energy Transition Advisory — Geneva Commodity Trading's Sustainability Strategy Gap** — Geneva commodity trading houses (Trafigura, Vitol, Mercuria, Gunvor) face EU Taxonomy alignment, net-zero transition planning, ESG disclosure. Geneva consulting firms with commodity sector knowledge have natural advantage but most lack dedicated energy transition practices. Advisory: net-zero strategy, EU Taxonomy alignment, ESG reporting framework implementation.

4. **Healthcare Regulatory Strategy — Geneva Biotech/Healthtech Cluster Consulting Gap** — Geneva healthcare cluster (HUG, Swissmedic, biotech startups) creates demand for regulatory strategy (Swissmedic/EU MDR), market access consulting, digital health compliance. Geneva consulting firms with healthcare regulatory expertise limited. Advisory: Swissmedic regulatory strategy for healthtech, market access for medtech entering Swiss market, digital health compliance.

5. **Trade Association Management Consulting — Geneva's IO Concentration Creates Niche** — Geneva hosts ICHA, FIA, WEF, WHO, WTO, major trade associations. Trade association management consulting: governance structure advisory, stakeholder management, regulatory monitoring, member engagement, event strategy. Advisory: IO governance frameworks, regulatory monitoring services, member engagement strategy.

6. **Consultant vs. Contractor Misclassification — Geneva Freelance/Platform Economy Advisory** — Federal Council revised employment law bill Q1 2026 targeting platform economy misclassification. BGer rulings 2025-2026 on platform worker status create retroactive risk. Geneva consulting firms using freelance consultants face misclassification liability. Advisory: contractor classification audits, freelancer contract frameworks, employment law compliance.
