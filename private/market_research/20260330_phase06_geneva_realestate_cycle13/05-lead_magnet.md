**Step 1: Inventory AI tools already in use**

**Step 2: Map data flows**

**Step 3: Assess nFADP Art. 5(c) obligations**
- Transparency: clients must know when AI processes their data
- Purpose limitation: AI only for stated purposes

**Step 4: Document lawful basis for AI processing**
