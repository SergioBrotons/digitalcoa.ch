**nFADP — ACTIVE:**
- Art. 5(c): AI processing of client/tenant data = active obligations (since Sept 2023)
- Tenant screening AI → discrimination risk
- Property valuation AI → misrepresentation exposure
- Client communication AI → confidentiality obligations
- DPIA likely required for high-risk AI processing

**EU AI Act GPAI — ACTIVE SINCE AUG 2, 2025:**
- ~8.5 months already operative
- Transparency obligations for EU-nexus agencies
- Applies if agency has EU clients or EU property transactions

**GAIGI — OPERATIVE ~7-10 MONTHS (Fall 2025):**
- Geneva Real Estate Industry Association AI governance framework
- Institutional members: NOT small agency focused
- Category awareness rising: window compressing
