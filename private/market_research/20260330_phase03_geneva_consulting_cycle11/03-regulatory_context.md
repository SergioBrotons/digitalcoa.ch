## Active Regulations

### EU AI Act GPAI (Operative since Aug 2, 2025)
- ~8.5 months already live
- Transparency obligations active
- Copyright documentation obligations active
- Incident documentation obligations active
- EU market nexus: applies to Swiss firms with EU clients, EU output, or EU importer/distributor role
- High-risk obligations: August 2, 2026 (~16 months)

### nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection — operative Sep 2023)
- Art. 5(c): active — AI processing of client data requires lawful basis
- Art. 22: DPIA requirement for high-risk processing

### Federal Council AI Action Plan
- Zero Draft: January 2026 (confirmed)
- First Draft: May 2026 (~2 months)
- Federal Council "lean approach" explicitly confirmed — no cross-sector Swiss AI law before end-2026
- Sector-specific approach only

### CoE AI Convention
- End-2026: consultation draft expected via FDJP
- Will crystallize AI governance standards for Swiss professional services

### Swissdamed (Jul 1, 2026 — ~12 weeks)
- Healthcare regulatory deadline creates Geneva cluster awareness
- Spillover effect on consulting firms with healthcare clients
