### Finding 02 | LETA Beneficial Owner Register — Operational Compliance Crunch
**Sector:** AML / Corporate Law / Geneva Regulatory
**Angle:** Switzerland's Federal Act on the Transparency of Legal Entities (LETA) creates a centralized federal beneficial owner register, operational from 2026. All Swiss legal entities must disclose ultimate beneficial owners (UBOs) meeting ≥25% threshold or control criteria. Non-compliance: civil liability, administrative sanctions, potential ComCo implications for shell company counterparties. Geneva-based trustees, holding structures, family offices, and commodity traders with opaque ownership face acute pressure. Advisory opportunity: UBO gap analysis, register filing, ongoing monitoring.
