### 1. SVIT FADP Branchenempfehlungen — Comprehensive Real Estate Data Protection Toolkit (No AI Component)
**Source:** SVIT Schweiz Datenschutzgesetz page
- SVIT published comprehensive FADP sector recommendations for real estate:
  - Branchenempfehlung Grundlagen (Version 1.2)
  - Branchenempfehlung Vorgehensempfehlung (Version 1.1)
  - Muster-Datenschutzerklärung (Version 1.1)
  - Richtlinie Datenschutz in der Immobilienwirtschaft (Version 1.0)
  - Richtlinie Auskunftsbegehren (Version 1.0)
  - Personendaten im Bewerbungsformular für Mietobjekte (Version 1.2)
  - Verwaltung von Stockwerkeigentümergemeinschaften (Version 1.0)
- **Key gap:** None of these documents address AI governance specifically — they are FADP data protection only
- **Implication:** Geneva real estate agents/brokers have FADP data protection guidance but zero AI governance guidance from their professional association

### 2. SVITpedia AI Chatbot — SVIT Testing AI in Member Services
**Source:** SVIT Schweiz homepage
- SVIT launched an AI chatbot ("SVITpedia") for member real estate questions
- **FADP Art. 21 implication:** SVITpedia processes member data → FADP applies; if it uses AI for automated responses, Art. 21 applies to member interactions
- **AI governance opportunity:** SVIT members (Geneva real estate agents) are indirectly exposed to AI governance through their association's own tools

### 3. Swiss Real Estate Forum 2026: June 11-12, 2026 — Right Before G7
**Source:** SVIT Schweiz
- SREF 2026: June 11-12, 2026 at BERNEXPO
- G7 is June 15-17, 2026 in Switzerland — Swiss-EU package potentially ratified/advancing
- **Implication:** AI governance likely to be discussed at SREF 2026 given June timing; Geneva real estate sector AI awareness may receive a boost

### 4. Real Estate Anti-Money Laundering Framework — Foundation for Forced Labor ESG Due Diligence
**Source:** SVIT Schweiz Geldwäschereigesetz page
- SVIT has dedicated AML guidance for real estate sector
- Real estate is a high-risk sector for money laundering → established due diligence framework
- **New product angle:** Real estate agents with AML due diligence experience can extend this framework to forced labor ESG documentation for property transactions involving traceable supply chains

### 5. Geneva Real Estate AI Governance Gap — Confirmed Most Acute After Healthcare
**Source:** Cross-phase synthesis
- FADP Branchenempfehlungen = data protection guidance only (no AI)
- No Swiss real estate-specific AI guidance found
- SVIT has AI chatbot but no AI governance guidance for members
- Swissdamed does NOT apply to real estate
- **Implication:** Geneva real estate agents/brokers face FADP obligations + EU AI Act GPAI obligations (for EU-facing activities) with no sector-specific guidance
