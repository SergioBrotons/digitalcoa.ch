# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Legal Decision-Makers

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "Geneva Legal Operations Maturity Assessment"
**Format:** Interactive PDF / Web-based assessment (10-15 questions)
**Target:** GC or Legal Operations Manager
**Value:** Benchmark their function against industry standards; reveals gaps in outside counsel management, e-billing, matter management
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, email, firm size, primary challenge
**Conversion path:** Assessment results → consultation offer → advisory engagement

### Option B: "The Geneva ELM Benchmark Report 2025"
**Format:** 20-page research report (EN + FR)
**Target:** C-suite legal leaders (GC, Managing Partner)
**Value:** Data-backed view of legal tech adoption, cost benchmarks, regulatory readiness
**Lead capture:** Company profile, role, email
**Conversion path:** Executive summary → full report → consultation

### Option C: "Legal Operations Playbook for Geneva Financial Services"
**Format:** Structured guide (30 pages)
**Target:** In-house legal at banks, asset managers, insurance
**Value:** Practical framework combining regulatory compliance + operational efficiency
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, role, AUM / firm size
**Conversion path:** Chapter 1 → full guide → discovery call

### Option D: "Regulatory Intelligence Briefing — FINMA 2026 Edition"
**Format:** Monthly or quarterly email briefing (5-minute read)
**Target:** GCs at regulated entities (banks, insurance, asset managers)
**Value:** Curated FINMA 2026 regulatory changes with legal operations implications (Guidance 01/2026, Circular 2026/1, AMLA revision, LETA, operational resilience)
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, regulatory focus areas
**Conversion path:** Briefing subscription → nurture sequence → advisory

## Lead Magnet Recommendation (Updated C97)

**Primary:** Option D (Regulatory Intelligence Briefing — FINMA 2026 Edition) — elevated priority due to exceptional 2026 regulatory cascade (6 major changes in H1 2026 alone). GCs are actively seeking to understand: crypto custody guidance, nature risk circular, AMLA advisor category, LETA beneficial ownership, BFSA cross-border, operational resilience full enforcement. This is a once-in-a-decade regulatory volume moment.

**Secondary:** Option C (Legal Operations Playbook) — remains highest strategic fit for long-form lead nurturing

**Rationale for elevating Option D:** The density of new FINMA regulatory output in early 2026 (Guidance 01/2026, Circular 2026/1, BFSA, AMLA revision, LETA, operational resilience) creates immediate, urgent demand for interpretation among Geneva GCs. A "FINMA 2026 Regulatory Radar" briefing positions DigitalCoa as the essential interpretive voice before GCs have time to develop their own frameworks.
