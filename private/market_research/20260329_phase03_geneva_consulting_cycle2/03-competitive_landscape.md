## Competitive Set Overview

Three broad categories competing for small Geneva consulting firms' attention:

1. **AI tool vendors** — platforms offering AI solutions
2. **Direct competitors** — other consulting advisors serving the same ICP
3. **Infrastructure providers** — platforms enabling better firm delivery

---

## Category 1: AI Tool Vendors

| Vendor/Platform | Offering | Threat to ICP | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| ChatGPT / Claude | General AI | Medium | Individual usage; no firm strategy; DPA concerns |
| Microsoft Copilot | Enterprise AI | Medium | Larger firms; expensive for small |
| Gemini for Workspace | AI in productivity suite | Medium | Google ecosystem; growing |
| Notion AI | Knowledge management | Low-Medium | Popular with consultants |
| Intercom / Zendesk AI | Client service AI | Low | More for client-facing service firms |

**Key insight:** Tool vendors create adoption complexity. They also raise the data governance question DigitalCoa.ch answers. Complementary, not competitive — at most, tool vendors may add an advisory layer (low-medium threat).

## Competitive Summary (Cycle 2)

### Who is winning?
- AI **tool vendors** are winning at individual consultant level (personal productivity)
- **No clear winner** has emerged for firm-level AI strategy and governance for small boutiques in Geneva

### The Gap (Confirmed in Phase 05)
There is no established Swiss/Geneva boutique advisory firm explicitly positioning around AI governance for small consulting firms. First-mover window is open.

### DigitalCoa.ch Position
- **Opportunity:** Trusted AI governance advisor for small Geneva consulting firms
- **Differentiation:** Not a tool vendor, not a generic business advisor — specialist in AI decision-making for knowledge-service firms
- **Unique angle:** nFADP compliance + AI strategy + implementation support — the stack no one else is offering to this segment
- **nFADP as entry point:** Every consultant using ChatGPT without a DPA is a potential lead

### Competitive Threats
| Threat | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Large consultancy (KPMG/Deloitte) launching SMB AI practice | Medium | They target mid-market+; expensive; slow |
| AI tool vendor adding advisory layer | Low-Medium | They remain tool-centric; governance ≠ tool selection |
| Free content / YouTube consultants | Low | Trust and depth requires paid advisory |
| Geneva boutique pivoting to AI governance | Low-Medium | First-mover advantage applies; act before they do |
