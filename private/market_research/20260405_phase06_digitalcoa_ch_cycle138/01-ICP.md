# 01-ICP.md — DigitalCoa.ch Service ICPs

## DigitalCoa.ch — Executive Advisory Services

### Refined ICP Definitions

**ICP 1 — Geneva Executive Decision-Maker (Primary)**
- **Role:** CEO, CFO, Managing Director, Board Chair, NED at Geneva-based companies (CHF 20M-1B revenue)
- **Concern:** Regulatory change impact, M&A decision, succession, geopolitical exposure, board governance
- **Trigger:** Board agenda item, regulatory deadline, M&A opportunity, advisory relationship transition
- **Buying behaviour:** Relationship-driven; trusted introduction preferred; evidence over marketing; 30-min briefing call before engagement
- **Budget authority:** CHF 100K-500K/year advisory; project or retainer
- **Language:** EN-primary; FR appreciated

**ICP 2 — Geneva UHNW / Family Office Principals**
- **Role:** UHNW individual, family principal, family office director
- **Concern:** Wealth preservation, succession, cantonal migration, governance, conflict-free advice
- **Trigger:** Family event, cantonal tax change, regulatory shift, advisor transition
- **Buying behaviour:** Trusted introduction essential; independent non-conflicted advisory critical differentiator
- **Budget authority:** CHF 200K-2M/year advisory; retainer preferred
- **Language:** EN-primary; FR for Geneva domestic

**ICP 3 — Geneva IO/NGO Executive (Secondary)**
- **Role:** Programme director, operations executive at ICRC, UNHCR, WFP, WHO, ITU
- **Concern:** Donor compliance, digital transformation, governance, regulatory alignment
- **Trigger:** Donor requirement, governance review, digital transformation mandate
- **Buying behaviour:** Committee-driven; peer reference; evidence-based
- **Budget authority:** CHF 50K-500K/project; donor-funded
- **Language:** EN-primary

### What These ICPs Do NOT Want
- Big 4 bank/law firm affiliated advisory with conflicts
- Vendor product pitches disguised as advisory
- Generic frameworks without Geneva-specific application
- Long sales processes before trust established

### Strategic Implication
DigitalCoa.ch converts via Brotons.net content → trusted introduction → 30-min briefing call → advisory engagement. Non-conflicted independent positioning is the primary differentiator.
