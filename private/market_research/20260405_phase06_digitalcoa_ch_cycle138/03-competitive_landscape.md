## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

**Big 4 Advisory (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY)**
- Dominate Geneva executive advisory; bank/audit conflicts; high pricing
- ICP perception: conflicted; expensive; layered teams
- DigitalCoa.ch differentiation: independent; non-conflicted; senior attention; Geneva regulatory depth

**Geneva Law Firm Advisory (Lenz & Staehelin, Vischer, Walder Wyss)**
- Deep Swiss legal expertise; premium pricing; relationship-driven
- ICP perception: legal-focused; limited independent candid counsel
- DigitalCoa.ch differentiation: regulatory + strategic advisory; non-legal framing; independent perspective

**Geneva Boutiques (Various)**
- Niche advisory; variable quality; limited breadth
- DigitalCoa.ch differentiation: Brotons.net institutional intelligence + independent positioning + service tiering clarity

**Independent Fractional CFO/GC Platforms (Various)**
- Growing; remote delivery; variable quality + institutional credibility
- DigitalCoa.ch differentiation: Geneva regulatory intelligence depth + Brotons.net trust pathway + governance quality documentation

| Competitor Type | Weakness | DigitalCoa.ch Positioning |
