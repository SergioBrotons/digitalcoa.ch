## Positioning Opportunity
- **Gap:** No clear independent EN-language platform owns "Swiss/European strategic interpretation" with a distinct voice
- **Brotons.net opportunity:** Own this specific niche — "The independent voice on European strategy and business"
- **Key differentiation:** Authentic, independent, intellectually rigorous, not AI-generated-feeling

## Competitive Moat Elements
1. **Independent voice** — not affiliated with any consulting firm or media company
2. **Consistent intellectual quality** — per SOUL.md: "intellectually strong and independent"
3. **Swiss credibility** — geographic authenticity in a trusted jurisdiction
4. **Curated quality** — not volume-driven, respects reader time
5. **Warm pathway to advisory** — content + service connection natural for Brotons.net owner
