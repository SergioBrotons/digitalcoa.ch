## Lead Magnet Sections (Cycle 54 Framework)

### Section 1: Your AI Footprint — Shadow AI Audit (Decision 0)
- What AI tools are you and your team currently using?
- Which handle client data?
- Which make or influence decisions autonomously? (Agentic AI = new category)
- Are they on your official inventory or invisible?

### Section 2: FINMA 08/2024 Compliance Gap Analysis
- Do you have an AI inventory?
- Is AI risk classified?
- Are responsibilities assigned?
- Is there independent verification of AI model development?
- Is output quality monitored continuously?

### Section 3: FADP AI Obligations — Client Data at Risk
- Are you disclosing AI use in client communications?
- Do clients have right to human review of AI-assisted decisions?
- Are you tracking if client data was used for model training?
- Privacy by Design implemented for AI tools?

### Section 4: CARF/CRS 2.0 — AML AI Monitoring
- Is AI monitoring of transactions documented?
- Are AI-driven alerts reviewed by qualified personnel?
- Is model validation current?

### Section 5: Agentic AI Risk — The 2026 Governance Gap
- Do you have autonomous AI agents executing decisions?
- Are autonomous decisions documented and reviewable?
- Do you have escalation procedures for AI-initiated actions?

### Section 6: Your Competitive Position
- Is AI governance a client trust differentiator?
- Can you demonstrate AI governance to prospects?
- Is your AI Swiss-hosted or US-cloud? (CLOUD Act data sovereignty)
