## First-Mover Opportunity — Confirmed Across All 5 Verticals

The competitive landscape analysis confirms a persistent structural gap:

1. **No boutique advisor** exists that specifically targets Geneva SMB professional practices (1–15 employees) for AI governance advisory
2. **All named competitors** serve enterprise, mid-market, or large firm clients
3. **The SMB governance gap** is real: 54% of Swiss SMBs testing AI, only 9% systematic use — governance is the missing layer
4. **DigitalCoa.ch is the first-mover** across all five verticals with a coherent, cross-vertical positioning
