# The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework

*A framework for Geneva consultants navigating GPAI, dual compliance, and Shadow AI risk*

---

## The Compliance Gap Is Already Real

GPAI transparency obligations have been active since **August 2, 2025**. That's 8.5 months of live obligations for consulting firms with EU nexus.

If your firm:
- Uses AI tools for client deliverables
- Advises clients on AI implementation
- Processes any EU-sourced data through AI systems
- Operates in EU-regulated sectors

...then GPAI transparency obligations already apply.

Meanwhile, **40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI skills** (PwC 2024). This creates a market paradox: consultants are being asked to advise on AI they may not themselves be governance-compliant on.

---

## Decision Framework

### Decision 0: Shadow AI Audit
What AI tools are your consultants using on client engagements? Shadow AI in consulting = firm liability for client data processed through unapproved tools.

### Decision 1: Do You Have EU Nexus?
If yes: GPAI transparency obligations are already active. High-risk obligations activate August 2, 2026 (~16 months).

### Decision 2: Is Your AI Use High-Risk?
High-risk AI processing requires DPIA. Consulting use cases to assess:
- AI-assisted client analysis
- AI processing third-party data
- AI outputs affecting client decisions

### Decision 3: Dual Compliance — nFADP + EU AI Act
For firms with EU nexus, compliance is dual:
- Swiss domestic: nFADP (active since Sep 1, 2023)
- EU: GPAI active; high-risk activates Aug 2, 2026
