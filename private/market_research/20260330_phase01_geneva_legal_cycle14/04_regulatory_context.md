## Active Now

### nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection)
- **Art. 5(c):** DPIA required for AI-based data processing — ACTIVE NOW
- **Art. 6(1):** Lawfulness of processing
- **Art. 31:** Automated individual decision-making (right to explanation)
- FDPIC confirmed (May 8, 2025): nFADP applies directly to AI-based data processing

### EU AI Act — GPAI (General-Purpose AI)
- **Operative since Aug 2, 2025** (~8+ months ACTIVE)
- **Transparency obligations:** LIVE NOW for EU-nexus firms
- **High-risk obligations:** Aug 2, 2026 (~15 months)
- **Applies to:** Firms placing GPAI on EU market, providing GPAI services to EU users, or acting as EU importer/distributor
- Geneva law firms with EU clients or EU-nexus AI tools are already subject
