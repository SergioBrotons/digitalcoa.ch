## Market Signals — Phase 01 Cycle 14

### 1. Governance Gap — Quantified (NEW)
- **69%** of legal professionals using AI globally
- **Only 34%** of firms have formal AI governance policies
- **86%** of firms increasing legal tech spending next 3 years
- **80%** planning to invest in tech tools
- → Gap between AI adoption and governance readiness = core advisory opportunity

### 2. FDPIC Enforcement Intensification (Confirmed)
- FDPIC 2024/2025 Annual Report (published Jul 1, 2025; covering until Mar 31, 2025):
  - **>1,000 new data protection cases**
  - **>360 data security breach reports**
  - **>100 interventions**
- Enforcement trajectory clearly intensifying
- FDPIC Feb 23, 2026 joint statement on AI-generated images (60+ DPAs) — cross-border enforcement coordination
- Law firms NOT yet targeted = timing signal still active but window shrinking

### 3. Geneva Law Firm AI Adoption
- Swiss firms piloting AI for legal research + administrative bottlenecks
- GenAI expected to expand into Swiss SMBs throughout 2026
- Small firms pragmatically using AI for drafting and summarizing
- Key barriers: data privacy concerns, lack of familiarity, ethical/compliance navigation
- **Professional secrecy / data residency**: Art. 13 Lawyer's Act + Art. 321 Swiss Criminal Code = concrete constraint for AI tool selection
- US CLOUD Act = risk for US-hosted AI tools; Swiss-hosted solutions required for client data
