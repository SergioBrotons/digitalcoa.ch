# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva NGO & Tech

## Geneva as NGO & Tech Hub

### Geneva NGO Ecosystem
- **~800 NGOs** registered in Geneva (Geneva International estimate)
- ~30 international organizations with headquarters in Geneva
- Geneva is world capital for multilateral diplomacy alongside NYC
- UN Office at Geneva: largest UN duty station globally
- Switzerland's neutrality creates unique convening power for peace/rights initiatives

### Geneva Tech Ecosystem
- **Emerging but growing** — Geneva-Lausanne corridor ("Health Valley")
- Fongit: Leading Geneva tech incubator (100+ startups)
- EPFL (Lausanne): Top European tech university → startup pipeline
- Swiss startup ecosystem rank: #2 in Europe after UK (Startup Genome 2024)
- Geneva fintech hub: Banks + tech + crypto (though Zug dominates crypto)
- Tech investment in Geneva region: ~CHF 500M-1B annually (estimates)

### Key Demand Signals

#### NGOs
1. **Digital transformation acceleration** — COVID-19 forced rapid digital adoption
2. **Data-driven impact measurement** — donors increasingly require evidence-based reporting
3. **Remote/hybrid work** — operational infrastructure still adapting
4. **AI exposure** — staff using personal AI tools without organizational policy

#### Tech
1. **AI feature integration** — most Geneva startups integrating AI into core product
2. **EU AI Act preparation** — Swiss startups targeting EU market must prepare
3. **Cybersecurity** — increasing attacks on startups (ransomware, phishing)
4. **Scale-up challenges** — Series A/B companies struggling with operational structure
