# 03 — Competitive Landscape: Geneva NGO & Tech Advisory

## NGOs — Competitive Landscape

### Advisory / Consulting Competitors
| Firm | Strength | Weakness | Geneva Presence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deloitte (nonprofit practice) | Global brand, nonprofit specialization | Expensive | Some |
| PwC (NGO sector) | Audit + advisory | Too expensive for small NGOs | Some |
| Management Sciences for Health | Health sector NGO expertise | US-centric | Weak in CH |
| Geneva-based boutiques (e.g., Tostan) | Local knowledge | Too small or specialized | Strong |
| IO-specific (UNICEF, UNDP consulting arms) | Deep IO relationships | Slow, bureaucratic | Limited |

### Technology Vendors
| Vendor | Relevance | Weakness |
|---|---|---|
| Salesforce Nonprofit CRM | Fundraising + program management | Expensive, complex |
| Microsoft (nonprofit program) | Discounted tech stack | Vendor lock-in |
| Google for Nonprofits | Tools + visibility | Data privacy concerns |
| Local Swiss nonprofit tech providers | Swiss data residency | Small, limited scale |

## Tech — Competitive Landscape

### Advisory / Consulting
| Firm | Strength | Weakness | Geneva Presence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Swiss venture capital firms (not advisory) | | | |
| Startup studios (Axa Venture Partners, etc.) | Investment focus | Not advisory | Some |
| Swiss ICT cluster | Network, some advisory | Publicly funded, slow | Strong |
| Legal boutiques (IP, tech law) | Legal specialization | Not operations | Strong |
| Big 4 tech practices | Full-service | Too expensive for startups | Some |

### Key Difference: NGOs vs. Tech
- **NGOs:** Need compliance + operations + digital strategy
- **Tech:** Need AI governance + product strategy + security

## Positioning Opportunity for DigitalCoa
- **For NGOs:** "AI governance for mission-driven organizations" — unique positioning; no one owns this
- **For Tech:** "AI governance for scaling startups" — fits with broader DigitalCoa AI advisory positioning

## Competitive Moat
1. **Dual-sector credibility** — few advisors can serve both NGO and tech
2. **International standard fluency** — understanding of UN/IO frameworks + Swiss tech ecosystem
3. **Bilingual EN+FR** — critical for Geneva NGOs
