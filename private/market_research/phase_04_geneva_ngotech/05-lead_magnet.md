# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva NGO & Tech

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "NGO AI Governance Quick-Start Guide"
**Format:** Bilingual PDF (10 pages, EN+FR)
**Target:** NGO Executive Directors, Operations Directors
**Value:** Practical steps to establish AI use policy for mission-driven organizations; includes nFADP compliance checklist
**Lead capture:** Name, organization, role, staff size
**Conversion path:** Guide → discovery call → advisory engagement
**Note:** Unique in market — no Geneva-specific NGO AI governance guide exists

### Option B: "Tech Startup AI Governance Playbook"
**Format:** Web-based interactive guide (digital-first)
**Target:** CTOs, CEOs of Geneva/Lausanne tech startups
**Value:** EU AI Act readiness checklist, ISO 42001 gap assessment, model governance basics
**Lead capture:** Name, company, stage, sector
**Conversion path:** Playbook → AI governance assessment → retainer advisory

### Option C: "Geneva Tech Week Briefing — AI Governance Edition"
**Format:** Event-driven one-pager (5 pages)
**Target:** Tech startup founders and CTOs attending Geneva Tech Week
**Value:** Curated AI governance priorities for Geneva tech sector
**Lead capture:** Name, company, email
**Conversion path:** Event distribution → follow-up webinar → advisory

### Option D: "Digital Resilience Framework for International Organizations"
**Format:** Detailed guide (30 pages)
**Target:** IO Operations Directors, IT Managers
**Value:** Comprehensive framework for AI adoption, cybersecurity, data governance for multilateral organizations
**Lead capture:** Name, organization, role
**Conversion path:** Long-form nurture → executive briefing → strategic engagement
**Note:** Very niche; very high-value; long sales cycle

## Lead Magnet Recommendation

**Primary for NGOs:** Option A (NGO AI Governance Quick-Start Guide) — most gap in market, bilingual required, directly actionable

**Primary for Tech:** Option B (Tech Startup AI Governance Playbook) — fits broader DigitalCoa AI governance positioning; can cross-sell to Phase 01/02 verticals

**Long-term:** Option D (IO framework) — highest authority, very long sales cycle

## Bilingual Considerations
- NGOs: French essential, English acceptable
- Tech: English primary, French useful for Geneva-specific positioning
- Both: Landing pages in EN + FR
