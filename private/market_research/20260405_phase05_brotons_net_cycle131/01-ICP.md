# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Brotons.net — C131

**Reader Personas:**
1. **Geneva CFO / Finance Director** — Geneva private bank, commodity trading house, or multinational CFO. Reads for regulatory change impact on financial operations. Primary topics: Basel IV, sanctions compliance, FX risk, SNB policy. Conversion target: briefing call, advisory engagement.

2. **Geneva CEO / Managing Director** — Geneva SME or subsidiary of multinational. Strategic decision-maker. Reads for Geneva-specific intelligence on EU-CH relations, regulatory environment, Geneva financial centre developments. Conversion target: relationship, referral, advisory.

3. **Geneva Diplomat / IO Professional** — Geneva-based international organization professional. Reads for commercial and regulatory intelligence that intersects with IO mandates. Primary topics: trade policy, sanctions, digital governance. Conversion target: professional network, consulting referral.

4. **Geneva NGO Director** — ICRC, UNHCR, WHO, WTO, ITU-affiliated professional. Reads for regulatory and commercial intelligence relevant to NGO operations and partnerships. Primary topics: technology governance, data protection, climate finance, procurement. Conversion target: advisory engagement, network.

**Reader Journey:**
- Discovery: Search (Geneva regulatory question), LinkedIn recommendation, IO colleague forward, Geneva institutional event
- First read: Single article, high-value insight consumed
- Second touch: Newsletter subscription (often via article CTA)
- Engagement: Newsletter open, click-through to related article
- Conversion: Briefing call request, advisory engagement inquiry, referral

**Pain Points for Content:**
- Finding Geneva-specific intelligence that is not generic legal database output
- Time-constrained executive who cannot read long reports but needs key implications
- EN+FR bilingual content need for Geneva FR-dominant professional context
- Overwhelmed by regulatory information from multiple sources without Geneva-specific framing

**Urgency Triggers:**
- Geneva institutional events (UNOG GA, WTO MC, ICRC Assemblée, ITU Plenipotentiary)
- Swiss regulatory deadline milestones (Basel IV Jan 2027, TCFD adoption)
- EU-CH bilateral negotiation milestones
- FINMA enforcement actions (relevant to reader's sector)

**Language Preference:** EN primary; FR essential for Geneva-based IO and institutional readers; DE secondary
**Content Consumption:** Mobile-optimized written, audio during commute, newsletter on desktop
