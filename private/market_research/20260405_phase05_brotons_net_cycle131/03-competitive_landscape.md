# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## Brotons.net — C131

## Competitors

**Legal Databases:** Wolters Kluwer (Lextiempo), Thomson Reuters (Westlaw), Swiss Legal, legalis.ch, DFR Law Geneva
- Strength: Comprehensive legal analysis, formal citation, regulatory text analysis
- Weakness: Decision-maker-unfriendly format, no Geneva institutional intelligence, FR-only or EN-only, no audio

**Independent Geneva Blogs:** Geneva Business News, Global Geneva magazine, Geneva Observer
- Strength: Geneva-focused journalism, institutional connections
- Weakness: Generalist news coverage, not regulatory/compliance analysis, no EN+FR depth

**LinkedIn Newsletter Writers:** Individual Geneva executives, Geneva chamber newsletters, Geneva law firm newsletters
- Strength: Direct executive access, professional credibility
- Weakness: Inconsistent publication cadence, firm-conflicted perspectives, no systematic framework

**Podcast/Audio:** The Economist (global), NZZ (German-language Swiss), Le Temps (FR Swiss), Geneva Business News podcast
- Strength: Audio format, professional journalism
- Weakness: Not Geneva-specific institutional intelligence, not bilingual EN+FR, not compliance-focused

**Brotons.net Differentiation:**
- Geneva institutional intelligence layer: between raw institutional source and formal legal analysis
- Bilingual EN+FR: uniquely positioned for Geneva's linguistic context
- Decision-maker framing: not legal research format, but strategic implications for executives
- Analytical depth without legal jargon: accessible to non-lawyer decision-makers

## Partnership Landscape

**IHEID:** Geneva Graduate Institute publishes research but lacks editorial analysis for practitioners. Partnership opportunity: IHEID research → Brotons.net analysis → IHEID member distribution.

**CCIG:** Chamber of commerce with 2,000+ member businesses. Weekly communications to members. Partnership opportunity: Brotons.net analysis as content for CCIG member communications; Brotons.net gains distribution.

**SFAMA:** Swiss fund and asset management industry association. Technical publications for members. Partnership opportunity: Brotons.net regulatory analysis for SFAMA members; Brotons.net gains financial services sector credibility.

**LinkedIn Newsletter:** Individual Geneva executives publish newsletters. Partnership opportunity: Cross-recommendation with complementary non-competing newsletters; joint Geneva institutional intelligence newsletter.
