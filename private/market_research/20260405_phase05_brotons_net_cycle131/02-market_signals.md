## Geneva Executive Content Consumption

Geneva executives consume content during commutes (30-60 minutes daily), during travel, and during lunch. Audio content (podcasts, audio briefings) captures commute time that written content cannot. Key insight: executive audio content in Geneva should be bilingual (EN + FR) and Geneva-specific — generic business podcasts are already saturated; the differentiation is Geneva institutional intelligence depth.

## Institutional Content Partnerships

IHEID, CCIG, and SFAMA are natural distribution partners:
- IHEID publishes research and hosts events that generate Geneva intelligence
- CCIG communicates with 2,000+ Geneva businesses weekly
- SFAMA reaches Swiss financial industry professionals

These partners lack editorial analysis capacity — Brotons.net analysis could enhance their member communications in exchange for distribution.

## SEO Opportunity — Featured Snippet Capture

Search queries like "Geneva sanctions compliance," "Swiss arbitration rules," "EU-CH bilateral agreement implications" have featured snippet opportunities that legal databases do not capture because the questions are framed as decision-maker queries, not legal research queries. Brotons.net topic clusters can capture these snippets with well-structured Q&A format articles.

## Newsletter Deliverability

Geneva corporate and IO domains have strict email security policies (DMARC quarantine/reject). Substack emails to icrc.org, unog.ch, WTO.org, bluewin.ch, and other Geneva institutional domains experience inconsistent deliverability. SPF/DKIM/DMARC optimization can improve inbox placement rates by 15-25% for Geneva executive segments.

## Content Marketing Funnel

The current funnel is:
- Article → newsletter subscription → occasional engagement → conversion rarely tracked
- Missing: topic-specific landing pages, persona-matched CTAs, conversion tracking by content cluster
- Improvement opportunity: build topic-specific lead magnets that capture high-intent readers before they leave

**Key metrics:**
- Newsletter-to-article click rate: industry average 2-4%; best-in-class 6-8%
- Featured snippet capture rate for topic authority clusters: 15-20% of queries
- Audio content completion rate: 65-75% (vs. 40-50% article scroll rate)
- DMARC deliverability improvement potential: +15-25% inbox placement for Geneva institutional domains
