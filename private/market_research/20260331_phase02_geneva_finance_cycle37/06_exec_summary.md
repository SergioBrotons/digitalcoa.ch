## Situation

Geneva boutique wealth management firms face a compounding regulatory pressure: **CRS 2.0 is live (Jan 1, 2026)** but **CARF is POSTPONED to 2027** (Swiss legislation still enters force Jan 1, 2026 with design constraints), FATF evaluation is looming, and 76% of Geneva FS firms are using AI — but 43% have NO formal AI policy (Wolters Kluwer Mar 2026). ### Art. 21 FADP — New Urgency Lever (Phase 01 Cycle 37)

Automated financial decisions (portfolio recommendations, risk assessments, credit decisions) must allow human review under Art. 21 FADP. AI-assisted WM tools are directly in scope.

### Dual Compliance Burden

WM firms with EU client nexus must comply with both FADP and EU AI Act (Aug 2, 2026 high-risk activation for credit/risk assessment AI).
