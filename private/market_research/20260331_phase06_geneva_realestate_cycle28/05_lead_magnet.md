# The Small Real Estate Agency AI Governance Question

*67% of Geneva real estate agencies use AI. Almost none have a governance framework for it.*

## The Governance Gap Is Widest Here

**67% AI adoption** — the highest of any sector in our Geneva portfolio research.

Your agency is likely using AI tools for:
- Property descriptions and listings
- Price estimation models
- Client communication drafting
- Property search optimization
- Marketing content generation

But do you have a written policy governing how these tools handle client data, property information, and personal details?

If the answer is no, you're in the same position as 67% of your Geneva peers — using AI faster than you're governing it.

---

## Retroactive Compliance: Govern the AI You Already Have

Unlike other professional sectors (anticipatory compliance — planning for upcoming regulations), Geneva real estate agencies have a unique entry point:

**Retroactive compliance.** The AI is already in use. The governance framework needs to catch up with what's already deployed.

This is more urgent, not less — because:
1. Shadow AI is already happening (employees using unapproved tools)
2. Client data is already flowing through AI systems
3. FDPIC enforcement is building across sectors

---

## The 4-Step Retroactive Compliance Path

### Step 1: Shadow AI Audit — "What AI Are You Already Using?"
Inventory every AI tool currently used by agents in your agency. No blame. Map the data each tool touches.

### Step 2: Data Classification — "What Data Is Being Processed?"
Property listings = public data. Client financial information = confidential. AI processing of which data requires what controls?

### Step 3: nFADP Obligations — "Do You Have Existing Legal Obligations?"
FADP has been operative since September 1, 2023. Property valuation AI likely qualifies as high-risk processing requiring DPIA.

### Step 4: Compliance Gap — "What Needs to Be Fixed?"
Document the gap between current AI use and required governance. This becomes your agency's AI action plan.
