## FADP Clinical AI Compliance

**Art. 21 FADP: Human Review**
AI-assisted clinical decisions must allow human review.
AI diagnostics, treatment recommendations, clinical notes = Art. 21 applicability.
**Life safety context**: human review is medical necessity, not just compliance.

**Art. 5(c) FADP: Transparency**
Must disclose AI involvement to patients.
AI-generated clinical reports = disclosure required.

**DPIA: Required for High-Risk Clinical AI**
- AI diagnostic imaging
- AI-assisted treatment planning
- AI clinical decision support
Document the assessment; update when AI tools change.
