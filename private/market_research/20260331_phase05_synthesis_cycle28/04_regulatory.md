## Cross-Vertical Compliance Principles

### FADP Applies Universally
No new Swiss AI law required. FADP's technology-neutral application already covers AI processing. All firms using AI on personal data have existing obligations.

### DPIA Required Across Verticals
High-risk AI processing requires Data Protection Impact Assessment. Verticals with confirmed high-risk use cases:
- Legal: contract analysis, due diligence
- Finance: client suitability analysis, AML monitoring
- Healthcare: diagnostic imaging, clinical decision support
- Real Estate: property valuation AI

### Shadow AI = Universal Risk
Employees using unapproved tools = firm's obligations breached. Universal across all 5 verticals.

### Managing Director Criminal Liability Universal
CHF 250,000 for intentional FADP failures. Applies to managing directors, partners, and practice owners across all verticals.
