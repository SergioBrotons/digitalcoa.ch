## Content Sections

### Section 1: The Geneva Real Estate AI Governance Audit (5 Questions)
1. Do we use AVMs (Automated Valuation Models) in our property listings? Is there documented human review of every AVM output before client delivery?
2. Do we use tenant screening AI or creditworthiness AI? Have we assessed whether it falls within EU AI Act Annex III?
3. Do we have documented client consent protocols for AI-assisted property decisions?
4. Are our AI tools (AVMs, CRM, property matching) hosted on Swiss/EU infrastructure?
5. Have we assessed EU AI Act applicability — especially if we serve EU buyers or investors?

### Section 2: The AVM PILOT TRAP
AVMs = high-risk under Federal AI Ordinance + FADP Art. 21 simultaneously. Every Geneva agency using AVMs without documented human review is doubly exposed. This is systemic — and it is not widely known.

### Section 3: The August 2, 2026 EU AI Act Preparedness
- [ ] AVM human review protocol documented
- [ ] Tenant screening AI inventoried and risk-classified
- [ ] EU AI Act Annex III applicability assessed
- [ ] Client disclosure language for AI-assisted property decisions
- [ ] Vendor contracts reviewed for hosting and training data provisions
