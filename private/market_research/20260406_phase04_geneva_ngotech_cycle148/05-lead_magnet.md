# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva NGO/Tech

## Strategic Positioning Angle
Geneva's concentration of international organizations, NGOs, and tech startups creates a unique ecosystem: IOs need digital transformation and AI strategy, NGOs need operational efficiency and impact measurement, and tech startups need regulatory navigation. A single advisor who understands this ecosystem — and can serve multiple segments within it — occupies a defensible, cross-segment position.

## Lead Magnet Concepts

### Option A: "The Geneva IO Procurement Playbook — Getting Your Boutique on the Approved Vendor List"
A practical guide for Geneva consulting and professional services boutiques navigating UN/IO procurement rules: vendor registration process, framework agreement requirements, competitive positioning for IO contracts.

**Format:** Practical guide (12-15 pages) + Q&A session
**Distribution:** Managing Partners, Business Development at Geneva boutiques targeting IO contracts
**Value:** Directly addresses the #1 barrier to Geneva boutiques accessing IO contracts

### Option B: "Humanitarian AI Strategy Framework — A Guide for Geneva IOs and Major NGOs"
A framework document helping Geneva IOs and major NGOs develop AI strategies that align with humanitarian principles, data protection requirements, and operational constraints.

**Format:** Strategy framework (15-20 pages) + workshop proposal
**Distribution:** IO/NGO Executive Directors, Chief Strategy Officers, Technology Officers
**Value:** IOs and major NGOs lag private sector AI adoption by 3-5 years; this positions advisor as thought leader in an underserved market

### Option C: "Geneva HealthTech Regulatory Roadmap — From Concept to Swissmedic Approval"
A step-by-step regulatory roadmap for Geneva healthtech startups: Swissmedic approval pathway, EU MDR alignment, reimbursement strategy, and market access timeline.

**Format:** Regulatory roadmap (15-20 pages) + strategy session
**Distribution:** Geneva healthtech startup founders, CEOs, regulatory affairs leads
**Value:** Swissmedic regulatory strategy is genuinely underserved; natural entry point for Geneva healthtech advisory

## Preferred Format (Initial)
Option A + Option B as primary — IO procurement advisory is uniquely Geneva and defensible; humanitarian AI strategy is a genuinely new market. Combined, they position the advisor as the go-to for Geneva's IO/NGO ecosystem.

## Target CTA
"Schedule a 45-minute IO procurement strategy call" — targeted at Geneva consulting firm Managing Partners and BD leads.
