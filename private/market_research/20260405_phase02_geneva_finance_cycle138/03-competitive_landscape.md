## Competitive Landscape

### Primary Competitors

**Big 4 Finance Advisory (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY Finance Advisory)**
- Dominate Geneva SMB/mid-market finance advisory; bank-affiliated; high pricing
- ICP perception: conflicted for clients with banking relationships; expensive; layered teams
- Differentiation opportunity: independent non-conflicted perspective; senior attention vs. junior teams

**Geneva Regional Banks (BCGE, UBS Geneva, Credit Suisse successor entity)**
- Relationship banking; internal finance advisory capacity
- ICP perception: product-driven; limited independent perspective
- Differentiation opportunity: objective regulatory analysis + finance function advisory

**Specialist Treasury / FX Advisory Boutiques**
- Niche treasury/FX advisory; limited Geneva SMB coverage
- Opportunity: Geneva SMB finance function CFO advisory (broader than treasury)

**In-house Finance Teams**
- Growing SMB preference for interim CFO + advisory model
- Opportunity: fractional CFO + finance function advisory; independent perspective

## Competitive Differentiation Angles

| Competitor Type | Weakness | Our Positioning |
|---|---|---|
| Big 4 finance | Conflicts; senior price + junior execution | Independent candid counsel; senior attention |
| Regional banks | Product-driven; limited objective view | Independent regulatory + finance advisory |
| Specialist boutiques | Narrow scope; limited Geneva SMB breadth | Broad finance function + regulatory advisory |
| Interim CFO | Transaction-focused; limited regulatory depth | Regulatory intelligence + finance advisory |
