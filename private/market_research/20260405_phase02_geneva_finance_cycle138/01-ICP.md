# 01-ICP.md — Geneva Finance SMB/Mid-Market

## Geneva Finance — SMB / Mid-Market

### Refined ICP Definition
CFO/finance directors and treasury professionals at:
- Geneva SMBs and mid-market companies (CHF 10M–500M revenue)
- Geneva professional services firms (law firms, consultancies, family offices)
- International organisations with Geneva treasury functions (UNOG, ITU, WIPO, WHO, ICRC)
- Cross-border businesses with CHF/EUR/USD exposure

### Financial Decision-Maker Profile
- **Role:** CFO, Finance Director, Treasurer, Managing Director
- **Concern:** Regulatory cost increases, FX exposure, funding costs, treasury efficiency
- **Trigger events:** SNB rate changes, FINMA regulatory changes, FX vol events, funding gaps, ESG reporting mandates
- **Buying behaviour:** ROI-focused; peer reference over vendor; concise actionable output
- **Budget authority:** CHF 50K–2M/year finance function spend; project-based preferred
- **Language:** EN-primary for international; FR for domestic/regulatory

### What This ICP Does NOT Want
- Generic treasury software vendor pitches
- Long regulatory summaries without Geneva/Swiss-specific action items
- Cross-border complexity without practical implementation pathways
- Big 4 bank-affiliated advisory (conflict concerns)

### Strategic Implication
Finance decision-makers in Geneva SMB/mid-market are reachable via Brotons.net content and CCIG peer networks. Decision-support framing (2-5 day diagnostic + actionable output) matches buying behaviour.
