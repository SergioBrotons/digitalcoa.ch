# Phase 06 — DigitalCoa.ch Audit | Cycle 93
**Completed:** 2026-04-03 17:18 UTC

## Key Findings

### Site is Professionally Structured — Strong Foundation
DigitalCoa.ch has a clean, professional site with clear positioning. The governance-first, decision architecture concept is well-executed. Service tiers, audience segments, and process flow are all clear and compelling.

### Critical Issue: French Version Non-Functional
/fr/ redirects to /en — the claimed bilingual site is actually English-only. This undermines Swiss market positioning (French-speaking Switzerland is a core market).

### Limited SEO Footprint
- Only 4 functional pages: /, /services, /about, /contact
- No blog, no articles, no thought leadership content
- No sitemap.xml or robots.txt
- No intermediate content pages between homepage and contact
- Prospects must go directly from homepage to contact (no nurturing path)

### 24 Market Research Findings = Content Opportunity
Phases 01-04 produced 24 NET-NEW Geneva market findings covering:
- Geneva Legal (Swiss sanctions, IRGC, FinCEN)
- Geneva Finance (MBaer scandal, Ormuz toll, hedge funds, credit advisory)
- Geneva Consulting (cybersecurity, scale-ups, digital sovereignty)
- Geneva NGO/Tech (AI governance, deepfakes, public health)

All directly relevant to DigitalCoa.ch's decision governance positioning. Currently unused.

### No Content Bridge Between Market Research and Site
The operator produces extensive market research but it never becomes site content. This is a significant gap between research output and business value creation.

### Conversion Path: Homepage → Services → Contact
Clear but compressed. No intermediate content (articles, case studies, frameworks). Prospects need to already understand the value proposition to convert.

3. **Add intermediate content pages** — Frameworks, case studies, or analysis pieces
