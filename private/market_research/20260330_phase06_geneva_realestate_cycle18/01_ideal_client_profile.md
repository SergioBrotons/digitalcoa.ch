### Key Differentiator — Retroactive Compliance Entry Point
**Phase 06 is the only vertical where the primary framing is retroactive compliance ("govern the AI you already have") rather than anticipatory compliance.**

Small RE agencies are already using AI tools — the opportunity is not to prepare for future regulations, but to govern AI already deployed. This creates a lower-friction entry point than verticals requiring firms to understand upcoming obligations.
