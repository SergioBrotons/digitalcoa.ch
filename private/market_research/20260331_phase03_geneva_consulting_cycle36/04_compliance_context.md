## Compliance Complexity (Consulting-Specific)

### Shadow AI in Consulting
Consultants using unapproved AI tools for client deliverables = highest-risk Shadow AI context:
- Client confidentiality breach (sharing client data with US-based AI tools)
- FADP breach (purpose, functionality, data sources not disclosed)
- GPAI breach (AI system transparency documentation absent)
- Professional liability (AI-generated recommendations without proper vetting)

### Failure Rate Signal
**73% of abandoned AI projects** in consulting due to skills gap = advisory entry beyond governance. Small consulting firms need help not just with compliance frameworks but with AI implementation that actually works.

## Compliance Entry Points

### Decision 0: Shadow AI Audit
Primary entry point: Map all AI tools currently used by consultants for client work. This surfaces FADP + GPAI exposure simultaneously.

### GPAI Readiness Assessment
Secondary entry point: Assess AI tools against GPAI transparency obligations for firms with EU-nexus.

### DPIA for High-Risk Consulting AI
Tertiary entry point: Data Protection Impact Assessment for AI tools processing client personal data (contract analysis, due diligence, HR consulting).
