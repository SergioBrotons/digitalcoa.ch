## Lead Magnet: "The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"

### Positioning
For Geneva-area small consulting firms (5–50 employees) and professional services firms that use AI tools for client work but lack formal AI governance. The framework surfaces GPAI obligations already operative, Shadow AI exposure, and dual compliance complexity (nFADP + GPAI).

### Core Value Proposition
**GPAI has been operative since August 2, 2025** (~8.5 months). Geneva consulting firms with EU clients have likely been in violation without knowing it. 43% of consulting firms have NO formal AI policy (Wolters Kluwer March 2026). 40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI skills (PwC 2024). Shadow AI in consulting = highest-stakes client confidentiality + FADP + GPAI exposure simultaneously.

### Primary Entry Point
**Shadow AI Audit (Decision 0)**: Map every AI tool used for client work before assessing GPAI obligations, FADP disclosure requirements, and dual compliance complexity.

### 9-Section Framework

1. **AI Tool Inventory** — Map every AI tool used by consultants for client work
2. **Shadow AI Risk Assessment** — Surface client confidentiality + FADP + GPAI exposure
3. **GPAI Readiness Check** — Assess AI tools against EU AI Act transparency obligations
4. **FADP Disclosure Audit** — Purpose, functionality, data sources for client data processing
5. **Client Confidentiality Assessment** — AI tools + client data + US servers exposure
6. **Dual Compliance Map** — nFADP + GPAI simultaneous obligations
7. **DPIA for High-Risk Consulting AI** — contract analysis, due diligence, HR AI
8. **Skills Gap Assessment** — 40% AI skills gap + 73% failure rate entry
9. **Remediation Roadmap** — 30/60/90-day sequence with GPAI anchor

### Urgency Framing
- **GPAI ~8.5mo already operative** — no ignorance defense for EU-nexus firms
- **43% no-policy** — structural gap, not temporary
- **Federal Council First Draft May 2026 ~8 WEEKS** — next external pressure event
- **IT consulting +28%** — M&A sector most exposed sub-segment
- **73% abandoned AI projects** — skills gap advisory entry

### Efficiency Lever
**65% of firms save 1–5 hours per week** with generative AI (Legartis 2026). AI governance isn't just compliance — it's protecting efficiency gains and enabling the next productivity wave.
