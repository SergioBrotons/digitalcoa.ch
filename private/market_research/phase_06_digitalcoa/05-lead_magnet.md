# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: DigitalCoa.ch Lead Generation

## Current Lead Path (Implicit)
1. Content discovery (LinkedIn/Substack/Brotons.net)
2. DigitalCoa.ch visit
3. CTA: "Request a Briefing Call" or similar
4. Briefing call
5. Engagement

## Lead Magnet Options

### Option A: "Executive AI Briefing — The Geneva Edition"
**Format:** In-person or Zoom briefing (60-90 minutes, no cost)
**Target:** C-suite executives at Swiss financial institutions
**Value:** Curated FINMA regulatory update + AI governance priorities + implications for their organization
**Lead capture:** Name, company, role, current AI initiatives (brief intake form)
**Conversion path:** Briefing → advisory engagement
**Note:** This is the existing implicit model — formalize it

### Option B: "The AI Governance Readiness Assessment"
**Format:** Self-service or guided assessment (online PDF)
**Target:** GCs, CIOs, Risk Managers at Swiss FIs
**Value:** Benchmark their AI governance against FINMA guidance and ISO 42001
**Lead capture:** Name, company, role, email
**Conversion path:** Assessment → briefing call offer

### Option C: "DigitalCoa.ch Regulatory Intelligence Briefing — Quarterly"
**Format:** Quarterly email (10-minute read)
**Target:** C-suite and senior executives
**Value:** Curated FINMA + EU AI Act regulatory developments with executive implications
**Lead capture:** Name, company, role
**Conversion path:** Subscription → trust building → advisory inquiry

### Option D: "Executive AI Strategy Workshop" (Half-Day)
**Format:** Paid workshop (CHF 5,000-10,000)
**Target:** Leadership teams at Swiss financial institutions
**Value:** Interactive AI strategy + governance workshop with framework tools
**Lead capture:** Company, team size, current AI maturity
**Revenue path:** Paid workshop → advisory engagement
**Note:** Higher commitment; qualifies leads further

## Lead Magnet Recommendation

**Primary:** Option A (Executive AI Briefing) — fits the current implicit model; direct, personal, qualifies leads naturally
**Secondary:** Option C (Regulatory Intelligence Briefing) — recurring touchpoint, low cost, builds trust over time
**Long-term:** Option D (Workshop) — higher ticket, creates deeper relationships, generates revenue

## Lead Qualification Criteria
- Role: C-suite or direct report with P&L or risk responsibility
- Sector: Financial services or professional services (legal, consulting)
- Geography: Switzerland primary
- Timing: Active problem or upcoming decision related to AI, regulatory change, or strategy
