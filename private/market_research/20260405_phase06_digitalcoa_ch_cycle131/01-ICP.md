# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## DigitalCoa.ch — C131

**Client Types:**
1. **Geneva CEO / Managing Director (SME/Family Business)** — Geneva SME or family business, CHF 20-200M revenue, decision-maker facing Geneva regulatory complexity (Basel IV, sanctions, EU-CH bilateral). Needs strategic guidance from an advisor who understands Geneva context without the conflicts of a law firm or bank.

2. **Geneva CFO / Finance Director (Non-Bank)** — CFO of Geneva trading house, Swiss subsidiary of multinational, or Swiss SME. Needs independent regulatory and strategic advisory on Basel IV, FX risk, sanctions compliance, or treasury decisions. Not served by their bank's advisory (conflict) or a law firm (too technical/expensive).

3. **Geneva NGO Executive Director** — ICRC, UNHCR, WHO, WTO-affiliated professional or Geneva NGO director. Needs Geneva-specific regulatory and operational advisory with Geneva institutional intelligence access. No Big 4 conflict; independent advisory relationship.

4. **Geneva Family Office / UHNW Decision-Maker** — Geneva-based family office or UHNW individual. Needs independent advisory on governance, succession, regulatory environment, and investment strategy that does not conflict with their private bank's product incentives.

**Decision-Maker Profile:**
- Age: 40-60
- Role: C-suite (CEO, CFO), Managing Director, Executive Director
- Language: EN primary; FR for Geneva institutional contacts
- Decision process: Relationship-based; trust precedes engagement
- Decision trigger: Regulatory change, board pressure, family succession event, expansion decision
- Budget authority: CHF 5,000-50,000/month for advisory retainer or CHF 15,000-100,000 for defined engagements

**Pain Points:**
- Geneva regulatory complexity without access to independent, Geneva-specific advisory
- Big 4 audit conflicts prevent their primary advisors from advising on the issues that matter most
- Law firm advice is too technical and expensive for strategic decision-making
- Bank advisory is product-conflicted (commission, lending, FX products)
- No neutral, Geneva-expert sounding board for strategic decisions

**Engagement Triggers:**
- Regulatory deadline (Basel IV, TCFD, sanctions enforcement)
- Board meeting requiring Geneva regulatory briefing
- Family business succession planning
- International expansion (EU, UK, US)
- M&A or partnership decision in Geneva context

**Geography:** Geneva primary; Vaud; Zurich as secondary
**Language:** EN primary; FR useful for Geneva institutional relationships
**Engagement Model:** Retainer preferred; defined-scope projects accepted; day-rate advisory sessions for initial relationship
