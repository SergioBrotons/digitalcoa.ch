2. **Independent professional accountability positioning — how to frame independence as decision-maker credibility signal** — Geneva prospects evaluating advisory relationships distinguish between "independent" and "firm-conflicted." The evidence: Big 4 audit conflicts prevent them from advising on Basel IV capital optimization for their own audit clients; Swiss private banks' product revenues create product-conflict incentives; law firm hourly billing creates scope-expansion incentives. DigitalCoa.ch's independence is a factual credential that must be explicitly communicated and demonstrated: no audit conflicts, no product revenues, no referral commissions. The advisory positioning is: "Independent because our business model is advisory fees only — not product distribution, not audit, not referral commissions." This framing must appear on DigitalCoa.ch explicitly, not just in conversation.

3. **Geneva briefing call follow-up sequence — decision-maker communication protocol** — The post-briefing-call follow-up is the highest-stakes moment in the advisory relationship. Geneva executives receive multiple proposals and proposals-for-discussion after every meeting. The differentiator is not speed (immediate follow-up feels sales-like) but precision (the right follow-up at the right time with the right content). The optimal sequence: (1) same-day: 2-sentence thank you email referencing specific discussion point (within 4 hours of meeting); (2) 48 hours: 1-page "what we discussed and what it means for you" memo; (3) 2 weeks: Geneva-specific intelligence update referencing a topic from the call; (4) 6 weeks: brief "what's changed" update. Each touchpoint must be short, Geneva-specific, and non-commercial in tone.

4. **Advisory engagement economics — CHF day rate benchmarks and retainer structure precision** — Geneva executive advisory engagements operate in a specific price range: CHF 3,500-5,500/day for senior advisory (2026 market rate for Geneva-based independent senior consultants); CHF 8,000-15,000/month for ongoing retainer (20-30 days equivalent at day rate). Advisory scope language matters: "strategic advisory session" (half-day, CHF 1,800-2,500), "decision-support engagement" (defined scope, 3-6 weeks), "ongoing advisory retainer" (monthly, 3-5 days/month). The pricing page on DigitalCoa.ch should use this language precisely. Avoid: "contact us for pricing" (uncertainty for decision-makers) and "custom pricing" (appears to hide pricing).

"Personal attention" is table stakes. These standards should appear on DigitalCoa.ch and be operationally upheld.
