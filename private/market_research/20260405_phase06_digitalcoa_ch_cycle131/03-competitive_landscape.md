# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## DigitalCoa.ch — C131

## Competitors

**Big 4 Advisory Practices (Deloitte, PwC, KPMG, EY):**
- Strength: Scale, brand recognition, multi-service capability, Geneva presence
- Weakness: Audit conflicts prevent advisory on audit client matters; expensive; impersonal; Big 4 audit relationship creates client discomfort on sensitive topics
- Geneva differentiation: Independent without Big 4 conflicts

**Swiss Private Bank Advisory (Lombard Odier, Pictet, Julius Baer, Vontobel, UBS Geneva, Credit Suisse Geneva):**
- Strength: Geneva brand, relationship depth, product access
- Weakness: Product-conflicted (commissions, FX, lending); cannot be truly independent on regulatory matters; product sales pressure
- Geneva differentiation: Independent advisory without product distribution conflicts

**Geneva Law Firms (Lenz Staehelin, Bär & Kahler, Kellerhals Carrard, Pestalozzi):**
- Strength: Legal expertise, Geneva reputation, regulatory knowledge
- Weakness: Expensive (CHF 600-1,200/hour); too technical for strategic advisory; hourly billing creates scope-expansion incentive
- Geneva differentiation: Strategic advisory at Geneva executive level without hourly billing conflicts

**Independent Boutiques (Strategic Search, Kendo, Geneva-based management consultants):**
- Strength: Independent, Geneva-focused
- Weakness: Often generalist; lack Geneva institutional intelligence depth; limited network
- DigitalCoa.ch differentiation: Geneva institutional intelligence depth + Brotons.net network

## Positioning

DigitalCoa.ch is the Geneva executive's independent strategic advisor — without Big 4 conflicts, without bank product incentives, and with Geneva institutional intelligence depth. The positioning statement for DigitalCoa.ch:

"For Geneva executives who need independent strategic guidance on regulatory complexity, market access, and institutional decisions — without the conflicts of audit firms, banks, or law firms. Geneva-specific. Independent. Advisory fees only."

**Competitive moat:** Brotons.net's Geneva institutional intelligence network and publishing output cannot be replicated by competitors.
