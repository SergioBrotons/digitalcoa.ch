# 02 — Market Signals
## DigitalCoa.ch — C131

## Independent Advisory Market

Geneva independent advisory market is underserved relative to Zurich. Key dynamics:
- Big 4 audit conflicts: Large accounting firms cannot advise their audit clients on Basel IV capital optimization, regulatory change management, or M&A due diligence in areas where they are auditor
- Swiss private bank conflicts: Banks' advisory is product-conflicted (commissions, FX, lending margins)
- Law firm conflicts: Hourly billing model creates scope-expansion incentive; some matters present conflicts
- Independent boutique advisory: Small but growing; Geneva-specific independent advisory is rare

## Peer Accountability Networks

Geneva CEO peer accountability networks (typically 6-10 non-competing CEOs, monthly facilitated sessions) are emerging as a distinct executive development category. Organizations: YPO (Young Presidents' Organization), Geneva CEO Network, Vistage Switzerland. The advisory opportunity: individual DigitalCoa.ch engagements embedded within or adjacent to peer accountability networks.

## Advisory Engagement Economics

2026 Geneva independent senior consultant day rate range: CHF 3,500-5,500/day
2026 Geneva ongoing retainer range: CHF 8,000-15,000/month (20-30 days equivalent)
Project-based engagement range: CHF 25,000-80,000 (3-6 week defined scope)
Entry-level advisory session: CHF 1,800-2,500 (half-day strategic session)

Pricing transparency matters for Geneva executive decision-makers. "Contact for pricing" creates friction. Precision pricing language (without full rate card disclosure) reduces decision friction.

## Post-Briefing Call Conversion

Geneva executives decide on advisory relationships based on trust and perceived Geneva expertise, not RFP processes. The critical conversion moment is the 48 hours post-briefing call. The differentiator is not speed but precision and Geneva-specific follow-up content.
