**Format:** Self-assessment framework + 30-minute advisory call offer (3-4 pages)
**Target:** Geneva CEO, CFO, Managing Director of Geneva SMEs, family businesses, and financial institutions
**Primary CTA:** Complete the Readiness Assessment; book a 30-minute call with a Geneva advisor

**Content Framework:**

1. **Geneva Executive Regulatory Readiness Self-Assessment (scored 1-5):**
   - Basel IV readiness: Capital planning status, FINMA engagement, internal capabilities
   - Sanctions compliance: SECO program coverage, screening systems, training
   - EU-CH contingency: Scenario planning status, decision triggers identified
   - Climate disclosure: TCFD preparation, board oversight documented
   - ICT risk: FINMA circular gap assessment status, third-party register
   - Digital assets: DLT strategy, FINMA authorization readiness

2. **Geneva Regulatory Deadline Calendar (6 months):**
   - Basel IV: Jan 2027 full implementation

   - Sanctions: SECO enforcement ongoing
   - FINMA DLT: Authorization pipeline active

3. **"What Your Peers Are Doing" (brief, non-attributable):**
   - How Geneva CFOs are preparing for Basel IV independently
   - How Geneva CEOs are managing EU-CH contingency planning
   - Why Geneva executives choose independent advisory over Big 4 or bank advisory

4. **Advisory Offer:**
   - "Complete this assessment in 10 minutes and book a 30-minute Geneva advisory call"
   - The call deliverable: specific Geneva regulatory implications for their situation
   - No sales pitch — advisory conversation only
