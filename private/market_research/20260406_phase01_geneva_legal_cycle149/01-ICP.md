# 01 — ICP (Ideal Client Profile) | Geneva Legal | C149 Phase 01

## ICP Segments for Geneva Legal Advisory Services

### Primary ICP: Geneva Large Financial Institutions (100+ employees)
- **Profile:** Swiss-headquartered banks, private wealth managers, asset managers, commodity trading houses with Geneva presence
- **Decision-makers:** General Counsel, Chief Compliance Officer, Chief Risk Officer, Head of Legal Operations
- **Pain points:** Basel IV AI model risk documentation; nFADP Art. 6 cross-border transfer compliance; FINMA enforcement exposure
- **Advisory need:** Regulatory compliance audits, enforcement defense, AI governance frameworks
- **Geneva-specific qualifier:** FINMA-supervised institutions with significant cross-border data flows and AI deployments

### Secondary ICP: Geneva International Commercial Companies
- **Profile:** Multinational corporations headquartered in Geneva or with major Geneva operations; commodity trading firms; watch/industrial exporters
- **Decision-makers:** Chief Legal Officer, General Counsel, Head of International Trade
- **Pain points:** Swiss Climate Due Diligence supply chain obligations; SICC vs. arbitration clause selection; nFADP adequacy for US/Asia data flows
- **Advisory need:** Cross-border contract strategy; Swiss law compliance frameworks; multi-jurisdictional dispute planning
- **Geneva-specific qualifier:** Companies with supply chain exposure + EU market access + Swiss regulatory obligations simultaneously

### Tertiary ICP: Geneva Employers (Large / Institutional)
- **Profile:** Geneva-headquartered large employers using or considering AI tools for recruitment, performance management, workforce analytics
- **Decision-makers:** General Counsel, HR Director, Chief People Officer, Data Protection Officer
- **Pain points:** GIG/LEG algorithmic discrimination exposure; nFADP employee monitoring compliance; no federal AI employment guidance
- **Advisory need:** AI employment tool legal audit; discrimination risk assessment; cantonal equality commissioner engagement
- **Geneva-specific qualifier:** Geneva cantonal equality commissioner (CMSHG) is more active than other cantons — elevated local scrutiny

### Quaternary ICP: Geneva Law Firms (Large Boutiques / International)
- **Profile:** Large Geneva law firms and international firms with Geneva offices; managing partner / risk management committees
- **Decision-makers:** Managing Partner, Risk Management Committee, Head of Professional Responsibility
- **Pain points:** OGE/RAV AI guidance compliance; attorney-client privilege + AI training data tension; AI-generated evidence handling
- **Advisory need:** AI use policy drafting; privilege audit for AI-assisted research; professional responsibility framework
- **Geneva-specific qualifier:** Geneva Bar has specific AI guidance trajectory; OGE issuing multiple guidance documents 2025-2026

## Engagement Model
- Regulatory compliance audit (fixed-fee, 4-8 weeks)
- AI governance framework (retainer, ongoing)
- Enforcement defense / regulatory engagement (hourly, contingent on outcome)
- Training / workshop (per session)

## Buying Signals
- FINMA supervisory examination letter referencing AI or data compliance
- New AI tool deployment in credit/lending/HR/operations
- Cross-border data transfer mapping exercise underway
- Climate disclosure obligations triggered by OCS scope
- Employee discrimination claim or cantonal commissioner inquiry
