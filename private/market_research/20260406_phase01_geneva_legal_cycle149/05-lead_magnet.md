## Lead Magnet: "Geneva AI & Digital Risk Compliance Audit — 6-Point Diagnostic"

### Positioning
A structured diagnostic framework for Geneva-based General Counsel and Chief Compliance Officers to assess their institution's current exposure across the six highest-urgency Geneva legal risk verticals: nFADP data transfers, Basel IV AI model risk, Swiss climate due diligence, SICC dispute planning, digital asset compliance, and AI employment tools.

### Format
- Digital self-assessment framework (PDF + spreadsheet)
- 6 diagnostic sections (one per vertical)
- Each section: 5-10 scored questions; red/amber/green risk rating; recommended next steps
- Executive summary page with prioritized action list
- No personal data required for access; firmographic capture (company type, headcount, sector) for lead routing

### Distribution
- LinkedIn organic post (Brotons.net angle: "Is your Geneva firm ready for these 6 regulatory deadlines?")
- Direct email to Geneva GC/CCO contacts (when authorized)
- Brotons.net thought leadership companion piece

### Conversion Path
Self-assessment → Download → Email nurture sequence → Executive briefing call (DigitalCoa.ch)

### Vertical-Specific Diagnostic Questions (Illustrative)

**nFADP Data Transfers:**
- Does your firm transfer personal data to US-based cloud vendors (AWS, Microsoft, Google Cloud)?
- Has your firm mapped all cross-border data transfer routes in the past 24 months?
- Does your firm have Safeguards+ documentation (SCCs + TIA) for US data transfers?

**Basel IV AI Model Risk:**
- Does your institution use AI/ML models for credit scoring or lending decisions?
- Are AI credit models documented to FINMA's model risk management standards?
- Has independent validation been performed on AI credit models in the past 12 months?

**Swiss Climate Due Diligence:**
- Does your company fall within OCS scope (large Swiss companies, >500 employees)?
- Have you mapped supply chain climate/deforestation risks under EUDR and Art. 964j OR?
- Is your team monitoring the Federal Council SDD implementing ordinance developments?

**SICC Advisory:**
- Are your international commercial contracts currently routed to ICC arbitration or Swiss courts?
- Have you considered SICC as an alternative for mid-value English-language Swiss law disputes?
- Does your legal team have SICC procedure experience?

**Digital Asset Compliance:**
- Does your institution hold or process crypto assets?
- Have smart contracts used in your operations been reviewed for Swiss OR Art. 1 enforceability?
- Are crypto assets held as collateral documented under SchKG Art. 37 requirements?

**AI Employment Tools:**
- Does your firm use AI tools for recruitment, performance evaluation, or workforce management?
- Has your AI HR tool usage been reviewed under GIG/LEG Art. 3 discrimination standards?
- Is there nFADP documentation for AI processing of employee personal data?
