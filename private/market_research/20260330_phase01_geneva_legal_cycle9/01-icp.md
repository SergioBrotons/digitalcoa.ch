# Phase 01 ICP — Geneva Legal / Small Law Firms
## Cycle 9 (2026-03-30)

## Addressable Profile
Small Geneva law firms, 2–5 attorneys; independent boutiques; practice areas: corporate, litigation, family, real estate, estate planning. Managing partner owns technology and compliance decisions. Geneva bar (OFL) membership.

## Addressable Problem
AI tools (legal research, document review, contract analysis, client communication) embedded in law firm workflows — but no dedicated AI governance advisory tier for small firm scale. EU AI Act GPAI obligations live since August 2, 2025. FDPIC enforcement portfolio building. No boutique-tier advisory for the 2–5 attorney segment. Window ~12-18 months before potential competitive entry.

1. **EU AI Act GPAI** — operative since August 2, 2025 (~7.5 months); transparency, copyright, incident documentation obligations active
2. **EU AI Act high-risk** — August 2, 2026 (~15 months; law firms with EU market nexus)
3. **FDJP CoE AI Convention consultation draft** — end-2026 (~9 months; will crystallize standards)
4. **nFADP** — AI processing of client data = Art. 5(c) active; FDPIC enforcement trajectory
5. **FDPIC enforcement gap (POSITIVE SIGNAL):** 4 confirmed AI investigations 2025-2026; law firms NOT yet targeted = timing signal
