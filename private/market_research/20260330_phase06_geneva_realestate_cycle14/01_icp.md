# Phase 06 — ICP: Small Geneva Real Estate Agencies
## Cycle 14 — 2026-03-30

> All findings carry-forward from Cycle 13 (Gemini quota exhausted).

## Core ICP Statement

**Primary:** Small Geneva real estate agencies or property managers (1–20 employees) with:
- AI tools in use: property valuation, tenant screening, marketing, CRM
- 67% AI adoption rate (HIGHEST of any portfolio vertical)
- Personal data processing under nFADP
- No existing AI governance framework

**Trigger:** GAIGI operative ~7–10 months; retroactive compliance framing

---

## Unique Entry Point: Retroactive Compliance
Unlike other verticals (anticipatory framing), RE entry is "govern the AI you already have."
Most RE firms are already using AI — they just don't have a governance framework.

This makes Phase 6 a lower-friction entry: no awareness-selling required, just framework provision.
