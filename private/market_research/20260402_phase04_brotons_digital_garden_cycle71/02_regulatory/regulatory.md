## Swiss AI Regulatory Landscape (for Brotons.net Content)

### Active Regulations
1. **FADP** (Federal Act on Data Protection): Applies directly to AI-based data processing; FDPIC confirmed May 2025
2. **FINMA Guidance 08/2024**: AI governance for financial institutions (December 18, 2024)
3. **Existing frameworks**: ISA, PrSA, PrLA, CO, CC, UCA, CopA — technology-neutral

### Regulatory Pipeline (2026-2027)
1. **Council of Europe AI Convention**: Switzerland signed March 2025; consultation draft for incorporation end 2026
2. **Federal Council AI rules**: Consultation draft by end of 2026
3. **EU AI Act extraterritorial**: August 2026 deadlines apply for Swiss firms with EU users
4. **Sustainable Finance Act**: Anticipated late 2026
