## Findings Unique to This Cycle

### 1. EDÖB: Swiss DSG Directly Applicable to AI (Sep 2025 confirmed) (NET-NEW detail)
- **What:** EDÖB explicit guidance: Swiss Federal Data Protection Act (DSG) applies directly to AI-assisted data processing
- **Source:** https://www.edoeb.admin.ch/de/ki-und-datenschutz (published Sep 24, 2025)
- **Content:** 
  - Transparency obligation: AI systems must disclose purpose, function, data sources
  - Human review right: right to oppose automated decisions or demand human review
  - Intelligent chatbots: must disclose when communicating with a machine
  - Face/body manipulation: must always be clearly disclosed
  - High-risk AI: requires appropriate protective measures
- **Significance:** DSG is technology-neutral; AI operators must build privacy by design; EDÖB enforcement authority confirmed
