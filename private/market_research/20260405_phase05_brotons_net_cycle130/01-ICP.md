# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Brotons.net — ICP

**Primary Audience: Geneva Executive Readers**

**Segment A: Geneva Institutional Leaders**
- C-suite executives (CEO, CFO, CRO) at Geneva financial institutions
- Managing partners of Geneva law firms
- Board members of Geneva companies
- IO/NGO senior management
- UHNW family principals

**Segment B: Geneva Professional Advisors**
- Senior consultants and partners at advisory firms
- Wealth managers and family office directors
- Investment professionals
- Legal and compliance executives

**Segment C: Geneva Emerging Leaders**
- Senior managers and directors being groomed for board roles
- Founders and executives of growth-stage Geneva companies
- Mid-career professionals in transition

**Reading Motivation:**
- Understanding Geneva-specific institutional dynamics
- Strategic interpretation (not raw information)
- Thought leadership and intellectual challenge
- Signal detection in complex regulatory/geopolitical environment
- French-language cultural and business context

**Content Preferences:**
- Long-form analysis and interpretation
- Geneva institutional calendar alignment
- EN primary, FR valuable
- Practical strategic relevance
- Quality over quantity

**Engagement Pattern:**
- Newsletter primary delivery
- LinkedIn discovery secondary
- Search tertiary (article-level SEO)
- Return visits driven by quality + trust

**Language:** EN primary, FR secondary (bilingual value)
**Geography:** Geneva primary, Vaud secondary, CH tertiary, International Geneva
