# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept
## Brotons.net

**Primary Purpose:** Audience development + trust building → warm pathway to DigitalCoa.ch

**Lead Magnet Options:**

**Option A: Geneva Regulatory Intelligence Briefing (Weekly/Quarterly)**
- Format: Executive briefing (PDF or email)
- Content: Top 3-5 Geneva regulatory developments, strategic implications, ahead-of-curve analysis
- CTA: Subscribe + forward to peers
- Differentiation: Independent + interpretive + Geneva-specific

**Option B: Brotons.net Reader Assessment (On-Demand)**
- Format: Self-assessment + consultation offer
- Content: Reader's Geneva strategic positioning, content fit, advisory pathway
- CTA: Advisory consultation
- Purpose: Lead qualification + engagement

**Option C: Institutional Calendar Content Bundle (Quarterly)**
- Format: Curated content package aligned with Geneva institutional calendar
- Content: Pre-event analysis, post-event interpretation
- CTA: Subscribe to calendar-aligned content

**Option D: Geneva Executive Audio Briefings (Newsletter Enhancement)**
- Format: Audio version of key analyses (5-8 min)
- Content: Same as written pieces, voice-delivered
- CTA: Audio subscription option
- Evidence: +30-40% completion rate improvement

**Recommended Primary:** Option A (Regulatory Intelligence Briefing) — pipeline foundation
**Recommended Secondary:** Option D (Audio Briefings) — engagement enhancement

**Distribution:**
- Newsletter (primary): Direct to readers
- LinkedIn (secondary): Organic reach + engagement
- Search (tertiary): Article-level discovery
- EN+FR bilingual: Swiss market differentiation

**Content-to-Client Pathway:**
1. Reader subscribes (newsletter)
2. Quality engagement builds trust
3. Content references advisory services (Brotons.net + DigitalCoa.ch)
4. Reader converts to advisory client
5. Repeat trust cycle
