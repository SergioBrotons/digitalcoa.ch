# 06 — Executive Summary: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## Research Question
What is the addressable consulting market in Geneva driven by regulatory change and business transformation?

## C118 Key Findings

### Finding 1: CSRD Sustainability Reporting Advisory — Demand Surge (NET-NEW C118)
- CSRD applies to Swiss companies with EU operations, EU-listed parents, or large EU subsidiaries
- Geneva companies needing double materiality assessment, ESG data infrastructure, reporting systems
- Advisory supply limited; demand significantly outpacing supply in Geneva
- **Implication:** CSRD implementation advisory = highest-demand, most-undersupplied consulting niche in Geneva

### Finding 2: ESG Advisory for Geneva Financial Institutions (NET-NEW C118)
- SFAMA sustainable finance guidelines requiring ESG integration in investment policy
- Geneva pension funds (caisse de retraite) integrating ESG into investment decisions
- Asset managers needing portfolio alignment analysis against EU taxonomy
- **Implication:** ESG advisory — investment policy review, portfolio alignment, trustee reporting

### Finding 3: Digital Transformation Legacy Modernization (NET-NEW C118)
- Geneva SMEs running legacy ERP and operational systems; security vulnerabilities
- Cybersecurity compliance (NCSC, OFCOM) driving infrastructure upgrade decisions
- DORA and FADP compliance adding complexity to digital transformation projects
- **Implication:** Digital transformation advisory — ERP modernization, cloud migration, cybersecurity compliance

### Finding 4: M&A Due Diligence Pressure — Valuation Adjustment (NET-NEW C118)
- Rising rates compressing multiples; buyers doing deeper due diligence
- Cross-border EU-Swiss M&A requiring regulatory clearance analysis
- Operational due diligence increasingly important post-pandemic
- **Implication:** Due diligence advisory — financial, ESG, operational, regulatory clearance

### Finding 5: Geneva Trade Facilitation — CEVA Corridor and Sanctions Compliance (NET-NEW C118)
- CEVA Logistics Geneva hub; Geneva as trade corridor for EU and global supply chains
- CARB implementation adding customs and supply chain complexity
- Sanctions compliance demand from Geneva commodity traders
- **Implication:** Trade facilitation advisory — customs, sanctions compliance, supply chain finance

### Finding 6: Human Capital / Talent Management Advisory (NET-NEW C118)
- Geneva labour market tightness; retention critical for professional services firms
- Remote work enabling Geneva talent to serve international clients
- Family business succession planning linked to talent retention
- **Implication:** Talent management advisory — retention strategy, succession planning, remote frameworks
