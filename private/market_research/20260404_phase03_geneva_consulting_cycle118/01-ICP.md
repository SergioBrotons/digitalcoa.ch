# 01 — ICP: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## ICP: Geneva Consulting Market

### Primary ICP
**Target:** Geneva-based SMEs and family businesses (CHF 10M-500M revenue)

**Profile:**
- Manufacturing, trading, professional services
- Pain: regulatory change (CSRD, DORA), digital transformation, succession
- Decision-maker: CEO, Owner, CFO
- Geography: Canton of Geneva; some Vaud
- Buying signal: regulatory deadline, ownership transition, growth plateau
- Size: 50-500 employees

### Secondary ICP
**Target:** Geneva headquarters of international companies

**Profile:**
- Regional hub for EMEA; need Geneva-competent advisory
- Pain: local regulatory compliance, Swiss-specific operational issues
- Decision-maker: Regional CFO, General Counsel, MD
- Buying signal: Swiss expansion, regulatory license requirement, compliance issue
- Size: 20-200 employees in Geneva

### Tertiary ICP
**Target:** UN agencies and international organizations (Geneva)

**Profile:**
- Budget pressure post-pandemic; efficiency mandates
- Pain: organizational redesign, digital transformation, results-based management
- Decision-maker: Director of Operations, HR Director, CFO
- Buying signal: budget review, strategic planning cycle
- Size: Variable; mission-specific
