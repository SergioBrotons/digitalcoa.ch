# 05 — Lead Magnet: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## Lead Magnet Strategy

### Primary Lead Magnet: "Geneva Regulatory Change Tracker"
**Format:** Monthly 1-page briefing on regulatory changes affecting Geneva businesses
**Distribution:** Email to CEO/CFO contacts; LinkedIn
**Content:** CSRD, ESG, cybersecurity, sanctions — what changed, what it means, what to do
**CTA:** Subscribe → Calendly discovery call

### Secondary Lead Magnet: "CSRD Readiness Assessment for Geneva SMEs"
**Format:** 10-point checklist + 30-min briefing
**Target:** CFOs, sustainability officers at Geneva SMEs with EU exposure
**CTA:** Download → nurture → discovery call

### Tertiary Lead Magnet: "Family Business Succession Planning Guide"
**Format:** 15-page guide; Geneva-specific; FR + EN versions
**Target:** Owners and CEOs of Geneva family businesses
**CTA:** Download → follow-up call → succession advisory engagement
