# 02 — Market Signals: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## Market Signals (Geneva Consulting, April 2026)

### Signal 1: CSRD Sustainability Reporting Advisory — Demand Surge
- CSRD applies to large Swiss companies with EU operations; voluntary for others
- Geneva companies with EU subsidiaries or EU-listed parent companies in scope
- Double materiality assessment, ESG data collection, sustainability reporting requirements
- **Signal:** CSRD gap analysis and implementation advisory = high demand, short supply
- **Source:** Federal Council CSRD position, EU CSRD directive, Swiss Chamber hints

### Signal 2: ESG Advisory for Geneva Financial Institutions
- SFAMA sustainable finance guidelines requiring ESG integration
- Swiss pension funds (caisse de retraite) integrating ESG into investment policy
- Geneva asset managers needing ESG portfolio alignment analysis
- **Signal:** ESG advisory — investment policy review, portfolio alignment, reporting

### Signal 3: Digital Transformation Legacy Modernization
- Geneva SMEs and industrial companies running legacy ERP, MES, supply chain systems
- CHF strength making offshore IT development attractive but local integration required
- Cybersecurity compliance (OFCIG, NCSC) driving infrastructure upgrades
- **Signal:** Digital transformation advisory — ERP modernization, cloud migration, cybersecurity compliance

### Signal 4: M&A Due Diligence Pressure — Valuation Adjustment
- Rising interest rates affecting deal multiples; buyers demanding more due diligence
- Swiss M&A market active but deal closures taking longer
- Cross-border EU-Swiss M&A requiring regulatory clearance analysis
- **Signal:** Due diligence advisory — financial, operational, ESG, regulatory clearance

### Signal 5: Geneva Trade Facilitation — CEVA Logistics Corridor
- CEVA Logistics (Geneva-based) expanding; Geneva as logistics hub connecting EU and global supply chains
- Customs procedures, trade finance, sanctions compliance for Geneva traders
- Switzerland-EU trade relationship uncertainty driving contingency planning
- **Signal:** Trade facilitation advisory — customs, sanctions compliance, supply chain finance

### Signal 6: Human Capital / Talent Management Advisory
- Labour market tightness in Geneva; retention and succession planning critical
- UHNW family offices competing for private banking talent
- Remote work enabling talent arbitrage — Geneva talent working for international clients
- **Signal:** Talent management advisory — retention strategy, succession planning, remote work frameworks

## Synthesis
Geneva consulting demand driven by:
1. CSRD regulatory change → sustainability reporting advisory
2. ESG integration → investment policy and portfolio advisory
3. Legacy systems → digital transformation
4. M&A market → due diligence support
5. Trade flows → sanctions and customs compliance
6. Labour market → talent and succession advisory
