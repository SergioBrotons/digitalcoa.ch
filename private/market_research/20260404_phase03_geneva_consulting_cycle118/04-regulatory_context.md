# 04 — Regulatory Context: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## Active Regulatory Themes Driving Geneva Consulting Demand

### Theme 1: CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)
- **Status:** EU directive; applies to Swiss companies with EU operations, subsidiaries, or EU-listed parents
- **Timeline:** First reporting cycle 2024-2025; full scope by 2026
- **Key documents:** EU CSRD text, ESRS standards, Federal Council position
- **Advisory angle:** Gap analysis, double materiality assessment, ESG data infrastructure, reporting

### Theme 2: ESG Integration (Swiss Financial Market)
- **Status:** SFAMA guidelines, Swiss pension fund ESG integration mandates
- **Key documents:** SFAMA sustainable finance guidelines, OR 2b, Federal Council action plan
- **Advisory angle:** Investment policy ESG integration, portfolio alignment, trustee reporting

### Theme 3: Digital Transformation + Cybersecurity
- **Status:** NCSC alerts increasing; OFCOM implementing cybersecurity framework
- **Key documents:** NCSC cybersecurity guidelines, OFCOM requirements, DORA
- **Advisory angle:** Security audit, transformation roadmap, DORA compliance

### Theme 4: Cross-Border M&A (EU-Swiss)
- **Status:** Active market; regulatory clearance requirements evolving
- **Key documents:** Cartel Act, foreign investment screening (in force 2023), InstA uncertainty
- **Advisory angle:** Deal structuring, regulatory clearance strategy, integration planning

### Theme 5: Trade Facilitation / Sanctions
- **Status:** SECO sanctions enforcement active; CARB implementation
- **Key documents:** SECO sanctions lists, CARB implementation rules, customs regulations
- **Advisory angle:** Compliance audits, supply chain due diligence, customs optimization

### Theme 6: Family Business Succession
- **Status:** Swiss succession law (OR, ZGB); cantonal variations in Geneva
- **Key documents:** Swiss inheritance law, cantonal succession rules, tax implications
- **Advisory angle:** Succession planning, ownership transition, family governance
