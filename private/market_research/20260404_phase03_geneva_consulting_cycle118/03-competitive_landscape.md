# 03 — Competitive Landscape: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting (C118)

## Competitive Set: Geneva Consulting

### Tier 1 — Global Consulting Firms (Geneva offices)
**McKinsey, BCG, Bain, Accenture, Deloitte, PwC**

- Strengths: brand, resources, global frameworks, case references
- Weakness: Geneva is secondary market; expensive; generic solutions
- **Threat:** Positioning as premium Geneva advisory despite not being Geneva-specialist

### Tier 2 — Swiss Boutiques
**Institut de Management de l'IMD,开,treprise Suisses**

- Strengths: Swiss market knowledge, language capability, relationships
- Weakness: Limited international reach, small scale, no specialized regulatory practice
- **Opportunity:** Specialist regulatory advisory as differentiator

### Tier 3 — Specialized Boutiques
**Sustainability firms, HR boutiques, digital transformation specialists**

- Highly specialized but narrow
- **Opportunity:** Generalist-regulatory advisory with sustainability, ESG, and transformation angles

## Underserved Niches (Differentiation Opportunities)
1. **CSRD implementation for Geneva SMEs with EU exposure** — highly underserved
2. **ESG integration for Geneva pension funds and asset managers** — growing demand
3. **Digital transformation with Swiss regulatory compliance** — cybersecurity + DORA + FADP
4. **Cross-border M&A advisory (EU-Swiss)** — complex, few specialists
5. **Trade facilitation + sanctions compliance** — niche of one
6. **Family business succession advisory** — Geneva SME demand, underserved

## Positioning Recommendation
**"The Geneva consulting firm for regulatory-driven transformation"**

- Not another strategy consultancy
- Not another IT implementation firm
- Specializes in: regulatory change → business transformation → measurable outcome
- Sector depth: Geneva financial institutions, family businesses, NGOs
