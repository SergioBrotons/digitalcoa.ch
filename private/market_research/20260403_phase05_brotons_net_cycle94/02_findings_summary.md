### Brotons.net Role in Ecosystem
Brotons.net functions as a business card / hub — not a content platform. Its purpose is to:
1. Establish credibility (lecturer role)
2. Route visitors to the three properties based on intent (advisory → DigitalCoa.ch, implementation → Cytria, writing → Substack)
3. Signal multilingual capability (EN/FR/ES)

This is a deliberate strategy, not a deficiency, if the goal is lead routing rather than content discovery.
