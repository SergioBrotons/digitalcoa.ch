## Competitive Dynamics (C124 Update)

### New Entrants / Shifts
1. **Supply chain due diligence boutiques** — new firms emerging focused on LkSG/Swiss supply chain compliance (Swiss Supply Chain Association forming)
2. **Geopolitical risk advisory** — Verisk, Marsh, Control Risks growing Geneva presence; geopolitical risk demand
3. **Finance function outsourcing (FPO)** — CFO-as-a-service providers multiplying; Baker Tilly, RSM expanding FPO services
4. **Digitalization consulting** — startups (Helion, Bluenove) positioning for SME digitalization; Swiss-wide
5. **Gender quota governance advisory** — Ethos (Geneva) and institutional advisory firms positioning; emerging market

### Positioning Gaps
- **SME productivity advisory** — Big 4 too expensive; accountants lack strategy skills; gap between CHF 30-150K advisory
- **Switzerland-EU institutional agreement advisory** — almost no one offering Swiss SME-focused advisory on this yet; wide open
- **LkSG Swiss alignment advisory** — German consultancies serving Swiss subsidiaries; limited Swiss-focused options
- **Geopolitical risk for SMEs** — Control Risks/Marsh too expensive; gap for mid-market
- **FPO for CHF 20-100M revenue** — too small for Big 4, too complex for accountancy firms

## Geneva ICP — Pain Point Alignment

### Geneva SME CHF 10-100M Revenue
- Digitalization gap: clear need, budget limited
- Switzerland-EU: generally unaware, need education first
- LkSG: less relevant for smaller SMEs; medium-large more affected
- Gender quota: not applicable (not listed)
- Geopolitical risk: minimal for small SMEs
- Interim CFO: strong fit for CHF 50-100M revenue

### Second-Tier Listed Geneva Companies
- Digitalization: not primary concern
- Switzerland-EU: moderate relevance for EU-exposed companies
- LkSG: depending on supply chain complexity
- Gender quota: immediate deadline; advisory need NOW
- Geopolitical risk: for internationally-exposed companies
- Interim CFO: less relevant (usually have full-time CFO)

### Family-Owned Mid-Market Businesses
- Digitalization: medium relevance
- Switzerland-EU: strong for export-oriented families
- LkSG: strong for commodity traders, manufacturers
- Gender quota: not applicable unless listed
- Geopolitical risk: strong for families with international operations
- Interim CFO: strong fit for succession period
