# Phase 03: Geneva Consulting — Lead Magnet Framework
## Cycle 35 | 2026-03-31

## Lead Magnet: "The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"
## 9-Section Framework

---

### Decision 0: Shadow AI Audit (Primary Entry)
What AI tools are your consultants already using with client data?
Shadow AI = FDPIC exposure + client contract exposure + GPAI copyright exposure.
"You've been using AI. Your clients haven't necessarily authorized its use on their matters."

### Section 1: GPAI Already Active — 8+ Months
GPAI transparency obligations have been operative since Aug 2, 2025. If your firm uses AI systems that interact with EU clients or process data with EU nexus, GPAI obligations are already binding. No grace period.

### Section 2: 43% NO Formal AI Policy (Primary Urgency)
Wolters Kluwer March 2026: 43% of consulting firms have NO formal AI policy. Combined with GPAI already active, this is a compounding risk.

### Section 3: 40% AI Skills Gap = Advisory Opportunity
40% of Geneva SMEs lack AI skills (PwC 2024). 73% abandoned AI projects due to skills gap. The gap creates demand for both governance AND implementation support.

### Section 4: Dual Compliance — GPAI + FADP
Consulting firms with EU nexus face both GPAI (EU AI Act) and FADP (Swiss) obligations simultaneously. A governance framework must address both.

### Section 5: 65% Efficiency Gain (NEW Phase 01 Cycle 35)
65% of firms save 1-5h/week with generative AI. 40% workflow reduction potential. ROI is proven — governance enables capture.

### Section 6: Data Residency for Client Data
Consulting firms handling client data need Swiss or EU-hosted AI solutions. CASUS zero data retention (Phase 01 NEW) = one validated option.

### Section 7: Andermatt May 26 — ~8 Weeks
Federal Council First Draft + FDPIC conference. Next external pressure event. Firms not already working on AI governance will feel the pressure.

### Section 8: First-Mover Window
~12-18 months before GPAI enforcement and Federal Council action make AI governance standard. GPAI already 8+ months live. Window is closing.
