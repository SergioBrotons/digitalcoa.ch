**Hook:** Patient data is sensitive personal data under Swiss law. Any AI tool processing patient records, scheduling, or communication = nFADP Art. 5(c) obligations. FDPIC has confirmed: healthcare is a priority enforcement sector.

- nFADP operative since September 1, 2023 — 2.5 years of non-compliance
- FDPIC 2024/2025 Annual Report (July 2025): healthcare + legal = FDPIC priority enforcement

- Only 42% of medical groups have formal AI governance policies (MGMA Jan 2026) — 58% have NOTHING

**DPIA requirement (Art. 22 nFADP):** Before using any AI system that processes patient data, a Data Protection Impact Assessment is mandatory. Most small practices have zero process for this.
