# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva NGO/Tech Sector

## EN Version

### Primary Concept: "Geneva Humanitarian Tech Monitor"

**Format:** Quarterly technology and governance briefing (2-3 pages)
**Distribution:** LinkedIn (Geneva IO/NGO executives, tech directors), Geneva humanitarian tech network, ICRC/UNHCR professional circles
**Content focus:**
- AI governance developments affecting Geneva UN/IO
- Digital transformation opportunities (ICRC, UNHCR programs)
- Geneva tech ecosystem updates
- One featured deep-dive on humanitarian technology

**Sample topics (C123 cycle):**
1. ITU AI governance principles — what Geneva IO needs to implement
2. ICRC digital supply chain modernization — partnership opportunity for Geneva tech
3. UNHCR biometric expansion — privacy governance framework requirements

**CTA:** "Developing AI governance for your organization? DigitalCoa.ch advisory workshop."
