# Phase 02 — Geneva Finance/SMB — Regulatory Context (Cycle 23)
**Vertical:** Boutique wealth management (Geneva)
**Cycle:** 23 | Date: 2026-03-30

## Regulatory Context

### Active Now
- **CARF/CRS 2.0:** operative since Jan 1 2026 (~3 months already live)
- **FINMA Circ. 08/2024:** baseline for AI use in financial services
- **FINMA Circ. 2026/1:** nature risks (Jan 1 2026)
- **nFADP Art. 5(c):** active NOW

### Near-Term (2026)
- **AMLA/LETA:** H2 2026 ~3–6 months (consultation concluded Jan 30, 2026)
- **30-day discrepancy reporting** to Transparency Register (LETA obligation)
- **SRO membership:** triggering activities (real estate transactions, non-operational entities, domicile services)
- **Swissdamed Jul 1 2026:** ~13 WEEKS (spillover for managers with healthcare clients)

### Medium-Term (end-2026+)
- **FATF mutual evaluation:** end-2026 or 2027-2028 (external pressure event)
- **Federal Council First Draft May 2026:** AI Action Plan milestone

### 3-Layer Compliance Stack
1. **Swiss domestic:** FINMA Circ. 08/2024, FINMA Circ. 2026/1, SRO rules
2. **Swiss international:** CARF/CRS 2.0 (live Jan 1 2026), AMLA, LETA, FATF
3. **EU extraterritorial:** EU AI Act (if EU-nexus), DORA

### Agentic AI 2026
- Autonomous AI agents creating new compliance risk category
- Not yet explicitly addressed by FINMA or CARF
- Emerging risk for WM firms using automated portfolio management AI
