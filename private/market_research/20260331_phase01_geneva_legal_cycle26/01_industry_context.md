# Phase 01 — Geneva Legal: Industry Context
## Cycle 26 | 2026-03-31

## ICP (Ideal Client Profile)
Small law firms in Geneva (2–20 attorneys)
- Using AI tools without formal governance policies
- Exposed to FADP Art. 5(c) obligations NOW
- No formal AI training or incident protocols
- Shadow AI present (employees using unapproved tools)
- Managing director faces personal criminal liability for intentional FADP failures

## Regulatory Baseline (Carry-Forward + Cycle 26 Additions)
- FADP: Directly applicable to AI since Sep 1, 2023 (technology-neutral)
- FDPIC May 8, 2025: explicit confirmation FADP applies to AI-based data processing
- FDPIC increased enforcement resources ~30% in 2025
- FDPIC Feb 23, 2026: joint statement on AI-generated images (60 DPAs) — child protection, erasure, transparency
- SBA AI Guidelines Jun 2024: non-binding baseline for Swiss lawyers
- ANBLES AI Working Group Report Feb 2026: primary source confirmed
- Federal Council: sector-specific approach, no cross-sector AI law before end-2026
- Federal Council second consultation underway (Cycle 24)
- CoE AI Convention signed March 2025; FDJP consultation draft end-2026
- EU AI Act: extraterritorial reach for Swiss firms with EU exposure; high-risk Feb 2, 2025; broad Aug 2, 2026
