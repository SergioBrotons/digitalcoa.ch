## Lead Magnet Framework (9-Section)

- 61% European law firm AI adoption; fewer have formal AI governance policies
- Shadow AI: employees using unapproved tools = FDPIC + professional liability exposure
- DPIA required for high-risk AI: contract analysis and due diligence likely qualify
- "Governance gap" widening confirmed (10th+ cycle)

### 2. What AI Tools Is Your Firm Actually Using?
- Shadow AI audit: document AI tools in use (approved + unapproved)
- FADP Art. 5(c): transparency obligations for automated data processing
- Privacy by Design: assess AI tools BEFORE procurement, not after deployment
- Due diligence chain: who is using what, with what data, on whose behalf

- Human oversight: AI assists, not replaces, professional judgment

- Legal sector ABSENT from FDPIC enforcement (1,053 complaints; 6 actions — transport, banking, retail, social media)

**Decision 0: Shadow AI Audit** (entry point)

**Decision 1: FADP Compliance Gap Assessment**

**Decision 2: Governance Framework Adoption**

- Training and incident reporting protocols
- Ongoing monitoring and annual review
