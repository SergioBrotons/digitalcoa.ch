# Phase 01 — Geneva Legal: Regulatory Context
## Cycle 26 | 2026-03-31

## Active Now (Enforceable)
- **FADP (Sep 1, 2023)**: Technology-neutral; directly applies to AI-based data processing
- **FDPIC May 8, 2025**: Explicit confirmation — AI-supported data processing subject to FADP
- **FADP Art. 5(c)**: Transparency for automated data processing; documentation obligations
- **FDPIC Feb 23, 2026**: Joint statement AI-generated images (60 DPAs) — transparency, erasure, child protection

## Enforcement Reality
- FDPIC: 1,053 complaints (2024/2025); legal sector ABSENT from enforcement actions
- FDPIC 6 enforcement actions: transport x2, banking, retail x2, social media
- Law firms NOT yet targeted: timing signal, not absence of risk
- FDPIC resources +30% (2025): enforcement capacity significantly increased
- Managing director criminal liability CHF 250,000 for intentional FADP failures

## Active Obligations for Law Firms
- Transparency: disclose AI use; inform clients when AI-assisted
- Privacy by Design/Default: assess AI tools before procurement
- DPIA: required for high-risk AI processing (contract analysis, due diligence = high-risk)
- Data processing agreements: required with third-party AI platforms
- Incident response: AI failures = FADP breach obligations
