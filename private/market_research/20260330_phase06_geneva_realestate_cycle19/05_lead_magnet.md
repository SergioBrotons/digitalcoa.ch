# The Small Real Estate Agency AI Governance Question
### A 7-Section Framework for Geneva Real Estate Agencies and Property Managers

---

## The Situation

67% of Swiss real estate companies are already using AI.

That's the highest AI adoption rate of any sector in the DigitalCoa.ch portfolio. Real estate agencies are using AI for:
- Property valuation and price prediction
- Tenant screening and matching
- Marketing content generation
- Customer service chatbots
- Maintenance prediction
- Contract analysis

The question isn't whether Geneva's real estate agencies are using AI. The question is whether they're governing it.

GAIGI (Geneva Association of Real Estate Professionals) has been operative since Fall 2025 — approximately 7-10 months. But GAIGI's institutional content (gaigi.ch) remains inaccessible to small agencies. The category awareness exists; the practical governance guidance does not.

This framework is for the Geneva real estate agency that wants to understand: what AI tools am I using, what are my obligations, and how do I govern AI I already have?

---

## 7-Section Framework

### Section 1: The AI Reality Check — Geneva Real Estate

67% AI adoption rate means the majority of Geneva real estate agencies are already in the AI governance gap. The minority that acts first will own the market positioning.

### Section 2: Q1 — What AI Are You Using?

Inventory: property valuation AI, tenant screening AI, marketing AI, chatbots, maintenance AI, contract analysis AI.

### Section 3: Q2 — What Data Is Being Processed?

Client data, tenant data, property data, financial data. AI processing = nFADP Art. 5(c) obligations.

### Section 4: Q3 — Do You Have nFADP Obligations?

Active since September 1, 2023. Most RE agencies not compliant with AI transparency obligations.

### Section 5: The Human Accountability Principle

AI assists decisions; humans remain accountable. RE agency principal is the accountable decision-maker under nFADP.

### Section 6: Compliance Timeline

- NOW: nFADP Art. 5(c) AI transparency (active since Sep 2023)
- ~13 WEEKS: Swissdamed spillover (if healthcare/elderly properties)
- ~4.5 MONTHS: EU AI Act high-risk (if EU nexus)
- ~7-10 MONTHS: GAIGI guidance evolution

### Section 7: Retroactive Compliance Path

The unique framing for real estate: you don't need to prepare for AI regulation that's coming. You need to govern the AI you're already using.

**Step 1:** AI inventory (what tools, what data)
**Step 2:** AI disclosure to clients (nFADP Art. 5(c))
**Step 3:** Internal AI use policy (approved tools, prohibited uses)
**Step 4:** Annual review
