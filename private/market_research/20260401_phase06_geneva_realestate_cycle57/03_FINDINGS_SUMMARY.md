## NET-NEW C57 Findings

1. **Foreign property purchase restrictions**: Swissinfo (April 2026) confirms restrictions are coming. Geneva luxury market shrinks. Agencies serving international HNWI buyers face reduced addressable market.

2. **G7 Évian**: Near-term transaction uncertainty for Geneva international property market. Security period may affect viewings, due diligence timelines, and international client data flows.

3. **Federal AI Ordinance 15+ months operative**: Most acute compliance gap in real estate. Not just belief-action gap — actual regulatory non-compliance.

4. **Labor shortage dual-purpose**: AI adoption in real estate serves compliance AND addresses agent shortage. Agencies must do both.
