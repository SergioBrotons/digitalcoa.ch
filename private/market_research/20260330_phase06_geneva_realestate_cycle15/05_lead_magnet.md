## Section 3: What You're Already Legally Obligated to Do

### nFADP Art. 5(c) — Active Since 2023

If your AI makes decisions about individuals based solely on automated processing — tenant selection, property recommendations, pricing decisions — you are already in violation without a documented DPIA and human oversight mechanism.

**Three things that apply to your AI right now:**
1. **Tenant screening AI**: automated decisions about individuals require DPIA + human review option
2. **Property matching AI**: automated recommendations based on personal data trigger nFADP Art. 5(c)
3. **Pricing algorithms**: if they affect individual decisions (offers, negotiations), DPIA required

**The enforcement signal:**
FDPIC has 4 AI investigations active (2025–2026). Real estate is a visible sector with client data, automated decisions, and cross-border elements.
