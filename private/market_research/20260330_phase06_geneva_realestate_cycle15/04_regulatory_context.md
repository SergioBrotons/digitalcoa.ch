# Phase 06: Geneva Real Estate — Regulatory Context
# Cycle 15 — 2026-03-30

## Regulatory Context

### Active Now (Operative)
1. **nFADP Art. 5(c)** — ACTIVE since 2023
   → AI-based automated decisions about individuals (tenants, buyers, sellers)
   → Tenant screening AI: automated decision-making requires DPIA + human review
   → Property matching AI: automated recommendations require same

2. **GAIGI** — ~7–10 months operative (Fall 2025 + late March 2026)
   → Geneva Real Estate Industry Association AI governance framework
   → Category awareness increasing; institutional legitimacy
   → Not SMB-specific advisory, but signals AI governance as legitimate category

3. **EU AI Act GPAI** — ACTIVE since Aug 2 2025 (~8.5 months)
   → EU nexus RE agencies: transparency obligations active

### Approaching Deadlines
4. **Swissdamed** — Jul 1 2026 (~12 weeks; no grace period)
   → Spillover into RE professional services
   → Property management AI with diagnostic elements: possible Swissdamed obligations

5. **EU AI Act high-risk** — Aug 2 2026 (~4 months)
   → High-risk AI in RE (if AI affects housing/essential services decisions)
   → EU nexus RE agencies face additional obligations

6. **Federal Council First Draft** — May 2026 (~2 months)
   → External pressure event for all Geneva professional services
   → RE firms: AI governance questions from clients/boards

### Retroactive Compliance Framing
- RE firms already using AI (67%) without governance framework
- Gap is current, not future
- Entry: "You're already using AI for tenant screening/property matching. Do you know what obligations that creates under nFADP Art. 5(c)?"
- Lower friction than anticipatory: no need to convince RE firm AI is coming
