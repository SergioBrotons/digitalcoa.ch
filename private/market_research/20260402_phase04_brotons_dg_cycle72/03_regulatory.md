# 03 — Brotons.net: Regulatory & Compliance Framework — C72 Update

## International AI Governance Frameworks

### UN AI Governance
- **UN General Assembly AI Resolutions**: 79/325 (June 2025) and others
- **UN Global Dialogue**: July 6–7, 2026, Palexpo Geneva — frontier technologies
- **ITU AI Events**: Geneva (confirmed C71)
- **GPAI**: Global Partnership on AI — OECD framework; Swiss participation

### Council of Europe AI Convention
- **Status**: Consultation draft end 2026 for Swiss incorporation
- **Framework**: Legally binding AI treaty; human rights focus
- **Switzerland**: Active in consultation process
- **Brotons.net opportunity**: First Swiss publication covering CoE AI Convention implications

### UNCITRAL Electronic Awards + AI
- **June–July 2026**: Finalizing texts on electronic arbitral award recognition
- **AI dimension**: AI-generated/assisted awards; authentication; enforceability
- **Geneva implication**: Swiss-seated arbitrations affected

### US — Switzerland AI Nexus
- **Section 301**: US trade tool affecting Swiss AI exports
- **GENIUS Act**: US stablecoin law — Swiss regulatory competitive pressure (Phase 02 C72)
- **AI trade**: Swiss AI services to US markets

## Swiss Domestic AI Regulation

### FINMA AI Guidance 08/2024
- **Status**: In force; Federal Council rules end 2026
- **Requirements**: Concrete governance; AI risk assessments; model governance; human oversight
- **Scope**: Banks, insurers, asset managers, OTFs
- **Geneva implication**: Major private banks and wealth managers affected

### Swiss FADP (Data Protection)
- **In force**: March 1, 2024 (aligned with GDPR)
- **AI dimension**: AI bias, transparency, automated decision-making rights
- **FDPIC enforcement**: AI cases under FADP

### Nature Finance + AI Compliance
- **SBA/BCG/Quantis**: Nature transition financing CHF 5.3B/year
- **AI angle**: AI tools for nature impact measurement, reporting, verification
- **Emerging compliance**: No specific framework yet; first-mover opportunity for analysis
