## NET-NEW Findings (C78 vs C77)

### 1. EDÖB AI Guidance — Full Text Analysis [NET-NEW Detail]
- **Source**: EDÖB (Swiss Federal Data Protection Commissioner) — edoeb.admin.ch/de/ki-und-datenschutz
- **Published**: September 24, 2025 (confirmed date)
- **Key confirmation**: Swiss DSG (Federal Data Protection Act) is technology-neutral → directly applicable to AI-supported data processing
- **Three core obligations**:
  1. **Transparenz (Transparency)**: Manufacturers, providers, users must disclose purpose, functionality, data sources of AI systems. Users of AI chatbots must disclose whether communicating with a machine. Right to object to automated data processing or demand human review of automated decisions.
  2. **Schutz der Grundrechte (Fundamental Rights Protection)**: High-risk AI processing requires DSFA (Datenschutz-Folgenabschätzung). Applications undermining DSG-protected privacy and informational self-determination are prohibited. Specifically prohibited: real-time facial recognition in public spaces, comprehensive life surveillance, Social Scoring.
  3. **Human Review**: Right to have automated decisions reviewed by a human — applies to both AI chatbots and automated individual decisions

### 2. EDÖB X/Grok Investigation — Closed [NET-NEW]
- **Date**: March 20, 2025 — EDÖB closes preliminary investigation
- **Issue**: X/Twitter using public posts to train AI Grok
- **Outcome**: Users can object to use of their public posts for AI training
- **Implication**: First EDÖB AI enforcement action — establishes precedent for AI training data rights

## EDÖB AI Guidance — Specific Prohibitions (Authoritative)
The following AI applications are explicitly prohibited under Swiss DSG:
1. **Real-time facial recognition in public spaces** (comprehensive biometric surveillance)
2. **Comprehensive life surveillance and scoring** (Social Scoring)
3. **Any application designed to undermine informational self-determination**

This creates a clear red line for Swiss AI adopters and is a direct executive communication opportunity for DigitalCoa.ch.
