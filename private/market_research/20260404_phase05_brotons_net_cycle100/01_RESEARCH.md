## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 1)

### EN
**Geneva Institutional Audience Content Map — Brotons.net Positioning**

Geneva institutional audience — content consumption patterns:
- Graduate Institute (IHEID) alumni: 10,000+ alumni globally; strong in Geneva, Brussels, Paris, NYC; alumni in international organizations, NGOs, governments, private sector; content consumption: academic-policy crossover pieces, executive education, events; LinkedIn as primary platform; IHEID alumni newsletter (bimonthly); Geneva International Film Festival side events
- CCIG (Geneva Chamber of Commerce): 3,000+ member companies; content needs: practical business intelligence (regulation, trade, talent), networking events, advocacy updates; CCIG publishes weekly newsletter, organizes events (Rencontres économiques); members are SMEs, multinationals, trading houses
- SFAMA (Swiss Fund and Asset Management): institutional investors, asset managers, pension funds; content needs: regulatory updates (FINMA, ESMA), industry data, best practice; SFAMA publishes position papers, quarterly data; members highly engaged with LinkedIn and specialized financial press (Fundana, Funds Magazine)
- Geneva international organization professionals (WTO, WHO, UNOG,ILO): content consumption: policy analysis, geopolitical risk, trade and health policy; LinkedIn dominant; internal knowledge management systems; limited time for external content
- Content consumption timing: Geneva professional audience most active on LinkedIn: Tuesday-Thursday 7-9am and 5-7pm; lower engagement Fridays and Mondays; IHEID alumni more active mid-week; CCIG members more active early week
- Newsletter engagement: open rates for Geneva B2B newsletters 25-35% (industry avg 20-25%); subject line framing critical for Geneva audience (prefer understated, substantive over clickbait)
- Where they get information: LinkedIn (primary discovery), direct email newsletters, Geneva-specific media (Le Temps, Tribune de Genève business, Bilan), Geneva business events
- Brotons.net positioning opportunity: serve as the English-language intelligence layer between Geneva institutional activity and global audience; IHEID/CCIG content bridge; SFAMA regulatory content in accessible format; Geneva multilateral intelligence

- Professionnels des organisations internationales genevoises (OMC, OMS, UNOG, OIT) : consommation : analyse politique, risque géopolitique, politique commerciale et sanitaire ; LinkedIn dominant ; temps limité pour contenu externe
