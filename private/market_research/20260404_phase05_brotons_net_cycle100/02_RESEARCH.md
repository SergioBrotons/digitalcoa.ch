## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 2)

### EN
**SEO Landscape for Geneva Thought Leadership — White Space Analysis**

SEO competitive landscape — Geneva thought leadership keywords:
- High competition (dominated by established players): "Geneva business news" (Le Temps, Bilan, Reuters Geneva), "Swiss financial regulation" (FINMA, Swissbanking.org), "Geneva trade" (WTO, ITC)
- Medium competition (some opportunity): "Swiss arbitration" (swissarbitration.org, chambers guides), "Geneva compliance consulting" (consultancy firms), "Geneva private banking" (banks, wealth managers)
- Low competition / white space (Brotons.net opportunity):
  - "Geneva regulatory intelligence" — no dedicated English-language aggregator; regulatory developments across FINMA/SECO/WTO/SN B scattered across multiple sources
  - "Swiss corporate governance" (mHRDD, CSRD) — low quality content from corporates; Brotons.net could own this
  - "Geneva trade policy analysis" — WTO publications are dense; accessible English-language analysis gap
  - "Swiss sanctions compliance" (Russia/SE CO) — specialized, low competition; growing with every new sanction package
  - "Geneva multilateral diplomacy" — very few English-language blogs; Graduate Institute journals are academic; Brotons.net could bridge academic-practitioner gap
  - "Swiss fund governance" (BVG, institutional investing) — institutional investor audience with limited accessible content
  - "DORA Switzerland financial institutions" — new (2025); low content volume; high value audience
  - "Swiss sustainable finance" — crowded but low quality; Brotons.net with rigorous analysis could differentiate
  - "Geneva economic intelligence" — generic but valuable; could become Brotons.net flagship keyword cluster
- Long-tail opportunity clusters: "what does DORA mean for Swiss banks", "Swiss arbitration vs. ICC", "Geneva commodity trading regulatory environment", "mHRDD Switzerland board obligations", "Swiss company ESG reporting CSRD"
- Technical SEO observations: Brotons.net likely has domain authority advantage over most Geneva corporate blogs; main SEO gap is content volume and internal linking structure
- Content format advantage: Brotons.net's longer-form analytical pieces (vs. news articles) align with Google's helpful content update preference for comprehensive, expert content
