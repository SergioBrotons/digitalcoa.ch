## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 3)

### EN
**Brotons.net Email List Growth — Lead Magnet and Conversion Strategy**

Email list growth for B2B thought leadership — Geneva context:
- Lead magnet formats that work for Geneva institutional audience:
  1. **Weekly Geneva Regulatory Radar** (PDF or brief): curated regulatory developments of past 7 days; format: 5 bullet points + 1 paragraph deep dive; 85% open rate benchmark for high-quality regulatory newsletters
  2. **Geneva Compliance Calendar**: annual calendar of regulatory deadlines, submissions, consultations; highly practical; low churn
  3. **"Geneva CFO Brief"**: monthly PDF briefing on Swiss finance/regulatory developments; targets CFO/finance director level — high-value audience
  4. **Deep-dive research reports**: original analysis (15-30 pages) on specific topics (e.g., "DORA for Swiss Asset Managers", "Swiss Succession Law for UHNWI"); gated content requiring email
  5. **Event-triggered alerts**: WTO ministerial conference summaries, FINMA enforcement round-ups, sanctions package analyses — timely, high engagement
- Email capture conversion benchmarks: B2B thought leadership site average 1-3% email capture rate from content views; curated regulatory content sites 4-6% (higher because audience has acute pain point)
- Best placement for email capture: mid-article inline CTA (not just sidebar/popup); after article conclusion before related posts; exit-intent popup on mobile
- Newsletter frequency vs. churn: Geneva B2B audience prefers substance over frequency; 1-2x/week is the sweet spot for high-value regulatory content; daily news-type emails have higher long-term churn
- Engagement optimization: personalized subject lines ("Your weekly Geneva regulatory brief"), concise preview copy, clear value proposition in every email, mobile-first design (60%+ opens on mobile)
- List segmentation opportunity: Brotons.net could segment by interest (trade policy, finance regulation, Geneva institutional) for targeted follow-up; enables DigitalCoa.ch lead qualification
- Lead scoring: email engagement (opens, clicks) as warm lead indicator for DigitalCoa.ch outreach; 3+ opens of DORA/Swiss sanctions content = qualified DigitalCoa.ch prospect
- Substack integration: if Brotons.net uses Substack for distribution, Substack's native discovery tools (notes, comments) can augment email list growth; but email list remains owned asset
