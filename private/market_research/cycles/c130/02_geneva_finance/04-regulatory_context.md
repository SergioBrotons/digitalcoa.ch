# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Finance

## Key Regulatory Frameworks

### 1. SNB Monetary Policy + Bond Program
- **SNB duration-targeted bond reduction** — progressively removing long-duration CHF instruments
- **Impact:** Life insurers losing CHF-matching assets; CHF 40-60B problem
- **Implication:** Alternative liability-matching advisory

### 2. FINMA BVV 49 / CAAM Capital Framework
- **BVV 49** — Swiss occupational pension fund investment regulations
- **CAAM** — FINMA's capital adequacy framework for banks
- **OTC derivatives** — capital treatment for commodity hedging derivatives
- **Financialization of physical commodities** — reduces capital requirements but creates margining exposure
- **Implication:** Commodity trading financialization advisory

### 3. FINMA TCFD-Aligned Climate Disclosures (Q4 2025)
- **TCFD framework** — climate-related financial disclosures
- **SFDR Art. 7-8** — principal adverse impact indicators
- **Scope 3 financed emissions** — required for first time for private banks serving UHNW
- **Non-reporting holding structures** — conflict with disclosure requirements
- **12-month adjustment period** — before enforcement
- **Implication:** Financed emissions methodology consulting

### 4. FINMA Circular 2026/1 — Nature-Related Financial Risks
- **In force:** January 1, 2026 (staged)
- **Mandatory for:** Banks and insurers
- **Integration requirements:** Governance, strategy, risk management
- **Self-regulatory provisions:** AMAS, SBA, SIA
- **Implication:** ESG/nature risk integration advisory

### 5. Swiss Withholding Tax Reform
- **Current:** 35% withholding tax on Swiss domestic bonds
- **Proposal:** Eliminate for qualified foreign investors
- **Impact:** CHF 50-80B foreign capital inflows if enacted
- **Legislative calendar:** Earliest 2027
- **Implication:** Pre-positioning impact assessment advisory

### 6. FX Hedging + Margin Requirements
- **CHF/USD post-tariff dynamics** — CHF strengthening compressing margins
- **FX forward margining** — daily mark-to-market cash flow impact
- **Structured FX products** — combining rate protection with margin facilities
- **Implication:** Structured FX hedging advisory for Geneva importers

### 7. Real Estate Fund Regulations
- **BVG/BVV 2** — occupational pension fund investment in real estate
- **Construction cost inflation** — compressing projected net returns
- **Fund covenant renegotiation** — development agreements with contractors
- **Implication:** Construction arbitration + covenant renegotiation advisory

### 8. Commodity Trading Regulatory Environment
- **FINMA supervision** — commodity trading houses under FINMA oversight
- **OTC derivative reporting** — EMIR-equivalent Swiss reporting obligations
- **Margining requirements** — uncleared OTC derivatives margin rules
- **Implication:** Margining risk management framework
