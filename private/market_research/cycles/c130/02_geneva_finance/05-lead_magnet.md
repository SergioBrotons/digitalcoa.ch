# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Finance Regulatory + Risk Intelligence

## Lead Magnet Concept: "Geneva Finance Risk & Regulatory Briefing"
Bi-monthly briefing covering 6-8 regulatory and market developments for Geneva financial institutions.

## Format
- 2-page PDF briefing (EN + FR parallel)
- Distributed via LinkedIn + direct email to Geneva CFOs, CROs, Risk Managers
- Gate: email capture via DigitalCoa.ch landing page

## Content Structure
1. **Regulatory Radar** — FINMA/SECO/SNB developments
2. **Market Stress Monitor** — CHF/USD, interest rates, margin conditions
3. **Disclosure Watch** — TCFD/SFDR/FINMA climate disclosure guidance
4. **Structured Product Spotlight** — FX hedging, duration matching, margining
5. **Action Items** — what to do this quarter

## C130 Lead Magnet Angles

### Angle 1: Life Insurance Duration Crisis
"The CHF 40-60 Billion Duration Matching Problem"
- SNB bond reduction removing long-duration CHF assets
- Life insurers forced into lower-rated alternatives
- Advisory need: alternative liability-matching strategy framework

### Angle 2: FINMA Scope 3 Financed Emissions
"FINMA's 12-Month Window for UHNW Financed Emissions Disclosure"
- Q4 2025 FINMA guidance requires Scope 3 financed emissions
- Non-reporting holding structures conflict
- Advisory need: financed emissions methodology + holding structure disclosure mapping

### Angle 3: Commodity Margining Risk
"Margining Calls Are Not Just a Back-Office Problem"
- Financialized hedging books creating margin calls
- Controllers lack internal expertise
- Advisory need: margining risk framework + stress testing methodology

## Distribution Channels
- LinkedIn (Geneva finance/risk management audience)

1. LinkedIn/Brotons.net → Lead Magnet landing page → Email capture
