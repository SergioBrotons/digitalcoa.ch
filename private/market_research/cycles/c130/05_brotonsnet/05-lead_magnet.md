# 05 — Lead Magnet: Brotons.net Audience Development

## Lead Magnet Concept: "Geneva Executive Intelligence Briefing"
Weekly or bi-weekly curated briefing of Geneva regulatory and institutional developments.

## Format
- 1-page PDF or email briefing (EN primary, FR executive summary)
- Geneva-specific; not available from any other single source
- Gate: email capture via Brotons.net landing page

## Content Structure
1. **This Week in Geneva** — top 3 institutional/regulatory developments
2. **Event Preview** — Geneva events in next 2 weeks with relevance scoring
3. **What It Means** — executive-level interpretation
4. **Action Item** — one thing to watch or do this week

## C130 Lead Magnet Angles

### Angle 1: Pre-Event Geneva Institutional Briefing
"Geneva Institutional Event Intelligence: Pre-Event Briefing Series"
- Systematic pre-event briefing content aligned with Geneva calendar
- ITU, WTO, WHO, ICRC, UN Geneva — major events covered 2 weeks in advance
- Traffic pattern: 3-7x baseline in event windows
- Differentiation: No comparable EN-language pre-event intelligence for Geneva

### Angle 2: Digital Garden Evergreen Deep Dives
"Geneva Regulatory Deep Dives: Evergreen Intelligence"
- Long-form Digital Garden pieces on key Geneva regulatory topics
- Continuously updated as regulatory landscape changes
- Featured snippet capture for Geneva search terms
- Compounding SEO value over time

### Angle 3: Switzerland-EU Institutional Bridge
"Switzerland-EU Watch: Biweekly Intelligence"
- Bilateral III negotiations tracking
- Data adequacy decision monitoring
- Regulatory divergence analysis
- Unique positioning: no comparable independent EN-language source

## Distribution Strategy
1. **LinkedIn newsletter** — algorithm-native format (test)
2. **Email newsletter** — direct to subscribers
3. **Brotons.net website** — canonical versions
4. **Substack syndication** — audience expansion

## Conversion Path
1. LinkedIn/Brotons.net discovery → Lead Magnet landing page → Email capture
2. Email nurture → Regular newsletter engagement
3. Regular engagement → Advisory consultation or referral

- Referral conversion rate
