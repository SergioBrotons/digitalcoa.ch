# 01 — ICP: Brotons.net Audience

## Primary Audience: Geneva Executives and Professionals

### Segment 1: Geneva Financial Institution Executives
- **Profile:** C-suite, senior management at Geneva private banks, commodity trading houses, financial institutions
- **Topics:** Regulatory intelligence, market developments, compliance strategy
- **Pain:** Information overload; need curated, authoritative Geneva-specific intelligence
- **Language preference:** EN primary, FR for Geneva-specific regulatory content

### Segment 2: Geneva Legal Professionals
- **Profile:** GCs, partners, senior associates at Geneva law firms
- **Topics:** Regulatory change, AI governance, arbitration developments
- **Pain:** Keeping pace with regulatory change; finding Geneva-specific analysis
- **Language preference:** EN + FR; legal terminology precision valued

### Segment 3: Geneva Consultants and Advisors
- **Profile:** Managing partners, senior consultants, advisors
- **Topics:** Market intelligence, regulatory advisory positioning, thought leadership
- **Pain:** Thought leadership differentiation; client intelligence needs
- **Language preference:** EN primary

### Segment 4: International Organization Professionals (Geneva)
- **Profile:** Directors, senior officers at UN agencies, WTO, WHO, ICRC
- **Topics:** AI governance, operational efficiency, climate finance
- **Pain:** Internal efficiency mandates; external market intelligence
- **Language preference:** EN primary, FR for Geneva-specific

### Segment 5: Geneva SMB Owners and Executives
- **Profile:** Owners and executives of Geneva SMBs in manufacturing, services, tech
- **Topics:** Regulatory compliance, business strategy, market access
- **Pain:** Limited access to Geneva-specific institutional intelligence
- **Language preference:** FR primary, EN for international topics

## Audience Pain Points (C130 Update)
1. **Information overload** — curated Geneva-specific intelligence valued
2. **Newsletter fatigue** — quality over quantity preferred
3. **Time constraint** — executive time scarce; depth in digestible format
4. **Geneva-specific gap** — generic business content doesn't address Geneva specifics
5. **Language precision** — EN regulatory terminology valued by FR-native professionals
6. **Passive disengagement** — 31% 90-day re-engagement gap identified

## Content Consumption Patterns

- Website: discovery + deep dive
- LinkedIn: awareness + distribution layer
- Event-driven: surge in pre/post event periods (3-7x baseline)
