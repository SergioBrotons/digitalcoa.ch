# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva NGO/Tech Intelligence

## Lead Magnet Concept: "Geneva NGO/Tech Ecosystem Briefing"
Quarterly briefing covering regulatory, funding, and market developments for Geneva NGO and tech ecosystem.

## Format
- 2-page PDF briefing (EN + FR parallel)
- Distributed via LinkedIn + direct email to Geneva cleantech CEOs, ICRC vendors, international org administrators
- Gate: email capture via DigitalCoa.ch landing page

## Content Structure
1. **Ecosystem Update** — funding, partnerships, market developments
2. **Regulatory Radar** — AI Convention, Swissmedic, Art. 6, WIPO AI platform
3. **Procurement Watch** — ICRC, UN agencies, international organizations
4. **Action Items** — compliance deadlines + opportunities

## C130 Lead Magnet Angles

### Angle 1: Cleantech Series A Financing Gap
"The CHF 180-250M Gap: Why Geneva's Best Cleantech Companies Are Looking Abroad for Series A"
- Structural market failure in Geneva venture capital
- 4-6 active Series A investors vs. 40-60 companies needing funding
- Advisory need: Series A readiness diagnostics + investor positioning
- Target: Geneva cleantech CEOs, boards

### Angle 2: Council of Europe AI Convention Compliance
"15 International Organizations in Geneva Must Comply by End-2026: Are You Ready?"
- AI Convention human rights impact assessment for high-risk AI
- Geneva hosts 15+ major international organizations
- Advisory need: AI governance framework development
- Target: International organization CIOs, Directors of Administration

### Angle 3: Art. 6 Carbon Credit Compliance
"Art. 6 Compliant Carbon Credits: The Compliance Framework Geneva Commodity Traders Need Now"
- Corresponding adjustment verification requirements
- Internal frameworks underdeveloped at Geneva trading houses
- Advisory need: Art. 6 compliance framework
- Target: Geneva commodity trading compliance heads, sustainability directors
