# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva NGO/Tech

## Key Regulatory Frameworks

### 1. Cleantech / Venture Capital
- **Swiss Startup Association** — industry data, Series A gap quantification
- **Swiss venture capital market** — seed available, Series A gap structural
- **Geneva cantonal economic development** — cleantech support programs
- **Implication:** Series A readiness advisory + investor positioning

### 2. ICRC Procurement + Humanitarian Tech
- **ICRC procurement rules** — qualification requirements, humanitarian operational requirements
- **ICRC IT budget** — CHF 180-240M (2025-2027)
- **Requirements:** offline-capable systems, data sovereignty, field access control
- **Preference:** Geneva-based vendors with humanitarian sector experience
- **Implication:** Vendor qualification advisory

### 3. WIPO IP + AI Disclosure
- **WIPO AI platform** — WIPO Translate + AI-assisted search; 40% adoption (Q1 2026)
- **AI disclosure question:** Whether AI-assisted drafting must be disclosed in patent applications
- **Swiss Federal Institute of IP** — guidance on AI-assisted invention
- **Cost allocation** — AI tools vs. human attorney time billing
- **Implication:** AI disclosure compliance + IP portfolio strategy

### 4. Council of Europe AI Convention
- **Signed:** March 2025
- **Implementation:** end-2026
- **Obligation:** human rights impact assessment for high-risk AI systems
- **Geneva organizations affected:** 15+ major international organizations
- **Implication:** AI governance framework development

### 5. Swissmedic SaMD + EU MDR
- **Revised MedDO (Medical Device Ordinance)** — 2025 implementation; aligns with EU MDR Annex XVI
- **Swissmedic pathway** — Swiss market authorization
- **EU MDR** — CE marking for EU market
- **Dual-track compliance** — operational complexity and cost burden
- **Geneva healthtech companies:** 35+
- **Implication:** Dual-track compliance strategy advisory

### 6. Art. 6 Carbon Markets
- **Art. 6 Paris Agreement** — COP28/COP29 corresponding adjustment standards
- **Requirements:** Carbon credits must meet corresponding adjustment for corporate claims
- **Geneva commodity trading houses** — trading Art. 6-compliant credits
- **Internal compliance frameworks** — underdeveloped for corresponding adjustment verification
- **Implication:** Art. 6 carbon credit compliance framework

### 7. Swiss Climate Finance Positioning
- **Geneva as climate finance hub** — institutional positioning
- **SIX** — carbon credit trading infrastructure
- **Art. 6 implementation** — ongoing
- **Implication:** Advisory at intersection of commodity trading + climate finance + compliance
