- **Advisory opportunity:** AI disclosure compliance + IP portfolio strategy
- **Implication:** New advisory sub-discipline emerging

### 4. Council of Europe AI Convention Implementation
- Signed: March 2025
- Implementation deadline: end-2026
- Obligation: human rights impact assessment for high-risk AI systems
- Geneva organizations affected: 15+ major international organizations
- **Advisory opportunity:** International organization AI governance framework development
- **Implication:** Owner approval needed for international organization relationships

### 5. Swissmedic SaMD + EU MDR Dual-Track
- Revised MedDO: 2025 implementation; aligns with EU MDR Annex XVI
- Dual-track compliance: Swissmedic (Swiss market) + EU Notified Body (CE marking)
- Geneva healthtech companies: 35+
- Compliance complexity and cost burden significant
- **Advisory opportunity:** Dual-track Swissmedic + EU MDR compliance strategy
- **Implication:** Regulatory complexity creates advisory demand

### 6. Art. 6 Carbon Credit Compliance
- Art. 6 integrity standards: COP28/COP29 established corresponding adjustment requirements
- Geneva commodity trading houses: trading Art. 6-compliant carbon credits
- Internal compliance frameworks for corresponding adjustment verification: underdeveloped
- **Advisory opportunity:** Art. 6 carbon credit compliance framework
- **Implication:** Emerging advisory niche at commodity-humanitarian-sustainability intersection
