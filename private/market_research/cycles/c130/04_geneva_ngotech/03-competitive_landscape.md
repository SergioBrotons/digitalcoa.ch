# 03 — Competitive Landscape: Geneva NGO/Tech Advisory

## Market Structure

### Cleantech Advisory
- **Tier 1:** Strategic consulting (McKinsey, BCG) — engaged by large cleantech; expensive, slow
- **Tier 2:** Swiss venture capital firms — investment focus, not advisory
- **Tier 3:** Switzerland Innovation,technopark companies — incubation, not strategic advisory
- **Gap:** Series A readiness advisory independent of VC conflict

### ICRC/Humanitarian Tech Advisory
- **Competitors:** International humanitarian logistics consultants (few, established)
- **Barrier:** ICRC procurement qualification; relationship-dependent
- **Gap:** Independent advisory for ICRC vendor qualification

### IP/AI-Adjacent Advisory
- **Competitors:** Major law firms (Lenz & Staehelin, Vischer), specialist IP boutiques
- **Gap:** AI disclosure compliance + IP portfolio strategy; emerging sub-discipline
- **Positioning:** Independent regulatory IP advisory without law firm conflict

### International Organization AI Governance
- **Competitors:** UN system consultants (expensive, relationship-dependent)
- **Gap:** Independent AI governance framework development for international organizations
- **Note:** Owner approval needed; relationship-dependent

### Healthtech Regulatory Advisory
- **Competitors:** Regulatory affairs consultancies (Biogen, NAMSA), Swissmedic insiders
- **Gap:** Swissmedic + EU MDR dual-track compliance strategy
- **Positioning:** Dual-track compliance as distinct advisory offering

### Art. 6 Carbon Advisory
- **Competitors:** Carbon market consultants, commodity trading compliance teams
- **Gap:** Art. 6 corresponding adjustment compliance framework for trading houses
- **Positioning:** Compliance framework at intersection of commodity trading + climate finance

## Competitive White Space

1. **Cleantech Series A readiness** — independent advisory without VC conflict
2. **ICRC vendor qualification** — independent advisory for ICRC procurement navigation
3. **AI disclosure compliance** — emerging IP sub-discipline; independent of law firm
4. **International org AI governance** — independent framework development
5. **Art. 6 carbon compliance** — compliance framework at trading-sustainability intersection
