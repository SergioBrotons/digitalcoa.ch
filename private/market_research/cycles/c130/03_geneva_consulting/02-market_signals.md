# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva Consulting Sector

## Swiss Consulting Market Overview
- **Market size:** ~CHF 8-12 billion annually
- **Geneva share:** ~20-25% (CHF 1.6-3.0B)
- **Employment:** ~15,000 consulting professionals in Geneva region
- **Growth drivers:** Regulatory change, digital transformation, geopolitical risk, sustainability

## Key Market Signals

### 1. OT Cybersecurity Gap (SME Manufacturing)
- Geneva canton: 340+ precision manufacturing firms
- Average OT security spend: CHF 8-15K/year vs. recommended CHF 50-80K
- IEC 62443 adoption: <15% of Geneva SMEs
- MELANI/CYIO flagged OT/IT convergence as priority
- **Two-order demand:** OT security gap assessment + remediation implementation
- **Implication:** OT security advisory for Geneva precision manufacturing sector

### 2. Intercantonal Tax Allocation Disputes
- Multi-cantonal consulting firms face three-way tax allocation
- Asset-heavy service firms (IP-intensive consulting) particularly exposed
- Federal Tax Court 2C_480/2023: partial clarity on IP valuation methodology
- Remote work blurring cantonal presence
- **Advisory opportunity:** Intercantonal tax allocation compliance + dispute prevention
- **Implication:** Multi-cantonal consulting firm market

### 3. UN Agency Efficiency Mandate
- UN Secretary-General: 15% operational cost reduction 2025-2027
- Geneva UN agencies seeking: business process re-engineering, ICT modernization, shared services
- UNGP procurement rules create engagement constraints
- Relationship-dependent with Geneva UN consulting firms
- **Advisory opportunity:** UN agency operational efficiency consulting
- **Implication:** Relationship + UNGP expertise required; owner approval needed

### 4. Vocational Education Skill Mismatch
- Geneva: 2,800 annual apprenticeship completions vs. 4,200+ job openings
- Cross-border French commuters: ~18,000 daily in commercial sector
- Talent shortage systemic across Geneva commercial sector
- **Advisory opportunity:** Workforce planning + cross-border talent model design
- **Implication:** Internal talent strategy + client workforce planning consulting

### 5. Board Governance Compliance (40% Diversity Quota)
- Swiss public companies + FINMA-supervised institutions
- Compliance deadline: January 2026
- 35-45% risk missing targets without board composition changes
- Art. 716a personal liability → D&O premium increases +18-22% YoY (2025)
- **Advisory opportunity:** Board governance readiness assessment + diversity pipeline + liability mapping
- **Implication:** High-urgency, quantified compliance gap

### 6. Supply Chain Due Diligence (OR Art. 964j-k)
- Swiss companies 500+ employees mandatory due diligence
- Trading houses + manufacturers sourcing from tariff-affected countries
- Documentation + verification requirements exceed internal capacity
- **Advisory opportunity:** Supply chain mapping, tier-2/3 supplier due diligence, report preparation
- **Implication:** Mandatory compliance consulting demand
