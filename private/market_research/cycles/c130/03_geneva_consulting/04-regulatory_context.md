# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Consulting

## Key Regulatory Frameworks

### 1. OT/IT Security: IEC 62443 + MELANI/CYIO
- **IEC 62443** — industrial automation and control systems security standard
- **MELANI/CYIO** — Federal programme for cybercrime + critical infrastructure
- **Adoption rate:** <15% of Geneva SMEs despite cantonal manufacturing concentration
- **Gap:** OT security spend CHF 8-15K vs. recommended CHF 50-80K
- **Implication:** OT security gap assessment + remediation

### 2. Intercantonal Tax Allocation: OR + FTA
- **Federal Tax Court 2C_480/2023** — IP valuation methodology for intercantonal allocation
- **Ambiguity remains** on implementation for IP-intensive consulting firms
- **Remote work** increasing intercantonal tax complexity
- **Implication:** Intercantonal tax compliance + dispute prevention advisory

### 3. UN Efficiency Mandate + UNGP
- **UN Secretary-General efficiency mandate** — 15% cost reduction 2025-2027
- **UNGP** — UN procurement guidelines for external consulting engagements
- **Geneva UN agencies** (ITU, WHO, UNOG, ILO, WTO) seeking business process re-engineering
- **Implication:** UN agency consulting (requires relationship + UNGP expertise)

### 4. Vocational Education: EFP + ORP
- **EFP** — Geneva cantonal office for vocational training
- **ORP** — Regional employment office
- **Mismatch:** 2,800 completions vs. 4,200+ openings annually in Geneva commercial sector
- **Cross-border commuters:** ~18,000 daily French workers in Geneva commercial sector
- **Implication:** Workforce planning + cross-border talent model consulting

### 5. Board Governance: AktG Art. 716a + Diversity Quota
- **40% board diversity quota** — Swiss public companies + FINMA-supervised; January 2026 deadline
- **AktG Art. 716a** — personal liability for board members for supervisory duties
- **D&O premium increases:** +18-22% YoY in 2025 for exposed sectors
- **35-45%** of affected companies risk missing diversity targets without changes
- **Implication:** Board governance readiness assessment + diversity pipeline + liability mapping

### 6. Supply Chain Due Diligence: OR Art. 964j-k
- **OR Art. 964j-k** — Swiss due diligence obligations (OECD Guidelines for MNEs)
- **LkSG alignment** — Swiss supply chain due diligence aligned with German LkSG
- **Scope:** Swiss companies 500+ employees
- **Documentation + verification requirements** exceed internal compliance capacity
- **Implication:** Supply chain mapping, tier-2/3 supplier due diligence, report preparation

### 7. D&O Liability: Art. 716a + FINMA Circular
- **Art. 716a AktG** — personal liability for supervisory board duties
- **FINMA governance circular** — extends liability expectations to FINMA-supervised institutions
- **Enforcement:** D&O claims + personal liability actions increasing
- **Implication:** Board liability mapping + governance readiness
