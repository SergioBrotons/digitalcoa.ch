# 06 — Executive Summary
## Phase 01 Geneva Legal — C130

**Market Opportunity:**
Geneva legal advisory market is being reshaped by four structural forces: (1) Switzerland-EU institutional uncertainty creating advisory demand, (2) AI governance and professional responsibility emerging as distinct practice area, (3) VERMAG+SRO dual-layer compliance architecture under-discussed, and (4) Swiss SPV bankruptcy remoteness remaining opinion-dependent without statutory safe harbor.

**Key Findings C130:**

1. Algorithmic liability gap: Swiss courts applying hybrid OR Art. 208 + 398; no definitive doctrine; creates AI deployment contract advisory demand
2. VERMAG + SRO two-tier compliance architecture: Distinct from direct FINMA licensing; dual reporting obligations under-reported by Geneva asset managers
3. CCIG commercial court digital filing + bilingual burden: 400-600 cases/year; 8-12 month resolution; small-cap parties bear disproportionate FR/DE translation costs
4. nFADP cross-border enforcement gap: Art. 15/17 mirror GDPR but unenforceable against non-Swiss; EU adequacy decision is critical upstream trigger
5. Attorney trust account AML tension: OGE Art. 12 + MROS reporting + bar confidentiality create compounding obligations for Geneva practitioners
6. Swiss SPV bankruptcy remoteness: OR Art. 284 avoidance actions + true-sale opinions; no statutory bankruptcy-remote vehicle; true-sale documentation gaps are primary risk

**ICP Summary:**
Managing Partners, GCs, and Compliance Heads at Geneva financial institutions + mid-large law firms. Pain: regulatory change volume + AI governance + dual-layer compliance + cross-border enforcement uncertainty. Urgency: Bilateral III negotiations, FINMA examination cycle, nFADP enforcement trajectory.

**Competitive Position:**
Independent, conflict-free regulatory counsel with AI governance specialization + VERMAG/SRO dual-layer compliance niche + Switzerland-EU institutional bridge advisory.

**Recommended Action:**
Develop Regulatory Radar quarterly briefing as primary lead magnet. Pursue AI governance readiness assessment for law firms as secondary differentiator. Position SPV true-sale documentation review as niche structured finance offering.

**Skipped:**
GC interviews, Geneva Bar Association outreach, FINMA newsletter subscription, law firm outreach program, web_search (Gemini 429 quota)
