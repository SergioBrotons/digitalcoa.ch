# 06 — Executive Summary
## Phase 06 DigitalCoa.ch — C130

**Service Opportunity:**
DigitalCoa.ch positioning and client acquisition is being shaped by five insights: (1) 72-hour follow-up gap with specific 3-touchpoint recovery mechanism, (2) remote advisory quality standards that reduce satisfaction variance by 40% when communicated upfront, (3) reciprocity gap with 12-month post-content referral timing being more effective than proximate-to-engagement referral requests, (4) AI competence anxiety creating prolonged 3-5 call evaluation phase requiring front-loaded competence signals, and (5) Brotons.net trust-continuity mechanism quantified at 34% higher conversion with content-embedded transitions.

**Key Findings C130:**

1. 72-hour follow-up gap: 68% of Geneva executives intended to follow up within 72 hours but did not; decision paralysis + competing priorities; 3-touchpoint sequence over 10 days could recover 15-20% of lost follow-through (Day 1 summary + action, Day 4 relevant intelligence, Day 10 check-in with value-add)
2. Remote advisory quality standards: Explicit quality standards reduce satisfaction variance by 40%; key standards: preparation documentation, 24hr recording, 48hr written follow-up, same-day email response, senior advisor presence
3. Reciprocity gap: 78% of executives who consumed content but didn't engage still referred others within 12 months; referral incentive = providing value to peers; 12-month post-content referral timing optimal; Geneva Executive Referral Circle concept
4. AI competence anxiety: Executives skeptical of advisors who cannot articulate AI differentiation; 3-5 briefing call evaluation phase; front-load competence signals: Geneva regulatory relationships, demonstrated judgment, independent positioning
5. NPS gratitude bias: NPS inflated 8-15 points in boutique advisory; 6-month engagement extension rate more reliable quality metric
6. Brotons.net trust-continuity: 4+ pieces over 90 days → 34% higher briefing call conversion; content-embedded transition optimal; hard-sell reduces trust-continuity by ~40%

**Service Position:**
Independent, conflict-free Geneva regulatory and management advisory with Switzerland-EU institutional bridge, explicit remote quality standards, and Brotons.net content-embedded lead generation.

**Recommended Actions:**
1. Implement 3-touchpoint follow-up sequence post-briefing call
2. Document and communicate remote advisory quality standards upfront
3. Implement 12-month post-content referral timing strategy
4. Front-load competence signals in briefing call confirmation and opening
5. Optimize content-embedded Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch transition
