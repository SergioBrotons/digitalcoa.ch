# 04 — Regulatory Context: DigitalCoa.ch Service Context

## Geneva Regulatory Landscape (Service Context)

### 1. Switzerland-EU Bilateral Framework
- **Bilateral III negotiations** — Q2 2026 critical phase
- **Market access + data adequacy** — key negotiation items
- **Contingency advisory demand** — companies preparing for various outcomes
- **Service implication:** Switzerland-EU institutional bridge advisory is core service

### 2. FINMA Regulatory Agenda
- **FINMA Circular 2026/1** — nature-related financial risks; January 2026 staged entry
- **FINMA TCFD-aligned disclosures** — Q4 2025 guidance; Scope 3 financed emissions
- **Board governance expectations** — Art. 716a personal liability
- **40% diversity quota** — January 2026 deadline
- **Service implication:** Board governance, FINMA compliance, climate disclosure advisory

### 3. Council of Europe AI Convention
- **Implementation end-2026** — human rights impact assessment for high-risk AI
- **Geneva international organizations** — 15+ affected organizations
- **Service implication:** AI governance framework development for Geneva organizations

### 4. Swiss AI Act / OGE Professional Standards
- **OGE/RAV guidance** — professional responsibility for lawyers using GenAI
- **Liability framework** — evolving algorithmic liability doctrine
- **Service implication:** AI governance + professional responsibility advisory

### 5. Supply Chain Due Diligence
- **OR Art. 964j-k** — mandatory for Swiss companies 500+ employees
- **LkSG alignment** — Swiss supply chain due diligence
- **Service implication:** Supply chain due diligence advisory

### 6. OT/IT Security
- **IEC 62443** — industrial control systems security standard
- **MELANI/CYIO** — federal cybercrime advisory
- **Service implication:** OT security gap assessment advisory for Geneva manufacturers

## Service Design Considerations

### Engagement Model
- **Primary:** Quarterly retainer (CHF 100-200K/year)
- **Secondary:** Project-based (CHF 20-80K)
- **Tertiary:** Day-rate advisory (CHF 2,800-4,500/day)
- **Remote delivery** with explicit quality standards

### Quality Standards (Communicate Upfront)
1. Session preparation documented with specific client references
2. Session recording available within 24 hours
3. Written follow-up within 48 hours
4. Same-day email response during engagement
5. Senior advisor present (not delegated)

### Follow-Up Sequence (Post-Briefing Call)
- **Day 1:** Concise summary + one specific action
- **Day 4:** Relevant Geneva intelligence relevant to their situation
- **Day 10:** Brief check-in with new value-add

### Referral Strategy
- 12-month post-content referral timing
- Geneva Executive Referral Circle concept
- Quarterly informal gathering of past clients + prospective referrals
