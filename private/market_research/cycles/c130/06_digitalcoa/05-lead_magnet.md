# 05 — Lead Magnet: DigitalCoa.ch Client Acquisition

## Lead Magnet Concept: "Geneva Executive Briefing"
Primary lead magnet: free Geneva Executive Briefing (45-60 minute call) for qualified executives.

## Secondary Lead Magnets
1. **"Geneva Regulatory Radar"** — quarterly written briefing on top 5 Geneva regulatory developments
2. **"Switzerland-EU Watch"** — bi-weekly intelligence update on bilateral negotiations
3. **"AI Governance Readiness Assessment"** — diagnostic tool for Geneva organizations

## Format
- **Executive Briefing:** 45-60 minute video call with senior advisor; Geneva-specific regulatory or strategic issue of client's choice
- **Geneva Regulatory Radar:** 2-page quarterly PDF; EN + FR parallel
- **Switzerland-EU Watch:** 1-page bi-weekly email update
- **AI Governance Readiness Assessment:** Structured diagnostic questionnaire with follow-up consultation offer

## Content Structure (Geneva Regulatory Radar)
1. **This Quarter's Top 5** — Geneva regulatory developments
2. **What It Means** — executive-level interpretation
3. **Action Items** — compliance deadlines, opportunities
4. **Briefing Offer** — invitation to schedule Executive Briefing
