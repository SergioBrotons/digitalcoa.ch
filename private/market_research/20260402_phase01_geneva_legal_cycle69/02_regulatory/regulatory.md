## Applicable Regulations

### 1. Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) — In Force July 1, 2023
- FDPIC: FADP applies directly to AI systems processing personal data
- DPIA (Art. 22): mandatory for high-risk AI systems
- Art. 21: profiling provisions relevant to AI decision-making
- Enforcement: FDPIC can issue orders, fines; GDPR-like but Swiss-specific

### 2. EU AI Act — GPAI Obligations In Force Since August 2, 2025
- General-Purpose AI (GPAI): obligations already in effect
- Transparency: GPAI providers must disclose training data, copyright, technical documentation
- conformity assessments: required for high-risk GPAI applications
- August 2, 2026: double deadline — HR AI conformity + legacy GPAI grandfathering expiry
- Extraterritorial: applies to AI systems placed on EU market OR whose output is used in EU

### 3. Federal AI Ordinance — In Force Since ~2024
- Federal Council sector-specific approach: no broad "Swiss AI Act"
- Sector-specific rules: finance (FINMA), healthcare (Swissmedic), etc.
- 16+ months operative: real estate, legal, consulting most exposed
