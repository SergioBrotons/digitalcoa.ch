## Content Strategy by Tier

### TIER 1 — Executive Decision Framework
All TIER 1 articles should include:
- Executive summary paragraph (2-3 sentences: what changed, why it matters, what to do)
- 3-4 specific action items or decision variables
- Board/governance framing
- Clear compliance or risk management angle
- CTA: Briefing call or consultation offer (pending owner confirmation)

### TIER 2 — Strategic Intelligence
- "Build or Buy" article: framework piece for healthcare IT decisions
- No hard CTA; authority-building content
- Connects to TIER 1 content through internal linking

## CTA Strategy
- TIER 1 articles: Briefing call CTA (pending owner confirmation)
- TIER 2 articles: No CTA / reference to TIER 1 content
- All articles: Internal linking between DigitalCoa content cluster
- All articles: Cross-link to relevant Brotons content where appropriate
