## FINMA Circ. 08/2024 — Key Requirements

1. **Governance structure** — Clear assignment of AI responsibility at management level
2. **AI inventory** — All AI tools documented with risk classification
3. **Risk management** — Model risk (bias, explainability, robustness), operational risk (IT, cyber, third-party)
4. **Robustness testing** — AI outputs validated before use in client-facing decisions
5. **Transparency** — AI use disclosed where material; results explainable
6. **Non-discrimination** — No unjustified bias in AI-assisted decisions
7. **Staff training** — Personnel using AI must be trained on risks and responsibilities
8. **Third-party risk** — Vendor due diligence for AI tools processing client/firm data
9. **Incident response** — AI failures must be detected, reported, remediated
10. **Ongoing monitoring** — AI performance and risk continuously tracked
