# Phase 02 — Geneva Finance/WM — Cycle 45
## Analysis: Growth Opportunity

## Market Size Estimate
- Geneva/Basel/Lugano independent asset managers: ~200-300 firms
- Target ICP: Boutique WMs with CHF 100M-2B AUM = ~150-200 firms
- Addressable (AI-using, no formal governance): ~120-160 firms
- Serviceable (within 12-18mo window): ~40-60 firms

## Primary Urgency Levers (Ranked)
1. 67-point governance gap — 95% scaled AI vs 28% formal = WIDEST IN PORTFOLIO
2. 91% of AI users using GenAI — nearly universal consumer-grade tool use
3. FINMA AI Desk established + on-site inspections active
4. CRS 2.0 live NOW + CARF design constraints active
5. May 2026 digitalswitzerland First Draft (~1 month away)
6. FATF 2026-2027/2028 mutual evaluation = external forcing function

## Unique to WM vs. Other Verticals
- FATF evaluation creates unique external pressure (not present in legal/consulting)
- FINMA on-site inspections already active (vs. FDPIC enforcement absent in legal)
- External vendor reliance = outsourcing risk layer unique to WM (AI tools are vendor-hosted)
- 91% GenAI = Shadow AI problem is primarily GenAI consumer tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- CARF/CRS 2.0 dual deadline = regulatory compliance layer on top of AI governance

## Entry Sequence
1. Shadow AI audit (Decision 0) — map all GenAI tools already in use
2. FINMA Circ. 08/2024 gap assessment — identify governance gaps
3. CARF/CRS 2.0 compliance review — existing reporting obligations
4. FADP compliance assessment — AI tools processing client data
5. AI governance framework development — policy, training, monitoring
