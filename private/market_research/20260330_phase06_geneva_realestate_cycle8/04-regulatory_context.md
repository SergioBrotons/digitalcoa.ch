# Phase 06 Regulatory Context — Geneva Real Estate / Small Agencies
## Cycle 8 (2026-03-30)

## Primary Regulatory Framework

### 1. GAIGI (Geneva AI Governance Institute)
- **Status:** Operative ~7-10 months (Fall 2025 launch + late March 2026 institutional legitimacy)
- **Nature:** Geneva industry framework for AI governance; accreditation and standards body
- **Boutique RE relevance:** Validates category awareness; establishes institutional legitimacy; agencies hearing about AI governance through GAIGI

### 2. nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection)
- **Status:** Operative since September 1, 2023 (~2.5 years)
- **Nature:** AI processing of client personal data = Art. 5(c); DPIA required under Art. 22
- **Boutique RE relevance:** RETROACTIVE EXPOSURE — most agencies already using AI without DPIA; remediation path needed

### 3. EU AI Act
- **Date:** August 2, 2026 (~15 months); GPAI operative since August 2, 2025 (~7.5 months)
- **Boutique RE relevance:** Agencies with EU market nexus (international listings, EU client base) face dual nFADP + EU AI Act obligations

### 4. Swissdamed (Cross-Sector Spillover)
- **Date:** July 1, 2026 (~12 weeks)
- **Boutique RE relevance:** Geneva professional community awareness trigger; healthcare practices deal with Swissdamed in Q2 2026; spillover to RE community
