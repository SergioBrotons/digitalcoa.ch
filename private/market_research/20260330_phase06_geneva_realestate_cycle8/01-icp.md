# Phase 06 ICP — Geneva Real Estate / Small Agencies
## Cycle 8 (2026-03-30)

## Addressable Profile
Small real estate agencies in Geneva, 2–15 agents; boutique agencies or property managers; commission-based revenue; international client base (WM clients, HNWIs, EU clients). Managing partner or agency principal owns technology decisions.

## Addressable Problem
67% AI adoption (highest of any portfolio vertical) with no corresponding governance framework. Geneva RE agencies using AI for property valuations, listing descriptions, client communications, market analysis — but GAIGI is only ~7-10 months old and not an advisory competitor. Retroactive compliance path is unique to RE — most agencies are already non-compliant and need remediation, not planning.

## Regulatory Urgency Stack (Cycle 8 — Carry-Forward)
1. **GAIGI operative ~7-10 months:** Fall 2025 launch + late March 2026 = institutional legitimacy now established; category awareness rising
2. **nFADP:** AI processing of client personal data = Art. 5(c) + DPIA (Art. 22); retroactive exposure (most agencies already non-compliant)
3. **67% AI adoption:** Highest of any portfolio vertical; widest gap between adoption and governance
4. **EU AI Act August 2, 2026 (~15 months):** Agencies with EU market nexus (international listings, EU clients) face dual nFADP + EU AI Act obligations
5. **Swissdamed Jul 1 ~12 weeks:** Spillover awareness; Geneva professional community talks about AI compliance in Q2 2026

## Timing Signal
- GAIGI ~7-10 months operative (institutional legitimacy established; category awareness rising)
- Retroactive compliance path: most Geneva RE agencies already non-compliant with nFADP — remediation, not planning
- Swissdamed Jul 1 ~12 weeks: spillover awareness across Geneva professional community
- No boutique RE AI governance competitor in **7+ consecutive cycles**

## Urgency Drivers
- 67% AI adoption = highest vertical = widest governance gap = largest market opportunity
- GAIGI institutional legitimacy validates category awareness — agencies are hearing about AI governance
- Retroactive framing unique across all 5 verticals: "you're already non-compliant, here's how to fix it" vs. anticipatory "you need to prepare"
- Swissdamed spillover creates community awareness

## First-Mover Rationale
Window: ~7-10 months (GAIGI established; category awareness rising; no boutique competitor). Retroactive compliance unique RE entry point. 67% AI adoption = structurally widest gap. No boutique RE AI governance advisory competitor confirmed 7+ cycles.
