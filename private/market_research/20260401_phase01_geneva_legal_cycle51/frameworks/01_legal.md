## FADP Compliance Stack (Art. 5c + Art. 21 + Art. 16 + DPIA + Privacy by Design)

### Layer 1 — Art. 5(c) FADP: Lawfulness of Processing
Any AI processing client data must have a legal basis. Using ChatGPT on a client contract without documented legal basis = FADP violation. Solutions: FABIAN (Swiss-hosted, zero retention), CASUS (Swiss-hosted, zero retention), or equivalent.

### Layer 2 — Art. 21 FADP: Right to Human Review
Automated decisions producing legal effects must allow human review. AI-assisted contract analysis, legal opinions, or case assessments that produce binding outcomes require a human review mechanism. Document this.

### Layer 3 — Art. 16 FADP: Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)
AI systems processing high-risk data (health, legal, financial) require a documented DPIA before deployment. For law firms: client matter data is high-risk by category.

### Layer 4 — Privacy by Design
AI tools must be selected based on: (a) data residency — Swiss or equivalent; (b) zero retention — no training on your data; (c) access controls — documented who can use which tools.

### Layer 5 — Documentation and Accountability
Maintain: AI tool inventory, data flows for each tool, documented legal basis for each processing activity, staff training records.

## FADP Explicit Prohibitions — New Urgency (C51)

**The FADP has red lines. Know where they are.**

FADP Art. 26 equivalent prohibits AI applications specifically designed to undermine privacy and informational self-determination:

- **Prohibited:** Mass real-time facial recognition in public spaces
- **Prohibited:** Social scoring systems that classify individuals by behavior
- **Prohibited:** AI applications designed to manipulate individual decision-making without transparency

**Law firm applications approaching prohibited territory:**
- Client surveillance tools that profile behavior without disclosure
- Automated case-outcome scoring that binds clients without review
- AI systems processing privileged communications without explicit consent architecture

**Action required:** Audit current AI tool stack against FADP prohibitions. Document compliance for everything else.
