## Why Real Estate Is Unique in the Portfolio

- **75% AI adoption** — highest of any vertical in the portfolio
- **~47-point governance gap** — 75% actual use vs. ~28% formal governance
- **FADP hard prohibitions apply** — tenant profiling, behavioral scoring without disclosure may be approaching prohibited territory
- **Tenant screening AI** — likely qualifies as EU AI Act Annex III high-risk (automated decision-making affecting fundamental rights)
- **Property valuation AI** — likely requires high-risk DPIA under FADP Art. 16
- **GAIGI institutional** — confirmed 404 (gaigi.ch not SMB-accessible — no public RE AI guidance)
- **No regulatory body** specifically overseeing AI in real estate (unlike FINMA for WM)
- **FADP 2.5 years operative** — no grace period, retroactive compliance required
- **FDPIC resources increased** — enforcement capacity now demonstrably exists

## FADP Hard Prohibitions — Real Estate Specific

**The FADP Art. 26 equivalent explicitly prohibits AI applications designed to undermine privacy and informational self-determination:**

Real estate applications approaching prohibited territory:
- **Tenant scoring systems** that profile rental applicants without transparency or disclosure
- **Buyer behavior analysis** that classifies individuals for marketing without consent
- **Algorithmic pricing** that discriminates based on protected characteristics
- **AI systems processing data about tenants/buyers** without documented legal basis
