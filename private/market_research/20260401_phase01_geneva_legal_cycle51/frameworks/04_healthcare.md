## Swissdamed: The Immediate Priority

### What Is Swissdamed?
Swissdamed is Switzerland's database for medical devices. It became operative in phases:
- **Phase 1** (2022): Registration of economic operators
- **Phase 2** (2024): Registration of devices
- **Phase 3 (July 1, 2026)**: Registration of AI-assisted medical devices — **NOW ~13 WEEKS OUT**

### What Triggers Swissdamed Registration for AI?
- AI tools used for diagnostic decisions (CDSS — Clinical Decision Support Systems)
- AI tools used for patient monitoring with automated alerts
- AI imaging analysis tools (radiology, pathology)
- Any AI tool that qualifies as a "medical device" under Swiss law

### The Dual Registration Burden
Swissdamed and EUDAMED are **not interconnected**. Practices serving both Swiss and EU patients may need dual registration.

## FADP in Clinical Context

Patient data is a **FADP high-risk category**. The obligations are more stringent:

- **Art. 5(c):** Legal basis required for any AI processing of patient data
- **Art. 16:** DPIA mandatory for AI systems processing health data
- **Art. 21:** Automated clinical decisions require human review mechanism
- **Art. 26 equivalent:** Prohibited AI applications — patient profiling/scoring without transparency may be in prohibited category
- **Breach notification:** Mandatory for AI-related patient data incidents

**NEW in C51:** FDPIC enforcement resources demonstrably increased. Healthcare is a likely enforcement priority given patient data = highest FADP risk category + active Swissdamed deadline.
