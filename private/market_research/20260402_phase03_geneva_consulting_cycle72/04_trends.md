# 04 — Geneva Consulting: Market Trends & Strategic Dynamics

## AI Consulting — Dominant Competitive Theme

### Big 4 AI Positioning (2026)
- **EY:** Top service category on website — AI Strategy Consulting, AI-powered M&A, AI Platform
- **PwC:** Trust-in-AI, Crypto Advisory, Cloud AI embedded in practice
- **KPMG:** Server error on insights; less visible AI presence
- **McKinsey/BCG:** Blocked (403) but known for AI practices

### Client Demand for AI Consulting
- AI strategy: Board-level demand for AI transformation roadmaps
- AI implementation: Moving from strategy to execution
- AI governance: FINMA AI Guidance compliance (end-2026 deadline)
- AI risk: Model governance, explainability, bias assessment

### AI Consulting Revenue Model
- Strategy projects: Fixed fee
- Implementation: Time + materials
- Managed services: Recurring revenue

## ESG / Sustainability Consulting

### Nature Transition — New Consulting Opportunity
- From Phase 02: CHF 5.3B/year financing need
- Consulting angle: Structuring mechanisms, advisory, project finance
- Banks (Phase 02): Key clients for nature transition advisory

### Traditional ESG
- Sustainability reporting: CSRD alignment for Swiss companies with EU nexus
- ESG due diligence: M&A transactions
- Climate risk: TCFD-aligned disclosure
