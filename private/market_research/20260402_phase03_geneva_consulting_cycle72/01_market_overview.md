# 01 — Geneva Consulting: Structural Overview

## Swiss Consulting Market

### Market Size
- Swiss consulting market: USD 3.74 billion (confirmed C71)
- Market segment: Professional services including management consulting, IT consulting, ESG advisory
- Geneva share: Major hub alongside Zurich; international firms concentrated
- SMB segment: Fastest growing (C71 finding)

## Geneva Consulting Ecosystem

### Big 4 Presence — Geneva
All major Big 4 firms maintain Geneva offices as part of their Swiss operations:

**PwC Switzerland**
- Offices: 13 across Switzerland and Liechtenstein
- Geneva role: Major office; consulting services spanning cloud, crypto, ESG, strategy
- Strategy&: PwC's strategy consulting brand present in Switzerland

**EY Switzerland**
- Geneva/Zurich: Primary offices
- AI positioning: Top service priority on website
- Strategy consulting: EY-Parthenon brand
- Transactions: M&A, valuation, infrastructure advisory

**KPMG Switzerland**
- Swiss presence: Audit and tax primary; consulting secondary
- Geneva: Office presence
- Note: More limited consulting presentation vs. PwC/EY

**Deloitte Switzerland**
- Geneva: Office presence
- Services: Audit, consulting, financial advisory, risk management

### Boutique Consulting — Geneva

**FONGIT Ecosystem**
- Role: Geneva tech innovation foundation supporting startups
- Startup count: 250+ supported
- Jobs created: 1,800+ in 10 years
- Capital raised: CHF 1B+
- Patents/products: 500+
- Note: These are FONGIT-supported companies, not consulting firms, but represent a client ecosystem for consulting

### Management Consultancies — Boutique

Swiss boutique consulting firms active in Geneva:
- Startups/small consultancies: FONGIT network
- Specialist boutiques: Strategy, sustainability, digital transformation niches
- Multinational internal consulting arms: Function within large firms

## Consulting Demand Drivers (2026)

### AI Adoption
- Big 4: AI positioned as primary competitive differentiator
- EY: AI strategy consulting explicitly on homepage
- PwC: Trust-in-AI, crypto advisory, cloud AI
- Client demand: AI transformation, strategy, implementation

### ESG / Sustainability
- All Big 4: Sustainability strategy and reporting services
- Nature transition financing (from Phase 02): CHF 5.3B/year need
- Regulatory pressure: Swiss ESG disclosure requirements

### Digital Transformation
- Cloud migration: PwC, EY active
- Cybersecurity: Dedicated practices across Big 4
- Technology strategy: PwC and EY

### Regulatory Change
- FINMA AI rules: Implementation consulting demand (end-2026)
- Stablecoin legislation: Compliance advisory (Phase 02)
- Nature transition: Advisory on financing mechanisms
