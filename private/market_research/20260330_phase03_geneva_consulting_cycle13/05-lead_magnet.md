## Section 2: The 3-Layer Compliance Stack

**Layer 1 — nFADP (Swiss Domestic):**
- Art. 5(c): AI processing of client data = active obligations NOW (since Sept 2023)
- Art. 22: DPIA for high-risk processing
- Applies to all Swiss firms processing personal data via AI

**Layer 2 — EU AI Act GPAI (EU Extraterritorial — LIVE SINCE AUG 2, 2025):**
- Transparency obligations: disclose when AI used in deliverables
- Copyright documentation: track IP/data used in AI training
- Incident documentation: log AI failures affecting outputs
- Applies if firm has EU clients, EU-intended outputs, or EU market presence

**Layer 3 — EU AI Act High-Risk (August 2, 2026 — ~16 months):**
- If firm places AI systems on EU market
- If firm operates AI with EU-intended outputs
- If firm acts as EU importer/distributor of AI systems
