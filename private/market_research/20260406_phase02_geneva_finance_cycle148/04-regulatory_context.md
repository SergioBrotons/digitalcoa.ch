# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Finance

## Key Regulatory Framework

### Swiss Financial Market Architecture
- **FINMA** — Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (independent)
- **SECO** — State Secretariat for Economic Affairs (sanctions, trade policy)
- **SNB** — Swiss National Bank (monetary policy, systemic stability)
- **SFAMA** — Swiss Funds & Asset Management Association (self-regulation for funds)
- **FINMA-FCB** — Financial Market Supervisory Authority–Federal Council interface

### Primary Regulatory Instruments (Geneva Finance)
| Regulation | Scope | Current Status |
|---|---|---|
| **FinIA** (Financial Institutions Act) | Asset managers, fund management companies, portfolio managers | Revision consultation Oct 2025; two new crypto license categories |
| **FinSA** (Financial Services Act) | Client categorization, prospectus, advice obligations | In force since 2020; ongoing amendments |
| **BankA** (Banking Act) | Banks, securities dealers | Post-CS/UBS TBTF revision underway |
| **ISA** (Information Security Act) | Cybersecurity, incident reporting | ISA amendments creating DORA-parity obligations |
| **AMLA** (Anti-Money Laundering Act) | KYC, AML, SAR obligations | Ongoing revision; enhanced for digital assets |
| **DOTA** (Digital Operational Resilience Act — EU) | ICT risk, incident reporting (extraterritorial for EU-accessing Swiss FIs) | DORA effective Jan 2025; Swiss parity via ISA amendments |
| **nFADP** | Personal data processing; AI training data consent (Art. 9) | In force 2023-2024; FDPIC guidance on AI data lacking |
| **CO** (Code of Obligations) | Corporate law, contract law, agent liability | AI civil liability via Art. 41 OR amendment proposed |

### Geneva-Specific Regulatory Considerations
- **Geneva Cantonal bank (Banque Cantonale de Genève — BCG)** — Cantonal bank guarantee, special regulatory status
- **CCIG (Chambre de commerce, d'industrie et des services de Genève)** — Trade chamber self-regulation
- **Geneva commercial court** — Bilingual FR/DE jurisdiction for financial disputes
- **Geneva cantonal tax (API/APG)** — Withholding tax, wealth tax implications for Geneva financial institutions
