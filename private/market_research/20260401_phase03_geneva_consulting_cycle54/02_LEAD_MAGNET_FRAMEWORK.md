## Lead Magnet Sections (Cycle 54 Framework)

### Section 1: Shadow AI Audit — What AI Are You Already Using?
- What AI tools do your consultants use for research, drafting, analysis, client deliverables?
- Which tools handle client data?
- Are AI-augmented deliverables documented for quality and compliance?
- Decision 0: govern the AI you already use before adopting more

### Section 2: GPAI Obligations — Are You a GPAI Model Provider?
- Do you develop, train, or provide access to AI models?
- Do your tools qualify as General Purpose AI under EU AI Act?
- Are your GPAI obligations (documentation, transparency, safety) already met?

### Section 3: FADP Compliance for Consulting Deliverables
- Are client deliverables processed through AI tools documented?
- Do clients have right to human review of AI-assisted consulting opinions?
- Is client data used in AI processing protected under FADP?

### Section 4: August 2, 2026 — GPAI High-Risk Deadline
- Do any of your AI tools qualify as high-risk AI systems?
- Have you conducted an AI risk assessment for client-facing deliverables?
- Is your AI governance proportionate to the consequence of AI-assisted errors?

### Section 5: AI-Augmented Client Work — Quality and Accountability
- Who is accountable when an AI-augmented deliverable contains an error?
- Do you have quality controls for AI-assisted analysis?
- Is your professional liability coverage adequate for AI-augmented services?

### Section 6: Your Geneva Positioning
- Geneva 2027 World AI Summit = Geneva as global AI governance hub
- Can you position within the Geneva AI governance ecosystem?
- Are your AI tools Swiss-hosted or international cloud?
