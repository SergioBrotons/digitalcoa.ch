# Lead Magnet Framework — Phase 05 Synthesis — Cycle 61

## Lead Magnet Topic
**"The Geneva AI Governance Portfolio" — Q2 2026 Edition**

## Core Question Being Asked
How many of the six Geneva AI governance obligations apply to your firm — and which ones are you already in breach of?

## Lead Magnet Format
Portfolio compliance overview with sector-specific breakdowns — PDF-ready executive briefing

## Angle
Geneva firms face a compound compliance challenge: six overlapping obligations (Federal AI Ordinance, EU AI Act GPAI, EU AI Act HR AI, FADP Art. 21, Swissdamed, Section 301) with three key deadlines in 2026 (April 15, June 15–17, August 2). Most Geneva firms don't know how many of these apply to them simultaneously. This briefing gives executives a clear-eyed view of their actual portfolio exposure.

## Target Persona
- Geneva firm executive (CEO, Managing Partner, CCO, COO)
- Multi-sector awareness (not sector-specialist)
- Making resource allocation decisions for compliance/AI governance
- Primary fear: discovering a compliance gap after an enforcement action
- Secondary fear: over-investing in compliance for obligations that don't apply

## Value Proposition
A structured portfolio assessment: how many of the six obligations apply to your firm, which are the most urgent, and what is the integrated governance response.

## Key Hook Points (from C61 findings)
1. Swiss-EU bilateral package confirmed absent — compound multi-framework cost is the baseline condition
2. August 2, 2026 double deadline confirmed (EU AI Act HR AI conformity + legacy GPAI grandfathering expiry)
3. Swissdamed July 1, 2026 (~91 days) — enforcement posture confirmed, not delayed
4. Section 301 forced labour angle — supply chain ESG intersects with compliance portfolio
5. G7 Évian June 15–17 (~75 days) — cross-sector regulatory event with near-term uncertainty

## Structure
1. The Geneva AI Governance Portfolio — six obligations mapped by sector
2. Your firm's obligation profile (checklist: which of the six apply to your firm?)
3. The three 2026 deadlines ranked by urgency
4. The compound cost insight — why treating frameworks separately is inefficient
5. Integrated governance response — one framework that covers multiple obligations
6. What to do before each deadline

"Need an integrated AI governance framework that covers your actual obligation portfolio — not a generic compliance template? DigitalCoa.ch helps Geneva firms build AI governance that addresses multiple frameworks simultaneously."
