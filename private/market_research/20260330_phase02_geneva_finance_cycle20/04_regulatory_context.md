## 3-Layer Compliance Stack (Confirmed)

**Layer 1 — Swiss Domestic**
- FinSA/FinSO conduct rules
- FINMA Circ. 2026/1 nature risks
- SRO membership obligations

**Layer 2 — Swiss International**
- CARF/CRS 2.0 operative (Jan 1 2026)
- AMLA/LETA H2 2026 (~3-6 months)
- FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027

**Layer 3 — EU Extraterritorial**
- EU AI Act high-risk (Aug 2 2026)
- GDPR + EU AI Act overlap for EU client data

The FATF evaluation is the forcing function — creates external pressure that elevates AMLA compliance to international scrutiny level.
