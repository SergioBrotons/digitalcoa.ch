## ICP: The Geneva Boutique Wealth Manager

### Demographics
- **Firm type:** Independent wealth manager, family office, multi-family office (Geneva-based)
- **AUM context:** CHF 50M–500M typically; price-sensitive on compliance costs; independent by choice
- **Technology posture:** AI-augmented portfolio management, risk analytics, client reporting; not Big Bank infrastructure
- **Decision-maker:** Founding partner or CIO (owns regulatory risk personally)
- **Client base:** UHNW/HNW individuals, families, family offices (Geneva, Vaud, international)
- **Regulatory posture:** FINMA-authorized, SRO member, FATF-subject; actively managing CARF/AMLA obligations

### Pain Points (Validated by Research)
1. **CARF compliance gap:** CARF/CRS 2.0 operative since Jan 1, 2026 — 3 months already elapsed with AI tools potentially not addressed
2. **AMLA/LETA uncertainty:** ~3-6 months to H2 2026 implementing ordinance; 30-day discrepancy reporting; SRO expansion obligations
3. **FATF evaluation pressure:** Switzerland 2026-2027 mutual evaluation; AI applications in AML/compliance are a specific review area
4. **EU AI Act confusion:** ~4.5 months to Aug 2 2026 high-risk obligations; EU-nexus firms face extraterritorial compliance
5. **AI tool compliance:** AI-augmented portfolio management, risk analytics, CRM, reporting — not all firms have addressed AI transparency under nFADP
6. **No boutique AI governance competitor:** 10th+ confirmation; market gap intact

### Trigger Events
- **FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027:** External pressure forcing AMLA documentation and AI governance review
- **CARF 3-month operative window:** Already measurable gap if AI tools not documented
- **AMLA/LETA implementing ordinance:** H2 2026 concrete deadline
- **EU AI Act Aug 2 2026:** For firms with EU client nexus
- **Swissdamed Jul 1 2026 (~13 WEEKS):** Indirect via healthcare client AI governance questions

### Timing Assessment (as of March 30, 2026)
- **CARF operative:** Since Jan 1 2026 — 3 MONTHS already elapsed
- **AMLA/LETA H2 2026:** ~3-6 months
- **FATF mutual evaluation 2026-2027:** Active evaluation period
