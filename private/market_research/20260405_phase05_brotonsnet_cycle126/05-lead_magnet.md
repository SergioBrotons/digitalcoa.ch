## Quality vs. Quantity

**Principle:** Every subscriber should be genuinely qualified Geneva executive.
- Quality signals: Role (CFO, CEO, MD, Board), Company (Geneva-based or Geneva-relevant), Engagement (opens, clicks)
- Quarterly list hygiene: Remove inactive subscribers; re-engagement campaign for dormant subscribers
- Goal: A smaller list of highly engaged Geneva executives is more valuable than a larger list of unqualified subscribers
