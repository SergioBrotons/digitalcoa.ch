# Messaging Framework — Geneva Healthcare AI Governance
## Phase 04 Cycle 44

## Core Message
**"Swissdamed has no grace period. Your AI-assisted clinical notes, diagnostic support, and patient communications may already be subject to registration. And 57% of AI tools produce errors. Do you know which ones you're using?"**

## Key Themes

### Theme 1: Swissdamed July 1 — No Grace Period
**The Clock Is Running**
> "Swissdamed becomes operative July 1, 2026 — approximately 13 weeks from now. There is no grace period. Medical practices using AI in patient care must register those tools. The question is whether you register proactively or reactively."

### Theme 2: 57% Hallucination = Patient Safety Issue
**Not Just Compliance**
> "57% of AI tools produce errors or hallucinations. In a medical practice, that's not a compliance problem — it's a patient safety problem. Before Swissdamed registration, you need to know which tools are in use and what the error rate means for your patients."

### Theme 3: FADP Directly Applicable
**No New Law Required**
> "FADP applies to your clinical AI tools today. FDPIC confirmed in May 2025: existing obligations already cover AI-supported data processing in healthcare. FADP Art. 21 requires human review for automated decisions affecting patients."

### Theme 4: Two-Timeline Urgency
**May 2026 + End-2026**
> "digitalswitzerland publishes its AI Action Plan First Draft in May 2026. Swissdamed is the immediate deadline. Federal Council legislation follows end-2026. You have one urgent deadline and one approaching."

## Tone
- Calm, precise, clinically serious
- Patient safety framing over compliance framing
- No hype, no scare tactics
