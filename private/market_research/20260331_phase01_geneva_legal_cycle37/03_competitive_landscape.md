## Competitive Entry Dynamics

### Why No One Is Serving This ICP

1. **Economics:** Small law firm (2–5 attorneys) billing is insufficient for traditional law firm advisory model
2. **Expertise gap:** AI governance requires combined FADP + AI + legal domain expertise — rare combination
3. **Market awareness:** Small law firms only now realizing AI governance is a compliance obligation
4. **Regulatory clarity:** FADP was ambiguous about AI obligations until 2025–2026 FDPIC guidance
5. **Geographic concentration:** Geneva small law market is specific enough to require localized approach

### What Would Attract Competition

- Swiss regulatory clarity (FADP consultation end-2026 could trigger entry)
- Enforcement signal (FDPIC targeting law firms would create urgency)
- EU AI Act enforcement (Swiss firms with EU nexus facing fines)
- First-mover success (early adopters proving ROI would attract followers)
