## Active Regulatory Framework

### 1. FADP / nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection)

**Status: ACTIVE — Since Sep 1, 2023 (2.5+ years)**

| Obligation | Relevant To | Status |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| Art. 5(c) — Purpose limitation, transparency | AI-supported data processing | ACTIVE |
| Art. 6 — Lawfulness of processing | AI tool data processing | ACTIVE |
| Art. 7-8 — Consent, legitimate interest | AI tool deployment | ACTIVE |
| Art. 22 — Automated decision-making | AI-generated legal analysis | ACTIVE |
| Art. 26 — Data protection by design | AI procurement | ACTIVE |
| Art. 27 — Data protection impact assessment (DPIA) | High-risk AI processing | ACTIVE |
| Privacy by Design / Privacy by Default | AI tool selection | ACTIVE |

**FDPIC Guidance (edoeb.admin.ch):**
- FADP applies to AI-supported data processing without new law
- FDPIC expects purpose + functionality + data sources disclosure for AI deployments
- FDPIC enforcement: legal sector ABSENT from 1,053 complaints (2024/2025 Annual Report)
- FDPIC enforcement: transport, retail, social media — NOT law firms (yet)

**Key Risk:** FADP Art. 5(c) transparency + Privacy by Design are immediate obligations for any law firm using AI tools. No grace period.

**Penalties:** Criminal liability route only (no direct fines under nFADP) — up to CHF 250,000 for intentional failures; managing director personally liable.
