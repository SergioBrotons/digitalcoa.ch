## FADP Compliance Stack (Law Firms)

### Layer 1: FADP Art. 5(c) — Legal Basis
Every AI-assisted data processing must have a documented legal basis (consent, contract, legal obligation, vital interests, public task, legitimate interests)

### Layer 2: Art. 16 — Privacy by Design + Default
Data protection must be considered from the earliest stages of AI planning and implementation

### Layer 3: Art. 21 — Human Review Right
Clients have the right to request human review of automated decisions producing legal effects (AI-assisted contract analysis, legal opinions, billing)

### Layer 4: Transparency Obligation
Purpose, functionality, and data sources of AI-based processing must be disclosed to data subjects (clients)

### Layer 5: DPIA — High-Risk Processing
Required for AI processing involving high risks to personality or fundamental rights (client data at scale)
