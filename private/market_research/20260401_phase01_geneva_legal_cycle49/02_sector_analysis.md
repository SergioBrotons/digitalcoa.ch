## Key Adoption Patterns

### The 3-Tier Adoption Pyramid
```
Tier 1: Individual Use (71%/67%) — solo/small firm lawyers using AI tools personally
Tier 2: Formal Firm Adoption (28%) — firms with formal AI policies/processes
Tier 3: Deep/Widespread (4%) — firms systematically embedding AI in workflows
```

### Shadow AI Problem
69% of individual lawyers using AI without formal firm policy = Shadow AI exposure at scale
Solo/small firm agility paradox: faster adoption + minimal governance = highest risk

### Growth Correlation
Growing firms 2x more likely to use AI vs stable/shrinking firms
AI governance = competitive advantage signal, not just compliance

## Geneva-Specific Factors

**International hub:** Geneva's concentration of private banks, trading houses, commodity firms creates elevated client data sensitivity
**Language:** French/English bilingual market — FR copy required for Geneva outreach
**Regulatory density:** Cross-border work (EU, UK, US) creates dual/triple compliance complexity
**Swiss data residency:** US CLOUD Act creates moat for Swiss-sovereign AI (FABIAN/CASUS)
