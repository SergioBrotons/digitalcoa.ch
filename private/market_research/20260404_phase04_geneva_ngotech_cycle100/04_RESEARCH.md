## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 4)

### EN
**UN/IO Reform and Efficiency Mandates — Consulting Demand from Geneva Institutional Ecosystem**

UN system reform context — 2025-2026:
- Our Common Agenda (Secretary-General António Guterres): launched 2021, implementation ongoing; calls for UN system-wide transformation, better coordination, increased relevance; 75+ commitments including data strategy, UN商务 Council reform, prevention agenda
- UN budget pressure: US (largest contributor at ~22%) demanding efficiency gains and funding reductions; European contributors similarly pushing value-for-money; UN Secretariat facing real-terms budget cuts despite expanding mandate
- Geneva-based UN agencies affected: WHO (largest UN agency, facing reform pressure post-COVID and US funding uncertainty), WTO (budget-constrained, leadership reform), ILO (reform agenda ongoing), ITU, UNHCR, WMO, WTO, IPU — all facing efficiency mandates
- UN reform consulting demand: strategy reviews, organizational restructuring, digital transformation, results-based management implementation, shared services (UN Shared Services Centre in Geneva), HR transformation
- Geneva permanent missions: 180+ embassies in Geneva; mission budget pressures creating demand for efficiency consulting, strategic planning, communication support
- INGOs (International NGOs) in Geneva: 450+ with UN special status; similar efficiency pressures; board governance reviews, strategy development, impact measurement, digital transformation
- Consulting opportunities for Geneva firms: UN procurement is open to Swiss companies; Swiss companies can bid on UN contracts; consulting firms with Geneva presence have natural advantage in UN system relationships
- Specific mandates: UN Digital Strategy (2022) — all agencies required to digitize; AI governance integration; cloud migration (Google Cloud partnership); Geneva agencies as implementation sites
- WEF Global Leadership Transition: UN Secretary-General reform agenda creates consulting demand for WEF engagement strategy; Geneva consultancies advising on multilateral engagement
- Advisory: UN system engagement strategy, UN procurement bid support, organizational efficiency assessments for UN agencies, digital transformation roadmaps, board governance for INGOs

### FR
**Réforme de l'ONU/IO et mandats d'efficacité — Demande consultative de l'écosystème institutionnel genevois**

Contexte de la réforme du système de l'ONU — 2025-2026 :
