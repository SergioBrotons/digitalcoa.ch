### Vector 01
- **Topic:** Outcome-Based and Value-Based Pricing Disruption — Geneva consulting business model shift
- **Finding:** Swiss consulting market shifting from time-based billing to outcome-based/value-based pricing models. Sustainability consulting particularly driving this change — CFOs demanding measurable ROI linked to efficiency gains, cost savings, and revenue growth. Boutique Geneva firms can differentiate on outcome delivery vs. Big 4 time-billing. Market projected to grow from USD 3.58bn (2025) to USD 3.74bn (2026). AI implementation services increasingly bundled as outcome-based deliverables rather than hourly engagements. Implications: Geneva boutique consultants need outcome measurement frameworks and client ROI documentation capabilities.
- **Source:** mordorintelligence.com; verdantix.com; freshfields.com; consultancygrowthnetwork.com; projectcor.com; inc.com
- **Signal Strength:** HIGH — documented market shift, CFO pressure driving adoption
- **Advisory Opportunity:** Geneva boutique firm pricing model transition advisory; outcome measurement framework design for Geneva consultants; ROI documentation methodologies for Geneva AI consulting engagements; value-based retainer structuring for Geneva advisory boutiques

### Vector 02
- **Topic:** Geneva SME Market — underserved boutique consulting segment vs. enterprise focus
- **Finding:** Swiss consulting market traditionally skewed toward large enterprises and financial institutions. SMEs (startups, mid-market, family businesses) in Geneva are underserved by boutique consulting — cost-prohibitive Big 4 retainers, lack of tailored SME offerings. Geneva's startup ecosystem (FONGIT, TechnoArk, ClimeBoost), family enterprise sector, and mid-market commodity traders represent untapped SME consulting demand. Boutiques offering modular, outcome-based, and fixed-fee engagements for SMEs have competitive advantage. Talent scarcity in Geneva for AI/sustainability skills driving consultant rates up — creating both pricing opportunity and SME affordability challenge.
- **Source:** whitelabeladvisory.de; sortlist.com; clutch.co; wtalent.com; mbдconsulting.ch; gcg.org
- **Signal Strength:** MEDIUM-HIGH — market gap documented, supply-demand imbalance
- **Advisory Opportunity:** Geneva SME advisory packages (fixed-fee strategy consulting); family enterprise succession consulting in Geneva; startup advisory (regulatory, strategy, digital) for Geneva entrepreneurs; modular consulting retainers for Geneva mid-market companies; commodity trading SME compliance advisory
- **Status:** NET-NEW C146 ✅
