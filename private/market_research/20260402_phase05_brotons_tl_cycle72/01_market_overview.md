# 01 — Brotons.net: Brand Identity — Geneva AI Governance Publication

## Brotons.net Core Identity

**Tagline position:** Geneva's AI governance lens — connecting multilateral institutions to Swiss regulatory reality.

**Core proposition:** Brotons.net is the only Swiss publication that connects Geneva's UN institutional ecosystem with Swiss domestic AI regulation through an international arbitration lens.

## What Brotons.net Covers

### Primary Coverage Areas
- UN system AI governance (ITU, UNCTAD, UNIDO, WIPO, ICC)
- Swiss AI regulatory developments (FINMA, FADP, Federal Council)
- International arbitration AI developments (ICC, UNCITRAL, Swiss Arbitration Centre, ASA)
- Geneva as AI governance hub
- AI consulting market intelligence (Swiss context)

### Coverage Style
- Analytical, not descriptive
- Structural, not headline-driven
- Swiss regulatory precision
- International context
- Calm, sharp, restrained tone (SOUL.md compliant)

## What Brotons.net Does NOT Cover
- General AI news without Swiss/Geneva connection
- Technology product reviews
- AI ethics without regulatory angle
- Generic decision-making philosophy
- News-aggregation content

## Brand Distinction

vs. Swiss legal blogs: More institutional depth, AI governance focus  
vs. International AI governance: Geneva/Swiss specificity  
vs. Law firm newsletters: Analytical independence, no client service promotion  
vs. Substack AI publications: Regulatory precision, Swiss institutional access
