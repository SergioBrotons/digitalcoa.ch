## DigitalCoa.ch Service Delivery Framework

### Remote Delivery Quality Standards

**DigitalCoa.ch delivers all services remotely (video, email, document sharing) with same quality as on-site advisory:**

**Video Call Standards:**
- Platform: Zoom preferred; Teams acceptable
- Setup: ICR/FH uses professional lighting, microphone, background
- Preparation: Client context reviewed; materials prepared and shared in advance
- During call: Notes taken; action items captured; follow-up promised
- After call: Summary email within 24 hours

**Document Sharing:**
- Platform: Shared secure folder (pCloud, Dropbox Business, or client-preferred)
- Format: PDF + editable version where relevant
- Version control: Clear file naming convention; version history maintained
- Security: No client confidential information in unencrypted form

**Email Standards:**
- Response time: Within 24 hours (business days)
- Tone: Professional, direct, no inflated claims
- Length: Concise; action items clearly stated
- CC: No unnecessary CC; direct communication preferred

---

### Geneva Client Onboarding

**For new DigitalCoa.ch clients:**

**Day 1:**
- Welcome email with engagement terms, work plan, communication protocols
- Shared folder setup (if applicable)
- Calendar invitation for kickoff meeting

**Week 1:**
- Kickoff meeting (60-90 min video call): Deep dive into situation
- Initial assessment provided
- Work plan refined based on kickoff discussion

**Ongoing:**
- Weekly status email (brief; 5-10 lines maximum)
- Bi-weekly substantive call (30-60 min)
- Deliverables as promised; no surprises

---

### Proposal Quality Standards

**Every DigitalCoa.ch proposal follows this structure (2 pages maximum):**

**Header:**
- Client name and context
- Date and proposal reference

**Situation Summary (1 paragraph):**
- Demonstrates understanding of client's specific situation
- No generic language; client-specific observations

**Proposed Approach (3-4 bullets):**
- What we will do
- How we will do it
- What makes this approach appropriate for this situation

**Deliverables:**
- Specific, concrete deliverables
- Numbered list
- Clear ownership (client vs. DigitalCoa.ch)

**Timeline:**
- Key milestones
- Realistic dates
- Dependencies noted

**Investment:**
- Clear pricing
- Payment terms
- No hidden costs

**Next Steps:**
- What happens after client approves
- Immediate first action

---

### Engagement Closure

**At engagement close:**

**Delivery summary:**
- What was delivered vs. what was proposed
- Any scope changes and reasons
- What client received

**Assessment:**
- What worked well
- What could be improved
- Client satisfaction pulse (NPS-style)

**Open items:**
- What remains outstanding
- Recommendations for next steps

**Future relationship:**
- Invitation to quarterly touchpoint
- Referral request (if appropriate)
- Maintenance of relationship without pressure

---

### Quarterly Touchpoint Protocol

**For ongoing retainer clients:**

**Pre-call preparation:**
- Review client's situation since last touchpoint
- Prepare 2-3 Geneva regulatory updates relevant to client
- Prepare questions specific to client's situation

**30-minute call structure:**
- 5 min: Catch-up on client's current situation
- 15 min: Discussion of regulatory/practical implications
- 10 min: Open questions; next steps

**Post-call:**
- Email summary within 24 hours
- Any follow-up items captured and actioned
- Invitation to next quarterly touchpoint
