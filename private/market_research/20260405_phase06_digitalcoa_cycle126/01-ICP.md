## DigitalCoa.ch Service Definition

### Core Service Offering

**Primary Service: Geneva Finance Regulatory Advisory**
- FINMA regulatory change advisory (stablecoins, DLT, TBTF 2.0, DORA)
- Swiss financial regulatory strategy (FINMA, SNB, BVG, SECO)
- Regulatory implementation support (project-based or embedded)
- Briefing call advisory (discovery + recommendations)

**Secondary Services:**
- Geneva Business Regulatory Advisory (AI governance, CARB, EU-CH agreement)
- Geneva NGO/Tech Advisory (humanitarian AI ethics, Swissmedic, UN/IO procurement)
- Geneva Consulting Advisory (M&A, family governance, interim CFO)

### Service Model

**Engagement Types:**

**1. Briefing Call (Entry Point)**
- Format: 30-minute video call (Zoom/Teams)
- Preparation: ICR/FH reviews client context before call
- Output: Initial assessment + 2-3 priority recommendations
- Next step: Project proposal or ongoing advisory engagement
- Conversion rate target: 40-60% of briefing calls convert to paid engagement

**2. Project-Based Advisory**
- Format: Defined scope, timeline, deliverables
- Duration: 4-12 weeks typical
- Pricing: CHF 15K-80K typical
- Use: Specific regulatory question, implementation project, strategic assessment

**3. Retainer/Ongoing Advisory**
- Format: Monthly/quarterly advisory relationship
- Duration: 6-24 months typical
- Pricing: CHF 8K-25K/month typical
- Use: Continuous regulatory monitoring, senior advisor access, trusted counsel

**4. Embedded Advisory**
- Format: On-site or remote presence for defined period
- Duration: 2-6 months
- Pricing: Day-rate or fixed fee
- Use: Regulatory implementation programs, transformation oversight

## Client Engagement Journey

### Stage 1: Discovery (Brotons.net → DigitalCoa.ch Pathway)

**Entry points:**
- Brotons.net article references DigitalCoa.ch for implementation support
- LinkedIn outreach from ICR/FH to Geneva executive
- Referral from Geneva trusted advisor (banker, lawyer, auditor)
- Direct search for Geneva regulatory advisory

**First contact:** Email or LinkedIn inquiry
**Response:** Personalized 2-paragraph response within 24 hours
**Next step:** Invitation to briefing call

### Stage 2: Briefing Call

**Preparation:**
- ICR/FH reviews client's LinkedIn/profile (background research)
- Identify 2-3 specific questions client wants to address
- Prepare brief reading (article or memo) relevant to client's situation

**Format:**
- 30 minutes; video call
- ICR/FH asks clarifying questions; listens more than talks
- Initial assessment provided by end of call
- 2-3 priority recommendations
- Honest assessment of whether DigitalCoa.ch engagement makes sense

**Post-call:**
- Follow-up email within 24 hours
- Summary of key points discussed
- Proposal (if appropriate) or explanation of why engagement not right fit

### Stage 3: Proposal

**Proposal format:**
- 2 pages maximum
- Situation summary (demonstrates understanding)
- Proposed approach
- Deliverables and timeline
- Investment
- Next steps

**Proposal tone:** Direct, professional, no inflated claims
**Proposal quality:** Demonstrates the same expertise that attracted the client

### Stage 4: Engagement

**Onboarding:**
- Welcome email with engagement terms, initial work plan, communication protocols
- First meeting: kickoff + deeper dive into specific situation

**During engagement:**
- Weekly status update (brief email)
- Bi-weekly substantive check-in (30 min call)
- Deliverables on time; no surprises

**Closing:**
- Summary of what was accomplished
- What remains outstanding
- Invitation to ongoing relationship (if appropriate)

### Stage 5: Ongoing Relationship

**Quarterly touchpoint:**
- Brief email with Geneva regulatory update relevant to client's situation
- Optional: 30-min call to discuss implications

**Referral generation:**
- Identify when client's network has relevant needs
- Warm introduction with client permission
- Maintains relationship without pressure
