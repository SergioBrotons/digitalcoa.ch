### Strategic Implications for DigitalCoa.ch

**Service Model:**
1. **Preferred engagement:** Quarterly retainer (CHF 100-200K/year) — sustainable revenue + sustained value
2. **Entry point:** Briefing call (free; 30 min) — demonstrates quality; converts at 40-60%
3. **Project range:** CHF 15-80K for defined engagements
4. **Remote delivery:** Video, email, document sharing — same quality as on-site

**Client Acquisition:**
- Briefing call as primary lead magnet
- Quarterly targets: 6-8 calls, 2-3 new engagements
- Annual targets: 25-30 calls, 8-12 new engagements, CHF 400-600K revenue
- Client concentration: No single client >25% of revenue

**Credibility Architecture:**
- Website: Service clarity + author expertise + Geneva specificity
- Brotons.net: Primary thought leadership platform
- LinkedIn: Social amplification + professional presence
- Thought leadership: What DigitalCoa.ch owns vs. co-creates vs. amplifies

**Referral Engine:**
- Week 1 post-close: Thank you + summary
- Month 1 post-close: Geneva regulatory update
- Month 3 post-close: Quarterly invitation + referral request
- Month 6+ post-close: Annual check-in
