## Market Signal 1: Quarterly Engagement Cadence + Retainer Model — The Geneva Advisory Sweet Spot

**Signal:**
Geneva executives prefer ongoing advisory relationships over one-off projects when regulatory complexity is sustained. The quarterly engagement cadence creates a predictable, high-value advisory model:

**Why quarterly works for Geneva:**
- Swiss regulatory change is continuous (FINMA guidance, SNB decisions, BVG implementation)
- Geneva executives value trusted advisor relationship over transactional project delivery
- Quarterly touchpoints maintain relevance without overloading busy executives
- Retainer creates predictable revenue for DigitalCoa.ch; predictable advisory cost for client

**Quarterly engagement structure:**
- Q1 (January): Year-ahead regulatory planning; FINMA supervisory priorities review
- Q2 (April): Mid-year regulatory check; any significant developments requiring adjustment
- Q3 (July): H2 regulatory outlook; planning for year-end and next year
- Q4 (October): Annual regulatory review; preparation for next year's regulatory agenda

**Pricing model:**
- Quarterly retainer: CHF 30K-60K/quarter (CHF 100K-200K/year)
- Equivalent to: 6-12 days of advisory/year at CHF 4-5K/day
- But with: Continuous availability, quarterly calls, ad hoc email support

**EN:** "Geneva regulatory complexity creates sustained advisory demand — quarterly retainer model (CHF 100-200K/year) balances client value and revenue predictability for DigitalCoa.ch."
**FR:** "La complexité réglementaire genevoise crée une demande de conseil soutenue — le modèle de acompte trimestriel (CHF 100-200K/an) équilibre la valeur client et la prévisibilité des revenus pour DigitalCoa.ch."

## Market Signal 3: Post-Engagement Follow-Up System — The Referral Engine

**Signal:**
The highest-value moment for generating referrals is immediately after an engagement closes successfully. Geneva executives who have a positive advisory experience are willing to introduce DigitalCoa.ch to colleagues — but only if asked in the right way, at the right time.

**Post-engagement follow-up framework:**

**Week 1 post-close:**
- Thank you note (personal, specific to what was accomplished)
- Summary of what was delivered vs. what client expected
- Genuine inquiry: "Is there anything else I should have covered?"

**Month 1 post-close:**
- Geneva regulatory update relevant to client's situation
- No ask; just value delivery

**Month 3 post-close:**
- Invitation to quarterly check-in call
- "Worth catching up on [regulatory development] implications"
- Ask: "Are there colleagues facing similar challenges?"

**Month 6+ post-close:**
- Annual check-in
- Introduce relevant new capability if applicable
- Maintain relationship without pressure

**Referral request timing:**
- Best: When client expresses satisfaction; when they mention a colleague's challenge
- Approach: "If you know someone facing [similar challenge], I'd be happy to have the same conversation"
- Format: Warm introduction preferred; direct referral with permission

**EN:** "Post-engagement follow-up is the highest-conversion referral mechanism — systematic, value-first touchpoints over 6 months post-close generate warm introductions without awkwardness."
**FR:** "Le suivi post-mandat est le mécanisme de parrainage à plus haute conversion — des points de contact systématiques axés sur la valeur sur 6 mois après la clôture génèrent des introductions chaleureuses sans malaise."

## Market Signal 6: Client Satisfaction Measurement Framework

**Signal:**
Geneva advisory relationships require systematic satisfaction measurement without being intrusive. The framework:

**NPS-style measurement adapted for advisory:**
- "How likely are you to recommend DigitalCoa.ch to a Geneva colleague facing a similar challenge?" (0-10)
- Follow-up: "What was most valuable about our engagement?"
- Follow-up: "What could we have done better?"

**Measurement cadence:**
- Post-engagement (Week 2): Initial satisfaction pulse
- 6-month post-close: Relationship satisfaction check
- Annual: Overall engagement satisfaction

**Response handling:**
- Detractors (0-6): Personal follow-up within 48 hours; understand issue
- Passives (7-8): Personal follow-up; understand what would move to Promoter
- Promoters (9-10): Request referral; public testimonial if willing

**Target NPS for DigitalCoa.ch:**
- Minimum: 50+ (strong advisory business)
- Goal: 70+ (excellent Geneva advisory reputation)

**EN:** "Client satisfaction measurement in advisory is relationship maintenance, not audit — systematic, value-first follow-up identifies issues early and generates warm referrals from satisfied clients."
**FR:** "La mesure de la satisfaction client en conseil est un maintien de relation, pas un audit — un suivi systématique et axé sur la valeur identifie les problèmes tôt et génère des parrains chauds auprès des clients satisfaits."
