### SECTION 2: Decision 1 — Do You Have EU AI Act GPAI Exposure?

**GPAI applies if:**
- You provide consulting services to EU-based clients, OR
- Your AI outputs are used by individuals or entities in the EU, OR
- Your AI outputs affect persons in the EU

**If YES:** GPAI transparency obligations are ACTIVE NOW. Disclose AI system + AI-generated content to clients.

**If NO:** Subject to nFADP Art. 5(c) but not GPAI. Proceed to Section 3.

---

### SECTION 3: Decision 2 — Are You Processing Personal Data via AI?

**nFADP Art. 5(c) applies if AI processes personal data (client data, third-party data, employee data).**

**If YES:** Transparency, purpose limitation, data minimisation obligations apply. Inform clients when AI processes their data.
