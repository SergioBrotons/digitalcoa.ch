## EN

# The Geneva Healthcare AI Governance Readiness Checklist
### Q2 2026 — HUG AI Pole, Swissdamed & FADP

**Swissdamed enforcement July 1, 2026: ~90 days remaining.**

Geneva's small medical practices and healthcare institutions face a triple-layer AI governance challenge: Swissdamed's enforcement window is closing, the FADP applies directly to AI systems, and the EU AI Act's extraterritorial scope captures AI diagnostic tools serving EU patients.

**This checklist covers:**
- Swissdamed July 1 readiness: what medical practices must do now
- FADP DPIA for real-time physiological AI systems
- HUG AI Pole as reference standard for Geneva healthcare AI
- EU AI Act Annex III: AI diagnostic tools likely in scope
- UVG + Swissdamed + FADP triple-layer for sexual violence victim services
- Federal Council sector-specific approach: what Swissmedic guidance means when it arrives
