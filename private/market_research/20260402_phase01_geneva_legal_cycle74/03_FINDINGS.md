### 1. Swiss Federal Supreme Court — 5 New BGE Decisions (Jan–Feb 2026)
Via SwissBlawg — these represent fresh Swiss highest-court jurisprudence:

- **BGE 1C_225/2025** (Feb 9, 2026): Land use planning — whether land can be assigned different above-ground and underground uses; implications for zoning classification as construction vs. non-construction zone. Landmark planning law question.

- **BGE 5A_114/2025** (Jan 13, 2026): International litispendance — whether filing an optional conciliation petition under Art. 199(2) CPC triggers international litispendance under Art. 9(2) PILA; consequences of defective authorization on international jurisdiction.

- **BGE 5A_844/2024** (Feb 16, 2026): Maintenance/alimony — burden of proof on the maintenance-obligated person to assert and prove the marital standard of living. Both marital and post-divorce maintenance require maintaining the standard of living established during the marriage.

- **BGE 7B_550/2024** (Jan 23, 2026): Criminal procedure — lawfulness of data imaging/mirroring before de-sealing order, obtained in a weapons investigation. Digital evidence procedure question.

- **BGE 1C_335/2024** (Feb 2, 2026): Administrative law — subject partially visible; Federal Supreme Court decision on administrative matter.
