## Service Module Architecture (6 Cross-Vertical Modules — C313 Post-Pause Framework)

The Geneva Cross-Vertical AI Accountability Briefing delivers one evidence architecture with six service modules. The six modules are the C313 post-pause framework — applicable across all four verticals (legal, finance, consulting, healthcare) with vertical-specific calibration for the professional accountability framework (Article 321 CC / Article 9 / Article 52 / Article 321 CP medical secrecy + Article 63).

### The Six Modules

1. **Post-Pause Attestation Loop Closure Audit** — CHF 2,200. Audits the attestation-completion workflow the Geneva professional uses in any vertical. Identifies the attestation-completion tools (AI assistant, attestation-completion vendor, copy-paste from EMR export, attestation-template AI) that process patient / client / mandate / engagement confidences through vendors the requesting authority has not separately evaluated. Evaluates whether the attestation response becomes generic (because every professional using the same workflow produces similar language). Produces a remediation plan for an attestation response anchored to vertical-specific evidence. Triggered by post-pause attestation cycle, mid-year carrier check-in, attestation-completion tool change, AI tool inventory change. Universal across verticals.

2. **Post-Pause Multi-Per-Period Documentation Refresh** — CHF 2,200. Audits the Geneva professional's documentation cycle against the post-pause audit cadence (annual + mid-year + quarterly + monthly depending on vertical and accountability channel). Identifies the freshness gap between the documentation cycle and the audit cadence. Produces a documentation cycle redesign matching the audit cadence. Delivers the documentation refresh at the audit cadence required (6 months for mid-year check-in, 3 months for institutional follow-up, 3 months for engagement follow-up, etc.). Universal across verticals.

3. **Post-Pause Authorization Chain Handover Documentation** — CHF 2,200. Documents AI authorization as a separate event from principal relationship authorization. Produces: (a) AI authorization template documentation for each vertical's principal relationship type (employment contract for legal / mandate agreement for finance / sub-consultant SOW for consulting / Convention de remplacement for healthcare); (b) authorization chain handover documentation triggered at each principal relationship event (hire date / mandate start / sub-consultant engagement / coverage period start); (c) AI tool inventory update at each handover (the new principal may use different AI tools); (d) authorization chain handover documentation refresh at each audit cadence. Universal across verticals with vertical-specific calibration.

4. **Post-Pause Vendor Sub-Processor Chain Audit (Swiss-Hosted Marketing Fallacy)** — CHF 2,200. Audits the vendor's per-tool sub-processor chain (authentication, telemetry, model inference, backup, parent-company cloud, parent-company sub-processors) against the professional accountability framework (Article 321 CC for legal / Article 9 nFADP for finance / Article 52 nFADP for consulting / Article 321 CP medical secrecy + Article 63 nFADP for healthcare). Identifies which sub-processors create extraterritorial exposure. Produces a per-tool sub-processor audit memo with vertical-specific professional accountability compatibility assessment. Universal across verticals with vertical-specific calibration.

5. **Post-Pause Named-Peer Benchmark Gap Analysis** — CHF 1,650. Monitors the named-peer AI governance artifacts published at Geneva professional conferences (Ordre des Avocats de Genève + Geneva Bar Association + SBA Day of Lawyers for legal; Geneva Private Banking Week + Geneva Family Office Forum + Swiss WM Association for finance; Geneva Strategy Network + Geneva Management Consulting Council + Swiss Consulting Association + IMC Switzerland for consulting; AMG Geneva specialty sections + FMH CME + SSMIG Journée + HUG Grand Rounds for healthcare). Maps the Geneva professional's current AI governance documentation against the named-peer benchmark. Produces a peer benchmark gap analysis with remediation priorities. Triggered by annual Geneva conference cycle, new named-peer artifact publication, attestation cycle preparation. Universal across verticals with vertical-specific calibration.

6. **Post-Pause AI Vendor Succession / Data Portability Planning** — CHF 2,200. Plans for AI vendor succession events (bankruptcy, acquisition, service termination, regulatory pivot). Includes: (a) pre-vendor-selection succession terms in vendor contracts with professional accountability continuity clauses (Article 321 CC / Article 9 / Article 52 / Article 321 CP medical secrecy + Article 63); (b) mid-vendor-relationship succession monitoring (vendor financial signals, acquisition signals, service-termination signals, regulatory pivot signals); (c) post-vendor-exit response architecture (data portability execution, controller-change documentation, successor-controller audit, patient / client / institutional client / engagement notification architecture, attestation disclosure). Triggered by AI vendor financial signal, vendor acquisition announcement, vendor service termination announcement, vendor contract renewal. Universal across verticals with vertical-specific calibration.
