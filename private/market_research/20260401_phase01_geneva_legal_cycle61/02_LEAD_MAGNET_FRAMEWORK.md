## Lead Magnet: "The Small Law Firm AI Governance Question" — April 2026 Edition EN/FR

### Core Proposition
Geneva's small and mid-size law firms face a five-layer regulatory convergence: FADP + Federal AI Ordinance + EU AI Act GPAI obligations (already in force) + Section 301 trade defence obligations + CoE AI Convention implementation. Most are doing almost none of this. This lead magnet positions DigitalCoa.ch as the authority that makes this manageable for small firms.

### Target Audience
- Partners and managing partners at small/mid-size Geneva law firms (5-50 lawyers)
- Sole practitioners in Geneva
- In-house legal teams at SMEs in Geneva
- Legal operations professionals

### Primary CTA
Download the framework → Book a 30-min AI governance briefing call

1. "I don't know which laws apply to our AI tools"

3. "Section 301 comments deadline is in 14 days — what does this mean for our clients?"
