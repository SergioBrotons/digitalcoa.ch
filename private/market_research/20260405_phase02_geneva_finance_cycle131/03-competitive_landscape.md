# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## Geneva Finance — C131

## Insurance and Reinsurance Advisory

**Key Players:** Swiss Re, Zürich Versicherungs Gruppe, Helvetia Versicherungen, Bâloise, Generali Schweiz, Movplus (Geneva captive specialist), Geneva reinsurance intermediaries

**Differentiation:** 
- Pure reinsurance tied assets authorization advisory is a narrow niche; most Geneva insurance brokers focus on primary brokerage rather than FINMA authorization work
- IFRS 17 internal capital allocation advisory is primarily offered by Big 4 actuarial practices (Deloitte, PwC) and specialist actuarial boutiques
- Independent advisory without audit conflict is rare — creates positioning opportunity

**Opportunity:** Independent FINMA reinsurance authorization advisory without audit conflict. Geneva life insurers and reinsurance intermediaries with Jan 1 2026 authorization needs represent a defined, time-sensitive advisory market.

## Treasury and Payment Infrastructure Advisory

**Key Players:** Aduno Group, Swisscard, Bank CIC treasury services, Raiffeisen Switzerland, BDO treasury advisory, treasury boutiques in Geneva (Axa, Lombard Odier treasury)

**Differentiation:**
- SIC5 migration advisory is primarily offered by system integrators (Accenture, Avaloq banking platform) and payment consultants
- Independent treasury advisory disconnected from platform implementation is underserved in Geneva
- ISO 20022 data migration is creating new data quality and treasury analytics opportunities

**Opportunity:** Independent SIC5/ISO 20022 treasury advisory without platform vendor conflict. Geneva treasury functions at commodity trading houses and private banks are underserved by independent advisors.

## Climate Risk and TCFD Advisory

**Key Players:** South Pole Group (Zurich), Carbon Trust Switzerland, WWF Switzerland, Climate Risks Switzerland (CRS), academic centres (ETH Zurich, University of Geneva), specialist ESG boutiques

**Differentiation:**
- TCFD-aligned climate risk quantification is dominated by specialist ESG boutiques and academic centres
- Big 4 have TCFD practices but are typically tied to audit/assurance relationships
- Scope 3 financed emissions quantification for investment portfolios is a distinct niche

**Opportunity:** TCFD/ISSB disclosure advisory for Geneva institutional investors and listed companies. Independent advisory without audit conflict in climate risk quantification and TCFD report design.

## ICT Risk Advisory

**Key Players:** INFOVISTA (Geneva), Akamai Switzerland, Cybermart (Zurich), BDO ICT audit, KPMG cyber, PwC operational risk, AdNovum, Liip (Geneva digital agency), Abacus Research

**Differentiation:**
- FINMA ICT risk circular gap assessment is primarily offered by Big 4 with existing audit relationships
- Independent ICT risk advisory for Geneva financial institutions is underserved
- Cyber incident response planning is dominated by IT security firms rather than regulatory advisory specialists

**Opportunity:** FINMA ICT risk circular gap assessment and third-party risk register development. Geneva FIs need independent assessment of ICT risk posture vs. FINMA expectations without Big 4 audit conflict.

## Private Credit / Direct Lending Advisory

**Key Players:**tp|

Swiss asset managers offering direct lending funds: Swiss Life Asset Managers, Beadel (Geneva-based), BlueOrchard (Geneva impact investor),Alternatives Capital (Zurich), Geneva family offices allocating directly, Helvetia and Bâloise insurance investment arms

**Differentiation:**
- Fund formation structuring for Swiss private credit is offered by Swiss private banks with AM arms and specialist fund lawyers
- Independent advisor positioning between institutional investors and direct lending fund managers is nascent
- Regulatory classification (FinMIA vs. AIFM vs. unregulated) advisory is primarily legal work

**Opportunity:** Independent advisory for Geneva institutional investors allocating to direct lending: manager selection, due diligence framework, regulatory classification navigation.
