# 05 — Lead Magnet
## Geneva Finance — C131

## Recommended Lead Magnet: "Geneva Financial Institution Regulatory Readiness Barometer 2026"

**Format:** Annual diagnostic framework + brief assessment (5-7 pages EN + executive FR summary)
**Target:** CFO, CRO, Head of Compliance at Geneva-based financial institutions (banks, insurers, asset managers, commodity trading houses with treasury)
**Primary CTA:** Download the Barometer; book a 30-minute regulatory readiness call

**Content Framework:**

1. **Regulatory deadline tracker** — Geneva-relevant regulatory deadlines H2 2026 through 2027:
   - Jan 1 2026: FINMA reinsurance tied assets authorization (new applications)
   - 2026: SIC5 instant payment migration milestones
   - 2026: Swiss TCFD/ISSB adoption (Federal Council decision expected)
   - Jan 2027: Basel IV/FAIR RWA floor full implementation
   - 2026-2027: FINMA ICT risk circular examination priorities

2. **Geneva financial institution readiness self-assessment** (scored 1-5):
   - Reinsurance tied assets: FINMA authorization application status
   - IFRS 17: Internal capital allocation framework maturity
   - SIC5/ISO 20022: Treasury system migration project status
   - TCFD/ISSB: Climate risk disclosure preparation status
   - ICT risk: Third-party risk register and resilience testing status
   - Direct lending allocation: Institutional investment decision framework

3. **Geneva-specific competitive context** (brief):
   - Which Geneva institutions are ahead on IFRS 17 internal capital allocation
   - TCFD adoption rates among SIX-listed Geneva institutions
   - Direct lending allocation trends among Geneva pension funds

4. **Advisory implications** (non-promotional):
   - Why each readiness gap matters for regulatory risk and competitive position
   - What independent advisory can address vs. what requires Big 4 or platform vendors

**Distribution:**
- Brotons.net as primary publication (EN + FR)
- LinkedIn newsletter excerpt (2 key findings)
- Email to Geneva CFO/CRO network (if/when available)
- DigitalCoa.ch as secondary distribution

**Differentiation from C130 lead magnet (Basel IV Readiness Index):**
C130 focused on Basel IV capital optimization. This barometer covers the broader regulatory portfolio of urgency items across insurance (reinsurance authorization, IFRS 17), payments (SIC5), climate disclosure (TCFD/ISSB), and operational risk (ICT circular) — complementary to the Basel IV work rather than duplicative.
