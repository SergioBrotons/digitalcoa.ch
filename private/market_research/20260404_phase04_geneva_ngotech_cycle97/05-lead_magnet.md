# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva NGO & Tech Decision-Makers

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "Geneva Startup Regulatory Readiness Assessment"
**Format:** Online checklist + results summary
**Target:** Tech startup founders and CEOs (Geneva/FONGIT network)
**Value:** Self-assess readiness across NCSC reporting, nFADP, CRS 2.0/CARF, LETA, AMLA
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, stage, sector, most urgent priority
**Conversion path:** Results → consultation → regulatory advisory retainer

### Option B: "NGO Cyber & Data Governance Framework"
**Format:** Practical guide (EN + FR)
**Target:** Executive Directors and Operations leads at Geneva NGOs
**Value:** Cyber incident response plan template + nFADP data governance checklist
**Lead capture:** Name, organization, sector, staff size
**Conversion path:** Guide → assessment → governance engagement

### Option C: "Geneva Fintech Regulatory Navigation Map"
**Format:** Visual one-pager + detailed annex
**Target:** Fintech founders navigating FINMA, AMLA, CRS 2.0
**Value:** Single reference for 2026 regulatory landscape; prioritization guide
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, product type, current regulatory status
**Conversion path:** Map → consultation → regulatory advisory

## Lead Magnet Recommendation (C97)

**Primary:** Option A (Regulatory Readiness Assessment) for tech startups — most scalable; targets highest-urgency segment; FONGIT referral channel potential

**Secondary:** Option B (NGO Cyber & Data Framework) — differentiated; no Geneva boutique is offering this specifically to NGOs

**Approach:** Launch Option A targeting FONGIT startups via FONGIT partnership; use Option B as secondary for NGO segment via Geneva International networks

## Bilingual Considerations (C97)
- Tech startups: English primary
- NGOs: French primary for Geneva-based; English for international organizations
