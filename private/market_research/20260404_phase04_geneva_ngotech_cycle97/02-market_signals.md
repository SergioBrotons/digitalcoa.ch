# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva NGO & Tech Sector

## Geneva Ecosystem Overview

### NGOs
- **Scale:** ~800 NGOs + 30 international organizations in Geneva; largest European NGO hub after Brussels
- **Types:** Humanitarian, development, human rights, environment, peacebuilding
- **Key bodies:** UNOG, ICRC, IFRC, WTO, WHO, WMO
- **Staff size:** 3-500+ depending on organization

### Tech Startups
- **Scale:** FONGIT supports 100+ startups; CERN spin-offs; startup corridor along Route des Acacias
- **Sectors:** SaaS, fintech, AI/ML, cybersecurity, health tech, clean tech
- **Stage:** Seed to Series B; median raise CHF 1-3M
- **Geneva advantage:** Proximity to corporates, IOs, banking expertise

### Key Demand Signals (Updated C97)

#### 1. Cyber Security — Escalating Threat (Ongoing)
- Swiss cyber threat level: HIGH (NCSC assessment)
- Mandatory reporting for critical infrastructure since April 2025
- AI-enhanced attacks emerging; phishing, ransomware, supply chain attacks
- **Implication:** Both NGOs (humanitarian data) and tech startups (IP, user data) need cyber governance frameworks

#### 2. Data Protection Enforcement — nFADP (NEW C97)
- Swiss DPA intensifying nFADP enforcement in 2026
- Tech startups with EU/Swiss user data face enforcement risk
- NGOs with beneficiary data face similar exposure
- **Implication:** Data governance policies and incident response plans needed

#### 3. Nature-Related Risk for Environmental NGOs (NEW C97)
- FINMA Circular 2026/1 signals increased expectation for nature risk assessment
- Environmental NGOs face donor/institutional expectations for nature risk reporting
- **Implication:** Framework for environmental NGOs to assess and report nature-related risks

#### 4. Fintech Regulatory Compliance (NEW C97)
- CRS 2.0/CARF (January 2026): crypto tax reporting for fintech startups with crypto exposure
- LETA beneficial ownership (H2 2026): startups with corporate structures need compliance process
- AMLA revision: fintech-adjacent firms may need SRO consideration
- **Implication:** Regulatory navigation advisory for fintech and crypto-adjacent startups
