# 01 — ICP: Geneva NGOs & Tech Startups

## Target Segments

### Segment A: Geneva-Based NGOs
- **Types:** International organizations (IOs), international NGOs (INGOs), Swiss NGOs, humanitarian agencies
- **Size:** Small offices (3-20 staff) to large missions (50-500+ staff)
- **Geneva concentration:** ~800 NGOs + 30 IOs; largest concentration in Europe after Brussels
- **Key concern:** Operational security, data governance, donor transparency, regulatory compliance in host countries

### Segment B: Geneva-Based Tech Startups
- **Types:** SaaS, fintech, AI/ML, cybersecurity, health tech
- **Stage:** Seed to Series B; typically 5-50 employees
- **Geneva hubs:** FONGIT, CERN, Pharma « corridor
- **Key concern:** Fundraising, regulatory compliance, IP protection, cyber security, GDPR/nFADP data handling

### Decision-Making Unit

#### NGOs
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|
| Executive Director / Country Director | Senior, mission-driven | Strategic direction, donor compliance, security |
| Head of Operations | Logistics/programs | Data management, operational security |
| Finance / Grants Manager | Compliance-focused | Donor reporting, audit requirements |

#### Tech Startups
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|
| CEO / Founder | Vision + execution | Fundraising, regulatory navigation, hiring |
| CTO / Tech Lead | Technical leader | Security, data governance, AI tool decisions |
| COO / Operations | Business operations | Compliance, vendor management |

### Key Pain Points (Updated C97)

#### NGOs
1. **Operational security** — cyber threats targeting humanitarian data
2. **Donor data governance** — GDPR/nFADP compliance for beneficiary data
3. **Nature/ESG reporting** — environmental NGOs facing new reporting expectations
4. **NEW — AI tool usage** — ChatGPT/AI for program data raises confidentiality concerns

#### Tech Startups
1. **Cyber security** — NCSC mandatory reporting (since April 2025) + AI-enhanced threats
2. **Data governance** — nFADP enforcement intensifying; user data exposure
3. **Regulatory navigation** — FINMA licensing, AML obligations, LETA compliance
4. **NEW — Crypto tax reporting** — CRS 2.0/CARF (January 2026) affects fintech with crypto exposure
5. **NEW — LETA beneficial ownership** — startups with corporate structures need beneficial owner process
