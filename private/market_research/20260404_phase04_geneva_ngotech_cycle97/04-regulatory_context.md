## 2026 Developments (NEW — C97 Priority)

### NCSC Mandatory Cyber Incident Reporting (Since April 2025) — Priority 1
- **What:** Critical infrastructure operators must report cyber incidents to NCSC
- **NGOs affected:** Humanitarian organizations classified as critical infrastructure
- **Tech startups affected:** Fintech, health tech, cloud infrastructure
- **Advisory opportunity:** Cyber incident response plan development; NCSC reporting procedure documentation

### nFADP Enforcement Intensifying (2026) — Priority 2
- **What:** Swiss DPA increasing enforcement activity
- **Tech startups:** User data handling, consent management, data breach notification
- **NGOs:** Beneficiary data, donor data under nFADP
- **Advisory opportunity:** nFADP gap assessment; data protection policy development

### CRS 2.0 / CARF (January 2026) — Priority 3
- **What:** Crypto assets included in automatic tax information exchange
- **Affected:** Fintech startups with crypto exposure
- **Advisory opportunity:** CRS 2.0 implementation briefing; crypto tax reporting gap assessment

### LETA Beneficial Ownership (H2 2026) — Priority 4
- **What:** Federal register of beneficial owners of Swiss legal entities
- **Affected:** Tech startups with Swiss corporate structures
- **Advisory opportunity:** LETA compliance checklist for startups
