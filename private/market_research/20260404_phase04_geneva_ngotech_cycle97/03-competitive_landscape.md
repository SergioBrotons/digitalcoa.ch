## Positioning Opportunity for DigitalCoa (C97)

### For NGOs
- **Gap:** No Geneva advisory firm offers practical cyber governance + data protection framework for small-to-medium NGOs
- **Entry:** Low-cost cyber/data governance checklist → framework → assessment

### For Tech Startups
- **Gap:** Regulatory navigation advisory at startup-compatible price points (vs. law firm fees)
- **Entry:** Regulatory readiness assessment → FINMA/AMLA/LETA gap analysis → ongoing advisory
- **Unique angle:** Bilingual EN+FR at startup scale — most Geneva startup support is English-only

## Competitive Moat Elements (C97)
1. **Bilingual EN+FR** — unique in Geneva startup advisory
2. **FONGIT relationship potential** — direct referral channel into 100+ startups
3. **2026 regulatory cascade awareness** — CRS 2.0/CARF, LETA, nFADP, NCSC reporting — few are helping startups
4. **NGOs + tech startups = two verticals, one advisory framework** — efficiency in content development
