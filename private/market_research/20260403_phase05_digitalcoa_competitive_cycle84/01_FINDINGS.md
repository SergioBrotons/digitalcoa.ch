**Finding (from C84 Phase 01):** DIFC Courts launched Digital Economy Court with AI/blockchain/robotics jurisdiction and 5-year growth strategy.

**DigitalCoa implication:**  
Geneva companies with AI products or AI-adjacent international operations now face a new strategic question: which dispute resolution forum for AI-related conflicts? Swiss arbitration vs DIFC DEC. This is a governance architecture question at the institutional level. DigitalCoa's positioning around "decision architecture for the algorithmic age" maps directly to this — organizations need frameworks for making the forum/governance decision before disputes arise.

**White space:** Proactive advisory for Geneva AI companies on dispute resolution governance architecture — before a dispute forces the decision under pressure.
