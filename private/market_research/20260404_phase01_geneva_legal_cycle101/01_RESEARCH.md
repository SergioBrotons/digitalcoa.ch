## Domain Knowledge Synthesis

### Swiss-US Data Transfer Framework
- **Swiss FDPIC adequacy decision (July 2023):** Switzerland granted adequacy to the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), following the EU's own DPF adequacy decision. This allows Swiss companies to transfer personal data to US companies certified under DPF without additional safeguards.
- **Swiss FADP vs. GDPR divergence:** The Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP, revised 2020, in force 2023) is broadly similar to GDPR but not identical. Cross-border transfer rules differ — FADP Art. 16 requires "adequate protection" but Switzerland has its own standard, not a direct copy of GDPR Chapter V.
- **Post-Schrems II context:** Swiss-US transfers previously required Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs). The FDPIC adequacy decision substantially reduced compliance burden but does not eliminate it — US government surveillance risks persist for sensitive data categories.
- **Swiss-US bilateral agreement negotiations:** Broader CH-US enhanced cooperation on data protection are ongoing but no formal treaty yet.
- **DPIA requirements:** For Geneva-based financial institutions, pharmaceutical companies, and international organizations transferring EU/CH personal data to US cloud providers (AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud), Data Protection Impact Assessments are mandatory under both GDPR and FADP.
- **Geneva-specific exposure:** UN organizations, IOs, NGOs headquartered in Geneva transfer significant volumes of sensitive personal data globally — the Swiss DPF certification adds a layer but DPA guidance from FDPIC continues to require careful transfer documentation.

### Advisory Entry Points
- DPF gap analysis for Geneva companies previously relying on SCCs
- FADP vs. GDPR transfer compliance audits for CH/EU dual-compliance entities
- US government surveillance risk assessment for sensitive data categories
- Cross-border data transfer policies for Geneva financial institutions and IOs
- DPIA drafting for US cloud provider onboarding
