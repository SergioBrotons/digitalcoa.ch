### Decision 0: Shadow AI Clinical Audit (Primary Entry)
What AI tools are your clinical staff already using — with patient data?
Shadow AI in clinical context = Swissdamed violation + FADP Art. 5(c) + potential life safety risk.
"AI may already be in your practice. Swissdamed doesn't care whether you knew."

### Section 1: Swissdamed July 1 — ~13 WEEKS NO GRACE (#1 in Portfolio)
NO GRACE PERIOD confirmed by Swissmedic. AI diagnostic imaging and AI symptom triage tools = high-risk SaMD with immediate July 1 obligations. If your practice uses any AI for clinical decision support, you are subject to Swissdamed starting July 1.

### Section 2: 43% NO Formal AI Policy (Primary Urgency — Cross-Phase)
Wolters Kluwer March 2026: 43% of practices have NO formal AI policy. Swissdamed makes this existential — not theoretical.

### Section 3: 30/60/90 Day Swissdamed Action Sequence
- Day 1–30: Inventory all AI tools used in clinical workflows
- Day 31–60: Assess Swissdamed classification for each tool
- Day 61–90: Implement governance + documentation for July 1 compliance

### Section 4: DPIA for Clinical AI
Data Protection Impact Assessment required for AI tools processing patient health data. FADP + Swissdamed双重要求. Clinical AI = almost certainly high-risk.

### Section 5: 57% AI Errors/Hallucinations Top Threat
In clinical context, AI hallucinations = potential life safety events. Governance isn't just compliance — it's patient safety.

### Section 6: Swissdamed/EUDAMED Dual Burden
Swissdamed and EUDAMED are NOT interconnected. Practices with devices in both systems face dual registration requirements. Plan for both.

### Section 7: CHUV Meditron May 2026 (~1 month away)
Major institutional AI adoption signals professional-grade AI entering clinical workflows. Smaller practices need governance frameworks to compete and comply.

### Section 8: 65% Efficiency Gain (NEW Phase 01 Cycle 35)
65% of firms save 1-5h/week with generative AI. 40% workflow reduction. Clinical efficiency gains are real — but only with governance that ensures patient safety.
