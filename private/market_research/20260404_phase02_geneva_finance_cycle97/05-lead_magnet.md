# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Asset Managers & Family Offices

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "Geneva Asset Manager Regulatory Readiness Assessment"
**Format:** Interactive checklist (15-20 items covering 2026 regulatory package)
**Target:** CIO, Head of Compliance, General Counsel
**Value:** Self-assess readiness across CRS 2.0/CARF, crypto custody, nature risk, BFSA, LETA, operational resilience
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, AUM range, role, most urgent 2026 priority
**Conversion path:** Assessment → priority findings → consultation offer → advisory engagement

### Option B: "The Geneva AM 2026 Regulatory Handbook"
**Format:** Structured guide (25-30 pages, EN + FR executive summary)
**Target:** C-suite at Geneva asset managers and family offices
**Value:** Single reference document covering all 6 major 2026 regulatory changes with practical implications
**Lead capture:** Firm profile, role, AUM, regulatory focus areas
**Conversion path:** Executive summary → full handbook → discovery call

### Option C: "CRS 2.0 / CARF Implementation Briefing — Geneva Edition"
**Format:** Focused briefing (8-10 pages)
**Target:** Head of Compliance, Tax, Legal Operations
**Value:** Practical implementation guide for crypto-asset tax reporting under CRS 2.0/CARF
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, crypto exposure (yes/no), AUM
**Conversion path:** Briefing → implementation consultation → ongoing advisory

### Option D: "Nature-Related Risk Framework for Geneva ESG Mandates"
**Format:** Framework document with worked examples
**Target:** ESG portfolio managers, Head of Risk
**Value:** Practical methodology for integrating FINMA Circular 2026/1 nature risk requirements into portfolio assessment
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, ESG AUM, mandate type
**Conversion path:** Framework → risk assessment engagement → portfolio advisory

## Lead Magnet Recommendation (C97)

**Primary:** Option B (The Geneva AM 2026 Regulatory Handbook) — the unprecedented volume of 2026 changes (6 major items) creates demand for a single authoritative reference. This positions DigitalCoa as the essential Geneva AM regulatory intelligence platform.

**Secondary:** Option C (CRS 2.0/CARF Implementation Briefing) — most technically urgent change; compliance teams need implementation guidance now.

**Rationale:** Asset managers are facing simultaneous implementation of CRS 2.0/CARF, crypto custody governance, nature risk frameworks, BFSA legal updates, LETA beneficial ownership, and operational resilience — they need a consolidated reference more than fragmented individual briefings.

## Bilingual Considerations (C97)
- Primary: English (Geneva AM community is highly international)
- French executive summary and key findings for local Geneva credibility
- Regulatory terminology in original language (FINMA, FINMA, AMLA, LETA, CRS, CARF)
