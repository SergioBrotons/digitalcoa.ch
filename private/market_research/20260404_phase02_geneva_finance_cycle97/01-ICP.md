# 01 — ICP: Geneva Asset Managers & Family Offices

## Target Segment: Geneva-Based Asset Managers and Family Offices

### Firm Profile
- **Asset Managers:** CHF 500M–50B+ AUM; typically licensed by FINMA as fund manager, asset manager, or family office structure
- **Family Offices:** Single or multi-family; CHF 100M–1B+ investable assets; Geneva-based with international portfolios
- **Locations:** Geneva primary; Zug for tax-efficient structures; Luxembourg for UCITS/AIFM wrappers
- **Distribution:** Swiss-domiciled funds; cross-border into EU, UK, Middle East, Asia

### Decision-Making Unit
| Role | Profile | Pain Points |
|---|---|---|
| Chief Investment Officer / Portfolio Manager | Investment professional | Portfolio risk, performance attribution, ESG reporting |
| Head of Risk / Risk Manager | Risk professional | Market risk, operational risk, regulatory reporting |
| General Counsel / Head of Legal | Legal executive | Contract review, regulatory change, compliance |
| COO / Operations | Non-investment executive | Technology, outsourcing, vendor management |
| Head of Compliance | Compliance professional | FINMA reporting, AML, ESG disclosure |

### Geneva Examples
- Lombard Odier (Geneva HQ, 150+ years, ~CHF 600B+ AUM)
- Pictet (Geneva HQ, ~CHF 500B+ AUM)
- Swisscanto (Zug/Geneva, part of ZKB)
- Gonet (Geneva private bank)
- Lombard General Reserve (Geneva-based)
- Syz Group (Geneva)
- Geneva-based family offices (multiple, often unlisted)

### Pain Points (Updated C97)
1. **Regulatory change overload** — 6+ major FINMA/legislative changes in H1 2026 alone
2. **CRS 2.0 / CARF implementation** — crypto assets now in automatic exchange; technical and legal infrastructure needed
3. **Nature-related risk integration** — FINMA Circular 2026/1 requires systematic climate/environmental risk integration
4. **Crypto custody governance** — FINMA Guidance 01/2026 creates new legal due diligence on custodians
5. **Third-party technology risk** — operational resilience requirements fully in force
6. **UK cross-border distribution** — BFSA creates new market access opportunity but requires legal framework updates
7. **NEW — LETA beneficial ownership** — AMs managing corporate entities must support beneficial owner reporting

### Buying Triggers (Updated C97)
- FINMA examination with technology risk findings
- CRS 2.0/CARF non-compliance risk
- Nature-related risk reporting requirement from institutional investors
- BFSA UK market access decision
- AUM growth creating operational strain
