# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva Asset Management

## Geneva Asset Management Market Overview

### Market Size
- **Swiss fund industry AUM:** ~CHF 1.4 trillion (SFAMA 2025)
- **Geneva share:** Significant concentration (Lombard Odier, Pictet headquartered in Geneva)
- **Private banking AUM:** ~CHF 2-3 trillion in Switzerland; Geneva is largest center
- **Family office segment:** Growing; estimated 100+ single/multi-family offices in Geneva region

### Key Demand Signals (Updated C97)

#### 1. 2026 Regulatory Cascade — Unprecedented Volume
- **6 major regulatory changes in H1 2026:** FINMA Guidance 01/2026, FINMA Circular 2026/1, BFSA, CRS 2.0/CARF, LETA, operational resilience
- This represents the most significant H1 regulatory volume since FinSA/FinIA (2020)
- **Implication:** Asset managers need external regulatory advisory to prioritize and interpret changes

#### 2. Crypto Regulation Maturing (NEW C97)
- **CRS 2.0 + CARF (January 2026):** Automatic exchange of information now includes crypto-assets
- **FINMA Guidance 01/2026:** Custody of crypto-based assets — supervisory expectations for custodians
- **Implication:** Asset managers with crypto exposure (directly or via funds) need legal infrastructure for tax reporting and custody governance

#### 3. ESG / Nature-Related Risks (NEW C97)
- **FINMA Circular 2026/1:** Nature-related financial risks — mandatory for banks and insurance companies; portfolio-level implications for AMs
- **SFAMA ESG disclosure requirements** already in place; enforcement intensifying
- **Implication:** Asset managers, especially those with ESG mandates, need nature risk integration frameworks

#### 4. Cross-Border Distribution (NEW C97)
- **BFSA UK-Switzerland (January 2026):** Mutual recognition opens UK market access for Swiss asset managers
- **Implication:** New legal/compliance framework required for UK distribution; Geneva AMs with UK client base benefit

#### 5. Operational Resilience — Full Enforcement (NEW C97)
- FINMA Circular 23/01 transitional periods concluded January 2026
- **Implication:** All requirements now fully in force; technology risk, cyber resilience, third-party governance mandatory
