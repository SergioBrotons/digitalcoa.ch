## Findings (Cycle 99 — Slot 1)

### EN
**Geneva Startup Ecosystem — Regulatory Environment and Scaleup Pipeline**

Geneva startup ecosystem characteristics:
- Strong early-stage (seed/pre-seed) via VCs, family offices, and institutional investors; weaker Series A/B bridge creating "scaleup gap"
- EPFL (Lausanne) and UNIGE (Geneva) talent pipeline feeding tech startups; computer science and engineering graduates increasingly choosing startup employment over banking
- Geneva-based startups in: fintech (wealthtech, regtech), AI/data, cybersecurity, sustainability tech, commodity trading tech
- Regulatory sandbox: FINMA sandbox relatively accessible for fintech experimentation; Swiss fintech license creating new category

Key challenges:
- Scaleup gap: Geneva startups frequently acquired at Series A/B by international buyers rather than scaling to maturity in Switzerland
- Talent retention: Geneva cost base making it difficult to retain engineering talent; Lausanne/Zurich competitors
- International markets: Swiss startups needing EU market access face regulatory complexity

Advisory opportunity: regulatory strategy for Geneva AI/fintech startups — navigating FINMA, FDPIC, and cross-border requirements as startups scale

### FR
**Écosystème startup genevois — environnement réglementaire et pipeline scaleup**

Caractéristiques de l'écosystème genevois :
- Fort au раннем этапе (seed/pre-seed) via VCs, family offices ; fossé Series A/B plus faible
- Pipeline de talents EPFL (Lausanne) et UNIGE (Geneva)
- Startups genevoises en : fintech (wealthtech, regtech), AI/data, cybersécurité, cleantech
- Sandbox réglementaire FINMA accessible ; licence fintech suisse créant de nouvelles catégories

Défis clés :
- Fossé scaleup : startups genevoises fréquemment acquises à Series A/B par des acheteurs internationaux
- Rétention des talents : base de coûts genevoise rendant difficile la rétention des ingénieurs
- Marchés internationaux : startups suisses nécessitant l'accès au marché UE face à la complexité réglementaire

Opportunité de conseil : stratégie réglementaire pour startups AI/fintech genevoises
