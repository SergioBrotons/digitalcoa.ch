# Phase 06 — Geneva Real Estate — Regulatory Context (Cycle 23)
**Vertical:** Small real estate agencies / property managers (Geneva)
**Cycle:** 23 | Date: 2026-03-30

## Regulatory Context

### Active Now
- **nFADP Art. 5(c):** tenant/prospect data processing obligations active NOW
- **GAIGI ~7–10 months operative:** Fall 2025 confirmed; institutional legitimacy

### Near-Term (2026)
- **Swissdamed Jul 1 2026 ~13 WEEKS spillover:** RE firms with healthcare clients face dual obligation
- **EU AI Act Aug 2 2026:** RE firms with EU market nexus (property marketing, leasing to EU clients)

### Retroactive Compliance Frame
- "Govern the AI you already have"
- Most RE agencies already using AI — governance gap is retroactive, not anticipatory
- Unique framing across all 6 verticals: all others use anticipatory framing
- Lower-friction entry: firms don't need to stop AI adoption; they need to govern it

### Compliance Stack
1. **nFADP Art. 5(c):** tenant data, prospect data, biometric data (if facial recognition)
2. **GAIGI:** professional standards for property valuation + AI advisory
3. **Swissdamed spillover:** health data in property transactions
4. **EU AI Act:** EU-nexus firms
