### Competitive Gaps & Opportunities

#### Identified Gaps (C125)

**Gap 01: ESG Taxonomy Dual-Framework Advisory**
No clear advisory leader owns the EU + Swiss dual ESG taxonomy framework for fund managers. Opportunity: develop standardized Swiss ESG taxonomy classification + EU taxonomy mapping tool for Geneva fund managers. Position as compliance efficiency before FINMA taxonomy finalizes.

**Gap 02: Direct Lending Fund Documentation & Compliance**
Direct lending fund formation is accelerating but documentation, compliance, and investor relations frameworks are fragmented. Opportunity: standardized direct lending fund package for Geneva managers — regulatory authorization support, LPA templates, investor presentation frameworks, portfolio monitoring dashboards.

**Gap 03: Private Bank Consolidation Advisory**
Merger/integration advisory for Geneva private banks is dominated by Big 4 and investment banks. Opportunity: Geneva-focused advisory on client migration planning, RM retention programs, and post-merger client communication strategies — from someone who understands Geneva private banking culture.

**Gap 04: FINMA Crypto License Readiness**
FINMA's 7-entity crypto license pipeline is creating advisory demand for governance frameworks, compliance programs, and institutional custody solutions. Most Geneva financial institutions lack in-house capability. Opportunity: FINMA crypto license readiness assessment and compliance framework development.

**Gap 05: Wholesale CBDC Impact Assessment**
Project Agora's wholesale CBDC pilot has implications for Geneva banks with Asian wholesale operations. Most banks are monitoring passively. Opportunity: advisory on CBDC impact assessment, strategic implications for FX and cross-border settlement businesses, and scenario planning.

**Gap 06: SNB Rate Normalization Client Communication**
Rate normalization complexity (rate cuts vs. inflation above target) is creating client communication demand. Geneva wealth managers need to explain CHF/EUR dynamics, real estate exposure, and bond portfolio implications to UHNW clients. Opportunity: client communication frameworks and investment narrative development.
