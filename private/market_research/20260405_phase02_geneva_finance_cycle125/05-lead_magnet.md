1. Swiss ESG taxonomy: key criteria and classification categories (FINMA consultation summary)

4. Disclosure requirements: pre-contractual and periodic — what's different for EU vs. CH

**Distribution:** SFAMA partnership; direct outreach to Geneva fund manager compliance teams
