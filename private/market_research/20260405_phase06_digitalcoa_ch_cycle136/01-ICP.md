# 01-ICP.md — DigitalCoa.ch

## DigitalCoa.ch — ICP Definition

### Primary ICP: Geneva Executive Decision-Makers
- **Profile:** C-suite (CEO, CFO, CSO, CRO), Board Members, Managing Partners at Geneva organisations
- **Sector focus:** Mid-market M&A targets (CHF 50-500M), Geneva family offices, UHNW individuals, IO sector executives
- **Decision type:** Strategic, high-stakes, irreversible or difficult-to-reverse decisions
- **Trigger events:** M&A decision, board sustainability governance, cantonal migration, IO departure, regulatory enforcement, succession
- **Buying behaviour:** Trust-based; peer-recommended; evidence of independent positioning; remote delivery comfort
- **Language:** EN-primary; FR for Swiss domestic/relationship building
- **Willingness to pay:** CHF 2,800-4,500/day for senior advisory; project retainer CHF 15-50K; annual advisory retainer CHF 100-200K

### Secondary ICP: Boards and Board Committees
- Foundation boards (Art. 80ff ZGB) needing governance advisory
- Family council and family board structures
- IO boards needing independent non-executive directors

### Tertiary ICP: Professional Advisory Intermediaries
- Geneva private bankers referring complex advisory mandates
- Geneva law firms requiring independent co-advisor for conflicted situations
- Geneva executive search firms recommending advisory to placed executives

### What DigitalCoa.ch Does NOT Want
- Clients seeking product-selling or implementation services (these conflict with advisory independence)
- Clients who want vendor-confirmed opinions rather than honest independent analysis
- Clients unwilling to engage with evidence-based reasoning
- Short-term transactional clients who do not value trusted-advisor relationships

### Strategic Implication
DigitalCoa.ch converts through trusted introduction (not inbound marketing), independent positioning (documented no-conflict), and demonstrated Swiss institutional intelligence (evidence of expertise). The pathway is Brotons.net content → trusted introduction → advisory engagement.
