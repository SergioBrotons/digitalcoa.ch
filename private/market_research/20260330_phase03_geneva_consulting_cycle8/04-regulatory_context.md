# Phase 03 Regulatory Context — Geneva Consulting / Small Firms
## Cycle 8 (2026-03-30)

## Primary Regulatory Framework

### 1. EU AI Act — GPAI (General Purpose AI)
- **Status:** Operative since August 2, 2025 (~7.5 months already live as of late March 2026)
- **Nature:** Transparency, copyright handling, incident documentation for GPAI model providers and deployers
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** If your firm uses ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, or any GPAI system for client work — you are a GPAI deployer; obligations are already active
- **Key GPAI obligations for deployers:**
  - Transparency: disclose AI use to clients (e.g., in proposals, reports, communications)
  - Copyright: ensure training data compliance for AI outputs used in deliverables
  - Incident documentation: log AI failures or harmful outputs

### 2. EU AI Act — High-Risk (Annex III)
- **Date:** August 2, 2026 (~15 months)
- **Nature:** Conformity assessment required before deploying high-risk AI systems
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** IT/tech sub-segment — if you deploy AI systems interacting with finance, healthcare, education, or critical infrastructure sectors (all prevalent in Geneva): likely Annex III high-risk classification

### 3. nFADP (Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection)
- **Status:** Operative since September 1, 2023 (~2.5 years)
- **Nature:** Swiss data protection; AI-based data processing = Art. 5(c) triggered (FDPIC May 8, 2025 confirmation)
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** Any AI system processing personal client data requires DPIA before deployment

### 4. FDJP Consultation (CoE AI Convention Implementation)
- **Timeline:** End-2026 (~9 months)
- **Nature:** Federal Council CoE AI Convention consultation draft; focus areas: transparency, data protection, non-discrimination, oversight
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** Will crystallize Swiss AI governance standards; triggers market-wide awareness spike; competitive landscape will shift post-consultation

### 5. Swiss Federal Council 2026 AI Action Plan
- **Timeline:** Sector-specific guidance in development
- **Nature:** Federal Council action plan for AI; sector-specific measures
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** Additional guidance layer beyond FDJP consultation

### 6. Swissdamed (Healthcare Data — Cross-Sector)
- **Date:** July 1, 2026 (~12 weeks)
- **Boutique consulting relevance:** Consulting firms with healthcare clients face immediate nFADP/spillover obligations; Geneva healthcare cluster (Phase 04) most affected
