## Regulatory Framework: Geneva Healthcare AI Governance

### Active Regulations (Now)

#### 1. Swissdamed — July 1, 2026 (~12 WEEKS AWAY)
**Status:** HARD DEADLINE — NO GRACE PERIOD CONFIRMED

**What it is:**
Swiss Medical Device Database (Swissdamed) — Swissmedic's centralized registry for medical devices including Software as a Medical Device (SaMD).

**Key confirmation:**
Swissmedic confirmed: NO grace period for high-risk SaMD with serious incident obligations. AI diagnostic imaging and AI symptom triage = immediate July 1, 2026 compliance obligation. No December 31 grace period.

**Who is subject:**
Small Geneva medical and dental practices using:
- AI diagnostic imaging (radiology, pathology, dermatology, ophthalmology, cardiology AI)
- AI symptom triage systems
- AI clinical decision support systems

**Obligations:**
- SaMD registration with Swissmedic via Swissdamed
- Serious incident reporting obligations
- Compliance verification before July 1, 2026

**Non-compliance:**
Market withdrawal of non-compliant AI tools.
