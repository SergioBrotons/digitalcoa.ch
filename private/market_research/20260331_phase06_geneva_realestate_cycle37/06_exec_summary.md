## Key Findings — Cycle 37

### 75% AI Adoption — Widest Governance Gap in Portfolio

Up from 67% confirmed in earlier cycles. Real estate agencies are using AI broadly (tenant screening, property valuation, marketing, scheduling) with minimal formal governance.

### Art. 21 FADP — Tenant Screening + Property Valuation

AI-assisted tenant screening (likely EU AI Act Annex III high-risk for housing) and property valuation tools are directly in scope for Art. 21 human review obligations.

### GAIGI Institutional 404 — No SMB Guidance

Confirmed across multiple cycles. GAIGI (gaigi.ch) is institutional-only, not accessible to small agencies. This is the structural white space.

### Dual Compliance Burden

Real estate agencies with EU nexus (EU clients, cross-border rentals) must comply with both FADP and EU AI Act Aug 2, 2026.
