### Pain Points (C123 Update)
1. **SNB rate normalization path uncertainty** — SNB cutting rates but inflation resurging; Geneva banks with floating-rate CHF exposure managing margin compression vs. credit risk
2. **FINMA crypto license pipeline** — Multiple applicants awaiting FINMA decision on crypto custody/exchange licenses; regulatory uncertainty for institutional crypto adoption
3. **Swiss ESG fund taxonomy revision** — EU taxonomy alignment pressure vs. Swiss sovereignty; Geneva ESG funds facing reclassification risk
4. **Cross-border payment digitization** — SNB wholesale CBDC trials, WJP bank corridor developments; Geneva commodity traders evaluating digital payment efficiency
5. **Swiss bank consolidation** — M&A activity among mid-tier Geneva private banks; integration advisory demand
6. **Alternative credit expansion** — Direct lending, private credit funds forming in Geneva; regulatory treatment unclear
