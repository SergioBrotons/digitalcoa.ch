### 3. Swiss ESG Fund Taxonomy Revision
**Status:** Federal Council consultation open (deadline April 2026)
**Key question:** Swiss equivalence with EU taxonomy vs. Swiss sovereignty
**Impact:** Fund reclassification risk, investor disclosure updates
**Geneva implication:** Pictet, Lombard Odier, Swisscanto ESG fund exposure

### 4. Project Helvetia — Wholesale CBDC Pilot
**Status:** Pilot phase with 6 Swiss banks, Q1 2026
**Scope:** Wholesale CBDC for interbank settlement; cross-border corridor (Geneva-Singapore)
**Impact:** Payment system efficiency, financial infrastructure evolution
**Geneva implication:** Commodity traders, Geneva banks in pilot; implementation advisory gap

### 5. Swiss Banking Consolidation Regulatory Pressure
**Status:** FINMA capital requirements, ESG integration mandates, cost-income pressure
**Deals:** 3 mid-tier Geneva banks in merger talks
**Impact:** Integration advisory demand, governance restructuring
**Geneva implication:** Integration management office (IMO) setup; FINMA approval process

### 6. Direct Lending / Private Credit — CISA Treatment
**Status:** 4 new funds registered Q1 2026; FINMA reviewing regulatory classification
**Key question:** Alternative fund vs. collective investment scheme classification
**Impact:** Fund formation structure, investor eligibility
**Geneva implication:** Pension fund allocation advisory, fund formation advisory
