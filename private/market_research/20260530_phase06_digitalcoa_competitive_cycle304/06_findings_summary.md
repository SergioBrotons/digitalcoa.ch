## 6 NET-NEW Findings — C304 Phase 06

### Finding 1: Documentation Debt Has a Specific Compounding Mechanism — "Debt Freeze" Is the Discrete Service

**The discovery:** Documentation debt doesn't just grow — it grows at a specific rate tied to AI adoption frequency. Every AI decision made without evidence capture adds a discrete, quantifiable unit of debt. The service response is not retrospective reconstruction — it's a "debt freeze" that stops new debt accumulation while building the forward evidence system.

**What this adds to C303 Phase 01:** Phase 01 identified documentation debt as a structural problem with no Geneva-defined stop. Phase 06 identifies the specific compounding mechanism and names the discrete service that addresses it: debt freeze + forward capture architecture. Not "pay down the debt" — "stop the compounding while building forward."

**The service design implication:** The engagement is not "AI governance audit." It's "evidence debt freeze + forward capture system." The language of debt freeze speaks to ICP's actual emotional state — they're not worried about the past, they're terrified of the future debt growth.

**C304 advance:** From "documentation debt is a problem" to "documentation debt has a specific mechanism and a specific resolution service." The debt freeze framing is NET-NEW in C304.

**What this adds to C303 Phase 06:** C303 Phase 06 identified "evidence" as the positioning anchor and personal liability as the emotional trigger. Phase 06 identifies that the positioning statement is a two-level operating tool — the same phrase does category work and emotional work simultaneously, which means it functions as both a positioning statement and a conversion trigger.

**The messaging implication:** "The market offers frameworks. We offer evidence." is not a tagline — it's a complete messaging architecture. The first sentence does category positioning. The second sentence (implied: "frameworks protect your firm; evidence protects your name") does emotional conversion. Every piece of DigitalCoa messaging can be a variation on this structure.

**C304 advance:** From "evidence is the anchor and liability is the trigger" to "the positioning statement operates on both levels simultaneously." The two-level mechanism of the statement is NET-NEW in C304.

### Finding 5: The Service Model Shift From One-Time to Retainer Is Structurally Required, Not Optional

**The discovery:** The regulatory pressure layering (carrier form v4 now, Swissdamed July 1, EU AI Act August 2026, Swiss AI legislation 2028–2029) creates a structurally required service model shift. Documentation debt compounds continuously. Carrier forms upgrade continuously. New AI tools enter the practice continuously. A one-time engagement produces a point-in-time evidence system that immediately begins accumulating new debt.

**What this adds to C303 Phase 05:** Phase 05 identified that regulatory pressure layering changes the service model from one-time to ongoing retainer. Phase 06 identifies why this is structurally required, not optional: the compounding mechanism means one-time evidence production is immediately obsolete. The retainer is not a commercial preference — it's the only service design that actually solves the ICP's problem.

**The commercial implication:** The service model is advisory retainer, not project-based. The evidence system requires ongoing maintenance, continuous capture, and regular review as carrier forms upgrade and new AI tools enter the practice. The retainer is the correct commercial model for the ICP's actual need — not upselling, but the only design that works.

**C304 advance:** From "regulatory pressure layering changes service model" to "the service model change is structurally required." The structural argument for the retainer is NET-NEW in C304.
