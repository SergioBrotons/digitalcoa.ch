**Signal:**

- ICRC Framework Agreements: standing offers for specific product/service categories
- UNHCR Global Framework Agreements (GFA): long-term agreements with pre-qualified vendors
- Partner's access to ICRC/UNHCR procurement: consortium and partnership structures
- Innovation procurement: ICRC Innovation Hub and UNHCR Innovation Section as entry points

Geneva-based humanitarian tech startups face significant barriers to entering these procurement pathways without understanding the frameworks and relationship-building requirements.

**Quantitative Context:**
- ICRC annual procurement volume: ~CHF 1.5-2B annually
- UNHCR annual procurement volume: USD 2-3B annually
- Geneva-based tech companies with humanitarian tech offerings: estimated 30-50 significant companies
- Current vendor qualification rate for new Geneva humanitarian tech entrants: estimated 10-20% (due to framework agreement lock-in)

**Implication for DigitalCoa.ch:**
Advisory opportunity: Help Geneva humanitarian tech companies navigate ICRC/UNHCR procurement frameworks, develop vendor qualification strategies, structure consortium partnerships for large framework agreements, and identify innovation procurement entry points as alternative pathways.

**EN:** "ICRC/UNHCR humanitarian procurement frameworks represent a complex but high-value market for Geneva tech companies — advisory on framework navigation, vendor qualification, and partnership structuring creates significant engagement value."
