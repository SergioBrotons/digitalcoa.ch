## Competitive Landscape Overview

**Geneva NGO/Tech Advisory Landscape — Assessment:**

The Geneva NGO/Tech market is served by fragmented competitive tiers with limited direct competition:

1. **International Development Consultants** — Large firms (McKinsey Development, BCG Open, Deloitte Social Impact) serving large IO engagements. Weak on: Geneva-specific context, humanitarian tech niche, Swiss regulatory depth.
2. **Swiss Tech Advisory Boutiques** — Swiss-focused firms with tech and innovation focus. Weak on: international organization ecosystem knowledge, humanitarian AI ethics depth.
3. **UN/IO System Advisors** — Former UN/IO staff offering advisory services. Variable quality; strong on internal knowledge; limited on emerging tech.
4. **Geneva Humanitarian Tech Specialists** — Small number of specialists at intersection of humanitarian operations and technology. Very few; fragmented.
