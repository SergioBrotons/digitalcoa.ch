## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 5)

### EN
**Geneva Interim Management Market — Capacity Augmentation for Complex Transitions**

Geneva interim management market — 2025-2026:
- What it is: interim management = placing experienced executive (C-suite or senior manager) in company on temporary basis (3-18 months); distinct from consulting — executive has operational authority and accountability, not advisory role
- Why companies use it: maternity/paternity leave coverage, sudden executive departure, transformation/project mandate requiring dedicated senior resource, crisis management, merger integration, startup scale-up leadership gap
- Geneva interim CFO market: most common interim assignment; financial restructuring, M&A process, system implementation, regulatory compliance project; day rates CHF 2,500-5,000 for experienced interim CFO in Geneva
- Geneva interim COO: operations transformation, process redesign, supply chain disruption management; day rates CHF 2,500-4,500
- Geneva interim HR Director: organizational restructuring, redundancies, cultural integration post-M&A; day rates CHF 2,000-3,500
- Interim management companies in Geneva: Top Management Partners (Geneva), Kuhn & Zwack (Munich/Zurich), Beratergruppe (Swiss), Allen & York (Zurich/Geneva); specialized practices serving commodity trading, banking, pharma sectors
- Consulting firm extension opportunity: boutique strategy/management consulting firms offering interim management as premium add-on; leverages existing client relationships, provides recurring revenue, bridges consulting and execution
- Differentiators in Geneva: language capability (FR/EN/DE/IT), sector expertise (commodity trading, pharma, finance, luxury), international experience (global company backgrounds), network of interim executives
- Pricing structures: daily rate (most common), fixed project fee, retainer for ongoing availability; margin for intermediary firms typically 15-25% on daily rate
- Advisory entry point: DigitalCoa.ch could position as interim CFO/COO provider for Geneva SMEs; content marketing on "when to hire an interim CFO" would be highly searchable by Geneva company owners

### FR
**Marché du management de transition à Genève — Augmentation de capacité pour transitions complexes**

Marché du management de transition genevois — 2025-2026 :
- Définition : management de transition = placement d'exécutif expérimenté (C-suite ou manager senior) dans une entreprise sur base temporaire (3-18 mois) ; distinct du conseil — exécutif a autorité opérationnelle et responsabilité
- Pourquoi les entreprises l'utilisent : remplacement congé maternité/paternité, départ soudain de exécutif, mandat de transformation/projet nécessitant ressource senior dédiée, gestion de crise, intégration post-fusion, gap de leadership dans scale-up startup
- Marché genevois du CFO interim : mission interim la plus commune ; restructuration financière, processus M&A, implémentation système, conformité réglementaire ; tarifs journaliers CHF 2 500-5 000 pour CFO interim expérimenté à Genève
- Structure de prix : taux journalier (le plus courant), honoraire fixe projet, retainer pour disponibilité continue ; marge pour firmes intermédiaires typiquement 15-25% sur le tarif journalier
