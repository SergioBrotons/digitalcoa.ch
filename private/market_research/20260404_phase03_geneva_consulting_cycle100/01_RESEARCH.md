## Findings (Cycle 100 — Slot 1)

### EN
**CFO-as-a-Service — Fractional Finance Leadership for Geneva SMEs**

CFO-as-a-Service market — Geneva context 2025-2026:
- Market definition: fractional CFO providing strategic finance leadership on part-time basis (typically 2-5 days/month); distinct from controller/bookkeeping services; includes board-level financial strategy, fundraising support, M&A advisory, financial modeling
- Geneva SME landscape: ~50,000 registered companies in Canton Geneva; majority are SMEs; Geneva has strong startup ecosystem (200+ startups per Geneva startup map); growth-stage companies (Series A/B) frequently lack full-time CFO
- Pricing benchmarks: Geneva/CH market rates for fractional CFO typically CHF 2,000-4,000/day; monthly retainer structures CHF 8,000-20,000 for 5-10 days/month engagement; significantly below full-time CFO cost (CHF 250,000-400,000/year total comp)
- Service scope: strategic financial planning, board reporting, investor relations (for funded companies), financial model development, covenant compliance, bank relationship management, financial controls design
- Target segments: Geneva-based Series A/B startups (need CFO for investor reporting), family-owned businesses in transition (succession, M&A), PE-backed portfolio companies, non-profit/association finance management
- Competitors: Big 4 firms offer CFO advisory but at enterprise rates; small boutique finance advisory firms; independent fractional CFOs; Geneva-based operators: CFO Partners, FinStart, multiple independent operators
- Differentiation opportunity: Geneva-specific sector expertise (commodity trading, luxury, pharma, fintech), bilingual EN/FR capability, international finance experience (SNB, Swiss banking background)
- Advisory implications: Brotons.net/DigitalCoa.ch positioning as fractional CFO provider, content marketing to startup ecosystem, IHEID alumni network leverage

### FR
**CFO-as-a-Service — Direction financière fractionnaire pour PME genevoises**

Marché CFO-as-a-Service — contexte genevois 2025-2026 :
- Définition du marché : CFO fractionnaire fournissant direction financière stratégique à temps partiel (typiquement 2-5 jours/mois) ; distinct des services controller/comptabilité ; inclut stratégie financière niveau conseil, support levée de fonds, conseil M&A, modélisation financière
- Paysage des PME genevoises : ~50 000 entreprises enregistrées dans le canton de Genève ; majorité sont PME ; Genève a fort écosystème startup (200+ startups selon carte startup genevoise) ; entreprises en croissance (Series A/B) lack fréquemment CFO à temps plein
- Références de prix : tarifs marché Genève/CH pour CFO fractionnaire typiquement CHF 2 000-4 000/jour ; structures de retainer mensuel CHF 8 000-20 000 pour 5-10 jours/mois ; significativement inférieur au coût CFO à temps plein (CHF 250 000-400 000/an total comp)
- Périmètre de services : planification financière stratégique, reporting au conseil, relations investisseurs (pour entreprises financées), développement de modèles financiers, conformité covenants, gestion relations bancaires, conception contrôles financiers
- Segments cibles : startups genevoises Series A/B (besoin CFO pour reporting investisseurs), entreprises familiales en transition (succession, M&A), entreprises en portefeuille PE-backed, gestion financière association/non-profit
