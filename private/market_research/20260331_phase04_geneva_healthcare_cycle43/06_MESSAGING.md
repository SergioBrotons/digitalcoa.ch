## Objection Handling

### "We're too small for Swissdamed"
→ Swissdamed applies to any practice using AI medical devices regardless of size.
→ No grace period confirmed by Swissmedic — ignorance is not a defense.

### "Our AI is just for scheduling"
→ Scheduling AI may still trigger FADP obligations.
→ If any AI is used in clinical context, Swissdamed may apply.

### "We'll register when we have time"
→ Swissdamed has no grace period. Registration takes time to prepare.
→ ~13 weeks is shorter than it sounds.
