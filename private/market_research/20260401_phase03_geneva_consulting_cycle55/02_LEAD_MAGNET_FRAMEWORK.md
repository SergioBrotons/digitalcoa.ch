## Agentic AI Three-Tier (Applicable to Consulting)
- AI assistants: low autonomy → research synthesis, report drafting
- AI agents: moderate autonomy → client data processing, deliverable assembly
- Agentic AI: high autonomy → autonomous analysis execution = maximum liability

## "Envelope vs. Letter" (Applicable to Consulting)
- Envelope (safe): client intake, scheduling, billing, reporting formatting, research synthesis
- Letter (risky): client strategy analysis, recommendations, advice drafting
