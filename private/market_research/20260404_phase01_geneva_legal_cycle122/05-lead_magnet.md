# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Legal

## Lead Magnet: "Geneva Legal Alert — 2026 Regulatory Precedent Watch"

### Concept
A periodic (quarterly) structured advisory note targeting Geneva law firm General Counsel, managing partners, and in-house legal functions at financial institutions. Each edition tracks 3-4 Geneva-relevant regulatory developments with practical implications.

### Content Structure
- **Format:** 2-page PDF + brief explanatory note; EN+FR available
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Distribution:** Email to Geneva legal decision-makers; LinkedIn
- **CTA:** "Request a 30-minute precedent briefing" → DigitalCoa.ch consultation booking

### C122 Edition: Spring 2026 Focus
1. **BGer Algorithmic Decision Ruling — Preparing Before the Precedent Drops**
   - What the ruling means for your institution
   - 5-point algorithmic governance checklist
   - Geneva-specific implications for FI credit-decisioning
2. **Swiss-US Trade Arbitration — When Your ICC Clause Meets a US Blocking Order**
   - Enforceability scenarios for Geneva-seated arbitration
   - Conflict-of-laws strategy for Swiss exporters
   - Practical steps for existing commercial contracts
3. **Freeport AML Enforcement — Art as Collateral: The New Exposure**
   - Who is affected (art dealers, family offices, banks financing art)
   - Geldwäschereigesetz compliance obligations
   - Geneva court case implications

### Differentiation
- No Geneva law firm or advisory currently publishes a structured "precedent watch" note
- Combines regulatory tracking with practical advisory framing
- Bridges Geneva legal and financial institution audiences
