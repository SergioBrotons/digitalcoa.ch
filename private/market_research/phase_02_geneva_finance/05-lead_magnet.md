# 05 — Lead Magnet: Geneva Finance

## Strategic Positioning Angle
Geneva financial institutions face a compounding regulatory burden: AI governance + operational resilience + crypto licensing + ESG disclosure — all simultaneously. Most lack a coherent framework to address these converging obligations. The advisor who can synthesize these into a unified "regulatory convergence" framework for Geneva financial institutions occupies a defensible position.

## Lead Magnet Concepts

### Option A: "The Geneva Regulatory Convergence Framework"
A structured advisory document mapping the 5-7 intersecting regulatory obligations (DORA parity, ISA, CRS 2.0, FinIA crypto, nFADP AI, FINMA nature risk, AI Convention) onto a single governance timeline with a prioritization matrix.

**Format:** Executive briefing (8-12 pages) + regulatory timeline wall chart
**Distribution:** Geneva finance C-suite, CRO, CCO
**Value:** Practical synthesis — no other provider offering this integrated view

### Option B: "FINMA AI Governance Checklist for Geneva Asset Managers"
A field-ready compliance checklist for Geneva-based portfolio managers and asset managers deploying AI in investment processes, client scoring, or risk management.

**Format:** Downloadable PDF checklist + 30-min complementary assessment call
**Distribution:** Compliance officers, portfolio managers, heads of technology at Geneva asset managers
**Value:** Immediate utility; low-friction entry point; surfaces consulting opportunity

### Option C: "Stablecoin License Readiness Assessment"
A structured diagnostic for Geneva fintechs and payment institutions assessing readiness for the new FinIA payment instrument institution license.

**Format:** Self-assessment tool + results briefing call
**Distribution:** Geneva fintech founders, CFOs, compliance teams
**Value:** Greenfield advisory opportunity; new license category = no established advisor incumbency

### Option D: "CRS 2.0/CARF Geneva Implementation Briefing"
Executive briefing on crypto-asset reporting obligations effective January 2026, with a focus on Geneva-based private banks and asset managers.

**Format:** 45-min briefing + Q&A session (virtual or in-person Geneva)
**Distribution:** Compliance, tax, and legal functions at Geneva financial institutions
**Value:** Immediate relevance; deadline-driven urgency; positions as FINMA-aware advisor

## Preferred Format (Initial)
Option A + Option D hybrid — Executive briefing synthesizing converging regulations as primary lead magnet, with Option B checklist as secondary low-friction entry point.

## Target CTA
"Schedule a 30-minute regulatory convergence briefing" — targeted at Geneva finance C-suite and CCO level.
