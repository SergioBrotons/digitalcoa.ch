## Competitive Positioning Gaps

### Underserved / Differentiated Opportunities
1. **AI governance for Geneva asset managers** — No Geneva boutique specifically positioned as AI governance advisor for FIs
2. **Stablecoin/crypto FINMA authorization advisory** — New FinIA categories = greenfield market
3. **DORA-parity compliance (Swiss FIs with EU operations)** — Cross-border regulatory gap not addressed by existing players
4. **Nature-related financial risk advisory** — FINMA Circular 2026/1 creates new disclosure compliance market
5. **CRS 2.0/CARF implementation advisory** — Crypto reporting creates new service line

### Competitive Threats
- Big Four regulatory practices scaling AI governance services
- International firms launching Swiss AI regulatory practices
- In-house legal teams absorbing work previously outsourced

## Geneva vs. Zurich Differentiation
- Geneva: private wealth, commodity trading finance, Geneva arbitration, NGOs/Mission finance
- Zurich: fintech, crypto exchanges, asset management, tech
