# 01 — ICP: Phase 04 Geneva NGO/Tech (C118)

## ICP: Geneva NGO/Tech Market

### Primary ICP — Geneva Tech Startups (Scaleup Stage)
**Target:** Geneva-area startups in Series A/B (CHF 2M-20M raised)

**Profile:**
- Sector: fintech, AI/ML, cybersecurity, climate tech, health tech
- Pain: scaling revenue, fundraising, team growth, regulatory navigation
- Geography: Geneva (Canton), some Lausanne (EPFL corridor)
- Decision-maker: CEO, Founder
- Size: 10-50 employees
- Language: French primary; English ubiquitous

### Secondary ICP — UN Agencies and International Organizations
**Target:** UN agencies with Geneva presence (UNOG, ITU, WMO, WHO, WTO, WIPO)

**Profile:**
- Budget pressure; efficiency mandates
- Pain: organizational transformation, digital modernization, cybersecurity
- Decision-maker: Director of Operations, CIO, HR Director
- Buying signal: strategic planning cycles, budget reviews
- Size: Variable; large headquarter operations

### Tertiary ICP — Climate Tech and Sustainability Ventures
**Target:** Climate tech startups and ventures in Geneva ecosystem

**Profile:**
- Carbon markets, clean energy, sustainable finance, environmental monitoring
- International scope; Geneva as global policy hub
- Pain: market access, regulatory alignment, scaling internationally
- Decision-maker: CEO, CSO
- Buying signal: climate policy milestones, carbon market developments
