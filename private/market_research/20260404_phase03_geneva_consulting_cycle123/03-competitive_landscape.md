## Competitive Dynamics (C123 Update)

### Competitive Shifts
1. **Big 4 regulatory practice growth** — DORA, Basel IV, ESG driving Big 4 regulatory advisory revenue +15% Geneva
2. **Transformation recovery niche emerging** — No dedicated boutique focused on Geneva digital transformation rescue
3. **Interim risk talent bundling** — Big 4 and specialist firms bundling interim + advisory
4. **Legal-advisory convergence** — Law firms (Lenz & Staehelin, Pestalozzi) expanding advisory non-legal components

### Positioning Gaps
- **DORA ICT risk framework implementation** — Big 4 dominant but overwhelmed; independent boutique gap
- **Basel IV SME credit restructuring** — Specific niche not well served by generalist advisors
- **Geopolitical de-risking strategic advisory** — Geneva-focused, event-driven; independent boutique opportunity
- **Board ESG governance** — Legal firms and Big 4; boutique independent advisory gap
- **Digital transformation rescue** — No dedicated Geneva boutique; major gap
