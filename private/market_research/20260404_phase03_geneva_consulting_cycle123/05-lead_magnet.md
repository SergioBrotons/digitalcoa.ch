# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Consulting Sector

## EN Version

### Primary Concept: "Geneva Executive Advisory — Regulatory & Strategic Briefing"

**Format:** Bi-monthly executive briefing (2 pages)
**Distribution:** LinkedIn (targeted to Geneva CFOs, CROs, CEOs, Board members), direct email to Geneva C-suite
**Content focus:**
- Top regulatory change affecting Geneva institutions (Basel IV, DORA, ESG)
- Strategic implication for Geneva executives
- Immediate action items
- One "what to watch" emerging issue

**Sample topics (C123 cycle):**
1. DORA implementation — what Geneva financial institutions need to do in Q3 2026
2. Basel IV SME credit — restructuring options for Geneva banks
3. Board ESG governance — FINMA examination preparation checklist

**CTA:** "Facing [specific regulatory challenge]? DigitalCoa.ch — executive advisory briefings."

---

### Secondary Concept: "Geneva Transformation Rescue — Case Study Series"

**Format:** Quarterly case study (1-2 pages, anonymized)
**Distribution:** LinkedIn (Geneva CIOs, CFOs, COOs), Geneva IT Leadership Forum
**Content focus:**
- Transformation program rescue scenarios
- What went wrong and how it was recovered
- Lessons for Geneva institutions
- DigitalCoa.ch capability demonstration
