## Brotons.net Content: Regulatory Framework (For Interpretation)

### 1. FADP — Core Content Pillar
- FADP operative since 2023
- FDPIC September 24, 2025: FADP directly applicable to AI-supported data processing
- DPIA requirement for AI systems processing personal data
- FDPIC posture: guidance-only; no formal enforcement
- Brotons.net angle: FADP as Swiss AI governance baseline; FDPIC as quiet regulator

### 2. EU AI Act — Core Content Pillar
- GPAI obligations in force since August 2, 2025
- August 2, 2026: double deadline (HR AI conformity + legacy GPAI grandfathering expiry)
- Annex III high-risk: AVMs, HR AI, credit scoring AI likely in scope for Swiss FIs
- Extraterritorial: placing on EU market / output used in EU
- Brotons.net angle: EU AI Act as external pressure on Swiss regulatory approach; Geneva FI exposure

### 3. UN AI Governance Ecosystem — NEW C70 Content Opportunity

- Brotons.net angle: UN AI governance framework emerging; Geneva positioning as multilateral AI hub
- Content opportunity: "What the UN AI Resolutions Mean for Swiss Businesses"
