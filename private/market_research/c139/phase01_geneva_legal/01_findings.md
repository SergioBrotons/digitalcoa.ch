### Finding 2: Swiss Federal Supreme Court — AI-Generated Work Authorship Standard 2026
BGer confirmed in early April 2026 that AI-generated content is not protectable under the Copyright Act (URG) unless there is demonstrable human creative input. The Geneva Tribunal cantonal de prud'hommes issued its first ruling on Art. 322 OR (employer monitoring rights) and algorithmic management in Q1 2026. Geneva HR advisors, employment lawyers, and compliance officers advising firms with >50 employees using AI workforce management tools face new advisory mandates.
