### The Problem You're Actually Facing

The client sent you an RFP. Section 7 is AI governance. It has a scoring matrix. You read the questions. You don't know what to put in them.

### What This Briefing Does

The Geneva Consulting Boutique AI Accountability Briefing is a 90-minute session. I come to your office — or we meet over video, whatever works. We cover four things:

**First: What your client is actually scoring.**

The RFP AI governance section isn't asking if you have a policy. It's asking for evidence: vendor contracts, supervision protocols, error identification processes, AI use records per engagement. I read these RFPs for a living. I'll show you what they're scoring, and I'll show you how to build the evidence they want.

**Second: Where your carrier attestation is exposing you.**

The renewal form has specific questions about client-facing AI use. The questions have evolved from your initial attestation. You signed something 18 months ago. The gap between what you certified and what you actually do is where the misrepresentation risk lives. I'll map your current practice against the carrier's current questions and show you exactly what needs to be documented.

**Third: The sub-consultant accountability gap.**

You're engaging sub-consultants on larger mandates. They're using AI. You don't have a record of what they did, what tools they used, what data they processed. You signed the carrier attestation that says you monitor AI use. You have no monitoring system for sub-consultant AI use. That's the gap. I'll build the delegation register that closes it.

**Fourth: Your personal nFADP Article 52 exposure.**

A broker asked you about personal nFADP coverage. You checked your policy. Administrative fines are excluded. Article 52 makes you personally responsible as managing partner for AI decisions. The fine is up to CHF 250,000. Your professional liability insurance doesn't cover it. The documentation that protects you isn't a compliance policy — it's evidence of specific authorization decisions you made, as managing partner, for specific AI systems, on specific client data. I'll build that evidence.

### Ce Que Ce Briefing Fait

Le Geneva Consulting Boutique AI Accountability Briefing est une session de 90 minutes. Je viens à votre bureau — ou nous nous rencontrons en visio, comme vous préférez. Nous couvrons quatre choses :

La documentation qui vous protège n'est pas une politique de conformité — c'est la preuve de décisions d'autorisation spécifiques que vous avez prises, en tant qu'associé directeur, pour des systèmes d'IA spécifiques, sur des données clients spécifiques.
