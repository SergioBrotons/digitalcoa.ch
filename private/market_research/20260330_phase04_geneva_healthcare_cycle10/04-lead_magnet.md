July 1, 2026. No grace period. No extensions. High-risk SaMD obligations apply immediately.

- Process diagnostic images (radiology AI, pathology AI, ophthalmology AI)
- Provide AI-based symptom triage or clinical decision support
- Constitute "software which is intended to be used for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes" under Swissmedic classification
