## Section 2: Swissdamed — The Hardest Deadline in Your Portfolio

Swissdamed hits July 1, 2026. Approximately 12 weeks away.

**There is no grace period.**

If your practice uses AI diagnostic imaging or AI symptom triage tools classified as high-risk SaMD (Software as a Medical Device), Swissmedic requires compliance from day one. No phase-in. No extension.

This is different from other regulations — it's harder and more immediate than anything else on your compliance calendar.

### Swissdamed — July 1, 2026 (12 WEEKS)
High-risk SaMD with serious incident obligations: immediate compliance required from July 1.
If you use AI diagnostic imaging or AI symptom triage: check your SaMD classification now.

### nFADP Art. 5(c) — Active Since 2023
AI-based decisions about patients made by automated processing: DPIA + human review mechanism required now.

### EU AI Act GPAI — Active Since August 2, 2025 (~8 months)
If your AI has EU users, outputs intended for EU markets, or you're operating as an EU importer/distributor: transparency obligations already live.
