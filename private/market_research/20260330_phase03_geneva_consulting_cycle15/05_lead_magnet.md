## Section 2: What's Already Live and Enforceable

### EU AI Act GPAI — Operating Since August 2, 2025

You've been under GPAI obligations for approximately 8.5 months.
If your consulting firm has EU nexus — EU clients, EU-intended outputs, or operates as an EU importer/distributor of AI — your transparency obligations are already live.

**GPAI transparency obligations you may already owe:**
- Technical documentation for each GPAI system
- Transparency to downstream users (your clients)
- Summary of training data and model characteristics
- Union AI Office notification for general-purpose AI models

**What "EU nexus" means for a Geneva consulting firm:**
- Your firm has EU-based clients
- Your AI outputs are intended for EU markets
- You're operating as an importer or distributor of EU-target AI systems
- Your consulting deliverables incorporate GPAI systems

If any of these apply, you're already potentially non-compliant.

## Section 4: The Dual Compliance Challenge

As a Geneva consulting firm with likely EU nexus, you're managing two concurrent compliance frameworks:

**EU AI Act (Extraterritorial)**
- GPAI transparency: ACTIVE NOW (8.5 months)
- High-risk obligations: Aug 2 2026 (~4 months)
- Applicable if EU nexus exists

**Swiss nFADP (Domestic)**
- Art. 5(c): ACTIVE since 2023
- AI-based automated decisions: DPIA + human review required
- Applies to AI processing personal data in Switzerland

**The Gap:**
Federal Council "lean approach" confirmed — NO cross-sector Swiss AI law before end-2026.
You're in a guidance vacuum with active extraterritorial obligations and domestic obligations converging.
