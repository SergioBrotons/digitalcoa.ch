## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary Segment
Small consulting firms in Geneva: 2–10 consultants, independent or boutique partnership, no in-house compliance team, using AI for client analysis, report generation, or operational optimization.

### Core Problem
GPAI transparency obligations are ACTIVE NOW (Aug 2 2025 — ~8.5 months live). Federal Council First Draft May 2026 (~2 months). Small consulting firms with EU nexus face immediate compliance obligations with no guidance and no boutique advisor.

### Timing Signal

- Federal Council Zero Draft Jan 2026 confirmed; First Draft May 2026 (~2 months away)
- Advisory window ~9 months to Aug 2 2026 high-risk activation
- 40% Geneva SMEs lack AI skills (PwC 2024) — structural awareness gap
