# 06 — Executive Summary: Phase 03 Geneva Consulting

## Research Question
What is the addressable market for advisory services targeting small Geneva consulting firms (2-15 employees)?

## Key Findings (C97 — NET-NEW)

### Finding 1: 2026 regulatory cascade reaches small consulting firms
Three 2026 developments directly affect small Geneva consulting firms: AMLA revision (new advisor category), LETA beneficial ownership (corporate consulting), nFADP enforcement intensification.

**Implication:** Small consulting firms that thought regulation was "for banks only" are now in scope. Advisory opportunity to help them understand and prepare.

### Finding 2: AMLA revision creates SRO affiliation obligation for some consulting firms
Firms advising on real estate transactions, entity creation/management/domiciliation may need SRO affiliation by H2 2026. Many don't know yet.

**Implication:** Urgent advisory opportunity — firms need to assess whether they're in scope before the effective date.

### Finding 3: AI tool usage without governance = nFADP exposure
Small consulting firms widely using ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude for client work — without data governance policies. Swiss DPA enforcement intensifying in 2026.

**Implication:** Client data confidentiality framework is the most immediate advisory need. Creates natural entry point for AI governance advisory.

### Finding 4: ISO 42001 as differentiation for small firms
Small consulting firms can obtain ISO 42001 AI management certification to differentiate from competitors. Few have done so.

**Implication:** Certification advisory pathway creates higher-value engagement than pure strategy work.

### Finding 5: No Geneva boutique has claimed the "AI governance + regulatory compliance for small consulting firms" space
Big 4 too expensive; AI tool vendors too product-focused; no Geneva-specific boutique advisory.

**Implication:** First-mover advantage for DigitalCoa if lead magnet and content strategy launched quickly.

### Finding 6: AI RFP requirements are emerging as buying trigger
Client RFPs increasingly ask about AI methodology. Small consulting firms without a clear answer lose competitive standing.

**Implication:** AI methodology documentation and governance framework creates both demand and urgency.
