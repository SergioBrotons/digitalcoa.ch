# 04 — Regulatory Context: Geneva Small Consulting Firms

## Key Regulations (Updated C97)

### Primary Regulations
| Regulation | Scope | Relevance to Small Consulting |
|---|---|---|
| nFADP | Swiss data protection | Client data in consulting deliverables; AI tool usage |
| Swiss AI Guidance | AI deployment | Tool selection, client data in AI systems |
| ISO 42001:2023 | AI management systems | Voluntary certification for differentiation |

| LETA (H2 2026) | Beneficial ownership | Corporate consulting engagements require beneficial owner identification |

### 2026 Developments (NEW — C97 Priority)

#### AMLA Revision — New "Advisor" Category (H2 2026)
- **What:** Legal advisors who participate in financial transactions related to real estate, entity creation/management/domiciliation become regulated entities
- **Impact on consulting firms:** If firm advises on real estate transactions, corporate formation, or domiciliation services → SRO affiliation required
- **Advisory opportunity:** SRO affiliation guidance; compliance gap assessment before H2 2026 effective date

#### nFADP Enforcement Intensifying (2026)
- **What:** Swiss DPA increasing enforcement activity in 2026
- **Impact on consulting firms:** Client data in AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot) = potential nFADP exposure; consulting deliverables containing personal data require proper handling
- **Advisory opportunity:** nFADP compliance framework for consulting firms; AI tool data governance policy

#### LETA Beneficial Ownership (H2 2026)
- **What:** Federal register of beneficial owners of Swiss legal entities
- **Impact on consulting firms:** Corporate consulting engagements involving identification of beneficial owners must follow new procedures
- **Advisory opportunity:** LETA compliance for consulting engagements; beneficial ownership identification process

#### BFSA UK-Switzerland (January 2026)
- **What:** Mutual recognition of financial services between Switzerland and UK
- **Impact on consulting firms:** Geneva consulting firms with UK-based clients or UK operations need to understand cross-border implications
- **Advisory opportunity:** BFSA cross-border advisory framework for consulting firms

## Implications for DigitalCoa Advisory (C97)
1. **AI data governance + nFADP framework** — most immediate need; consulting firms using AI tools without policy
2. **AMLA/SRO affiliation guidance** — timely before H2 2026 effective date
3. **ISO 42001 certification pathway** — differentiated positioning for small firms
4. **LETA compliance for corporate consulting** — niche but real need
