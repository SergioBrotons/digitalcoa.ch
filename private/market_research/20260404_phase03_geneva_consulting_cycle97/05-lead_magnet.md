# 05 — Lead Magnet Concept: Geneva Small Consulting Firms

## Lead Magnet Options Assessment

### Option A: "The Small Consulting Firm AI Decision Framework"
**Format:** Bilingual PDF guide (EN primary, FR adapted for Geneva)
**Target:** Managing partners of 2-15 person consulting firms
**Value:** Practical 3-axis decision framework + client data confidentiality checklist
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, specialization, biggest AI concern
**Conversion path:** Download → nurture sequence → AI readiness check → advisory engagement

### Option B: "AMLA Compliance Gap Assessment for Geneva Consulting Firms"
**Format:** Checklist + 1-page summary
**Target:** Managing partners whose firm may be affected by AMLA advisor category
**Value:** Self-assess whether H2 2026 AMLA changes apply to your firm; gap analysis
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, transaction types, current SRO status
**Conversion path:** Assessment → consultation → SRO affiliation support

### Option C: "ISO 42001 AI Management Certification — Geneva Small Firm Guide"
**Format:** Practical guide (EN + FR executive summary)
**Target:** Managing partners seeking AI differentiation
**Value:** Path to ISO 42001 certification; competitive differentiation; client trust signal
**Lead capture:** Name, firm, AI tool usage, certification interest level
**Conversion path:** Guide → ISO 42001 gap assessment → certification support

## Lead Magnet Recommendation (C97)

**Primary:** Option A (AI Decision Framework) — most broadly applicable; addresses the primary pain point for all small consulting firms regardless of AMLA exposure

**Secondary:** Option B (AMLA Compliance Gap Assessment) — most timely for firms potentially affected by the H2 2026 changes; creates urgency

**Approach:** Lead with Option A for broad capture; retarget Option B to firms in scope for AMLA advisor category

## Bilingual Considerations (C97)
- English primary (managing partners often international)
- French adaptation essential for Geneva credibility
- Regulatory terms in original (nFADP, AMLA, LETA, ISO 42001)
