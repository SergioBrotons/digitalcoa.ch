# 02 — Market Signals: Geneva Small Consulting Sector

## Geneva Consulting Market Overview

### Market Size
- **Swiss management consulting market:** ~USD 3.58B (2025), 4.48% CAGR
- **Geneva share:** Switzerland's #2 consulting hub; ~10% rate premium over Zurich
- **Small firm segment (2-15 employees):** ~600+ firms in Geneva region
- **Addressable segment:** Firms with AI adoption gaps and client data governance needs

### Key Demand Signals (Updated C97)

#### 1. AI Tool Adoption Anxiety (Ongoing)
- Individual adoption common (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude)
- Firm-level AI governance absent in most small firms
- Client pressure building: RFPs increasingly ask about AI methodology
- No Geneva boutique has claimed the "AI governance for small consulting firms" space

#### 2. Regulatory Exposure Expanding (NEW C97)
- **nFADP enforcement intensifying 2026:** Swiss DPA more active; client data in consulting engagements under greater scrutiny
- **AMLA revision (H2 2026):** Firms advising on real estate transactions, entity creation/management may need SRO affiliation
- **LETA beneficial ownership (H2 2026):** Corporate consulting engagements require beneficial ownership identification process
- **BFSA UK-Switzerland (January 2026):** Consulting firms with UK clients face new cross-border considerations

#### 3. ISO 42001 Differentiation Opportunity (NEW C97)
- ISO 42001:2023 AI management system standard — emerging as market signal for AI-responsible firms
- Small consulting firms can obtain certification to differentiate from competitors
- Advisory opportunity: ISO 42001 gap assessment and implementation support
