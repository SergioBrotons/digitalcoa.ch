## Positioning Opportunity for DigitalCoa (C97)
- **Gap:** No Geneva boutique advisory firm has claimed "AI governance + regulatory compliance for small consulting firms"
- **Differentiation:** Practical, Geneva-specific, bilingual (EN+FR), affordable entry-point
- **Entry product:** Low-cost AI readiness assessment → framework guide → optional ISO 42001 gap assessment
- **Unique angle C97:** 2026 regulatory cascade (AMLA, LETA, nFADP) creates cross-sell opportunity from AI governance into regulatory compliance

## Competitive Moat Elements (C97)
1. **Bilingual EN+FR** — unique in Geneva advisory space for small consulting
2. **Practical "decision framework" not academic theory** — small firms need actionable guidance
3. **2026 regulatory awareness** — first mover on AMLA/SRO affiliation for consulting firms
4. **ISO 42001 advisory** — differentiated from tool vendors and generic business advisors
