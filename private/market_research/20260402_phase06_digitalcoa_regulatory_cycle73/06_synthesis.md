## Regulatory Landscape Summary for DigitalCoa.ch Positioning

### The Pattern
Organizations face a **stacked regulatory environment** — multiple AI, data, trade, and financial regulations converging simultaneously. The common thread: they all require **decision governance frameworks**, which is exactly DigitalCoa.ch's core offering.

### Decision Governance vs. Regulatory Compliance
**Compliance:** Do X because the law says so.
**Decision Governance:** Design systems so your organization makes the right decision about X before the regulator asks.

DigitalCoa.ch's positioning ("I build boundaries before bots") is well-suited for this stacked regulatory environment.
