# 02 — Data Protection / FADP

## C73 NET-NEW Findings

### Neurotechnology + Human Rights Data Dimensions
- **Source:** Geneva Academy working papers, January 29, 2026
- Neurotechnology development raises FADP/Human Rights data questions
- Brain data = sensitive personal data under Swiss and international frameworks
- Geneva Academy guidance for states and businesses on neurotechnology data governance
- **Implication for DigitalCoa.ch clients:** Organizations developing or using neurotechnology-adjacent AI need data governance frameworks
