# 03 — Competitive Landscape
## Geneva NGO/Tech — C131

## FINMA DLT Authorization Advisory

**Key Players:** Lambda Crypto (Zurich), Crypto Valley Labs (Zug), FONGIT (Geneva), Swiss DLT Association, law firms with DLT practices (MME Zurich, Wenger & Vieli, Lenz Staehelin)

**Differentiation:**
- Most DLT advisory is legal work (license application, regulatory mapping)
- Pre-authorization operational readiness advisory (business model, governance, capital adequacy) is underserved
- Incubation programs (FONGIT) provide some support but not deep regulatory advisory

**Opportunity:** Pre-authorization operational readiness assessment for Geneva blockchain start-ups. Advisory without legal conflict — positioning between legal counsel and management consulting.

## Humanitarian Technology Advisory

**Key Players:** ICRC Innovation Bureau (internal), UNHCR Innovation Section (internal), Tech Against Terrorism (UN initiative), DiploFoundation (Geneva-based), Geneva Tech Campus initiatives, World Economic Forum (Global Technology Governance Council)

**Differentiation:**
- ICRC/UNHCR innovation offices manage partnerships internally
- External advisory for technology firms partnering with IOs is thin
- Geneva humanitarian tech consultancies are few; most are legal or policy boutiques

**Opportunity:** Technology transfer structuring and testing agreement design for tech firms seeking IO partnerships. Independent advisory not tied to any IO procurement relationship.

## WIPO Standards Governance Advisory

**Key Players:** WIPO Standards Division (internal), Swiss Federal Institute of Intellectual Property (IPI), Geneva tech firms with standards engagement, tech policy boutiques (Geneva Internet Platform, DiploFoundation)

**Differentiation:**
- WIPO engagement is primarily through formal process participation
- Independent advisory for Geneva firms on standards engagement is underserved
- IPI (Swiss IP office) provides some guidance but not strategic standards positioning

**Opportunity:** WIPO standards engagement strategy advisory for Geneva tech firms. Advisory on AI authorship disclosure requirements under expanded PROOF platform.

## Article 6 / VCMI Carbon Market Advisory

**Key Players:** South Pole Group (Zurich), Carbon Trust Switzerland, Climate Focus (Amsterdam with Geneva presence), Geneva commodity trading compliance desks, Xpansiv (carbon market platform)

**Differentiation:**
- Carbon market advisory is primarily for corporate emissions reduction strategy
- Article 6 corresponding adjustment documentation is a specific niche within carbon advisory
- Geneva trading house compliance teams handle internally; external advisory rare

**Opportunity:** Article 6/VCMI compliance documentation advisory for Geneva carbon market participants. Specialized documentation and claim defensibility — distinct from general carbon strategy advisory.

## Humanitarian Data Governance Advisory

**Key Players:** ICRC Data Protection Office (internal), UNHCR Data Protection Officer, DiploFoundation (Geneva digital governance), Swiss Red Cross data protection, academic centres (University of Geneva digital governance)

**Differentiation:**
- ICRC/UNHCR data protection offices handle internal compliance
- External advisory for humanitarian data governance is primarily academic or policy-focused
- Commercial data protection consultancies rarely serve humanitarian organizations

**Opportunity:** Data governance framework design for Geneva humanitarian organizations. Data sharing agreement structuring. FADP/GDPR compliance for cross-border IO data flows — independent advisory without IO procurement conflict.

## Climate Finance Advisory

**Key Players:** SECO (Swiss development cooperation), SDC (Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation), adelphi (Berlin with Geneva presence), Palladium (international development advisory), Geneva cleantech firms' internal export teams

**Differentiation:**
- SECO/SDC are government agencies; advisory on their programs is limited
- International development consultancies (Adelphi, Palladium) serve large institutional clients
- Swiss cleantech export advisory is underserved at SME level

**Opportunity:** Climate finance eligibility advisory for Geneva cleantech SMEs. Project structuring for Green Climate Fund and SECO bilateral climate finance access. Development impact measurement framework design.
