# 04 — Regulatory Context
## Geneva NGO/Tech — C131

## FINMA DLT Act and Crypto Custody

**Legal basis:** DLT Act (in force since 2021); FINMA Guidance 01/2026 on crypto custody (published January 12, 2026)
**Key requirements:** DLT license for tokenized securities and crypto custody; operational resilience, credit risk management, custody risk, bankruptcy segregation requirements
**Pre-authorization readiness:** Business model documentation, governance framework, capital adequacy planning, AML compliance program
**Status:** Framework operative; first FINMA authorizations granted; pipeline active for Geneva-based applicants

## WIPO Standards and AI Authorship

**Legal basis:** WIPO Standards (ST.26, ST.96 for patent applications); WIPO PROOF (digital timestamp certification); WIPO AI and IP discussion
**Key requirements:** AI-generated works disclosure under WIPO PROOF expansion; patent application requirements for AI-assisted inventions
**WIPO emerging technology observatory:** Developing governance frameworks for AI and blockchain; standards engagement process open to stakeholder input
**Status:** Standards development ongoing; AI authorship disclosure requirements evolving

## Paris Agreement Article 6 / VCMI

**Legal basis:** Paris Agreement Article 6 (bilateral cooperation); Switzerland concluding bilateral Article 6.2 agreements with partner countries; VCMI (Voluntary Carbon Markets Integrity) initiative claims requirements
**Key requirements:** Corresponding adjustment documentation for carbon credits used in corporate climate claims; VCMI claim defensibility documentation
**Swiss adoption:** Switzerland active in Article 6 bilateral negotiations; domestic carbon market infrastructure linked to Article 6 compliance
**Status:** Article 6 bilateral agreements concluding; VCMI claims under increasing scrutiny; corresponding adjustment documentation becoming standard requirement

## Humanitarian Data Protection

**Legal basis:** Swiss FADP (in force since 2023); EU GDPR (for EU-funded humanitarian operations); ICRC/UNHCR data protection frameworks (internal rules)
**Key requirements:** Cross-border data sharing agreements; FADP data protection impact assessments; GDPR compliance for EU-funded operations; humanitarian exemptions application
**ICRC specific:** ICRC data protection framework based on humanitarian law principles; unique challenges (missing persons, family reunification)
**Status:** FADP operative; GDPR applies to EU-funded operations; ICRC/UNHCR data governance standards in place

## Climate Finance — Green Climate Fund / SECO

**Legal basis:** Green Climate Fund governing instrument; SECO bilateral climate finance framework; Swiss Climate Policy framework
**Key programs:** Green Climate Fund project preparation facility; SECO climate finance grants and loans; Swiss Development Finance institutions
**Eligibility:** Project-based; development impact requirements; environmental and social safeguards
**Status:** Active funding windows 2024-2027; SECO bilateral climate finance calls for proposals ongoing
