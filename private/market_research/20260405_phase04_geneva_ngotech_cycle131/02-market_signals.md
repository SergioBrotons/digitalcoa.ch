# 02 — Market Signals
## Geneva NGO/Tech — C131

## Geneva Blockchain/DLT Start-up Ecosystem

FINMA's DLT Act framework (operative since 2021, with Guidance 01/2026 strengthening crypto custody expectations) is providing sufficient clarity for blockchain start-ups to progress from proof-of-concept to authorization. Geneva-based fintech incubators report increased inbound from tokenized securities, digital asset custody, and humanitarian impact measurement start-ups. Key gap: pre-authorization operational readiness advisory (business model, governance, capital adequacy) before formal FINMA application.

## Humanitarian Technology Innovation

Geneva ICRC Innovation Bureau and UNHCR Innovation Section are actively seeking technology partnerships. Key areas: biometric identity (family reunification), satellite imagery logistics, low-cost medical devices (WHO), AI translation for diplomacy. Advisory opportunity: technology transfer structuring and testing agreement design. ICRC IT modernization budget estimated CHF 180-240M (2025-2027) creating implementation partner demand.

## WIPO Emerging Technology Governance

WIPO's emerging technology observatory developing governance frameworks for AI and blockchain. Standards development process ongoing. WIPO PROOF platform (blockchain-based timestamp certification) being expanded to AI-generated works. Geneva tech firms need advisory on standards engagement and AI authorship disclosure requirements.

## Carbon Market Compliance — Article 6 / VCMI

Switzerland concluding bilateral Article 6.2 agreements (carbon cooperation with partner countries). VCMI claims by Geneva multinationals require corresponding adjustment documentation. Geneva commodity traders and airlines are primary market for Article 6 compliance advisory. Voluntary carbon market integrity is under scrutiny — documented compliance is essential for claim defensibility.

## Humanitarian Data Governance

Cross-border data sharing between Geneva IOs and field operations subject to: Swiss FADP, EU GDPR (EU-funded operations), and organization-specific data protection frameworks. Data sharing agreements and FADP/GDPR compliance for humanitarian operations is distinct from standard corporate data protection advisory.

## Geneva Cleantech and Climate Finance

Swiss/Geneva cleantech firms targeting emerging market climate finance: Green Climate Fund, Climate Investment Funds, SECO/SDC bilateral climate finance. Advisory gap: international development finance expertise (project structuring, development impact measurement) combined with Swiss export advisory (SERV, FINMA).
