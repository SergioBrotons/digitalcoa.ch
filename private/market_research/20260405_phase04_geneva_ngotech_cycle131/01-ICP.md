# 01 — Ideal Client Profile
## Geneva NGO/Tech — C131

**Firm Types:**
- Geneva-based blockchain and DLT start-ups (pre- and post-FINMA authorization)
- Cleantech firms in Geneva and Vaud (solar, water, sustainable agriculture)
- International organizations with Geneva-based innovation/technology offices (ICRC, UNHCR, WHO, WTO, ITU)
- Humanitarian technology firms (dual-use: humanitarian + commercial applications)
- Carbon market participants (Geneva commodity traders, airlines, multinationals with Geneva presence)
- Geneva tech incubators and accelerators (FONGIT, GreenTec)
- Swiss-based impact investors and climate finance vehicles

**Decision-Maker:**
- ICRC Innovation Bureau Director / UNHCR Innovation Section Head
- WHO Digital Health Unit Director / ITU Technology Development Director
- CEO/CTO of Geneva blockchain start-ups
- FONGIT/GreenTec incubator program directors
- Geneva cleantech firm CEO / CFO
- Carbon market desk head (commodity trading houses)
- Swiss development finance program managers (SECO/SDC)

**Pain Points C131:**
- FINMA DLT Act pre-authorization operational readiness — governance, capital, business model documentation
- ICRC/UNHCR technology partnership — IP licensing, testing agreements, technology transfer
- WIPO AI/blockchain standards — engagement process and disclosure requirements
- Article 6/VCMI carbon market compliance — corresponding adjustment documentation
- Cross-border humanitarian data governance — FADP/GDPR compliance for IO data sharing
- Climate finance access — Green Climate Fund, SECO bilateral climate finance eligibility

**Urgency Triggers:**
- FINMA DLT authorization application milestones
- ICRC/UNHCR technology partnership solicitation rounds
- WIPO standards submission deadlines (ongoing)
- Article 6 bilateral agreement implementation dates
- VCMI claim audit season (Q4 annual)
- Green Climate Fund replenishment and funding windows (every 4 years; next 2024-2027)
- SECO/SDC bilateral climate finance call for proposals

**Geography:** Geneva + Vaud (Lausanne/Geneva biotech corridor); GenevaTechCampus; GreenTec (Plan-les-Ouates)
**Language:** EN primary; FR useful with IO organizations
**Budget Cycle:** IO innovation budgets typically Q4 planning for following year; climate finance tied to GCF replenishment cycles
