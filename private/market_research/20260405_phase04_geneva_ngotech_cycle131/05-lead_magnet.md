# 05 — Lead Magnet
## Geneva NGO/Tech — C131

## Recommended Lead Magnet: "Geneva Technology Governance Guide — From Standards to Implementation"

**Format:** Annual guide with sector profiles (12-15 pages EN + executive FR summary)
**Target:** CTO, CEO, and General Counsel of Geneva-based tech firms (blockchain, AI, humanitarian technology, cleantech); Innovation Directors at Geneva IOs (ICRC, UNHCR, WHO, WTO)
**Primary CTA:** Download the Guide; book a governance readiness assessment

**Content Framework:**

1. **Geneva tech governance landscape 2026:**
   - FINMA DLT Act: What authorization requires and what pre-authorization readiness means in practice
   - WIPO emerging technology standards: AI authorship disclosure, PROOF platform integration, blockchain IP considerations
   - Swiss FADP for tech firms: Data protection obligations for Geneva-based tech companies

2. **Sector-by-sector governance profiles:**
   - Blockchain/DLT firms: FINMA DLT authorization checklist, pre-authorization readiness framework
   - AI firms: WIPO AI standards engagement, AI authorship disclosure requirements, PROOF integration
   - Humanitarian tech: ICRC/UNHCR technology partnership framework, testing agreement design, IP licensing
   - Carbon tech: Article 6 compliance documentation, VCMI claim defensibility framework
   - Cleantech: Climate finance eligibility, Green Climate Fund project structuring basics

3. **Geneva-specific advantages:**
   - WIPO standards engagement: How Geneva firms can influence international IP governance
   - Geneva IO partnerships: Where to access ICRC/UNHCR/WHO technology needs identification
   - Swiss innovation incentives: R&D tax incentives and OCE innovation vouchers for tech firms

4. **Technology governance gap self-assessment (scored):**
   - Regulatory compliance checklist (FINMA DLT, WIPO standards, FADP)
   - Partnership readiness (IO technology partnership framework maturity)
   - Climate finance eligibility assessment (Green Climate Fund, SECO bilateral)

**Distribution:**
- Brotons.net as primary publication (EN + FR)
- LinkedIn newsletter excerpt (2 key governance findings)
- GenevaTechCampus and FONGIT distribution (if partnership approved)
- DigitalCoa.ch as secondary distribution

**Differentiation:**
No single guide covers the intersection of FINMA DLT regulation, WIPO AI standards, and Geneva IO technology partnership frameworks. Positioning as the definitive technology governance reference for Geneva tech firms.
