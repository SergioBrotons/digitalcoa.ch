### Competitive White Space Summary

**The confirmed gap:**
- **Target:** Small Geneva law firms (2–5 attorneys)
- **Need:** Practical, accessible AI governance guidance + implementation
- **Price point:** CHF 800–2,500 for initial assessment/framework
- **Format:** Workshop, checklist, or abbreviated advisory retainer

**What exists:**
- Large firm publications (not accessible to small firms)
- ANBLES silence (professional body not acting)
- FABIAN PRIVACY LEGAL (closest boutique; privacy/security broadly, not legal AI governance specifically)

**What does NOT exist:**
- A Geneva-based boutique offering AI governance specifically for small law firms
- A Geneva bar-affiliated AI governance program for small firms
- A practical, price-accessible AI governance product for 2–5 attorney firms

## First-Mover Advantage Assessment
**Window:** ~12–18 months before potential competitive entry
**Conditions that would trigger competitive entry:**
- ANBLES publishes AI guidance (triggers market education, reducing differentiation)
- 1–2 boutique competitors emerge (reduces white space)
- FDPIC issues specific guidance for legal sector (reduces advisory need)
- Large firm launches accessible small-firm product (unlikely given economics)

**Current status:** Window intact — no competitive erosion observed in Cycle 19 research
