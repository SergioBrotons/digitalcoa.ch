## ICP: Boutique Wealth Manager (Geneva)

### Core Characteristics
- **Size:** Boutique/independent wealth managers; typically CHF 500M–5B AUM; 3–20 staff
- **Geography:** Geneva; often serve UHNWI/HNWI clients across Romandie + international
- **Regulatory status:** FINMA-licensed or SRO-member; subject to FINMA supervision, AMLA, LETA, CARF/CRS
- **AI exposure:** Uses AI for portfolio analysis, client reporting, compliance monitoring, AML transaction screening
- **Primary pressure:** CARF operative Jan 1, 2026 (~3 months live as of today); AMLA/LETA H2 2026 (~3–6 months); FATF mutual evaluation 2026–2027

### Timing Signal
- **CARF/CRS 2.0:** Live since Jan 1, 2026 — already operative, not upcoming
- **AMLA/LETA:** H2 2026 (~3–6 months) — consultation machinery active; 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register
- **FATF evaluation:** 2026–2027 — international compliance event driving urgency
- **FINMA Guidance 08/2024:** Current baseline; no 2026 specific AI circular published
- **SIF active (Mar 27, 2026):** "Switzerland as a fintech nation" confirms Swiss government's continued fintech engagement

### 3-Layer Compliance Stack
1. **Swiss domestic:** FINMA Guidance 08/2024 + CARF/CRS 2.0 (live Jan 1 2026) + AMLA/LETA H2 2026
2. **Swiss international:** FATF mutual evaluation 2026–2027 + OECD transparency
3. **EU extraterritorial:** EU AI Act (if EU nexus) — high-risk obligations Aug 2 2026

### Pain Points
- CARF live obligations already in effect (3 months)
- AMLA compliance machinery active; 30-day discrepancy reporting to Transparency Register
- Advisor scope expansion under AMLA: lawyers, notaries, trustees, CSPs must join FINMA-recognized SRO
- No Geneva-specific FINMA AI guidance published in 2025–2026
- FATF evaluation adds international compliance pressure

### Competitive Landscape
- **10th+ consecutive confirmation:** No boutique Geneva wealth manager AI governance competitor
- AI Risk, MTF Solutions, TA Advisory Geneva: new entrants in adjacent spaces
- Big 4 and large institutions: not SMB-focused
