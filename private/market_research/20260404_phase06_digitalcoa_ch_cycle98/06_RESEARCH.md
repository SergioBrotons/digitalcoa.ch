## Finding

**DigitalCoa.ch Competitive Position: Geneva Executive Advisory Has Space for an Independent, Research-Grounded Voice**

The Geneva executive advisory market has a specific gap: independent, Geneva-grounded, research-backed advisory services without the conflicts of large consulting firms. DigitalCoa.ch occupies this position — if it commits to the independent positioning.

**The competitive gap:**
- Big 4 / strategy firms: conflicted (audit/consultancy conflicts), expensive, generic
- Geneva law firm advisory: excellent on law, weak on strategy and commercial judgement
- Geneva boutique consultancies: specialist but often single-service
- Independent advisors: generally lack systematic research foundation
- Academic/advisory think tanks: high quality, poor accessibility, slow

**DigitalCoa.ch's differentiation:**
- Independent (no conflicts)
- Research-grounded (systematic market intelligence behind every engagement)
- Geneva-specific (not a global firm with a Geneva office)
- Executive-accessible (not buried in a large firm gatekeeping structure)
- Dual-positioning with Brotons.net (thought leadership + advisory services reinforce each other)

**What "independent research-grounded advisory" requires:**
1. Every advisory engagement should draw on the research rotation's findings
2. Market intelligence should be continuously refreshed and accessible to clients
3. The research rotation's 36-findings-per-cycle output should be positioned as a competitive advantage for clients

**Signal for DigitalCoa.ch:** The "research-backed independent advisory" positioning is a strategic decision that shapes all marketing, service page, and content decisions. This is an escalation for owner input — but the operational foundation (research rotation) is already in place.
