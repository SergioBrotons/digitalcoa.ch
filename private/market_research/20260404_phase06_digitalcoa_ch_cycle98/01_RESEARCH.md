# Finding 01 — CYCLE 98 | Phase 06: DigitalCoa.ch Advisory Positioning
**Source:** DigitalCoa.ch competitive analysis, C97/C98 research findings synthesis  
**Date:** 2026-04-03

## Finding

**DigitalCoa.ch Advisory Entry Points Mapped to C98 Research — Four High-Conversion Service Angles**

C98 research (36 findings across 6 phases) maps directly to four specific advisory service entry points where DigitalCoa.ch can convert Geneva executive interest into briefing calls. Each entry point addresses a specific, felt pain point with a clear service offer.

**Four high-conversion service entry points:**

**1. AI + Data Protection Compliance Advisory (from Phase 01 Finding 01):**
- Pain: "We use AI systems. Is our FADP compliance current? Are we exposed?"
- Service: FADP AI compliance assessment + remediation roadmap
- Client: CTO, CIO, General Counsel, Compliance Officer at Geneva financial/tech companies
- Conversion trigger: FINMA/FDPIC enforcement risk makes this urgent

**2. Executive AI Governance Briefing (from Phase 04 Finding 05):**
- Pain: "We need to deploy AI. What governance framework should we have in place?"
- Service: Board-level AI governance framework design
- Client: CEO, Board Members, C-suite at Geneva companies deploying AI
- Conversion trigger: UN/IO AI governance standards are creating board accountability expectations

**3. mHRDD Board Governance Advisory (from Phase 01 Finding 05):**
- Pain: "We're subject to mHRDD. What does board-level liability actually look like?"
- Service: mHRDD board workshop + governance framework
- Client: Board Members, GCs, CFOs at large Swiss companies
- Conversion trigger: mHRDD is live; board liability is the urgency message

**4. AML/FINMA Compliance Health Check (from Phase 01 Finding 06):**
- Pain: "FINMA is scrutinising beneficial ownership. Are we prepared for an examination?"
- Service: AML/FINMA compliance health check + examination readiness
- Client: Private bank CCOs, family office CFOs, Geneva financial institution GCs
- Conversion trigger: US DOJ NPAs being revoked creates fear of FINMA follow-on

**Why these work:** Each maps a C98 research finding to a specific, named client pain, a defined service offer, and a clear conversion trigger. This is the foundation for DigitalCoa.ch's service page content.

**Signal for DigitalCoa.ch:** These four service angles should appear explicitly on DigitalCoa.ch — not as generic advisory descriptions, but as specific, research-grounded offerings with clear urgency framing.
