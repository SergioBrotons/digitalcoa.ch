### Finding 01 | CSRD Phase 2 Wave — Swiss EU-Listed Company Implementation Rush
**Sector:** Sustainability / Corporate Reporting / Geneva Consulting
**Angle:** CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) Phase 2 (reports due 2026 covering FY2025) requires double materiality assessments and detailed sustainability disclosures for large EU-listed Swiss companies and EU-parented Swiss subsidiaries. Many affected entities underestimated Phase 1 and are now scrambling as Phase 2 deadlines approach. Geneva-based sustainability consultants report acute demand for: Phase 1 gap remediation, Phase 2 double materiality assessment refresh, data infrastructure setup, and auditor coordination. Advisory opportunity: CSRD catch-up programs, data quality remediation, auditor-ready disclosure packages.

**Differentiation from prior cycles:** C125/C126 focused on CSRD as a general framework and compliance baseline; C127 focuses specifically on the Phase 2 implementation crunch — the second wave of a known regulatory deadline creating acute demand.

**Confidence:** High (domain knowledge)

### Finding 02 | DORA ICT Third-Party Risk — TPRM Consulting Demand
**Sector:** Regulatory Consulting / ICT Risk / Geneva Financial Services
**Angle:** DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act) applies to Swiss financial institutions with EU operations or EU counterparties, and FINMA has aligned its ICT risk framework expectations accordingly. The critical and often under-addressed dimension: ICT third-party risk management (TPRM). Financial institutions must map critical ICT subcontractors, assess concentration risk, and build exit plans. Geneva banks and fintechs face acute demand for TPRM program build-outs. Advisory opportunity: ICT TPRM framework design, critical supplier mapping, DORA gap assessment, tabletop exercises for ICT outage scenarios.

**Differentiation from prior cycles:** C124/C125 mentioned DORA in passing as a regulatory context item; C127 isolates TPRM as the specific, under-delivered consulting deliverable within the DORA framework.

**Confidence:** High (domain knowledge)
