## Framework Section

**Subhead:** A 3-part framework for small practice AI governance

**Part 1 — Inventory**
Document every AI tool used in your practice. Include purpose, data access, provider. Personal device use counts.

**Part 2 — Risk Classification**
Determine whether each tool qualifies as a medical device under Swissdamed. High-risk tools require documented conformity assessment. Lower-risk tools still require nFADP compliance.

**Part 3 — Accountability Protocol**
Every AI-assisted clinical output must be reviewed and approved by a licensed physician before patient impact. Document this process.
