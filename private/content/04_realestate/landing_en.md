## Framework Section

**Subhead:** Retroactive Compliance: The Small Agency Path

The framing for real estate agencies is different from most professional services. You are not being asked to stop using AI. You are being asked to govern the AI you are already using.

**Step 1 — Inventory**
Document every AI tool currently used in your agency. Include purpose, data access scope, provider jurisdiction. Count personal devices.

**Step 2 — Classify**
Separate AI tools by data sensitivity: client-facing data tools (highest risk), market research tools (medium), administrative tools (lower). This determines your nFADP exposure level.

**Step 3 — Account**
Assign a named point of accountability for AI use in client-facing contexts. Document a review process for AI-assisted outputs that involve client data or property information.

**Step 4 — Maintain**
Set an annual review cycle. AI tool use evolves faster than most firms track.
