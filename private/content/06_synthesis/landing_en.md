## Why the Sequence Matters

Swissdamed is the hardest deadline in the entire portfolio — ~13 weeks, no grace period. It belongs at the top of any outreach sequence for Geneva-area professional services.

The others follow by urgency and ICP readiness:

**Phase 1 — Healthcare (Swissdamed-first)**
Thirteen weeks. Hard deadline. No grace period. The Swissdamed entry is the strongest cold-call hook available across all six verticals.

**Phase 2 — Legal + Wealth Management (regulatory-active)**
Both verticals have operative regulatory obligations (nFADP Art. 5(c) active since 2023; CARF operative since Jan 2026). Both have quantified governance gaps. Both respond to regulatory deadline framing.

**Phase 3 — Real Estate + Consulting (market-educating)**
Both verticals have high AI adoption but lower immediate regulatory pressure. The framing centers on retroactive compliance (Real Estate) and GPAI obligations (Consulting). Both ICPs are ready for a framework conversation.

**Phase 4 — Independent Advisors + Cross-vertical synthesis**
The synthesis page functions as the portfolio anchor — for buyers who don't fit one vertical cleanly, or who want to understand the full picture before engaging.
