## Framework Section

**Subhead:** The 3-Layer Compliance Stack for Boutique Wealth Managers

**Layer 1 — Swiss Domestic: nFADP**
AI processing of any client personal data requires lawful basis, purpose limitation, and data subject rights processes. High-risk AI requires a documented DPIA.

**Layer 2 — Swiss International: FINMA + FATF + CARF**
CARF is live. FATF evaluation is active. AI systems used in AML/KYC, client suitability, or investment advice must be documented, auditable, and consistent with FINMA supervisory expectations.

**Layer 3 — EU Extraterritorial: EU AI Act (for EU-nexus firms)**
If you have EU-based clients or process data through EU-based infrastructure, the EU AI Act applies. GPAI obligations have been active since August 2, 2025. High-risk AI obligations activate August 2, 2026.
