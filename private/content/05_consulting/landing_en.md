## Framework Section

**Subhead:** The 3-Axis Framework for Small Consulting Firms

**Axis 1: GPAI Transparency (EU AI Act, if applicable)**
Does your firm use AI tools that serve EU clients or process EU-based data? If yes, GPAI transparency obligations are active. You must disclose AI-generated content, maintain technical documentation, and have an acceptable use policy.

**Axis 2: nFADP Domestic Compliance (Swiss)**
Does any AI tool used in client projects process personal client data? If yes, nFADP Art. 5(c) data controller obligations are active. DPIA may be required under Art. 22 for high-risk processing.

**Axis 3: Client Expectation Management**
Your clients — particularly institutional clients — are increasingly asking about AI use in project delivery. Having a documented AI use policy is becoming a client expectation, not just a regulatory requirement.
