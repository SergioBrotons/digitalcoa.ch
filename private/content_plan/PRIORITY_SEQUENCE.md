## Why This Order

**Healthcare (1st)**: Hardest deadline in the entire portfolio. Swissmedic confirmed NO grace period.
~13 weeks until Jul 1 2026. Every day of delay = exposure for AI diagnostic tools already in use.
The Swissdamed entry is the strongest cold-call hook across all 6 verticals.

**Legal (2nd)**: 46-point governance gap (69% individual AI use vs 23% formal firm policies) is the
strongest quantified urgency signal across all verticals. FDPIC enforcement gap = advisory white space.
Every Geneva managing partner understands the FDPIC risk. No boutique competitor serves this ICP.

**Finance/WM (3rd)**: CARF has been operative since Jan 1 2026. FATF evaluation is the forcing function.
76% Geneva FS AI adoption (highest sector rate) but no boutique AI governance competitor.
Wealth managers respond to regulatory deadline framing.

**Real Estate (4th)**: 67% AI adoption = highest of any vertical. GAIGI creates category awareness.
Retroactive compliance framing ("govern the AI you already have") is a lower-friction entry point.
RE agencies are used to compliance relationships (notaires, AML).

**Consulting (5th)**: GPAI obligations have been active since Aug 2 2025 (~8 months already).
Federal Council First Draft May 2026 will trigger market-wide awareness spike.
Consultants are natural buyers of frameworks.

**Cross-vertical (6th)**: Synthesis page for buyers who don't fit one vertical cleanly.
Portfolio approach reinforces breadth and depth simultaneously.
