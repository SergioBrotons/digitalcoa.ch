# Geneva Signal Scout
## Intelligence Workflow

### Objective
Identify high-value leads in Geneva: Law firms, Wealth Management, Family Offices experiencing governance or AI transition signals.

---

## Sources

### 1. Registre du Commerce de Genève (RCG)
- https://register-of-commerce-ge.ch/
- Monitor new registrations, appointments, departures
- Focus: "Managing Director", "CEO", "Head of AI", "Chief Digital Officer"

### 2. LinkedIn Signals
- Company updates from: Pictet, Lombard Odier, UBP, Edmond de Rothschild, Mirabaud, Syz
- Keywords: "AI governance", "Chief AI Officer", "digital transformation", "data strategy"
- NEW for Geneva region

### 3. Swiss Legal/Regulatory
- FINMA announcements
- OFJ (Ordonnance sur les Institutions Financières)
- Data protection updates (PFADP/LPD)
