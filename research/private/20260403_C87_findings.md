- **Type:** Projet budget bis — presented late March 2026
- **Charges:** CHF 11.32B (+CHF 436.8M / +4% vs 2025)
- **Revenues:** CHF 10.72B (+CHF 93.1M / +0.9%)
- **Operating mode:** Douzièmes provisoires since December 2025

- **Tax revenue:** Revised down CHF 154M (conjoncturel slowdown, wholesale trade)
- **SNB revenue:** Revised up +CHF 78M
